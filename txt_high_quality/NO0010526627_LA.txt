The Bondholders are, through their subscription, purchase or other transfer of Bond bound by the terms o f the Bond Agreement and other Finance Documents, as the initial Bondholders in the subscription agreements granted authority to the Bond Trustee to finalize and execute the Bond Agreement on their behalf while all Bond transfers are subject to the terms of this Bond Agreement and all Bond transferees are, in taking transfer of Bonds, deemed to have accepted the terms of the Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and will automatically become parties to the Bond Agreement upon completed transfer having been registered in the Securities Register, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 18.1.

2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds o f the Bonds shall be employed for (i) refinancing of the existing DOFSUB02 bond (ISIN NO 001 045320.2) and (ii) general corporate purposes.
