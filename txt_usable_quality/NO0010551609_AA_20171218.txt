This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement No. 4") is dated on 18 December 2017 between Lotos Exploration and Production Norge AS (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee AS (previously Norsk Tillitsmann ASA) (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement No. 4
The Amendment Agreement No, 4 comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement " 11 per cent Lotos Exploration and Production Norge AS Second Priority Secured Callable Bond Issue 2009/2013" dated 5 November 2009 (the "Bond Agreement") with ISIN NO001 055160.9 (the "Bond Issue"), as previously amended by an amendment agreement dated 9 November 2011 (the "Amendment Agreement No. 1"), an amendment agreement dated 8 November 2012 (the "Amendment Agreement No. 2"), and an amendment agreement dated 24 October 2016 (the "Amendment Agreement No. 3"), based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' Meeting dated 18 December 2017 (the "Bondholders' Meeting").
2. Incorporation of defined terms
Unless contrary indication appears, a term defined in the Bond Agreement has the same meaning in this Amendment Agreement No. 4. The principles of construction set out in the Bond Agreement shall have effect as if set out in this Amendment Agreement No. 4.
3. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) Clause 10.2.2 shall be amended to read asfollows:
"Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least five (5) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
4. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. The Bond Agreement, Amendment Agreement No. 1, Amendment Agreement No. 2, Amendment Agreement No. 3 and this Amendment Agreement No. 4 will constitute together the entire agreement between the paities with respect to the above mentioned Bond Issue.
5. Conditions precedent
This Amendment Agreement No. 4 is subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a) this Amendment Agreement No. 4 duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) copies of all necessaiy corporate resolutions necessary to enter into this Amendment Agreement No. 4; and

5. Representations and Warranties
All representations and warranties made by the Issuer under clause 7.1 of the Bond Agreement shall be deemed repeated by signing this Amendment Agreement No. 4.

6. Governing Law
All and any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement No. 4 (including but not limited to its execution) which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law by the Oslo District Court.

7. Commencement

The amendments to the Bond Agreement will enter into force at the date of this Amendment Agreement No. 4.
