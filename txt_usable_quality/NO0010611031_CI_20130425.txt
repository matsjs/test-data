JASPER INVESTMENTS LIMITED (SGX: FQ7) (Company Registration No. 198700983H) JASPER EXPLORER COMMENCES DRILLING 1st WELL FOR CNOOC CONGO SA
The Company wishes to announce that its drillship, the "Jasper Explorer" has commenced drilling operations at CNOOC Congo SA's first well offshore Republic of Congo. Under the contract with CNOOC Congo SA, the "Jasper Explorer" is to drill two firm wells plus one option well. All wells are to be drilled in direct continuation of one another.
By Order of the Board 25 April 2013
