This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 1 March 2017 between Golden Energy Offshore Service AS (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee").

1.

The Scope of the Amendment Agreement

The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement "FRN Golden Energy Offshore Services AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2014/2017" - ISIN 001 0711732, dated 28 May 2014 and amended 26 April 2016, based on the resolution adopted at the Bondholders' Meeting on 27 February 2017.

2.

Amendments to the Bond Agreement

The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

(i) The Issuer's obligation to comply with the Bond Agreement's provision to obtain market valuation reports semi-annually is waived as of 31 December 2016 and up until 31 December 2017.

(ii) The Asset Cover Ratio covenant (Clause 13.6 (a) Asset Cover Ratio) is waived until 31 December 2017.

3.

Confirmation of the Bond Agreement

All other provisions of the Bond Agreement, as amended, remain in full force and effect.

4.

Miscellaneous

4.1

Incorporation of terms

Unless the context otherwise require, references to Clauses herein are references to the Clauses of the Bond Agreement.

The provisions of Clause 18.7 (Dispute resolution and legal venue) of the Bond Agreement shall be incorporated into this Amendment Agreement as if set out in full herein, and as if references in that Clause to "this Bond Agreement" are references to this Amendment Agreement.

4.2

Additional Finance Document

This Amendment Agreement constitutes a Finance Document for the purposes of the Bond Agreement.

4.3

Invalidity

If at any time any provision of this Amendment Agreement is or becomes illegal, invalid or unenforceable in any respect under any law of any jurisdiction, neither the legality, validity or enforceability of the remaining provisions nor the legality, validity or enforceability of such provisions under the law of any other jurisdiction shall in any way be affected or impaired.

4.4

Effectiviness

The amendments to the Bond Agreement shall be effective from the date of this Amendment Agreement.
