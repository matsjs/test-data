Notification of Put Option (Realisation Event) and exercise of Call Option
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is acting as bond trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Global Investments Group Finance Ltd. is the issuer (the "Issuer").
Notification of Bondholders Put Option (Realisation Event) The Issuer has in a letter to the Bond Trustee o f 14 October 2015 notified that a Realisation Event has occurred. The Net Proceeds from the Realisation Event amount to USD 14,330,098. The right of pre-payment under the put option is limited in aggregate for all Bondholders to 50% o f the Net Proceeds, amounting to USD 7,165,049. Any prepayment under the Put Option shall be made pro rata to exercising Bondholders, and be limited in aggregate to the pro rata share of the exercising Bondholders' share in the total Bonds issued and outstanding at any time. Any prepayment under the Put Option will be at par value - plus accrued interest.
The put option must be exercised by the Bondholders within 60 calendar days after 14 October 2015, i.e. no later than Sunday 13 December 2015. The settlement date for the put option will be Friday 18 December 2015.
Information on the technical process of the redemption mechanism under the Put Option will be provided at a later stage.
Exercise of Issuer's Call Option The Issuer has in accordance with a letter to the Bond Trustee o f 14 October 2015 also notified that the Issuer will exercise the Call Option to redeem Bonds with a nominal value o f USD 100,000,000 in accordance with clause 10.2.1 (i) of the bond agreement dated 20 September 2012.
The Bonds will be redeemed on 27 November 2015 at 105 % o f par value - plus accrued interest. The redemption will be made pro rata in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository, and the record date will be end of business 24 November 2015. The Bonds that will be redeemed cannot be traded after this date.
