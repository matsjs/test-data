This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 10 February 2010 between Interoil Exploration and Production ASA (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the agreement Loan Agreement " 12.5 per cent Interoil Exploration and Production ASA Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010" - ISIN N0001036280.9, based on a voluntary offer from the Borrower that was extended to the Bondholders at the Bondholders' meeting held 28 January 2010. The votes that were cast at the Bondholders meeting were cast based on the Borrowers offer to pay an increased interest on the Loan.
2. Amendment to the Loan Agreement
The Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) Clause 9.1 shall read:
" The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 12.5 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on each Interest Payment Date each year, the first interest Payment Date being in September 2007.
From and including 28 January 2010 until the Loan is repaid in full, the Loan shall accrue interest at the interest rate set out in Clause 11.1 (12.50 per cent per annum plus 5.00 percentage points per annum), as if the Loan was declared in Default on 28 January 2010. In the event that the Loan is subsequently declared in Default, the interest rate shall not be increased further, beyond the 12.50 percent per annum plus 5.00 per cent per annum."
3. Confirmation of the Loan Agreement
All other provisions of the Loan Agreement remain in full force. The Loan Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Loan.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
