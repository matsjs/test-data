aRUBICON OFFSHORE INTERNATIONAL Rubicon Offshore Holdings Limited Group Management Financial Statements For the period ended 31 March 2013
Background Rubicon is an offshore oilfield service company focused on the ownership and operation of two floating, production, storage and off-loading vessels ("FPSOs"). Management review of the first quarter 2013 Rubicon is pleased to present its latest management report. Rubicon Intrepid is contracted to the Galoc Production Company for deployment at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines. The vessel achieved uptime of over 99.9% during the quarter, continuing the good levels of uptime achieved since production re-start in early April 2012, following the turret upgrade. Preparations for the Phase 2 development of the field are proceeding, with 1st oil from Phase 2 now expected to be achieved in the fourth quarter of 2013.
