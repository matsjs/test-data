SUMMONS TO BONDHOLDERS' MEETING
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") acts as trustee for the holders of bonds (the "Bondholders") in the following loans issued by PA Resources AB (the "Company") NO 001 060572.8 " 12,25% PA Resources AB Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2011/2016" (the "Bond Loan") as regulated in the bond agreement dated 3 April 2011 (the "Bond Agreement").
The Company has prior to convening this meeting approached Bondholders holding more than 2/3 of the principal face amount of the Bond Loan (together the "Consenting Bondholders"). The Consenting Bondholders have committed to support and vote in favour of the matters referred to herein.
All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Bond Agreement unless otherwise stated.
The information regarding the operation and financial status of the Issuer and market conditions is provided by the Issuer, and NTM expressly disclaims any liability whatsoever related to such information.

1.

Background

The Bond Agreement was entered into 3 April 2011, partly to refinance two existing bonds, (i) the 10 per cent PA Resources AB Senior Secured Bond Issue 2006/2011 with Call Option with ISIN NO 001 031388.5 issued by the Issuer, and (ii) the FRN Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012 with ISIN NO 001 035799.9 issued by Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd (together the "Existing Bonds"). The Existing Bonds will only be refinanced in part, and will remain in force until they are due.

2.

The Proposal to the bondholders in the Bond Loan

The Company proposes the following resolution to be passed by the bondholders in the Bond Loan:

(i) to include the following definition of Existing Bond Issues:

Existing Bond Issues: means (i) the 10 per cent PA Resources AB Senior Secured Bond Issue 2006/2011 with Call Option with ISIN NO 001 031388.5 issued by the Issuer, and (ii) the FRN Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012 with ISIN NO 001 035799.9 issued by Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd.
