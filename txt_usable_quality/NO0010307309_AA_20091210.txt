CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

a)

The provisions of Clause 2 (Amendments to the Original Loan Agreement) shall be effective

only if and when the Loan Trustee has received all of the following documents and other

evidence in form and substance satisfactory to the Loan Trustee:

(I)

certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Borrower resolving to sign

this Amendment Agreement;

(ii)

evidence of completion of the Conversion; and

(iii)

evidence of the establishment of the security set out in Clause 2.3 in favour of the

Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), including legal opinions, satisfactory to

the Loan Trustee.

b)

The Loan Trustee shall notify the Borrower, the Guarantors and the Bondholders promptly upon

being satisfied with the conditions precedent set out in paragraph a) above.

c)

If the conditions in paragraph a) above have not been fulfilled or waived by the Loan Trustee

by 31 January 2010 (or by such later date as may be agreed in writing between the Loan

Trustee and the Borrower), then this Amendment Agreement shall thereupon become null and

void and neither of the parties shall have any rights against the other party hereunder.
