Issue of PIK bonds and payment of January 2018 interest
Nordic Trustee ASA acts as trustee for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bonds") issued by Latina Offshore Limited as issuer.
Reference is made to the written resolution adopted by the Bondholders 17 January 2018 (the "Resolution"), and the notice (the "Notice") dated 18 January 2018 with respect to the issue of PIK bonds in relation to the Separated Claim as defined in the Notice.
Bondholders are hereby informed that:
1) PIK bonds was issued to the holders o f the Separated Claim at 19 January 2018 (not per 18 January 2018 as stated in the Notice).
2) A part payment of the January 2018 Interest Payment (as defined in the Resolution) in the amount of USD 8,600,000 was paid to the holders thereof per 22 January 2018. The part payment constitutes 63.28255% of the January 2018 Interest Payment with the remaining portion to fall due on 30 January 2018 pursuant to the Resolution.
