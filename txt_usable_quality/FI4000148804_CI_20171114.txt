We refer to the terms and conditions of the Bonds, originally dated 26 March 2015 (the "Terms and Conditions"). Terms defined in the Terms and Conditions shall have the same meaning in this Notice of Early Redemption unless given a different meaning herein.
Notice is hereby given that Containerships will exercise its call option right in relation to the Bonds in accordance with Clause 10.3 (Voluntary Total Redemption) of the Terms and Conditions. This Notice of Early Redemption is irrevocable.
