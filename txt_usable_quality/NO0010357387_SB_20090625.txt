Summons to Bondholders' Meeting - Further postponement of interest payment
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM" or the "Trustee") acts as loan trustee for the bond loan with ISIN NO 001035738.7 (the "USD 175 million Bond" or the "Loan") where Nexus 1 Private Limited is the Borrower (the "Borrower", "Nexus", or the "Company").
All capitalized terms used herein and not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned thereto in the agreement for the Loan (the "Loan Agreement") or this summons to the Bondholders' meeting unless otherwise stated.
The information regarding the operational and financial status o f the Company and market conditions is provided by the Company, and the Loan Trustee expressly disclaims any liability whatsoever related to such information.
Background
Please refer to the Summons to Bondholders' Meeting dated 9 June 2009.
In the Bondholders' Meetings held 17 June 2009 the interest payments due 7 June 2009 related to the USD 175 million Bond were deferred to 24 June 2009.
The Company is still in discussions with the Bondholders and lenders under the bank facility (the "Banks"), but as no firm agreement has been secured a further deferral of interest payments is imperative. The Banks have agreed to not treating Nexus as being in default until 6 July 2009, the Bank are releasing the required funds to finance the operational expense for the Company until 6 July 2009, and the Banks are agreeing to continue its discussions with the Bondholders Committee.
For further information about the Company, please also refer to the web site www.nexusfp.no.

The Proposal
The Company proposes the following (the "Proposal"):
The interest payments originally due 7 June 2009, and deferred to 24 June 2009, to be further deferred until 6 July 2009, subject to the Banks (a) not treating Nexus as being in default until 6 July 2009, (b) releasing the required funds to finance the operational expense for the Company until 6 July 2009, and (c) agreeing to continue discussions with the Bondholders' Committee.
Evaluation of the Proposal NTM puts the request forward to the Bondholders without further evaluation or recommendation, and amplifies that each Bondholder cast its vote on the Bondholders' meeting based on its own judgement.
It is recommended that the Bondholders consult with their own financial, legal and tax advisors to evaluate the consequences of the Proposal. All Bondholders must be aware that even if the Proposal is accepted, the Company still has challenges going forward and the Company might be unable to meet its commitments in the future.

Bondholders are hereby summoned to a Bondholders' meeting in each Loan:
Time: 3 July 2009 at 13.00 (Oslo time). Place: The premises of Norsk Tillitsmann ASA, Haakon VII's gate 1, Oslo

The interest payments originally due 7 June 2009, and deferred to 24 June 2009, to be further deferred until 6 July 2009, subject to the Banks (a) not treating Nexus as being in default until 6 July 2009, (b) releasing the required funds to finance the operational expense for the Company until 6 July 2009, and (c) agreeing to continue discussions with the Bondholders Committee.
