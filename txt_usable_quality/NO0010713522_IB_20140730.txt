RE: Access to Sanjel Corporation's Data Room for Bondholders and Potential Bondholders
Sanjel Corporation ("Sanjel" or the "Company") maintains a secure Data Room which contains the Company's confidential information for use by bondholders and potential bondholders. The Data Room website is only for use by the holders and prospective holders of 7.50% Sanjel Corporation Callable Bonds Issue 2014/2019 ("Bonds") issued pursuant to the terms of the Bond Agreement between Sanjel Corporation ("Sanjel") and Nordic Trustee ASA dated June 18, 2014. Please follow the instructions below in order to be granted access to this website in order to review certain confidential information of Sanjel and its affiliates or associates. Bondholders If you are a bona fide holder of the bonds, please contact Nordic Trustee at post(a>nordictrustee.com and provide proof of your bondholding. Nordic Trustee will then provide instructions on how to gain access to the secure data room. Potential Bondholders If you are a prospective investor in the bonds, please proceed to www.sanjel.com/lenderrelations and follow the instructions on the web page in order to gain access to the secure data room. Additionally, you can reach Sanjel's Lender Relations team via email at lendersrelationsPsaniel.com with any questions.
