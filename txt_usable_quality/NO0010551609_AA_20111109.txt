This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement No. 1") is dated on 9thNovember 2011 between Lotos Exploration and Production Norge AS (the "Issuer") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement No. 1
The Amendment Agreement NO 1 comprises amendments of the agreement "11 per cent Lotos Exploration and Production Norge AS Second Priority Secured Callable Bond Issue 2009/2013" dated 5 November 2009 (the "Bond Agreement") with ISIN NO001 055160.9 (the "Bond Issue"), based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 9th November 2011 (the "Bondholders' Meeting").
2. Incorporation of defined terms
Unless contrary indication appears, a term defined in the Bond Agreement has the same meaning in this Amendment Agreement No. 1. The principles of construction set out in the Bond Agreement shall have effect as if set out in this Amendment Agreement No. 1.
3. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) The definition o f "Interest Payment Date "shall be amended and restated to be read asfollows:
""Interest Payment Date" means 13 November each year and the Maturity Date, except 13 November 2011. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention."
(ii) The definition o f "Maturity Date " shall be amended and restated to be read asfollows:
""Maturity Date" means 13 November 2014 or an earlier maturity date as provided for in this Bond Agreement. Any further adjustment may be made according to the Business Day Convention."
(in) Clause 9.1 shall be amended and restated to be read asfollows:
"The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a coupon rate as follows (the "Fixed Rate");
