Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee") is appointed loan trustee for the Loans (as defined below) issued by Master Marine ASA (the "Borrower" or the "Company") on behalf of the bondholders (the "Bondholders").

All capitalised terms used herein shall, unless otherwise stated, have the meaning ascribed to them in the loan agreement for the Convertible Bond dated 30 May 2007 and the loan agreement for the High Yield Bond dated 13 June 2008 (the "Loan Agreements").
