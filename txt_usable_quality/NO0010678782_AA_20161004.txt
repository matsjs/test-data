1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement

The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the amended and restated bond Agreement 5.70% GlobalConnect A/S Senior Secured Bond Issue 2013/2018 - ISIN 001 0678782 dated 13 March 2015 (the "Bond Agreement"), based on resolution by the Bond Trustee to correct certain drafting errors in the Bond Agreement as further set out in a notice to the Bondholders dated 31 August 2016.

2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement

The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

(i) The definition of "Permitted Financial Indebtedness litra (i)" shall read:

"any refinancing o f any Permitted Financial Indebtedness in (b) - (g) above"

(ii) New definition of "Acquisition Facilities" shall read:

"means anyfuture secured Financial Indebtedness provided by commercial and/or mortgage institutes (DK: Realkreditinstitutter) to any Group Company with the purpose o ffinancing assets not already covered by the Security Interest, for additional investments infibre cables and related equipment, telehousing activities and related equipment"

3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
5. Commencement
The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence at the date hereof.
