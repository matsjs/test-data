Oro Negro Drilling Pte. Ltd. Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019
Mexico City, Mexico. September 21, 2017. Pursuant to the terms of the amended and restated bond agreement between Oro Negro Drilling Pte. Ltd. (the "Company") and Nordic Trustee ASA, as trustee of the holders (the "Bondholders") of the 7.50% Oro Negro Drilling Pte. Ltd. Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019, the Company announces that in order to preserve its operations and activities in the ordinary course of business, Perforadora Oro Negro, S. de R.L. de C.V. ("Perforadora"), an affiliated entity of the Company, voluntarily filed for an insolvency proceeding (concurso mercantil).
Perforadora believes that the filing will allow it to protect its assets and operations and provide the framework for negotiations with its creditors for the conservation o f its business. In accordance with applicable Mexican law, any provision in agreements that worsens the situation o f Perforadora as a result of the filing of the petition will not be deemed effective and any actions taken in violation of the foregoing and/or applicable law shall result in liability.
Perforadora and the Company reiterate their commitment to continue carrying out their operations and activities in the ordinary course of business, professionally, safely and transparently as they have done to date.
