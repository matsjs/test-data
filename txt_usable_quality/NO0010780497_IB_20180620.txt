New super senior loan facility
Nordic Trustee AS (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the holders of the bonds (the "Bondholders") in the abovementioned bond issue (the 'Bond Issue") issued by Hilleren Invest AS (the "Issuer"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement, dated 14 December 2016 and made between the Bond Trustee and the Issuer. Reference is made to the summons to Bondholders' Meeting dated 19 March 2018, where the Issuer under Clause 3 (IV) proposed that it shall be allowed to assume a new super senior loan facility in the maximum amount of NOK 3,500,000 on equal or better terms than the Bond Issue and ranking ahead of the Bond Issue in priority (such funds to be used in accordance with a budget). The proposals put forward by the Issuer were approved by the Bondholders in the Bondholders' Meeting on 6 April 2018. The Bond Trustee wants to inform the Bondholders that the Issuer and/or its subsidiaiy Moldalia Utvikling AS is currently investigating the possibility to assume such super senior financing, and that such financing might be imminent.
