Notice of a Written Bondholders' Resolution
Nordic Trustee AS acts as bond trustee for the bondholders in the above mentioned bond issue issued by Latina Offshore Holding Limited.

· The payment of the Remaining January Interest Payment was effectuated through the Norwegian Securities Depository on 2 March 2018
· The payment of USD 510,000 to redeem Bonds at 102% of par value (i.e., to redeem a nominal amount of USD 500,000 in Bonds) will be effectuated through the Norwegian Securities Depository on 8 March 2018 with 6 March 2018 as record date.
