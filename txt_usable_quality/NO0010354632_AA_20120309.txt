WHEREAS:

(A) The Borrower and the Loan Trustee entered into a loan agreement dated 9 February 2007, as amended on 23 July 2010 and 23 August 2010 (the "Loan Agreement") in respect of the bond issue "FRN Sea Production Ltd Callable Senior Secured Bond Issue 2007/2012" ISIN NO 001 035463.2.

(B)

Under the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, the Borrower issued a bond loan

in a principal amount of USD 130,000,000 (the "Loan").

(C)

Pursuant to a resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 7 February 2012 (the

"Resolution"), the Bondholders have approved the terms of a sale of the M/V Sea Cat

and accompanying amendments to the Loan Agreement (the "Amended Loan

Agreement") to facilitate such sale (the "Sale").

(D) On the terms set forth herein, the parties have agreed to enter into this Amendment Agreement to provide for the amendment of the Loan Agreement,

1.

DEFINITIONS

Capitalized terms not otherwise defined in this Amendment Agreement shall have the meanings given to them in the Amended Loan Agreement.

2.

AMENDMENT OF THE EXISTING LOAN AGREEMENT

The Parties hereby agree that with effect from 7 February 2012, the Loan Agreement shall be and is hereby amended as follows:

2.1 The second paragraph of Clause 10.4 of the Loan Agreement shall be amended to insert the following at the end of the existing paragraph:

"...provided that if the Aframax Tanker known as M/V Sea Cat (the "Sea Cat") is sold for a purchase price which realises net cash sale proceeds of USD 5,000,000 or more, the Borrower shall use the net sale proceeds to pay the interest due on the Loan on the Interest Payment Date of 14 February 2012 (to the extent such interest has not already been paid in full), and use the full balance of the net sale proceeds to redeem outstanding Bonds at a price equal to 101% of par value (plus

UKDOCS/1070253.1

any accrued interest on the redeemed amount), and where the sale of the Sea Cat is completed prior to the Interest Payment Date of 14 February 2012, the net sale proceeds shall be transferred to the Loan Trustee upon completion of the sale and held by the Loan Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders for application as provided above on 14 February 2012."

2.2 Clause 13.2(k) of the Loan Agreement shall be deleted and replaced with the following:

"not make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions to its shareholders until all amounts outstanding under the Loan have been paid in full".

2.3 The definition of "Maturity Date" shall be amended so that the words "14 February 2012" are replaced with "13 April 2012".

3.

FINANCE DOCUMENTS

This Amendment Agreement is a Finance Document for the purpose of the Amended Loan Agreement.

Each of the parties to this Amendment Agreement acknowledge and agree that, with effect from 7 February 2012, each reference in any Finance Document to the Loan Agreement shall be construed as a reference to the Amended Loan Agreement.

Each of the parties to this Amendment Agreement acknowledge and agree that, except as expressly provided In this Amendment Agreement or in the Amended Loan Agreement, each of the Finance Documents to which it is a party remains in full force and effect.

4.

AUTHORISATION OF THE LOAN TRUSTEE

By passing the Resolution, the Bondholders have granted authority to the Loan Trustee to finalise and execute this Amendment Agreement, the Amended Loan Agreement and the Finance Documents on their behalf and to take all other actions the Loan Trustee reasonably determines are necessary or desirable in order to implement the terms of the Sale.

5.

MISCELLANEOUS

Invalidity of any Provision

If any provision of this Amendment Agreement is or becomes invalid, illegal or unenforceable in any respect under any law, the validity, legality and enforceability of the remaining provisions shall not be affected or impaired in any way,

Counterparts

This Amendment Agreement may be executed in any number of counterparts and all of those counterparts taken together will be deemed to constitute one and the same instrument.

6.

GOVERNING LAW AND SUBMISSION TO JURISDICTION

Governing Law

This Amendment Agreement and all disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Amendment Agreement between the Loan Trustee, the Bondholders and the Borrower shall be governed by Norwegian law.
