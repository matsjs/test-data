Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") acts as bond trustee for the holders of the 15 per cent Interoil Exploration & Production ASA Senior Callable Bond Issue 2010/2016 (the "Bond") issued by Interoil Exploration & Production ASA (the "Issuer" or "Interoil").
Reference is made to the summons to Bondholders' Meeting dated 6 January 2015 (the "Summons") and the information letter to the Bondholders regarding record dates dated 20 January 2015 (the "Information Letter"). All capitalised terms used herein but not otherwise defined shall have the meanings assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bond between, amongst others, the Issuer and the Bond Trustee (acting for and on behalf of the holders of the Bonds (the "Bondholders")) dated 8 September 2010 (the "Bond Agreement"), the Summons and the Information Letter.
As part of the restructuring a portion of the Bond was converted into 65,000,000 new shares in Interoil. The Bond Trustee has held these on behalf of the Bondholders in anticipation of the expiry of the offer period for the mandatory offer from Andes Energia pic for the remaining shares in Interoil. The offer period expired on 18 March 2015, and the shares will be distributed pro rata to the Bondholders as of the Record Date on or about 24 March 2015. The Bondholders will receive 104,838.71 shares in Interoil per Bond with face value ofNOK 500,000 held, rounded down to the nearest share.
Note that the shares will be delivered under a temporary ISIN (NO 001 0729999) and cannot be traded on Oslo Stock Exchange ("OSE") before a listing prospectus is approved by Finanstilsynet (the Norwegian FSA). Upon approval of the listing prospectus the shares will become listed on OSE under the same ISIN (NO 001 0284318) and ticker code (lOX) as the currently outstanding Interoil shares. The shares are currently expected to be tradable on OSE on or about 25 March 2015.
