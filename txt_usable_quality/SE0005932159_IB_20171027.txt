Conditional notice of early voluntary total redemption
Stockholm. 27 October 2017
Orexo AB (publ) (the "Company") hereby gives notice to all holders of the Company's SEK 1.000,000,000 senior unsecured callable floating rate bonds 2014/2018 with ISIN SE0005932159 (the "Bonds") of exercise of its option for early voluntary redemption of the Bonds in full, in accordance with section 11,3 {Early voluntaiy redemption by the Issuer (call option)) o f the terms and conditions for the Bonds (the "Terms and Conditions").
Unless otherwise defined in this notice, all words and expressions defined in the Terms and Conditions shall have the same meaning in this notice.
The early voluntary total redemption will occur on 22 November 2017 (the "Early Redemption Date"). The total outstanding amount of the Bonds is SEK 342.000. 000. The Bonds will be redeemed at 101.00 per cent, of the Nominal Amount {i.e. SEK 1,010,000 per Bond, the Nominal Amount being SEK 1.000. 000) together with accrued but unpaid Interest from, but excluding, the preceding Interest Payment Date on 9 November 2017, up to, and including, the Early Redemption Date (the "Call Option Price").
The Call Option Price will be disbursed to Holders registered as owners of Bonds on the applicable Record Date, which will be 15 November 2017. In conjunction with the redemption, the Bonds will be delisted from the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Stockholm.
This notice of early voluntaiy total redemption is irrevocable but conditional upon the successful issue of new bonds at a minimum aggregate amount of SEK 300,000,000 prior to the Record Date stated in the paragraph above (the "Condition"). The fulfilment of the Condition will be confirmed to the Holders through a press release. Should the Condition not be fulfilled by the Record Date stated in the paragraph above or has not othenvise been waived by the Company (at the Company's sole discretion), the Bonds will not be redeemed in accordance with the terms set out in this notice which will, in such case, be confirmed to the Holders through a press release.
This conditional notice of early voluntary total redemption is governed by Swedish law. This notice is, for the avoidance of doubt, not an offer to purchase or sell financial instruments.
Stockholm, 27 October 2017, Orexo AB (publ)
