VOLUNTARY OFFER TO BUY BACK IMSK04 AND IMSK09 PRO BONDS

I.M. Skaugen SE (OSE: IMSK) (the "Company") today, 5 March 2012, announces a voluntary offer for the following of the Company's outstanding bonds (the "Offer"):
 up to a nominal amount of NOK 125 million (the full outstanding amount excluding bonds held by the Company) in the bond issue IMSK04 (ISIN: NO 0010367899); and
 up to a nominal amount of NOK 2.5 million (the full outstanding amount excluding bonds held by the Company) in the bond issue IMSK09 PRO (ISIN: NO 0010587991).

The voluntary offering price is 100.00% (par) for IMSK04 and 102.60 % for IMSK09 PRO (plus any unpaid interest accrued).

Bondholders accepting the Offer will, subject to the limitations set out below, have the option to choose one of two forms of settlement:
 exchange into I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (to be named IMSK12, with ISIN: NO 0010636632) with final maturity date 27 February 2015 and a coupon of NIBOR + 825 bps; or
 cash payment

Exchange into I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (IMSK12) will, however, only be possible to the extent that I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (IMSK12) bonds are available for exchange settlement.

At the time of announcing the Offer, an undisclosed amount of bonds in I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (IMSK12) is available for exchange settlement. This amount is subject to market change. All exchanges shall be carried out on the following conditions:
 Price 100.00% (par) for I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (IMSK12) (plus any unpaid and accrued interest);
 the nominal exchange ratio of bonds tendered to bonds acquired shall be 1:1; and  "first come, first serve" principal.

To the extent that I.M. Skaugen SE 12/15 (IMSK12) bonds are not available for exchange settlement all bonds tendered in the Offer will be settled in cash.

The Offer commences at 5 March 2012 and expires at 1600 CET on 23 March 2012 (the "Offer Period"). In order to accept the Offer, relevant bondholders must validly tender their Bonds by the end of the Offer Period using the attached acceptance form in Appendix 1 (the "Acceptance Form").

The result of the Offer and subsequent allocation is expected to be announced on 26 March 2012. Settlement date for any transactions pursuant to the Offer is expected to take place on 29 March 2012.

The Company may, at its sole discretion amend or terminate the Offer at any time within the Offer Period. Details of any such amendment or termination will be announced on Newsweb (www.newsweb.no) as soon as practically possible after any such decision is made.

ABG Sundal Collier Norge ASA and Swedbank First Securities have been engaged as advisors and receiving agents (the "Agents") in connection with the Offer. Any acceptance of the Offer shall be communicated to the Agents as set out in the Acceptance Form.
