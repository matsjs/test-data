3.

Waiver of priority with regard to Security Interests under the Bond Agreement

Pursuant to the terms and conditions of the Co-ordination Agreement, the Bond Trustee accepts and agrees that the Yard might enforce the Senior Security (as defined in the Co ordination Agreement) to seek coverage for any part o f the Senior Secured Sum which is overdue and payable by the Issuer to the Yard.

In accordance with the terms of the Co-ordination Agreement, any overdue and payable amounts under the Bond Agreement will be subject to the priority o f the Senior Security (as defined in the Co-ordination Agreement), in particular as set out in Clause 2 and Clause 4 of the Co-ordination Agreement with regard to the enforcement o f the Security Interests.

The detailed terms related to the parties' rights and obligations with regard to the Security Interests are set out in the Co-ordination Agreement.

4.

Confirmation of the Bond Agreement

In the event o f any inconsistency between the terms o f the Co-ordination Agreement and those o f the Bond Agreement, the terms o f the Co-ordination Agreement shall prevail and apply. Save as aforesaid, all other provisions o f the Bond Agreement shall remain unchanged by this Amendment Agreement and shall remain in full force and effect.

The Issuer and the Bond Trustee agree that the Bond Agreement and the Amendment Agreement jointly constitute the entire agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee with regard to the Bonds.
