Information to Bondholders - Payment of Cash Consideration
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Trustee") acts as loan trustee for the bond loans with ISIN NO 001 035738.7 in the amount of USD 37,956,000, and with ISIN NO 001 037520.7 in the amount of USD 29,069,000 (together the "Residual Loans"). Nexus Floating Production, Ltd (the "Company") is the Borrower under the Residual Loans. All capitalized terms used herein and not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the respective loan agreements for the Residual Loans, as amended and restated from time to time (together, the "Bond Loan Agreements"). The Company has paid the Cash Consideration to the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) in consideration for the release of the Bondholders' claims in respect of the Residual Loans and the cancellation of the Bond Loan Agreements. The Cash Consideration, net of fees and costs, will be distributed to the Bondholders on a pro rata basis, calculated by reference to the aggregate amount o f the Residual Amounts outstanding under each o f the Residual Loans. Please note that the payment may take more time than expected as several payments must be registered manually based on the information provided in the Forms of Notification and Registration submitted by each Bondholder participating in the Restructuring. The payment will constitute a payment of principal equal to 3.58075 % of the Residual Amounts outstanding under each of the Residual Loans. The Record Date is set to 21 November 2012, and the Payment Date will be set to 23 November 2012. Yours sincerely Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
