aRUBICON OFFSHORE INTERNATIONAL Rubicon Offshore Holdings Limited Group Management Financial Statements For the nine months ended 30 September 2012
Background Rubicon is an offshore oilfield service company focused on the ownership and operation of two floating, production, storage and off-loading vessels ("FPSOs"). Management review of the third quarter 2012 Rubicon is pleased to present its latest management report. Rubicon Intrepid is contracted to the Galoc Production Company for deployment at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines. The vessel achieved uptime of over 97% during the quarter. Rubicon Vantage is located at the Bualuang Field, offshore Thailand, where it is contracted to Salamander Energy. The vessel continued to achieve good levels of uptime which, during the 3rd quarter, was 99.8%.
Rubicon Vantage at the Bualuang Field, offshore Thailand
