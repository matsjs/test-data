(A) Solteq Oyj (the "Issuer"); and (B) Nordic Trustee Oy (acting on behalf of the Bondholders) (the "Trustee"). Capitalized terms used herein shall unless otherwise indicated have the meaning given to them in the Terms and Conditions (as defined below).
1. BACKGROUND On 1 July 2015 (the "Issue Date"), the Issuer and the Trustee entered into the Terms and Conditions for Solteq Oyj Up to EUR 40,000,000 Senior Unsecured Fixed Rate Notes , ISIN: FI4000157631, issued by the Issuer (the "Terms and Conditions"). The Parties have subsequent to the Issue Date noted that the Terms and Conditions have an erroneous definition of the term "Book-Entry Securities System". The Parties are now entering into this Letter in order to correct the definition of the term "BookEntry Securities System" to the Terms and Conditions.
2. AMENDMENTS TO THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS On the basis of Clause 18 (Amendments and Waivers) of the Terms and Conditions, the Parties hereby agree that the following definition is corrected in section 1.1 (Definitions) of the Terms and Conditions to read as follows: "Book-Entry Securities System" means the Infinity-system being part of the book-entry register maintained by the CSD or any other replacing book-entry securities system.
3. MISCELLANEOUS Except as otherwise provided in this letter, the Terms and Conditions shall remain unchanged in full force and effect in accordance with its terms. This Letter is designated as a Finance Document.
4. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION Clause 24 (Governing law and jurisdiction) of the Terms and Conditions shall apply to this Letter mutatis mutandis.
