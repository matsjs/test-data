Notice of Conditional Redemption
NorCell Sweden Holding 2 AB (publ) (the "Issuer")
287,058,000 10%% Senior Notes due 2019 (the "Notes")
ISIN: XS0704038115 (Regulation S) and XS0704041929 (Rule 144A) Common Code: 070403811 (Regulation S) and 070404192 (Rule 144A)
Redemption Date: July 4, 2014
Notice is hereby given pursuant to paragraph 5(b) of the Notes and the terms of the Indenture (the "Indenture") dated November 14, 2011 as amended from time to time among, inter alios, the Issuer, Deutsche Trustee Company Limited, as trustee (the "Trustee"), Deutsche Bank AG, London Branch, as principal paying agent (the "Paying Agent"), Deutsche Bank Luxembourg, S.A., as registrar, Luxembourg paying agent and transfer agent and Nordea Bank AB, as security agent, that the Issuer has elected to redeem 35% of the Notes outstanding on July 4, 2014 pursuant to Sections 3.03 and 13.01 of the Indenture, subject to the satisfaction or waiver of the Issuer's own Conditions Precedent (as defined below).
The terms and conditions of the redemption are as follows:
1. The redemption date for the Notes will be July 4, 2014 (the "Redemption Date"). The record date, on which any holder of Notes must hold any Notes to be entitled to the Redemption Price (as defined below), will be July 3, 2014.
2. The redemption price of the Notes is 110.750% of the principal amount of the Notes to be redeemed, plus accrued and unpaid interest from April 5, 2014 (the last Interest Payment Date for which interest on the Notes was paid) to (but not including) the Redemption Date, and Additional Amounts, if any (the "Redemption Price").
3. The Notes called for redemption must be surrendered to Deutsche Bank AG, London Branch as Paying Agent, at Winchester House, 1 Great Winchester Street, London EC2N 2DB United Kingdom, Facsimile No.: +44 (0)207 547 6149, Attention: Trust & Securities Services or Deutsche Bank Luxembourg, S.A., 2, Boulevard Konrad Adenauer, L-1115 Luxembourg, Facsimile: +352 473 136, Attention: Trust & Securities Ops, with copies to: Deutsche Trustee Company Limited, Winchester House, 1 Great Winchester Street, London EC2N 2DB, United Kingdom, Facsimile: +44 (0)207 547 6149, Attention: Trust & Securities Services to collect the Redemption Price.
4. 35% of the then outstanding principal amount of the Notes shall be redeemed on the Redemption Date on a pro rata basis as determined by the Trustee in accordance with the terms of the Indenture.
5. Unless the Issuer defaults in making such redemption payment or the Paying Agent is prohibited from making such payment pursuant to the terms of the Indenture, interest on the Notes called for redemption shall cease to accrue on and after the Redemption Date.
6. The Notes will be redeemed in accordance with paragraph 5(b) of the Notes and Section 3.03 of the Indenture.
7. The ISIN and Common Code numbers in relation to the Notes being redeemed are as set forth above. No representation is made as to the correctness or accuracy of such numbers listed in this

Notice of Conditional Redemption or printed on the Notes. Reliance may be placed only on the other identification numbers printed on the Notes.
8. The aggregate principal amount of Notes to be redeemed on the Redemption Date shall be 100,470,300, and the aggregate principal amount of Notes to be outstanding after the redemption shall be 186,587,700.
9. The Issuer's obligation to redeem any of the Notes on the Redemption Date is conditioned upon (i) the completion of an initial public offering by the Issuer's indirect parent, Com Hem Holding AB (publ) (the "IPO Entity") in a manner reasonably satisfactory to the Issuer in its discretion and receipt of funds by the IPO Entity in the determination of the Issuer sufficient to, among other things, (a) pay the Redemption Price for the Notes and pay all related expenses on the Redemption Date, (b) pay the redemption price and all related expenses on the relevant redemption date in connection with the conditional redemption of all outstanding 300,413,785 12.4% Senior PIK Notes due 2019, issued by the Issuer's direct parent, NorCell 1B AB, (c) refinance all or part of the senior secured credit facility dated August 3, 2011 and pay all fees and expenses in connection therewith, (d) pay all fees and expenses in connection with the initial public offering by the IPO Entity (the "Financing Condition") and (ii) the receipt of funds by the Issuer directly or indirectly from the IPO Entity prior to the Redemption Date in an amount sufficient to pay the Redemption Price for the Notes and all related expenses on the Redemption Date following the conversion of proceeds received from the IPO Entity from Swedish Krona into Euros (the "Currency Exchange Condition" and, together with the Financing Condition, the "Conditions Precedent"). Accordingly, none of the Notes shall be deemed due and payable on the Redemption Date unless and until the Conditions Precedent are satisfied or waived by the Issuer. The Issuer will inform holders of Notes by press release at least one day prior to the Redemption Date as to whether the Conditions Precedent will be satisfied or waived. If the Conditions Precedent are not satisfied or waived, any Notes previously surrendered to the Paying Agent shall be returned to the holders thereof.
Terms used but not defined herein have the meanings ascribed to them in the Indenture.
