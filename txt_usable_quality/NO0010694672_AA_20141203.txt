This amendment agreement (the "Agreement") has been entered into on 3 December 2014 between:
(1) CURATO HOLDING AS (originally named SPV 3 AS) (a company existing under the laws of Norway with company no. 998 407 184) as issuer (the "Issuer");
(2) CD HOLDING n AS (originally named SPV 2 AS) (a company existing under the laws of Norway with company no. 998 406 250) as parent (the "Parent");
(3) CURATO RØNTGEN AS (a company existing under the laws of Norway with company no. 918 041 958) as guarantor (the "Guarantor"); and
(4) NORDIC TRUSTEE ASA (previously Norsk Tillitsmann ASA) (a company existing under the laws of Norway with company no. 963 342 624) as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee").
BACKGROUND
(A) By a bond agreement originally dated 20 November 2013 and made between, inter alios, the Issuer, the Parent, the Guarantor and the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) (the "Bond Agreement"), the Bondholders have agreed to make available to the Issuer a loan in the maximum amount of NOK 900,000,000.
(B) Pursuant to a contemplated merger of the Issuer and Curato AS, with the Issuer as surviving entity (the "Merger"), the parties have agreed to make certain amendments to the Bond Agreement, including but not limited to releasing Curato AS as guarantor and restating the security interest over certain assets.
1. INTERPRETATION 1.1 Definitions
In this Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Effective Date" means:
(a) The date on which (i) the Bond Trustee confirms to the Issuer that is has received each of the documents listed in Clause 3 (Conditions Precedent) in a form and substance satisfactory to the Bond Trustee and (ii) the Merger has entered into effect; or
(b) Such other date as the Issuer and the Bond Trustee may agree.
1.2 Incorporation of defined terms In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:
(a) Unless expressly defined in this Agreement (including its recitals) or a contrary intention appears, capitalised terms defined in the Bond Agreement shall have the same meaning in this Agreement.
(b) The principles of construction set out in Clause 1.2 (Construction) of the Bond Agreement shall have effect as if set out in this Agreement.
3

(c) In accordance with the terms of the Bond Agreement, each party hereto designates this Agreement as a Finance Document.
2. AMENDMENT With effect from the Effective Date, the Bond Agreement shall be amended to delete the obligations of Curato AS thereunder, which shall be continued by the Issuer as surviving entity after the Merger.
3. CONDITIONS PRECEDENT The obligations of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to this Agreement are subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents and other evidence in form and substance satisfactoiy to the Bond Trustee:
(a) this Agreement, duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or similar official document for each Obligor, evidencing that it is validly registered and existing and (ii) the Articles of Association of each Obligor;
(c) copies of necessary corporate resolutions from each Group Company to execute the relevant Finance Documents to which it is a party;
(d) The restatement agreement to the share pledge agreement over the shares in Curato Röntgen AS (including restatement of perfection);
(e) The restatement agreement provided by the Issuer (as surviving entity pursuant to the Merger) to the security agreement originally dated 19 December 2013 and entered into by Curato AS (including restatement of perfection); and
(f) Any statements or legal opinions required by the Bond Trustee in relation to the relevant Finance Documents.
4. FEES AND EXPENSES The Issuer shall promptly on demand pay to the Bond Trustee the amount of all costs and expenses (including legal fees) incurred by Bond Trustee in connection with the negotiations, preparation and execution of this Agreement and any other documents or matters related to or directly arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.
5. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES (a) The Issuer makes the representations and warranties as set out in Clause 7 (Representations and warranties) of the Bond Agreement:
(i) on the date of this Agreement; and
(ii) on the Effective Date,
by reference to the facts and circumstances existing on such dates. (b) The Issuer acknowledges that the Bond Trustee has entered into this Agreement
in full reliance on the representations and warranties made by it pursuant to

paragraph (a) above. 6. CONTINUITY AND FURTHER ASSURANCE 6.1 Continuing obligations
The provisions of the Bond Agreement and the other documents related thereto shall, save as amended by this Agreement, continue in full force and effect. 6.2 No repetition of activities Save as otherwise expressly stated in this Agreement, no activities already performed by any Obligor in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Bond Agreement before the date o f this Agreement shall be repeated by such Obligor. 6.3 Further assurance The Issuer shall, at the request of the Bond Trustee and at its own expense, do all such acts and things necessary or desirable to give effect to the amendments effected or to be effected pursuant to this Agreement. 7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND LEGAL VENUE (a) This Agreement and all disputes arising out of, or in connection with this
Agreement between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and any Obligor, shall be governed by Norwegian law. (b) All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Agreement between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and any Obligor, shall, subject to paragraph (c) below, be exclusively resolved by the courts of Norway, with the District Court of Oslo as sole legal venue. (c) This Clause 7 is for the benefit of the Bond Trustee only. As a result, the Bond Trustee shall not be prevented from taking proceedings relating to a dispute in any other courts with jurisdiction. To the extent allowed by law, the Bond Trustee may take concurrent proceedings in any number ofjurisdictions.
