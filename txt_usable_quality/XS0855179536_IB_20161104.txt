Reference is hereby made to:
(1) the indenture dated November 15, 2012 between, inter alios, Perstorp Holding AB (publ) (the Issuer) and certain of its subsidiaries (the Guarantors) and The Bank of New York Mellon, London Branch (the Trustee) (as so amended or supplemented, the Senior Secured Notes Indenture) pursuant to which the Issuer has issued the Senior Secured Notes; and
(2) the indenture dated November 15, 2012 between, inter alios, the Issuer, the Guarantors and the Trustee (as so amended or supplemented, the Second Lien Notes Indenture, and together with the Senior Secured Notes Indenture, the Indentures) pursuant to which the Issuer has issued the Second Lien Notes.
Capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined herein, shall have the meaning assigned to such terms in the respective Indentures.
Pursuant to Section 3.03 of each of the Indentures, the Issuer hereby provides notice of the following information relating to the redemption of its Notes, in whole:
The redemption date shall be December 5, 2016 (the Redemption Date).
The record date shall be December 2, 2016 (the Record Date).
The Notes are to be redeemed according to Section 5(d) of each Global Note thereof.
The redemption is subject to certain conditions, as set forth below, including the completion of the offering of the Issuer's Senior Secured Notes due 2021 and Second Lien Notes due 2021.

The Senior Secured Notes will be redeemed at a redemption price (the Senior Secured Redemption Price) of:

(i) 100.000% of the principal amount thereof, plus

(ii) accrued and unpaid interest and Additional Amounts, if any, to the Redemption Date, in a total amount of 1,350,000 for the Euro Senior Secured Notes and $1,847,222 for the Dollar Senior Secured Notes.

The Second Lien Notes will be redeemed at a redemption price (the Second Lien Redemption Price) of:

(i) 100.000% of the principal amount thereof, plus

(ii) accrued and unpaid interest and Additional Amounts, if any, to the Redemption Date, in a total amount of $12,436,112.

The Senior Secured Redemption Price and the Second Lien Redemption Price together constitute the Redemption Price.

Unless the Issuer defaults in making such redemption payment, interest on the Notes called for redemption shall cease to accrue on and after the Redemption Date.

Payment of the Redemption Price will only be made upon presentation and surrender of the Notes to the relevant Paying Agent at the places set forth below:

Principal Paying Agent The Bank of New York Mellon, London Branch
One Canada Square Canary Wharf
London E14 5AL United Kingdom

Luxembourg Paying Agent The Bank of New York Mellon (Luxembourg) S.A.
Vertigo Building, Polaris 2 - 4 Rue Eugene Ruppert, L-2453
Luxembourg

The method of delivery is at the option and risk of the Holder. On the Redemption Date, the redemption payment will become due and payable.

Notwithstanding anything herein to the contrary, the completion of the redemption described herein is subject to certain financing conditions, including the successful refinancing of the Notes, as well as the satisfaction of any additional conditions required pursuant to the Indentures.
