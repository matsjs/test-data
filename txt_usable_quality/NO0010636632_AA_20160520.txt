This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 20 May 2016 between I.M. Skaugen SE (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement "FRN I.M. Skaugen SE Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2012/2016" - ISIN 001 063663.2, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 22 April 2016.
2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) New definition of "Maturity Date" shall read:
"Maturity Date means 17 February 2017 or an earlier maturity date as providedfor in the Bond Agreement. Anyfurther adjustment may be made according to the Business Day Convention. "
(ii) Clause 10.1 ("Maturity") shall read as follows:
"The Bonds shall mature infull on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at 101.5% o fpar by the Issuer. "
(iii) The Equity ratio covenant in Clause 13.5 b) of the Bond Agreement (Equity not be less than 25% of Total Assets) shall be deleted in its entirety.
(iv) In addition to interest payments made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year in accordance with Clause 9 in the Bond Agreement, interest accrued on the Bonds from and including 27 May to, but excluding, 30 June 2016 shall be paid on 30 June 2016.
(v) Clause 10.3shall be amended to read:
"The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (Call Option) with settlement date at any timefrom 27 February 2015 to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at 101,50 per cent o fpar plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
The applicable call price above shall be determined on the basis o f the settlement date o f the Call Option.
Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders ten Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.

3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
5. Commencement
The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence at 22 April 2016.
