Oslo, 3 February 2014
NOTICE OF PARTIAL REPAYMENT OF THE 9.75% BOND LOAN
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond loan (the "Bond Loan" or the "9.75 Bond Loan"). The Bond Loan is registered in the Norwegian Securities Depository (the "VPS"). The Loan Trustee has now received amount of approximately NOK 55.6 million from the Petromena ASA bankruptcy estate (the "Estate"). The amount represents a preliminary dividend payment (the "Dividend") from the Estate to the Petromena creditors. Further is reference made to earlier resolutions at bondholders meetings in the Bond Loan regarding funding o f the acceleration o f the Bond Loan and the corresponding security, funding of several litigation issues in connection with the acceleration o f the Bond Loan, and security for certain advisors. As a result of the funding and security requirements, the Loan Trustee has retained amounts from funds received on behalf of the Bondholders. The Loan Trustee has, in the present situation where several of the litigation issues are about to be finalised, made an assessment o f the funding and security requirements going forward. Based on this assessment it is decided that the amount retained by the Loan Trustee related to the 9.75 Bond Loan may be reduced by paying an amount of NOK 25.0 million (the "Released Amount") to the Bondholders. Payment to the Bondholders The aggregate o f the Dividend and the Released Amount - NOK 80.6 million - will be paid to the Bondholders in the 9.75 Bond Loan. The payment will be made to the Bondholders as of 4 February 2014 (Record Date) with payment on 6 February 2014 (Payment Date). The payment will constitute a payment of principal equal to 18.46735 % of remaining outstanding amount prior to the payment.
Bondholders requesting further information may contact Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
