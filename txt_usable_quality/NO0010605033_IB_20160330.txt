Information to Bondholders - No interest payment or instalment 30 March 2016
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond" or the "Bond Issue") issued by Havila Shipping ASA as issuer (the "Company" or the "Issuer").
Reference is made to the announcement from the Issuer on Newsweb on 17 February 2016, where the Company states that is continues its negotiations with its financial creditors with an aim to reach an agreement that that will enable the Company to come through the current market challenges, and that during the negotiating period the company will not pay interest, amortizations or other commitments to its financial creditors.
The interest and instalment payments to the Bondholders in HAVI06/07 on 30 March 2016 were not effectuated, and the Issuer is as such in a payment default.
Reference is also made to the information letter to Bondholders on 23 Februaiy 2016 on Stamdata, where the Bond Trustee informs that a RoR Letter has been issued to the Issuer. The purpose of the RoR Letter is to enable the Company to continue its negotiations with its financial creditors without any of the parties changing their creditor position. The Bond Trustee has not received any instructions to accelerate the Bonds and negotiations will therefore continue.
Please refer to www.stamdata.no and www.newsweb.no for further information from time to time from the Bond Trustee and the Issuer.
