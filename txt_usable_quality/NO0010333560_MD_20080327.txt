Re. Interest Payment due March 2008
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (NT) is the appointed Loan Trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issues (the "Bond Issues") in which Thule Drilling ASA is the issuer.
In view of the special circumstances at the QGM yard in the UAE, NT and Thule Drilling have had a close dialogue over the past few months, main purpose being to ensure that the interests of the bondholders and shareholders of the Company is being protected in an appropriate manner.
NT has been informed by the Company that it is not able to meet the interest payments due in March in a timely manner. Pursuant to the Loan Agreements, the Company has a remedy period of five banking days. The Company and the Loan Trustee are continuing their discussions to resolve this situation, and would like to stress that these discussions are conducted in the spirit of cooperation to ensure that the Company is adequately financed to deal with the challenges at the QGM yard, ultimate objective being to preserve and protect the interests of both the bondholders and shareholders of the Company for the long term. Further information will be given as soon as possible.
