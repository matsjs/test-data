BACKGROUND:

(A)

The Parties have entered into the bond agreement originally dated 2 September 2013 for the

bond issue with ISIN NO 001 068656.1 described as FRN E FORLAND AS Senior Secured

Callable Bond Issue 2013/2018 (the "Original Agreement") for the purposes set out in Clause

2.3 (Purpose and utilization) of the Original Agreement.

(B)

On 7 June 2018, the Bondholder's meeting approved to certain amendments to the Original

Agreement as further set out in the summons to the Bondholder's meeting dated 24 May 2018

(the "Summons"),

(C)

The Parties have agreed to enter into this Agreement to implement the approved amendments

to the Original Agreement as set out in the Summons.

NOW IT IS HEREBY AGREED AS FOLLOWS:

1

DEFINITIONS

In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires, terms defined in the Original Agreement shall bear the same meaning when used herein and in the preamble hereto.

2

EFFECTIVE DATE

The provisions of Clause 3 (Amendments to the Original Agreement) shall be effective from the date on which the Bond Trustee confirms in writing to Issuer that the conditions precedent set out Schedule 1 (Conditions Precedent) have been either waived or fulfilled to its satisfaction (the "Effective Date").

3

AMENDMENTS TO THE ORIGINAL AGREEMENT

The Parties agree that from the Effective Date, the Original Agreement shall be amended as follows:

3.1

Amendment to existing definitions

a)

The definition of "Cash and Cash Equivalent" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended

to read:

""Cash and Cash Equivalent" means at any date, the amounts (expressed in NOK or NOK equivalent) on such date o f the then current market value of:

(a) cash in hand or amounts standing to the credit of any current and/or on deposit accounts; and

in each case, to which the Issuer is beneficially entitled at that time and which is available to the Issuer. Any amount standing to the credit of any Issuer Accounts pledged and/or blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee shall be regarded as Cash and Cash Equivalents as long as no Event of Default has occurred under this Agreement"

b)

The definition of "Change of Control Event" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to

read:

""Change of Control Event" means if any person or group (as such term is defined in section 1-3 of the Norwegian Limited Companies Act), save for In Nord Vest /AS and related parties (as such term is defined in section 1-5 of the Norwegian Limited Companies Act) becomes the owner, directly or indirectly, of more than 50% of the outstanding shares and/or voting rights of the Issuer."

c)

The definition of "Group" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to read:

""Group" means the Parent, the Internal Bareboat Charterer (if applicable), and the Issuer and its direct and indirect Subsidiaries from time to time, each a "Group Company."

d)

The definition of "Maturity Date" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to read:

""Maturity Date" means 30 June 2021. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention."

e)

The definition of "Mandatory Redemption Event" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be

amended to read:

""Mandatory Redemption Event" means (save if it constitutes a Total Loss Event) that:

(a) Fugro Saltire is sold or disposed of; or

(b) any o f the Vessels is expropriated or hijacked (in each case, exceeding twenty-one (21) days)."

f)

The definition of "Share Pledges" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to read:

""Share Pledges" means the first priority pledges granted by the Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over its shares in (i) the Issuer and (ii) (if applicable) the Internal Bareboat Charterer."

3.2

""Relevant Interest Payment Date" shall have the meaning ascribed to it in Clause 11.1.4."

b)

A new definition of "Scheduled Instalments from June 2018" shall be added to Clause 1.1

""Scheduled Instalments from June 2018" shall have meaning ascribed to it in Clause 11. 1. 1."

4

CONTINUED FORCE AND EFFECT

Except as amended and supplemented by this Agreement, the Original Agreement shall continue in full force and effect, and the Original Agreement and this Agreement shall be read and construed as one instrument.

5

NO DEFAULT INTEREST

The Parties agree that no default interest shall accrue on the Scheduled Instalments falling due on the Interest Payment Date in June 2018 as set out in Clause 11.1.1 of the Original Agreement.

6

DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND LEGAL VENUE

a)

This Agreement and all disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Agreement between

the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and any Obligor, shall be governed by Norwegian law.

b)

All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Agreement between the Bond Trustee,

the Bondholders and any Obligor, shall, subject to paragraph c) below, be exclusively resolved

by the courts of Norway, with the District Court of Oslo as sole legal venue.

c)

Paragraph b) above is for the benefit of the Bond Trustee only. As a result, the Bond Trustee

shall not be prevented from taking proceedings relating to a dispute in any other courts with

jurisdiction. To the extent allowed by law, the Bond Trustee may take concurrent proceedings

in any number of jurisdictions.
