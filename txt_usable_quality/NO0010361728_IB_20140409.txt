aRUBICON OFFSHORE INTERNATIONAL Rubicon Offshore Holdings Limited Group Management Financial Statements For the year ended 31 December 2013
Background Rubicon is an offshore oilfield service company focused on the ownership and operation of two floating, production, storage and off-loading vessels ("FPSOs"). Management review of the fourth quarter 2013 Rubicon is pleased to present its latest management report. Rubicon Intrepid is contracted to the Galoc Production Company for deployment at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines. The vessel continued to achieve excellent uptime of over 99% during the quarter (99.8% for the whole of 2013). Production of 1st oil from Phase 2 of the Galoc Field was achieved on 9 December 2013.
Rubicon Intrepid at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines Rubicon Vantage is located at the Bualuang Field, offshore Thailand, where it is contracted to Salamander Energy. Production uptime during the fourth quarter of 2013 was 99.8% (99.9% for the whole of 2013).

Recent events On 1 January 2014, Rubicon Vantage's mooring line parted, resulting in damage to the risers and other sub-sea equipment. Production was shut-in immediately after the incident and work commenced to reinstate/repair the affected equipment. This exercise was completed and production re-commenced on 13 February 2014.
