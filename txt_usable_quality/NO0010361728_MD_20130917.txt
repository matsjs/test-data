Rubicon Offshore Holdings Limited Group Management Financial Statements For the six months ended 30 June 2013
Background Rubicon is an offshore oilfield service company focused on the ownership and operation of two floating, production, storage and off-loading vessels ("FPSOs"). Management review of the second quarter 2013 Rubicon is pleased to present its latest management report. Rubicon Intrepid is contracted to the Galoc Production Company for deployment at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines. The vessel continued to maintain excellent uptime of over 99.9% during the quarter (over 99.9% for the first half of 2013). Production of 1st oil from Phase 2 of the Galoc Field remains on track to be achieved in the fourth quarter of 2013.
