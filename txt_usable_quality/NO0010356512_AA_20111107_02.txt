This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 7 November 2011 between American Shipping Company ASA (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee"). 1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments o f the agreement Loan Agreement FRN Aker American Shipping ASA Senior Unsecured Callable PIK Bond Issue 2007/2012 ISIN 001 035651.2, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 25 March 2011. 2. Amendment to the Loan Agreement The Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows: (i) Clause 13.2 (a) shall read:
"ensure that the Group maintains Equity o f more than USD 80 million." 3. Compensation
A compensation o f 0.10 per cent flat o f the principal outstanding amount under the Loan Agreement shall be due and payable by the Borrower 6 Banking Days after this Bondholders' meeting. 4. Confirmation of the Loan Agreement All other provisions of the Loan Agreement remain in full force. The Loan Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Bond issue. 5. Governing Law Disputes arising out o f or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Norwegian Courts. 6. Commencement The amendments to the Loan Agreement will commence at the date of the Bondholders' meeting.
