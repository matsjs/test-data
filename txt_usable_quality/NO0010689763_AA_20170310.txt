AFFIRMATION OF GUARANTEES

3.1 The Huntington Subsidiary confirms that, notwithstanding the amendments effected by this Agreement, its obligations as Guarantor under the Guarantee shall continue in full force and effect and shall extend to the liabilities and the obligations of the Issuer under the Bond Agreement as amended and restated pursuant to Clause 2.1 above and the other Finance Documents (as applicable and subject to such limitations as set out in the Second Amended and Restated Bond Agreement).

3.2 Any payment by the Huntington Subsidiary to the Bond Trustee under the Guarantee shall not operate to discharge the Outstanding Bonds.

3.3 Except as expressly modified by this Agreement, all terms and provisions of the Bond Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and are hereby ratified and confirmed in all respects by the Parties as if herein set forth in their entirety. All references in the Bond Agreement to "this Agreement", "hereof', "hereby", "hereto", and the like shall: (i) from the Step 1 Effective Date, mean the Second Amended and Restated Bond Agreement; and (ii) from Completion, mean the Third Amended and Restated Bond Agreement.

3.4 Each of the Issuer and the Huntington Subsidiary undertakes that it shall, promptly upon written request by the Bond Trustee, execute and deliver any further instruments or documents and do all such acts and things as the Bond Trustee may reasonably require in connection with making any amendments to the Guarantee to reflect the amendments to the Original Bond Agreement effected by this Agreement and the extension and affirmation of the liabilities and obligations subject to the Guarantee pursuant to Clause 3.1 above.

3.5 The Huntington Subsidiary further confirms by its signature on this Agreement that, notwithstanding the amendments to the Bond Agreement effected by this Agreement on Completion, the Guarantee shall survive any release and discharge of the Outstanding Bonds in accordance with the provisions of the Third Amended and Restated Bond Agreement and that it shall remain liable under the Guarantee following such release and discharge.

3.6 For the avoidance of doubt, the confirmation of the Guarantee in this Clause 3 is a confirmation of an obligation on the part of the Huntington Subsidiary pursuant to Clause 13 of the Second Amended and Restated Bond Agreement which exists as at the date of this Agreement and such confirmation shall not constitute an expense of the administration of the Huntington Subsidiary pursuant to paragraph 99 of Schedule B1 of the Insolvency Act 1986 and Rule 2.67 o f the Insolvency Rules 1986.
