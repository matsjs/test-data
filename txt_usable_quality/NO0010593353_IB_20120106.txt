Oslo, 6 January 2012
INFORMATION TO BONDHOLDERS Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is the bond trustee for the abovementioned bond issue. Emerging Europe Land Development AS has not been able to repay the bonds on or before the agreed maturity date, 31 December 2011. Consequently, the agreed default conversion mechanism will apply according to which the bonds in addition to accrued interest will be converted to equity in the company at a subscription price of NOK 1 per share. On 15 December 2011, the general meeting of shareholders in the company approved the issuance of shares to the bondholders according to the agreed default conversion mechanism. Norsk Tillitsmann ASA will subscribe for the shares on behalf of the bondholders in the subscription period starting 10 January 2012. It is expected that the share capital increase is registered in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises ultimo January 2012 following which the new shares will be transferred to the VPS-accounts of each of the bondholders.
