Escrow Account:

The Issuer shall prior to issuance of the Bonds establish a blocked escrow account in NOK. Once all Pre-Settlement Conditions Precedent (as described below) have been satisfied, the net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of fees, legal costs and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall be transferred to the Escrow Account. The Escrow Account shall be pledged on first priority in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) under the Escrow Account Pledge, and blocked such that no withdrawals can be made therefrom without the Trustee's written consent.

The funds in the Escrow Account shall only be (i) used by the Issuer according to the Purpose of the Bond Issue as described above or, alternatively, (ii) in the event that the Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent (as described below) are not satisfied for any reason within 90 days after the Settlement Date used in connection with early redemption of all Bonds at (a) par value together with accrued interest thereon if such failure to comply with the Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent is beyond the control of the Issuer and (b) otherwise at the rate applicable as set out under "Mandatory Redemption".

Before the release from the Escrow Account, all the Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent (as described below) shall be complied with.
