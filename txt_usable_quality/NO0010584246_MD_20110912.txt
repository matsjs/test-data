Information to Bond Holders
Floatel Superior Ltd. hereby gives you notice that due to a successfully completed amalgamation between Floatel International Ltd. ("the Parent") and Aqua Pellentesque Ltd. ("Aqua"), an event triggering the Put Option period as defined in the Bond Agreement and regulated under Clause 10.3, has occurred on 9thSeptember 2011.
Aqua is a newly established company formed by a consortium of current shareholders of the Parent. Wideluck Enterprises Ltd., a wholly owned subsidiary of Keppel FELS, will be the largest shareholder of the amalgam ated company, with a shareholding of approximately 80.1%. The amalgamated company will keep the same name as the Parent - Floatel International Ltd.
Existing Senior lenders for Floatel Superior and Floatel Reliance have given their consent to keep their current commitments to the Parent unchanged and to transfer those to the new amalgamated company. The new amalgamated Floatel International Ltd. will pursue the identical business strategies as the Parent with no changes to the m anagem ent team, corporate structure or to the assets.
