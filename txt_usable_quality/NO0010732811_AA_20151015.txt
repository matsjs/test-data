This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on i 3 October 2015 between VIZRT Group AS (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement "FRN Aksjeselskapet av 17. oktober 2014 Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2015/2021" with ISIN 001 0732811, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 25 September 2015.
2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) The definition o f "Material Group Companies" in Clause 1.1 shall be amended to read as follows:
""Material Group Companies" means each Subsidiary of the Issuer representing more than 10% of either (i) the total gross assets of the Group on a consolidated basis (for the avoidance of doubt, excluding goodwill and intra-group transactions) or (ii) the Consolidated EBITDA according to (x) the latest Financial Reports or (y) in respect of the first testing date under clause 13.4(h) only, pro forma best estimates o f EBITDA and aggregate gross assets as estimated by the management o f the Group, acting reasonably and in good faith."
(ii) Clause 13.4(h)(ii) shall be amended to read as follows:
"Compliance with the conditions set out in paragraph (i) above shall be tested (x) for the first time on the date falling 120 days after the Disbursement Date (unless the Target is at the date undergoing a process of solvent liquidation, in which case the condition shall be tested on the earlier of (i) as soon as practicable after the completion o f the liquidation process and subsequent corporate restructuring and (ii) 31 March 2016 and (y) thereafter on an annual basis 60 days after the Financial Statements have been, or should have been, supplied by the Issuer and by reference to such Financial Statements or, in respect of the first testing date only, by reference to pro forma best estimates o f EBITDA and aggregate gross assets as estimated by the management o f the Group, acting reasonably and in good faith, provided in each case that the gross assets and EBITDA of Group Companies which are not required to become Guarantors in accordance with the Security Principles, shall be disregarded in the calculations."
