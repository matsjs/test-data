2. CONFIRMATION OF THE BOND AGREEMENT All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force.

3. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND APPLICABLE LAW
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.

4. EFFECTIVE DATE AND CONDITION SUBSEQUENT
This Agreement shall become effective when signed by both Parties (the "Effective Date").
The amendments under this Agreement shall be subject to the Issuer's completion of the exchange offer as described in the summons to the Bondholders' Meeting held on 30 June 2016 (the "Exchange Offer"). In the event that the Issuer does not launch the Exchange Offer within one month o f the Bondholder's Meeting or in the event that the share capital increase pertaining to the Exchange Offer is not completed and registered in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises within three months following the Bondholders' Meeting, the amendments hereunder shall be terminated and the original clauses under the Bond Agreement be reinstated as o f 1 October 2016.
