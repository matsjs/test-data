OSA GOLIATH PTE. LTD. - ISSUANCE OF ADDITIONAL LIQUIDITY BONDS
Reference is made to the Bondholders' meeting held on 24 March 2014 and the summons thereto, as published on Stamdata on 7 March 2014 (the "Summons"), and the consequential Liquidity Bond Issue pursuant to the Bond Agreement dated 5 May 2014, between OSA Goliath Pte. Ltd. as Issuer, and Nordic Trustee ASA as Bond Trustee (the "Liquidity Bond Agreement").
In order to keep funding (i) the working capital requirements of the Issuer, including in relation to the day-to-day management and operation and OSA Goliath (the "Vessel"), and (ii) the Issuer's costs obligations to Nordic Trustee ASA as bond trustee for the holders in the Original Bond Issue, including, without limitation costs in relation to the judicial sale of the Vessel in Aruba (which is intended to maximize sales proceeds recoverable by the Issuer), the Issuer intends to offer the existing Bondholders in the Original Bond Issue and the Liquidity Bond Issue to participate in a Funding Bond Issue (as defined in the Summons) in an aggregate amount of USD 6,500,000. As previously agreed, holders of the bonds issued pursuant to the Liquidity Bond Agreement have a right of first refusal. The bond issue will be a tap issue under the Liquidity Bond Agreement. Pareto Securities AS and Pareto Securities Pte. Ltd. (the "Arranger") has been appointed as arranger for the Funding Bond Issue. Interested Bondholders may obtain a term sheet from the Arranger by sending a request, together with evidence of their current holdings of Bonds under the Liquidity Bond Issue and the Original Bond Issue to rolf.kristiansen(5)paretosec.com and iohn.biornsen(5)paretosec.com. and/or to your sales contact at Pareto Securities.
All subscriptions must include proof of current holding in the Liquidity Bond, which must include the identity of the beneficial holder associated with the holding (including amount held per beneficial holder). As the current holding in the Liquidity Bond is the main allocation criteria, subscriptions without information on beneficial holders may be discarded without further notice.
Minimum subscription amount will be USD 200,000, with increments of USD 100,000.
