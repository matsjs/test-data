Notice of written procedure for notes issued by Scandinavian Air Ambulance Holding AB (publ)
(Stockholm , 19 Septem ber 2014)
Scandinavian Air Ambulance Holding AB (publ) has requested that the holders of its senior unsecured notes with ISIN SE0005249679 approve a proposal for amendment of the terms and conditions of the notes in order to change the financial reporting requirement and to extend the period for listing of the notes.
CorpNordic Sweden AB, acting in its capacity as agent for the noteholders under the terms and conditions of the notes, has on request of the issuer on 19 September 2014 initiated a written procedure in which the noteholders can approve or reject a request made by the issuer. The request involves amendments of clauses 11.3.1, 9.5.2 and 10.1.1 of the terms and conditions of the notes in order to change the financial reporting requirement from quarterly to semi-annual and to extend the period for listing of the notes to 15 December 2014.
Voting forms must be received by the agent no later than 13 October 2014.
To be eligible to participate in the written procedure a holder must fulfill the formal requirements of being a Noteholder (as defined in the terms and conditions of the notes) on 25 September 2014.
The formal notice of the written procedure has been sent by ordinary mail to direct registered noteholders and registered authorised nominees in accordance with the terms and conditions of the notes.
A copy of the notice with more information on the written procedure and how to participate can be downloaded from the agent's webpage: www.corpnordic.com
