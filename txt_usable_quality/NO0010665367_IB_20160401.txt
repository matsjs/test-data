Information to Bondholders Nordic Trustee ASA acts as trustee for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bonds") issued by Otium AS as issuer. Capitalised terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement dated 17 December 2012, as amended, unless otherwise stated herein. Reference is made to the proposal adopted by the Bondholders' meeting at 21 July 2015 and the adoption thereunder of a PIK interest rate equal to 3 months NIBOR + a margin of 2.00 % per annum for the duration of the 24 month moratorium. The moratorium ends at the Maturity Date of the Bonds at 17 July 2017. We hereby inform that the said PIK interest will be settled at the Maturity Date, and that no new bonds will be issued at Interest Payment Dates.
