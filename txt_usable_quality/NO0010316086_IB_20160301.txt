FINAL PAYMENT TO THE BONDHOLDERS IN THE 9.75% BOND LOAN
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Loan Trustee") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders of bonds (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond loan (the "Bond Loan" or the "9.75 Bond Loan"). The Bond Loan is registered in the Norwegian Securities Depository (the "VPS").
In accordance with a ruling from the Oslo Bankruptcy Court dated 21 December 2015 may the proceedings o f the Petromena ASA bankruptcy estate be concluded and the remaining bankruptcy estate funds can be paid to the creditors. Subsequent to the court ruling has the bankruptcy estate may the final dividend payment to the creditors and finalised the bankruptcy estate activities. The Loan Trustee has in regards to the payment to the creditors on behalf o f the Bondholders received an amount of NOK 35,043,925. The received amount will be paid to the Bondholders (the "Final Dividend Payment").
In order to facilitate of the acceleration of the Bond Loan and the corresponding security, funding of several litigation issues in connection with the acceleration o f the Bond Loan, and security for certain advisors, has the Loan Trustee previously retained amounts (the "Retained Amounts") from funds received on behalf o f the Bondholders. As a consequence of the litigation issues being finalised, the security being recovered and that the bankruptcy estate activities are concluded, the remaining part o f the Retained Amounts, equal to NOK 6,274,088, will be paid to the Bondholders together with the Final Dividend Payment.
The Final Dividend Payment and the payment o f the remaining Retained Amounts together constitute the Final Payment. The 9.75 Bond Loan will subsequent to the Final Payment be closed down, the VPS registration will be suspended and any remaining claims abandoned.
Final Payment to the Bondholders
The Final Payment in an aggregate amount of NOK 41,318,013 will be distributed to the Bondholders as of 3 March 2016 (Record Date) with payment on 7 March 2016 (Payment Date). The payment will constitute a payment of principal equal to 11.60690 % of remaining outstanding amount prior to the payment.
