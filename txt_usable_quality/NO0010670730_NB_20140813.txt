Oslo, 13 August 2014
Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Today a Bondholders' Meeting was held pursuant to a summons of 30 July 2014. There were sufficient Voting Bonds present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 99.34 per cent of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as set out in the summons to the meeting. A waiver fee of 0.25% of the total outstanding amount of the Bond Issue will be distributed to the Bondholders with record date today and payment date within 10 Business Days after this Bondholders' Meeting.
