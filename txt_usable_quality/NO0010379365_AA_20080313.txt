This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is signed on 2 June 2009 and made between Bergen Group ASA (previously named Bergen Yards Holding AS) (the "Issuer") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee") to document the agreement reached between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee on or before 13 March 2008. 1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the agreement "FRN Bergen Yards Holding AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010" - ISIN 001 037936.5 (the "Bond Agreement", Capitalized terms and expressions used herein shall bear the meaning ascribed to such term or expression in the Bond Agreement unless otherwise defined herein. 2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows: The definition of "Subsidiaries" shall be amended to read:
"(i) Bergen Group Shipbuilding AS (Norwegian org no 983 343 120), (ii) Bergen Group Maritime Services AS (Norwegian org no 982 720 583), (Hi) Bergen Group Offshore AS (Norwegian org no 983 371 787), and (iv) Bergen Group Technology AS (Norwegian org no 925 705 829). " 3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. The Bond Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Bond issue. 4. Governing Law Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
6. Commencement The amendments to the Bond Agreement contemplated herein will be effective as of the date of execution by this Amendment Agreement by the Issuer and the Bond Trustee.
