Final dividend payment from bankruptcy estate
Nordic Trustee ASA acts as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where DP Producer was issuer (the "Issuer"). Following the conclusion of the bankruptcy proceedings the Bond Trustee will make a final dividend payment to the Bondholders. This dividend will constitute a payment of principal equal to 0.94267% of remaining outstanding amount prior to the payment. The record date is set to 13 November 2015, and settlement date is 16 November 2015. After the dividend payment the Bond Issue will be terminated, the registration in the Norwegian Securities Depository (the "VPS") will be cancelled and any remaining claims will be abandoned. Bondholders requesting further information may contact Nordic Trustee ASA.
