NOTICE OF PARTIAL REPAYMENT OF THE 9.75% BOND LOAN, THE 10.85% BOND LOAN AND THE FRN BOND LOAN
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond loans (together the "Bond Loans"). The Bond Loans are registered in the Norwegian Securities Depository (the "VPS").
Reference is made to earlier resolutions at several bondholders' meetings in the Bond Loans regarding retainer of advisors and their indemnity requirements, funding of the acceleration of the Bond Loans and the corresponding security, funding of several litigation issues in connection with the acceleration o f the Bond Loans. Reference is also made to the approved restructuring plan (the "Plan") related to the chapter 11 proceedings involving PetroRig I, PetroRig II and PetroRig III the ("Debtors"). As a result of the funding and security requirements, the Loan Trustee has retained significant amounts from funds received on behalf of the Bondholders.
The Loan Trustee has now received the expected payments under the Plan (regarding the last payment see below under the FRN Bond Loan), and the Debtors are now in the course of being liquidated. Consequently, the Loan Trustee has reassessed the funding and security requirement going forward, and while several of the litigations processes are still in progress and it is presently not possible to predict when these processes may be completed, the Loan Trustee has determined that some of the retained amounts may be distributed to the bondholders as outlined below.
The 9.75% Bond loan:
An amount of USD 6.2 million will (after being exchanged to NOK) be paid to the Bondholders as on 3 October 2012 (Record Date) with payment on 5 October 2012 (Payment Date). The payment will constitute a payment of interest equal to 8.10841 % of remaining outstanding amount.
The 10.85% Bond loan:
An amount of USD 4.8 million will be paid to the Bondholders as on 3 October 2012 (Record Date) with payment on 5 October 2012 (Payment Date). The payment will constitute a payment of principal equal to 1.86453 % of remaining outstanding amount prior to the payment.

The FRN Bond loan:
Loan Trustee has for the benefit o f the Bondholders in the FRN Bond Loan received an amount of approximately USD 3.7 million as the last payment related to the Plan. This amount will together with a release of retained funds from the Loan Trustee in an amount of USD 10.1 million, be distributed to the Bondholders. The aggregate amount, USD 13.8 million will (after being exchanged to NOK) be paid to the Bondholders as of as on 3 October 2012 (Record Date) with payment on 5 October 2012 (Payment Date).
