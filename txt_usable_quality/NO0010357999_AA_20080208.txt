This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 8 January 2008 between Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd (the "Issuer") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the agreement Bond Agreement FRN Didon Tunisia Pty Ltd Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012" - ISIN 001 035799.9.
2. Aumendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended with insertion of a new Clause 13.2 (h) which shall read:
"not, from Disbursement Date, raise any new debt orfinancial obligations. "
3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. The Bond Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Bond issue.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
5. Commencement
The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence immediately.
