Notice to Bondholders - actions required for Bondholders wanting to subscribe for shares in OSA Recovery SPV, Ltd.

Nordic Trustee ASA is appointed as Bond Trustee for the above mentioned bond issues.

Reference is made to the summons dated at the date of this notice (the "Summons"), for the Bondholder's Meeting held on 1 March 2016 at 13:00 hours (1 pm) (Oslo time) (the "Meeting"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning ascribed to them in the Summons.

Subscription for shares in OSA Recovery SPV, Ltd.

Company:

OSA Recovery SPV, Ltd.

Application Period:

The period from and including 1 March at 14:00 hours (2 pm) (Oslo time) to and including 4 March 2016 at 17:00 hours (5 pm) (Oslo) hours CET

Allocation Notice:

Notice of allocation of shares will be given via VPS to each potential investor subscribing for shares on or about 7 March 2016.

Settlement Date:

Payment of the subscription amount must be received in full by the Company before the settlement date which will be on or about 9 March 2016. Settlement date will be confirmed in the Allocation Notice.

Issue Date:

The shares in the Company will be issued on or about 10 March 2016.

Conditions for subscription:

Subscription of shares will be subject to the terms and conditions set forth in the Subscription Document, the Summons and the Memorandum of Association and the Articles of Association of the Company (the "Articles").

The Subscription Document and the Articles are made available from Nordic Trustee ASA as Bond Trustee from beginning of the Application Period, subject to proof of holding in the OSA Bonds and/or Goliath Senior Secured Bonds.

All subscriptions must include proof of holding in the OSA Bonds
