Results from written procedure and notice of amendments Nordic Trustee & Agency AB (publ) is the appointed agent under the terms and conditions in the abovementioned bond issue (the "Terms and Conditions") Terms defined in the Terms and Conditions shall have the same meaning when used in this notice, unless otherwise defined herein. On 26 September 2017, a notice of Written Procedure was sent to the Holders, regarding certain amendments of the Terms and Conditions of the Bonds. The voting period expired on 20 October 2017. A sufficient number of votes was obtained, in order to form a quorum, and at least two thirds (2/3) of the Adjusted Nominal Amount for which Holders were voting, voted in favour of the Request (as defined in the notice of Written Procedure). It is therefore resolved that the Holders have agreed to amend the Terms and Conditions. The amendments shall be effective as per the date of 20 October 2017. A consent fee of 0.02 per cent. of the Nominal Amount of the respective Holders' holdings of Bonds (i.e. SEK 200 per Bond) will be paid to those who are registered as Holders in the Issuer's debt register (kept by Euroclear Sweden) on 27 October 2017 and is expected to be paid on 3 November 2017.
