This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on 24 August 2009 between Nexus 1 Private Limited (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the agreement Loan Agreement " 10.5 per cent Nexus 1 Private Limited Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012" - ISIN NO 001 035738.7, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 24 August 2009.
2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) The definitions of "Senior Debt" and "Senior Security" shall be amended by replacing the references to "USD 320 million" with references to "USD 340 million"; and
(ii) The first paragraph of Clause 13.2(f) shall read:
"ensure that the Senior Debt does not exceed a principal amount of USD 340 million unless the Vessel is signed on a firm contract for operations that includes contractual requirements for changes or upgrades to the Vessel that as a consequence require extra financing, in which case the Senior Debt may be increased beyond the aforesaid USD 340 million i f '
3. Confirmation of the Loan Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. The Loan Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Bond issue.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
5. Commencement
The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence at the 24 August 2009.
