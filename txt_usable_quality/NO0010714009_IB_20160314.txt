Regarding Bond Issue with ISIN NO 001 071400.9
We refer to the Bond Agreem ent of 25 June 2014 between Havila Holding AS (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee ASA concerning the Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue with ISIN NO 001 071400.9 (the "Bond Agreement" and "Bond Issue" respectively) where Nordic Trustee ASA serves as Bond Trustee. Words with capital letter are defined in the Bond Agreement.
We refer to the release by Havila Shipping ASA on 29 February 2016 of the Fourth Quarter 2015/Preliminary Accounts 2015 (the "Accounts"). Note 2 of the Accounts specifies that the bank and bond debt of Havila Shipping ASA is classified as current liabilities as Havila Shipping ASA has suspended payments of interest and amortization to its financial creditors as announced on 17 February and these creditors may at any time on their own initiative make demands for serving the debt. Furthermore, we are informed that a coupon payment of app. 12 million NOK was due by Havila Shipping ASA on 29 February 2016 but has not been paid and remain outstanding.
The Bond Agreem ent Section 15-1 letter (c) provides for cross-default in a num ber of scenarios involving inter alia Havila Shipping ASA (defined as "HAVI" in the Bond Agreement) including item (i) payment default, item (ii) suspension of payments of debts and item iv) where a creditor becomes entitled to declare any financial indebtedness due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default.
The Bond Trustee has received instructions by certain Bondholders holding in aggregate more than 20 per cent of the Bonds and accordingly, on the basis of the above and with reference to the Bond Agreement Section 151, the Bond Trustee hereby declares the Bond Issue to be in default.
