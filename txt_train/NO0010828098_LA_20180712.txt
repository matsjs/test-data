<<<

b) no Event of Default is continuing or would occur upon the incurrence of new  Financial Indebtedness.

Distribution Test  The Distribution Test is met if:  (a) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than 2.50x; and

<<<

b) no Event of Default is continuing or would occur upon the Restricted Payment.  12.3. Testing

<<<
