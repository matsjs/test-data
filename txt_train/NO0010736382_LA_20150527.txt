STATUS OF THE BONDS, PAYMENT BLOCKAGE, TURNOVER OBLIGATION AND SECURITY

<<<

Status of the Bonds

<<<

The Bonds shall constitute subordinated debt obligations of the Issuer. The Bonds shall  rank:

<<<

a) prior to all classes of shares (including preference shares) in the issuer and any other  obligations of the Issuer expressed by their terms to rank junior to the Bonds;

<<<

(b) pari passu without any preference among themselves;  (c) at least pari passu with any Subordinated Indebtedness;  (d) junior to all unsubordinated obligations of the Issuer.

<<<

Payment Blockage

<<<

No payment shall be made by the Issuer in respect of the Bonds (a "Payment Blockage") if  and for so long as any unsubordinated Financial Indebtedness of the Issuer, for which the  amount of such Financial Indebtedness or commitment for Financial Indebtedness is of a  total of USD 100 million, or equivalent thereof in other currencies, is:

<<<

a) due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default  (however described);

<<<

b) subject to a payment default;

<<<

c) subject to any other default (however described), to the extent that the creditors to  such Financial Indebtedness (directly or indirectly through their common creditor  representative or agent) have served a payment blockage notice to the Issuer with a  copy to the Bond Trustee (such payment blockage notice in respect of any such  default lasting for a maximum of 179 days),

<<<

in each case unless the Payment Blockage has been waived by the creditors to such  Financial Indebtedness (directly or indirectly through their common creditor representative  or agent).

<<<

The Issuer shall promptly notify the Bond Trustee of the occurrence of any of the events in  Clause 8.2.1 (a) and (b).

<<<

A Payment Blockage shall not release the Issuer from its obligation to pay amounts due  under the Bond Agreement, and a non-payment will constitute an Event of Default in  accordance with Clause 15.1.

<<<

A Payment Blockage shall not prevent the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders)  from making claims in the insolvency estate of the Issuer.

<<<

The Bond Trustee shall not be liable towards the Issuer or any other person for any  payments made in violation of a Payment Blockage.

<<<

Turnover Obligation

<<<
