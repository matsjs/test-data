Oslo, 4 May 2007
NOTICE OF MANDATORY REDEMPTION
We refer to the above mentioned bond issue ("Loan"). Sealift Ltd ("Sealift") announced last Friday that it has entered into an agreement to merge with the Dockwise group ("Dockwise"). The merger will be completed as a share for share transaction. As an integrated part of the transaction, Sealift will sell its 6 shipowning subsidiaries to Dockwise Transport B.V. and thus trigger the mandatory redemption of the bonds at 105 % plus accrued interest. The Loan and interest until 29 May 20071 will be repaid in connection with the interest payment date 14 May 2007.
Best regards for Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
