Oslo, 29 September 2009
Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 29 September 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 21 September 2009 (the "Summons") regarding a proposed restructuring. On 27 September 2009 Eitzen Chemical ASA added some elements to the Proposal included in the Summons. The addition to the Proposal (the "Additional Proposal") was described in a letter to the bondholders dated 27 September 2009, and made public trough a stock exchange notice. The amended proposal, over which voting took place, was the Proposal together with the Additional Proposal together (the "Proposed Resolution"). There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The Proposed Resolution obtained 91.17 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. Subject to reservations regarding the Equity Issue and amendments related to the Syndicate Lenders, as described in the Summons, the Bond Agreement will be changed accordingly.
