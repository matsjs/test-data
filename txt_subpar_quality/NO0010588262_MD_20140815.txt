Exercise of Call Option
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is acting as bond trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Norwegian Car Carriers ASA is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The Issuer has in accordance with a letter to the Bond Trustee of 15 August 2014 notified that the Issuer will exercise the Call Option in accordance with clause 10.2.1 (e) and 10.2.2 of the bond agreement to repay the entire Bond Issue. The Bond Issue will be repaid in full on 29 September 2014 at 101 % of par value - plus accrued interest from the interest payment date in March 2014 (29 March 2014). Record date is set to 25 September 2014. Bondholders requesting further information may contact Nordic Trustee ASA.
