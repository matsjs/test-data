Oslo, 13 July 2010
Proposed restructuring- Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 13 July 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held in the above captioned Loans pursuant to a summons o f 2 July 2010 regarding a proposed restructuring of the Borrower. Voting procedures were carried out separately for each of the Loans.
ISIN NO 001 032535.0 There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 88.86 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement.
ISIN NO 001 036280.9 There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum.
A portion of the votes with significance for the outcome, were cast in favour with a condition that the majority requirement at Bondholders' meetings o f the New Bond (as defined in the summons dated 2 July 2010) is adjusted from 2/3 to 9/10 (the "A djustm ent"). Reference is made to the section "Bondholders' Meetings" of the term sheet attached to the summons.
The proposed resolution with the Adjustment obtained 91.33 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Loan Agreement.
