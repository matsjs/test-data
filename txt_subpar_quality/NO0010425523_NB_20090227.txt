PROPOSED WAIVER IN RESPECT OF FINANCIAL COVENANT REQUIREMENT - NOTICE FROM BONDHOLDERS' MEETING
On 27 February 2009 a repeated Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 19 February 2009.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100% of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
The Bondholders approve that for the purpose o f computing Equity in respect of Clause 14.3 (b), the Borrower shall disregard from such computation, the effect o f any non-cash gain or loss from the change in fair market value o f any interest rate hedging arrangements entered into and maintained by the Borrower, or any consolidated subsidiary, from time to time.
As compensation to the Bondholders, the Borrower offers a one-time waiver fee o f 0.50% (flat) o f the face value o f the Bonds, payable on the fifth banking day following the Bondholders meeting, provided that the Bondholders meeting grants the proposed waiver.
