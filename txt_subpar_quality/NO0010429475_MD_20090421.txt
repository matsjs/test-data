Change of Control Event - Put Option
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where Roxar ASA is the Borrower.
Capitalised expressions used herein shall bear the same meaning as set out in the Loan Agreement unless the context otherwise requires or specified herein.
This notice constitutes the requirements as set out in clause 13.1 (h) under the Loan Agreement.
Roxar ASA has given notice that one of their shareholders, through a voluntary offer, has reached an ownership of more than 50 per cent.
This constitutes a Change of Control Event according to the Loan Agreement, which means that each Bondholder has the right of pre-payment (Put Option) of the Bonds at a price of 101 % of par value. The Put Option is however conditional upon consent from the Facility Agent for the lenders under the Bank Loan Agreement, meaning if the Facility Agent declines to give its written consent the Loan will mature in whole on the Maturity Date at 111 % of par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within thirty - 30 - Banking Days (put period) following this notice, and at the latest on the 9 of June 2009 Each Bondholder must, in order to exercise the Put Option, contact his VPS account manager (who will notify the Borrower's Paying Agent).
As consent from the Facility Agent is a condition for the exercise of the Put Option as well as payment, the payment will take place on the 30 of June 2009 (fifteen - 15 - Banking Days after the end of the put period).
