This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on x March 2014 between Fana Sparebank Boligkreditt AS (the "Issuer") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee").
1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the agreement Bond Agreement "FRN Fana Sparebank Boligkreditt AS Covered Bond Issue 2014/2020 (Extended Maturity 11 February 2021)" - ISIN 001 0703564.
2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement
The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i) Clause 3.5.2 shall read:
"Notwithstanding Clause 3.5.1 the Issuer undertakes to keep an overcollateralisation in the Cover Pool of at least 11 % while the bonds are outstanding. If the parent bank's rating is changed to A2 or better, the Issuer's commitment o f overcollateralisation will lapse."
3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement
All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. The Bond Agreement and this Amendment Agreement constitute together the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the above mentioned Bond issue.
4. Governing Law
Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court.
5. Commencement
The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence at the date of this Agreement.
