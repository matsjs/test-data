Oslo, 3 February 2014
NOTICE OF PARTIAL REPAYMENT OF THE 10.85% BOND LOAN Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Loan Trustee") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond loan (the "Bond Loan" or the "10.85 Bond Loan"). The Bond Loan is registered in the Norwegian Securities Depository (the "VPS"). The Loan Trustee has now received amount of approximately NOK 41.13 million from the Petromena ASA bankruptcy estate (the "Estate"). The amount represents a preliminary dividend payment (the "Dividend") from the Estate to the Petromena creditors. In addition Petromena Limited received settlement from one o f the litigations undertaken by the Loan Trustee and as a consequence, the Loan Trustee as pledgee over the shares in Petromena Limited has received an amount of USD 5.00 million (the "Petromena Limited Payment") to be applied as payment under the 10.85 Bond Loan. Payment to the Bondholders The aggregate o f the Dividend (after being exchanged to USD) and the Petromena Limited Payment - USD 11.56 million - will be paid to the Bondholders in the 10.85 Bond Loan. The payment will be made to the Bondholders as of 3 February 2014 (Record Date) with payment on 6 February 2014 (Payment Date). The payment will constitute a payment of principal equal to 4,91921 % of remaining outstanding amount prior to the payment.
Bondholders requesting further information may contact Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
