Stockholm, 2 November 2015 Notice from Written Procedure - Correction
On 1 October 2015, a summons to bondholder's meeting was announced, by way of procedure in writing, regarding a request for waiver. The time for reply expired on 29 October 2015. The bondholders have therefore given their consent to the waiver as set out in the notice to the written procedure.
