Oslo, 5 February 2009
Cancelled Bondholders' Meeting
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the bond issue with ISIN NO001032437.9 (the "Loan") issued by Peterson AS (the "Borrower" or the "Company"). In accordance with the summons to bondholders meeting for the Loan, an information meeting for the bondholders was conducted on the 3 February 2009. In light of the views promoted by the bondholders during the information meeting the Company has entered into further discussions with the bondholders through the Loan Trustee, and is prepared to put forward a revised proposal to the bondholders as soon as these discussions is finalized. As the preparation of a revised proposal will not be ready for consideration at the scheduled bondholders meeting on the 6 February 2009, and to allow the bondholders sufficient time to review the new proposal, the scheduled bondholders meeting is cancelled. A new bondholders meeting will as soon as possible be summoned in accordance with the provisions of loan agreement.
