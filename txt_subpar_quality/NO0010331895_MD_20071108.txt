Oslo, 8 November 2007

Repayment of the entire loan under the Bond Issue - status

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Neptune Marine Invest AS (the "Company") is the issuer. The Bond Issue is encumbered by the Company's two drillships "Neptune Explorer" and "Neptune Discoverer" (the "Drillships"). Pursuant to an Amendment Agreement that came into force on 10 October 2007, the entire loan under the Bond Issue with all interest and fees fell due on 1 November 2007.
We refer to Loan Agreement (as amended) and our letter to all Bondholders dated 2 November 2008. The entire loan under the Bond Issue with all interest and fees became due on 1 November 2007. The Loan Trustee has today been informed that the refinancing of the Bond Issue will not be completed within the grace period (which ended today 8 November 2007). Further information on the situation going forward will be given to the Bondholders as soon as possible. Meanwhile, the Loan Trustee will make the necessary preparations for recovering all claims under the Bond Issue.

Kind regards, Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
