Information to Bondholders
Nordic Trustee AS (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the holders of the bonds (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") issued by Brødrene Greger AS (the "Issuer") pursuant to the bond agreement dated 30 September 2014 (the "Bond Agreement").
All capitalised terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Bond Agreement and summons to Bondholders' Meeting dated 20 June 2017 and 4 October 2017 (the "Summons") unless otherwise states.
Reference is made to the proposals set out in the Summons and the Amendment Agreement.
i. Step 1: Settlement Date 31 October 2017 with Record Date 27 October 2017 The bondholders of the Interest Claims due on the 31 January 2017 will receive Bonds for the amount of NOK 2,393,637. The bondholders of the Interest Claims due on 30 April 2017 will receive Bonds for the amount of NOK 2,302,681. The bondholders of the Interest Claims due on 31 July 2017 will receive Bonds for the amount of NOK 1,652,000. The bondholders of the Interest Claims due on 31 October 2017 will receive Bonds for the amount of NOK 1,600,000.
(The "Interest Claims").
The Interest Claims will receive payment in Bonds at the nominal amount of NOK 1 per Bond.
ii. Step 2: Settlement Date 3 November 2017 with Record Date 1 November 2017 The Bond Issue will be reduced with an amount of NOK 3,484,223 (the Interest Claims NOK 7,948,318 less NOK 4,464,096).
iii. Step 3: Settlement Date 8 November 2017 with Record Date 6 November 2017 NOK 58,464,096 of the Outstanding Bonds will be converted into 5,103,900 new ordinary shares in the Issuer, equivalent to 90% of the total shares in the Issuer after conversion, each with a nominal value of NOK 2.
The Outstanding Amount after step 1 - 3 will be NOK 26,000,000.
Please be aware that, due to technical matters when distributing the amounts and bonds, the numbers set out above might not be the exact numbers received (but estimated).
