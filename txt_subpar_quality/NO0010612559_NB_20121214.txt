Notice to Bondholders
On 7 December a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 21 November 2012. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 80.65 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted. It was decided that the Borrower should make a payment to the Bondholders of the amount corresponding to 0.75 % of the face value of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Payment"). The Loan Trustee has been informed that the Payment has now been transferred to the Bondholders.
