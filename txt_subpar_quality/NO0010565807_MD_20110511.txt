ADJUSTMENT OF THE CONVERSION PRICE Norsk Tillitsmann ASA acts as Loan Trustee for the convertible bond issue with ISIN NO 001056580.7 issued by Marine Harvest ASA (the "Borrow er"). The Borrower has announced a dividend of NOK 0.80 per share with ex date 10 May 2011. Also, the Borrower announced a dividend payment of NOK 0.05 with ex date 8 December 2010. That dividend was below the threshold for adjustment, and was not adjusted for in December 2010, but is included in this current adjustment. The Conversion Price will be adjusted accordingly, from EUR 0.7494 to EUR 0.6547 effective on 10 May 2011, which is the date the shares traded ex-dividend.
