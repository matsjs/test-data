Notice of Bondholder Call on Thursday 25 August 2011 at 9am New York time/2pm London time/ 3pm Oslo time.
Bondholders are invited to a call which shall be held on Thursday 25 August 2011 at 9am New York time/ 2pm London time/ 3pm Oslo time. Bondholders that wish to attend the call must register their interest with Bingham McCutchen (London) LLP ("Bingham") before 5pm London time on Wednesday 24 August 2011. In order to register their interest, Bondholders should email Hannah Wright at Hannah.Wright@bingham.com. confirming: (a) the name of the Bondholder; (b) the principal face amount of the Bonds held by the relevant Bondholder (which information shall be held by Bingham and AMA on confidential terms); and (c) the name of the individuals that will be attending the call. The call will be held on an operated assisted basis and only those Bondholders that have provided the information referred to above by 5pm London time on Wednesday 24 August 2011 shall be able to access the call.
