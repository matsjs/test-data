Oslo, 31 July 2012 Correction to Summons to Bondholders' Meetings
Reference is made to the summons to bondholders' meeting, dated 30 July 2012, for the two above captioned bond issues (the "Summons"). The Company has informed the Loan Trustee that a correction to the Summons is needed. The Summons shall be amended as follows:
· The last section of Paragraph 2.3 shall be deleted. The section read: "The Company has informed the Loan Trustee that Majority Holders have entered into voting agreements with the Company agreeing to vote their Existing Bonds and/or New Bonds in favour of the Proposal as set forth in paragraph 3 (Proposal) below."
· "Majority Holders" shall not be defined and the references made to "Majority Holders" shall be deleted.
The Proposal described in Paragraph 3 is not changed.
