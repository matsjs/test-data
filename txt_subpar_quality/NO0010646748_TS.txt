Put Option:

The Bondholders may require the Bond Issue redeemed at the end of each 12 month period from the Settlement Date as follows (Put Option):

(i)

at the Interest Payment Date in July 2013 at 96 % of par plus accrued

interests on the redeemed amount, and

(ii) at the Interest Payment Date in July 2014 at 97 % of par plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount, and

(iii) at the Interest Payment Date in July 2015 at 98 % of par plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount, and

(iv) at the Interest Payment Date in July 2016 at 99 % of par plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount, and

Exercise of the Put Option shall be notified in writing to the Issuer at least sixty business days prior to the settlement (execution) date of the Put Option. The issuer can limit the total redemption of each 12 month period to a maximum of 20 % of the total Loan Amount.
