Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Today a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to the summons of 21 October 2013 and the amendments and undertakings with respect to the proposed refinancing plan and new bond terms of 4 November 2013 as applicable.
ISIN: NO 001 054844.9 -12.90 % Norwegian Energy Company ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2009/2014:
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 93.42 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
ISIN: NO 001 059230.6 - FRN Norwegian Energy Company ASA Senior Unsecured Bond 2010/2013:
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 69.55 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
