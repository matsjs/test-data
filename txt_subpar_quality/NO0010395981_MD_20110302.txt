Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") acts as loan trustee for the above captioned bond issue, where Seadrill Ltd. (the "Borrower") is the borrower. The terms and conditions of the loan are set out in a loan agreement between the Borrower and NT dated 2 November 2007 (the "Loan Agreement"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Loan Agreement or in this letter. On 24 February 2011 the Borrower announced a dividend payment o f USD 0.675 per share plus an extraordinary dividend payment o f USD 0.20 per share, a total of USD 0.875 per share. The Conversion Price is adjusted according to the Loan Agreement, from USD 28.49 to USD 27.80, effective on 2 March 2011 which is the ex-dividend date.
