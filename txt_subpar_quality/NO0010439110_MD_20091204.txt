Fee for consent to the proposal on the upcoming Bondholders' meeting
Reference is made to the Summons to Bondholders' meeting dated 26 November 2009 (the "Summons"). The fee will be paid at Maturity Date (for the avoidance of doubt that is the new Maturity Date - 28 January 2010), and will be 2.5 % p.a, on the NOK 42,000,000 due, for the 50 days deferral of Maturity Date.
