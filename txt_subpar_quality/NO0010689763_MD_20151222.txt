Declaration of an Event of Default and enforcement of pledged Escrow Account - Payment to Bondholders

Nordic Trustee ASA (formerly Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee")) acts as trustee for the holders of the bonds (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue" or the "Bonds").

Reference is made to the summons to bondholders' meeting dated 14 December 2015 (the "Summons"), and the notice from that respective meeting dated 21 December 2015, informing that the proposed resolution was adopted.

Today the Bond Trustee has declared an Event of Default and accelerated the Bonds. Furthermore, the Bond Trustee has enforced the pledge of the Escrow Account, and taken control of the USD 57,000,631 held in that account. The Bond Trustee will retain USD 7,000,631 as Escrow Account Retention Amount, as defined in the Summons, and USD 50,000,000 will be paid to the Bondholders as described below.

Payment
