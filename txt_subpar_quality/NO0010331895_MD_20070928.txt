Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Neptune Marine Invest AS (the "Company") is the issuer.
Pursuant to the resolution passed at the bondholders' meeting held on 31 August 2007, the entire Bond Issue becomes due on 1 October 2007. We have been informed by the Company that it will not to be able to close the current refinancing negotiations with its new lenders in time to be able to redeem the Bond Issue (with interest) within 1 October 2007.
The Company and the Loan Trustee would like to stress that there is an ongoing process where every party are doing their outmost to secure a refinancing on terms acceptable to the Company, and that the Company and the Lenders expect to enter into a firm Term Sheet with respect to the complete refinancing within end of business on Monday 1 October 2007. Further information will be given to the bondholders as soon as possible. Summons for a bondholders' meeting will be distributed early week 40.
