Oslo, 5 September 2011
Exercise of Call Option Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders of the bonds in the above mentioned loan with ISIN NO 001 058092.1 (the "Loan") issued by Remedial Cayman Limited (the "Company"). The Company has today given notice to NT that the Company will redeem USD 66,224,029 of the Loan at par value (100 %) plus accrued interest as soon as possible. NT has agreed to waive the required call notice period of 30 Business Days. The Record Date is end of business 5 September 2011. The Payment Date will be 7 September 2011. The principle amount which will be redeemed is USD 66,224,029 and the accrued interest to be paid is USD 8,275,967 in total a payment of USD 74,500,000. This payment reflects the final payment due to Bondholders in connection with the disposal of the assets of the Company and its group and, following the application of such payment, it is intended that the affairs of the Company and its group will now be wound up on a solvent basis. A final balancing payment may be made to Bondholders upon completion of the windingup of the Company, to the extent that any remaining funds are available to the Company after taking into account the costs and expenses of the winding-up process.
