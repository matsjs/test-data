Skeie Drilling & Production ASA - Notice from Bondholders' Meetings On 26 November 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held in each of the above mentions bond issues pursuant to summons of 18 November 2010 regarding mandatory redemption etc. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum for the July 2013 Bond. The proposed resolution obtained 98.59 % of the votes in the July 2013 Bond and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The resolution for the July 2013 Bond was adopted as set out in the summons. There were not sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum for the March 2013 Bond, and no valid resolution was made for this Bond, however the proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes. A repeated Bondholders meeting may be summoned for the March 2013 Bond within short time.
