Oro Negro Drilling Pte. Ltd. Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019
Mexico City, Mexico. July 1, 2016. Oro Negro Drilling Pte. Ltd. (the "Company") announced today that, in light of liquidity constraints, it is unable to make US$ 54.59 million in payments, including US$ 46.0 million of principal, due July 1, 2016 on its 7.50% Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019. The Company is engaged in constructive discussions with its stakeholders, including Pemex and an ad hoc group of the bondholders, to strengthen its financial position in this challenging industry environment.
