Delphin Kreuzfahrten GmbH - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 June 2008 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 10 June 2008 regarding the approval of a new restructuring plan. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
1. Efforts shall be made by the Company to sell the Vessel as soon as possible. 2. The Loan shall mature on or about the date when the settlement payment for the
Vessel takes place (the "New Maturity Date "), however no later than 1 August 2008. 3. The maturity price for the Loan at the New Maturity Date shall be 104% o f par,
however, if a Memorandum o f Agreement for the sale of the Vessel has not been entered into within 7July 2008, the maturity price shall be 109% o fpar. 4. Interest for the Loan (14,25%, based on par) is due on the dates set out in the Amendment Agreement, however payment o f such interest may take place at the New Maturity Date, with the addition on any compound interest (14,25 %o). The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting by request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
