Oslo, 1 September 2010
Partial repayment/distribution to the bondholders
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as the Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where Sea Production Ltd is the borrower (the "Borrower").
The Borrower has sold the Aframax Tanker "MV Sea Jaguar" (the "Vessel").
As a result of the sales, a partial repayment/distribution to the Bondholders in the above mentioned bond issue has been carried out on 31 August 2010 at 102 % of par value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount. In total, an amount of USD 5,593,762.94 was distributed to the Bondholders. To facilitate the pro-rata partial redemption, all bonds were split to USD 1.
The redemption was carried out according to the provisions set out in Clause 10.3 in the Loan Agreement amended in accordance with the resolution in the Bondholders' Meeting 15 July 2010.
