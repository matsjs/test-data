Information to Bondholders - extension of the second term of the Orlando Licence

Nordic Trustee ASA acts as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Iona Energy Company (UK) Limited is the issuer (the "Issuer").

All capitalised terms used herein and not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned thereto in the bond loan agreement for the Bond Issue originally dated 26 September 2013 (as amended on 3 June 2014, and as amended and restated on 17 April 2015, 10 March 2017 and 13 April 2017) (the "Bond Agreement").

Reference is made to the notice published on 22 June 2017 informing that the OGA has confirmed the extension of the second term of the Orlando Licence to 30 June 2019, and consequently, that the Deferred Bond Repayment Conditions have been satisfied, thereby triggering the payment by the Issuer of the Deferred Bond Repayment Amount as a repayment of principal under the Outstanding Bonds.

The Deferred Bond Repayment Amount (less the Final MIP Amount, which will be settled in accordance with Clause 9.2.2 of the Bond Agreement) will be paid to Bondholders as follows:

Record Date:

10 July 2017 (end of business)

Settlement Date: 12 July 2017

Repayment Amount: USD 2,975,000 will be paid as a principal payment only at par value, equal to

85% of the Outstanding Bonds. No interest will be paid.

Bondholders remain entitled to any amounts paid to the Bond Trustee in respect of the Huntington Claim and the West Wick Consideration pursuant to Clause 9.4.1 of the Bond Agreement.
