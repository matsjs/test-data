Information to Bondholders - Second payment of PIK interest for the term period ending on 14 September 2017
Nordic Trustee AS (formerly known as Norsk Tillitsmann ASA) (the "Bond Trustee") acts as bond trustee for the holders (the "Bondholders") of the Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012 (the "Bonds") issued by Sea Production Ltd (the "Issuer"), and guaranteed by Puffin Ltd ("Puffin") and Wisdom Shipping (S) Pte. Ltd. ("Wisdom"), pursuant to the bond agreement dated 9 February 2007 (as amended and restated from time to time) (the "Bond Agreement") between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee.

It has today been performed a payment of interest by issuing of additional Bonds (the "PIK Interest") as a second payment of interest for the term period ending on 14 September 2017 (the "Interest Payment Date"), as the original payment of PIK Interest on the Interest Payment Date was insufficient.

Payment of interest was on the Interest Payment Date done by PIK Interest with interest rate of 5%. In accordance to the Bond Agreement clause 9.1 (b) (ii) payment by PIK Interest has a fixed rate of 7%. An additional 2% PIK Interest has therefore been paid out today to the Bondholders.

Please note the following details in respect of the payment:

13 September 2017 26 September 2017 USD 195 444 (constituting 0.16666% o f the outstanding amount at the Interest Payment Date).
