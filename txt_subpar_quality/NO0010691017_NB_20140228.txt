Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Today a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 14 February 2014. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. Proposed resolution (i) extension of maturity date to 30 June 2014 obtained 98.59 % of the votes, and proposed resolution (ii) increasing the loan amount obtained 98.12 % of the votes. Both proposals were adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as set out in the summons to the meeting.
