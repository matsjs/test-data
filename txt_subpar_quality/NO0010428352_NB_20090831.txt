Amendment to Bond Loan Agreement - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 31 August 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 20 August 2009 regarding a proposal to amend the Bond Loan Agreement. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 95.97 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed accordingly. I addition, as a reply to a question brought up by a bondholder, the company promised to provide the bondholders with quarter reports for the remaining term of the Loan.
