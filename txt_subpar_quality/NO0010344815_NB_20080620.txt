PROPOSAL TO AMEND MATURITY DATE IN LOAN AGREEMENT - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting On 20 June 2008 at 1000 hours a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons dated 11 June 2008 regarding a proposal to amend the maturity date in the Loan Agreement. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows: A. Amendment of the Loan Agreement
1. New maturity datefor the Loan is 23 July 2008 ( "New Maturity Date "). 2. Interest payment due 23 June 2008 (the "June Payment Date") is postponed to the New
Maturity Date), however, the Borrower will have the option to pay the due interest at the June Payment Date. 3. The Borrower shall have the option to call the Loan at any time from and including the June Payment Date to the New Maturity Date at 105% o fpar plus accrued interest. 4. Interest o f the Loan shall be NIBOR plus 9.75%from and including the June Payment Date to the New Maturity Date. 5. Compound interestfrom the June Payment Date o fNIBOR plus 9.75% shall be paid at the New Maturity Date. B. Effectiveness and execution of the resolution: Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is given power o f attorney to enter into the necessary agreements in connection with decisions made on the bondholders' meeting as well as to carry out the necessary completion work, including make other necessary amendments o f the Loan Agreement. The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting by request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
