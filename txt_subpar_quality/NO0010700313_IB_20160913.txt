Oslo, 13 September 2016 Payment to Bondholders Nordic Trustee ASA acts as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue where Dannemora Magnetit AB is the issuer (the "Issuer") and Dannemora Mineral AB is the parent guarantor. Reference is made to the Press Release dated 18 March 2016 regarding Dannemora Mineral AB filing for bankruptcy The Bond Trustee has received the amount of SEK 8,302,055.24 from the Dannemora Mineral AB bankruptcy estate (the "Estate") for distribution to Bondholders. The Bond Trustee has withheld SEK 310,000 to cover fees and external expenses. The Bondholders will receive SEK 7,992,055 which constitutes approximately 38.0574% of the outstanding principal of SEK 21,000,000. The amount represents a preliminary dividend payment from the Estate.
The distribution will be carried out in accordance with the payment details below:
Amount: SEK 7,992,055 Record Date: 12 September 2016 Payment Date: 13 September 2016
The dividend will be paid to the Bondholders pro rata as partly repayment of the principal.
