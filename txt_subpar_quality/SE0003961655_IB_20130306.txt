Medstop AB - Early Redemption of Bonds
To holders of the 700,000,000 SEK 2011/2016 bonds (ISIN SE0003961655) issued by Medstop AB ('Medstop')
Oriola-KD Holding Sverige AB has on the 3rd of June, 2013, acquired 100% of the shares in Medstop. According to the terms and conditions for the bonds, such change of control constitutes an event which grants Medstop the option to call all outstanding bonds at 102.75 per cent of the nominal amount plus accrued interest. Medstop announces that it will exercise its right to call the bonds.
The bonds will be redeemed on the 2nd of July, 2013. Repayment of the nominal amount, redemption premium and accrued interest will be effectuated to holders registered with the CSD (Euroclear Sweden AB) on record date 25th of June, 2013. The last trading date to have a transaction registered on record date is June 19th, 2013.
A formal notice of the early redemption will be sent by post to the holders.
