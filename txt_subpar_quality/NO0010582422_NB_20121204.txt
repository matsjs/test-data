Notice from Bondholders' Meeting - Request for waiver and amendment
On 4 December 2012 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 19 November 2012 (the "Summons").
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum.
The Proposal described in the Summons was amended by a supplemental offer from the Company;
"1. The Waiver Request and Proposal: In connection with the waiver request regarding the (i) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA covenant, and (ii) the Booked Equity covenant, the Company offers the extraordinary redemption ofNO K 47.5 million to be carried out at a price equal to 102% o fpar plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount (the previous offer was at a price equal to 101% o fpar plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
