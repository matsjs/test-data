Ability Drilling ASA - Notice to Bondholders

Oslo, 22 May 2009

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") is appointed as loan trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Loan") where Ability Drilling ASA is the issuer (the "Issuer").

On May 13 2009, the Issuer announced that Ability Drilling ASA had sent its creditors a notice o f temporarily suspension o f payments, lasting to 13 June 2009. The Issuer informed that the notice was following the situation occurred as a consequence of delayed rig deliveries from the supplier TTS Sense, and an escalated conflict level between the Issuer and TTS Sense. The Issuer further informed that this step was taken to secure equality between the company's creditors.

After this announcement, it was the intention to summon for a bondholders' meeting, and all necessary preparations for the summons was effectuated.

On May 20 2009, the Issuer announced that Ability Drilling ASA on May 20 received a copy o f a bankruptcy petition filing from TTS Sense, delivered to Nordhordaland tingrett 19 May 2009.

The process related to the summons for a bondholders' meeting was therefore temporarily suspended. The questions related to the bankruptcy petition are to be considered by the board.

Please find attached announcements from the Issuer. Further information regarding the situation will be available at www.stamdata.no.

NT is in dialogue with the Issuer and will monitor the situation closely.

Ability Drilling suspends payments temporarily
Ability Drilling is today sending its creditors notice of temporarily suspension of payments, lasting to 13.06.2009. The notice is following the situation occurred as a consequence of delayed rig deliveries from supplier TTS, and an escalated conflict level between Ability Drilling and TTS. The company takes this step to secure equality between the company's creditors.
In the notice to the creditors, the management expresses a preliminary evaluation of the values in the company, concluding that there are enough assets to cover all legitimate claims from creditors, including long term debt.
However, short term it will be necessary to dispose assets not included in a future business plan, to secure a payment plan to the benefit of all creditors.
The company will inform on further plans when appropriate.

Bankruptcy petition received
Ability Drilling has today received a copy of a bankruptcy petition filing from TTS Sense, delivered to Nordhordaland tingrett 19 May 2009.
