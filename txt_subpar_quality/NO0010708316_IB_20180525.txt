Exercise of Call Option
Nordic Trustee AS (the "Bond Trustee") acts as bond trustee for the Bondholders in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Ocean Yield ASA is the issuer (the "Issuer"). Redemption amount: NOK 1,000,000,000.00

Redemption price: 101% of the said Redemption amount, plus accrued interest on the redeemed

amount up to the Settlement Date.

4 July 2018.
