Sea Production
Sea Production Ltd. Financial Information Third Quarter 2015
Background
This financial information has been prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards.
General Information
Sea Production Ltd. and its subsidiaries own and operate two floating, production, storage and off loading ("FPSO") vessels.
Financial Statements
Income Statement (Third quarter 2014 figures in brackets)
The Group's revenue in the quarter was US$3.5 million (US$4.7 million), with EBITDA of US$2.4 million (US$2.9 million).
All charter revenues relate to Crystal Ocean. Front Puffin was in Keppel Shipyard in Singapore undergoing refurbishment and upgrade work in preparation for its new contract.
Operating expenses for the quarter amounted to US$0.2 million (US$0.8 million) and administrative expenses for the period were US$0.9 million (US$1.0 million).
Total depreciation for the quarter was US$3.8 million (US$3.8 million). The Group's vessels are depreciated on a straight-line basis over their economically useful lives, taking into account their estimated residual value. Assessment of the remaining economic life of the assets is done on a yearly basis. Capital costs incurred in relation to a specific contract are depreciated over the expected life of the relevant contract. At the end of the contract any remaining balance is written-off.
Net financing charges for the period were US$8.7 million (US$2.2 million). Interest expense on the outstanding US$ bonds was US$7.3 million (US$2.7 million).
The net result for the quarter was a loss of US$10.4 million (loss of US$3.5 million).
Balance Sheet
Current assets as at 30 September 2015 were US$13.0 million (US$6.9 million) and non-current assets were US$141.6 million (US$139.6 million). The Company has a 5 year callable bond loan with a nominal value of US$96.8 million outstanding as at 30 September 2015 and a final maturity of 14 March 2016. The Company had no other borrowings at the end of the quarter. The Company's equity as at 30 September 2015 was US$8.3 million (US$44.5 million).

Group Update Third Quarter 2015 Crystal Ocean continued its charter with Blue Marine. As referred to above, Front Puffin has been in the shipyard in advance of deployment on contract with Folawiyo Aje Services Limited for operations at the Aje Field, offshore Nigeria. These works are expected to be completed early in Q1 2016, at which point the vessel will be ready for sailaway to Nigeria. US$ bonds The Group has been in discussions with an ad hoc group of holders of the US$ bonds with a view to rescheduling the maturity of the bonds and an agreement was reached to further extend the maturity to 14 March 2016.
