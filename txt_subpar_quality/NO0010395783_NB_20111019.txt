Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 October 2011 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 11 October 2011 regarding to vote on Plan of Reorganisation. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 99.92 % of the votes, 0.38 % voted against the Plan of Reorganisation. The votes will be submitted to the Bankruptcy Court in accordance with the voting instructions.
