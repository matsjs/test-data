Information to bondholders - change of margin Nordic Trustee ASA is appointed as Bond Trustee for the above mentioned Bond Issue where Helgeland Boligkreditt AS is the issuer. As Bond Trustee we have been informed that Helgeland Boligkreditt's covered bonds have obtained a credit rating o f Aaa from Moody's. In accordance with the bond agreements clause 1 «Margin», the margin shall be adjusted from 0.55 percentage points p.a. to 0.4 percentage points p.a. given that the covered bonds obtain a credit rating equivalent to AA+ or AA1 (from S&P, Fitch or M oody's) or better within 12 months from issue date. The margin shall be adjusted from and including the first coupon date after such credit rating. The margin will consequently be 0.4 percentage points p.a. from and including the coupon date 18 June 2014.
