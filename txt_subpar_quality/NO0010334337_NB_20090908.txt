Oslo, 8 September 2009
Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 8 September 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 31 August 2009 regarding a vote over a temporary waiver of the value adjusted equity ratio covenant in the Loan Agreement. The Loan consists of a NOK tranche and a USD tranche with separate ISINs. According to the Loan Agreement the bondholders meeting was held for the Loan as a whole with joint voting for the two tranches. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 99.38 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. Eitzen Chemical ASA is given a waiver accordingly.
