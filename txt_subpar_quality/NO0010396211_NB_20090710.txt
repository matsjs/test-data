Conversion of debt - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 10 July 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 2 July 2009 regarding a financial restructuring of the Borrower which involves conversion of debt to equity. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 85.26% of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The bonds will subsequently be subject to the terms as set out in the resolution in the summons.
