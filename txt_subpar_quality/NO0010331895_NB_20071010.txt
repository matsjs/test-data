Proposal to new repayment schedule and exercise of call option - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 10 October 2007 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 1 October 2007 regarding Proposal to new repayment schedule and exercise of call option.
There where sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form at quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100.00 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
"The repayment of the entire Loan/Bond Issue (with accrued interest/overdue interest) shall be postponed from 1 October 2007 until the Refinancing (as described in the summons for this bondholders ' meeting) is available to the Borrower/Company). The entire Loan/Bond Issue shall be redeemed at 106.5 % o f par value plus interest/overdue interest. The Borrower/Company shall pay interest and overdue interest in accordance with the Loan Agreement from 1 October 2007 until repayment is completed, but no later than 1 November 2007. For the avoidance o f doubt, overdue interest has already accrued on the instalment and interest payment that should have been made 5 September 2007. Interest shall be calculated on an Actual/360 day countfraction.
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is given power o f attorney to enter into the necessary agreements in connection with decisions made on the bondholders 'meeting as well as to carry out the necessary completion work, including make other necessary amendments of the Loan Agreement. "
The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting be request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
