Information to Bondholders - PIK Interest
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue ("the "Bonds" or the "Bond Issue") issued by Sea Production Ltd. as issuer (the "Issuer"). Capitalised terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement dated 9 February 2007 (as amended and restated) (the "Bond Agreement"), unless otherwise defined herein. The Bond Trustee has received notice from the Issuer that it, pursuant to Clause 9.1 of the Bond Agreement, opts to pay accrued interest on the Bonds due on 14 November 2016 by way of PIK Interest (and not cash). The interest payment will thus be made in the form of additional Bonds based on the face value, and calculated based on the total number of Bonds on the Interest Payment Date. The interest rate used as basis for the PIK Interest is 7.00 percentage points per annum.
