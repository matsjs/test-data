Change of Control Event - Put Option
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where Ocean Rig ASA is the Borrower. Dryships Inc. has in a press release o f today given notice that they together with a wholly owned subsidiary have acquired shares in Ocean Rig ASA and reached a total holding of more than 50 percent o f the shares and votes. This constitutes a Change o f Control Event according to the Loan Agreement, which means that each Bondholder has the right o f pre-payment (Put Option) o f the Bonds at a price o f 100 % of par value. The Put Option must be exercised within sixty - 60 - Banking Days following this notice, and at the latest on 22 o f July 2008. Each Bondholder must, in order to exercise the Put Option, contact his VPS account manager (who will notify the Borrower's Paying Agent). The put date {payment date) will be ninety - 90 - days following the date when the Paying Agent received the Put Option request. Interests accrued to, but not including, the put date will be paid on the put date.
