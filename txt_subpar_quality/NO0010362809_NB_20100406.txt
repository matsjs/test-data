PROPOSAL TO POSTPONE MATURITY - NOTICE FROM BONDHOLDERS' MEETING On 6 April 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 22 March 2010 regarding postponing the Maturity Date of the Loan. The Loan Agreement will be amended to reflect the resolution as set out in the summons.
