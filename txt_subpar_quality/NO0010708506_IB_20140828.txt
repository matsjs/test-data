August 2014

VERITAS PETROLEUM SERVICES BV H1 2014

Financial highlights
1. Total revenue + 3.2% on plan. 2. Normalised EBITDA (before exceptional items) + 3.6% on plan 3. Liquidity 2.23 x covenant requirement 4. Cash at bank + US$ 3.6m on plan due to timing of exceptional items and cash
management 5. FQT revenue stream on plan 6. BQS revenues +15.8% on plan 7. CAPEX spend higher than plan due to timing and commencement of new
project to improve customer efficiency through our production tools.
Marketing highlights
 New branding introduced `VPS'  All group company names being updated accordingly  Market position retained and increasing on the BQS sector  Customer reaction positive
Management highlights
 CEO relocated to Europe  New COO appointed for Europe  Group Financial Manager appointed and based in Rotterdam  Group HR Manager appointed and based in Rotterdam
Operational highlights
 Carve out from Det Norske Veritas completed (IT in July)  ERP system implemented and fully functional  External review of logistics determines that in house service is best in class  Freight rates re-negotiated with positive outcome  HQ now based in Rotterdam  Relocated facilities in Germany, Norway and UK  Shared service centre operational in Singapore
