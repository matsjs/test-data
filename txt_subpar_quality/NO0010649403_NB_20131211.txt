Notice from Bondholders' Meeting

Oslo, 11 December 2013

On 11 December 2013 Bondholders' Meetings were held pursuant to summons of 25 November 2013.

There were sufficient Bondholders present at both meetings to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained (i) 96.77% of the votes in the 2015 Bond Issue and (ii) 100% of the votes in the 2016 Bond Issue. The proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement for both Bond Issues.
