The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement "FRN Troms Offshore Supply AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2016" - ISIN 001 0674328, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting on 2 September 2013. 2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows: (i) The definition of "Maturity Date" shnll read:

Confirmation of the Bond Agreement All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force.
