Completion of Sale of Remedial ESV Solution
18 March 2011 - Remedial Cayman ESV2 Limited (the "Seller"), a wholly owned subsidiary of Remedial Cayman Limited ("Remedial Cayman"), entered into an asset purchase agreement with Spring Capital Resources Inc. (the "Purchaser") on 10 February 2011 in connection with the sale of the Remedial ESV Solution to the Purchaser (the "Asset Purchase Agreement").
Remedial Cayman hereby announces that the conditions precedent to completion of the Asset Purchase Agreement were satisfied or waived today and accordingly the Seller has completed the sale of the Remedial ESV Solution to the Purchaser in accordance with the terms of the Asset Purchase Agreement.
Remedial Cayman, based in Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands, built Elevating Support Vessels (ESVTM) as well intervention and construction support units for petroleum operating companies and others worldwide. For more information, visit www.remedialoffshore.com.
