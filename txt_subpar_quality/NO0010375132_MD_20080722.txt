Mandatory Redemption - Partly
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where Marine Subsea AS is the Borrower. Marine Subsea AS has given notice in a press release o f 24 of June 2008 that they have sold one o f the Barges that is pledged in favour o f the Loan. This constitutes a Mandatory Redemption according to the Loan Agreements which means that 20 % o f the Loan (equal to USD 34 000 000,-) shall be redeemed at a price o f 107 % of par value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount. The partial redemption of the Loan will be carried out by drawing o f lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment o f individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds). An amount equal to the redeemed amount plus interest will be placed in an escrow account, pledged and blocked in favour of the Loan Trustee on behalf of the bondholders.
The date o f redemption (payment date) will be 03 o f September 2008.
