Oslo, 16 November 2011
NOTICE OF PARTIAL REPAYMENT OF THE 10.85% BOND LOAN As trustee for the 10.85% Bond Loan Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") has received notice from Petromena Limited that it will distribute an amount of USD 45.0 million. The amount is derived from funds hold by Petromena Limited that has been pledged to the 10.85 Bond Loan. In addition NTM has received USD 5.0 mill from the sale of the rig Petrolia formerly owned by Petromena Limited. Out of the aggregate amount of USD 50.0 million, USD 7.5 million will be paid to NTM as security for costs incurred in relation to the enforcement process in relation to the 10.85 Bond Loan. In addition an amount of USD 5.0 mill will be paid to NTM as security for the indemnity provided to AMA in accordance with the resolution adopted at the bondholders meeting held on 18 February 2009. The remaining amount of USD 37.5 million will be repaid to bondholders as on 17 November 2011 ("Record Date") with payment on 18 November 2011 ("Payment Date"). The payment will constitute a payment of interest equal to 14,20454 % of remaining outstanding amount.
