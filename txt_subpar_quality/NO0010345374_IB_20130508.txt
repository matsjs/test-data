EFFECTIVE DATE OF PLAN OF REORGANISATOIN OF DEBITOR B+H OCEAN CARRIERS UNDER CHAPTER 11 OF THE BANKRUPTCY CODE Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is appointed trustee for the above captioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") issued by B+H Ocean Carriers Ltd. (the "Borrower") in the outstanding amount of NOK 25,000,000. All capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined herein, shall have the same meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bond Issue (the "Bond Agreement") or this notice. Reference is made to the information notice to the Bondholders dated 15 January 2013, relating to the joint plan of reorganization (the "Plan") of the Borrower under chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. The Bond Trustee has been notified that on 2 May 2013 the Plan has been made effective by the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of New York. The Bond Trustee has been informed that there may be made two distributions to the Bondholders, the first distribution is expected to be made during third quarter 2013. Further notice will be published at Stamdata. Upon completion of the distributions to the Bondholders, the Bond Issue will be redeemed and the Bond Agreement will be terminated.
