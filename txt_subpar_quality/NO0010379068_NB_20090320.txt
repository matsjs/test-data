Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 20 March 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 11 March 2009 regarding a proposal to amend the Bond Loan Agreement. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this Bondholders meeting obtained 89,75 % o f the votes (the "Resolution"). The proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Loan Agreement. The Bond Loan Agreement will therefore be changed to reflect the Resolution adopted in today's Bondholders' meeting.
