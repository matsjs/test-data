Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where PetroProd Ltd is the Borrower. The partial redemption o f the Loan will be carried out by drawing o f lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds). An amount equal to the redeemed amount plus interest will be placed in an escrow account upon sale o f the vessels, pledged and blocked in favour o f the Loan Trustee on behalf o f the bondholders until the date for redemption. The date o f redemption (payment date) will be 16.01.2009
