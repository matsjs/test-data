Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 March 2010 Bondholders' meetings were held pursuant to summons of 4 March 2010 (the "Summons") regarding temporary waiver of financial covenants.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at both meetings to form a quorum. The proposed Proposal (as set out in the Summons) obtained (i) in respect of the Fixed Bond, 52.85 % of the votes, and (ii) in respect of the FRN Bond, 16.93 % of the votes. Hence, the proposed Proposal was not adopted by the Bondholders' meetings.
However, at the Bondholders' meetings, some of the Bondholders casted their votes subject to Engagement of Financial Advisers (on terms and as defined below) (the "Amended Proposal"). The Amended Proposal obtained (i) in respect of the Fixed Bond, 100 % of the votes, and (ii) in respect of the FRN Bond, 99.47 % of the votes. Hence, the Amended Proposal will be adopted if and when the Borrower accepts such conditions.
Engagement of Financial Advisers The Loan Trustee shall be entitled to immediately appoint First Securities ASA, Fearnley Fonds ASA and Nordea Bank Norge ASA (collectively, the "Financial Advisers") to provide financial advisory services to the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders. These services will i.a. include conducting a business review of the Borrower and its subsidiaries. The key terms of Engagement of the Financial Advisers shall include the following:
(i) The Financial Advisers shall be engaged by the Loan Trustee (for and on behalf of the Bondholders in all bonds issued by the Borrower).
