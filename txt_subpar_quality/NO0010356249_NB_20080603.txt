Delphin Kreuzfahrten GmbH - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 3 June 2008 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 23 May 2008 regarding a request to Bondholders related to continuation of the Amendment Agreement of 7 March 2008.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 95.33 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement, with the following wording;
A continuation o f the Amendment Agreement o f 7March 2008 is approved.
The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting by request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
The interest and amortisation due on 30 May was not paid as scheduled, and the Borrower is in breach of its obligations according to the Amendment Agreement. The Bondholders' meeting was based on information prior to this situation. The Loan Trustee has, based on discussions in conference call with the Bondholders 30 May, granted the Borrower a short grace period until a new Bondholders' meeting is held.
