NOTICE OF PARTIAL REPAYMENT OF THE 10.85% BOND LOAN As trustee for the 10.85% Bond Loan Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") has received amount of approximately USD 12.65 million designated for payment to the bondholders in the 10.85% Bond Loan. The amount is paid by TOT Drilling Limited ("TOT") (name recently changed from Larsen Oil & Gas Limited) on the basis of a court decision awarding NTM an amount of USD 12,624,908. The received amount will be distributed to the bondholders in the 10.85% Bond Loan as on 22 March 2012 ("Record Date") with payment on 26 March 2012 ("Payment Date"). The payment will constitute a payment of interest equal to 2.30540 % of remaining outstanding amount (a total of USD 6,086,259.40) and a payment of principal equal to 2.48626 % of remaining outstanding amount (a total of USD 6,563,723.00). To facilitate the payment of principal each bond will be split into 100,000 bonds - thus reducing the face value of each bond from USD 100,000 to USD 1.
