Exercise of Call Option

Oslo, 20 November 2013

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA has received notification from Realkapital European Opportunity AS regarding exercise of its Call Option.
An amount of NOK 23,568,413 will be repaid with settlement date 7 January 2014 at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest). The record date will be 3 January 2014.
The repayment will be carried out on a pro rata basis, and to carry this out there will be made a split of the face value of the Bonds from NOK 500,000 to NOK 1.
