Notice from Bondholders' Meeting in the six above bonds
On 10 November 2011 a Bondholders' meeting in all of the six above bonds were held pursuant to summons of 24 October 2011 regarding the restructuring proposal outlined in the summons.
The proposals put forward in the summons were carried in all of the bonds, as follows:
For ISIN NO 001 039164.2- Voyageur Bonds
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolutions, relevant for this bond, obtained 99.82 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
For ISIN NO 001 036696.6 - Piranema Bonds
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolutions, relevant for this bond, obtained 99.55 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement.
For NO 001 059362.7 - Unsecured Bonds
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolutions, relevant for this bond, obtained 99.27 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement.
