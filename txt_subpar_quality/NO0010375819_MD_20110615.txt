Notice to Bondholders - Mandatory Redemption

Oslo, 15 June

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Bond Trustee (the "Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue where Mosvold Supply Pic is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The terms and conditions of the bond issue are set out in a bond agreement between the Trustee and the Issuer dated 28 June 2011 (the "Bond Agreement"). All capitalized terms have the meaning assigned to them in the Bond Agreement.
The Issuer has given notice in a press release of 30 May 2011 that they have terminated the Construction Contract of one o f the Vessels with Hull No.: 7048.
This constitutes a Mandatory Redemption Event according to the Bond Agreement Clause 10.4. The Remaining Loan will therefore be redeemed on 16 June 2011 according to this provision at a price of 105% of par value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount.
