Mandatory Prepayment Event - partial repayment to the bondholders
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as the Bond Trustee for the above mentioned Bond Issue where Polarcus Alima AS is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The Issuer has notified that the vessel "M/V Polarcus Samur" (the "Vessel") has been sold. As a result of the sales, a partial repayment/distribution to the Bondholders in the above mentioned bond issue will be carried out on 13 February 2013 at 106 % of par value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount. To facilitate the pro-rata partial redemption, all bonds will be split to USD 1. Record Date is set to 11 February 2013. The redemption is carried out according to the provisions set out in Clause 10.4 in the Bond Agreement.
