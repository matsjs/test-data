Proposal to appoint AMA Capital Partners as financial advisor and to authorise the Loan Trustee to appoint a rig broker - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 18 February 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held in the above mentioned loans (the "Bond Loans") with ISIN NO 001031608.6 (the "NOKm 2,000 Bond Issue"), ISIN NO 001039578.3 (the "USDm 300 Bond Issue") and ISIN NO 001035264.4 (the "NOKm 1,600 Bond Issue") pursuant to summons of 9 February 2009 regarding a proposal to appoint AMA Capital Partners as financial advisor and to authorise the Loan Trustee to appoint a rig broker.
In respect o f the NOKm 2,000 Bond Issue:
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 50.52 % of the votes, which, according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement, was not sufficient for adoption. The Loan Agreement will remain unchanged.
In respect o f the USDm 300 Bond Issue:
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution (as set out in the summons to this Bondholders' Meeting) obtained 71,62 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement. The Bond Agreement will, if required, therefore be changed following to this.
In respect o f the NOKm 1,600 Bond Issue:
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution (as set out in the summons to this Bondholders' Meeting) obtained 71.22 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement. The Bond Agreement will, if required, therefore be changed following to this.
