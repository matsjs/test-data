Oslo, 26 August 2014 FINAL DISTRIBUTION TO BONDHOLDERS Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is appointed bond trustee for the above captioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") issued by B+H Ocean Carriers Ltd. (the "Borrower") in the outstanding amount of USD 12,625,517. All capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined herein, shall have the same meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bond Issue (the "Bond Agreement") or this notice. Reference is made to the information notice to the Bondholders dated 15 January 2013, relating to the joint plan of reorganization (the "Plan") of the Borrower under chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. Further reference is made to the information notice dated 8 May 2013, relating to the effective date of the Plan. The Bond Trustee has received the final distribution from the liquidating trust of the Borrower (BHO Liquidating Trust). The final distribution will be carried out in accordance with the payment details below:
· Amount: 0.30354 per cent of nominal amount of Bond Issue · Record Date: 29 August, 2014 · Payment Date: 2 September, 2014 The payment will be made pro-rata as partly repayment of principal at par value. Following the final distribution to the Bondholders, the Bond Issue will be redeemed and the Bond Agreement will be terminated.
