Oslo, 8 January 2015
Exercise of Call Option
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is acting as bond trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Hurtigruten ASA is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The Issuer has in accordance with a letter to the Bond Trustee of 8 January 2015 notified that the Issuer will exercise the Call Option to repay the entire Bond Issue in accordance with clause 10.3.1 and 10.3.2 of the bond agreement dated 16 March 2012 and as amended pursuant to an amendment agreement dated 25 November 2015. The Bond Issue will be repaid in full on 23 January 2015 at 112.5 % of par value - plus accrued and unpaid interest. Bondholders requesting further information may contact Nordic Trustee ASA.
