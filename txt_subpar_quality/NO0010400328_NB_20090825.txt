Waiver for the Change of Control Put Option Clause - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 25 August 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to the summons o f 17 August 2009, regarding a request for a time limited waiver for the Change o f Control clause of the Loan Agreement.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100% o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement. The Bonds will therefore be subject to the following waiver:
The Bondholders waive the Change of Control Put Option under clause 10.3 o f the Loan Agreement. The waiver shall be effective for a period o f two months from the date of the Bondholders Meeting. The scope o f the waiver is limited to Change o f Control events occurring during the time the waiver is effective.
