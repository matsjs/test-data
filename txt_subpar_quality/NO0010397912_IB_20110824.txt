Change of Control Event - Put Option
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Loan where Hansa Property Group ASA is the borrower (the "Borrower"). The Loan Trustee has been given notice that the merger between the Borrower and Selvaag Bolig AS, with Selvaag Bolig AS as acquiring company, became legally effective 23 August 2011. This constitutes a Change of Control Event according to the Loan Agreement, which means that each Bondholder has the right of pre-payment (Put Option) of the Bonds at a price of 101 % of par value. The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent o f the Loan (the "Paying Agent") of the pre-payment request. The put date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the repayment request. The Put Option must be exercised within 25 October 2011.
