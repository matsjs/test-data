Change of Control Event - Put Option
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the holders (the "Bondholders") of bonds in the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue"). All capitalised terms used but not defined herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bond Issue (the "Bond Agreement") between Norwegian Car Carriers ASA (the "Issuer") and the Bond Trustee dated 28 September 2010. The Bond Trustee has been informed that Car Carrier Investments AS has purchased approx. 95.6 % of the shares in the Issuer, and a Change of Control Event has occurred. According to the Bond Agreement, a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") at a price of 100 % par plus accrued interest is triggered by a Change of Control Event. The Put Option may be exercised at the latest on 23 June 2014. The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to their respective Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the pre-payment request.
