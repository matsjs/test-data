Oslo, 8 November 2013
DISTRIBUTION TO BONDHOLDERS
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is appointed bond trustee for the above captioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") issued by B+H Ocean Carriers Ltd. (the "Borrower") in the outstanding amount of USD 13,500,000.
All capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined herein, shall have the same meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bond Issue (the "Bond Agreement") or this notice.
Reference is made to the information notice to the Bondholders dated 15 January 2013, relating to the joint plan of reorganization (the "Plan") of the Borrower under chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. Further reference is made to the information notice dated 8 May 2013, relating to the effective date of the Plan.
The Bond Trustee has received the initial distribution from the liquidating trust of the Borrower (BHO Liquidating Trust). The initial distribution will be carried out in accordance with the payment details below:
· Amount: 6.47765 per cent of nominal amount of Bond Issue
· Record Date: 12 November, 2013
· Payment Date: 14 November, 2013
The payment will be made pro-rata as partly repayment of principal at par value. In connection with the payment, the denomination of each Bond will be converted into USD 1.
In order to facilitate the second and final distribution (minor) to the Bondholders, the Bond Trustee may at its own discretion instruct to extend the Maturity Date of the Bond Issue in the Norwegian Securities Register until at the latest 30 June, 2014.

Yours sincerely,

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
