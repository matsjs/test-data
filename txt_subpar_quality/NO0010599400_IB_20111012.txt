Change of Control Event - Put Option
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Bond Trustee for the above mentioned Bond Issues where Aker Drilling ASA is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The Bond Trustee has been given notice that the acquisition of all outstanding shares in the Issuer by Transocean Services AS became legally effective 11 October 2011. This constitutes a Change of Control Event according to the Bond Agreements, which means that each Bondholder has the right of pre-payment (the "Put Option") of the Bonds at a price of 100 % o f par value. The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent o f the Bonds (the "Paying Agent") o f the pre-payment request. The put date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the repayment request. The Put Option must be exercised within 10 January 2012.
