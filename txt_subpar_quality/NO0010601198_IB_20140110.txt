To the bondholders in: ISIN: NO 001 060119.8 - 11.75 per cent Dannemora Mineral AB Callable Senior Secured Bond Issue 2011/2016
Oslo, 10 January 2014 Notice to Bondholders On 30 December 2013, a Bondholders' meeting voted in favour of the issuance of super senior secured bonds as a short-term bridge loan to be constituted in the form of a SEK 21.000. 000 Norwegian law secured, guaranteed, discounted, super senior bond to be issued by Dannemora Magnetit AB on or before 10 January 2014. We can now confirm that this new super senior bond has been issued and that the first drawdown of SEK 10,000,000 of the short-term bridge loan has been made. The proceeds from the new super senior secured bonds will finance the working capital needs o f Dannemora Mineral AB (publ) and its subsidiaries. Subject to the terms o f the bond loan agreement, the issuer may make a second SEK 5.000. 000 drawdown on 27 January 2014 and a third (and final) drawdown of the remaining loan amount on 3 February 2014.
Yours faithfully Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
