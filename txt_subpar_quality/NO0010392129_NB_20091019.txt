Notice from Bondholders' Meetings
On 19 October 2009 three bondholders' meetings were held pursuant to summons of 9 October 2009 and addendum to summons of 15 October 2009 (together the "Proposed Proposal"), There were sufficient Bondholders present at all three meetings to form a quorum. The Proposed Proposal obtained (i) in respect o f the February Bond, 100 % o f the votes, (ii) in respect o f the June Bond, 100 % o f the votes, and (iii) in respect o f the Convertible Bond, 98.55 % o f the votes. The Proposed Proposal was therefore approved by all three bondholders' meetings.
