Proposed financial restructuring - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 18 March 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 1 March 2010 regarding a financial restructuring of the Borrower. The voting results for the respective bond issues are as follows:
ISIN N0001040032.8 - The 2009 Bond There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
ISIN N0001036650.3 - The 2011 Bond There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.

ISIN N0001054567.6 - The 2012 Bond There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.

Consequently, all three bond issues accepted the Proposed Restructuring as set out in the summons, and the Proposed Restructuring may be implemented upon completion of the conditions precedent.
