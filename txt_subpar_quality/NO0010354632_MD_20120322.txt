Partial Redemption

Norsk Tillitsmann ASA acts as loan trustee for the bond issue "FRN Sea Production Ltd Callable Senior Secured Bond Issue 2007/2012" with ISIN NO 001 035463.2 (the "Loan") where Sea Production Ltd. (the "Company") is the issuer and borrower.
Pursuant to the Proposal (as set out in the summons dated 12 of March 2012) and adopted by the bondholders' meeting on 21 of March 2012, the Company shall apply an amount of USD 37,000,000 in partial redemption of the Loan at par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) on 26 of March 2012. The record date will be 22 of March 2012 (end of business).
