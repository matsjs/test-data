CASE NO. 09-084819KON-NOHO: ABILITY DRILLING ASA, BANKRUPTCY ESTATE - PRELIMINARY DISTRIBUTION NO. 3

The administration of the Estate refers to the previous preliminary distributions, paid 2 May 2011 and 12 December 2011.

TTS has notified the Estate that the company will make an early redemption of the remaining loan with NOK 55,000,000 on 1 June 2012.

The Estate Administrator, in an application to Nordhordland District Court dated 27 April 2012, has sought approval to conduct a further preliminary distribution of the above mentioned bankruptcy estate. The District Court consented to such distribution in its letter dated 2 May 2012.

Through the previous distributions, claims with Class I and Class II priority have received full coverage. The Estate will in this distribution recommend 17% coverage for unsecured claims, thus the unsecured creditors will have received 48% coverage of their claims.

The distribution is subject to the condition that payment from TTS will take place and be received by the Estate on 1 June 2012, as notified by TTS.

As with the previous distributions of funds, those claims that are not yet recommended by the Administration of the Estate as payable will be allocated a dividend.

Bostyret viser til tidligere forhåndsutlodninger til kreditorene, avholdt 2,5.2011 og 12.12.2011.
