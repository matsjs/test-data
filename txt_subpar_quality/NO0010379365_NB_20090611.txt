Oslo, 11 June 2009
Proposal to amend Loan Agreements - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 11 June 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of June 2 2009 regarding a proposal to amend Loan Agreements.
For the FRN Bergen Yards Holding AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2008 with ISIN NO 001039550.2, (the "NOK 400 million Loan") there were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 77.05 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement. The Loan Agreement will therefore be changed to reflect the resolution.
For the FRN Bergen Yards Holding AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010 with ISIN NO 001037936.5, (the "NOK 250 million Loan") there were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 97.39 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement. The Loan Agreement will therefore be changed to reflect the resolution.
