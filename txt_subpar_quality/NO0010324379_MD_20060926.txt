CHANGES OF LOAN AGREEMENT - ADOPTED RESOLUTION
On 22 September 2006 a bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 14 September 2006.
The following resolution was adopted:
1. Changes to the Loan Agreement
(i) The Loan will be increased to a maximum amount ofN O K 385,000,000 (Norwegian krone threehundred eightyfivemillion). (ii) The interest rate on the Loan will be increased to NIBOR plus 4.75 percentage points. (iii) The amount limitfo r the established security will be increased with NOK 85.000. 000 however, fo r the Real Estate Mortgage the increased security will be established on priority after an amount ofN O K 25,000,000 (NOK 35,000,000 at Ranheim) (i.e. third priority). (iv) Additionalfirst priority security in the amount ofNO K 385,000,000 will be established over all the shares in all the plant owning companies. (v) Covenants containing (i) negative pledge, (ii) prohibition on raising additionalfinancial debt and (iii) prohibition on sale o f assets, will be imposed on the plant owning companies.
The Loan Trustee is authorized to enter into an amendment agreement with the Borrower implementing the changes under (i) to (v) above to the Loan Agreement, with effectfrom 28 September 2006.
2. Approval o f acceptancefee
I f the Bondholders meeting votes in favour o f the proposed changes o f the Loan Agreement as set out in 1 above, Bondholders registered as owner o f the Bonds at the date o f the Bondholders meeting will be entitled to an acceptance fee o f 1 per cent o f their holding o f Bods in the Loan (bases on the original NOK 300.000. 000 bond loan). The fe e will be disbursed five Banking Days after the date o f the Bondholders meeting.
