Oslo, 24 M y 2013 Redemption of parts of Bond Issue - USD 862,215 Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the holders o f bonds in the "5 per cent NEC Convertible Callable Bond Issue 2012/2015" with ISIN NO 0010640790 (the "Bond Issue"), where Norse Energy Corp. ASA (the "Issuer") is the issuer. Reference is made to the summons to Bondholders' Meeting dated 10 May 2013 (the "Summons") and the resolutions made by the extraordinary general meeting of the Issuer on 31 May 2013. The execution of the restructuring as proposed in the Summons, and approved by the Bondholders' Meeting, is well underway. As described in clause 2.7 o f the Summons, one of the elements in the restructuring is that any part o f the gross proceeds from the Rights Issue (as defined in the Summons) exceeding USD 1,000,000 shall be used to repay the Bond Issue. Based on subscriptions in the Rights Issue, the amount to be repaid is USD 862,215. The redemption will be carried out at par value and on a pro rata basis.
