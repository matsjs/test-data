Notice from Bondholders' Meeting

On 19 October 2012 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 3 October
2012.

For ISIN NO 001 054844.9 - NOR04 Bond:

There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 97.74 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.

For ISIN NO 001 059230.6 - NOR05 Bond:

There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement.

For ISIN NO 001 060632.0 and NO 001 060633.8 - NOR06/07 Bond:

There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 96.62 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.

Payment of compensation fee

Subject to an approval of the Equity Issue of minimum NOK 400 million by the extraordinary general meeting of the Company 26 October 2012, the Issuer will pay a 1 % (flat) compensation fee on 2 November 2012, with record date end of business today.
