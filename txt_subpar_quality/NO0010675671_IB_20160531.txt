Completion of Recapitalisation
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue.
With reference to the bond summons dated 11 March 2016 and the bondholder resolution made on 18 March 2016, the Bondholders are hereby informed that closing on the transaction occurred on 30 May 2016.
Bonds Accordingly, Bonds in the amount of USD 139,948,481 have been discharged, reducing the outstanding principle amount from USD 180,000,000 to USD 40,051,519. However, as closing occurred on 30 May 2016, rather than 27 May 2016 as contemplated in the documentation, Bonds in the amount of USD 210,000 was issued to the Bondholders on 30 May 2016, increasing the outstanding principle amount to USD 40,261,,519.
Cash Further, USD 989,860 was paid to the Bondholders on 30 May 2016 (being 0.54992 % of the USD 180,000,000 outstanding principle amount before the discharge of Bonds).
Depository receipts As consideration of the discharge of Bonds described above, depository receipts, each representing a share in Sterling Resources Ltd, was issued to the Bondholders on a pro rata basis, being 78.84923 depository receipts per Bond before the discharge of Bonds.
