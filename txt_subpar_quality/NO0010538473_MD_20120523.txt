EXECUTION OF THE RESTRUCTURING Norsk Tillitsmann ASA acts as bond trustee for the bond issues with ISIN NO 001 053847.3 and ISIN NO 001 064285.3 (Original ISIN NO 001 060599.1) (the "Bond Loans") with Blom ASA ("Blom") as Borrower. Reference is made to the Bondholders' meeting for the Bond Loans held 24 April 2012 (the "Bondholders' Meeting"), and to the stock exchange notice published by Blom 23 May 2012.
FOR THE 2009 BOND ISSUE Conversion to shares: The conversion to shares, apply to all bondholders who did not choose the alternative Exchange Offer described below. The bonds will be converted into Blom ASA shares at par plus accrued unpaid interest to 24 April (29 days at 10.28 % p.a). The shares will be delivered post the reverse share split (100:1), and the conversion price is NOK 10. The numbers of shares which will be delivered per NOK 1 of bonds owned are 0.100828. Record Date: End of Business 3 May 2012 Delivery Date of shares: 30 May 2012 The bonds will no longer be registered in VPS as of 24 May 2012, The Exchange Offer: The Exchange Offer applies to those bondholders who actively chose the Exchange Offer. The bonds will be exchanged into the new subordinated convertible bond issue. The exchange ratio is 100:31. The sum of old bonds at par plus accrued unpaid interest to 24 April 2012 times 0.31 is the number of bonds which will be received in the new subordinated convertible bond issue. Delivery date of new subordinated convertible bonds: 24 May 2012
