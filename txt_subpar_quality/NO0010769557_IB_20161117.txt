1.

SUBSCRIPTION FOR LIQUIDITY BONDS

Interested Bondholders may notify their subscription interest for Liquidity Bonds (pursuant to the terms and conditions set out in the Summons and in the Term Sheet) to DNB Markets (the "Receiving Agent"), by way of email to bond.syndicate@dnb.no by sending a request, together with proof of holdings as of the Record Date (being 9 November 2016). Proof of holdings may include transcript of holdings from VPS/prime broker duly legible, or other evidence of holdings in the form of an up to date screen shot or custodian letter. DNB Markets acts solely as receiving agent in respect of the Liquidity Bonds, and shall have no liability in respect of any statements or descriptions contained in the Summons and its appendixes.

Subscription for Liquidity Bonds will end on 21 November 2016, with final allocation expected to be sent out shortly thereafter. Settlement is expected on or about 28 November 2016.

Contact details to DNB Markets for notification of subscription interest for Liquidity Bonds:

Email: bond.syndicate@dnb.no

2. VOTING FOR WRITTEN RESOLUTION
Voting Period: The Bond Trustee must have received all votes necessary in order for the Written Resolution to be passed with the requisite majority before 21 November 2016 at 17:00 (Oslo time) (the "Voting Period").

How to vote: A duly signed Voting Form (as attached to the Summons) with proof of holdings as of the Record Date (being 9 November 2016) attached must be received by the Bond Trustee no later than at the end of the Voting Period and must be submitted by scanned e-mail or telefax. Proof of holdings may include transcript of holdings from VPS/prime broker duly legible, or other evidence of holdings in the form of an up to date screen shot or custodian letter.
