Partial repayment Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM" or the "Trustee" or the "Bond Trustee") acts as trustee for the holders of bonds (the "Bondholders") in the loan agreement dated 30 May 2007 with ISIN NO 001 036763.4 described in the loan agreement "11.625 per cent SeaMetric International AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012" (the "Bond Agreement") where SeaMetric International AS is the issuer (the "Borrower", the "Company" or "SeaM etric"), and whereby the Bondholders have made available a loan in the maximum aggregate amount of USD 105,000,000 (the "Loan"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the Bond Agreement, unless otherwise stated. SeaMetric filed for bankruptcy on 13 January 2010, and the liquidation proceedings has now resulted in a preliminary final distribution from SeaMetric estate in bankruptcy. On 26 January 2012, a total of NOK 16 000 000 was distributed among all creditors with unsecured claims. The Bond Trustee received an amount of NOK 14,033,334.55 on behalf of the Bondholders. After deducting fees and cost, the Bond Trustee will carry out a partial distribution (via VPS) in the abovementioned Loan on 8 of February 2012. The distribution will correspond to 4.30095 % (flat) of face value. The record date for the distribution will be 3 of February 2012. The funds do not cover the entire Loan including interest and expenses. Accordingly, the Bondholders will have an uncovered claim against the company. Any surplus of the retained funds and any additionally recovery of the Loan will be distributed later.
