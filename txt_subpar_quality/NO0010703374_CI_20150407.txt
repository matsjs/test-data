METRO EXPLORATION HOLDING CORP.
Report Q3 2014
Business Activities Metro Exploration Holding Corporation (the "Company"), incorporated in Liberia on January 4, 2008. The Company is privately owned. The main activities of the Company are to hold participations in companies which operate in the drillship sector. The Company as of 30 September 2014 holds investment in Deep Sea Metro Ltd, a company incorporated in Bermuda in 2008. The Company owns sixty percent (60%) of Deep Sea Metro. Deep Sea Metro is the parent company of a group that owns and operates ultradeepwater drill ships. As at September 30, 2014 the group owns and operates two ultradeepwater drill ships
Ownership Metro Exploration Holding Corporation (the "Company"), incorporated in Liberia on January 4, 2008. The Company is privately owned. As of 30 September 2014 the authorized share capital was 500 registered shares without par value and the paid-in capital was USD 412,724,146.-
Financial Information The financial statements have been prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards ("IFRS") under the historical cost convention. The operating profit in Q3 2014 was USD 2.2 million compared to USD 0.8 million profit in H1 2014. An increase of USD 1.4 million which is mainly due to the profit in associates of USD 2.8 million for the H1 2014 The net loss in Q3 2014 was USD 2.9 million compared to USD 6.2 million for H1 As of 30 September2014 the net participation to Deep Sea Metro was USD 535.8 million compared to USD 529.3 million as of 30 June 2014. On the 14 February 2014 the company issued a senior secured callable bond of USD 186 million, for a period of two years maturing on 14 February 2016. The interest payments shall be made quarterly in arrears on the interest payment dates each year. The interest rates are a) from 14 February 2014 to 14 August 2014 at a fixed rate of 8% p.a., b) from 15 August 2014 to 14 February 2015 at a fixed rate of 10% p.a. , c) from 15 February 2015 to 14 August 2015 at a fixed rate of 11.25% p.a. , d) from 15 August 2015 to 14 February 2016 at a fixed

The bond proceeds were used USD 49.5 million as equity into Deep Sea Metro , USD 91 million as capital return to shareholders , USD 7.3 million for bond issuance fees and expenses , USD 16.7 million pledged into the interest reserve account to cover the first year interest payments (currently USD 9.3 million as the two first coupons of USD 3.7million each have pain paid on 14 May 2014 and 14 Aug. 2014 respectively), USD 12 million as additional equity into Deep Sea Metro and the balance was remaining for general corporate purposes.

The Cash and cash equivalents at 30 September 2014 were USD 0.8 million. An amount of USD 9.3 million is classified as restricted cash, this amount is deposited into an non-interest reserve account and will be used to cover 6 months interest due to bond holders. The bond issuance cost (fees & expenses) was USD 7.3 million which will be amortized within the next two years. (up to 14 February 2016 ) . On 30 September 2014 the net of amortization amount being USD 5.0 million is classified as deferred charges. On 30 September 2014 the payables trade & others was USD 2.3 million which is consisting of USD 2.3 million interest accrued for the period 14 August to 30 September 2014. On 30 September 2014 the total equity was USD 357.9 million . On the 30 September 2014 the retained loss was USD 54.8 million , where the USD 40.6 was the impact of the participation into Deep Sea Metro , USD 9.8 million was the bond interest cost, USD 2.3 million the amortization of the bond fees & expenses and the rest of USD 2.1 million was the company's accumulated operating loss.

These financial statements should be read together with the annual financial statements for 2013 which include a full description of the account policies. These policies have been consistently applied to all the years presented, unless otherwise stated.
