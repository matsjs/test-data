Sea Production
Sea Production Ltd. Financial Information Third Quarter 2013
Background
This financial information has been prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards.
General Information
Sea Production Ltd. and its subsidiaries own and operate two floating, production, storage and off loading ("FPSO") vessels.
Financial Statements
Income Statement (Third quarter 2012 figures in brackets)
The Group's revenue in the quarter was US$4.7 million (US$2.9 million), with EBITDA of US$3.2 million (US$0.1 million negative).
All charter revenues relate to Crystal Ocean. Front Puffin was laid-up.
Operating expenses for the quarter amounted to US$0.7 million (US$2.3 million) and administrative expenses for the period were US$0.8 million (US$0.7 million).
Total depreciation for the quarter was US$3.8 million (US$3.7 million). The Group's vessels are depreciated on a straight-line basis over their economically useful lives, taking into account their estimated residual value. Assessment of the remaining economic life of the assets is done on a yearly basis. Capital costs incurred in relation to a specific contract are depreciated over the expected life of the relevant contract. At the end of the contract any remaining balance is written-off.
Net financing charges for the period were US$2.6 million (US$3.0 million). Interest expense on the outstanding US$ bonds was US$2.6 million (US$3.1 million).
The net result for the quarter was a loss of US$3.7 million (loss of US$2.8 million).
Balance Sheet
Current assets as at 30 September 2013 were US$4.9 million (US$4.1 million) and non-current assets were US$153.7 million (US$167.7 million). The Company has a 5 year callable bond loan with a nominal value of US$76.4 million outstanding as at 30 September 2013 and a final maturity of 14 February 2014. The Company had no other borrowings at the end of the quarter. The Company's equity as at 30 September 2013 was US$60.1 million (US$69.6 million).

Group Update Third Quarter 2013 Crystal Ocean continued its charter with Blue Marine. The charter period is three years' firm, with extension options of up to a further two years. Front Puffin has been laid-up pending the securing of a new contract. Board of Directors, November 2013
