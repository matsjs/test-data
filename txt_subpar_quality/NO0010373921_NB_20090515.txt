Proposal to postpone interest, reduce Equity Ratio and accept De-merger Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 15 May 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 7 May 2009 regarding Proposal to postpone interest, reduce Equity Ratio and accept De-merger. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this bondholders' meeting obtained 96.30 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will be changed to reflect the resolution.
