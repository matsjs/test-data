Proposal to new repayment schedule and exercise of call option - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 November 2007 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 9 November 2007 regarding Proposal to new repayment schedule and exercise of call option.
There where sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form at quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 94.62 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
"The repayment o f the entire Loan/Bond Issue (with accrued interest/overdue interest) shall be postponed from 1 November 2007 until the Refinancing (as described in the summons for this bondholders ' meeting) is available to the Borrower/Company), but no later than 14 December 2007. The entire Loan/Bond Issue shall be redeemed at 108 % of par value plus interest/overdue interest. The Borrower/Company shall pay interest and overdue interest in accordance with the Loan Agreement until repayment is completed. Interest shall be calculated on an Actual/360 day countfraction.
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is given power o f attorney to enter into the necessary agreements in connection with decisions made on the bondholders ' meeting as well as to carry out the necessary completion work, including make other necessary amendments o f the Loan Agreement. ''
The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting be request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
