2. The Proposal:
The proposed restructuring consist of the elements described in this paragraph (the "Proposed Restructuring").
In brief, the Borrower proposes to convert the Bond Issues in their entirety, including accrued interest and amortization, to shares in the Borrower. Each of the Bond Issues will be converted at a different conversion price to reflect the position of the respective Bonds.
The conversion of debt to equity will be made to accommodate a new equity issue, to improve the liquidity situation of the Borrower.
As part of the restructuring the Borrower shall carry out an equity issue of minimum NOK 30 000 000 at a subscription price of NOK 0.10 per share (the "New Equity Issue").
Shares resulting from the conversion of the Bond Issues and shares delivered as a result of the Equity Issue shall be delivered simultaneously to the Bondholders and the subscribers in the New Equity Issue.
2.1. The 2009 Bond: Upon fulfilment of the Conditions Precedent (defined below) the Bondholders accept the following.
For the 2009 Bond, the entire outstanding principal amount of NOK 50 000 000 shall be converted to shares in the Borrower at NOK 0.105. Shares for the conversion of principal shall be delivered to bondholders based on their holdings on record at the time of conversion.
The Interest amount of NOK 3 250 000 that was due 10 June 2010, but not paid, shall be converted to shares in the Borrower at NOK 0.10, by delivery of shares to bondholders on record as of the ex-coupon date for that interest payment.
Interest accrued after 10 June 2010 shall be written off without any compensation.

3. Power of Attorney
The Bondholders irrevocably grant the Loan Trustee a Power of Attorney, on their behalf, upon satisfaction of the Conditions Precedent (where applicable), to implement the elements of the Proposed Restructuring, hereunder to subscribe for shares in connection with the conversion of the Bond Issues. The Loan Trustee is granted power of attorney to make any amendments of the Loan Agreements, and may on behalf of the Bondholders enter into any agreements necessary to accomplish the Proposed Restructuring. The Loan Trustee is also granted the authority, on behalf of the Bondholders, and at the sole discretion of the Loan Trustee, to agree to any minor adjustments of the Proposed Restructuring and the Conditions Precedent needed to facilitate the implementation of the Proposed Restructuring, hereunder to accept delays in /amend the time-schedule of the implementation.
4. Conditions Precedent
The agreement of the Bondholders to the Proposed Restructuring as outlined herein, is conditional upon fulfilment of the following (the "Conditions Precedent"):
a) Bondholder Meetings in each of the 2009 Bond, the 2011 Bond and the 2012 Bond has approved the Proposed Restructuring; and
b) The Borrower has obtained all the necessary corporate resolutions required to issue the number of new shares needed for the Proposed Restructuring, hereunder the conversion of the Bond Issues and the New Equity Issue; and
c) The New Equity Issue of minimum NOK 30 000 000 at an issue price of NOK 0.10, is subscribed for and paid-in of which Waterford has committed to subscribe for minimum USD 3 000 000 equivalent; and
d) No new information is disclosed before the implementation of the Proposed Restructuring resulting in a material adverse change in the premises on which the Proposed Restructuring is based upon; and
e) Any Legal Opinion the Loan Trustee may reasonable request related to the restructuring to be submitted by the Borrower; and
f) The Proposed Restructuring is in its entirety implemented on or about 20 August 2010 (Drop Dead Date).
5. Bondholders' meeting:
The request is put forward to the Bondholders without further evaluation or recommendations from the Loan Trustee. The Bondholders must independently evaluate whether the proposed changes are acceptable.
