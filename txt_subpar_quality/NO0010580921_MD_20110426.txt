Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") is appointed as loan trustee for the holders of the bonds in the above mentioned loan with ISIN NO 001 058092.1 (the "Loan") issued by Remedial Cayman Limited (the "Company").
The Company has today given notice to NT that the Company will redeem USD 4,604,625 of the Loan at par value (100 %) plus accrued interest as soon as possible. NT has agreed to waive the required call notice period of 30 Business Days.
The Record Date is end of business 26 April 2011. The Payment Date will be 28 April 2011.
The principle amount which will be redeemed is USD 4,604,625 and the accrued interest to be paid is USD 395,375 in total a payment of USD 5,000,000.
