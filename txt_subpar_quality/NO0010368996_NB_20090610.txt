Proposal to authorise payment of Bingham's fees as legal advisor, AMA Capital Partners' fees as financial advisor and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA's fees as loan trustee - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 10 June 2009 a Bondholders' meeting in the above mentions bond issues (the "Loans") was held pursuant to summons of 28 May 2009 regarding a proposal to authorise payment of Bingham's fees as legal advisor, AMA Capital Partners' fees as financial advisor and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA's fees as loan trustee.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum for both Bond Loans. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this bondholders meeting obtained (i) in respect of the 10.85 Bond Issue, 87.65 % of the votes, and (ii) in respect of the FRN Bond Issue, 88.30 % of the votes. The proposal was therefore adopted by both Bond Issue.
