Proposal to amend Bond Loan Agreements - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 11 March 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 25 February 2009 regarding a proposal of certain amendments of the Bond Loan Agreements o f the above mentions bond issues (the "Bond Loans"). There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum for both Bond Loans. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this bondholders meeting obtained (i) in respect o f the NOKm 2,000 Bond Issue, 90.49 % o f the votes, and (ii) in respect of the USDm 300 Bond Issue, 98.16 % o f the votes (the "Resolution"). Subject to the conditions set out in the summons, the Bond Loan Agreements will be changed to reflect to Resolution adopted in today's Bondholders' meetings.
