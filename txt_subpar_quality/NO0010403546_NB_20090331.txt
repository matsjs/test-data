Proposal to amend Bond Loan Agreements - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 31 March 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 23 March 2009 regarding a proposal o f certain amendments o f the Bond Loan Agreements of the above mentions bond issues (the "Bond Loans"). There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum for both Bond Loans. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this bondholders meeting obtained (i) in respect o f the NOKm 400 Bond Issue, 98,46 % o f the votes, and (ii) in respect o f the NOKm 165 Bond Issue, 96,18 % o f the votes (the "Resolution").
Subject to the conditions set out in the summons, the Bond Loan Agreements will be changed to reflect to Resolution adopted in today's Bondholders' meetings.
