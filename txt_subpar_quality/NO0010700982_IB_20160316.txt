ORO NEGRO SIGNS CONTRACT EXTENSION WITH PEMEX
Mexico City, Mexico, March 15, 2016. Perforadora Oro Negro, S. de R.L. de C.V. ("Oro Negro") and Pemex Exploration y Production have signed an amendment to the outstanding charter lease for Oro Negro's Decus rig. The amendment is effective as of October 26, 2015 and provides a one year extension of the term of the Decus contract.
