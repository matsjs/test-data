Rescheduling of Interest payment and installment - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 8 June 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons dated 28 May 2010. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 47.3 % of the votes, which, according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement, was not sufficient for adoption. The Bond Agreement will remain unchanged. However, based on the number of votes in favour of the proposal and in concurrence with the Bondholders present at the meeting, the Loan Trustee has extended the grace period.
