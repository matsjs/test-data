Early redemption - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On, 12 November 2010 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 4 November 2010, regarding a proposed early redemption of the Loan.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 89.52% o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Loan Agreement. The Loan Agreement will therefore, subject to the conditions set out in the resolution, be amended to reflect the resolution as set out in the summons to this Bondholders' meeting
