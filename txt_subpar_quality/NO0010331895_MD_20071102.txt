Repayment of the entire loan under the Bond Issue - status
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where Neptune Marine Invest AS (the "Company") is the issuer. The Bond Issue is encumbered by the Company's two drillships "Neptune Explorer" and "Neptune Discoverer" (the "Drillships"). Pursuant to an Amendment Agreement that came into force on 10 October 2007, the entire loan under the Bond Issue with all interest and fees fell due on 1 November 2007.
The Company has for a period of time negotiated the terms for refinancing of i.a. the Bond Issue (the "Refinancing") and a final Commitment Letter with respect to the Refinancing has today been signed with the lenders under the refinancing. The Company has advised that only a few conditions precedent are currently outstanding. All conditions for the closing of the Refinancing are expected to be settled early next week with redemption of the Bond Issue to take place within the grace period pursuant to the bond agreement clause 15.1 (a) and not later than November 8th 2007. If redemption has not taken place within this period, or it is become clear that the Refinancing will not be completed within this period, the Loan Trustee will initiate the recovery procedure of the Bond Issue.
