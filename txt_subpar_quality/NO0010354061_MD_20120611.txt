Notice to Bondholders - Preliminary distributions
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") acts as loan trustee for the loan mentioned above (the "Loan") made available under a loan agreement dated 7 February 2007 (the "Loan Agreement") between Ability Drilling ASA (Company No 989 761 846) as borrower (the "Company" or the "Borrower") and NTM. The bankruptcy of the Borrower was initiated subsequent to a bankruptcy petition received by Nordhordland District Court on 27 May 2009 (the "Filing Date"). A partial re-distribution (the "Partial Distribution") will be carried out on 13 June 2012 to the holders of claims under the Loan Agreement. The Partial Distribution equals 24.59809 % of the claims as of the Filing Date (e.g. including any relevant premium, unpaid interest and any overdue interest), which equals:
(1) in respect of the unpaid interest of 13 May 2009, 24.79275 % (flat) of the due, unpaid interest amount; and
(2) in respect of the principal amount (i.e. the Bonds), 27.30338 % (flat) of the face value o f the Bonds.
The record date is 11 June 2012 (End of Business).
