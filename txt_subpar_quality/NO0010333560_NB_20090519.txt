Proposal to ratify the appointment of certain advisers and to sanction the future appointment of certain advisors - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 May 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 8 May 2009 regarding Proposal to ratify the appointment of certain advisers and to sanction the future appointment of certain advisors in connection with possible future enforcement of security and to facilitate any ongoing negotiations with Thule Drilling ASA.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution as set out in the summons to this Bondholders' meeting obtained 97.64 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Loan Agreement.
