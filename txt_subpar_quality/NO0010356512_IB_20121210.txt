SUMMONS FOR BONDHOLDER INFORMATION MEETING

1. Background

We refer to the above mentioned loan (the "Bond" or the "Bond Issue") where American Shipping Company ASA (formerly Aker American Shipping Company ASA) is the Borrower, hereinafter referred to as "AMSC", the "Company" or the "Borrower". On 27 September 2011, the Maturity Date of the Bond Issue was extended by six years, from 28 February 2012 to 28 February 2018.

All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the bond agreement for the Bonds (the "Bond Agreement") between the Company and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (the "Trustee") dated 23 February 2007.

The Company will, upon request from a bondholder, provide information in respect of the fact that Overseas Shipholding Group ("OSG") has recently filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code.
