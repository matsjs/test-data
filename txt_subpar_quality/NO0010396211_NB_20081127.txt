Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 27 November 2008 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 14 November 2008 regarding amendment of Loan Agreement. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
The Bondholders approve postponing the interest payment due on 13 November 2008 to 18 December 2008, and thereafter the Loan Trustee is given power o f attorney to extend the waiver period on weekly basis in its own discretion, acting in the best interests o f the Bondholders until 4 January 2008. Compound interest shall be calculated on the Remaining Loan and the interest payment amount which was due 13 November 2008 with 17per centp.a from and including the due date 13 November 2008 until the date paym ent is made.
The Bondholders may obtain a copy o f the minutes from the meeting by request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
