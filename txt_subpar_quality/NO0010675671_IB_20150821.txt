Payment of Amendment Fee
In accordance with resolution on a Bondholder's Meeting held on 8 May 2015, an Amendment Fee was paid to the Bondholders in accordance with the Amendment and Restatement Agreement No. 2, Clause 3.2 litra b) (the "Amendment Fee"), without any reduction of the principal amount. The Amendment Fee was an amount of USD 750,000, being 0.37037% of the face value of the Outstanding Bonds and was paid to the Bondholders on a pro rata basis. The record date was 14 August 2015, and the payment date was 21 August 2015.
