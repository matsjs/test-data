Oslo, 16 December 2016 Exercise of Par Call Option Nordic Trustee ASA acts as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the bondholders (the "Bondholders") in the above mentioned bond issue where Petrolia SE is the issuer (the "Issuer"). Capitalised terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Bond Agreement dated 29 January 2016 and the Amendment Agreement dated 8 December 2016 (the "Bond Agreement"). The Issuer has in accordance with a letter to the Bond Trustee dated 13 December 2016, notified that the Issuer will exercise the Par Call Option pursuant to Clause 10.2 of the Bond Agreement. The Issuer will call 75% of the Net Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value - plus accrued interest. The principal amount which is called will be settled with shares in the Issuer, where the price for each share shall be USD 0.5. Interest due on 21 January 2017 shall be settled with cash 23 January 2017.
