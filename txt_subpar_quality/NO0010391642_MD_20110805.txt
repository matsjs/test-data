CORRECTION - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Reference is made to the Notice from Bondholders' meeting dated 5 August 2011 which were posted on www.stamdata.no shortly after the meeting. The percentage of votes obtained in some of the loans were misquoted in the Notice. The correct percentages of votes obtained were: For ISIN NO 001 039164.2- Voyager Bonds There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 98.16 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. For ISIN NO 001 036696.6 - Piranema Bonds There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 99.51 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. For NO 001 059362.7 - Unsecured Bonds There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
