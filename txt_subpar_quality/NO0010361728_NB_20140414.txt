Oslo, 14 April 2014
Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Today a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to the summons of 7 April 2014. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 97.54 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as set out in the summons to the meeting. For sake of good order, please note that the Issuer introduced the following new event of default clause to be part o f the proposal to the Bondholders: "15.1(g): if, in respect of FPSO #1 or FPSO#2, any member o f the Group enters into a new contract for its employment, or enters into any contract(s) for any capital expenditure or structural change(s) with an aggregate value in excess of US$5 million, without the approval of a Bondholders' meeting" As the proposal has been approved by the Bondholders, the following terms will apply to the Bond Issue: (i) the Maturity Date is reset to 16 June 2014, (ii) for the Interest Period from and including 16 April 2014 the total Margin will be increased by 2% p.a. to 28% p.a., implying that the Coupon will be LIBOR + 28% p.a., (iii) on the new Maturity Date, a Restructuring Fee of USD 1,418,481.82 (i.e. 1% flat o f the outstanding amount of bonds at the restructuring in 2012) will be payable to those Bondholders who own the bonds on the new Maturity Date.
