Confirmation hearing on Joint Plan of Reorganization
Background Norsk Tillitsmann ASA acts as loan trustee for the above mentioned bond loans with ISIN N0001033158.0 and ISIN N0001042986.3 where MPF Corp. Ltd is the borrower. Jointly the two loans are referred to as (the "Loans"). Reference is made to the letter to the bondholders in the Loans dated 14 May 2010 regarding the Joint Plan of Reorganization (the "Plan"). The hearing for the Plan was held on 9 June 2010 and the Plan has been confirmed. The Litigation Trust, created by the Plan for the purpose of pursuing any claims on behalf of holders of General Unsecured Claims (including the Loans), was increased from $50,000 to $125,000. The only recovery that General Unsecured Creditors will realize under the Plan is from any potentially successful prosecution of such claims.
