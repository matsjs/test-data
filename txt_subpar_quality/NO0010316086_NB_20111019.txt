Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 19 October 2011 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 11 October 2011 regarding instructions to the Bond Trustee to pay the aggregate amount of USD 5,000,000 from 9.75% Bond recoveries to the 10.85% Bondholders. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement.
