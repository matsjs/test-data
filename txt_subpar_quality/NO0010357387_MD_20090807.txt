Postponement of interest payment
Reference is made to the resolution made at bondholder meetings in the two above captioned bond issues. The bondholders authorized the Bond Trustee, upon fulfilment of certain conditions, to defer interest payments in the bond issues for a certain period of time.
The trustee has received acceptances from more than 50% of the bondholders in each of the bond issues, and a statement that the Banks prolong the standstill period. On this basis the Bond Trustee hereby make the following deferrals of interest payments:
N0001035738.7: The interest payment originally due 7 June 2009 is deferred from 7 August 2009, until 21 August 2009, on such terms as set out in the resolution.
N0001037520.7: The interest payment originally due 25 June 2009 is deferred from 11 August 2009, until 25 August 2009, on such terms as set out in the resolution.
