Notice of partial repayment of due, unpaid claim
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Loan") issued by PetroProd Ltd (the "Borrower"). All capitalized terms used but not defined herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the loan agreement for the Loan (the "Loan Agreement"). On the 8 of April 2009 the Loan Trustee declared an Event of Default under the Loan Agreement. The Borrower is currently in provisional liquidation, and Simon Whicker and Kris Beighton have been appointed joint provisional liquidators ("JPLs"). In accordance with the resolution passed on a Bondholders' meeting, the CJ70 Contract has been terminated with a USD 8 million cash settlement (the "Payment"). The JPLs have earlier given its permission for the Loan Trustee to exercise its pledge over the funds in the Escrow Account (the "Escrow Moines"). After deduction cost, fees and expenses of the Loan Trustee and its advisors, USD 18,511,575.98 will now be distributed to the Bondholders.
The Record Date will be 25 of November 2009 (End of Business) with Settlement date (payment date) on 30 of November 2009.
