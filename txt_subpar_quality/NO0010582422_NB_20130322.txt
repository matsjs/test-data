Information to Bondholders - CALL
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as bond trustee (the "Bond Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Loan") where Bergen Group ASA is the Borrower. Reference is made to the notice from the Borrower dated 4 March 2013 regarding exercise of Call Option. The Bond Issue will be repaid as follows: Record Date: End of business 25 March 2013 Payment Date: 27 March 2013 Call price: 102 % of par plus accrued interest Due to technical limitations during the ex-coupon period, payment of the principal at 102 % of par will be made on 27 March 2013 and payment of interest will be made on 9 April 2013. Interest will be aggregated for the full interest period until 9 April - and as a consequence of the call, an amount corresponding to 13 days of interest will therefore be deducted from the payment of principal and the 2 % premium made on 27 March 2013.
