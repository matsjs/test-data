Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 9 February 2012 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 31 January 2012 regarding a proposed amendment of the Loan Agreement. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 38.83 % o f the votes, which, according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement, was not sufficient for adoption. The Bond Agreement will remain unchanged.
