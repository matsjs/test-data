NOTICE FROM BONDHOLDERS MEETING - PROPOSED APPROVAL IN RESPECT OF FINANCIAL COVENANT AND PAYMENT-IN-KIND

On 25 February 2008 at 10:00 hours, a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 17 February 2009.

After the date of the Summons, the Borrower has decided to offer compensation through a one-time fee of 0.10 per cent of the par value of the Bonds to the Bondholders for the proposed changes, payable if the proposal was accepted at the Bondholders' meeting.

There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 83.01% of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:

(1) The Bondholders approve that for the purpose o f computing Equity in respect o f Clause 13.2 (a), the Borrower shall disregardfrom such computation, the effect o f any non-cash gain or loss from the change in fair market value o f any interest rate hedging arrangements entered into and maintained by the Borrower, or any consolidated subsidiary, from time to time.

(2) The Bondholders approve that the Borrower at its sole discretion make any o f the interest payments until the Maturity Date as additional Bonds (Payment-in-Kind).

(3) As compensation to the Bondholders, the Borrower shall pay a one-time fee of 0.10 per

cent o f par value o f the Bonds. The fee is conditional upon the Bondholders meeting

accepting the proposal. The fee shall payable on or about 11 March 2009, and shall be

distributed to the Bondholders based on their holdings as per the date o f the Bondholders
