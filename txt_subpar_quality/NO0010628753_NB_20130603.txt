Oslo, 3 June 2013 Notice from Bondholders' Meeting On 3 June 2013 Bondholders' Meetings were held pursuant to summons of 16 May 2013. There were sufficient Bondholders present at both meetings to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes in both Bond Issues. The proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement for both Bonds. Pursuant to the adopted resolution the Issuer shall pay to the Bondholders a consent fee equal to 1.375 per cent flat on the face value of the Outstanding Bonds in each Bond Issue (the "Consent Fee"). The Consent Fee is payable on 10 June 2013. The record date of the payment shall be 4 June 2013.
