Fee payment following the restructuring
On 29 September 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 21 September 2009 (the "Summons") regarding a proposed restructuring. On 27 September 2009 Eitzen Chemical ASA (the "Borrower") added some elements to the Proposal included in the Summons. One of the elements included in the addition to the proposal was an up-front fee equal to 0.75 %. The proposal was adopted by the Bondholders' meeting on 29 September 2009, and the conditions, to which the Bondholders' acceptance was subject, have been raised within the deadline which was 30 November 2009. Payment of the 0.75 % fee The payment date will be 4 December 2009 with record date 1 December 2009.
