PEMEX ISSUES CERTIFICATE OF ACCEPTANCE FOR IMPETUS
Mexico City, Mexico, June 1, 2016. On May 29, 2016 Perforadora Oro Negro, S. de R.L. de C.V. ("Oro Negro") completed the checklist for its Impetus rig and, consequently, Pemex Perforation y Servicios, EPS issued the certificate of acceptance for said rig to begin operations.
