NSA BONDCO LIMITED: BUY BACK OFFER TO BONDHOLDERS IN THE BOND ISSUE ISIN NO 001 0744246 COMPLETION OF BOND BUY BACK
28 October 2015
To the bondholders in:
ISIN: NO 001 0744246 - 12 per cent. NSA Bondco Limited Secured Callable Bond Issue 2015/2020 (the "Bonds")
Reference is made to the Buy-Back Offer made by NSA Bondco Limited (the "Company") to Bondholders in its announcement of 22 September 2015 (the "Announcement") and subsequent amendment notification of 28 September 2015.
The Buy-Back Offer process was completed through the Manager, Arctic Securities. A total face value of $36,359,150 of Bonds was tendered and accepted at a weighted average price of 54.71% of face value.
