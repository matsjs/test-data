Maturity Extension of the Bonds
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") acts as trustee for the holders o f the bonds in the above mentioned issue (the "Bonds") with ISIN NOOOl 070031.3 issued by Dannemora Magnetit AB (the "Company").
On 25 February 2014, a meeting of the Bondholders approved the proposals contained in the Bondholder Meeting summons dated 18 February 2014. As a result, NTM is empowered to agree (on behalf of the Bondholders) to implement further extensions to the maturity date of the Bonds at its reasonable discretion (in consultation with its advisers).
In the context of continuing discussions and negotiations with the Company regarding a potential restructuring of its capital structure, NTM, having consulted its advisers, has exercised its discretion referred to above and agreed to extend the maturity date of the Bonds to 21 March 2014.
In consideration for the extension of the maturity date, the Company has agreed to increase the redemption premium applicable to the Bonds from 115% to 120%.
