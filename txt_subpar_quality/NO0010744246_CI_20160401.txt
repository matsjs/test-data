To: Holders of the 12% secured callable bonds due 2020 (ISIN NO 001 0744246) (the "Bonds") issued by NSA Bondco Limited (the "Bondholders")
From: NSA Bondco Limited (the "Issuer")
Date: 1 April 2016
The Issuer refers to the bareboat charter between Norshore Management AS (the "Charterer") and the Issuer regarding the vessel known as "Norshore Atlantic" dated 31 August 2015 (the "Charterparty"). Terms not otherwise defined in this release shall have the same meaning given to them in the Charterparty.
The Issuer would like to inform the Bondholders that:
1) the Issuer received notice from Norshore on 19 February 2016 that the Vessel is moored along wharf #10 at Johor Port (Pasir Gudang) in Malaysia;
2) the Vessel remains to be off contract;
3) the Issuer has not received any payment from the Charterer in respect of the January 2016 and February 2016 Charter Hire, which was due for payment on 10 February 2016 and 10 March 2016, respectively;
4) further to the notice issued by the Issuer on 15 January 2016, the November 2015 and December 2015 Charter Hire, which was due for payment on 10 December 2015 and 10 January 2016, respectively, remains outstanding; and
5) the Issuer has sent a reservation of right letter to the Charterer in respect of all the payment defaults under the Charterparty.
