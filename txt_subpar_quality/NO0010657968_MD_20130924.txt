Adjustment of Exchange Price
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") acts as loan trustee for the above-captioned bond issue in which Siem Industries Inc. is the borrower (the "Borrower"). The Bonds are exchangeable into shares in Subsea 7 S.A. ("Subsea 7"). The terms and conditions of the loan are set out in a bond agreement between the Borrower and NT dated 10 September 2012 (the "Bond Agreement"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Bond Agreement or in this letter.
Subsea 7 paid a USD0.60 cash dividend per share on 12 July 2013. Subsea 7 announced that the shares of Subsea 7 traded ex-dividend as of 3 July 2013. As a result of the dividend payment, the Exchange Price is adjusted accordingly. The new Exchange Price is USD28.29, effective on 3 July 2013 which was the first date that the shares o f Subsea 7 traded ex-dividend.
