Proposal in connection with a voluntary offer to convert NOK tranche to USD tranche - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 18 February 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to a summons dated 9 February 2009.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 98,66% of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement, The Bond Agreement will therefore be amended as follows;
1) The nominal value o f the Bonds in the USD Tranche shall be changed to USD 1 byperforming a split o f each o f the issued Bonds in the USD Tranche with face value o f USD 100,000 into 100,000 Bonds withface value o f USD 1 each. The voting rights o f the USD Tranche Bonds shall be adjusted accordingly based on the exchange rate between USD and NOK on the exchange date.
2) The USD Tranche shall be increased with the aggregate nominal amount of the Bonds in the NOK Tranche that are tendered to the Voluntary Exchange Offer (as described in the summons to this Bondholders 'meeting), and the NOK Tranche shall be decreased correspondingly.
3) The covenant restricting the Borrower to assume additional Financial Indebtedness shall be waived in connection with the Voluntary Exchange Offer o f the Loan and the NOK Convertible (as described in the summons to this BondholdersJ meeting).
4) The margin o f the USD Tranche shallfrom and including 30 March 2009 be increased by 0.50 percentfrom 5.00 %p.a. to 5.50% p.a.
