Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 27 June 2011 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 17 June 2011 regarding change of Borrower. For ISIN NO 001 031750.6 (NEC02): It was a repeated Bondholders' meeting with no minimum bondholder presence required to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 97.51 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed inter alia by changing the Borrower.
For ISIN NO 001 0481542 (NEC03): There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed inter alia by changing the Borrower.
For ISIN NO 001 046035.5 (NEC04 RET): It was a repeated Bondholders' meeting with no minimum bondholder presence required to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed inter alia by changing the Borrower.
