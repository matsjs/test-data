This agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") is dated on January 26th, 2018 between Brødrene Greger AS (the "Issuer") and Nordic Trustee AS (the "Bond Trustee"). 1. The Scope of the Amendment Agreement
The Amendment Agreement comprises amendments of the Bond Agreement originally entered on the 30 September 2014 and amended in amendment agreement entered on the 26th October 2017 in "8 per cent Brødrene Greger AS Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019" - ISIN 001 0715246, based on resolution adopted at the Bondholders' meeting dated 7 December 2017. 2. Amendment to the Bond Agreement The Bond Agreement is hereby amended as follows: (i) The definition of "Security Interests" shall read:
a) the Escrow Account Pledge; b) each Mortgage; c) each Charge over Machinery and Plant; d) the Issuer Share Pledge; and e) the Subsidiary Share Pledge.
The definition of "Charge over Inventory" and "Factoring Agreement" shall be deleted from the Bond Agreement. 3. Confirmation of the Bond Agreement All other provisions of the Bond Agreement remain in full force. 4. Governing Law Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Amendment Agreement which are not resolved amicably shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law in the Oslo City Court. 5. Commencement The amendments to the Bond Agreement will commence at the date of this amendment agreement.
