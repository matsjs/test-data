Notice from Bondholders' Meeting Today a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to the summons dated 29 January 2015. At the meeting 49.71 % of the outstanding bonds were present. To the Bondholders information, the proposed resolution obtained 53.71% of the votes, which was not sufficient to meet the requirement of 2/3 of the Bonds present voting in favour of the resolution.
