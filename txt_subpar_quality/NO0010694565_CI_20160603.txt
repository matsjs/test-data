PRESS RELEASE
EXTENSION OF CAA FOR WORLD SAPPHIRE
Reference is made to the notices dated 4 March and 25 May 2016, where World Wide Supply AS (the "Company") informed about the blocking situation for the the Company's two remaining vessels on charter to Petrobras. Petrobras made an application to the Agencia National de Transportes Aquaviarios ("ANTAQ") to be allowed to keep the vessels on charter for additional 60 days each in spite of the vessels being blocked. It was informed 25 May 2016 that the Company has been advised that Petrobras has received approval by ANTAQ in respect of World Emerald for a 60 days extension. The Brazilian Certificate of Chartering Authorisation ("CAA") of the vessel World Emerald would originally expire on 21 June, but received an approval for 60 days extension, and the CAA of the vessel World Sapphire will expire on 10 August. The Company has now been advised that Petrobras also has received approval by ANTAQ in respect of World Sapphire for a 60 days extension. Further extensions cannot be applied for.
