Notice to the Bondholders
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee") for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Loan") where PA Resources Norway AS is the borrower (the "Borrower").
All capitalized terms used herein and not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned thereto in the agreement for the Loan (the "Loan Agreement").
Reference is made to the announced transaction (the "Transaction") between PA Resources AB (the "Seller") and Bayemgas Norge AS (the "Buyer") to sell PA Resources Norway AS (the Borrower) including all assets. The transaction is now formally closed.
In connection with the Transaction, NOK 61 million o f the bonds has been discharged. The current Remaining Loan is as o f today NOK 269 million.
Further, the Borrower has exercised its Call Option, and the entire Remaining Loan will be repaid at 104 % of par (plus accrued interest) on the Interest Payment Date in June 2009 (the Call Date).
Furthermore, in connection with the Transaction, the existing encumbrances o f the Loan (i.e. the security over the Oselvar Interest and the Volve Interest, as well as the parent company guarantee from the Seller) have been replaced with cash collateral (on a pledge and blocked escrow account). The cash collateral covers the Remaining Loan, remaining interest amount until the Call Date, and the call premium.
