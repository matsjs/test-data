Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 6 January 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 16 December 2008 regarding amendment o f Loan Agreement.
There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
1. Interest Payment due December 19th
Unpaid interest due December 19th (the "Due Interest Amount'') shall bear overdue interest equal to the original coupon plus 5% p.a. The Due Interest Amount (including overdue interest)falls due no later than 5thFebruary 2009.
2. Interest rate
The original coupon shall be increased by 5%p.a. from (and including) December 19th until the Due Interest Amount (including overdue interest) is paid in accordance with item (1) above.
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is given power o f attorney to enter into the necessary agreements in connection with decisions made on the bondholders ' meeting as well as to carry out the necessary completion work.
