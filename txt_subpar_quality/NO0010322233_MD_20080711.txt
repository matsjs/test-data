Bond Loan in SAGA Oil ASA
(Stavanger, July 11, 2008) SAGA Oil ASA has initiated negotiations with Norsk Tillitsmann to extend the maturity date of ISIN NO 001 032 223.3 12.00 per cent SAGA Oil ASA Bond Issue 2006/2008 Call and Warrants maturing 14thof July 2008. The background for these negotiations are that SAGA Oil ASA has not yet completed its ongoing financing process and is therefore not in a position to repay the bond loan on the maturity date. SAGA Oil ASA will provide further information in due course.
About SAGA Oil ASA SAGA Oil ASA was established in October 2005 tofocus on exploration, development and production o f oil and gas assets in Russia and the FSU. The company's founder has more than 20 years o f business experience in the Soviet Union and Russia. SAGA Oil currently holds two production licenses in Orenburg region through its Russian subsidiary. The company started productionfrom thefirst license in 2007, and it will gradually build up its production during 2008 and will start developing its new acreage. SAGA Oil has it's headquarter in Stavanger, Norway, and has around 80 employees in Norway and Russia. The company's shares are listed at the OTC market (Over the Counter-List) in Oslo, Norway.
