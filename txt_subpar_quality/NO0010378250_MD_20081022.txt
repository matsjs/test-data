Reorganization of the Sølvtrans Group - Information to the Bondholders
The Solvtrans Group have recently been reorganization. The scope of this reorganisation has been to enable most of the ship-owning activities of the group to be entered in the Norwegian tonnage tax regime.
The reorganization has been carried out as a drop-down demerger/merger (the "Demerger"), implying that the ship-owning activities of the group (including related assets, rights and obligations etc) other than the activity based in Chile, has been transferred to Solvtrans Shipping AS ("Solvtrans Rederi"). Assets, rights and obligations that are not comprised by the Norwegian tonnage tax regime remain in other parts of the group.
