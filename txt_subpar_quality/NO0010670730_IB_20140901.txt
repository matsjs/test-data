Exercise of Call Option
Nordic Trustee ASA (the "Bond Trustee") is acting as bond trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Bond Issue") where AGR Petroleum Services Holdings AS is the issuer (the "Issuer"). The Issuer has in accordance with a letter to the Bond Trustee of 1 September 2014 notified that the Issuer will exercise the Call Option in accordance with clause 10.2.1 (i) and 10.2.2 of the bond agreement to repay the entire Bond Issue. The Bond Issue will be repaid in full on 15 September 2014 at 110 % of par value - plus accrued interest. Record date is set to 11 September 2014. Bondholders requesting further information may contact Nordic Trustee ASA.
