Oslo, 3 October 2013
Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
Today a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 17 September 2013 (the "Summons"). There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 96.01 % of the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as set out in clause 2 of the Summons. Further, pursuant to the proposal, all Bondholders shall receive a one-time amendment fee of 0.25% (flat) of the face value of the Bonds (the "One-Time Fee"). The One-Time Fee will be paid on 8 October 2013 with record date end of business 4 October 2013. Yours sincerely
