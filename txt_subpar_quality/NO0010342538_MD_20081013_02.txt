Oslo, 13 October 2008
Insolvency proceedings
The Borrower has today published the following press release:
'The Board o f Directors in Svithoid Tankers will apply for insolvent liquidation The Board o f Directors in Svithoid Tankers AB decided yesterday on Sunday the 12th o f October to investigate the possibilities for a company reorganisation. After having reviewed the circumstances this morning it has become clear that the prerequisites do not exist. The Board of Director has therefore decided to apply for insolvent liquidation. Board o f Directors in Svithoid Tankers AB Svithoid Tankers may be required to disclose the information provided herein pursuant to the Swedish Securities Markets Act and/or the Swedish Financial Instruments Trading Act. The information was provided for public release on Monday October 13, 2008 at 12.45p.m. Swedish time."
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA will closely monitor the process and will come back with further information later. Such information may be published on the web site www.stamdata.no.
