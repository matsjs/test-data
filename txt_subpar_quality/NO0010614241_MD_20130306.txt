Notice to Bondholders - Consent Fee Payment

Oslo, 6 March 2013

On 7 February a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 21 January 2013 (the "Summons"). The proposed resolution obtained 100 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement.
The Bondholders' Meeting extended the Maturity Date to 24 June 2014 in accordance with the Amendment Agreement appended to the Summons. As Compensation, the company shall make a consent fee payment o f 2 % (flat) of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Consent Fee Payment").
The Consent Fee Payment will be made on 12 March 2013 with record date on 8 March 2013.
