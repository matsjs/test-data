Oslo, 8 June 2012 Information to Bondholders Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is the Bond Trustee for the above mentioned Bond Issue. Referring to the notice to the Bondholders dated 1 June 2012 regarding the Borrower's filing for reorganisation under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code in the United States. As consequence of the reorganisation under Chapter 11, there will probably not be made any coupon payments related to the Bond Issue during the period in which the Borrower is protected from bankruptcy under Chapter 11. As such, the coupon payment due on 12 June 2012 will not be made. The Bond Trustee will notify the Bondholders in case any coupon payment will be made during this period. Interest on the Bonds will continue to accrue. The Bond Trustee will monitor the situation closely and revert to the Bondholders with further information when available.
