Notice to Bondholders
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") is appointed as loan trustee for the above mentioned bond issues (the "Loan") where Cecon ASA is the issuer (the "Cecon"). With reference to the press release dated January 20th 2009, regarding the cancellation notice of the DnB Nor bank facility, we have been informed by Cecon that Cecon will not be able to meet the interest payments on the Loan due February 5th 2009 in a timely manner. Pursuant to the Loan Agreement, Cecon has a remedy period of five banking days. The board and management of Cecon and its advisers are working closely with NT to evaluate the situation. These discussions are positive in the spirit of mutual cooperation. A summons to bondholders meeting will be called within shortly.
