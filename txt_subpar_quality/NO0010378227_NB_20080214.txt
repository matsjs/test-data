Waiver of financial covenants -Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 14 February 2008 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 7 February 2008 regarding granting a waiver of financial covenants in the Loan Agreement. A total of 53.38% of the outstanding Bonds were represented at the meeting. There where sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form at quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 8.20 % of the votes, which, according to the voting requirements of the Bond Agreement, was not sufficient for adoption. The Bond Agreement will remain unchanged. The Bondholders may obtain a copy of the minutes from the meeting by request to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA.
