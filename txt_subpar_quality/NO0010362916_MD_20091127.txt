Restructuring process is initiated
Background
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA is appointed as Loan Trustee for the above mentioned Bond issues where Petrojack ASA is the borrower (the "Borrower").
Reference is made to the Borrowers stock exchange announcement on 27 November 2009 where preliminary results as per 3rd quarter 2009 is presented. The announcement is found here www.newsweb.no . The Borrower has informed that the company is in a distressed situation.
Initial discussions with the Borrower regarding the situation and a possible restructuring plan has started. We are about to engage an advisor to support us, on behalf of the Bondholders, in the discussions. Further information will be provided at a later stage.
No interest payment 30 November 2009
According to the Loan Agreements, both the NOK 2012 Bond and the USD 2012 Bond have interest payments falling due on 30 November 2009. However, in the prevailing situation there is no room to make the interest payments now and payments are deferred.
