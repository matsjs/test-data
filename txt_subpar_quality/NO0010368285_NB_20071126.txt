Request for cosent to temporarily waiver of Equity Ratio covenant - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 26 November 2007 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons o f 16 November 2007 regarding a request for consent to temporarily waver o f Equity ratio covenant.
There where sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form at quorum. The proposed resolution obtained 100 % o f the votes, and the proposal was adopted according to the voting requirements o f the Bond Agreement. The Bond Agreement will therefore be changed as follows:
1. The Equity Ratio Covenant is temporarily waived, implying thatfor aperiod o f 9 months o f the date o f the Bondholders' meeting, the Equity Ratio Covenant shall be 30 % or higher. For the avoidance o fdoubt, the Book Equity Ratio Covenant shall thereafter be at least 40%.
2. The Bondholders shall receive a waiverfee o f 1.0% (flat) o f their holdings of the Bonds. The waiver fee will be paid to the Bondholders registered as Bondholders end o f business on the day o f this summoned Bondholders' meeting (i.e. 26 November 2007) and will be due 4 December 2007.
