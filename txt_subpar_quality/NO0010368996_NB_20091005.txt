Proposal to enter into an agreement in relation to the termination of the CJ70 Contract - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 5 October 2009 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 25 September 2009 regarding a Proposal to enter into an agreement in relation to the termination of the CJ70 Contract.
