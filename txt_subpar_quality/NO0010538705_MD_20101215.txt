Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") acts as loan trustee for the above captioned bond issue, where Seadrill Ltd. (the "Borrower") is the borrower. The terms and conditions of the loan are set out in a loan agreement between the Borrower and NT dated 26 September 2009 later amended in a Amendment Agreement dated 2 October 2009 (the "Loan Agreement"). All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Loan Agreement or in this letter. On 30 November 2010 the Borrower announced a dividend payment of USD 0.65 per share. The Conversion Price is adjusted according to the Loan Agreement, from USD 22.65 to USD 22.18, effective on 16 December 2010 which is the ex-dividend date.
