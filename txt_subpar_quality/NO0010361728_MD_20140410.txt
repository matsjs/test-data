Re. Interest Payments and Cash Sweep Redemption due 16 April 2014

On 16 April 2014 interest payments and cash sweep redemption fall due. Just for clarification, the following will be carried out by VPS:

Amounts of Outstanding Bonds:

USD 97,212,605.00

Cash Coupon Amount due:

USD 3,946,042.04

Amounts of PIK Bonds due:

USD 2,430,315.00

Cash Sweep Redemption:

USD 5,324,704.00

New Amounts of Outstanding Bonds: USD 94,318,206.00

The record date for the Cash Sweep Redemption will be 14 April 2014.

For the Interest Period from and including 16 April 2014, the total Margin will be increased with 2 % p.a. to 28% p.a., implying that the Coupon will be Libor + 28,00% p.a.

On the new proposed Maturity Date 16 June 2014, a Restructuring Fee in the aggregate amount of USD 1,418,481.82 (i.e. 1% flat of the outstanding amounts of bonds at the restructuring in 2012) will be payable. The Restructuring Fee will be payable to those who owns the bonds on the new Maturity Date. The Restructuring Fee will be approx. 1.50393 % (flat) of the amount of Outstanding Bonds after the Interest Payment and Cash Sweep have been carried out on 16 April 2014.

The aforesaid is subject to the Bondholders' Meeting to be held on 14 April 2014 approve the proposal set out in the Summons dated 7 April 2014.
