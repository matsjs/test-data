In accordance with resolution on a Bondholder's Meeting held on 17 March 2014, a Restructuring Fee was paid to the Bondholders in accordance with the Amendment Agreement No. 6/ the Amended and Restated Loan Agreement Clause 14.4 litra a) (the "Restructuring Fee"), without any reduction of the principal amount.
