Re. Sale of Chekovo Limited, Owner of "Thule Power" - Mandatory Redemption
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NTM") acts as Loan Trustee for the existing bond issues mentioned above (the "Bond Issues") in respect of which Thule Drilling ASA is the borrower (the "Borrower").
The Bond Issues are i.a. encumbranced by the Borrower's jack-up "Thule Power" (by mortgage over the said jack-up, an upstream guarantee from the rig owning company Chekovo Limited ("Chekovo") and share pledge over the shares issued by Chekovo.
On 31 December 2007 the Borrower announced that Chekovo is sold to interests in Saudi Arabia. Pursuant to the Sale and Purchase Agreement (the "SPA"), the delivery of shares shall take place on 31 March 2008.
The delivery of the shares in Chekovo triggers partly mandatory redemption as follows:
in respect of the First Priority Bond Issue, redemption of 50 % of the outstanding bonds at 105 % of par plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount; and in respect of the Second Priority Bond Issue, redemption of 50 % of the outstanding bonds at 102 % of par plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount.
Subject to the transaction is completed, the partial redemption will be carried out by drawing lots between the bonds (the drawing will be carried out randomly by the Norwegian Registry of Securities on 18 January 2008), and repayment/settlement will be on or about the date where shares are delivered pursuant to the SPA.
In connection with the partial redemption, the security related to "Thule Power" and/or Chekovo will be discharged.
