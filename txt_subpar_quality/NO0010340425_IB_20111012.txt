Oslo, 12 October 2011
Notice to Bondholders - sale of vessel
Reference is made to the previous notices. The Bond Issue is secured inter alia over the vessel M/S Laurita. The vessel has since bankruptcy been laid up at Drydocks World Dubai and the yard has maintained a retention right over the vessel. On October 11, 2011 the vessel was sold to Ramunia Holdings Berhad in cooperation between the Bondholders and the yard. The total sale price of the vessel was USD 82.5 million. A part of the sale sum of the vessel will be paid out to the Bondholders following satisfaction of yard claims, costs and expenses. A separate notice will be published later at Stamdata showing the revenue to be paid out. The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders Committee will continue the work to enforce further pledged equipment assets. Best regards For Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
