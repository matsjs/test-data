REQUEST FOR CONSENT TO EARLY REDEMPTION - Notice from Bondholders' Meeting
On 25 May 2007 a Bondholders' meeting was held pursuant to summons of 14 May 2007 regarding a request from the Borrower for consent to early redemption.
