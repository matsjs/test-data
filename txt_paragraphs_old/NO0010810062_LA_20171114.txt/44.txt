2.4 Status of the Bonds The Bonds and each other payment obligation under or in relation to the Finance Documents shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer and each relevant Obligor, and shall be secured on a first priority basis in certain assets of the Obligors as set out in these Bond Terms, and otherwise rank at least pari passu with the claims of the Obligors' other unsubordinated creditors, except for obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law. All payment obligations under or in relation to the Finance Documents shall rank ahead of any subordinated capital.
