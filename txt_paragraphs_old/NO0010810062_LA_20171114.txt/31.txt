"Put Option Threshold Amount" means USD 10,000,000.
"Qualified Pledged Account" means a dedicated bank account held in the name of Issuer and designated as a "Qualified Pledged Account" by notice from the Issuer to the Bond Trustee. If the Issuer establishes a Qualified Pledged Account, the Issuer shall ensure that the Qualified Pledged Account shall, prior to transferring any amounts to such account, be pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Obligors' obligations under the Finance Documents.
