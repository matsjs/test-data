13.7 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation, provided that the Issuer may become a Norwegian public liability company.
13.8 Nature of business
The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that each other Group Company will, procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Group from that carried on at the date of these Bond Terms, and which is to (directly or indirectly) own, develop and operate Hydrocarbon Assets.
13.9 Operations
The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that each other Group Company will, ensure that the operations of the Group are conducted in accordance with acknowledged practices related to the oil and gas industry in all material respect.
