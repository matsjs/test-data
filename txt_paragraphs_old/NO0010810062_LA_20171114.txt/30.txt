"Put Option Amount" means:
(a) with respect to a Minor Asset Disposal Event: the post-tax consideration received or receivable by the relevant Group Company in each transaction constituting a Minor Asset Disposal;
(b) with respect to a Share Disposal Event: the post-tax consideration received or receivable by the relevant Group Company in each transaction constituting a Share Disposal Event; and
(c) with respect to a Minor Total Loss Event: the higher of (i) the reduction in fair market value of the relevant Group Company's ownership interest in the relevant Hydrocarbon Asset due to the Put Option Event and (ii) the post-tax (if applicable) amount of insurance proceeds received by the relevant Group Company within 210 days after the occurrence of the Minor Total Loss Event.
