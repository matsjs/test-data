10. REDEMPTION AND REPURCHASE OF BONDS
10.2 Voluntary early redemption - Call Option
(a) The Issuer may redeem all or parts of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Call Option") on any Business Day from and including:
(i) the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2018 at a price equal to 107.50 per cent, of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond value (plus accrued unpaid interest on the redeemed amount;
(ii) the Interest Payment Date in November 2018 to, but not including, 16 February 2019 at a price equal to 105.00 per cent, of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond value (plus accrued unpaid interest on redeemed amount);
(iii) 16 February 2019 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2019 at a price equal to 104.375 per cent of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond value (plus accrued unpaid interest on redeemed amount);
