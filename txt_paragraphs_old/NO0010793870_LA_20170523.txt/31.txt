10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption 10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer. 10.2 Call Option
14
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem all, but not only parts, of the Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) with settlement at any time from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 102.5% of par value plus accrued interest on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
