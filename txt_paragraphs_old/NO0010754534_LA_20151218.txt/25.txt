2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 400,000,000 (Norwegian kroner fourhundredmillion).
The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The Initial Issuer will be in the amount of NOK 200,000,000 (Norwegian kroner twohundredmillion).
The Face Value is NOK 500,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Bulk Industrier AS Senior Secured Open Callable Bond Issue 2015/2018".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0754534.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
