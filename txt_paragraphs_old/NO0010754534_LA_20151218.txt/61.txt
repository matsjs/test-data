Compliance with laws
13.4 Special covenants
(a) Distributions
The Issuer shall not pay, make or declare any Distribution, whether in cash or in kind, after the Issue Date in 2015 or in excess of NOK 5,000,000 in aggregate each financial year thereafter.
(b) Financial indebtedness restriction
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
(c) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not create or permit to subsist any mortgage, pledge, lien or other encumbrance over any of its present or future assets or its revenues, other than the Permitted Security.
