Insurance
(i) The Issuer shall procure that reasonable and satisfactory' Insurances of the Rig and all relevant equipment related thereto shall be in place at all times, with reputable insurers of financial standing as approved by the Bond Trustee, and the Rig shall be adequately insured against such risks, including but not limited to, Hull & Machinery and Hull Interest, Protection & Indemnity as per industry standards (including contractual liability extensions), Loss of Hire, Requisition Compensation, Expropriation Risk and War Risk insurance. The Issuer shall also procure that any additional insurances required under any law or Project Document shall be maintained over the Rig.
(ii) The aggregate value of the Hull and Machinery and Hull Interest insurances (including War Risks) of the Rig shall at all times be the higher of (i) the full market value of the Rig as determined by a valuation performed by an independent third party expert, and (ii) 120% (one hundred and twenty per cent) of the aggregate outstanding amounts under the Finance Documents. Any deductibles in respect of claims under the Hull and Machinery insurance shall in each event not exceed USD 2,500,000 (US Dollars two million five hundred thousand) for any one occurrence or such higher amount as the Bond Trustee otherwise agrees.
