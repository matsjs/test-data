9.
INTEREST
9.1.1
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 11.0% (eleven point zero per cent) per annum from and including the Issue Date (the "Fixed R ate").
9.1.2 Interest payments shall be made quarterly in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates, the first Interest Payment Date being on 4 March 2015 and the last Interest Payment Date being on the Maturity Date.
9.1.3
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date or, as the case may be, the Maturity Date.
