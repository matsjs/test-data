(b)
If the deadline is set out in weeks, months or years, the deadline shall end on the day in the last
week or the last month which, according to its name or number, corresponds to the first day the
deadline is in force. If such day is not a part of an actual month, the deadline shall be the last
day of such month.
(c)
If a deadline ends on a day which is not a Business Day, the deadline is postponed to the next
Business Date.
