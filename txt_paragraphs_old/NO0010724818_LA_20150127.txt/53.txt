In the event that the conditions precedent for the Disbursement, as set out in Clause 6.2.1, (but excluding the Pre-Operational Security) are not satisfied or waived by the Bond Trustee in accordance with the provisions of Clause 6.2.2 above, within 90 (ninety) days of the Issue Date, the Bonds shall be redeemed (a) at par value (together with accrued interest) if such failure to comply with the conditions precedent set out in Clause 6.2.1 is, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee beyond the control of the Ultimate Parent Group, or (b) otherwise at the applicable Mandatory Prepayment rate as set out in Clause 10.3 (Mandatory Redemption). 6.3 Authorised payments
Notwithstanding Clauses 6.1 to 6.2 above, the Bond Trustee shall be authorized to transfer from the Escrow Account any Authorised Payments due and owing related to the issue of the Bonds, as evidenced through invoice(s) from the Manager, to the extent such fees and costs have not been deducted from the proceeds transferred to the Escrow Account on the Issue Date.
