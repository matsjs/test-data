11.1 Covenant to pay
11.1.1
The Issuer will on any Payment Date (or any other due date pursuant to any Finance Document) unconditionally pay to or to the order of the Bond Trustee all amounts due under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document.
11.1.2 The covenant contained in Clause 11.1 shall be for the benefit of the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders.
11.2 Paym ent mechanics
11.2.1
If no specific order is made by the Bond Trustee under Clause 11.1, the Issuer shall pay all amounts due to the Bondholders under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in connection with its securities account in the Securities Depository.
