13.1 Each Bondholder may exercise one or more of his Conversion Righl(s) at the Conversion Price at any time during the Exercise Period provldod that notification thereof Is given pursuant to clause 13.4.
13.2 The Conversion Right cannot be separated from the Bond.
13.3 The number of Shares to be Issued on exercise of a Conversion Right shall be determined by dividing the principal amount of the relevant Bond or Bonds (subject to clause 13.8) by the Conversion Price In effect on the relevant Conversion Date. The Conversion Price shall be subject to adjustment pursuant to clause 10,3,14 and 15.
13.4
In order to exercise a Conversion Right, the Bondholder shall deliver to the Paying Agent (via Its Account Manager) a duly completed, Irrevocable and signed exercise police. Request for conversion takes place by the Bondholder notifying his Account Manager of the number of Bonds which shall be converted. The Account Manager will then promptly forward the request to the Borrower (via the Paying Agent).
13.5
Conversion will be effected by a set-off of the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted against the Issuing of the whole number of Shares resulting from dividing the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted by the Conversion Price (subject to clause 13.8). Any excess amount beyond the whole number of Shares converted by the Bonds shall fall to the Borrower and accordingly fractions of Shares will not be Issued or transferred upon exercise of a Conversion Right and no cash payment will be made In lieu thereof.
