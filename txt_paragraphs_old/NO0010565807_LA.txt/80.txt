17 Fees and expenses
17.1 The Borrower shall pay an annual fee to the Loan Trustee, the amount of which Is set out In a separate agreement between the Borrower and the Loan Trustee.
17.2
Tho Borrower shall cover all expenses in connection with the Loan, such as preparation of the Loan Agreement, the listing of the Bonds on the Exchange (if applicable) end registration and administration of the Loan In the Securities Depository In accordance with the agreement between the Borrower and the Securities Depository.
17.3
The Borrower shall cover any and all public fees In connection with the Loan. Any public fees or taxes on the sales of Bonds In the secondary market shall be paid by the Bondholders, unless otherwise decided by law or regulation.
