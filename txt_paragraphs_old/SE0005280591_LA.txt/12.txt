5.2
If, due to the existence of an obstacle referred to in Condition 20.1 it is not possible to
determine the Interest Rate for an Interest Period, the Interest Rate for the preceding Interest
Period shall apply. As soon as the obstacle has been removed, the Interest Rate shall be
determined for the current Interest Period, which shall apply from the second (2nd) Business
Day following such determination until (and including) the last day of such Interest Period.
