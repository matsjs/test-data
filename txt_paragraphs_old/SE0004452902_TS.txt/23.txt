9
Default interest
9.1 If the Company fails to pay any amount due, the Company shall pay default interest on such amount at a rate corresponding to the Interest Rate plus two percentage units (Sw: procentenheter), from the date such payment was due up to and including the date of actual payment. Accrued default interest shall not be capitalized.
9.2 If the delay is due to an existence of an obstacle for the Company, the Agent, the CSD or the Issuing Agent respectively, as set out in Section 21.1, the default interest shall not exceed the relevant Interest Rate.
10 Early redemption by request of the Company
10.1 All Bonds, but not less than all, can be redeemed early at the option of the Company on any Early Redemption Date. The Company can exercise its option by giving the Holders not less than thirty (30) days' and not more than sixty (60) days' notice in accordance with Section 18 (Notices). The notice shall be irrevocable and state the Early Redemption Date and the relevant Record Date.
10.2
The Bonds shall be redeemed at the Early Redemption Amount together with accrued interest in accordance with Section 6 (Interest) from the preceding Interest Payment Date up to and including the relevant Early Redemption Date.
