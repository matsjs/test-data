11 Change of Control Offer
11.1 If a Change of Control occurs, then each Holder shall have the right to require that the Company purchases such Holder's Bonds in whole or in part (equal to SEK ten thousand (10,000) or an integral multiple of SEK ten thousand (10,000), in excess thereof), at a purchase price (the "Change of Control Purchase Price") in cash in an amount equal to 100% of the principal amount of such Bonds, plus any accrued and unpaid interest, if any, to, but not including, the date of purchase (the "Change of Control Purchase Date") (subject to the rights of holders of record on relevant record dates to receive interest due on an interest payment date), pursuant to the offer described below (the "Change of Control Offer"); provided, however, that the Company shall not be obliged to repurchase Bonds as described under this Section 11 in the event and to the extent that it has unconditionally exercised its right to redeem all of the Bonds as described under Section 10 (Early redemption by request of the company).
11.2 The Company shall within 30 days of any Change of Control notify the Agent thereof and give written notice of such Change of Control to each Holder in accordance with Section 18 (Notices), stating, among other things:
(a) that a Change of Control has occurred and the date of such event;
(b) the Change of Control Purchase Price and the Change of Control Purchase Date which shall be fixed by the Company on a Business Day not less than thirty (30) days' and not more than sixty (60) days' from the date of such notice;
(c) that any Bond not tendered shall continue to accrue interest and unless the Company defaults in payment of the Change of Control Purchase Price, any Bonds accepted for payment pursuant to the Change of Control Offer shall cease to accrue interest after the Change of Control Purchase Date; and
