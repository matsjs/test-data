Subsidiaries' distributions
The Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company creates or permits to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right of any such Group Company to:
(a) pay dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders;
(b) service any Financial Indebtedness owed to the Issuer;
(c) make any loan to the Issuer; or
(d) transfer any of its assets and properties to the Issuer
if the creation of such contractual obligation is reasonably likely to prevent the Issuer from complying with any of its obligations under the Bond Terms.
