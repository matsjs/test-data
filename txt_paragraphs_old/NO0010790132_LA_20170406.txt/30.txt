The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds, together with the net proceed from the issuance of the Senior Second Secured Bonds, towards (i) the refinancing of the Existing Debt, (ii) payment of Transaction Costs, and (iii) general corporate purposes.
2.4 Status of the Bonds
The Bonds will constitute direct, general, unconditional, senior debt obligations of the Issuer and shall at all times rank at least pari passu with all direct and unsecured obligations of the Issuer, except those obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law, and without any preference among them.
