12. INFORMATION UNDERTAKINGS
12.1 Financial Reports (a) The Issuer shall prepare Annual Financial Statements in the English language and make them available on its website no later than four (4) months after the expiry of each financial year.
(b) The Issuer shall prepare Interim Accounts in the English language and make them available on its website no later than two (2) months after the expiry of each relevant financial quarter.
(c) After the Bonds have been listed on an Exchange, the Annual Financial Statements and the Interim Accounts shall, in addition, be made available in accordance with the rules and regulations of such Exchange.
