The Group does not have any treasury shares. Total equity consists of share capital, fair value reserve, reserve for invested non-restricted equity, translation differences and retained earnings.
Reserve for invested non-restricted equity Safari Finco 1 Oy's investment in 2009 to reserve for invested non-restricted equity.
Translation differences
Translation differences include those foreign exchange differences arising from the translation of the financial statements of foreign companies, where the functional currency is other than Euro, into Euros. Also gains and losses from quasi-equity loans are reported in translation differences.
Retained earnings
The profit for the year and changes in ownership of company's own shares are booked to retained earnings.
Dividend per share
The Board of Directors' proposal is that no dividend shall be paid for the financial year ended December 31, 2013.
