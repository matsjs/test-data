The Shareholders' Agreement provides that no action or decision may be taken by Luxco 1 or any Group Company in respect of certain matters without the prior written consent of shareholders holding 66.66% or more of the voting rights in respect of the ordinary shares in Luxco 1.

 approval of any change to the name of Luxco 1 or nature of the business of the Group;
