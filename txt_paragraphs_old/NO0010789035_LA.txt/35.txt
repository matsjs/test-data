CONDITIONS FOR DISBURSEMENT
6.1 Conditions precedent for disbursement to the Issuer
(a) Disbursement of the net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of legal costs of the Managers and any other costs and expenses incurred in connection with the Bond Issue) to the Issuer is subject to the receipt or waiver by the Bond Trustee (no later than 2 Business Days prior to the Issue Date) of the following documents (in form and content satisfactory to the Bond Trustee):
(i) the Bond Terms duly executed by all parties thereto;
(ii) copies of all corporate resolutions of the Issuer required for the Issuer to issue the Bonds and execute Finance Documents to which it is a party;
(iii) certified copies of the Issuer's articles of association and of a full extract from the relevant company register in respect of the Issuer evidencing that the Issuer is validly existing;
