The Issuer shall not, or permit any member of the Group to, engage directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any Related Party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the relevant member of the Group's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the relevant member of the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(g) Transactionswith shareholders,directors or affiliated companies
The Issuer shall not engage directly or indirectly in any transaction between the Issuer and (i) any direct or indirect shareholder thereof, (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business, and on comprercial terms, not less favourable to the Issuer or any other company within the Group than would have prevailed in an amis' length transaction with a third party.
All such transactions shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-8 of the Private and Public Limited Companies Acts 1997.
