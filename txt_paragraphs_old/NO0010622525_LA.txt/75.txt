14.4 The Issuer shall cover all public fees in connection with the Bonds and the Finance Documents. Any public fees levied on the trade of Bonds in the secondary market shall be paid by the Bondholders, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation, and the Issuer is not responsible for reimbursing any such fees.

14.6 The Issuer shall deduct before payment to the Bondholders at source any applicable withholding tax payable pursuant to law.
