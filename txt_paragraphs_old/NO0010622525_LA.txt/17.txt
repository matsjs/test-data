"Parent Group" means the Parent with all its current and future Subsidiaries.
"Paying Agent" means any legal entity as appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee who acts as paying agent on behalf of the Issuer with respect to the Bonds.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest.
"Quarterly Financial Reports" means the interim unaudited reports of the Issuer and the Parent, respectively (unconsolidated and consolidated basis for the Group and the Parent Group (as the case may be)) as of each Quarter Date, such accounts to be prepared in accordance with IFRS, and include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary or report from the Board of Directors.
"Related Party" means subsidiary of the parent in which the Parent owns more than 5 % of the shares..
"Relevant Record Date" means a date agreed upon between the Bond Trustee and the Paying Agent and VPS in connection with the repayment as set out in Clauses 10.3 (Call Option) and 10.5 (Mandatory Redemption).
"Retention Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank in order to service interest on this Bond Issue and to be funded in accordance with Clauses 8.2 and 13.4(b). The account shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Retention Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the Account Bank for the amount standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Retention Account (according to Norwegian law), in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders).
"Securities Register" means the securities register in which the Bond Issue is registered.
