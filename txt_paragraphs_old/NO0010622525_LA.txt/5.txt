"Assignment of Management Agreements" means a first priority assignment of the Business Management Agreement in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders), including declaration of subordination of any amounts payable by the Issuer and/or the Owning Companies to the claims of the Bondholders upon an Event of Default. The assignment shall also include direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights provided 60 days prior notice, and the Issuer and/or the Owning Companies shall give notice and obtain consent and acknowledgement from the Vessel Managers under the Business Management Agreement.
