"Shareholder Loan" means the loan in the amount of approximately USD 60,000,000 granted by the Parent to any member of the Group in connection with the reorganization of the Parent Group, subject to the Subordination Agreement.
"Stamp Duty Escrow Account" means an account held by the Issuer, into which an amount sufficient to cover any potential stamp duty which may incur in Cyprus shall be transferred on Disbursement, such amount to be determined by Cyprus local counsel. The Stamp Duty Escrow Account shall be blocked and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.

"Stamp Duty Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the Account Bank for the amount standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Stamp Duty Escrow Account (according to Norwegian law), in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders).
"Subordination Agreement" means a subordination agreement entered into between the Bond Trustee, the Issuer and the Parent, to include, inter cilia, provisions on full subordination (both in terms of payment of interest, principal and priority) and that there shall be no enforcement rights under the Shareholder Loan as long as any amount under the Bond Issue is outstanding.
"Subsidiary" means an entity over which another entity or person has a determining influence due to (i) direct and indirect ownership of shares or other ownership interests, and/or (ii) agreement, understanding or other arrangement. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of another entity or person if such entity or person has such number of shares or ownership interests so as to represent the majority of the votes in the entity, or has the right to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
"Taxes" means all present and future taxes, levies, imposts, duties, charges, fees, deductions and withholdings, and any restrictions and or conditions resulting in a charge together with interest thereon and penalties in respect thereof and "Tax" and "Taxation" shall be construed accordingly.
"Total Loss Event" means an actual or constructive total loss of any of the Vessels.
