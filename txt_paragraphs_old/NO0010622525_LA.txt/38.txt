(b) Power and authority
The Issuer and the Owning Companies have the power to enter into and perform, and has taken all necessary corporate action to authorise their entry into, performance and delivery of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document to which they are parties as well as the transactions contemplated by those Finance Documents.
(c)
Valid, binding and enforceable obligations
This Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document constitute (or will constitute, when executed by the respective parties thereto) legal, valid and binding obligations of such parties, enforceable in accordance with their terms, and (save as provided for therein and upon registration of applicable Security Interests in the public registers of the jurisdictions concerned) no further registration, filing, payment of tax or fees or other formalities are necessary or desirable in order to render said documents enforceable against the Issuer or the Owning Companies, or in order for any Security Interest created (or to be created) by any Security Documents to constitute a valid, perfected and enforceable Security Interest in accordance with the terms and conditions of such Security Document.
(d) Non-conflict with other obligations
The entry into and performance by the Issuer and the Owning Companies of the Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document to which they are parties, and the transactions contemplated thereby, do not and will not conflict with (i) any present law or regulation or present judicial or official order; (ii) the Issuer or the Owning Companies' memorandum of association, bye-laws or other constitutional documents; or (iii) any document or agreement which is binding on the Issuer or the Owning Companies or any of their assets.
