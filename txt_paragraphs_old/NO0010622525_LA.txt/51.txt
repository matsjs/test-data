10.2.2 The Cash Sweep Redemption, if applicable, shall take place no earlier than the Interest Payment Date falling in July 2012.
10.2.3 If any part of the Cash Surplus Amount includes amounts not relating to gross earnings ("NonQualified Earnings"), then the Issuer shall redeem the Bonds on a pro-rata basis as follows:
(i) firstly, an amount equal to the Non-Qualified Earnings shall be redeemed at a price equi valent to the price of the Call Option as set out in Clause 10.3; and then
(ii) secondly, the remaining Cash Surplus Amount shall be redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.2.1.
10.2.4 The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercise of the Cash Sweep Redemption at least fifteen ( 15) Business Days prior to the applicable Interest Payment Date.
