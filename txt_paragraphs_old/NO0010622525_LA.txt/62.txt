(b) Retention Account
The Issuer shall in connection with the release from the Escrow Account deposit an amount of USD 6,375,000 (i.e. equal to six (6) months interest on the Bond Issue) from the Escrow Account to the Retention Account. Such amount shall be used to pay the first interest payment. Thereafter, the Issuer shall ensure that the Retention Account is built up with monthly payments of 1/6 of the amount payable to meet the next interest payment according to Clause 9 (Interest) and next ordinary principal payment pursuant to Clause 10.1 (Maturity).
For the avoidance of any doubt, the transfer to the Retention Account shall take place regardless of whether any earnings are actually payable to ARV1 and ARV2.
