The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that none of its Subsidiaries shall, cease to carry out its business or change the nature of its business, except for mergers and de-mergers under (d) below if such transactions would not have a Material Adverse Effect.

The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Subsidiary shall, sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets (including, but not limited to, the Vessels) or operations (in connection with a sale to a third party or an internal reorganization), unless the Bonds are redeemed in full in accordance with Clause 10.5 (Mandatoiy Redemption), except for mergers and de-mergers permitted under (d) below.
