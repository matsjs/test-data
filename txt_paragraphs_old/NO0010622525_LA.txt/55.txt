10.5.2 For the avoidance of doubt, the redemption price shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date the repayment is earned out.
10.5.3 Upon the occurrence of a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
10.5.4 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.5 (Mandatory Redemption), the entire amount on the Accounts may be used as partly payment for the redemption.
10.5.5 The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory redemption according to this Clause 10.5 (Mandatory Redemption), at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption. On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date of such mandatory redemption.
