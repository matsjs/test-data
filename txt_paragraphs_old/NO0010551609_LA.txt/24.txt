Status of the Bonds and security
8.3 The Bonds shall be senior debt of the Issuer, save that they shall be subordinated to the First Priority Debt, The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Issuer (save for the First Priority Debt and such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application, and save that the Security Interests shall rank with a second priority behind the First Priority Security, if and when established) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
8.2 The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest and expenses, shall be seemed by the Security Interests,
8.3 Until the First Priority Field Pledge is established, the Second Priority Field Pledge may be established on first priority, The Security Agent hereby undertakes on behalf of the Bondholders to allow for and provide all necessary assistance for the establishment of the First Priority Field Pledge and to subordinate the Second Priority Field Pledge to rank behind the First Priority Field Pledge, as soon as practicably upon notice from the Issuer that the First Priority Debt will be created requiring the First Priority Security to be established.
