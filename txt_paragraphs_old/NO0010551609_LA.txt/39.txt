(c) Debt Restrictions
The Issuer or any member of the Group shall not incur any additional Financial Indebtedness in excess of the aggregate equivalent of USD 20 mill other than (i) the Permitted Indebtedness and/or (ii) as contemplated by this Bond Agreement.
The Issuer shall procure that for any new debt that Is to be counted as Permitted Indebtedness due to being subordinated to the Bonds, such debt shall; (i) be fully subordinated to the Bonds and a subordination agreement shall have
been entered into with the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) in respect of such loans, (ii) have no interest payable before the Field commences production, (iii) have no cash installments prior to the Maturity Date, (iv) have interest rates are on market terms, (v) Suspended Interests on such loans shall accrue but not become payable before the Bonds with accrued interests have been fully paid.
