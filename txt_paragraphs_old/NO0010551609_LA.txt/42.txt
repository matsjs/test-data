(b) Breach o f other obligations The Issuer or any Material Subsidiary fails to duly perform any other material covenant or obligation pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any of the Finance Documents, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure Will be remedied and is remedied within 15 - fifteen - Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
(c) Cross default The aggregate amount of the Issuer's Financial Indebtedness or commitment for Financial Indebtedness falling within paragraphs (i) to (iv) below exceeds a total of NOK 10 million, or the equivalent thereof in other currencies and;
(i) such Financial Indebtedness or guarantee is not paid When due nor Within any originally applicable grace period,
(ii) such Financial Indebtedness is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described),
(iii) any commitment for such Financial Indebtedness is cancelled or suspended by a creditor as a result of an event of default (however described), or
(iv) any creditor becomes entitled to declare such Financial Indebtedness due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described).
(d) Misrepresentations Any representation, warranty or statement (including statements in compliance certificates) made under this Bond Agreement or in connection therewith is or proves to have been incorrect, inaccurate or misleading in any material respect when made or deemed to have been made.
