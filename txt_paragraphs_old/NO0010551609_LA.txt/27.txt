10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date November 2010 at 101.0 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) at any time from Interest Payment Date November 2010 to the Maturity Date at 100.0 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee: and the Bondholders and at least fifteen Business Days prior to the
settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
