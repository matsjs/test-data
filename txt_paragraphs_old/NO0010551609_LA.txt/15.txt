2.3 Purpose arid utilization
2.3.1
The purpose of the Bonds is (i) to finance the development of the Field, (ii) for general corporate purposes and (iii) repayment of a bridge loan of USD 7 million to Grupa Lotos S.A, obtained by the Issuer as a bridge financing pending the issue of the Bonds, provided, however, both for (i) and (ii) that the use of the proceeds of the Bonds is related to financing of the petroleum exploration and production activities associated with the Field and with the Other Licenses.
