The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount ofNOK 200.000. 000 (Norwegian kroner two hundred million). The first tranche will be NOK 100.000. 000 (Norwegian kroner one hundred million).
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Otium AS Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2012/2015".
The ISEN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 066536.7.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied towards the general corporate purposes.
