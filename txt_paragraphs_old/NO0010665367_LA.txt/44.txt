13.4 Special covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions From Issue Date to Maturity Date the Issuer shall not, within a calendar year make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other capital distributions or loans to its shareholders (including any transaction with similar effect) that constitutes more than, on a consolidated basis, 50% of the net profit after taxes of the Group based on the audited accounts for the previous accounting year.
(b) Insurance The Issuer shall, and the Issuer shall procure that each Material Subsidiary will, maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters adequate insurance or captive arrangements with respect to its properties and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as are consistent with prudent business practice.
