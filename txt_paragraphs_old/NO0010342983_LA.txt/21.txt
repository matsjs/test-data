The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
3 Listing
3.1 The Loan will initially not be listed. The Loan, may, at the Borrowers discretion, later be applied for listing on Oslo Børs or Oslo Børs ASAs Alternative Bond Market (ABM.). If the Loan is listed on Oslo Børs, the Loan may be transferred to ABM without a mandatory Bondholders' meeting and without a mandatory repurchase offer of the Loan to the Bondholders.
4 Registration in a Securities Depository
4.1 The Loan must prior to disbursement be registered in a Securities Depository according to the Securities Depository act and the conditions from the Securities Depository.
