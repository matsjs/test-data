2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 1,000,000,000(Norwegian krone one billion), herein referred to as "the Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "FRN Sevan Drilling AS Callable Bond Issue 2006/2012".
The registration number (ISIN)of the Loan will be NO O01 034298.3.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
2.2 The net proceeds of the Loan shall be used for construction of the Rig, included payments under the Construction Contracts and payments to the suppliers of the Equipment.
