10.4 Mandatory Repurchase due to a Change of Control Event or Listing Failure
(a) Upon a Change of Control Event or a Listing Failure occurring, each Noteholder shall have the right to request that all, or only some, of its Notes be repurchased at a price per Note equal to 101.00 per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest, during a period of sixty (60) Business Days following a notice from the Issuer of the Change of Control Event pursuant to Clause 12.1(d)(ii) or the Listing Failure pursuant to Clause 14.8(c) (as applicable, the "Mandatory Repurchase Notice") (after which time period such right shall lapse).
