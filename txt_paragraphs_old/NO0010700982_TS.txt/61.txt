Vantage Drilling Company:
Means Vantage Drilling Company, an international offshore drilling contractor listed on the AMEX Stock Exchange in New York under the symbol "VTG".
Rig Covenants:
Standard covenants related to the Rigs including but not limited to: (i) maintenance of insurances, (ii) no sale of Rigs without redemption of the Bond Issue (see Mandatory Prepayment), (iii) maintenance of class, (iv) maintenance of flag, name and registry, (v) Rigs to be kept in a good and safe condition and repair consistent with prudent ownership and industry standards, and (vi) operation in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
Maintenance of Insurances:
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of insurances of each of the Rigs and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times, hereunder to retain each of the Rigs in class. During operation of each of the Rigs, the Charterer shall ensure that it runs proper maintenance of each of the Rigs according to planned maintenance system.
Each of the Rigs shall also be adequately insured (including war risk) against (i) Hull & Machinery risks at least to the full value of each of the Rigs and in aggregate at least 120% of the outstanding amount under the Finance Documents, (ii) third party liability insurance as per industry standards, (iii) mortgagee interest insurance, (iv) loss of hire and requisition compensation, (v) expropriation risk and (vi) any additional insurance
