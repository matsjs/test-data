Total Loss Prepayment:
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than sixty (60) days following the Total Loss Event redeem (a) 100% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related to all Rigs or (b) if related to 1 or more Rigs, 30% of the outstanding Bonds for each Rig.
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Total Loss Prepayment provision, the relevant amount on the Escrow Account, the relevant Rig Owner Earnings Account and the Issuer Debt Service Account or any of the insurance proceeds may be used as part payment in relation to the Total Loss Prepayment.
