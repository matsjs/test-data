The following financial covenants shall be complied with at any time:
(i) Liquidity: The Issuer undertakes to ensure that the Issuer (on consolidated basis) has access to free and available cash in excess of USD 5 million per Rig (as applicable).
(ii) Current Ratio: The Charterer shall at all times maintain a Current Ratio of minimum 1:1.
(iii) Asset Coverage Ratio: The Issuer shall at all times maintain a minimum Asset Coverage Ratio of 120% on a consolidated basis.
"Asset Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of the aggregated Charter Free Market Values of each of the Rigs to the amount outstanding under the Bond Issue.
"Charter Free Market Values" means the fair market value of each of the Rigs in USD determined as the arithmetic mean of independent valuations of each of the relevant Rigs obtained from two independent and wellreputed sale and purchase brokers familiar with the market for such rigs appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Trustee. Such valuation shall be made on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms as between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts
