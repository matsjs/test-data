The Issuer shall irrevocably and unconditionally instruct the Trustee and the Paying Agent to pay any fees and costs (including legal costs) due and owing related to the Bond Issue as evidenced through invoice from the Sole Arranger using the proceeds on the Escrow Account, to the extent such fees and costs have not been deducted from the proceeds transferred to the Escrow Account.
The Issuer shall procure that all payments from each Rig Owner as borrower under the relevant Internal Loans and all other amounts receivable by the Issuer shall be paid directly by each Rig Owner to the Issuer Earnings Account.
The Issuer Earnings Account shall be pledged in favor o f the Trustee, but not blocked (unless there is an outstanding Event of Default for which the Trustee has issued a notice), and amounts standing to the credit o f the Issuer in the Issuer Earnings Account shall be utilized by the Issuer to service the Bonds as and when such payments under the Bonds are due.
The Issuer shall on a monthly basis ensure that an amount equivalent to 1/6 o f the next coupon payment under the Bond Issue is transferred from the Issuer Earnings Account to the Issuer Debt Service Account. Furthermore, the Issuer shall, on a monthly basis commencing twelve (12) months after Settlement Date ensure that an amount equivalent to 1/6 of the next Scheduled Amortization (as defined above) is transferred from the Issuer Earnings Account to the Issuer Debt Service Account.

Rig Owner Earnings Account:
Each Rig Owner shall procure that all its earnings under any Bareboat Charter and all its other net earnings relating to its Rig and any insurance or sale proceeds shall be paid to the relevant Rig Owner Earnings Account. Each Rig Owner Earnings Account shall be pledged and blocked in favor of the Trustee with a standing order to make the payments referred to in the Application of Earnings under Bareboat Charter referred to above.
