Information covenants
The Issuer undertakes to comply with the following information covenants:
(a) The Issuer shall distribute to the Agent consolidated annual financial statements of the Issuer and Uarda2 and quarterly financial statements of the Issuer (in case of the Issuer, only to the extent not published on the website of the Issuer), as soon as they are available but no later than 180 days after the end of each financial year and 60 days after the end of the relevant interim period.
(b) The Issuer shall procure that each set of annual and quarterly financial statements includes a balance sheet, profit and loss account (or equivalent income statement) and cashflow statement (including complete footnotes to such annual financial statements). In addition, the Issuer shall procure that each set of annual financial statements shall be audited.
(c) Each set of financial statements delivered pursuant to these Terms and Conditions shall:
(i) be accompanied by a Compliance Certificate signed by authorised signatories of the Issuer, which shall, upon the request by one or more Noteholders, be made available by the Agent to the requesting Noteholder(s); and
(ii) be prepared in accordance with Swedish GAAP.
