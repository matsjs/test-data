The Series 1 Notes and the Series 2 Notes. The Series 1 Notes are fixed interest rate securities issued by the Issuer pursuant to the Terms and Conditions of the Notes and the Series 2 Notes are floating interest rate securities issued by the Issuer pursuant to the Terms and Conditions for the Notes.
Secured notes with a fixed interest rate and a floating interest rate, respectively.
The Notes are secured by share security in the company owning the property Solna Uarda 5 and its holding company and a pledge over a promissory note (by which the proceeds from the Notes are lent on to one such holding company) together with mortgage certificates in an aggregate amount of SEK 34,608,000 in the above named property (including an obligation to take out and pledge further mortgage certificates under certain circumstances), in each case as further described in the Terms and Conditions.
The Notes are senior secured debt of the Issuer. The Notes shall, to the extent not covered by the Security granted under the Security Documents, rank pari passu with all other unsecured debt of the Issuer other than those mandatorily preferred by law and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
The Issuer undertakes to apply for listing of the Notes with NASDAQ OMX and will use its best efforts to achieve such listing no later than within sixty (60) days after the Issue Date. The listing is expected to occur no later than on 16 April 2013.
