Auditor
Deloitte AB has been the Company's auditor since 2005 with Svante Forsberg as the auditor in charge. Svante Forsberg was replaced with Kent Åkerlund as the auditor in charge at the annual general meeting 2013. Both Svante Forsberg and Kent Åkerlund are authorised public accountants and members of the Swedish Institute of Authorised Public Accountants, FAR SRS. Deloitte's office address is Rehnsgatan 11, 113 79 Stockholm, Sweden.
Material agreements
The Company is not a party to any material agreements outside of the ordinary course of business which could result in an entity within the Group having a right or an obligation that could materially affect the Company's ability to meet its obligations under the Notes to the Noteholders.
