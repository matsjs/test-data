OPTIONAL REDEMPTION
The Issuer has the right to redeem the Notes at any Interest Payment Date in accordance with (a), (b) and (c) below.
(a) Prior to May 15, 2014, all or a portion of all outstanding Notes on a pro-rata basis at its Nominal Amount plus accrued and unpaid interest together with the Applicable Premium.
"Applicable Premium" means, for the purposes hereof, the higher of:
(i) 1.00% of the principal amount of the redeemed Notes; or
(ii) the excess of: (A) the (I) net present value of the redemption price of such Notes as of May 15, 2014 pursuant to paragraph (c) below (calculated using a discount rate equal to the yield of the Swedish Government Bond SGB 1041, 6.75% with a maturity date on May 5, 2014 (or if such bond should have been redeemed a Swedish Government Bond maturing on or around May 15 2014) plus 0.50%) plus (II) all remaining scheduled interest payments (but excluding accrued but unpaid interest to the redemption date) on such Notes until May 15, 2014; over
(B) the principal amount of such redeemed Notes on such redemption date.
