§12
EVENTS OF DEFAULT
The Trustee, on behalf of the Noteholders, is entitled to terminate the Notes together with any accrued interest thereon either immediately or at such time as the Trustee, acting on behalf of the Noteholders, may determine upon occurrence of any of the following events:
(a) Non-payment
the Issuer fails to make timely payment of interest or any other amount on the Notes, unless such failure to pay is not remedied within 5 Banking Days; or
(b) Breach o f other obligations
the Issuer, in some respect other than that which is specified under section a) fails to perform its obligations pursuant to these terms and conditions, or otherwise act in contravention of such terms and conditions, provided that the Trustee, on behalf of the Noteholders, has demanded that the Issuer rectify such situation and the Issuer has failed to do so within 20 days thereafter; or
