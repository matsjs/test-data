10.3.1
The Issuer may replace any ALXO02 Bond at the VPS Escrow Account, by no later than at the date o f replacement funding up the applicable amount on the Cash Escrow Account and still maintain the Conditional Call Option provided always that no amounts are disbursed from the Cash Escrow Account. For the avoidance of doubt, the Conditional Call Option will be maintained upon redemption of any ALXO02 Bonds on the VPS Escrow Account as the funds deriving from such settlement will (as set out in the VPS escrow pledge agreement) be transferred to the Cash Escrow Account.
10.3.2 Exercise of the Conditional Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least fifteen (15) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Conditional Call Option.
10.3.3
On the settlement date of the Conditional Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
