13.3.6 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.3.7 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation or otherwise amend its constitutional documents unless related to issuance of new shares to the Parent in connection with conversion of debt to equity or otherwise, provided that such shares are subject to the Share Pledge.
