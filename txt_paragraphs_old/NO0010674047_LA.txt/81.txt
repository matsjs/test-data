15.1.10 Charterer or Charter Guarantor default

15.1.11 Litigation
Any event occurs relating to any pending or actual litigation, arbitration or administrative process against the Issuer which could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect.

The Parent, its Subsidiaries or the Issuer repudiates or evidences an intent to repudiate a Transaction Document.
