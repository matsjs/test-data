Conditions Precedent
6 .1 The Bonds will be issued subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it prior to the Issue Date:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by the persons authorised by the certificate of registration or other relevant documentation;
(b) copies of all necessary corporate resolutions to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents;
(c) the agreement set forth in Clause 14,2, duly executed;
(d) evidence that that all necessary resolutions for approval and completion of the Transaction have been passed by the bondholders' meeting of the Original Convertible Bond; and
(e) confirmation that the Bonds have been registered in the Securities Register.
6.2 Subject to the conditions set out in Clause 6.1 having been met or waived, the Bonds will be issued on the Issue Date.
