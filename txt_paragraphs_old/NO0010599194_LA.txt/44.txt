18,3 Amendments and waivers
18.3.1 All amendments of this Bond Agreement shall be made in writing, and shall unless otherwise provided for by this Bond Agreement, only be made with the approval of all parties hereto.
18.4.2 All waivers of any covenant in the Original Convertible Bonds shall apply correspondingly to the covenants pursuant to this Bond Agreement, however, provided that bonds in the Original Convertible Bond are still outstanding.
18.5 Notices, contact information
18.5.1 Written notices, warnings, summons etc to the Bondholders made by the Bond Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Register with a copy to the Issuer. Information to the Bondholders may also be published at the web site www.stamdata.no.
18.5.2 The Issuer's written notifications to the Bondholders shall be sent via the Bond Trustee, alternatively through the Securities Register with a copy to the Bond Trustee.
