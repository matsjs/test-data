2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1
The Issuer has resolved to issue a Series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 16,800,000 (Norwegian kroner sixteen million eight hundred thousand) (the "Loan"), being equal to the interest payment due on 4 February 2011 under the Original Convertible Bond.
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 1 each (par value for each Bond) and shall rank pari passu among themselves,
The Bond Issue will be described as "Zero Coupon Metallkraft AS Senior Unsecured Callable Convertible Bond Issue 2011/2014".
