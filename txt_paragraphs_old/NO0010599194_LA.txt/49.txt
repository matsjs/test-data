B.4 In order to exercise a Conversion Right, the Bondholder shall deliver to the Paying Agent (via its Account Manager) a duly completed, irrevocable and signed Conversion Notice,
Conversion will be effected by a set-off of the total nominal value of the Bonds (at 100% of par) to be converted against the issuing of the whole number of Shares resulting from dividing the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted by the Conversion Price.
B.5 The Issuer is responsible for ensuring that:
(a) the share capital increase is registered in the company register as soon as possible,
