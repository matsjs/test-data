16.7
If the number of votes or replies are equal, the opinion which is most beneficial for the
Issuer, according to the chairman at a Holders' Meeting or the Trustee in a Written
Procedure, will prevail. The chairman at a Holders' Meeting shall be appointed by the
Holders in accordance with Clause 16.6.
