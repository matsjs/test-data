INTEREST
10.1
The Bonds will bear Interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal Amount from, but
excluding, the First Issue Date up to and including the relevant Redemption Date. Any
Subsequent Bond will, however, carry Interest at the Interest Rate from, but excluding, the
Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to its issuance up to and including the
relevant Redemption Date.
