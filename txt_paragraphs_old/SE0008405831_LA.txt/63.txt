Security
The Issuer shall ensure that all shares in Pledged Companies, which from time to time are owned by the Issuer or Vivskä, and all Shareholder Loans, which from time to time are provided by the Issuer or Vivskä, are pledged in favour of the Trustee and the Holders (represented by the Trustee) as first ranking security in accordance with pledge agreements satisfactory to the Trustee (acting reasonably).
Repayment of Shareholder Loans
The Issuer shall procure that no repayments (except for payment of interest thereunder) under any Shareholder Loans are made unless such repayments are deposited on to a Deposit Account. The Trustee shall be obliged to release the security interest under the relevant Shareholder Loans Pledge Agreement simultaneously with the receipt of such repayments on the relevant Deposit Account. Upon request by the Issuer, the Trustee shall be obliged to release an amount specified by the Issuer from the relevant Deposit Account, provided that such amount shall be used for the purpose of financing Permitted Transactions and such release of funds from the relevant Deposit Account shall take place in direct connection with the granting of security over new Shareholder Loans or new shares of Pledged Companies (as the case may be).
