4.1
Date and on any subsequent date when issuance of Subsequent Bonds take place, the Issuing
Agent shall transfer the Net Proceeds to the Escrow Account. For the purpose of securing
that the applicable Conditions Precedent for Disbursements have been fulfilled before a
disbursement of Net Proceeds and for the purpose of securing that the Net Proceeds of the
Initial Bond Issue will be used by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 4.2 and that the Net
Proceeds of any Subsequent Bond Issue will be used by the Issuer in accordance with Clause
4.3, the Escrow Account will be pledged in favour of the Trustee and the Holders
(represented by the Trustee).
