The Issuer shall procure that no repayments (except for payment of interest thereunder) under any Shareholder Loans are made unless such repayments are deposited on to a Deposit Account. The Trustee shall be obliged to release the security interest under the relevant Shareholder Loans Pledge Agreement simultaneously with the receipt of such repayments on the relevant Deposit Account. Upon request by the Issuer, the Trustee shall be obliged to release an amount specified by the Issuer from the relevant Deposit Account, provided that such amount shall be used for the purpose of financing Permitted Transactions and such release of funds from the relevant Deposit Account shall take place in direct connection with the granting of security over new Shareholder Loans or new shares of Pledged Companies (as the case may be).
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that Vivskä does not, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its assets (present or future) to secure any loan or other indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer and Vivskä have a right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
12.10 Dealings with related parties
12.11
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that all other Group Companies will, conduct all dealings with the direct and indirect shareholders of the Group Companies (excluding other Group Companies) and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders at arm's length terms.
Insurance
12.12
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that all other Group Companies and Associated Entities keep its properties insured to an extent which is customary for similar properties on the relevant geographical market with one or more reputable insurers. The insurance cover shall inter alia include full value insurance and third party liability insurances.
