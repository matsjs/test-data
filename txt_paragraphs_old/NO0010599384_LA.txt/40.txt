Maturity of the Bonds, Call Option, Change of Control and Mandatory
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in lull on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in February 2013 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in February 2014 at 104.5 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in February 2014 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in February 2015 at 103.0 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, and
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in February 2015 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 101.5 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
