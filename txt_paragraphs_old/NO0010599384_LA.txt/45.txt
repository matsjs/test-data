Mandatory Redemption in connection with (i) a Sale Event shall be carried out prior to or in connection with such transaction, and (ii) a Total Loss Event shall be carried out at the earlier of receipt of the insurance proceeds relating to such Total Loss and the Total Loss Date if (b) of the definition of Total Loss Date is applicable and 180 days after the Total Loss Date if (a) or (c) of the definition of Total Loss Date is applicable.
10.4.2 Mandatory Redemption shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption.
10.4.3
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
10.4.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be made pro rata among the Bondholders and shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
