(a) Intra-group transactions All transactions between any companies in the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
(b) Transactions with shareholders, directors and affiliated companies All such transactions shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-8 of the Private and Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
(c) Ownership to Rig Owners, Contract Companies and Rigs Save for what is pennitted pursuant to other provisions of this Agreement the Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Material Subsidiary shall, sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any of the Rig Owners, the Rigs or the Contract Companies.
