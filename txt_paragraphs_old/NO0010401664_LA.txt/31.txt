In the event of conversion pursuant to this Clause 10.4, the Borrower shall as soon as possible, but in no event later than on the Conversion Date issue to and in the names of the Bondholder the number of new Shares which are necessary in order to fulfil the Borrower's obligations to issue new Shares to the respective Bondholder.
