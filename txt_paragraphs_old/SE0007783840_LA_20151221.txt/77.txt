12.18.1 The Issuer shall, in accordance with the Trustee Agreement: (a) pay fees to the Trustee; (b) indemnify the Trustee for costs, losses and liabilities; (c) furnish to the Trustee all information reasonably requested by or otherwise required to be delivered to the Trustee; and (d) not act in a way which would give the Trustee a legal or contractual right to terminate the Trustee Agreement.
