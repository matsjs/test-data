Clause 1 - Interpretation The following definitions shall be amended in Clause 1.1 (Definitions):
The definition of "Margin" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to read: "Margin" means 8.25 percentage points per annum, with effectfrom and including 13 March 2017.
The definition of "Maturity Date" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions) shall be amended to read: "Maturity Date" means 13 June 2020. Any adjustment will he made according to the Business Day Convention.
