17.3
The Bond Trustee may refrain from convening a Holders' Meeting or instigating a Written
Procedure if (i) the suggested decision must be approved by any person in addition to the
Holders and such person has informed the Bond Trustee that an approval will not be given,
or (ii) the suggested decision is not in accordance with applicable laws.
