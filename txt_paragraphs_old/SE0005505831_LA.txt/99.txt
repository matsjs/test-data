18.7
Without amending or varying these Terms and Conditions, the Bond Trustee may prescribe
such further regulations regarding the convening and holding of a Holders' Meeting as the
Bond Trustee may deem appropriate. Such regulations may include a possibility for Holders
to vote without attending the meeting in person.
