14.
CONDITIONS SUBSEQUENT
Evidence, in form and substance satisfactory to the Bond Trustee, showing that:
(a) (to the extent not provided as a Condition Precedent for Disbursement) that the Existing Indebtedness has been fully repaid or acquired by the Group shall be provided by the Issuer to the Bond Trustee immediately after the Conditions Precedent for Disbursement have been fulfilled and the payments from the Escrow Account have been made, but not later than 5 calendar days after the Issue Date; and
(b) that all Existing Security has been released with no remaining obligations of the Group Companies shall be provided by the Issuer to the Bond Trustee as soon as possible after the Conditions Precedent for Disbursement have been fulfilled and the payments from the Escrow Account have been made, but not later than 21 calendar days after the Issue Date.
