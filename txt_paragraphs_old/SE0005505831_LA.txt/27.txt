2.1
The aggregate amount of the bond loan will be an amount of up to EUR 160,000,000 and
will be represented by Bonds, each of an initial nominal amount of EUR 100,000 or full
multiples thereof (the "Initial Nominal Amount"). The nominal amount of each Bond will
be the Initial Nominal Amount, less the aggregate amount by which each Bond has been
redeemed or prepaid in part pursuant to Clause 11 (Redemption and repurchase of Bonds)
(the "Nominal Amount"). The maximum total nominal amount of the Bonds is
EUR 160,000,000. All Bonds are issued on a fully paid basis at an issue price of 100.00
