8.3
The Bond Trustee shall only have to examine the face of a power of attorney or other proof
of authorisation that has been provided to it pursuant to Clause 8.2 and may assume that it
has been duly authorised, is valid, has not been revoked or superseded and that it is in full
force and effect, unless otherwise is apparent from its face.
