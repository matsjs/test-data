The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Initial Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 335,000,000 (U.S. Dollar three-hundred-and-thirty-six million), divided into (i) . Tranche A Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 250,000,000 (U.S. Dollar twohundred-and-fifty-million) (plus the principal amount of any Tap Bonds and any Additional Bonds) and (ii) Tranche B Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 85,000,000 (U.S. Dollar eighty-five-million) (plus the principal amount of any Additional Bonds).The Initial Bonds shall be issued at a price of 93 % of par value.
2.2.2
Tap Bonds The Issuer may resolve to issue Tap Bonds, without the approval of the Bondholders, in an aggregate principal amount of up to USD 50,000,000.
For the avoidance of doubt, existing Bondholders shall not be required to purchase any Tap Bonds, and purchasers of any Tap Bonds shall become Bondholders for the purpose of this Bond Agreement.
The Tap Bonds shall be offered to the Tranche A Bondholders on a first refusal basis.
2.2.3
General
The Face Value of the Bonds is USD 1 and the minimum subscription and allotment amount is USD 200,000 and integral multiples of USD 100,000 thereof. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
