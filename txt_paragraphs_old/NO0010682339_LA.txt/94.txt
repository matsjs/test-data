(ii) any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving splitting such member of the Project Group into two or more separate companies or entities,
if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(b) Upon any de-merger, sub-division or other reorganisation permitted pursuant to paragraph (a)(ii) above, the Obligors shall procure that any new entity formed in such event shall accede to this Bond Agreement and any other relevant Finance Document as a Party and Obligor according to an accession agreement in the form and substance acceptable to the Bond Trustee and the terms of this Bond Agreement, including but not limited to the restrictions, covenants and representations contained herein and including those which apply to the existing Issuer Subsidiaries, shall apply to such new entity in full.
(c) The Issuer and the Project Guarantor shall within 3 months of the Issue Date commence a merger with the Issuer as the surviving entity and which shall be completed as soon as reasonably possible thereafter, provided that:
(i) there will, in the Bond Trustee's sole opinion, be no adverse effect on the Bonds or the Second Lien Bonds; and
(ii) the Issuer will accede to all obligations of the Project Guarantor under the Security Documents (as relevant, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee).
16.5 Continuation of business No member of the Project Group shall cease to carry on its business.
