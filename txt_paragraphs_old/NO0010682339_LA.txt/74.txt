STATUS OF THE BONDS AND SECURITY
Status
The Bonds shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer. The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Issuer other than obligations which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt. The Bonds shall be secured on first priority (subject to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement) on certain assets of the Issuer and the Guarantors as set out in this Bond Agreement
9.2
Security
The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest, costs and expenses, shall be secured by the Security Interest.
10. INTEREST
10.1 Interest rate
(a) The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of fifteen per cent. (15%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate"). The accrued interest on the Outstanding Bonds up to and excluding 15 July 2015 shall be settled through the issuance of additional Bonds to the Bondholders (the "Additional Bonds") (for the avoidance of doubt, additional Tranche A Bonds shall be issued to the Tranche A Bondholders and additional Tranche B Bonds shall be Issued to the Tranche B Bondholders).
