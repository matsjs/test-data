REGISTRATION IN THE SECURITIES DEPOSITORY
Registration
The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall prior to disbursement be registered in the Securities Depository according to the Norwegian Securities Depository Act (Act 2002/64) and the terms and conditions of the Securities Depository. Principal and interest accrued will be credited the bondholders through the Securities Depository.
4.2
