"Pledge of Material Contract Receivables" means a first priority pledge (subject to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement) over any proceeds to be received by the Issuer or its Subsidiaries incorporated in Sweden from any material Project Documents with building contractors to the Issuer or its Subsidiaries incorporated in Sweden, to the extent permitted under the relevant agreement or otherwise agreed with the relevant counterparty.
"Prepayment Facility" means a prepayment facility of up to the equivalent amount of USD 50,000,000 with one or more banks or partners for the purpose of receiving prepayment from the relevant counterparty for consignments of iron ore concentrate in advance of the due date thereof.
"Project Budget" means the combined budget of operational and capital expenditure and trade payables associated with the Kaunisvaara Project which shall;
(a) in all events include a budget of costs related to the logistics chain through Narvik port, including, but not limited to any capital expenditures related to the terminal to be constructed at Pitkajarvi and required roads, railroads, buildings, machinery and equipment in relation thereto, a copy of which shall be delivered by the Issuer to the Bond Trustee on or prior to the Issue Date; and
(b) be reasonably detailed and include the timing of all operational and capital expenditures at least on a quarterly basis, forming the basis for the assessment of whether the Drawdown Conditions are met. In assessing the sources of cash, any operational cash flow estimated in the Drawdown Period shall at the time of drawdown be based on fair assessments and assumptions inter alia on prevailing FX rates, iron ore price and production profile.
"Project Construction Plan" means the detailed plan for the construction of the Kaunisvaara Project.
"Project Documents" means the material project agreements set out in Schedule 3.
"Project Group" means the Project Guarantor, the Issuer and the Issuer Subsidiaries.
"Put Option" has the meaning given to it in Clause 11.3.
