The Bond Trustee is entitled to require that the amount on each security document set out above shall be increased if the value of the relevant assets is, in the reasonable opinion of the Bond Trustee, significantly higher than the amount on the relevant security assets. This above right for the Bond Trustee to demand additional security and/or to increase the amount on any security document, shall not impose any obligation whatsoever on the Bond Trustee to make any enquiries or take any steps in order to verify the existence or value of any assets which are or may become subject to security.
