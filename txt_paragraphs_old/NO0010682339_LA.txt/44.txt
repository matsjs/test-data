The proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of fees, legal costs of the Manager and the Bond
Trustee and their advisors and any other costs and,expenses, including legal fees, in
relation to the Restructuring and Master Restructuring Term Sheet) shall be applied
towards (i) financing various costs, capital expenditure and working capital in connection
with the development of the Kaunisvaara Project, (ii) refinancing of the Trade Supplier
Liquidity Funding and/or the Commercial Creditor Short Term Funding and (iii) transfer of
funds as provided for in Permitted Upstream Advances (subject to Clause 16.11).
