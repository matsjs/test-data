15.3 Financial statements (a) The Issuer shall without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and Interim Accounts, and make them available on the Parent Guarantor's website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 180 days after the end of the financial year (for Financial Statements) and 90 days after the end for the relevant quarter (for Interim Accounts).
(b) The Parent Guarantor shall without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and Interim Accounts and make them available on the Parent Guarantor's website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 150 days after the end of the financial year (for Financial Statements) and 90 days after the end of the relevant quarter (for Interim Accounts).
