13.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Intra-Group Transactions All transactions between Group Companies shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions.
(b) Transactions with third parlies The Issuer shall procure that no Group Company may, without the prior written consent o f the Bond Trustee, engage directly or indirectly in any transaction with any third parly (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange o f assets or the rendering of any service), except pursuant to the reasonable requirement o f the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that arc not less favourable to the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(c) Transactions with shareholders, directors and affiliated companies The Issuer shall cause all transactions between any Group Company and (i) any shareholder thereof not part o f the Group, (ii) any director or senior member o f management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) or any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, to be entered on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Group Company than would have prevailed in arms' length transaction with a third party.
