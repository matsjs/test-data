Intercreditor Agreement .................
Pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement, the liens securing the Senior Secured Notes are first-priority liens that will rank equally with the liens that will secure certain future indebtedness. Certain future hedging obligations may be secured on a pari passu or senior basis to the Senior Secured Notes. The Collateral is secured by security documents for the benefit of holders of the Senior Secured Notes, counterparties to certain future hedging agreements and holders of certain other future indebtedness and obligations secured on a pari passu or junior basis, as permitted by the Indenture and the Intercreditor Agreement.
