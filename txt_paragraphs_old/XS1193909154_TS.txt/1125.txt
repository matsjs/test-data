13. PENSION COSTS AND PENSION OBLIGATIONS
Norske Skog has various pension schemes in accordance with local conditions and practices in the countries in which the group operates. A total of 2 872 current and former employees are covered by such schemes. Of these, 1 076 people are covered by defined benefit plans and 1 796 people by defined contribution plans.
