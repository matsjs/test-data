6. OPERATING SEGMENTS
Publication paper includes newsprint and magazine paper. Newsprint includes standard newsprint and other paper qualities used in newspapers, inserts, catalogs etc. Magazine paper includes the paper qualities super calendered (SC) and light weight coated (LWC). Magazine paper is used in magazines, catalogs and advertising materials.
Activities that are not part of the operating segments are included in other activities.
The comparative figures for 2013 have been restated in accordance with the new segment structure.
