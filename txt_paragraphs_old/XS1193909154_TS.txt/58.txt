The Senior Secured Notes may be treated as issued with original issue discount (``OID'') for U.S. federal income tax purposes. U.S. investors in the Senior Secured Notes will be subject to tax on OID (as ordinary income) as it accrues, in advance of the cash attributable to that income (and in addition to stated interest) if the Senior Secured Notes are treated as issued with OID for U.S. federal income tax purposes.
