Market Risk
a) Interest rate risk
The goal of interest rate risk management is to secure the lowest possible interest rate payments over time within acceptable risk limits. In the current challenging situation in the publication paper market, Norske Skog has secured most of the interest rate payments by primarily paying fixed interest rates on its loan obligations.
