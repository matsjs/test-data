NO DIRECT ACTIONS BY HOLDERS
A Holder may not take any action or take any legal steps whatsoever against the Issuer or with respect to the Transaction Security or Guarantees to enforce or recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate, support or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation (Sw.fdretagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (Sw. konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other jurisdiction) of the Issuer (or a Subsidiary) in relation to any of the liabilities of the Issuer under the Finance Documents.
