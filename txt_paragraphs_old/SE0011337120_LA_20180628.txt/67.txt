12.4 Mandatory partial prepayment
12.4.1
The Issuer shall ensure that an amount equal to any Excess Cashflow is paid into the Mandatory Prepayment Account within three (3) Business Days of delivery of a Financial Report pursuant to paragraph (a) or (b) of Clause 13.16.
12.4.2 Provided that any funds stand to the credit of the Mandatory Prepayment Account:
(a) on the date falling twenty-four (24) months after the Issue Date,
(b) on the date falling thirty (30) months after the Issue Date,
(c) on the date falling thirty-six (36) months after the Issue Date,
(d) on the date falling forty-two (42) months after the Issue Date,
