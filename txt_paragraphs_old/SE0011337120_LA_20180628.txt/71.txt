13.3 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer and the Subsidiaries have a right to incur Financial Indebtedness that constitute Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.4 Leasehold Rights (a) All Wind Turbines over which the Restricted Group has any Ownership Interests must be located on real property which is subject to Leasehold Rights in favour of a Restricted Group Company that has Ownership Interests over the relevant Wind Turbine.
(b) The terms and conditions of the Leasehold Agreements shall not be materially amended and any new Lease Agreements entered into with respect to new Leasehold Rights shall be on terms and conditions materially corresponding to the terms and conditions of the Lease Agreements existing on the Issue Date.
