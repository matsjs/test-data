(c) Financial Covenants
(i) Minimum Book Equity The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Book Equity o f minimum USD 200 million.
(ii) Value Adjusted Equity Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Value Adjusted Equity Ratio of 30% or higher.
(iii) Liquidity The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains Cash and Cash Equivalents plus available and unused commitments under any credit lines (in excess of 12 months) of the Group in an amount o f no less than USD 50,000,000.
The Issuer undertakes to comply with the above Financial Covenants at all times, such compliance to be certified by the Issuer with each annual financial statement and quarterly financial statement. All Financial Covenants shall be calculated on a consolidated basis for the Group during the term of the Bonds.
