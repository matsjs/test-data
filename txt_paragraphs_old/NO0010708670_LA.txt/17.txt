The Issuer has resolved to issue a series o f Bonds in the maximum amount o f NOK 700 000 000,- (Norwegian kroner seven hundred million).

The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Siem Offshore Inc. Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2014/2019".
The ISIN o f the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0708670.
The tenor o f the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.

The net proceeds from the Bonds shall be used for general corporate purposes.
