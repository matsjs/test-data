13.4.4 Financial Support restrictions
(a) Financial Support in connection with Permitted Financial Indebtedness;
(b) intra-group loans granted by the Issuer to the Parent:
(c) intra-group loans granted by the Issuer to any Subsidiary of the Issuer:
(d) short term trade credit given on normal terms in the ordinary course of business of the Issuer: and
(e) any existing loans made or to be made by the Issuer to its employees in relation to certain uncollected payroll taxes in relation to past exercises of share options under the Group's share option scheme, up to an aggregate amount of GBP 500,000.
