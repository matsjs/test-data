12.1 Maintenance Covenants
(a) The Maintenance Covenants are met if:
(i) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than 3.00;
(ii) the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 2.25; and
(iii) the Group holds Cash and Cash Equivalents of a minimum amount of USD 5,000,000.
(b) The Maintenance Covenants shall be tested on each Reference Date. However, the first test date for the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA and the minimum cash threshold as set out in Clause 12.1(a)(iii) shall be the first Reference Date falling 6 months after the Acquisition has been completed and the first test date for the Interest Coverage Ratio test shall be the first Reference Date falling 9 months after the Acquisition has been completed.
