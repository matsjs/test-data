16.1
The right to receive payment of the Nominal Amount shall be statute-barred and
become void ten (10) years from the Redemption Date. The right to receive payment of
interest shall be statute-barred and become void three (3) years from the relevant due
date for payment. The Company is entitled to any funds set aside for payments in
respect of which the Bondholders' right to receive payment has been statute-barred and
has become void.
