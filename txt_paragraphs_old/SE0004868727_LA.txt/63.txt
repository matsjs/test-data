14.3
When a new Issuing Agent has been appointed, the resigning Issuing Agent shall bear
no responsibility for acts or omissions during the time after the replacement of the
Issuing Agent. The Issuing Agent's successor, the Company and the Bondholders shall
have the same rights and obligations among themselves as they would have had if such
successor would have been the original Issuing Agent.
