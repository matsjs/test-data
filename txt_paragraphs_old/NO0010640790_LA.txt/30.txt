8.2 The Bonds shall be secured with a second priority pledge over 100% of the outstanding shares in NEH1 to be established by the Share Pledge Agreement. This share pledge shall have the same priority (on a pro rata basis) as the current pledge in the shares in NEHI securing the Old Bonds but be ranked below the bonds in NEC06 that shall have a preferential right and accordingly be ranked above the Bonds and the other Old Bonds with respect to such security.
9 Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 5 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrear on the Interest Payment Dates on a quarterly basis, the first Interest Payment Date being 2 July 2012.
