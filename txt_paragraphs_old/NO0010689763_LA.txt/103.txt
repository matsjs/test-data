Conditions Precedent for Subsequent Disbursements
Each and any release of funds from the Escrow Account after the First Disbursement shall only be used for the purposes as set out in Clause 2.3 (Purpose and utilization) or 2.4 (Other Capex Withdrawals) and be subject to conditions precedent as for the First Disbursement as set out in Clause 6.2.1(a) and (b) above. Each Release Notice from the Escrow Account to fund the Orlando and Kells Field Development Costs shall specify the details of the nature of such costs, and whether they are payable directly by the Issuer or through a "cash call" or joint interest billing invoice from the relevant operator.
Conditions Precedent for withdrawals from the BP Structured Energy Derivative Escrow Account
The Issuer may make withdrawals from the BP Structured Energy Derivative Escrow Account by issuing a BP Structured Energy Derivative Escrow Account Drawdown Notice to the Escrow Account Bank and the Bond Trustee, provided that the aggregate sum of such withdrawals is sufficient to terminate all of the outstanding Tranche 1 Call Options.
