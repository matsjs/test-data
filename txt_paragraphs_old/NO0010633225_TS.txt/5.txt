Rig: Deep Driller 1:
A Singapore flag Baker Marine Pacific Class 375 rig, constructed at PPL Shipyard in 2006.
Security:
All amounts outstanding under the Finance Documents, including but not limited to interests and expenses, shall be secured by:
Pre-Settlement Security:
(i) a first priority pledge over the Escrow Account (as defined below) (according to Norwegian law) (the "Escrow Account Pledge").
Pre-Disbursement:
(ii) a first priority mortgage over the Rig including all relevant equipment being legally part of the Rig under Singapore law (the "Mortgage"), (including a deed of covenants supplemental to the Mortgage and to the security thereby created between the Issuer and the Trustee);
(iii) a first priority assignment under Singapore law in any relevant insurances related to the Rig and the equipment related thereto (the "Assignment of Insurances");
