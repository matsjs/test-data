Pre-Disbursement:
Any release from the Escrow Account shall be subject to certain conditions precedent as customary for these types of transactions, including but not limited to:
a) a duly executed release notice from the Issuer;
b) satisfactory documentation evidencing that the amount to be released shall be applied in accordance with the Purpose of the Bond Issue;
c) no (potential) Event of Default has occurred and is continuing;
d) necessary corporate resolutions from the Issuer and the Guarantor to execute the Security Documents (unless delivered Pre-Settlement);
