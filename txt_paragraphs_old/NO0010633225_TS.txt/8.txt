Inter-creditor Agreement
The Central Bank of India ("CBI"), also representing other banks and financial institutions, have entered into loan facility agreements with Aban Offshore Limited, secured by inter alia second priority security rights to the Rig and the insurances related thereto (the "Second Priority Security") which is expected to remain following Issue Date. The Bond Trustee is to enter into a new inter-creditor agreement with CBI which will be (i) in all material respects on the same terms and conditions as the inter creditor agreement currently in force with CBI and (ii) to the satisfaction of the Bond Trustee.
Escrow Account: Earnings Account(s): Retention Account:
The Issuer shall prior to the Issue Date establish the Escrow Account and the net cash proceeds from the Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account in connection with the issuance of the Bonds. The Escrow Account shall be pledged in favor of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and blocked. The monies in the Escrow Account shall only be used according to the Purpose of the Bond Issue.
Before the release (all or nothing) from the Escrow Account takes place, all the Pre Disbursement Conditions Precedent (as described below) shall be complied with.
All earnings of the Issuer and all other net earnings related to the Rig and the Rig's Drilling Contract shall be paid directly from the relevant contracting party under the Drilling Contract to the Earnings Account of the Issuer.
The Earnings Account shall be pledged in favor of the Trustee (on behalf of the bondholders), but not blocked unless there is an Event of Default occurring.
The Issuer shall Pre-Disbursement establish the Retention Account where at any time cash and cash equivalents are to be deposited in accordance with the Issuer's Financial Covenant pertaining to Minimum Cash.
