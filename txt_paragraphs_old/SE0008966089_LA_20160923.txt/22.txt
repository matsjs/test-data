4.
CONDITIONS FOR DISBURSEMENT
4.1
The Issuer shall provide to the Agent, prior to the issuance of the Initial Bonds the
following, in form and substance satisfactory to the Agent:
(a) the Finance Documents duly executed by the Issuer and the Agent;
(b) a copy of a resolution from the board of directors of the Issuer approving the issue of the Initial Bonds, the terms of the Finance Documents, and resolving to enter into such documents and any other documents necessary in connection therewith;
