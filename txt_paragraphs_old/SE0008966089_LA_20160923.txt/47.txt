The Issuer may not make any dividend, whether in cash or in kind, unless such distribution is (i) effected in cash and (ii) permitted as per the following. The Issuer may, during each financial year, make dividend up to a portion of the Group's operating profits less tax and dividend on preference shares for the previous financial year as derived from its audited annual report, corresponding to fifty (50) per cent, if:
(a) the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 1.5x, and
(b) the Equity Ratio exceeds 25 per cent.
Notwithstanding what is stated in this Clause 11.3, the Issuer is not restricted to make dividend in respect of preference shares.
Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not incur (or agree to incur) or have outstanding any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer has a right to incur and have outstanding Financial Indebtedness that constitute Permitted Debt.
