13.5
If the Bondholders instruct the Agent to accelerate the Bonds, the Agent shall promptly
declare the Bonds due and payable and take such actions as may, in the opinion of the
Agent, be necessary or desirable to enforce the rights of the Bondholders under the
Finance Documents, unless the relevant Event of Default is no longer continuing.
