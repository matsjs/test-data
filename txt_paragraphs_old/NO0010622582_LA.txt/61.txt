18.6 Dispute resolution and legal venue
All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Bond Agreement between the Issuer, the Bond Trustee, and the Bondholders shall be exclusively resolved by the courts of Norway, with the District Court of Oslo as sole legal venue.
