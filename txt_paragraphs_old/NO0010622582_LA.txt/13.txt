2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount o f USD 36,100,000 (U.S. Dollar thirty-six million one hundred thousand).
The Bond Issue will be described as " 15 per cent Sevan Marine ASA Callable Senior Secured Bond Issue 2011/2012".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 062258.2.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity' Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used exclusively towards the working capital needs of the Issuer and its Subsidiaries:
(a) in an amount of up to USD 30,000,000 to pay necessary capital expenditures in relation to the upgrade project of the Voyageur FPSO, and
(b) the remainder to provide for working capital for continued operations of the Issuer.
