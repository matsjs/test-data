10.3 Mandatory Redemption
10.3.1
If the Issuer receives any dividend payment in relation to the Sevan Drilling Shares, the Issuer shall promptly upon receipt of the dividend payment use the amount of dividend so received in mandatory redemption of Bonds, at the redemption prices set out in Clause 10.2.1 (i) and (ii) above. On the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the relevant redemption amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any redeemed amount shall be applied first in the repayment of accrued interest and thereafter in the repayment of principal.
