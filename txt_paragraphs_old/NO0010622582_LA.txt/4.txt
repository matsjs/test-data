"Escrow Account Pledge" means an agreement whereby the Issuer pledges on a first priority basis to the Bond Trustee, on behalf of the Bondholders, the Escrow Account and any of the Issuer's claims against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account.
