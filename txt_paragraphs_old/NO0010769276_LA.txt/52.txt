14.12 New Market Loans
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will issue any Market Loans with a final maturity date prior to or on the Final Maturity Date.
15. Events of Default and Acceleration of the Bonds
Each of the events or circumstances set out in this Clause 15 (other than Clause 15.11 (Acceleration o f the Bonds) is an Event of Default.
