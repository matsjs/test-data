14.4 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, incur any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer and the Subsidiaries have a right to incur Financial Indebtedness that constitute Permitted Debt, if such Permitted Debt is incurred on market terms (or better).
14.5 Share Issue
The Issuer shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, issues any additional shares, other than any share issue in Mistral Energi AB provided that such share issue in Mistral Energi AB does not result in Hancap Facade AB ceases to own or control, directly or indirectly, 49 per cent. of the share capital or voting rights in Mistral Energi AB.
14.6 Disposal of Assets
