165
General
The Notes
The Notes will, upon issuance:
 be general senior obligations of the Issuer;
 be secured as set forth below under ``--Security'';
 rank pari passu in right of payment with any existing and future indebtedness of the Issuer that is not subordinated in right of payment to the Notes (including the guarantee by the Issuer of the Senior Credit Facilities and the Senior Secured Notes);
 rank senior in right of payment to any existing and future obligations of the Issuer that are expressly subordinated in right of payment to the Notes;
 be effectively subordinated to any existing and future secured Indebtedness of the Issuer and its Subsidiaries that is secured by Liens senior to the liens securing the Notes or secured by property and assets that do not secure the Notes, to the extent of the value of the property and assets securing such Indebtedness (including the Senior Secured Credit Facilities and the Senior Secured Notes);
