The Guarantees
On the Issue Date, the Notes will be Guaranteed by BidCo. During the Post-Completion Period, the Issuer shall procure that the Notes, subject to the Agreed Security Principles, are Guaranteed by each of the Subsidiaries of BidCo that Guarantees the Senior Secured Credit Facilities. As of the date of this Offering Memorandum, such Subsidiaries represent all of the direct: and indirectSubsidiaries of BidCo.
