Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none o f its Subsidiaries will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any loan or other indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to retain, provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security
11.4
Clean down period
The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year, there shall be a period of 5 consecutive calendar days during which the Working Capital Facility (for the avoidance of doubt, not including finance leases) shall be undrawn. At least 6 months shall elapse between two clean down periods.
