At a Holders' Meeting, the Issuer, the Holders (or the Holders' representatives/proxies) and
the Agent may attend along with each of their representatives, counsels and assistants.
Further, the directors of the board, the managing director and other officials of the Issuer and
the Issuer's auditors may attend the Holders' Meeting. The Holders' Meeting may decide
that further individuals may attend. If a representative/proxy shall attend the Holders'
Meeting instead of the Holder, the representative/proxy shall present a duly executed proxy
or other document establishing its authority to represent the Holder.
