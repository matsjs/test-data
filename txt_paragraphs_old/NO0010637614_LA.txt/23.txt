6.2.2
The Consideration Bonds in the VPS Escrow are held by the Bond Trustee as nominee for the relevant subscribers, and shall be delivered to the Issuers as settlement for Bonds in the Exchange Tranche settlement as provided for in Clause 6.4 below. The Existing Bonds in the VPS Escrow shall be excluded from the ordinary settlement of interest payment and repayment of principal on such Existing Bonds, and the Bond Trustee shall instruct the paying agent for the Existing Bonds that these are to be settled in the Exchange Tranche settlement.
