8.1 The payment obligations of the Issuers and the Guarantor under the Finance Documents (including but not limited to the Outstanding Bonds with accrued interest and expenses) shall constitute senior debt and rank at leastpari passu with the claims of other creditors except for obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law.
8.2 The payment obligations of the Issuers and the Guarantor under the Finance Documents (including the Outstanding Bonds with accrued but unpaid interest and expenses) shall be secured by the following Security Interests in favor of the Bond Trustee (acting on behalf of the Bondholders):
Pre release from Escrow Account:
(i) a first priority pledge under Norwegian law over the Escrow Account (the "Escrow Account Pledge").
Post release from Escrow Account:
(ii) a first priority mortgage under Singapore law over each of the Rigs including all relevant equipment being legally part of the Rigs (the "Mortgages"), also including a deed of covenants supplemental to the Mortgage and to the security thereby created between the Issuers and the Bond Trustee;
(iii) a first priority assignment under Singapore law in any insurances related to the Rigs and the equipment related thereto (the "Assignment of Insurances");
(iv) a first priority assignment from the relevant Issuer under Singapore law, of any Sub-Charter Agreements, (the "Sub-Charter Assignment");
