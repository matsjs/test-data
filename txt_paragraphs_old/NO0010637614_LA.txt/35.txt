10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall be repaid as follows:
10 % of the Loan Amount shall be repaid on the Interest Payment Day in March 2014
- The remaining Outstanding Bonds shall be repaid at Final Maturity Date
All repayments shall be made at 100% of Par Value plus accrued interest. Payment shall be made on the subsequent Banking Day if the relevant Interest Payment Date is not a Banking Day.
