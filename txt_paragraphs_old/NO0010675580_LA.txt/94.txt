13.8 Charterer and covenants The Issuer shall procure that the Charterer and the Operator comply with the covenants in this Clause 13.8. Non-compliance by the Charterer and /or the Operator of these covenants shall constitute an Event of Default under this Bond Agreement.
(a) Mergers
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Charterer with any other companies or entities.
(b) De-mergers
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) carry out any de merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Charterer into two or more separate companies or entities.
