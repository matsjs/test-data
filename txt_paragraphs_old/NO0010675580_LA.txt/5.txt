1.1 Definitions In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account and the Debt Service Account.
"Additional Equity" means an additional amount of equity raised by the Ultimate Parent of no less than USD 80,000,000 in gross proceeds to, among other purposes, ensure a full financing of the Rig upon delivery from the Yard.
"Agent" means ABN AMRO Bank N.V., acting as agent under the Senior Bank Facility and the Permitted Bridge Loan Facility.
"Assignment of Beta Bareboat Charter" means the second priority, after the First Priority Facilities, assignment of the Beta Bareboat Charter (including step-in rights) under English law. The Issuer shall give notices of and obtain consents and acknowledgements of such assignment from the Charterer.
"Assignment of Earnings" means the second priority, after the First Priority Facilities, assignment of earnings and receivables under the Beta Bareboat Charter (including all security granted by the Charterer thereunder) under English law. The Issuer shall give notices of and obtain consents and acknowledgements of such assignment from the Charterer.
"Assignment of Earnings under the Petrobras Contracts" means the second priority, after the First Priority Facilities, assignment of the earnings of the Charterer and the Operator, respectively, under the Petrobras Contracts (including all security granted by Petrobras thereunder) under Brazilian law, subject to a consent and acknowledgement of such assignment from Petrobras which the Charterer and the Operator, respectively, shall use its best efforts to obtain.
