(u) Permitted Distributions
The Issuer shall ensure that the Permitted Distributions shall be subject to full subordination to the obligations of the Issuer under the Bonds pursuant to a subordination and turnover agreement with the Bond Trustee, save for dividend payments as described in paragraph (c) in the definition Permitted Distributions. The Permitted Distributions made pursuant to paragraph (a) in the definition Permitted Distributions shall be made at least on a monthly basis.
(v) Subordinated Loans
(i) The Issuer shall ensure that any Subordinated Loan shall, inter alia, (i) be fully subordinated to the Bonds subject to a subordination and turnover agreement with the Bond Trustee, (ii) have a maturity date later than the Maturity Date, and (iii) have any accrued interest accumulated and added to the principal of such loan facility during the term of the Bonds.
