13.5 Rig and Insurance covenants (a) Maintenance
From the Delivery Date, the Issuer shall keep or shall procure that the Rig 1s kept in good, safe and efficient condition and state of repair consistent with prudent ownership and appropriate industry standards.
(b) Insurance
(i) From the Delivery Date, the issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of insurance of the Rig and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times, hereunder to retain the Rig in class.
(ii) From the Delivery Date, the Issuer shall procure during the operations of the Rig that the Charterer runs proper maintenance of the Rig according to a planned maintenance system.
