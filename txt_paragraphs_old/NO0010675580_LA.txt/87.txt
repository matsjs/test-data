(l) Negative pledge
The Ultimate Parent shall ensure that no security is created or permitted to exist over any of the shares issued by or the assets of the Parent (other than assets which are not subject to Security pursuant to the First Priority Facilities and the Bond Issue), the Issuer, the Rig Manager, BassDrill International Ltd., the Operator and the Charterer or any guarantee or indemnity (or other arrangement with similar effect) for any Financial Indebtedness is provided by any of the foregoing, other than the Security and guarantees under the First Priority Facilities and the Bond Issue.
(m) No distributions
(i) The Ultimate Parent shall not, and shall procure no member of the Group will, demand any dividend payments or other distributions from the Issuer other than (i) Permitted Distributions, (ii) repayments of any Permitted Shareholder Loan, as long as no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing, and (iii) repayments to the Charterer and the Operator of any loan pursuant to the Internal Credit Facility.
(ii) The Ultimate Parent shall ensure that any dividends paid by the Issuer to the Parent pursuant to paragraph (c) of the Permitted Distributions definition is made by creating a claim of the Parent against the Issuer and that such claim constitutes a Subordinated Loan from the Parent to the Issuer.
