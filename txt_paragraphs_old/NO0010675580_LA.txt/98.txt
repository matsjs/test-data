(l) Application of Earnings
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) change the order of payments pursuant to Clause 13.3(aa).
(m) No Financial support
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance to any party other than the (i) Guarantee and the Security granted by it as security for the First Priority Facilities and the Bond Issue, (ii) guarantees issued for the benefit of third parties in the ordinary course of business (including guarantees relating to employment of the Rig), and (iii) loans pursuant to the Internal Credit Facility.
(n) No Financial indebtedness
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) incur or permit to remain outstanding, any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than the Financial Indebtedness arising under (i) any Subordinated Loans, (ii) any loan pursuant to the internal Credit Facility and (iii) guarantees permitted under Clause 13.8(m)(ii).
(o) Negative pledge
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) create or permit to subsist any security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for (i) any Security granted as security for the First Priority Facilities and the Bond Issue or (ii) any lien or security arising by operation of law or (iii) any lien or security over an asset which has been provided on retention of title terms in the ordinary course of business or operation of the Rig.
