(c) De-mergers
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that the Issuer does not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that the Parent does not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Parent into two or more separate companies or entities, if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(d) Disposal of business
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that the issuer does not sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets (including but not limited to the Rig) or operations in connection with a sale to a third party or an internal reorganization, unless the Bonds are redeemed in full in accordance with Clause 10.4.
(e) Arm's length transactions
The Ultimate Parent shall ensure that any transaction with any direct or indirect shareholder of the Ultimate Parent or with any Group Company shall be on arms' length terms and for fair market value.
