(d) Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(e) Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets (including, but not limited to, the Rig) or operations, unless the obligations under the Senior Bank Facility are repaid in full and the Bonds are redeemed in full in accordance with Clause 10.4.
(f) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not enter into any transaction with any person, including any direct or indirect shareholder of the Parent or with any Group Company except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
