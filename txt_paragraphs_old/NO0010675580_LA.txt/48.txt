8.3 The Bondholders rights under the Finance Documents will also be governed by (i) the Intercreditor Agreement to be entered into between, amongst others, the Issuer, the Parent, the Agent and the Bond Trustee. 8.4 The Bond Trustee shall be authorized to enter into an Intercreditor Agreement reasonably satisfactory to the Bond Trustee, containing, inter alia, (I) a 270 day standstill period on enforcing the Bond Issue or take any other actions against the Obligors, plus an extension if the Agent is diligently pursuing an enforcement action and (ii) an option for the Bondholders to purchase first priority debt within 90 days from the Agent's notice that it is accelerating the Senior Bank Facility, Further, such Intercreditor Agreement may allow the lenders under the Senior Bank Facility, following a default under the Senior Bank Facility, to defer amortization payments and/or to increase the maximum aggregated amount by USD 15,000,000 in aggregate; provided, however, the aggregate outstanding amount of the Senior Bank Facility and the Permitted Bridge Loan Facility shall not exceed USD 150,000,000 (adjusted for the Bank Instalments).
