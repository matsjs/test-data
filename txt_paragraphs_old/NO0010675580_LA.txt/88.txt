13.7 Parent covenants The Issuer shall procure that the Parent complies with the covenants in this Clause 13.7. Non-compliance by the Parent of these covenants shall constitute an Event of Default under this Bond Agreement.
(a) Annual audited reports
The Parent shall prepare unconsolidated annual audited reports and unaudited quarterly reports and make such reports available on its website in the English language as soon as they become available, and no later than 120 days after the end of the financial year and not later than 90 days after the end of the relevant quarter, provided always that such preparation and publication of reports are in accordance with applicable rules and regulations. Such reports shall be prepared in accordance with IFRS, and include a profit and bss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement, all notes and management commentary or report from the Board of Directors for the Parent.
(b) Mergers
(i) The Parent shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Parent with any other companies or entities if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
