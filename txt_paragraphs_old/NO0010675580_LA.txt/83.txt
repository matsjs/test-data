13.6 Ultimate Parent covenants The Issuer shall procure that the Ultimate Parent complies with the covenants in this Clause 13.6. Non-compliance by the Ultimate Parent of these covenants shall consitute an Event of Default under this Bond Agreement.
(a) Annual audited reports
The Ultimate Parent shall prepare consolidated and unconsolidated annual audited reports and consolidated and consolidating unaudited quarterly reports and make such reports available on its website in the English language as soon as they become available, and no later than 120 days after the end of the financial year and not later than 90 days after the end of the relevant quarter, provided always that such preparation and publication of reports are in accordance with applicable rules and regulations. Such reports shall be prepared in accordance with US GAAP, and include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement, all notes and management commentary or report from the Board of Directors for each of the Ultimate Parent, the Parent and the Issuer.
(b) Mergers
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that the Issuer does not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities.
