10.4.6
The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing (i) immediately about any Mandatory Prepayment Event or any Total Loss Event and (ii) at least ten Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption according to this mandatory redemption. On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date of such mandatory redemption.
10.4.7 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.

Covenant to pay
