(ee) No distribution
The Issuer shall not during the term of the Bonds, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, service any Subordinated Loans under which the Issuer is a borrower or make any other distribution to the Parent or its shareholder or any other member of the Group (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any share issued by any party) other than the Permitted Distributions, repayments of any Permitted Shareholder Loan, repayments of the Permitted Bridge Loan Facility, and/or repayments of any loan under which the Issuer is a borrower pursuant to the Internal Credit Facility.
(ff) Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not create or permit to subsist any security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect other than (i) the Security under the First Priority Facilities and the Bond Issue, (ii) as permitted as Security for the Permitted Bridge Loan Facility pursuant to this Bond Agreement, or (iii) any lien or security arising by operation of law.
