0) Transactions
The Charterer shall not (and shall procure that the Operator does not) engage, directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Charterer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Charterer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(k) Accounts
The Charterer shall upon the request of the Bond Trustee facilitate a review of its accounts by an auditor appointed by the Bond Trustee at the Issuer's cost and have satisfactory control systems in place which may also be subject for review. Such requests shall not be made more than once every twelve months, save that it may be requested at any time following the occurrence of an Event of Default.
