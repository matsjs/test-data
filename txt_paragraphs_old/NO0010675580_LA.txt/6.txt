"Assignment of Earnings" means the second priority, after the First Priority Facilities, assignment of earnings and receivables under the Beta Bareboat Charter (including all security granted by the Charterer thereunder) under English law. The Issuer shall give notices of and obtain consents and acknowledgements of such assignment from the Charterer.
"Assignment of insurances" means the second priority, after the First Priority Facilities, assignment of Insurances (including third party liability insurance) under English law. The issuer shall give notices of and obtain consents and acknowledgements of such assignment from the insurers.
"Assignments of Internal Credit Facility" means the Issuer Assignment of internal Credit Facility, the Charterer Assignment of Internal Credit Facility and the Operator Assignment of Internal Credit Facility.
