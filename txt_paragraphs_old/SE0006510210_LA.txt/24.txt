7.
RIGHT TO ACT ON BEHALF OF A HOLDER
7.1
If any Person other than a Holder wishes to exercise any rights under these Terms and
Conditions, it must obtain a power of attorney (or, if applicable, a coherent chain of powers
of attorney), a certificate from the authorised nominee or other sufficient proof of
authorisation for such Person.
