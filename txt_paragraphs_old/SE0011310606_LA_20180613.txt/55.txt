6.2
Those who according to assignment, Encumbrance, the provisions of the Swedish
Children and Parents Code (Sw. foraldrabalken (1949:381)), conditions of will or deed
of gift or otherwise have acquired a right to receive payments in respect of a Bond shall
register their entitlements to receive payment in accordance with the Financial
Instruments Accounts Act.
