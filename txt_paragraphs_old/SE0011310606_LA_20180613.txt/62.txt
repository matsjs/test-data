8.2

or any other payment shall be deposited in a certain bank account, such deposits will
be effected by the CSD on the relevant payment date. In other cases, payments will be
transferred by the CSD to the Bondholder at the address registered with the CSD on
the Record Date. Should the CSD, due to a delay on behalf of the Issuer or some other
