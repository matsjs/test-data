14.2 Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if the Leverage Ratio (adjusted in accordance with Clause 14.4 (Calculation Adjustments)) does not exceed 3.00:1 for the relevant test period.
14.3 Calculation of Leverage Ratio
Leverage Ratio shall be calculated as follows:
(a) the calculation shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than two (2) months prior to the payment of the relevant Restricted Payment; and
(b) the amount of Net Debt shall be measured on the relevant testing date so determined, but be increased by any Restricted Payment for which the Leverage Ratio is tested.
