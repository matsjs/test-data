For the purposes of a second Bondholders' Meeting or second Written Procedure pursuant to this Clause 17.10, the date of request of the second Bondholders' Meeting pursuant to Clause 18.1 or second Written Procedure pursuant to Clause 19.1, as the case may be, shall be deemed to be the relevant date when the quorum did not exist.
