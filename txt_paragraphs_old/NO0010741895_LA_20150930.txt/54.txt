13.3.4 Continuation o f business
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that each o f the Material Subsidiaries shall not cease to carry out its business. Further, the Issuer shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of the business o f the Group from that carried on at the date o f this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
