Issuer's acquisition of Bonds
The Issuer and any Group Company have the right to acquire and own Bonds (Issuer's Bonds). The Issuer's or any Guarantors' holding of Bonds shall without undue delay be cancelled, and a Guarantor shall, if required by the Securities Depository for the purposes of such cancellation, without undue delay transfer such Bond to the Issuer for cancellation.
