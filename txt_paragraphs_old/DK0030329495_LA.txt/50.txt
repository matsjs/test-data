Status of the Bonds, Guarantees and Security Interests
8.1 The Bonds shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer secured by the Security Interests. The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu without any preference among themselves with all other obligations of the Issuer (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt, subject to the priority granted to the RCF Lenders and certain hedging counterparties pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement.
8.2 The Guarantees shall constitute senior debt obligations of the relevant Guarantor secured by the Security Interests of the relevant Guarantor. The Guarantees shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the relevant Guarantor (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt, subject to the priority granted to the RCF Lenders and certain hedging counterparties pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement.
