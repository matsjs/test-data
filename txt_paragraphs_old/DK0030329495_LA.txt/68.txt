(i)
first, in or towards payment of any unpaid fees, costs and expenses of the
Bond Trustee under the Finance Documents;
(ii) secondly, in or towards payment of any accrued interest due but unpaid under the Bond Agreement, pro rata and without any preference or priority of any kind; and
