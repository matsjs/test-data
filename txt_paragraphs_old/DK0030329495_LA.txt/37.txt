2. 2 The Bonds
2.2.1 On the Issue Date, the Issuer has resolved to issue Bonds in the amount of DKK 900,000,000 (Danish kroner nine-hundred-million).
The denomination of each Bond is DKK 750,000 (seven-hundred-and-fifty-thousand Danish kroner) and multiples ofDKK 750,000 thereof. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between each other and without any preference between each other.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN /RX per cent AX IV EG Holding III ApS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2020".
