7.1.1.4 Non-conflict with other obligations
The entry into and performance by it of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document to which it is a party and the transactions contemplated thereby do not and will not conflict with (i) any applicable law or regulation or judicial or official order; (ii) its constitutional documents; or (iii) any agreement or instrument which is binding upon it or any of its assets.
7.1.1.5 No Event ofDefault
(i)
No Event of Default exists or is likely to result from the making of any
drawdown under this Bond Agreement or the entry into, the performance of,
or any transaction contemplated by, any Finance Document.
