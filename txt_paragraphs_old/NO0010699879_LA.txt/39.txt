10 Maturity of the Loan and Call Option
10.2 The Borrower may redeem parts of the Loan or the entire Loan as follows (Call Option): (i) at (European option) any time prior to 30 June 2016 at 101.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
Should the Borrower exercise its Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed of this (the Bondholders in writing via VPS) no later than 30 - thirty Banking Days before the date of redemption.
Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.

10.3 The Borrower may exercise its Extension Option with minimum 30 Business Days' notice to the Loan Trustee. The Extension Option may be exercised until the earliest of (i) the Bank Refinancing Date and (ii) 30 June 2016.
Upon exercise of the Extension Option the Margin shall be amended to 8.50 percentage points. At the end of every 12 month period as from the effective date of the exercised Extension Option to the date which the Bonds have been repaid, the Margin shall be increased by 0.50 percentage points.
