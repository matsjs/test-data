Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
NIBD:
(on a consolidated basis) the Group's interest-bearing debt minus Cash and Cash Equivalents.
NIBD to Vessel Value Ratio:
the ratio of NIBD to Vessel Value.
Vessel Value:
the charter free fair market value of the vessels of the Group as determined within the past 12 months by an independent broker reasonably acceptable to the Trustee
Outstanding Loan:
Remaining Loan less Borrower's Bonds.
