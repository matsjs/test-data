1 Definitions
American Tanker, Inc:
an indirect wholly owned subsidiary of the Borrower (formerly named Aker American Shipping, Inc.).
Aker Philadelphia Shipyard, Inc:
a wholly owned subsidiary of the Aker Philadelphia Shipyard ASA.
Banking Day:
a day when (i) the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions and (ii) commercial banks are open for business in New York.
Bank Refinancing Date: BNPP Credit Agreement:
the date the loans advanced under the BNPP Credit Agreement are repaid in full.
