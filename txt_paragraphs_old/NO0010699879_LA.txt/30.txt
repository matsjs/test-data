2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower issued on 27 February 2007 a series of Bonds in the initial amount of NOK 700,000,000 (Norwegian krone seven hundred million), which was converted into a USD bond issue pursuant to the First Amendment and Restatement Agreement, such conversion undertaken based on the principles set out in the First Amendment and Restatement Agreement and announced by the Loan Trustee on or about the date hereof. Such converted amount herein referred to as "the Loan". The payment of interest may partly be paid as additional Bonds (Payment-in-Kind, cf Clause 9.5), which shall be deemed to constitute a part of the Loan.
