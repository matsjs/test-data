"Group Loans" means any loans or loan guarantees provided by the Issuer or any of the Holding Companies to another Group Company not being the Issuer or any of the Holding Companies.
"Holding Companies" means all the wholly owned underlying administrative service companies and holding companies that carry only investments, cash, and debt in the balance sheet as reported in the financial statements of the Issuer.
"NAV" means the net asset value in accordance with GAAP on a consolidated basis for the Issuer and the Holding Companies.
"Subordinated Loans" means any loan with a term extending beyond the final maturity date of the Bonds which are contractually unsecured and subordinated to any outstanding amount under the Bonds or any other senior debt of the Issuer and that contains repayment provisions to the effect repayment of principal or payment of interest shall not be made during the term of the Bonds, always provided that subordinated loans granted by a company in the Aker group to the Issuer after the issue date for the bond issue with ISIN N0001056168.1 (being 17 December 2009) shall not be deemed to be a "Subordinated Loan". In this sub-clause, "group" shall have the meaning as defined in the Norwegian Public Companies Act § 1-3.
"Total Debt" means the Issuer's debt or other financial instrument that has the commercial effect of a borrowing, including guarantees for loans but excluding Subordinated Loans.
"Total Equity" means the booked equity of the Issuer in accordance with GAAP, but excluding booked goodwill acquired after the date of this Bond Agreement (however, if a change in GAAP applicable for the treatment of pensions or derivative transactions occurs, any adverse effects on the accounts shall be excluded when determining Total Equity).
