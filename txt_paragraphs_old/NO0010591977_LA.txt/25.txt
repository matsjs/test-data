10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows
(Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including Interest Payment Date in November 2013 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in November 2014 at 104 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, and
(ii) at any time from and included Interest Payment Date in November 2014 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 102 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
