2.1 Binding nature o f the Bund Agreement
2.1.2
The Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds.

2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue the Bonds in the maximum aggregate amount of NOK 600,000,000 (Norwegian kroner six hundred million).
The Fixed Tranche consists of a series of Bonds at an amount of NOK 275,000,000 (Norwegian kroner twohundredandseventyfivemillion), with denominations o f NOK 1.00 each whereas the FRN Tranche consists of a series o f Bonds at an amount of NOK 325,000,000 (Norwegian kroner threehundredandtwentyfivemillion), with denominations of NOK 1.00.
2.2.2. The Tranches will rank pari passu between each other, and the Bonds under each Tranche will rank pari passu between each other.
