Fixed Rate
9.2 Interest FRN Tranche
9.2.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the FRN Tranche from and including the Issue Date at the Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
9.2.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being on 27 July 2011.
9.2.3
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
9.2.4
The day count convention in respect of the calculation of the payable interest amount shall be "Actual/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in which payment is being made is divided by 360 (the "Day Count Factor").
