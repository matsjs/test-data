10.3 10.3.1
Change o f control
Upon the occurrence o f a Change o f Control, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price o f 100 % of par plus accrued interest.
10.3.2 The Put Option must be exercised within sixty (60) days after the Issuer has notified the Bondholders of a Change o f Control. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control has taken place.
