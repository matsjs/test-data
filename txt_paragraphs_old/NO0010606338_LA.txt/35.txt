10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds (all or nothing) as follows (the "Call Option"):
(i) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2013 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2014 at a price equal to 106 % o f par value, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount;
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2014 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2015 at 104 % o f par value, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount; and
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2015 to, but not including, the Maturity Date at 102 % o f par value, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.
