(c) Cross default If for the Issuer or any Group Company: (i) any financial indebtedness or guarantee is not paid when due nor within any
originally applicable grace period (or, if there is no applicable grace period, within five (5) Business Days of its due date),
(ii) any financial indebtedness is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described),
(iii) any commitment for any financial indebtedness is cancelled or suspended by a creditor as a result of an event of default (however described), or
(iv) any creditor becomes entitled to declare any financial indebtedness due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described),
provided always that the aggregate amount of the financial indebtedness or guarantee (or commitment for any financial indebtedness) falling within paragraphs (i) to (iv) above for the Issuer and/or Group Company exceeds a total of NOK 10 million (or the equivalent amount in any other currency or currencies). For the avoidance of doubt, this paragraph (c) does not apply to any Financial Indebtedness o f any Group Company to another Group Company.
