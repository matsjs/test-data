The Issuer shall ensure that the Group at any time maintains (i) Book Equity of minimum USD 125,000,000 and (ii) an Equity Ratio of minimum 30.00%.

The Issuer shall ensure that the Group at any time maintains a minimum o f Cash and Cash Equivalents o f USD 15,000,000.
