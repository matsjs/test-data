Financial Covenants (a) The Issuer undertakes to comply with the following financial covenants during the
term of the Bond Issue:
(i) Minimum Liquidity: Starting on the DSRA Termination Date, the Issuer shall maintain a Liquidity of an amount equal to a minimum of 6 months Floating Rate from the DSRA Termination Date (calculated by reference to the prevailing Floating Rate at the start of each 6 months period); and
(ii) Loan-to-Value Ratio: The Issuer shall ensure that the Loan-to-Value Ratio does not exceed 65.00 per cent.
(b) The Issuer undertakes to comply with the above Financial Covenants at all times (except for the covenant in Clause 13.5(a)(i), which shall apply at all times from the DSRA Termination Date) during the term of the Bond Issue, such compliance to be measured on each Quarter Date (except for the Loan-to-Value Ratio which shall be measured on an annual basis) and certified by the Issuer with each annual financial statement and quarterly financial statement on the respective Reporting Date.
