The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities.
De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities, other than in connection with a Permitted Partial Disposal.
13.3.5
Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature or scope of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement or as contemplated by the Bond Agreement.
13.3.6
Disposals (a) The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets
or operations other than a Permitted Partial Disposal.
