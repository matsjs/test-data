2.2.1
The Bonds The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
2.2.3 The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Digiplex Fet AS Senior Secured Bond Issue 2014/2019".
2.2.4 The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0712870.
2.2.5 The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
