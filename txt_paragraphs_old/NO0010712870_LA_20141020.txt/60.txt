10.4 10.4.1
Upon the occurrence of a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall immediately upon receipt of insurance proceeds and in all circumstances no later than 180 days after the date on which the Total Loss Event occurred, redeem the Bonds at 100 per cent, of par value (plus accrued interest).
10.4.2
If the Bonds are redeemed according to Clause 10.3 (Mandatory Prepayment) or Clause 10.4 (Total Loss Prepayment), the entire amount in the Accounts, together with any insurance proceeds, may be applied to prepay the Bonds.
10.5 10.5.1
Change of control
Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at a price of 101 per cent, of par plus accrued interest on redeemed Bonds.
