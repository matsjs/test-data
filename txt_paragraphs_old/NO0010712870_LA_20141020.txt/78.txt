Earnings Account (a) The Issuer shall procure that Evry pays all amounts due under the Evry Lease
Agreement directly into the Earnings Account.
(b) To the extent other lease agreements than the Evry Lease Agreement are entered into by the Issuer, the Issuer shall procure that each lessee pays all amounts due under the relevant lease agreements directly into the Earnings Account.
(c) The Earnings Account shall be pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), but not blocked. The Account Bank shall waive any set-off rights in respect of the Earnings Account.
(d) Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default which is continuing, the Earnings Account shall be blocked and the Issuer shall no longer be entitled to make any withdrawals from the Earnings Account.
13.4.4 Escrow Account:
(a) The Escrow Account shall be pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to the Escrow Account Pledge and blocked so that no withdrawals can be made from such account without the Bond Trustee's prior written consent pursuant to Clause 6 (Conditions Precedent). The Account Bank shall waive any set off rights to such account.
(b) The Bond Trustee shall have free access to information from the Account Bank of the bank balance on the Escrow Account.
