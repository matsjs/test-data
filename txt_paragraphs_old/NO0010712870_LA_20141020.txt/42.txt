In the event of non-compliance with the Final Disbursement Test, the remaining funds held in the Escrow Account shall be used to prepay the Bonds (on a pro-rata basis) at 100 per cent, of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds) by no later than 5 Business Days after the Construction Completion Date.
6.7 Non-Compliance Notice
If the Agent has provided a Non-Compliance Notice to the Bond Trustee pursuant to the terms of the Agent Agreement, no funds shall be released from the Escrow Account until the Agent has confirmed to the Bond Trustee that the Issuer has complied with the terms of Clause 13.3.15 (Financing of Cost Overruns and Variation Orders) and Clause 13.3.16 (Additional funding of Debt Service Retention Account upon Delay(s)).
6.8 Waiver of conditions precedent The Bond Trustee may, in its sole discretion, waive the deadline or requirements for documentation as set out in this Clause 6 (Conditions Precedent).
