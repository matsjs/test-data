13.3.15 Financing of Cost Overruns and Variation Orders
(a) If the Issuer incurs a Project Cost that constitutes a Cost Overrun or a Variation Order, the Issuer shall fund the amount of such Cost Overrun (the "Cost Overrun Amount") or Variation Order (the "Variation Order Amount") with the proceeds from new equity contributions or new Subordinated Loans, unless the Issuer promptly can demonstrate to the Agent that it has funds already available to it from operations and/or applying available funds on the Escrow Account (including the Surplus Funds) or otherwise which in aggregate is sufficient to complete the Project after taking into account the relevant Cost Overrun and/or Variation Order.
