"Margin" means:
(a) from and including the Issue Date until but excluding the First Interest Payment Date following the Evry Acceptance Date, 4.00 per cent, per annum; and
(b) from and including the first Interest Payment Date following the Evry Acceptance Date, 3.75 per cent, per annum.
"Market Value" means the value of the Property as determined by an independent appraiser appointed by the Issuer and acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
"Material Adverse Effect" means an event or circumstance which has a material adverse effect on:
(a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Issuer;
(b) the Issuer's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents; or
(c) the validity or enforceability of any Finance Document.
