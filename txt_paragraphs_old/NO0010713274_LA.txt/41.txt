(f) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not engage in, or permit any member of the Group to engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Group's business (which also includes or shall include investments and/or transactions in vessels constructed by any Group Company) and upon fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to the Issuer or such member of the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction for the relevant assets at the time.
(g) Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
(h) Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies shall, carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all aspects and comply in all respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time. Breach of these obligations shall be regarded as non-compliance only if such breach would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.4 Special covenants
(a) Listing
In the event of any initial public offering of the Issuer's shares after which such shares shall be quoted, listed, traded or otherwise admitted to trading on a stock exchange or regulated market (an "Equity Listing Event"), the Issuer shall ensure that the shares in the Issuer remain listed on such stock exchange or regulated market for the remaining term of the Bond Issue. Hereunder, the Issuer is currently contemplating an Equity Listing Event on the Oslo Stock Exchange, and if completed, the Issuer shall ensure that the shares in the Issuer remain listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange during the term of the Bond Issue.
