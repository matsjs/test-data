13.3.7 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation without the prior written consent of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), such consent not to be unreasonably withheld.
13.3.8 Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each Group Company and the Parent Guarantor shall, perform its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
