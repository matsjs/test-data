13.3.5 Disposal o f business
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
(i)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions
customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect,
(iii) but provided that this Clause shall not apply to any of the matters permitted in Clause 13.4.8 below.
