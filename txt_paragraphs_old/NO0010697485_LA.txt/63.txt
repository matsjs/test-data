13.4.2 Dividends restrictions
Any un-utilized portion of the permitted dividend pursuant to the above in 2016 (on the basis of the annual accounts for 2015) and onwards may be carried forward to any subsequent financial year.
Notwithstanding the above, in any year the Issuer may pay to the Parent Guarantor a fee for services rendered as invoiced, up to an amount of USD 500,000.
13.4.3 Financial Indebtedness restrictions
Neither the Issuer nor the Parent Guarantor shall, and they shall procure that no Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (including guarantees), other than;
(i) the Senior Bank Facilities (and the guarantees thereunder as permitted in this Bond Agreement);
(ii) the Junior Debt Facilities (and the guarantees thereunder as permitted in this Bond Agreement);
(iii) any Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Finance Documents;
