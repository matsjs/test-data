2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 400,000,000 (U.S. Dollar four hundred million only).
The Face Value is USD 100,000 each with a minimum subscription and allotment amount of USD 200,000 and integral multiples of USD 100,000 thereof. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "10 per cent Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2013/2019".
