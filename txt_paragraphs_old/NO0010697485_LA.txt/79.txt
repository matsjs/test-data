16.1.7 Creditors 'process
Any Obligor has a substantial proportion of the assets impounded, confiscated, attached or subject to distraint, or is subject to enforcement of any Security over any of its assets, and such action is not is discharged and/or lifted within ten (10) Business Days.
16.1.8 Impossibility or illegality
It is or becomes impossible or unlawful for any Obligor to fulfil or perform any of the terms of any Finance Document to which it is a party.
