13.4 Special covenants
13.4.1 Negative pledge
Neither the Issuer nor the Parent Guarantor shall, and the Issuer shall ensure that no Group Company shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance over any of its present or future respective assets (including, but not limited to, the shares in the Subsidiaries) or its revenues, other than the encumbrances granted to secure any of the following:
(i)
the Senior Bank Facilities;
(ii) the Junior Debt Facilities;
(iii) any derivative hedging related to the Group's hedging policy other than any hedging arrangements entered into for speculative purposes, entered into with any of the Senior Finance Parties or any of the Junior Finance Parties;
(iv) obligations incurred by a Subsidiary of the Issuer towards its trade creditors (inter alia suppliers, shipyards and other counter parties) in the ordinary course of business and as part of the normal operations of such Subsidiary;
