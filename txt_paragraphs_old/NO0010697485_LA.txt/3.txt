"Attachment" means an attachment to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Escrow Account" means an escrow account in the name of the Issuer, with the Securities Depository, subject to the Bond Escrow Account Pledge and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Bond Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Bond Escrow Account and any securities credited to the Bond Escrow Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the holders of the Temporary Bonds).
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bond Guarantees" means the Bond Parent Guarantee and the Bond Subsidiary Guarantees.
"Bond Parent Guarantee" means an on-demand guarantee from the Parent Guarantor in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) guaranteeing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Bond Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Bond Security Interests.
