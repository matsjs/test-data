14.2 New Subsidiary Guarantors
14.2.1
If any Subsidiary of the Issuer becomes a Vessel Owner or a Charterer, the Issuer undertakes to procure that such Subsidiary becomes a Subsidiary Guarantor and issues a Subsidiary Guarantee in favour of the Bond Trustee no later than contemporaneously with becoming a Vessel Owner or Charterer.

15.1 The Issuer shall cover all costs and expenses incurred by it or the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent) in connection with this Bond Agreement and the fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, including in connection with the negotiation, preparation, execution and enforcement of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), the listing of the Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository.
