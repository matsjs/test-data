13.4.2 Dividends restrictions
The Issuer shall ensure that (a) the Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind, repurchase of shares or make other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer and any cash payment (interest or amortizations) under inter company loans from shareholders) or other distributions or transactions until 2016 and (b) none of the Subsidiaries shall (i) grant any loans to the Issuer's (direct or indirect) shareholders or (ii) serve any loans (interest payments or instalments/repayments) from the Issuer's (direct or indirect) shareholders until 2016. In 2016, the said restrictions are lifted and any such transactions shall be permitted (including for dividend payment in 2016 based on the audited annual accounts for the financial year 2015) provided always that the aggregated transfer of value from the Issuer and its Subsidiaries to the Issuer's (direct or indirect) shareholders ("Dividend Distributions") shall not exceed the lesser of (i) 25% of the consolidated net profit after taxes per financial year for the Group, based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year and (ii) USD 15 million.
