"Bond Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Bond Security Interests.
"Bond Security Interests" means:
(a) the Escrow Account Pledge;
(b) the Bond Escrow Account Pledge;
(c) the Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge; and
(d) the Bond Guarantees.
"Bond Subsidiary Guarantees" means each of the on-demand guarantees from each of the Subsidiary Guarantors in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) guaranteeing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 17.
