"Junior Finance Parties" means the lenders, hedging providers and other finance parties under the Junior Debt Facilities.
"Junior Parent Guarantees" means each of the guarantees issued or to be issued by the Parent Guarantor for the obligations of the relevant borrower(s) to the relevant Junior Finance Parties under any Junior Debt Facility.
"Leverage Ratio" to be calculated as the ratio of Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA, with the adjustments as set out in Clause 13.5.2 {Leverage Ratio).
"Liquidity" means, at any given time, the aggregate book value of:
(a) cash in hand or amounts standing to the credit of any current and/or on deposit accounts with an acceptable bank;
(b) time deposits with acceptable banks and certificates of deposit issued, and bills of exchange accepted, by an acceptable bank;
(c) any amount at all times available for drawdown under any working capital facility with an acceptable bank, but excluding credit lines which are not renewed at least three (3) months before maturity, however so that this time limit for renewal shall not apply to the extension and amendment of the Existing Revolving Credit Facility for as long as it is renewed by the entering into of the Amended Revolving Credit Facility latest by its maturity at the end of January 2014;
in each case to which any Group Company is beneficially entitled at that time and to which the Issuer has free and unrestricted access and which is not subject to any encumbrance. For the avoidance of doubt, any cash standing to the credit of the Issuer at the Debt Service Reserve Account shall not be included in the calculation.
