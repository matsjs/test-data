"Debt Service Reserve Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer held with the Paying Agent, blocked and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under

this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the Issuer's claim against the account bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Debt Service Reserve Account, where the account bank has waived any set-off rights.
"Decisive Influence" shall mean a determining influence due to (i) direct and indirect ownership of shares or other ownership interests, and/or (ii) agreement, understanding or other arrangement. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of another entity or person if such entity or person has such number of shares or ownership interests so as to represent the majority of the votes in the entity, or has the right to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 19.2.
"EBITDA" means, for any Relevant Period (on a consolidated basis for the Group), earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortization.
"Escrow Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer held with the Paying Agent, blocked and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) (prior to the Disbursement Date, as security for the holders of Bonds which are not Temporary Bonds only) as security for the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the Issuer's claim against the account bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account, where the account bank has waived any set-off rights.
