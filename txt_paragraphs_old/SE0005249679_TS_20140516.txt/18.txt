USE OF PROCEEDS
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Notes, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Notes, for (i) initially advance payments of reserved new helicopters, repayment of debt, and general corporate purposes of the Issuer and the Group and (ii) over time, payment of reserved helicopters in part or in full.
