Financial undertakings
The Issuer shall ensure that on any Relevant Date the aggregate of cash, cash equivalent investments and the undrawn available commitments under any working capital facility of the Group shall not be less than the higher of (i) SEK 20,000,000 or (ii) an amount equal to 5 % of the total outstanding amount as per the Relevant Date of Financial Indebtedness permitted under Clauses 11.1.3 i-ii, iv-vi, viii and x.
