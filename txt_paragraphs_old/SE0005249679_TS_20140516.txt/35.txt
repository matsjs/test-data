9.3.1
The Issuer may redeem all, but not some only, of the outstanding Notes in full:
(a) any time from and including the First Call Date to, but excluding, the first Business Day falling four (4) years after the First Issue Date at an amount per Note equal to 104.50 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest;
(b) any time from and including the first Business Day falling four (4) years after the First Issue Date to, but excluding, the date falling three (3) months before the Final Maturity Date at an amount per Note equal to 102.25 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest; and
(c) any time from and including the date falling three (3) months before the Final Maturity Date to, but excluding, the Final Maturity Date, at an amount equal to 100 per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest.
