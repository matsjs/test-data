2.2 The Bonds
The Face Value is USD 200,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "8 per cent Polacus Limited Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2018".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 001 0680150.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied towards:
(a) refinancing of the USD 60 million bond (ISIN: NO 0010590300); and
(b) refinancing of the USD 35 million convertible bond at maturity (ISIN: NO 0010445943).
