INTEREST
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue
Date at a fixed rate of eight per cent. (8.00%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2
Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates
each year, the first Interest Payment Date falls in December 2013.
