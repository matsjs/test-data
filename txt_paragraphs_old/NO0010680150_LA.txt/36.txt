10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (Call Option):
(a) with settlement date any time from and included the Interest Payment Date in June 2016 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in June 2017 at 104.00% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount;
(b) with settlement date any time from and included the Interest Payment Date in June 2017 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in December 2017 at 102.00% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(c) with settlement date any time from and included the Interest Payment Date in December 2017 to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 101.00% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
