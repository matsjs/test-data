The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of NOK 100,000,000 (Norwegian la-oner one hundred million).The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount o f NOK 13,000,000 (Norwegian kroner thirteen million).
The Bond Issue will be described as "15 per cent Precise Prediction AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2012/2014 with Warrants".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) o f the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0660004.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.2.2
The Bond Issue is a Tap Issue, under which subsequent issues may take place after Issue Date up to the maximum amount described in clause 2.2.1, running from the Issue Date and to be closed no later than 5 Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will on the issuing of additional Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
The Issuer is under no circumstance obliged to issue warrants as part o f any future Tap Issue.
