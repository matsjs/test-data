Purchase and transfer of Instruments
5.1 Subject to the restrictions set forth in this Clause 5, the Instruments are freely transferable and may be pledged.
5.2 Instrumentholders may be subject to purchase or transfer restrictions with regard to the Instruments, as applicable from time to time under local laws to which a Instrumentholders may be subject (due e.g. to its nationality, its residency, its registered address, its place(s) for doing business). Each Instrumentholders must ensure compliance with local laws and regulations applicable at own cost and expense.
5.3 Notwithstanding the above, a Bondholder which has purchased the Bonds in contradiction to mandatory restrictions applicable may nevertheless utilize its voting rights under this Bond Agreement.
