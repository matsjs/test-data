18.6 Notices, contact information
18.6.1
Written notices, warnings, summons and other communications to the Bondholders made by the Bond Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Register with a copy to the Issuer and the Exchange. Information to the Bondholders may in lieu of such requirement in the immediate preceding sentence be published at the web site www.stamdata.no.
18.6.2 The Issuer's written notifications to the Bondholders shall be sent via the Bond Trustee or alternatively through the Securities Register with a copy to the Bond Trustee and the Exchange
18.6.3
Unless otherwise specifically provided, all notices or other communications under or in connection with this Bond Agreement between the Bond Trustee and the Issuer shall be given or made in writing, by letter, or facsimile. Any such notice or communication shall be deemed to be given or made as follows:
(a) if by letter, when delivered at the address of the relevant Party; (b) if by facsimile, when received.
