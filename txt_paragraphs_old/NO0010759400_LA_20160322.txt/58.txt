13.4.6 Allotment of the property, gnr. 128, bnr. 2 in municipality of Bergen
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure, as soon as possible, that the part of the property with title number gnr. 128, bnr. 2 in the municipality of Bergen that is currently owned by the Issuer is partitioned so that it obtains an individual title number in the land registry that will enable the Bond Trustee to register the Additional Security. Following the completion of the partitioning, the Issuer shall immediately ensure that the Additional Security is established.
13.4.7 The Issuer shall, as soon as it becomes pertinent, ensure that the Additional Security is pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee.
13.4.8
The Bond Trustee acting on behalf of the Bondholders shall enter into an agreement with the Manager and the Issuer, under which the Manager shall undertake to perform certain advisory services related to the monitoring of the progress reports and the progression of any planning applications up to and including the month the planning approval is delivered to the Issuer.
The Manager may at its own discretion engage advisors to assist in the performance of these advisory services. Costs related to such advisors shall be covered by the Issuer. The Issuer shall provide the Manager with such information that the Manager finds necessary to monitor progress and that the Issuer has provided to the Trustee.
