13.3.5 Disposal of business
Nordic Trustee ASA
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
(i)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions
customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.3.6 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
