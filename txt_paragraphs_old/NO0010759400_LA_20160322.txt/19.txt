The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds. The NOK Tranche consists of a series of bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 28,200,000 (Norwegian kroner twenty-eight million two hundred thousand), with denominations of NOK 25,000. The SEK Tranche consists of a series of bonds at a maximum amount of SEK 16,800,000 (Swedish kroner sixteen million eight hundred thousand), with a denominations of SEK 25,000.
The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "20 % Hilleren Tomteselskap AS Junior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2016/2017".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be ISIN N00010759392 (NOK Tranche) and ISIN NO0010759400 (SEK Tranche).
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
