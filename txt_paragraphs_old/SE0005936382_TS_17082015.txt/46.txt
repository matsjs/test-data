The Issuer is under no obligation to gross-up any payments under the Finance Documents which are subject to withholding tax, public levy or any other similar withholding or deduction.
INTEREST
Each Initial Note carries Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the First Issue Date up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Note will carry Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Issue Date (in respect of Notes issued before the first Interest Payment Date) or the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to their issuance (in respect of Notes issued after the first Interest Payment Date) up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date.
