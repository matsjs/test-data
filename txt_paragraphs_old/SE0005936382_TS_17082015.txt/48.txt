Issuer's purchase of Notes
Voluntary total redemption (call option)
The Issuer may redeem all, but not some only, of the outstanding Notes in full:
(a) any time prior to the First Call Date, at an amount per Note equal to 100.00 percent, of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest, plus the Applicable Premium;
(b) any time from and including the First Call Date to, but excluding, the first Business Day falling thirty (30) months after the First Issue Date at an amount per Note equal to 104.00 percent, of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest;
(c) any time from and including the first Business Day falling thirty (30) months after the First Issue Date to, but excluding, the first Business Day falling thirty (36) months after the First Issue Date at an amount per Note equal to 103.00 per cent, of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest;
