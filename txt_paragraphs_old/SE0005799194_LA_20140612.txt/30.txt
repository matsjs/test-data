5.2
The Issuer shall ensure that the Security Documents and all documents relating thereto are
duly executed (in favour of the Agent and the Holders (as represented by the Agent), if
applicable) and that such documents are legally valid, perfected, enforceable and in full force

the Agent to at all times maintain the security position envisaged hereunder.
