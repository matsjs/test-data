Financial Indebtedness
12.5
Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of the Subsidiaries, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to retain, provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
12.6
Clean down period
The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year, there shall be a period of 5 consecutive calendar days during which the amount outstanding under the Permitted Credit Facility shall amount to zero. Not less than 3 months shall elapse between two clean down periods.
