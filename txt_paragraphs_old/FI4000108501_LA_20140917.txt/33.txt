10.2.2
Financial Indebtedness
Except as provided under Clause 10.2.2, the Issuer shall not (and shall procure that no other Group Company will) incur any Financial Indebtedness, provided that the Issuer and such Group Company may incur Financial Indebtedness if:
(a) no Event of Default is continuing or would occur as a result thereof; and
(b) the Incurrence Test is met.
Notwithstanding Clause 10.2.1, the Issuer and any other Group Company may incur Financial Indebtedness:
(a) arising under the Finance Documents;
