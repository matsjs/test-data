13.5 Financial Covenants
13.5.1 The Issuer shall ensure that the Equity Ratio of the Group, on each Compliance Certificate Date, shall not fall below 25%.
13.5.2 The Issuer shall ensure that the Liquidity of the Group at all times shall not fall below USD 75,000,000.
