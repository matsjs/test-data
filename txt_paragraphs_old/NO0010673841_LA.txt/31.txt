9
Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value o f the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at the Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made quarterly in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falling in June 2013.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
