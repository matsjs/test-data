(a) Non-payment The Issuer fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the reasonable opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure will be remedied, and payment in full is made, within five (5) Business Days following the original due date.
(c) Cross default For the Issuer or any Material Subsidiary, the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness falling within paragraphs (i) to (iii) below exceeds a total o f USD 20 million, or the equivalent thereof in other cuirencies;
(i) any Financial Indebtedness is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period,
(ii) any Financial Indebtedness is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), or
(iii) any creditor becomes entitled to declare any Financial Indebtedness due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described).
