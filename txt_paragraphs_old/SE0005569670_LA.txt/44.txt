10.3.2
Redemption in accordance with Clause 10.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than fifteen (15) Business Days' notice to the Noteholders and the Agent. Any such notice is irrevocable but may, at the Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent. Upon expiry of such notice and the fulfilment of the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is bound to redeem the Notes in full or part at the applicable amounts as the case may be. The applicable amount shall be an even amount in Swedish Kronor and paid to the person who is registered as a Noteholder on the Record Date prior to the relevant Redemption Date
