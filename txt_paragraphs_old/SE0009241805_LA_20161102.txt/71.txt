21.1
A Bondholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer to enforce or recover
any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate, support
or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation
(foretagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other
jurisdiction) ofthe Issuer in relation to any ofthe liabilities of the Issuer underthe Finance
Documents.
