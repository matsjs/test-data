2.1 Binding nature of the Bond Agreement
2.1.2
This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata Ol' such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 200,000,000 (US Dollars twohundred million).
The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount ofUSD 130,000,000 (US Dollars onehundredandthirty million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 200,000 each and shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Hoegh LNG Holdings Ltd. Senior Unsecured Callable Open Bond Issue 2015/2020".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 0010010739683.
