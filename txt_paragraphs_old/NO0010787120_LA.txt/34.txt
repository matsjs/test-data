2.3 Use of proceeds (a) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue (net of fees and legal cost of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other cost and expenses incurred in connection with the Bond Issue) exclusively (i) for repayment of Existing Debt, and (ii) general corporate puiposes, including acquisitions.
