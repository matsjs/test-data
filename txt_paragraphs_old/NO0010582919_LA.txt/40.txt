10 Maturity of the Bonds and
Redemption
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in M l on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1
The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue (Call Option) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date falling on 21 M y 2011 to, but excluding, the Maturity Date at 102 per cent of par plus accrued interests on the par value of the redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty (30) days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
