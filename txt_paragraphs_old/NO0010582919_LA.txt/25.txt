2.2 The Bonds
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and shall rank pari passu amongst themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA Callable Bond Issue 2010/2014".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0582919.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used in connection with the issuance of the Performance Guarantee and for general corporate purposes of the Issuer.
