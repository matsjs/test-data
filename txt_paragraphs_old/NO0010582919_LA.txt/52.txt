13.5 Preservation o f equity, Security Interests, Accounts and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions Save as provided in paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not within a calendar year declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares, repayment of subordinated loans, any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity (but always distributions of bonus shares not affecting its equity).
(b) Permitted distributions To the extent the Debt Service Account fully covers the Outstanding Bonds (including accrued interest and any other amount under this Bond Agreement), the Issuer may (i) pay dividends (or make any other distributions to its shareholders), (ii) buy back its own shares and/or (iii) enter into total return swaps.
