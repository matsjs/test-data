"Obligors" means the Issuer and the Guarantor and "Obligor" means either one of them.
"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
"Paying Agent" means any legal entity as appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee who acts as paying agent on behalf of the Issuer with respect to the Bonds.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest.
"Performance Counter Guarantee" means a counter guarantee acceptable to Santander as security for granting the Performance Guarantee.
"Performance Guarantee" means the guarantee granted to National Oilco on specified terms as a prerequisite for the Contract totaling 10% of the Contract value, issued as guarantee for the Guarantor's performance of the Contract according to its terms including price and time.
"Performance Guarantee Account" means a bank account in the name of the Issuer in Handelsbanken where cash collateral proceeds are deposited as security for Handelsbanken for issuing the Performance Counter Guarantee.
"Performance Guarantee Account Pledge" means a second priority pledge over the Performance Guarantee Account, on priority after Handelsbanken's first priority pledge, subject to the necessary consent and/or inter-creditor agreements which may be required by Handelsbanken.
"Pledge of Inter-company Claims" means a first priority security interest in any intra Group loan provided by any Group Company to the Guarantor.
