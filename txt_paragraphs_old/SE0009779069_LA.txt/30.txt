3. Use of Proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Bonds, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Bonds, for (i) financing the acquisition of the P-N Vik 22, P-N Vik 29 and the Fjardingen Properties (together with the Initial Equity Injection and the Subsequent Equity Injection), (ii) deposit an amount of SEK 5,000,000 to the Reserve Account; and (iii) payment of Transaction Costs.
