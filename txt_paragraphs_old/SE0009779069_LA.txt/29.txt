2. Status of the Bonds
(a) The Bonds are denominated in SEK and each Bond is constituted by these Terms and Conditions. The Issuer undertakes to make payments in relation to the Bonds and to comply with these Terms and Conditions.
(b) By subscribing for Bonds, each Bondholder agrees that the Bonds shall benefit from and be subject to the Finance Documents and by acquiring Bonds.
(c) The initial nominal amount of each Bond is SEK 1,000,000 (the "Initial Nominal Amount"). The Total Nominal Amount of the initial Bonds is SEK 300,000,000.
