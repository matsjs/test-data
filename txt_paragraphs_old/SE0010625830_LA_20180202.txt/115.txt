AMENDMENTS AND WAIVERS
20.1
Subject to the Intercreditor Agreement, the Issuer and the Agent (acting on behalf of the
Noteholders) may agree to amend the Finance Documents or waive any provision in a
Finance Document, provided that:
(a) the Agent is satisfied that such amendment or waiver is not detrimental to the interest of the Noteholders as a group;
(b) such amendment or waiver is made solely for the purpose of rectifying obvious errors and mistakes;
(c) such amendment or waiver is required by applicable law, a court ruling or a decision by a relevant authority; or
