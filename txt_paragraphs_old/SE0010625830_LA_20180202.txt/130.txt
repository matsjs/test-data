A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer or any Guarantor or
amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate, support or
procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation
(företagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other
jurisdiction) of the Issuer or any Guarantor in relation to any of the obligations and
liabilities of the Issuer or the Guarantor (as applicable) under the Finance Documents. Such
steps may only be taken by the Agent (including, as applicable, in its capacity as Security
Agent).
