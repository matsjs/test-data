Voluntary total redemption (call option)
The Issuer may redeem all, but not some only, of the outstanding Notes in full:
(a) any time from and including the First Call Date to, but excluding, the first Business Day falling twenty-four (24) months after the First Issue Date at an amount per Note equal to 103.00 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest;
(b) any time from and including the first Business Day falling twenty-four (24) months after the First Issue Date to, but excluding, the first Business Day falling thirty (30) months after the First Issue Date at an amount per Note equal to 101.50 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest;
(c) any time from and including the first Business Day falling thirty (30) months after the First Issue Date to, but excluding, the Final Maturity Date at an amount per Note equal to 100.75 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid Interest; and
(d) notwithstanding paragraph (c) above, provided that the redemption is financed in full by way of one or several Market Loan issues, at any time from and including the first Business Day falling three (3) months before the Final Maturity Date to, but excluding, the Final Maturity Date, at an amount equal to 100 per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest.
