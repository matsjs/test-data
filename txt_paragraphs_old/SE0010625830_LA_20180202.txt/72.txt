GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS
Restricted Payments
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will:
(a) pay any dividends on shares;
(b) repurchase any of its own shares,
(c) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity (bundet eget kapital) with repayment to shareholders;
(d) repay principal or pay interest under any loans from shareholders or Affiliates; or
(e) make other similar distributions or transfers of value (värdeöverföringar) within the meaning of the Swedish Companies Act to its shareholders or Affiliates.
