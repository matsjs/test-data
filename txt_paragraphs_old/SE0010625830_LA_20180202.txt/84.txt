14.2
Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) the Interest Coverage Ratio (adjusted in accordance with Clause 14.5 (Calculation Adjustments)) is equal to or greater than 3.00:1 for the relevant test period; and
(b) the Leverage Ratio (adjusted in accordance with Clause 14.5 (Calculation Adjustments)) does not exceed:
