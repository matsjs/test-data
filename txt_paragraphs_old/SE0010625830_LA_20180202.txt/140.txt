28.2
The Issuer submits to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the City Court of Stockholm
(Stockholms tingsrätt). The submission to the jurisdiction of the City Court of Stockholm
shall however not limit the right of the Agent (or the Noteholders, as applicable) to take
proceedings against the Issuer in any court which may otherwise exercise jurisdiction over
the Issuer or any of its assets.
