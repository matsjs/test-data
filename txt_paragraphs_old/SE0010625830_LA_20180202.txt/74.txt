Notwithstanding Clause 13.1.1, a Restricted Payment may be made by the Issuer, if at the time of the Restricted Payment:
(a) no Event of Default is continuing or would result from such Restricted Payment;
(b) the Issuer successfully meets the requirements of the Incurrence Test (for the avoidance of doubt, on a pro forma basis taking into account such Restricted Payment); and
(c) the amount of the Restricted Payment does not exceed the Permitted Distribution Amount,
provided that any such paid out Permitted Distribution Amount shall decrease the Permitted Distribution Amount accordingly.
Changes of business
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Group from that carried on as of the First Issue Date.
