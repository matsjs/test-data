2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds to a maximum amount of USD 15,000,000, herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and rank pari passu.

The international security identification number (ISIN) of the Loan/Bonds will be NO 001 039621.1.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
