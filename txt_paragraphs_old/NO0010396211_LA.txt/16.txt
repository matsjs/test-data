2.2 By subscribing to the Loan, each Bondholder will be allocated 50,000 Warrants for each allotted Bond, so that the total number of Warrants to be issued in relation to the Loan shall be 7,500,000.
For each Warrant the Warrantholder is entitled to exercise the Exercise Right and with that the right to purchase one Share at the Exercise Price on the conditions set out in Clause 13 (subject to adjustments as provided in Clauses 14 and 15).
The Warrants shall be separately tradable from the Bonds, subject to applicable securities regulations.
The Warrants shall be registered in the Securities Depository.
The international securities identification number (ISIN) of the Warrants will be NO 001 039734.2.
3 Listing
3.1 The Loan/Bonds will initially not be listed on an Exchange. An application may be made for listing on Oslo Børs or Oslo Børs ASA's Alternative Bond Market (ABM) or OTC at the discretion of the Borrower.
