If the Conditions Precedent for First Disbursement have not been fulfilled on or before ninety (90) calendar days following the First Issue Date, the Issuer shall redeem all Bonds at a price equal to one hundred (100.00) per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with any accrued but unpaid interest.
The redemption of the Bonds shall (i) be executed on the first Interest Payment Date (taking into account the rules and regulations of the CSD) and (ii) be made by the Issuer giving not less than ten (10) Business Days' notice to the Holders and the Agent, where such notice shall state the relevant Interest Payment Date on which the redemption shall be made, the redemption amount and the relevant Record Date.

The Incurrence Test is met if the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than:
(a) three (3.00) until and including 31 December 2017;
(b) two point seventy-five (2.75) from and including 1 January 2018 until and including 31 December 2018; and
(c) two point fifty (2.50) from and including 1 January 2019 until and including the Final Redemption Date.
