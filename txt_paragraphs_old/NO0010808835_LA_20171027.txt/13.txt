"Interest Rate" means the percentage rate per annum which is the aggregate of the Reference Rate for the relevant Interest Period plus the Margin.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for the quarterly period ending on each 31 March, 30 June, and 30 September in each year, prepared in accordance with GAAP.
"Interim Fourth Quarter Accounts" means, where applicable, the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for the quarterly period ending on each 31 December in each year, prepared in accordance with GAAP.
"Intra Group Loan" means any subordinated intra group loans granted to the Issuer from any Group Company, provided (i) that any such debt is set-off against dividends from the lending company whenever it pays dividends to the Issuer; and (ii) that the aggregate amount of such intra group loans is not more than NOK 50 million.
"Intra Group Receivable" means:
(a) any intra group loan granted by the Issuer to any Group Company prior to the Issue Date; and
(b) any senior intra group loan granted by the Issuer to any Group Company after the Issue Date, provided that (i) the term of such loan is not longer than twelve (12) months; and (ii) that the aggregate amount of such intra group loans is not more than NOK 30 million.
"IPO Subsidiaries" means Norlandia Health & Care Group AS (registration number 917 933 367) and/or Otiga Group AS (registration number 913 664 949) or, if relevant, an intermediate holding company o f such subsidiary established for the sole purpose of facilitating an Initial Public Offering of any of the aforementioned entities.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bonds.
