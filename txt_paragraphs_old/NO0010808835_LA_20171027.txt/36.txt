6.2 Settlement in cash or in kind (a) The Bonds shall be settled either in (i) cash, or (ii) in kind by delivery of Roll-Over Bonds (from Existing Bondholders in accordance with the Existing Bondholders' RollOver). The settlement process shall be further specified in a separate application form.
(b) Applicants delivering Roll-Over Bonds will receive the accrued interest on the RollOver Bonds up until the Issue Date and a two per cent, (call) premium, each payable in cash on the Issue Date.
6.3 Distribution Disbursement of the proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds is conditional on the Bond Trustee's confirmation to the Paying Agent that the conditions in Clause 6.1 (Conditions precedent fo r disbursement to the Issuer) have been either satisfied in the Bond Trustee's discretion or waived by the Bond Trustee pursuant to paragraph 6.1 (b) above.
