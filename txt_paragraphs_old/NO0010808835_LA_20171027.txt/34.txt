CONDITIONS FOR DISBURSEMENT, ETC
(a) Issuance of the Bonds and release of the net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses incurred in connection with the Bond Issue) to the Issuer shall be conditional on the Bond Trustee having received in due time (as determined by the Bond Trustee acting reasonably) prior to the Issue Date each of the following documents, in form and substance satisfactoiy to the Bond Trustee:
(i) these Bond Terms duly executed by all parties thereto;
(ii) certified copies of all corporate resolutions of the Issuer required for the Issuer to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents to which it is a party;
(iii) a certified copy of a power o f attorney from the Issuer to relevant individuals for their execution of the Finance Documents to which it is a party, or extracts from the relevant register or similar documentation evidencing such individuals' authorisation to execute such Finance Documents on behalf o f the Issuer;
(iv) certified copies of the Issuer's articles of association and of a full extract from the relevant company register in respect of the Issuer evidencing that the Issuer is validly existing;
(v) evidence that the Bond Defeasance Account has been opened and that the Defeasance Account Pledge has been established and perfected;
(vi) evidence that the call notice for the exercise of the call option in respect of all the Existing Bonds has been issued or will be issued by the Issuer on the Issue Date;
(vii) confirmation by the Manager that the amounts and the Roll-Over Bonds necessary for the settlement of the Bond Issue has or will be received and, in respect of Roll-Over Bonds, that such Roll-Over Bonds will be deleted prior to the call settlement date under the Existing Bonds (such confirmation to be in a form and substance satisfactoiy to the Bond Trustee);
