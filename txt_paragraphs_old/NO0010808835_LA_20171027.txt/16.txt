"Maximum Issue Amount" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 2.1 (Amount, denomination, ISIN and tenor).
"Net Debt" means for an applicable point in time the aggregate principal amount of all obligations of the Issuer in respect of interest bearing Financial Indebtedness, but (a) excluding any such obligation in respect of Subordinated Shareholder Loans, (b) including, in the case of Finance Leases only their capitalised value, and (c) deducting the amount of Cash and Cash Equivalents held by the Issuer.
"Net Debt to Capital Ratio" means the Issuer's Net Debt divided by the Issuer's Capital.
