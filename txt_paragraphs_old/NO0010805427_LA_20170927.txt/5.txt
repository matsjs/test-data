"Cash Sweep Amount" means on an Interest Payment Date the Excess Cash Amount less any amount required for the payment of cash interest on such Interest Payment Date.
"Charge over Equipment" means the charge over the BOP and its equipment by the Issuer in favour of the bond trustee for the Existing Bonds.
"Charterparty" means any charterparty entered or to be entered into between the Issuer and a charterer in respect of the Vessel.
"Code" means the U.S. Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended.
"Debenture" means the English law debenture between the Issuer and the bond trustee for the Existing Bonds creating security for the Issuer's obligations under the Existing Finance Documents.
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
When determining the relevant person's number of voting rights in the other person or the right to elect and remove members of the board of directors, rights held by the parent company of the relevant person and the parent company's Subsidiaries shall be included.
