9.4
The Issuer shall, if it intends to pay any part of the interest by issuing Additional Bonds, as
soon as practicable and in any event not less than ten (10) Business Days prior to the relevant
Interest Payment Date, give notice to the Bond Trustee and the Paying Agent of the amount of
interest that will be paid in cash and/or Additional Bonds, respectively, which notice shall be
irrevocable.
