17.2 Liability and indemnity
The Bond Trustee is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders or the Issuer as a result of gross negligence or wilful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set out in this Bond Agreement. Such liability is limited to the maximum amount set out in Clause 2.2. The Bond Trustee is not liable for the content of information provided to the Bondholders on behalf of the Issuer.
17.2.2
Subject to Clause 17.2.3 and 17.2.4, the Issuer is liable for, and shall indemnify the Bond Trustee fully in respect of, all losses, expenses and liabilities incurred by the Bond Trustee as a result of gross negligence or wilful misconduct by the Issuer (including its directors, management, officers, employees, agents and representatives) to fulfil its obligations under the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including losses incurred by the Bond Trustee as a result of the Bond Trustee's actions based on misrepresentations made by the Issuer in connection with the establishment and performance of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document.
17.2.3
The Parties acknowledge and agree that their recourse to the Issuer for any claim, action, demand or right arising against the Issuer or its directors or officers pursuant to the Finance Documents shall be limited in any event to the Issuer's assets only.
17.2.4
The Parties agree not to seek before any court or governmental agency to have any director or officer of the Issuer held liable for any actions or inactions of the Issuer or any obligations of the Issuer under the Finance Documents, except if such actions or inactions are the result of gross negligence or wilful misconduct of such director or officer.
