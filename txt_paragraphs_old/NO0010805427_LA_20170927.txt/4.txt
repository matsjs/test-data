"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 16.
"BOP" means certain well control equipment known as a blow-out preventer and related equipment owned by the Issuer.
"Business Day" means any day on which the commercial banks in Norway and New York are open for general business, and can settle foreign currency transactions.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the Payment Date occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments of interest and/or principal (as the case may be) will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day (No Adjustments of Business Day).

"Call Option Repayment Date" means the date specified in the notice delivered in exercise of the Call Option in accordance with Clause 10.2.
"Cash Sweep Account" means the Issuer's account held with the Paying Agent and pledged (but unblocked) on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Existing Finance Documents.
"Cash Sweep Amount" means on an Interest Payment Date the Excess Cash Amount less any amount required for the payment of cash interest on such Interest Payment Date.
"Change of Control Event" means if and when any person or a group of persons acting in concert other than Refectio, directly or indirectly, acquires Decisive Influence over the Issuer (other than upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event).
