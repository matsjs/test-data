Except as existing on the Issue Date, the Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither Midco nor the Property Company, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or Security over any of their assets (present or future) to secure any Financial Indebtedness, unless such Security or guarantees constitute Permitted Security.
13.5 Financial Support
Except as existing on the Issue Date, the Issuer shall not and shall procure neither Midco nor the Property Company provide any loan or security or guarantees to or for the benefit of any third party.
13.6 Nature of Business
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by the Issuer, Midco and the Property Company as of the Issue Date if such change is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.7 Disposal of Assets
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of the shares in Midco or all or substantially all of its assets and shall procure that neither Midco nor the Property Company sell all or substantially all of their assets or operations, including the Valsjoberget Property, to any person.
