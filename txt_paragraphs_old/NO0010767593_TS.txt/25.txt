13. General Undertakings
13.1 General
The Issuer undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that the Property Company and Midco will) comply with the undertakings set out in this Clause 13 for as long as any Bonds remain outstanding.
13.2 Distributions
Except as done in accordance with the purpose of the Bonds in the Issue Date, the Issuer shall not, (i) pay any dividend on its shares, (ii) repurchase any of its own shares, (iii) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders, (iv) make any repayments on loans to the direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, or any Affiliates of the Issuer (other than to Midco or the Property Company); or (v) make any other similar distribution or transfers of value to the direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, or any Affiliates of the Issuer.
13.3 Financial Indebtedness
Except as existing on the Issue Date, the Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither Midco nor the Property Company, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness, unless such Financial Indebtedness constitute Permitted Debt.
