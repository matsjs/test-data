On each Interest Payment Date to but excluding 28 March 2017, the interest (rounded down
in kind interest ("PIK Interest") on the par value of the Bonds (including, for the avoidance
of doubt, any Additional Bonds) and paid on the Maturity Date. On any Interest Payment
date from and including 28 March 2017, if cash interest payment by the Issuer due on such
Interest Payment Date would result in the total amount on the Debt Service Account being
less than USD 10,000,000, the interest (rounded down to the nearest USD) shall be
capitalised on such Interest Payment Date and paid as PIK Interest on the par value of the
Bonds (including, for the avoidance of doubt, any Additional Bonds) and paid on the
Maturity Date.
