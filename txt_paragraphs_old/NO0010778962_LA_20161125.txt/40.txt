10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds (in whole or in part) at any time from and including:
(a) the Issue Date to, but excluding, the Interest Payment Date falling on 28 December 2017 at a price equal to 102% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(b) the Interest Payment Date falling on 28 December 2017 to, but excluding, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 101% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount),
(the "Call Option").
10.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and to the Bondholders at least ten Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository.
