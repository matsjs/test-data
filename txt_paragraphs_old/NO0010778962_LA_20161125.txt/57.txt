16.1.3
If a resolution by or an approval of the Bondholders is required, such resolution shall be passed at a Bondholders' Meeting, see however Clause 16.5 and Clause 17.1. Resolutions passed at Bondholders' Meetings shall be binding upon all Bondholders and prevail for all the Bonds.
16.1.4
The Parent is the nominee shareholder of the Issuer and the Bondholders are the beneficial owners of the shares in the Issuer. Any voting rights in respect of the shares in the Issuer shall be with the Bondholders. The Bondholders' Meeting represents the supreme authority of the Bondholders community in all matters relating to the Parent's shares in the Issuer, and has the power to make all shareholder decisions in respect of such shares.
