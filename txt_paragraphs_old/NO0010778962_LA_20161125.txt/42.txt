Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at a price of 100% of par plus accrued interest.
10.3.3
The Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the redemption request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end of the sixty days exercise period of the Put Option.
10.3.4
On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.3.1) and any unpaid cash interest and uncapitalised payment in kind interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.4 10.4.1
Mandatory Prepayment
Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event, a Total Loss Event or a Put Option (each as defined in the Existing Bond Agreement) being triggered or exercised under the Existing Bond Agreement, the Bonds shall be prepaid in accordance with the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement following the procedure set out in Clause 10.1 above.
