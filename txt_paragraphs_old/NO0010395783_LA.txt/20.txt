The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as " 10.85 per cent PetroMena ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 039578.3.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds o f the Loan shall be employed for financing the planned acquisition of the Rig for USD 225 million, such acquisition to be finally approved by the general assembly o f the Borrower and Petrolia Drilling ASA and for financing the construction program o f the New Building Rigs by financing payments under the relevant Construction Contract(s) and management cost related to the construction o f the New Building Rigs. This may include the establishment o f performance bonds towards charterers to secure employment of the Rig and the New Building Rigs. In addition, the proceeds may be used for servicing the Loan.
