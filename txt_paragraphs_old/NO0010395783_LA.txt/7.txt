The Bondholders are bound by the terms of the Loan Agreement as the Loan Trustee in the subscription agreements was granted authority to finalise and execute the Loan Agreement on behalf of the Bondholders.
Ail Bond transfers shall be subject to the terms of this Loan Agreement and the Trustee and all Bond transferees shall, in taking transfer of Bonds, be deemed to have accepted the terms of the Loan Agreement where by all such transferees shall automatically become parties to the Loan Agreement upon completed transfer having been registered in the VPS, without any further action required or formalities to be complied with.
