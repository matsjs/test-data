Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 10.85 % - ten point eighty five per cent - per annum. Interest payments shall be made quarterly in arrears on each Interest Payment Day, the first Interest Payment Date falls in February 2008.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless a) the last, day o f the period is the 3 1st day o f a month but the first day o f the period is a day other than the 30th or 3 1st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month o f February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-dav month.
