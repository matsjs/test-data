Cash Flow:
the payments from Pemex to the Rig Manager under the Pemex Contract, or any subsequent operational contracts after the expiry of the Pemex Contract entered into with an operator for the Rig.
Cash Sweep Escrow Account:
account(s) established in the name of the Guarantor to be blocked and pledged on first priority in favor o f the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), in which the bank operating the accounts has waived any set-off rights.
Cash Sweep Escrow Account Pledge:
the Norwegian law first priority pledge in favor o f the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the Guarantor's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Borrower in the Cash Sweep Escrow Account, serving as collateral security for the Borrower's and/ or the Guarantor's fulfilment of its payment obligations (included payment of principal, interest and expenses) under the Finance Documents.
