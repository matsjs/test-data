Bondholder or a successive, coherent chain of powers of attorney or proofs of authorisation
8.3
A Bondholder (whether registered as such or proven to the Trustee's satisfaction to be the
beneficial owner of the Bond as set out in Clause 8.1) may issue one or several powers of
attorney to third parties to represent it in relation to some or all of the Bonds held or
beneficially owned by it. Any such representative may act independently under the Finance
Documents in relation to the Bonds for which such representative is entitled to represent
the Bondholder and may further delegate its right to represent the Bondholder by way of a
further power of attorney.
