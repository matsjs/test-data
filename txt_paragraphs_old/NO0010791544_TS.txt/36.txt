BONDS IN BOOK-ENTRY FORM
7.1
The Bonds will be registered for the Bondholders on their respective Securities Accounts
and no physical notes will be issued. Accordingly, the Bonds will be registered in
accordance with the Norwegian Securities Register Act and the CSD Regulations.
Registration requests relating to the Bonds shall be directed to an Account Operator.
7.2
The Issuer shall at all times ensure that the registration of the Bonds in the CSD is correct.
