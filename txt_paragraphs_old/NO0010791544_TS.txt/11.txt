"Norwegian Securities Register Act" means the Norwegian Act Securities Register Act of 2002 no. 64 (Nw. verdipapirregisterloven).
"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to act as its paying agent with respect to the Bonds in the CSD.
"Permitted Debt" means any Financial Indebtedness:
(a) incurred by the Issuer under the Bonds;
(b) incurred under any Construction Financing;
(c) constituting Subordinated Loans;
(d) in relation to any Intercompany Loans;
(e) commonly entered into as part of the Group Companies' daily business (e.g. leasing arrangements or similar), provided that the total outstanding amount does not exceed NOK 2,000,000 at any given time; and
(f) arising as a result of a refinancing of the Bonds in full.
