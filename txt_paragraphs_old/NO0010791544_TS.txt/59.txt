15.8
15.8.1
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or Security over any of its assets (present or future), provided however that the Issuer and other Group Companies has a right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
15.9
Financial support
15.9.1
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, grant any loans, give any guarantees or otherwise voluntarily assume any financial liability (whether actual or contingent), to or for the benefit of any third party, other than any Permitted Debt.
