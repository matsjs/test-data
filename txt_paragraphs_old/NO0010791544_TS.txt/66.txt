16.2
The Trustee may not accelerate the Bonds in accordance with Clause 16.1 by reference to a
specific Event of Default if it is no longer continuing or if it has been decided, on a
Bondholders Meeting or by way of a Written Procedure, to waive such Event of Default
(temporarily or permanently).
