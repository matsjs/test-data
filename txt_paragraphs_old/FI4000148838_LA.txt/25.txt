4.2
The Issuing Agent shall pay the net proceeds from the issuance of any Subsequent Notes to
the Issuer on the later of (i) the Issue Date of such Subsequent Notes and (ii) the day on
which the Agent notifies the Issuing Agent that it has received the following in form and
substance satisfactory to it:
(a) a copy of a resolution from the board of directors of the Issuer approving the issue of the Subsequent Notes and resolving to enter into documents necessary in connection therewith;
