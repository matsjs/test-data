5
Merger and De-merger
5.1 If the Parent Issuer decides on a statutory merger (in accordance with prevailing legislation from time to time) in which the Parent Issuer is the acquired company, the Warrants shall, on completion of the merger, be transformed to rights to subscribe shares (warrants) in the acquiring company, on terms adjusted for the exchange ratio applied in the merger.
Warrantholders shall receive written notification of the merger from the Parent Issuer (through the Securities Depository (if the Warrants are registered therein)) no later than two Banking Days after the announcement referred to in the Companies Act § 13-14 has been made.
5.2 Should the Parent Issuer decide on a merger, in which the Parent Issuer is the acquiring company, and shareholders in the acquired company receive settlement in the form of Shares or a combination of Shares and cash, no adjustment will be made to the Exercise Price.
