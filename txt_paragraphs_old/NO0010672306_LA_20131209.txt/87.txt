The Tranche C2 Bonds will be described as the "Cecon Shipping 1 AS Tranche C2 Senior Secured Callable and Putable Bond Issue 2014/2015".

(a) to the Davie Entities from time to time to fund construction of Hull 718 as set forth in the Construction Budget Schedule if the Parent Issuer (acting as the agent for Cecon 1) delivers to the Bond Trustee an officer's certificate substantially in the form attached hereto as Attachment 3;
(b) at the written request of the Parent Issuer, to the Parent Issuer in an aggregate amount not exceeding USD 2,500,000; or
(c) to such person(s) and in such amounts as instructed by the Bond Trustee (acting on the instructions of holders of 75% of the Tranche C2 Bonds that are Voting Bonds).
For the avoidance of doubt, Bondholders in respect o f the Tranche A Bonds, Tranche B Bonds, Tranche B2 Bonds and Tranche Cl Bonds shall not be required to purchase the Tranche C2 Bonds, and purchasers of the Tranche C2 Bonds shall become "Bondholders" for purposes of this Bond Loan Agreement.
