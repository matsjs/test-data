(b) The Put Option must be exercised within two months after the Issuer has given notification as set out in Clause 13.1Q (the "Exercise Long Stop Date").
(c) The Put Option may be exercised by a Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. The applicable Issuer shall redeem that Bondholder's Bonds on the date that is 15 Banking Days after the Exercise Long Stop Date. The applicable Issuer shall provide written notice to the Bond Trustee at least 7 Banking Days before the proposed redemption date, confirming that it has sufficient funds to redeem all Bonds in respect of which a Put Option has been exercised.
