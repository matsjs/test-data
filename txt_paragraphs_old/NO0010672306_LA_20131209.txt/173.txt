On the Exercise Date (a) if the Shares are registered in the Securities Depository, the Paying Agent shall transfer the number of Shares which are to be issued to the Warrantholder5s account in the Securities Depository against crediting the Parent Issuer of the amount paid and (b) if the Shares are not registered in the Securities Depository, the Parent Issuer shall register the new Shares in its shareholder register.
