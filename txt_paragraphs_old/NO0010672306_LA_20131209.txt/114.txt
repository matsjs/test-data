10.5 Tranche C3 Maturity Date and Call Option.
(a) The Tranche C3 Bonds will mature in whole on the Tranche C3 Maturity Date at par (100% of the principal face amount) plus all accrued and unpaid interest plus, if applicable, the Tranche C3 Profit (less any Tranche C3 Profit that has been paid due to a partial redemption of such Bonds pursuant to Clause 10.5(b) below).
(b) Subject to Clause 10.8(d), Cecon 1 may at any time (on one or several occasions) redeem all or part o f the Tranche C3 Bonds (the "Tranche C3 Call Option") at par (100% of the principal face amount) plus accrued and unpaid interest on the redeemed Tranche C3 Bonds to, but not including, the applicable redemption date, without additional premium or penalty; provided that in the event no Tranche C l Bonds remain outstanding on the Tranche C3 Issue Date and at least USD 55 million of the Tranche C2 Bonds have been issued, the pro rata portion of the Tranche C3 Profit with respect to the portion of the aggregate principal amount so redeemed shall immediately become due and payable.
10-6 Tranche D Maturity Date and Call Option.
(a) The Tranche D Bonds will mature in whole on the Tranche D Maturity Date at par (100% of the principal face amount) plus all accrued and unpaid interest.
(b) Subject to Clauses 10.8(d), Cecon 3 may at any time (on one or several occasions) redeem all or part o f the Tranche D Bonds (the "Tranche D Call Option") at par (100% of the principal face amount) plus accrued and unpaid interest on the redeemed Tranche D Bonds to, but not including, the applicable redemption date, without additional premium or penalty.
10.7 Additional Bonds Maturity Date and Call Option.
(a) Any Additional Bonds (other than the Tranche B2 Bonds, the Tranche C2 Bonds and the Tranche C3 Bonds) will mature in whole on the applicable Maturity Date at par (100% of the principal face amount) plus the Additional Bonds Profit (less any Additional Bonds Profit that has been paid due to a partial redemption of such Bonds pursuant to Clause 10.7(b) below).
