10.5.3
The NCG Leverage Ratio Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice o f the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent o f the redemption request. The settlement date o f the NCG Leverage Ratio Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end o f the thirty (30) days exercise period o f the NCG Leverage Ratio Put Option.
10.5.4
On the settlement date o f the NCG Leverage Ratio Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.5.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
