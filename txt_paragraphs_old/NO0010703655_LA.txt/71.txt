(f)
Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction (including the Acquisitions) with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value, save for any intra group transactions carried out between (a) any companies within the HoldCo Group; (b) any companies within the Group not being part of the HoldCo Group; or (c) any company within the HoldCo Group and any company within the Group not being part o f the HoldCo Group, provided that any transfer of value as compared to fair market value is transferred to the company within the HoldCo Group and such transfer is permissible by the applicable law.
(g)
Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
