(b) No dividend restrictions in the Group

The Issuer shall procure that no Group Company (other than the Issuer) shall create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or Security) restricting any such Group Company from paying dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders to the extent such contractual obligation is reasonably likely to prevent the Issuer from complying with its payment obligations under the Bond Agreement.
(c) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that none of the other companies in the HoldCo Group, create permit to subsist or allow to exist any Security over any of its present or future respective assets or its revenues, other than (i) any lien arising by operation of law in the Ordinary Course o f Business, (ii) the Security Interests; and (iii) any Mandatory Buy Back Offer Pledge.
(d) Financial support restrictions
The Issuer, and any other company in the HoldCo Group, shall not grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting Security) ("Financial Support") to any party, other than (i) guarantees granted by the Issuer in the Ordinary Course o f Business (ii) the Third Party Loans, and (iii) any intercompany loans granted from the Issuer to a HoldCo Company.
