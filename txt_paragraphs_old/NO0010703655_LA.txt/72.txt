13.4
Special covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind, repurchase o f shares or make other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer), or grant loans, make any payment or prepayment on the Shareholder Loans (regardless of who is the creditor) or make other distributions or transactions implying a transfer o f value to its shareholders exceeding NOK 5 million for each year, provided always that immediately after such a transaction the Issuer complies with the financial covenants it has undertaken to comply with during the term o f the Bond Issue.
The Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company repurchases shares of the Issuer, grants loans or makes other distributions or transactions implying a transfer of value to the shareholders o f the Issuer, unless such transfer o f value is deemed permitted as set forth above.
For the avoidance of doubt, the Acquisitions shall not be prohibited by this Clause 13.4(a).
