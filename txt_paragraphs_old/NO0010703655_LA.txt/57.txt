Disposal o f assets / Mandatory Buy Back Offer
(i) the Issuer shall deposit the proceeds from the disposal into an account in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations detailed in this Clause 10.4 (the "M andatory Buy Back Offer Pledge");
(ii) the Issuer shall give notice to the Bond Trustee o f the occurrence of such event, giving reasonable details thereof, (a "Mandatory Buy Back Notice"); and
(iii) each Bondholder shall have a right to sell and the Issuer shall be obligated to buy back Bonds during a period of 30 days following the Mandatory Buy Back Notice at 100% of par value, plus accrued interests up to and including the settlement date for such offer.
10.4.2
The Mandatory Buy Back Offer shall be limited to the proceeds received by the Issuer or such other company in the HoldCo Group (as the case may be) in the Mandatory Buy Back Event and shall be applied pro rata between the Bondholders that have decided to sell.
10.4.3
To the extent the sale proceeds exceeds the amount necessary to buy back Bonds, any surplus is to be released to the Issuer from the blocked account and the Issuer shall be released from the Mandatory Buy Back Offer Pledge. Bonds repurchased by the Issuer pursuant to a Mandatory Buy Back Offer shall immediately be discharged.
