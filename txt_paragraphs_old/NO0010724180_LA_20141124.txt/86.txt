3
Continuing guarantee
This Guarantee is a continuing guarantee and will extend to the ultimate balance of all sums payable by the Issuer under the Finance Documents, regardless of (i) any intermediate payment or discharge in whole or in part or (ii) the Bond Trustee enforcing any other security (if any) granted for the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
