Continuing guarantee
4
Dispute resolution and legal venue
The Guarantor irrevocably and unconditionally agrees that:
(a)
this Guarantee and all disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Guarantee
between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and the Guarantor shall be governed by
Norwegian law.
(b) all disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Guarantee shall, subject to Clause 4(c) below, be exclusively resolved by the courts of Norway, with the District Court of Oslo as sole legal venue.
