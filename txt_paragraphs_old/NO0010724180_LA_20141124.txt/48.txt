13.4.4 Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make any loans or other distributions or payments to its shareholders (including but not limited to payment of principal or interest on any shareholder loans), or other transactions implying a transfer o f value to its shareholders, whether in cash or in kind, (a "Distribution"), exceeding 50% of Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year.
Notwithstanding the above, the Issuer may repurchase shares as part of its employee incentive program for an aggregate consideration of up to NOK 5,000,000 in any financial year, however, always provided that no Event of Default has occurred or is threatened and the Liquidity is no less than NOK 8,000,000 tested pro forma for any repurchase of shares to be made, in which tire pro forma testing shall be made prior to making any such repurchase.
Any un-utilised portion of the permitted Distribution pursuant to the above may not be carried forward to any subsequent financial year.
