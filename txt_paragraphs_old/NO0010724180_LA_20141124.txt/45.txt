13.3.5 Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations to any person not being a member of the Group, unless:
(a) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(b) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
Furthermore, the Issuer shall not sell or transfer title to any of its intellectual property rights to any third party, except that it may do so to a wholly owned subsidiary if all the shares in that subsidiary are pledged to the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for all amounts outstanding under the Finance Documents, including but not limited to principal, interest, fees and expenses.
