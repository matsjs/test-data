13.4.1 Negative pledge The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, create, incur or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance over any of its present or future respective assets or revenues, other than the encumbrances granted to secure the following:
(b) any security granted under any hedging arrangements entered into on market terms and as part of the ordinary course of business of the Issuer and for non-speculative purposes;
(c) any lien arising by operation of law;
(d) any security arising under any retention of title, hire purchase or conditional sale arrangement or arrangements having similar effect in the ordinary course of trading; and
(e) any netting or set-off arrangement entered into by the Issuer in the ordinary course of its banking arrangements for the purpose of netting debit and credit balances of the Issuer.
13.4.2 Financial Indebtedness restrictions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
