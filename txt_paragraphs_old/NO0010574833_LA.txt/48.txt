10.2 Call Option 10.2.1. The Issuer may redeem the entire Bond Issue as follows:
(i) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2011 up to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2012 at a price equal to 107 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount;
(ii) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2012 up to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2013 at a price equal to 106 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount;
(iii) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2013 up to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2014 at a price equal to 105 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount;
(iv) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2014 up to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2015 at a price equal to 104 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount;
(v) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2015 up to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2016 at a price equal to 103 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount; and
(vi) from the Interest Payment Date in May 2016 up to, but not including, Maturity Date at a price equal to 102 % plus accrued interest on redeemed amount.
