18.2.3
All moneys amount covered by the Defeasance Pledge shall be applied by the Bond Trustee, in accordance with the provisions of this Bond Agreement, to the payment to the Bondholders of all sums due to them under this Bond Agreement on the due date thereof.
Any excess funds not required for the payment of principal, premium and interest to the Bondholders (including any expenses, fees etc. due to the Bond Trustee hereunder) shall be returned to the Issuer.
18.3 Assignment o f the Second Priority Loan
No provision in this Bond Agreement shall prevent the restructuring or refinancing of the Second Priority Loan including by way of an assignment or other transfer of debtor positions from the Parent to the Issuer as contemplated under and in accordance with the Second Loan Agreement, and the Issuer shall in such event be entitled to grant to the lenders of the Second Priority Loan second priority Security Interest in all the same assets of the Issuer and the Charterer as being subject to the first priority Security Interest in favour of the Bond Trustee under this Bond Loan Agreement and constituting the Security Documents hereunder. Such second priority Security Interest shall be subject to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement as amended after the implementation of the assignment of the Second Loan as provided for herein.
