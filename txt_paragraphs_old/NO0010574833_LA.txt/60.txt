13.3.3 Continuation o f business
(i) The Issuer shall not cease to carry out its business, or change its type of organization or jurisdiction of organization.
(ii) The Issuer shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature or scope of the business of the Issuer or the Charterer from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement or as contemplated by this Bond Agreement. Specifically, the Issuer shall not invest or take part in any other activity than solely related to the ownership and operation of Rig.
13.3.4 Disposal o f business
The Issuer shall not be entitled to sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless the Loan is repaid in full pursuant to Clause 10.2 or Clause 10.4.
