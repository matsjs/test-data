"Issuer Accounts" means the Issuer Earnings Account, the Issuer Operating Account and the Issuer Retention Account.
"Issuer Earnings Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank into which any earnings of the Issuer (including earnings from any Bareboat Charter or Drilling Contract) shall be paid. The account shall be pledged on 1st priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Issuer Operating Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank (in the event the Drilling Contract is entered into directly by the Issuer) into which budgeted operating expenses of the Issuer in connection with operation of the Rig shall be paid. The account shall be pledged on 1st priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Issuer Retention Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank into which such amounts necessary in order to service (i) interest and instalments on this Bond Issue, and (ii) interest on the Second Priority Loan will be paid. The account shall be pledged on l sl priority and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Issuer's Bonds" means Bonds owned by the Issuer, any party or parties who has decisive influence over the Issuer, or any party or parties over whom the Issuer has decisive influence.
"Issuer Share Pledge" means the 1st priority share pledge granted by the Parent over all of the shares (100%) in the Issuer, including letters of resignation from all board members as well as a covenant to obtain letters of resignation from future board members.
"Manager" means Pareto Securities AS, Dronning Mauds gt. 3, NO-0115 Oslo, Norway, being the manager for the Bond Issue.
