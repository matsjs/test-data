13.6 Rig Covenants
13.6.1 Ma intenance and insuranee
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance and insurance of the Rig and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times. To the extent a Bareboat Charter has been entered into, the Issuer shall ensure that (i) the Charterer and/or the Rig Manager run proper maintenance of the Rig according to planned maintenance systems, (ii) the Charterer remains responsible for all operating costs relating to the Rig and (iii) the Charterer reserves sufficient amount for survey costs on a monthly basis.
The Rig shall be adequately insured (including war risk) against hull & machinery risks at least to the full value of the Rig and at least 125 % of the outstanding amount under the Finance Documents, and a third party liability insurance as per industry standards, as well as any additional insurance required under any law or Drilling Contract. The insurances, and any loss payee clause or similar provisions therein, shall be in accordance with the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan or other insurance plan with at least equally favourable terms (in the reasonable opinion of the Issuer).
