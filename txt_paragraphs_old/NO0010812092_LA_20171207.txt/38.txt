2.4 Status of the Bonds (a) The Bonds will constitute senior and secured debt obligations of the Issuer and will rank (i) pari passu between themselves and (ii) at least pari passu with all other senior creditors of the Issuer (except in respect of claims mandatorily preferred by law) and (iii) subject to the super senior status of any Permitted Hedging Obligations, pari passu with the other Secured Parties in respect of the Security.
(b) Pursuant to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement, any Hedge Counterparty will receive (i) the proceeds from any enforcement of the Transaction Security and certain distressed disposals and (ii) any payments following any other enforcement event prior to the Bondholders, in each case in accordance with the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement.
