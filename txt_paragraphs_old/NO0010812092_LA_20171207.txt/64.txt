13.8 Disposals The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company or the Parent will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of:
(a) with respect to the Parent, any shares in the Issuer;
(b) with respect to the Issuer, any shares in the Operating Company; or
(c) with respect to the Issuer or any other Group Company, all or a substantial part of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to
