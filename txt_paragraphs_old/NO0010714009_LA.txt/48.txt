13.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Intra-group transactions All transactions between the Issuer and any of the companies in the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
