10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part at any time from and including the Issue Date to, but not including the Maturity Date at 101% of par plus accrued and unpaid interests on the redeemed amount.
The applicable call price above shall be determined on the basis of the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depositoiy).
