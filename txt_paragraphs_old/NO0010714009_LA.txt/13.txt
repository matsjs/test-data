"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
"Manager" means the manager for the Bond Issue, being Sparebank 1 Markets AS.
"Margin" means:
(i)
from and including the First Interest Accrue Date to the First Interest
Payment Date, ten percentage points (10.00%) per annum; and
(ii) from and including the First Interest Payment Date to the Maturity Date, six point sixty percentage points (6.60%) per annum.
"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on: (a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Issuer and/or the Group taken as a whole, (b) the Issuer's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents or (c) the validity or enforceability of the Finance Documents.
