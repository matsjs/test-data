Day Count Fraction
Whenever interest is computed on the basis of a year (the "deemed year") which contains
fewer days than the actual number of days in the calendar year of calculation, such rate of
interest shall be expressed as a yearly rate for the purposes of the Interest Act (Canada) by
multiplying such rate of interest by the actual number of days in the calendar year of
calculation and dividing it by the number of days in the deemed year.
