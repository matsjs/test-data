(f) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not and shall ensure that no Guarantor shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any Security over any of its present or future respective assets (including, but not limited to, the shares in the Subsidiaries) or revenues, other than the encumbrances granted to secure any of the following:
(i) the Senior Bank Facilities;
(ii) any derivative hedging related to the Group's hedging policy, entered into with any of the Senior Finance Parties (or their affiliates);
(iii) obligations incurred by any Obligor towards its trade creditors (inter alia credit from suppliers, prepayments from customers and similar obligations incurred towards other counter parties) in the ordinary course of business and as part of the normal operations of such Obligor;
(iv) any liability incurred by the any Obligor to financial institutions in respect of bid or performance bonds, advance payment guarantees or letter of credit in the ordinary course of business of the Group;
(v) any liability incurred in respect of the Credit Card Facilities and the Cash Management Arrangements;
