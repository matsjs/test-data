22 (39)
(i) Distributions - Issuer
The Issuer shall not:
(i) declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind;
(ii) repurchase of shares or make other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer);
(iii) make any payment (interest or amortizations) under any loans from any shareholders or on any instrument having a similar effect as a loan; or
(iv) grant any loans or make other distributions or transactions constituting a transfer of value to its shareholders;
(items (i)-(iv) collectively referred to as the "Distributions") in excess of CAD 20 million during the fiscal year ended 30 April 2015, and thereafter, during each fiscal year, 40% of the consolidated net annual profit after taxes per financial year for the Group, based on the Financial Statements for the previous financial year beginning with the results of the fiscal year ended 30 April 2015.
Any un-utilized portion of the permitted Distributions pursuant to the above may be carried forward to any subsequent financial year.
