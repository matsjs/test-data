(j) Distributions - Guarantors
The Issuer shall not permit any of its Guarantors to create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right of any such Subsidiary to pay dividends or make other distributions or extend loans to its shareholders, other than such restrictions included in any of the Senior Bank Facilities.
(k) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not engage in, or permit any Guarantor to engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), on terms which are materially less favourable to the Obligor than would be obtainable or reasonably expected to be obtainable at the time in an arm's length transaction.
(l) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation, and shall ensure that no Guarantor changes its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation without the prior written consent of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), such consent not to be unreasonably withheld.
