STATUS OF THE BONDS AND GUARANTEES
8.1
The Bonds shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer. The Bonds and each
Guarantee shall rank at least pari passu with all other senior unsecured obligations of the
Issuer and the respective Guarantor, as the case may be (save for such claims which are
preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general
application), and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
