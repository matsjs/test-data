(m) Senior Debt Facilities restriction The Issuer shall ensure, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies shall ensure, that total amount of Senior Debt Facilities which is secured against the UDW Units shall never constitute more than USD 550 million in average per UDW Unit (the "Cap").
