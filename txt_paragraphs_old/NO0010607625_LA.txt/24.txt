Status of the Bonds and security
The Bonds are unsecured.
Interest
Subject to Clause 13.3(n), the Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 9.50 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falling in October 2011.
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
