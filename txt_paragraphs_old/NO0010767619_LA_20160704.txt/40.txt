Financial Covenant Cure
If the Issuer is in breach with any of its Financial Covenants, then it may enter into a shareholder loan ("Cure Subordinated Loan") with its shareholders to mitigate that covenant breach. Such shareholder loan shall be subordinated with no cash interest payments. It may be redeemed at any time (disregarding the Dividends restrictions above), provided that the Issuer is in compliance with the Financial Covenants immediately thereafter on a pro forma basis. The Cure Subordinated Loan shall be treated as equity when calculating the Equity Ratio.
