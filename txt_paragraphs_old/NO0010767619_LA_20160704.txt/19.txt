2. THE BONDS 2.1 Amount, denomination and ISIN of the Bonds
(a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 1,000,000,000 (ONE BILLION NORWEGIAN KRONER).
(b) The Bonds are denominated in NOK.
