13.8 Pari passu ranking
The Issuer's obligations under the Bond Agreement shall at all times rank at least pari passu with the claims of all its other unsubordinated creditors save for those whose claims are preferred solely by any bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application.
