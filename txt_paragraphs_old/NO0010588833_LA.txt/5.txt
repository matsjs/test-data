"Financial Indebtedness" means any indebtedness incurred in respect of:
(a) moneys borrowed, including acceptance credit;
(b) any bond, note, debenture, loan stock or other similar instrument;
(c) the amount o f any liability in respect o f any lease, hire purchase contract
which would, in accordance with GAAP, be treated as a finance or capital
lease;
(d) receivables sold or discounted (other than any receivables sold on a non
recourse basis);
