CONDITIONS FOR DISBURSEMENT
The Issuer shall provide to the Agent, prior to the issuance of the Bonds the following, in
form and substance satisfactory to the Agent:
(a) the Finance Documents duly executed by the Issuer and the Agent;
(b) a copy of a resolution from the board of directors of the Issuer approving the issue of the Bonds, the terms of the Finance Documents, and resolving to enter into such documents and any other documents necessary in connection therewith; and
(c) the articles of association and certificate of incorporation (as at the date of (b)) of the Issuer; and
(d) evidence that the person who has signed the Finance Documents, the Agency Agreement and any other documents in connection therewith on behalf of the Issuer is duly authorised to do so.
