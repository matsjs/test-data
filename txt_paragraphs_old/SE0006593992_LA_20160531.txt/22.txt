7.
PAYMENTS IN RESPECT OF THE BONDS
7.1
Any payment or repayment under the Finance Documents, or any amount due in respect
of a repurchase of any Bonds, shall be made to such person who is registered as a
Bondholder on the Record Date prior to an Interest Payment Date or other relevant due
date, or to such other person who is registered with the CSD on such date as being
entitled to receive the relevant payment, repayment or repurchase amount.
