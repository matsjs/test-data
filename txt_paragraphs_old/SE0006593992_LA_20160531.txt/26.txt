INTEREST
8.1
Each Initial Bond carries Interest at the Interest Rate calculated on the Nominal Amount
up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Bond will carry
Interest at the Interest Rate calculated on the Nominal Amount from (but excluding) the
Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to its issuance up to (and including) the
relevant Redemption Date.
