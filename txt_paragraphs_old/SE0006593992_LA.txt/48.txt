14.11 Environmental compliance and claims
(i) comply with all Environmental Laws, including in relation to human health and conditions on workplace;
(ii) obtain, maintain and ensure compliance with all requisite Environmental Permits; and
(iii) implement procedures to monitor compliance with and to prevent liability under any Environmental Law, in each case where failure to do so has or is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
(b) The Issuer shall, promptly upon becoming aware of the same, inform the Agent of any claim, proceeding or investigation in respect of any such Environmental Law against any member of the Group which is current, pending or threatened where which, if determined against that member of the Group, has or is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
14.12 Interest Rate Hedging of Senior Credit Facility
(a) The Issuer shall ensure that interest rate hedging arrangements in respect of not less than 100 per cent, of the aggregate amount of the outstanding loans under the Senior Credit Facility are entered into.
(b) With respect to any Senior Credit Facility to be entered into in connection with the issuance of the Bonds, interest rate hedging arrangements shall be entered into within ten Business Days from the Closing Date, and, with respect to any new Financial Indebtedness incurred under paragraph (g) of the definition of "Permitted Debt", interest rate hedging arrangements shall be entered into within twenty Business Days from the incurrence of such debt.
