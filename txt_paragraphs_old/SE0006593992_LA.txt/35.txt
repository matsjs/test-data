11. Transaction Security
11.1 Granting of the Transaction Security
(a) As continuing Security for the due and punctual fulfilment of the Secured Obligations, the Issuer and the Guarantors (subject to corporate law limitations) grant the Transaction Security and the Guarantees to the Secured Creditors as represented by the Security Agent on the terms set out in the Security Documents, the Guarantee Agreement and the Intercreditor Agreement.
