3. Use of Proceeds
The Net Proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds (the Initial Bonds and all Subsequent Bonds) shall be applied towards on-lending to Estancia Logistik Holding by way of the Bond Proceeds Loan for the purpose of financing the New Portfolio Acquisition (including payment of any acquisition costs relating thereto).
