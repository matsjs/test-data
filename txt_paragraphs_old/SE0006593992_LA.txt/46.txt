14.5 Dealings with Related Parties
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that its Subsidiaries, conduct all dealings with the direct and indirect shareholders of the Group Companies (excluding other Group Companies) and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders at arm's length terms, provided that the granting and receiving of Shareholder Loans shall always be considered to be made at arm's length terms.
14.6 Disposal of Assets
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Subsidiary, sell or otherwise dispose of shares in any Subsidiary or of all or substantially all of its or that Subsidiary's assets, or operations to any person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries, unless (i) with respect to disposals of any Property or Group Company owning a Property, the net proceeds from such disposal shall be applied against repayment of the loans under the Senior Credit Facilities in an amount equivalent to the release price stipulated in the Senior Credit Facility Documents for such Property or Group Company owning such Property, and (ii) provided that, it does not have a Material Adverse Effect. The Issuer shall notify the Agent of any such transaction and, upon request by the Agent, provide the Agent with any information relating to the transaction which the Agent deems necessary (acting reasonably).
14.7 Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
14.8 Loans Out
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide any loan to any third party outside the Group.
