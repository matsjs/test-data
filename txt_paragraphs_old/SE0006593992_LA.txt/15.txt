"LTV Ratio" means at any time in relation to the Holding Group, the aggregate of the Net Interest Bearing Debt of the Holding Group as a percentage of the Value of all Properties owned by the Holding Group.
"Maintenance Test" means the test of the financial maintenace covenants as set out in Clause 13.1 (Maintenance Test).
"Market Loan" means any loan or other indebtedness where an entity issues commercial paper, certificates, subordinated debentures, bonds or any other debt securities (including, for the avoidance of doubt, medium term note programmes and other market funding programmes), provided in each case that such instruments and securities are or can be subject to trade on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm or any other regulated or unregulated recognised market place.
"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on:
(a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Group taken as a whole;
(b) the Group's ability to perform and comply with the Finance Documents; or
(c) the validity or enforceability of the Finance Documents.
"Net Proceeds" means the proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds which after deduction has been made for the Transaction Costs, including fees, payable by the Issuer to Nordic Fixed Income AB and Swedbank AB (publ) as bookrunners for the services provided in relation to the placement and issuance of the Bonds.
