"Securities Act" means the U.S. Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
"Security Agent" means the security agent, appointed by the Secured Creditors pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement, holding the Transaction Security on behalf of the Secured Creditors, initially being Swedbank AB (publ).
"Security Documents" means:
(a) the share pledge agreement, dated on or about the Closing Date, between the Issuer and the Security Agent with respect to the shares in the Estancia Logistik Holding;
(b) the share pledge agreement, dated on or about the Closing Date, between Estancia Logistik Holding and the Security Agent with respect to the shares in the other Guarantors, the New Portfolio Target and the Kompassen Target;
(c) the intercompany loan pledge agreement, dated on or about the Closing Date, between the Issuer and the Security Agent with respect to the Bond Proceeds Loan; and
(d) if applicable, the intercompany loan pledge agreement, dated on or about the Closing Date, between Estancia Logistik Holding and the Security Agent with respect to material long term intra-group loans provided by Estancia Logistik Holding to the other Guarantors.
"Senior Credit Facility" means a credit facility to be provided to a Propco by Swedbank AB (publ) (or any other bank) for acquisition of shares and assets and refinancing of existing debt of the relevant Propco (and any refinancing, amendments or replacements thereof), amended from time to time (as the case may be) including any hedging required under such credit facility and where such hedging arrangements have been entered into by the relevant Propco or Estancia Logistik Holding.
"Senior Credit Facility Creditors" mean the finance parties under the Senior Credit Facility Documents.
