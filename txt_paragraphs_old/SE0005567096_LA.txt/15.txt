"Transaction Costs" means all fees, costs and expenses incurred by a Group Company in connection with the Bond Issue, a Subsequent Bond Issue and the listing of the Bonds on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm.
"Written Procedure" means the written or electronic procedure for decision making among the Holders in accordance with Clause 16 ( Written Procedure).
