2.2 The Bonds
NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN BW Offshore Limited Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2012/2017".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 063807.5.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization 2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used for general coiporate purposes.
