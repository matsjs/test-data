10 MAT^URITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION 10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Final Maturity Date, and shall be repaid by the Issuer at 100% of par value plus accrued and unpaid interests.
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part (the "Call Option") as follows: (a) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in March 2016 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in March 2017 at a price equal to 103.50% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount); (b) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in March 201 7 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in March 2018 at a price equal to 102.50% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount); (c) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in March 2018 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2018 at a price equal to 101.00% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount); and (d) any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2018 to, but not including, the Final Maturity Date at a price equal to 100.50% of par value (plus accrued interests on redeemed amount).
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried outpro rata between the Bonds (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.2.4 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
