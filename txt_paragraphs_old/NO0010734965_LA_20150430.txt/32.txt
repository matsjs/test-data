10 MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Final Maturity Date, and shall be repaid by the Issuer at 100% of par value plus accrued and unpaid interests.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part (the "Call Option") as follows: (a) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2017 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2018 at a price equal to 103.50% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount);
(b) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2018 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2019 at a price equal to 102.50% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount);
