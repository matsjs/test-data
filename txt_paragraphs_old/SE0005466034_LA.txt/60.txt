13.3
Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the acceleration of
the Bonds constitute escrow funds {redovisningsmedel) and must be held on a separate
interest-bearing account on behalf of the Bondholders and the other interested parties. The
Agent shall arrange for payments of such funds in accordance with this Clause 13 as soon
as reasonably practicable.
