16.2.2 The Bondholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Bond Tmstee. A request for a Bondholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Bond Taistee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
16.2.3 If the Bond Trustee has not summoned a Bondholders' Meeting within ten Business Days after having received a valid request, then the requesting party may summons the Bondholders' Meeting itself.
