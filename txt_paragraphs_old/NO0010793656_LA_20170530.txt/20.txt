"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 17.4.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
"Security Interests" means:
(a) the Share Pledge;
(b) the Sponsor Facilities Assignment; and
(c) the DSRA Pledge.
"Share Pledge" means the first priority Norwegian law share pledge to be granted by the Holding Company in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Bondholders) over all of its shares in the Issuer together with, inter alia, executed notices and acknowledgements, as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Sponsor Agent" means Nordic Capital VII Limited.
"Sponsor Funds" means Nordic Capital VII Alpha, L.P. and Nordic Capital VII Beta L.P.
