13.5 l Dividends
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind, repurchase of shares or make any loans or similar transactions (including, but not limited to, (i) total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer, (ii) cash service of Subordinated Loans, and/or (iii) cash service of the Sponsor Facilities Agreement (except in relation to the Security Agent's exercise of its rights related to the Sponsor Facilities Assignment), or other distributions or transactions implying a transfer of value to its direct or indirect shareholders.
13.5.2 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than the Permitted Indebtedness.
