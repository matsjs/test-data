17.2 Liability and indemnity
17.2.1
The Bond Trustee (including in its capacity as Security Agent) is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders and the Issuer as a result of gross negligence or wilful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set out in this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document.
