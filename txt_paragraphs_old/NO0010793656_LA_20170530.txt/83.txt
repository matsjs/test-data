19.4.3
Upon written request from a Bondholder to the Bond Trustee, the Bond Trustee shall, to the extent permitted by law, regulation and the relevant documents and within a reasonable time (and always subject to capacity of the Bond Trustee and the payment of a reasonable fee to the Bond Trustee), provide such Bondholder with a copy of any Finance Documents or any other statements, certificates, or legal opinions that the Bond Trustee has requested and received in relation to the Bond Issue and which it has in its possession (including but not limited to the Financial Statements provided pursuant to Clause 13.2.1 (c) and the Interim Accounts provided pursuant to Clause 13.2.1 (d).
19.5 Amendments
All amendments of this Bond Agreement shall be made in writing, and shall unless otherwise provided for by this Bond Agreement, only be made with the approval of all parties hereto
