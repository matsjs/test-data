9.1 Interest shall accrue on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 16 per cent. (16.00%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 On each Interest PIK Date, the interest shall be added to the principal o f the Bond Issue by issuance of Bonds in principal amount equal to the accrued interest (the "PIK Bonds"). Such PIK Bonds shall be issued pro rata to each Bondholder (adjusted down to avoid fractions of Bonds) on the relevant Interest PIK Date.
9.3 The relevant interest amount shall accrue and be compounded quarterly in arrear and calculated based on a period from, and including, the Issue Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest PIK Date, and thereafter from and including, that Interest PIK Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest PIK Date.
