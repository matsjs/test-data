10.4 Sale Event
Upon a Sale Event, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer or the Rig Owning Company (as the case may be) receives the proceeds following such Sale Event and after redemption of the JACKT01 following such Sale Event, redeem all the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds).
10.5 Total Loss
Upon the occurrence of a Total Loss, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer receives the insurance proceeds following such Total Loss, redeem all the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds)
