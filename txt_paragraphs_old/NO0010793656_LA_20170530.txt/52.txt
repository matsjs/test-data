The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities if such merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization has a Material Adverse Effect.
13.3 3 De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
13.3.4 Continuation o f business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
13.3.5 Ann 's length transactioiis
The Issuer shall not engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
