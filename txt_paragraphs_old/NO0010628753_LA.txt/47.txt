Fees and expenses
14.2 The expenses and fees payable to the Bond Tmstee shall be paid by the Issuer and are set forth in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Tmstee. Fees and expenses payable to the Bond Tmstee which, due to the Issuer's insolvency or similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the payments to the Bondholders.
14.3 The Issuer shall cover all public fees in connection with the Bonds and the Finance Documents. Any public fees levied on the trade of Bonds in the secondary market shall be paid by the Bondholders, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation, and the Issuer is not responsible for reimbursing any such fees.
14.4 In addition to the fee due to the Bond Tmstee pursuant to Clause 14.2 and normal expenses pursuant to Clauses 14.1 and 14.3, the Issuer shall, on demand, cover extraordinary expenses incurred by the Bond Tmstee in connection with the Bonds, as determined in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Tmstee.
14.5 The Issuer is responsible for withholding any withholding tax imposed by applicable law on any payments to the Bondholders.

Events of Default
15.1 The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Tmstee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events (which shall be referred to as an "Event of Default") if:
(a) Non-payment Any member of the Debtor Group fail to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Tmstee, it is obvious that such failure will be remedied, and payment in full is made, within 5 - five - Business Days following the original due date.
