2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 1,400,000,000 (Norwegian kroner onebillionfourhundredmillion).
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Songa Offshore SE Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2011/ 2016".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 062875.3.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1
The net proceeds from the Bonds shall be employed for general corporate purposes including financing of the initial installment of the 2ndnewbuild harsh environment semi-submersible rig to be constructed on Daewoo Shipbuilding & Marine Engineering Co., Ltd. pursuant to a building contract dated 6 September 2011. The amount of such initial installment is USD 113 million.
