(e) Disposal o f business The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no member of the Debtor Group shall, be entitled to sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its or that member of the Debtor Group's assets or operations to any person not being a member of the Group, unless:
(i) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
The Issuer shall notify the Trustee of any such transaction, and upon request provide relevant details thereof.
