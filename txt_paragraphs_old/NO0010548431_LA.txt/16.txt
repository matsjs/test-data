Listing
The Bonds will not be listed on any Exchange on the Issue Date, but the Issuer shall subsequently apply for a listing of the Bonds on Oslo Bors.
If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
Registration in a Securities Register
The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall be registered in the Securities Register according to the Securities Register Act and the conditions of the Securities Register, effective from and including the Issue Date.
The Issuer shall promptly arrange for notification to the Securities Register of any changes in the terms and conditions of this Bond Agreement. The Bond Trustee shall receive a copy o f the notification.
