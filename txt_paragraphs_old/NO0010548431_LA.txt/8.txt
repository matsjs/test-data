"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on (a) any Obligor's ability to perform their obligations under this Bond Agreement; or (b) the validity or enforceability of this Bond Agreement and any Security Documents.
"Material Subsidiary" means any Subsidiary o f the Issuer:
(i) whose total consolidated assets represent at least 5% o f the total consolidated assets of the Group, or
(ii) whose total consolidated net sales represent at least 5% of the total consolidated net sales of the Group,
I f there is a dispute as to whether or not a member o f the Group is a Material Subsidiary, a certificate o f the auditors o f the Issuer will be, in the absence of manifest error, conclusive.
"Maturity Date" means 20 November 2012 or an earlier maturity date as provided for in this Bond Agreement. Any further adjustment may be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Nemo" means the Issuer's participating interest at any time in Norwegian production license 148 together with Issuer's corresponding rights, interests, entitlements and obligations under all existing and effective agreements relating to such interest.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means the sum o f all interest bearing debt of the Group on a consolidated basis according to GAAP including financial leases, but except for debt financing of tax exploration expenditure which is tax refundable on the Norwegian Continental Shelf, and further reduced with free cash and cash equivalents (i.e. cash in hand or amounts standing to the credit o f any current and/or on deposit accounts with an acceptable bank) as shown in the relevant accounts at any time.
