(d) Continuation o f business The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that any Group Company shall not, cease to carry out its business and/or make a material change to the general nature or scope o f its business from that carried on at the date o f this Bond Agreement, or as contemplated by this Bond Agreement, (in each case) if this would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(e) Disposal o f business The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that any Group Company shall not be entitled to sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
(i) the transaction is earned out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
For the sake of clarity; a transfer o f Oselvar and Nemo from the Issuer to any wholly owned subsidiary o f a Guarantor shall not be affected by b) and e) above.
14.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Intra-group transactions All transactions between any companies in the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions o f applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 o f the Companies Act.
