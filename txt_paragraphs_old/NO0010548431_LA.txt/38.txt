12.4 Interest in the event o f late payment
12.4.1
In the event that payment o f interest or principal is not made on the relevant Payment Date, the amount outstanding shall bear interest from the Payment Date at an interest rate equivalent to the interest rate according to Clause 10 plus 5.00 percentage points per annum.
12.4.2 The interest charged under this Clause 12.4 shall be added to the defaulted amount on each respective Interest Payment Date relating thereto until the defaulted amount has been repaid in full.
12.4.3 The outstanding amounts shall bear interest as stated above until payment is made, whether or not the Bonds are declared to be in default pursuant to Clause 16.1 (a), cf. Clauses 1 6 .2 - 16.4.
