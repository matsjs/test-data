Definitions
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 14 and 19.2 and other Clauses may be defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Adjusted EBITDA" means the Group's earnings before exploration costs, financial items, taxes, depreciation and amortization and write-downs according to GAAP, calculated on a 12 month rolling basis.
"Attachment" means any attachments or appendices to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bond Reference Rate" means 3 months NIBOR.
