12.5 Irregular payments
12.5.1
In case of irregular payments, the Bond Trustee may instruct any Obligor or Bondholder o f other payment mechanisms than described in Clause 12.1 or 12.2 above. The Bond Trustee may also obtain payment information regarding Bondholders' accounts from the Securities Register or Account Managers.
