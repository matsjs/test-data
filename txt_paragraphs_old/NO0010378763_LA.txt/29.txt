13.3 The Conversion Price shall be adjusted pursuant to Clauses 14 and 15.
Request for conversion takes place by the Bondholder notifying its Account Manager of the number of Bonds which shall be converted. The Account Manager shall then promptly forward the request to the Paying Agent. The Paying Agent shall carry the conversion into effect at the Conversion Date.
Conversion will be effected by a set-off of the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted against the issuing of the whole number of shares resulting from dividing the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted by the Conversion Price. Any excess
