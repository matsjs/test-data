Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 9.00 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made semiannually in arrears on each Interest Payment Date, the first interest Payment Date being in January 2008.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless:
(a) the last day of the period is the 31 St day of a month but the first day of the period is a day other than the 30th or 31 st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or
(b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
