10 Maturity of the Loan, Call Option and Change of Control
10.1 The Loan will run without installments and mature in whole on the Maturity Date at par (100%).
10.2 The Borrower may redeem parts of the Loan or the entire Loan as follows (Call Option): (i) any time from the Disbursement Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in July 2008 at 140 % of par, and
(ii) any time from and included the Interest Payment Date in July 2008 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in January 2009 at 150 % of par.
For the avoidance of doubt, interest accrued but not yet due on a redemption date in respect of a call will not be paid.
Should the Borrower exercise its Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed of this (the Bondholders in writing via VPS) no later than thirty (30) Banking Days before the date of redemption.
Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this Clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
