13.5 The Borrower is responsible for ensuring that:
(b) the Remaining Loan is being written down within the Conversion Date, and
(c) Shares issued upon conversion are registered in the Securities Depository on the Conversion Date.
If the share capital increase is not registered in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises at the Conversion Date, the Borrower shall ensure that the shares following the conversion shall be temporarily registered in the Securities Depository (with temporary ISIN).
13.6 Shares issued upon conversion give rights in the Borrower as of the registration of the share capital increase in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises, unless the general meeting of the Borrower has resolved differently.
14 Adjustment of the Conversion Price
14.1 In the event of a new issue of Shares in which shareholders have preferential rights to subscribe for the new shares, shall
(i) if the Shares are listed at an Exchange, a new Conversion Price be calculated as follows:
(Shareprice x number of shares before share issue) + (share issue price x number of new shares)
