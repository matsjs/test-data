9. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND CALL OPTION 9.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
9.2 Call Option 9.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds (all or nothing) ("Call Option") from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 102,00 % of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).

9.2.2 The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercising of the Call Option at least fifteen (15) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
9.2.3 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds, in respect of each such Bond, the aggregate principal amount of all Bonds held by such Bondholder (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued on such Bonds up to (but not including) the relevant settlement date.
9.2.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
