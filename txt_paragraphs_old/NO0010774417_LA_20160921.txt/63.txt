Cross default
An event occurs in relation to the Issuer or any other Group Company where;
(i) any Financial Indebtedness or guarantee is not paid when due nor within any applicable grace period;
(ii) any Financial Indebtedness is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described);
