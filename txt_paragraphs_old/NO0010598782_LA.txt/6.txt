1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses may be defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Additional Margin" means an increase of margin, and hence also an increase of the coupon rate, in the event that the Adjusted Equity Ratio is below 30% on any Reporting Date. The applicable coupon rate for the interest period commencing immediately succeeding such Reporting Date, shall be increased by 0.50 percentage points for every 0.25 percentage points the Adjusted Equity Ratio is below 30%.
For the avoidance of doubt, if the latest reported Adjusted Equity Ratio is 29.8 %, the Additional Margin for the following interest period shall be 0.00 percentage points per annum. Further, if the latest reported Adjusted Equity Ratio is 27.5 %, the Additional Margin for the following interest period shall be 5.00 percentage points per annum.
