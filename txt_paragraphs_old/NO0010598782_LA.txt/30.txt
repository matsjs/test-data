10.1 Maturity
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in January 2012 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in January 2013 at 106.50 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount;
(ii) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in January 2013 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in January 2014 at 105.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount;
(iii) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in January 2014 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in January 2015 at 104.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(iv) at any time Rom the Interest Payment Date in January 2015 to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 102.50 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
