2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 150,000,000 (U.S. Dollar one hundred and fifty million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as " 12 per cent Chloe Marine Corporation Ltd. Second Lien Callable Bond Issue 2011/2016".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 062886.0.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
