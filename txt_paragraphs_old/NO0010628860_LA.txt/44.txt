10.4.2 Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
10.4.3 If the conditions precedent set out in Clause 6.2 {Conditions Precedentfor Utilisation) are not in place within 60 days after the Issue Date, the Bonds shall be repaid at a price equal to 101% of par value plus accrued interest. All proceeds on the Escrow Account may be used for such purpose.
10.4.4 The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory redemption according to this clause 10.4, at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption. On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date of such mandatory redemption.
10.4.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
