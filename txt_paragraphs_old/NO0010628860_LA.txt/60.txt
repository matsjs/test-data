13.6 Vessel covenants (a) Insurance
(i) The Issuer shall maintain or ensure that the Vessel is insured against such risks, including but not limited to, hull and machinery, protection & indemnity (including maximum cover for pollution liability for the highest amount then available in the insurance market for vessels of similar age, size and type as the
30(48)
Vessel), hull interest and/or freight interest and war risk insurances (including acts of terrorism and piracy, blocking and trapping), as per the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan of 1996, version 2010, in such amounts, and with first class insurers. All insurers shall have minimum S&P rating of BBB+, and the P&I Insurer shall be member of an international group of P&I Clubs.
(ii) The hull and machineiy insurance shall be at least equal to hundred and twenty five per cent (125%) of the aggregate of the Bond Issue and the Senior Credit Facility plus the mark-to market exposure under any Swap Agreements from time to time, Further, the aggregate insurable value of the Vessel shall be at least equal to the higher of the Vessel's Market Value and hundred and twenty per cent (120%) of the aggregate of the outstanding amounts under the Finance Documents and the Senior Credit Facility, and the hull and machinery insurance shall at all times cover at least eighty per cent (80%) of the insurable value.
(iii) The Issuer shall procure that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) is noted as second priority mortgagee in the insurance contracts, together with the confirmation from the underwriters to the Bond Trustee thereof that the notice of assignment with regards to the insurances and the loss payable clauses are noted in the insurance contracts and that standard letters of undertaking are executed by the insurers.
(iv) Within reasonable time prior to the expiry date of the relevant insurances, the Issuer shall procure the delivery to the Bond Trustee of a certificate from the insurance broker(s) through whom the Insurances referred to in a) have been renewed and taken out in respect of the Vessel with insurance values as required by b), that such Insurances are in full force and effect and that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Lenders) has been noted by the relevant insurers.
(v) If any of the insurances referred to in a) have been taken out on conditions other than the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan of 1996, version 2010 (as amended from time to time) and/or form part of a fleet cover, the Issuer shall procure that the insurers shall undertake to the Bond Trustee that they shall neither set-off against any claims in respect of the Vessel any premiums due in respect of other Vessels under such fleet cover or any premiums due for other insurances, nor cancel this insurance for reason of non-payment of premiums for other Vessels under such fleet cover or of premiums for such other insurances, and shall undertake to issue a separate policy in respect of the Vessel if and when so requested by the Bond Trustee.
(vi) The Issuer shall procure that the Vessel always is employed in conformity with the terms of the instruments of insurances (including any warranties expressed or implied therein) and comply with such requirements as to extra premium or otherwise as the insurers may prescribe.
