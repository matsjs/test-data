(b) Title
The Issuer will hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the Vessel and, save for insurances taken out by the Subsidiary (if any), the insurances taken out in respect of the Vessel, free of any and all Encumbrances except for those expressly permitted under this Bond Agreement.
(c) Class, flag, name, registry
The Issuer shall ensure that the Vessel maintains its class, flag and name, and that it remains registered in Bermuda or other reputable ship registry acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
