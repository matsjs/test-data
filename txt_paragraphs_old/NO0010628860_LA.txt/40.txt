.10 MATURITY OF THE BONDS, CALL OPTION AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer plus accrued and unpaid interest.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may the entire Bond Issue (all or nothing) as follows (Call Option):
(a) anytime from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 2 years after the Issue Date at a price equivalent to the sum of:
(i) the present value on the relevant record date of 108 % of the par value; and
(ii) the present value on the relevant record date of the remaining coupon payments (less any accrued but unpaid interest) through and including the interest payment day 2 years after the Issue Date,
