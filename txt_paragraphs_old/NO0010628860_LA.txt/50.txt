13.3 General Covenants (a) Paripassu ranking
The Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu with the claims of all its other unsubordinated creditors save for those whose claims are preferred solely by any bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application and any claims under the Senior Credit Facility as set out in the Intercreditor Agreement.
