The Issuer undertakes for so long as any amount is outstanding under the Bonds to comply or, as relevant, procure the compliance with the financial covenants set out in this Clause 12.
The Equity Ratio of the Group shall
a) at any time not be less than thirty five (35) per cent, and
b) at the time of any issue of Market Loans, including Subsequent Bonds, or resolution to distribute dividend (or similar value transfer to the shareholders) not be less than 40 per cent., taking the issue of such Market Loan or dividend into consideration on a pro forma basis.
