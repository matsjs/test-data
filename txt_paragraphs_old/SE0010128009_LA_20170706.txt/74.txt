PRESCRIPTION
The right to receive repayment of the principal of the Bonds shall be prescribed and
become void ten (10) years from the Redemption Date. The right to receive payment of
interest (excluding any capitalised interest) shall be prescribed and become void three (3) years from the relevant due date for payment. The Issuer is entitled to any funds set aside for payments in respect of which the Bondholders' right to receive payment has been
prescribed and has become void.
