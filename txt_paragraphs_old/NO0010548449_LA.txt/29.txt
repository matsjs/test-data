Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
The Bonds shall run without instalments and mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
Call Option The Issuer may redeem parts o f the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from the Disbursement Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2011 at 108 % o f par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2011 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2012 at 106 % of par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2012 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2013 at 105 % o f par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(iv) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2013 to, hut not including, the Maturity Date at 103 % o f par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.
Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures o f the Security Depository).
