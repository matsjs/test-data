11.3.3 On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each o f the Bondholders holding Bonds to be pre-paid, the principal amount of each such Bond and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
11.3.4 For the avoidance o f doubt, the Put Option is an option exercisable at the sole discretion o f the Bondholders, and each Bondholder may elect not to exercise such Put Option and to continue to hold its Bonds.
11.4 Mandatory Redemption 11.4.1 If any of the Issuer's shares in the Guarantors are sold, the Issuer shall redeem 100 %
of the Outstanding Bonds as follow:
(i) if occurring anytime from Disbursement Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2011, at 108% o f par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount); and
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2011 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2012 at 106 % o f par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2012 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2013 at 105 % of par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(iv) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2013 to, but not including, the Maturity Date at 103 % of par, plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
