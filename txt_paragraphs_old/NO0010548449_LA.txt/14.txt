The Bonds will be in denominations o f NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) o f the Bond Issue will be NO 001 054844.9.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue and the 2012 Bond Loan shall exclusively be employed to refinance the existing bond loans NOR01 (NO 001037906.8) and NOR02 (NO 001037907.6). Listing
The Bonds will not be listed on any Exchange on the Issue Date, but the Issuer shall subsequently apply for a listing of the Bonds on Oslo Bors.
If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
Registration in a Securities Register
The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall be registered in the Securities Register according to the Securities Register Act and the conditions o f the Securities Register, effective from and including the Issue Date.
