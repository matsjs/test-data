10 Interest
Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 20 February 2010.
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
Interest shall be calculated on the basis o f a year of 360 days with twelve 30-days months (30/360), unless (i) the last day o f the calculation period is the 31 st day of a month but the first day
of the calculation period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day o f a mouth, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or (ii) the last day of the calculation period is the last day o f the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month).
