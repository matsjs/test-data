5.3
The Issuer and the Trustee shall at all times be entitled to obtain information from the debt
register (skuldbok) kept by the CSD in respect of the Notes. For the purpose of carrying out
any administrative procedure that arises out of the Finance Documents, the Issuing Agent
shall be entitled to obtain information from the debt register kept by the CSD in respect of
the Notes.
