Voluntary total redemption (call option)
9.3.1
At any time on or after the First Call Date, the Issuer may redeem all, but not some only, of
the outstanding Notes at an amount per Note equal to 101.45 per cent. of the Nominal
Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest;

9.3.2
Redemption in accordance with Condition 9.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than fifteen (15) Business Days' notice to the Noteholders and the Trustee. The notice from the Issuer shall specify the Redemption Date and the Record Date on which a person shall be registered as a Noteholder to receive the amounts due on such Redemption Date. The notice is irrevocable but may, at the Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent. Upon fulfilment of the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is bound to redeem the Notes specified in the notice at the applicable amount on the specified Redemption Date.
