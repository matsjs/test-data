"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Råfisklaget Guarantee" means a guarantee, currently in the amount of NOK 8,520,000 issued by DNB Bank ASA on behalf of John Greger AS in favour of Norges Råfisklag as security for John Greger AS' obligations under the Råfisklaget Line o f Credit. The Råfisklaget Guarantee may vaiy on the basis o f the availability of fish in the market and seasonal changes, and the Råfisklaget Gurantee amount may be increased to an amount up to NOK 10,000,000.
"Råfisklaget Line of Credit" means the line of credit made available by Norges Råfisklag to John Greger AS in connection with the purchasing system of Norges Råfisklag, currently in the amount of NOK 17,040,000. The amount available under the Råfisklaget Line of Credit may vary due to availability of fish in the market and seasonal changes. The Råfisklaget Line of Credit may be increased up to NOK 20,000, 000.
"Råfisklaget Security" means the security provided by the Issuer and John Greger AS in the form of (i) a cash deposit, as of the date of this Bond Agreement in the amount of NOK 6,000,000 and which is deposited on a separate bank account with DNB Bank ASA, such bank account being blocked and pledged in favour of DNB Bank ASA, and (ii) two first priority factoring agreements (Norw.: factoringpanf) entered into between John Greger AS and DNB Bank ASA in a total amount of NOK 27.000. 000.
The amount of the cash deposit may be increased to an amount up to NOK 8.000. 000.
"Securities Depository" means the securities depository in which the Bond Issue is registered, being Verdipapirsentralen ASA (VPS) in Noiway.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 17.4.
