Maturity of theBonds and Redemption
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part with settlement at any time horn and including (Call Option):
(a) the date falling one (1) year after the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 2 years after the Issue Date at a price equal to 104% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(b) the date falling two (2) years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 3 years after the Issue Date at a price equal to 102.5% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount); and
