Change of C ontrol............................................If the Issuer experiences a change of control, the holders o f the NOK Senior Secured Notes will have the right to require the Issuer to offer to repurchase the NOK Senior Secured Notes at a purchase price equal to 101% of their principal amount, plus accrued and unpaid interest and additional amounts, if any, to the date of purchase.
