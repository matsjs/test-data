10.4 Upon a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of the bonds at a price of 101 % of par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within the Change of Control Period.
The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent of the Loan (the "Paying Agent") of the pre payment request. The Put Option payment date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the pre-payment request.
