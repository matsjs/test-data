9 Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 13 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on 30 January and 30 July each year, the first interest Payment Date being 30 January 2009.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis o f a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless
a) the last day of the period is the 3 1st day of a month but the first day of the period is a day other than the 30th or 3 Ist day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or
b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
