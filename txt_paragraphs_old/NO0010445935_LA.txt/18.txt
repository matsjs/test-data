2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 55,000,000 (US dollar fifty five million), herein referred to as the "Loan". The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and shall rank pari passu among themselves. The Loan will be described as " 13 percent Polarcus Limited Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2008/2013". The registration number (1SIN) of the Loan will be NO 0 0 10445935. The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date or earlier pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth herein. The net proceeds o f the Loan shall be employed to part-finance the construction of the Vessel through the Vessel Owning Company; hereunder to make payments under the Construction Contracts, operating expenses and other payments related to construction of the Vessel including without limitations advance payments to the Yard and equipment suppliers (the "Purpose of the Loan").
