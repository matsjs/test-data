13." Cash transfer restriction
The Issuer shall procure that no cash or cash equivalent assets are transferred from any Group Company to the Issuer unless such transfer is made for the purpose of satisfying an obligation of the Issuer or making a Restricted Payment which is due within three (3) months from such transfer.
13 s
Dealings with related parties
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each other Group Company will, conduct all dealings with persons other than Group Companies, directly or indirectly, wholly-owned by the Issuer, at arm's length terms, other than if permitted pursuant to Clause 13.1.2.
