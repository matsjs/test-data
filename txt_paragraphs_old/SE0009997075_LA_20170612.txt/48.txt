5.5
A redemption due to a Conditions Precedent Failure shall be made by the Issuer giving
notice to the Noteholders and the Agent promptly following the date when the Conditions
Precedent Failure occurs. The Issuer is bound to redeem the Notes in full at the applicable
amount together with any accrued but unpaid interest on a date specified in the notice from
the Issuer, such date to fall no later than ten (10) Business Days after the effective date of
the notice.
