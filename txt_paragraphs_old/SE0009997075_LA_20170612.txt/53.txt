7.
RIGHT TO ACT ON BEHALF OF A NOTEHOLDER
7.1
If any person other than a Noteholder wishes to exercise any rights under the Finance
Documents, it must obtain a power o f attorney or other proof of authorisation from the
Noteholder or a successive, coherent chain of powers o f attorney or proofs o f authorisation
starting with the Noteholder and authorising such person.
