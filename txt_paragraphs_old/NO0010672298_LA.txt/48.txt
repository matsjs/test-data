2.5 Tranche D Bonds. Subject to the conditions set forth in Clause 6, Cecon 3 may issue the Tranche D Bonds on the Tranche D Issue Date in an aggregate principal amount of USD 100,000,000 (U.S. Dollar one hundred million).
The Tranche D Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1,000.00 each and will rank pari passu between themselves and with all other senior indebtedness of Cecon 3.
The registration number (ISIN) and title of the Tranche D Bonds will be determined on or about the Tranche D Issue Date.
The term of the Tranche D Bonds is from and including the Tranche D Issue Date to the Tranche D Maturity Date.
