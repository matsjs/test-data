2 The Bonds
2.1 Binding nature of this Bond Loan Agreement. By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Loan Agreement and each other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with.
