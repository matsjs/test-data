"Limitation" means a limitation applicable to the Guarantee, the Guarantor Security Documents and the Guarantor Parent Security Documents, limiting the aggregate amount which may be recovered under or pursuant to all these documents, to the amount of the Proceeds Loan (Norwegian: begrenset helgaranti! begrenset helkausjon).
"Loan-to-Value Ratio" means the ratio of (i) the Outstanding Bonds less any amounts standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account and the Minimum Liquidity to (ii) the Market Value.
"Manager" means the manager for the Bond Issue, being Pareto Securities AS of Dronning Mauds gt. 3, 0115 Oslo, Norway.
"Margin" means 3.75 percentage points per annum.
"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means any:
(a) Property Disposal Event;
(b) Leasehold Disposal Event;
(c) Guarantor Disposal Event; or
(d) Total Loss Event.
