8.4.9 Enforcement
(a) The Bond Trustee shall not be obliged before taking steps to enforce this Guarantee or any Security Interest granted by the Guarantor:
(i) to obtain judgement against the Issuer, any Security Provider or any third party in any court or other tribunal;
(ii) to make or file any claim in a bankruptcy or liquidation of the Issuer, any Security Provider or any third party; or
(iii) to take any action whatsoever against the Issuer, any Security Provider or any third party under the Finance Documents, except the giving notice of any payment due hereunder,
and the Guarantor hereby waives all such formalities or rights to which it would otherwise be entitled or which the Bond Trustee would otherwise first be required to satisfy or fulfil before proceeding or making any demand against the Guarantor hereunder, except as required hereunder or by law.
