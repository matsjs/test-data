"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo, Norway.
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Change of Control Event" means if:
(a) the Sponsors cease to hold, directly or indirectly, a minimum of 50.00% in aggregate of the outstanding shares and/or voting rights of the Issuer; or
(b) any person or group (as such term is defined in the Norwegian Limited Liability Companies Act § 1-3) (other than the Sponsors or any indirectly or directly owned subsidiary of the Sponsors (or any of them)) becomes the owner, directly or indirectly, of more than 50.00% of the outstanding shares and/or voting --ights of the Issuer.
"Compliance Certificate" means a certificate substantially in the form set out in Attachment 1 (Compliance Certificate).
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
