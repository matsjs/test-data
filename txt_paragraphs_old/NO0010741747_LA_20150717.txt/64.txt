Upon the occurrence of a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall immediately upon receipt of insurance proceeds and in all circumstances no later than 180 days after the date on which the Total Loss Event occurred, redeem the Bonds at 100% of Face Value (plus accrued interest on redeemed Bonds).
10.4.4
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Mandatory Prepayment clause, any insurance proceeds together with the entire amount in the Escrow Account, the Issuer's Earnings Account and the Guarantor's Earnings Account however limited, in respect of the Guarantor's Earnings Account to the Limitation, may be applied to prepay the Bonds.
