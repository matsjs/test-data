10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (the "Call Option"):
(a) with settlement date on a Business Day at any time from and including the Interest Payment Day falling 3 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Day falling 3 years and 6 months after Issue Da<.e at a price equal to 101.75% of Face Value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed Bonds); and
(b) with settlement date on a Business Day at any time from and including the Interest Payment Day falling 3 years and 6 months after Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100.50% of Face Value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed Bonds).
10.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
