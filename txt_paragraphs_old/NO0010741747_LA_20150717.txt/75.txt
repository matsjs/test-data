(h) Negative pledge
None of the Obligors shall create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any Security over any of its present or future respective assets or its revenues, other than:
(i) Security securing the Secured Obligations;
(ii) any Security arising by operation of law and in the ordinary course of trading; and
(iii) any Security arising under retention of title arrangements (such as finance leases) in the ordinary course of business.
