INTERPRETATION
"Account Bank" means Handelsbanken, Norwegian Branch of Svenska Handelsbanken AB (publ) or any other reputable international bank selected by the Issuer with at least A rating from Standard & Poor's Rating Services or Fitch Ratings Ltd, or A3 rating from Moody's Investors Services Limited.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Additional Security" means additional security granted by the issuer in the form of cash deposited a blocked account with the Account Bank, such account to be pledged by the Issuer on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee as security for the Secured Obligations.
"Attachment" means the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Average Remaining Contract Term" means the weighted average remaining contract term for the Ulven Data Centre and the Rosenholm Data Centre taken together, calculated by adding up the remaining contractual lease payments for the two data centres (disregarding any extension options or rights of early termination), and dividing that with the total annual rent for the two data centres for that relevant year, and then multiplying with the occupancy rate calculated on a total, fixed white space area of 4,845 m2 (being the total white space area as at the date of this Bond Agreement (5,700 m2) adjusted for 15% natural vacancy of data centres).
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
