13.2.2
The Issuer shall in connection with the publication of its financial reports pursuant to this Clause 13.2 (Information Undertakings), confirm to the Bond Trustee in writing the Issuer's compliance with the covenants in this Clause 13 unless the Bond Trustee explicitly waives such requirement, and specify compliance with the financial covenants set out in Clause 13.4 (Financial Covenants). Such confirmation shall be undertaken in a Compliance Ce'tificate signed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chief Financial Officer of the Issuer. In the event of non-compliance, the Compliance Certificate shall describe the non compliance, the reasons therefore as well as the steps which the Issuer has taken and will take in order to rectify the non-compliance.
