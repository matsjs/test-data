18.5 Amendments
18.5.1 All amendments of this Bond Agreement shall be made in writing, and shall unless otherwise provided for by this Bond Agreement, only be made with the approval of all parties hereto.
18.6 Notices, contact information
18.6.1 Written notices, warnings, summons etc to the Bondholders made by the Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Register with a copy to the Issuer. Information to the Bondholders may also be published at the web site www.stamdata.no.
18.6.2 The Issuer's written notifications to the Bondholders shall be sent via the Trustee, alternatively through the Securities Register with a copy to the Trustee and the Exchange.
18.6.3
Unless otherwise specifically provided, all notices or other communications under or in connection with this Bond Agreement between the Trustee and any Obligor shall be given or made in writing, by letter, or telefax. Any such notice or communication addressed shall be deemed to be given or made as follows:
(a) if by letter, when delivered at the address of the relevant Party; (b) if by telefax, when received.
