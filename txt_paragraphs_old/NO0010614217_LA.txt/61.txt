13.7.3
Ownership o f Rig Owning Subsidiaries and Rigs
(a) The Issuer shall at all times be the owner of 100 % of the shares in the Rig Owning Subsidiaries and shall not sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any of the Rig Owning Subsidiaries, and shall cause each of the Rig Owning Subsidiaries not to issue or sell any new shares, treasury shares or other ownership interest, to any third party.
(b) The Issuer shall not sell, assign or otherwise dispose of the Purchase Contract.
(c) The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig Owning Subsidiaries shall not sell, assign or otherwise dispose of their ownership of or interest in the Rigs.
13.7.4
Inspection o f the Rigs
At the request of the Trustee, the Issuer shall allow an inspector (appointed by the Trustee) or a surveyor (appointed by the Trustee to provide a technical report) to inspect the Rigs at any time on giving the Issuer reasonable notice, such inspection to be for the account of the Issuer.
