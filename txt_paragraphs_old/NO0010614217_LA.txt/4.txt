"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set forth in Clause 16.
"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo.
"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Interest Payment Date falls on a day that is not a Business Day, that date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day.
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set forth in Clause 10.2.
"Change of Control Event" means if any person or group (as such term is defined in the Norwegian Limited Liability Companies Act § 1-3) other than (a) Sector Omega AS and entities under discretionary management control of the same, including without limitation Sector Speculare (Private Equity) IV, or (b) a company holding minimum an investment grade corporate issuer rating by minimum one of the Rating Agencies (Moody's, S&P or Fitch), becomes the owner, directly or indirectly, of 50.00% or more of the outstanding shares and/or voting capital of the Issuer.
"Earnings" means all moneys payable to the Issuer or any of the Guarantors under any contracts entered into in respect of the Rigs (whether in the form of hire, termination fees, damages for breach or otherwise), including but not limited to any drilling or other employment contracts for the Rigs and the Leasing Agreements.
"Encumbrance" means any encumbrance, mortgage, pledge, lien, charge (whether fixed or floating), assignment by way of security, finance lease, sale and repurchase or sale and leaseback arrangement, sale of receivables on a recourse basis or security interest or any other agreement or arrangement having the effect of conferring security.
"Escrow Account" means an account(s) in the name of the Issuer with the Escrow Account Bank, pledged on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents and blocked in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the Escrow Account Bank has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Account Bank" means DnB NOR Bank ASA.
