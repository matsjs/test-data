Interpretation
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses are defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Accounts" means the Issuer's Earnings Account, the Rig Manager's Earnings Account and the Rig Owning Subsidiaries' Earnings Accounts.
"Assignment of Earnings and Insurances" means the first priority assignment by the Issuer and the Guarantors of the Earnings and the Insurances in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Contracts" means the first priority assignment of any external contracts for the Rigs entered into with operators/oil companies by the Rig Manager, in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Purchase Contract" means the first priority assignment by the Issuer of the Purchase Contract in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Leasing Agreements" means the first priority assignments of the Leasing Agreements by each of the Rig Owning Subsidiaries in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Receivables" means the first priority assignment by the Issuer of the Receivables in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
