13.7.5
Drilling contracts and other external contracts
The Issuer shall procure that no amendments, supplements, variations or waivers of any terms of any external contracts for the Rigs entered into with operators/oil companies are made if any such amendment, supplement, variation or waiver would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.7.6
Additional capital requirements in respect o f the Rigs
The Issuer shall procure that any additional capital requirements related to the Rigs incurred by any Group Company shall be financed by equity or subordinated unsecured loans/bonds in the Issuer (with pay-in-kind interest, no amortization and maturity after the Bonds).
