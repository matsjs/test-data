10.3.2
Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event (other than a Total Loss Event) the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer is receiving the proceeds following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event, redeem a portion of the outstanding Bonds at a price as follows:
(a) if occurring anytime from the Issue Date to, but not including, the interest payment day 2 years after the Issue Date, at a price equal to 112.0% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(b) if occurring anytime from and included the interest payment day 2 year after the Issue Date to, but not including, the interest payment day occurring 3 years after the Issue Date, at a price equal to 108.0% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount); and
(c) if occurring anytime from and including the interest payment day 3 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at a price equal to 104.0% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
