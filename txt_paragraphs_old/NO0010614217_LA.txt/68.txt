16.2 Procedural rulesfo r Bondholders' meetings
16.2.1 A Bondholders' Meeting shall be held at the request of:
(a) the Issuer, (b) Bondholders representing at least 1/10 of Voting Bonds, (c) the Exchange, if the Bonds are listed, or (d) the Trustee.
16.2.2 The Bondholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Trustee. A request for a Bondholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Trustee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
16.2.3 If the Trustee has not summoned a Bondholders' Meeting within 10 - ten - Business Days after having received such a request, then the requesting party may summons the Bondholders' Meeting itself.
