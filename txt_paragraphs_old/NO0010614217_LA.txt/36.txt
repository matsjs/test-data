10.2 Call Option
The Issuer may redeem a portion of the Bonds up to maximum 50% of the Outstanding Bonds at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in June 2012 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in June 2013 at a price equal to 109.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount). Partial redemption according to this Clause 10.2.1 may only take place once and no Security Interest shall be released in connection with such redemption.
10.2.2 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds (all or nothing) at any time from and including:
(a) the Interest Payment Date in June 2012 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in June 20l3at a price equal to 109.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount)
(b) the Interest Payment Date in June 2013 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in June 2014 at a price equal to 106.50% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount); and
(c) the Interest Payment Date in June 2014 to, but not included, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 104.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.2.3
In connection with redemption according to Clause 10.2.2, any amounts on the Accounts may be applied for such full redemption. In connection with partial redemption according to Clause 10.2.1, a portion (relative to the percentage redeemed) of the total amount on the Accounts may be used, such portion shall as a maximum be equal to the portion of the Outstanding Bonds being redeemed.
