10.2.4 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.5 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
10.2.6
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.2.7 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
