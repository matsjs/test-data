13.6.3
The Issuer shall procure that each of the Rig Owning Subsidiaries shall establish an earnings
account. All Earnings received related to the Leasing Agreements for the Rigs, any insurance
payment received under the Insurances, and all interest and payment of proceeds paid to the
Rig Owning Subsidiaries under intercompany loan agreements with other members of the
Group shall be paid directly from the relevant contracting party to the relevant Rig Owning
Subsidiaries' Earnings Account.
The Issuer shall procure that cash on the Rig Owning Subsidiaries' Earnings Accounts shall only be used for:
