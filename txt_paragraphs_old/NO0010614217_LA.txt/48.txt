13.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Sale-leaseback The Issuer shall ensure that no Obligor shall enter into any sale and leaseback transaction.
(b) Intra group debt The Issuer shall ensure that all intra-group loans, if any, from the Rig Owning Subsidiaries to the Issuer, from the Rig Manager to the Issuer and from the Rig Manager to the Rig Owning Subsidiaries shall be entered into on terms which ensures that such loans (i) be subordinated to the Bonds and the Guarantees (as the case may be), (ii) have PIK interest (pay in kind), (iii) have no amortization and (iv) have maturity after the Bond;
The Intercompany Loan shall be established no later than at the time of disbursement of the funds from the Escrow Account.
Total interest on and amortization of the Intercompany Loan and the Existing Intercompany Loans shall as a minimum secure a cash flow to the Issuer which is equal to the interest and amortization payable by the Issuer on the Bond Issue. Any repayments and/or prepayments of the Intercompany Loan and/or the Existing Intercompany Loans shall be used by the Issuer to repay or prepay (as the case may be) the Bonds and so that the outstanding amount to the Issuer under the Intercompany Loan and the Existing Intercompany Loans shall at least be equal to the outstanding amount in respect of the Bonds from time to time.
