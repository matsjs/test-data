(i) Financial indebtedness restriction The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig Owning Subsidiaries and the Rig Manager shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than (i) any indebtedness arising in the ordinary course of business for working capital purposes and as part of the daily operation of the relevant Rig Owning Subsidiary or the Rig Manager, (ii) derivative transactions in respect of currency and interest hedging, (iii) any intra-group loans from the Issuer and any intra-group loans granted by the Rig Manager to any of the Rig Owning Subsidiaries, and (iv) debt of the Rig Manager related to office warehouse and trucks in the amount of approximately USD 500,000.
(j) No investments The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig Owning Subsidiaries do not make any investments or capital expenditures, other than solely related to the ownership in and operation of the Rigs.
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company shall, acquire any new rigs or make any other new investments (excluding for avoidance of doubt the investment in the New Rigs) unless the aggregate total costs of such new investments (including ancillary equipment and required working capital), measured on a rolling basis over the last six months, is financed at least 40% by new equity issued by the Issuer, i.e. the total new investments made by the Group in the last rolling six month period must be financed in total by minimum 40% new equity issued by the Issuer.
(k) Ownership to Guarantors The Issuer shall not sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any Guarantor, and shall cause each Guarantor not to issue or sell any new shares, treasury shares or other ownership interest, to any third party.
(l) Negative pledge The Issuer shall ensure that the Guarantors shall not create, incur or allow to exist any Encumbrance on any of their assets except the Security Interests.
