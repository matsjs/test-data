The Bonds

2.1.2
The Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds.
2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 60,000,000 (U.S. Dollar Sixty Million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1.00 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
