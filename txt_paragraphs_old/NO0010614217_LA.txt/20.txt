2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 60,000,000 (U.S. Dollar Sixty Million).
The Bond Issue will be described as "13 per cent Global Rig Company ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2011/2015".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0614217.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds (net of legal costs, fees to the Trustee and the Manager and any other cost or expense incurred in connection with the issue of the Bonds) shall be employed for financing the purchase of the New Rigs including any additional equipment required for the New Rigs and for working capital purposes related to the New Rigs.
