"Defeasance Security" shall have the meaning given to it in paragraph a)(i) of Clause 18.2.
"Debt Service Reserve Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer held with the Accounts Bank or another Nordic bank with operations in Norway or any other first class international bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee, to be used for interest payments under the Bond Issue, blocked (save for interest payments and amortization to be paid in accordance with the terms of the Bonds) and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, including interests, costs and expenses.
"Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the issuer's claims against the account bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Debt Service Reserve Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the relevant account bank has waived any set-off rights.
