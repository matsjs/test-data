13.5 Financial covenants 13.5.1 Liquidity The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a minimum Liquidity of NOK 25,000,000.
13.5.2 Book Equity The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Book Equity of no less than NOK 350,000,000.
13.5.3 Compliance with financial covenants
The financial covenants following from Clause 13.5.1 and 13.5.2 shall be complied with at all times, and such compliance shall be measured on each Quarter Date by reference to, and certified by the Issuer with, each annual financial statement and quarterly financial statement, substantially in the form set out in Attachment 1 (Compliance Certificate).
