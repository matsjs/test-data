Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Accounts Bank" means Nordea Bank Norge ASA.
"Account Pledges" means (i) the Bond Escrow Account Pledge, (ii) the Escrow Account Pledge and (iii) the Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Assignment of Subordinated Loans" means the first priority assignments by a Subsidiary of all its present and future monetary claims under Subordinated Loans.
"Assignment of Intercompany Loans" means the first priority assignments by the Issuer of all its present and future monetary claims under Intercompany Loans.
