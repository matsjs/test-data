In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clause 15 occurs and is
interest, costs and expenses to be in default and due for immediate payment if:
(i)
the Bond Trustee receives a demand in writing that a default shall be declared
from Bondholders representing at least 1/5 of the Voting Bonds, and the
Bondholders' Meeting has not decided on other solutions, or
(ii)
the Bondholders' Meeting has with simple majority decided to declare the
Outstanding Bonds in default and due for payment.
