13.3.5 Disposal of business The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
a)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for
such transactions;
b)
such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect; and
c)
the Bonds are redeemed, subject to and in accordance with Clause 10.3.
