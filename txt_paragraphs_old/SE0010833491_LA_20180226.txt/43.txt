Market Loans
Issuer shall procure that (i) no Group Company other than the Issuer issues any Market Loan, (ii) any Market Loan issued by the Issuer meets the Incurrence Test (calculated pro forma including the relevant Market Loan) and (iii) no Group Company maintains, prolongs or provides any guarantee or security over any of the Group's present or future assets to secure any Market Loan.
