13.5 Financial covenants
During the term of the Bonds, the Group on a consolidated basis shall comply with the following financial covenants at all times:
(i) Interest Cover Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain an Interest Cover Ratio of minimum 4.0.
(ii) Leverage Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain a Leverage Ratio of maximum 4.0.
(iii) Total Loan to Value Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain a Total Loan to Value Ratio of maximum 75%.
