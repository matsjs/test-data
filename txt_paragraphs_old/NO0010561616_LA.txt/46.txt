2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at an initial amount of the USD equivalent calculated using the official USD/NOK fixing rate of exchange provided by the Norwegian Central Bank at close of business in Oslo on the date falling two Business Days prior to the Issue Date of NOK 390,000,000 (Three Hundred Ninety Million Norwegian Kroners) which such amount may be increased in accordance with Clause 9.3, herein referred to as "the Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as Marine Subsea Cyprus Holding Limited Exchange Bond -- Series II Bond Issue 2009/2019.
The registration number (ISIN) o f the Loan will be NO 001 056161.6.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
