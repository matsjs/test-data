11.2.3. Payment by the Borrower in accordance with this Clause 11.2. shall constitute good discharge of its obligations under Clause 11.1.1.
11.2.4 Each Bondholder, as registered in the Securities Register, has to provide the Paying Agent (either directly or through its account manager) with USD bank account details (the "Designated Account") to which all payments to such Bondholder shall be made. If, as a result of any currency exchange settlement agreement existing between the Bondholder's bank and the Paying Agent, or other situations due to the Bondholder's bank, cash settlement to the Designated Account is delayed, due payment hereunder shall be considered to have been made at that date the Paying Agent has made cash settlement to the account of the Bondholders' bank and no default interest shall apply for the delay.
11.2.5 All amounts payable under this Loan Agreement and any other Finance Document shall be payable in the same currency as the Bonds are denominated in. If, however, the Bondholder has not given instruction as set out in Clause 11.2.4, within 5 Business Days prior to a Payment Date, the cash settlement may be exchanged into NOK and credited to a NOK bank account registered with the Bondholders account in the Securities Register (if applicable) and in case of any payment delay as a result thereof, no default interest shall apply for the delay.
