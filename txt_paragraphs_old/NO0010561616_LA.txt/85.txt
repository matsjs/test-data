19. Bondholder Community
19.1 All Bond transfers are subject to the terms of this Loan Agreement and all Bond transferees are, in taking a transfer of Bonds, deemed to have accepted the terms o f this Loan Agreement and the other Finance Documents and will automatically become parties to the Loan Agreement, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with.
19.2 Loan Trustee as security agent:
(a) Each Bondholder appoints the Loan Trustee to hold the benefit of the Loan Security documents on its behalf and to act as its agent and trustee under and in connection with the Loan Security documents.
(b) Each Bondholder authorises the Loan Trustee to:-
(i) execute the Loan Security documents in its own name and as agent and trustee on behalf o f the Bondholders; and
(ii) take such action and to exercise and carry out all the discretions, authorities, rights, powers and duties as are specifically delegated to the Loan Trustee in the Loan Security documents, together with such powers and discretions as are incidental thereto, in accordance with the instructions of the Bondholders.
