13.2 During the term of the Loan, the Obligors shall (unless the Loan Trustee or the Bondholders' meeting (as the case may be) in writing has agreed to otherwise) comply with the following general covenants:
(b) not, and ensure that no other member of the Group shall,
(i) sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations,
(ii) change the nature of its business, or
(iii) merge, demerge or in any other way restructure its business,
in a manner which might jeopardise any Obligor's fulfilment o f its obligations under any of the Finance Documents.
Notwithstanding the aforesaid, no Event of Default shall occur if the Barge Subsidiaries sell any of the Barges on arm's length terms and the sales proceeds are used to repay the Loan in accordance with Clause 10.4 (Excess Cash Sweep).
Notwithstanding the aforesaid, it is understood that the Parent and the Borrower shall be allowed to restructure the Group, provided that the Borrower's ownership in Marine Subsea Consafe Limited is at least 40 %.
