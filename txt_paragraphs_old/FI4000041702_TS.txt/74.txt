8. Redemption and Purchase
8.1
Redemption at Maturity
Unless previously redeemed or purchased and cancelled as provided below, the Issuer will redeem the Notes in full at their principal amount on 4 April 2017 (the "Redemption Date").
8.2
Early Redemption
On 4 April 2015 or any time thereafter (the "Issuer Call"), the Issuer may, in its sole discretion, subject to having given not less than 30 nor more than 60 days' notice to the holders of the Notes in accordance with Condition 15, redeem all (but not some only) of the outstanding Notes (the "Issuer Call"). In the event the Issuer decides to redeem the outstanding Notes pursuant to the Issuer Call on or after 4 April 2015 but before 4 April 2016, such redemption will take place at 104.875 per cent of their principal value together with interest accrued to but excluding the date of redemption. In the event the Issuer decides to redeem the outstanding Notes pursuant to the Issuer Call on or after 4 April 2016 but before the Redemption Date, such redemption will take place at 102.4375 per cent of their principal value together with interest accrued to but excluding the date of redemption.
