Talvivaara Mine
General
The Talvivaara mine is located in Eastern Finland approximately 35 kilometres south-east of the town of Kajaani and approximately 30 kilometres south-west of the town of Sotkamo. The Talvivaara deposits comprise two different polymetallic ore bodies, Kuusilampi and Kolmisoppi. The deposits are located approximately three kilometres apart.
