Clearing and settlement
The Notes issued are linked to the account-based book entry system of Euroclear Finland; consequently, no physical securities will be issued. Clearing and settlement in connection with trading in Notes takes place in the OM system of Euroclear Finland. Holders of the Notes are dependent on the book entry system of Euroclear Finland in order to obtain payments under the Notes.
