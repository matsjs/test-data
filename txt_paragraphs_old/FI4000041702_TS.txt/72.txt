4. Subscription of the Notes
The Notes shall be offered for subscription through a book-building procedure. The subscription period will commence and end on 28 March 2012.
Bids for subscription must be submitted to (i) Sampo Bank plc, Danske Markets / Fixed Income Sales, Hiililaiturinkuja 2, Helsinki, FI-00075 SAMPO BANK, Finland, telephone + 358 10 513 8756 or +358 10 513 8750, or (ii) Nordea Bank Finland Plc, Nordea Markets / Institutional Sales, Aleksis Kiven katu 9, FI-00020 NORDEA, Finland, telephone +358 9 369 50880.
The Issuer shall accept the final subscriptions. The Issuer may, in its sole discretion, reject a subscription in part or in whole. The Issuer shall decide on the procedure in the event of over-subscription.
Subscriptions must be paid for as instructed in connection with the subscription. Notes subscribed and paid for will be registered to the book-entry accounts of the subscribers on a date advised in connection with the issuance of the Notes in accordance with the Finnish legislation governing book-entry system and book-entry accounts as well as regulations and decisions of Euroclear Finland.
5. Issue Price
The issue price of the Notes is 100 per cent.
