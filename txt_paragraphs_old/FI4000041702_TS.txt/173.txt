Financing Agreements
On 14 May 2008, Talvivaara issued senior unsecured convertible bonds with a nominal value of EUR 84.9 million due 20 May 2013. The current conversion price (the share subscription price) is GBP 5.7931 (EUR 7.2703). At the current conversion price, the 2013 Bonds are convertible into 11,677,591 fully paid shares in Talvivaara. The conversion period of the 2013 Bonds commenced on 30 June 2008 and ends on 14 May 2013. The interest rate applied to the 2013 Bonds is

The values of the liability component and the equity conversion component of the 2013 Bonds were determined at the issuance. The fair value of the liability component, included in the current borrowings, was calculated using a market interest rate for an equivalent non-convertible loan. The residual amount, representing the value of the equity conversion option, is included in shareholders' equity in other reserves net of income taxes. As at 31 December 2011, an equity component of EUR 10.9 million for the 2013 Bonds was recognised in equity.
Senior Unsecured Convertible Bonds due 2015
On 16 December 2010, Talvivaara issued senior unsecured convertible bonds with a nominal value of EUR 225.0 million due 16 December 2015, of which EUR 1.8 million had been converted into shares in Talvivaara as at 31 December 2011. The current conversion price (the share subscription price) is GBP 6.8937 (EUR 8.2117). At the current conversion price, the 2015 Bonds are convertible into 27,180,636 fully paid shares in Talvivaara following the resolution by the Extraordinary General Meeting of Shareholders of Talvivaara on 27 January 2011 to issue special rights in relation to the 2015 Bonds. The conversion period of the 2015 Bonds commenced on 17 February 2011 and ends on 10 December 2015. The interest rate applied to the 2015 Bonds is 4.00 per cent per annum payable semi-annually in arrear, and the yield to maturity is 6.50 per cent, reflecting a redemption price of 114.5% at maturity. Talvivaara must repay the 2015 Bonds in one instalment on the maturity date, 16 December 2015, to the extent the 2015 Bonds have not been converted into shares in Talvivaara.
In January 2011, an Extraordinary General Meeting of Talvivaara resolved to approve the proposal of the Board of Directors for the issue of special rights in relation to 2015 Bonds. The value of the equity component for the 2015 Bonds was determined after resolution of the Extraordinary General Meeting and it is recognised in equity. As at 31 December 2011, an equity component of EUR 9.0 million for the 2015 Bonds was recognised in equity.
