G. Edward Haslam has been the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Talvivaara since 2006. On February 16, 2012, Talvivaara announced that Mr. Haslam will retire from the role of Chairman; however, subject to his reappointment at Talvivaara's Annual General Meeting of shareholders on 26 April 2012, he will continue as a Non-Executive Director. Mr. Haslam is currently the Non-Executive Director of Aquarius Platinum Ltd, where he serves as the Chairman of the Remuneration and Succession Planning Committee and as a member of the Audit Committee. Mr. Haslam is also the Senior Non-Executive Director of Namakwa Diamonds Ltd and the Senior Independent Non-Executive Director of Centamin Egypt Limited. Between 1987 and 2004, Mr. Haslam worked at Lonmin Plc, serving on the board of directors between 1999 and 2000 and as Chief Executive Officer between 2000 and 2004. Mr. Haslam has also held various positions with Cluff Gold Plc, Falconbridge Nickel Mines and British Steel Corporation.
Pekka Perä is the founder of Talvivaara and has been a member of the Board of Directors and the Chief Executive Officer of Talvivaara since its incorporation in 2003. On February 16, 2012, Talvivaara announced that Mr. Harri Natunen, who joined Talvivaara on 19 March 2012, succeed Mr. Perä as Chief Executive Officer with effect from 26 April 2012, following Mr. Perä's decision to retire from active operational duties. Subject to his reappointment at Talvivaara's Annual General Meeting of shareholders on 26 April 2012, Mr. Perä will become Executive Chairman of the Board of Directors. Mr. Perä is currently the Chairman of the board of directors of Talvivaara Management and a member of the board of directors of VK-Electric Oy. Prior to joining Talvivaara, he worked as the Project Manager of Mining at Arctic Platinum Partnership between 2001 and 2003 and as a Project Manager at Pyhäsalmi Mine Oy. In addition, Mr. Perä has held various positions with the Outokumpu Group in Finland and elsewhere between 1991 and 1999. Mr. Perä holds a Master of Science degree in Mining.

Saila Miettinen-Lähde has been a member of the Board of Directors of Talvivaara since 2007 and the Chief Financial Officer since 2005. She has also been appointed as the deputy Chief Executive Officer of Talvivaara as of April 1, 2012. In addition, Ms. Miettinen-Lähde served as Vice President of Business Development at Orion Pharma between January 2000 and October 2000, as a Director of the Finnish National Fund for Research and Development between 1998 and 1999 and in various positions at Leiras Oy between 1993 and 1998. Ms. Miettinen-Lähde holds a Master of Science degree in Engineering.
D. Graham Titcombe has been the Deputy Chairman and a member of the Board of Directors of Talvivaara since 2007. Mr. Titcombe is currently a senior independent director of the board of directors of Cap-XX Ltd. Mr. Titcombe served in a number of senior positions at Johnson Matthey plc and was a member of their board of directors for twelve years retiring as Group Managing Director in 2002. Mr. Titcombe was a non-executive director of Wagon Plc between 1996 and 2003, and Chairman of Infast Plc between 2002 and 2005 and a senior independent director of the board of directors of PolyFuel Inc until 2008.
Eileen Carr has been a member of the Board of Directors of Talvivaara since 2007. Ms. Carr is a founder and director of Bunree Resource Management Ltd. Ms. Carr is currently the Deputy Chairman of Cluff Gold plc and she was the Chairman of the board of directors of Cluff Gold plc between 2007 and 2010. Ms. Carr has held a number of senior positions within the mining industry. She was the Chief Financial Officer and Talvivaara Secretary of Alexander Mining plc between 2005 and 2008 and a non-executive director of Goldstar Resources NL between 2003 and 2009. Ms. Carr has also served as the Chief Financial Officer of European Goldfields between 2002 and 2003 and as the Chief Financial Officer and Company Secretary of Monterrico Metals plc between 2002 and 2007. Prior to this, she was Finance Director and Company Secretary of Cluff Mining plc between 1997 and 1999 and of Cluff Resources plc between 1993 and 1996. Ms. Carr holds a Master of Science degree in Management.
