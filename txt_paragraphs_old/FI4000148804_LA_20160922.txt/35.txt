10. Redemption and Repurchase of the Bonds
The Issuer shall redeem all, but not only some, of the outstanding Bonds in full on the Final Redemption Date with an amount per Bond equal to 101 per cent. of the Outstanding Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest. If the Final

Redemption Date is not a CSD Business Day, then the redemption shall occur on the CSD Business Day determined by application of the Business Day Convention.
10.2 Group Company's purchase of Bonds
Each Group Company may, subject to applicable law, at any time and at any price purchase Bonds provided that such purchase is made through a public offer. Bonds held by a Group Company may at such Group Company's discretion be retained, sold or, if held by the Issuer, cancelled.
