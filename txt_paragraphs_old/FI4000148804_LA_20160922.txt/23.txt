"Subsequent Bonds" means any Bonds issued after the First Issue Date on one or more occasions.
"Total Nominal Amount" means the total aggregate Nominal Amount of the Bonds outstanding at the relevant time.
"Total Loss Event" means an event resulting in a major damage and/or significant value loss to a New Vessel or the Existing Vessel.
"Transaction Costs" means all fees, costs and expenses, stamp, registration and other taxes incurred by the Issuer or any other member of the Group in connection with (A) the raising of financing of the Group, including the Bond Issue and the issuance of Subsequent Bonds and (B) the listing of the Bonds.
