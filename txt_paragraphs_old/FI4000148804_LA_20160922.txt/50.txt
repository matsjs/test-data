13.6 Negative Pledge
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to (A) provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security, and (B) retain, but not prolong or renew, any existing
