11.9 Counterparts of the Agreement
This Agreement may be executed in any number of counterparts with the Pledgor and the Security Agent signing different counterparts and all such counterparts when taken together shall be deemed to constitute one and the same instrument. Delivery of a copy of an executed counterpart of a signature page to this Agreement by telefax or e mail shall be effective as delivery of a manually executed counterpart of this Agreement.
