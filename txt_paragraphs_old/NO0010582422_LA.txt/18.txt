"Securities Register" means the securities register in which the Bond Issue is registered.
"Security Documents" means any document establishing, recording, confirming or preserving any security interest over any Security Interest relating to any Finance Document.
"Security Interests" means the Guarantees and any Encumbrances or other security (hereunder any guarantee) created (or to be created) by the Security Documents securing the obligations of the Obligors under any Finance Documents, including but not limited to;
(a) From the Issue Date and until release from the Escrow Account; (i) the Escrow Account Pledge; and (ii) the Pledge over Settlement Bonds,
(b) After the release from the Escrow Account and thereafter, (i) the Share Pledges; and (ii) the Guarantees.
"Settlement Bonds" means bonds under the Existing Bonds which will be used as payment for Bonds.
"Share Pledges" means: (i) the first priority pledge over all of the Issuer's shares in the Sub Holding Companies; and (ii) the first priority pledge over all of Bergen Group Offshore's shares in Bergen Group Rosenberg,
in the amount set out in Clause 2.2.1 plus interest and expenses, in favour o f the Bond Trustee (on behalf o f the Bondholders), securing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
"Share Pledge Release Event" means that the Borrower require the relevant Share Pledge over one or more o f the Sub Holding Companies or Bergen Group Rosenberg to be released and discharged in connection with raising a Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
