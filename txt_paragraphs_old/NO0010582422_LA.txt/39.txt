10.3.2 The Repayment Date shall be the date falling not more than thirty Business Day afterthe Issuer in writing has notified the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and (if relevant) the Exchange that a Mandatory Redemption Event has occurred.
10.3.3
On the Repayment Date, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect o f each such Bond, the principal amount o f such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
Partial repayment shall be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures o f the Security Depository).
10.3.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
