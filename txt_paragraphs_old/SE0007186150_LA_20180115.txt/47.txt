13.10 Disposal of Assets
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Subsidiary will, sell or otherwise dispose of any business, assets or shares in any Subsidiary other than:
(a) a disposal, divestment or discontinuation of the Issuer's business relating to Freddy (whether linked to Freddy Store AB or any other Group Company);
(b) a merger between Suomen Lisaravinne Oy to Func Food Finland Oy with Func Food Finland Oy as surviving entity;
(c) disposals made by a Group Company to another Group Company;
(d) disposals made in the ordinary course of business of the disposing entity;
(e) disposals of obsolete and redundant assets;
