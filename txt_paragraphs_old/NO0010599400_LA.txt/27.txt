The Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire tenn of the Bonds.
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of NOK 940,000,000 (Norwegian kroner nine hundred and forty million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Aker Drilling ASA Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2011/2016".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 059940.0.
The tenor of the Bonds is front and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed to partly refinance the outstanding bond issue AKD02 with ISIN NO 001 057171.4, repay existing subordinated loans to Aker ASA and for general corporate purpose.
