13.5 Preservation o f equity, and Financial Covenants
13.5.1
Service o f Subordinated Capital The Issuer may only repay the Subordinated Capital if (i) no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing under any Finance Documents, and (ii) additional equity of the Issuer is raised in the amount equal to the amount repaid of Subordinated Capital.
13.5.2
Dividends and other distributions Subject to clause 13.5.1, the Issuer shall not within a calendar year declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders, whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation repurchase of shares, any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect (and reductions in its share capital or equity to its shareholders (but always distributions of bonus shares not affecting its equity)) exceeding 50 % of net income (adjusted for any write downs and after taxes paid) based on the consolidated accounts of Issuer for the previous financial calendar year. If the capacity is not utilized one year, the unutilized capacity may be rolled over to the following years.
