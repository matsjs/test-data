14.5
If the Noteholders instruct the Agent to accelerate the Notes, the Agent shall promptly
declare the Notes due and payable and take such actions as may, in the opinion of the
Agent, be necessary or desirable to enforce the rights of the Noteholders under the Finance
Documents, unless the relevant Event of Default is no longer continuing and subject to
Clause 14.2.
