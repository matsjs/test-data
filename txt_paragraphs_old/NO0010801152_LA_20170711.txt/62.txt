Mergers
The Issuer shall not enter into a merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving consolidating its assets and obligations with any company or entity where the Issuer or is not the surviving entity.
13.10 Demergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies, unless such separate companies are (directly or indirectly) wholly-owned by the Issuer.
