24.
NOTICES
24.1
Written notices to the Noteholders made by the Agent will be sent to the Noteholders via
the CSD with a copy to the Issuer. Any such notice or communication will be deemed to be
given or made via the CSD, when sent from the CSD.
(a) The Issuer's written notifications to the Noteholders will be sent to the Noteholders through the CSD with a copy to the Agent.
