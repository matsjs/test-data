The notice pursuant to Clause 17.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for the
meeting, (iii) agenda for the meeting (including each request for a decision by the
Noteholders), (iv) the day on which a person must be Noteholder in order to exercise
Noteholders' rights at the Noteholders' Meeting, and (v) a form of power of attorney. Only
matters that have been included in the notice may be resolved upon at the Noteholders'
Meeting. Should prior notification by the Noteholders be required in order to attend the
Noteholders' Meeting, such requirement shall be included in the notice.
