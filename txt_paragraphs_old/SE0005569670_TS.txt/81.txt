Notices
Any notice or other communication to be made under or in connection with the Finance Documents:
a) if to the Agent, shall be given at the address registered with the Swedish Companies Registration Office (Sw.; Bolagsverket);
b) if to the Issuer, shall be given at the address registered with the Swedish Companies Registration Office (Sw.; Bolagsverket); and
