15.3 Acquisitions
No Obligor shall, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), if such acquisition would have a Material Adverse Effect.
15.4 Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of: any shares in, or any assets or operations of, any Group Company to any Person (not being the Issuer or any other Group Company) (each a "Restricted Disposal"), unless:
(i) such Restricted Disposal is carried out at fair market value;
(ii) on arm's length basis;
