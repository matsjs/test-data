3.
STATUS OF THE BONDS
The Bonds constitute direct, general, unconditional, unsubordinated and unsecured obligations of the Issuer and shall at all times rank pari passu with all direct, unconditional, unsubordinated and unsecured obligations of the Issuer and without any preference among them.
4.
USE OF PROCEEDS
The Net Proceeds shall be used for property acquisitions, refinancing of existing bond debt and general corporate purposes. The proceeds from any Subsequent Bond Issue shall be used for general corporate purposes.
