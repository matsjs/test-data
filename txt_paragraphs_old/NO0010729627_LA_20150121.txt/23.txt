Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bond Issue from the Escrow Account to the Issuer is subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents in satisfactory form to it:
(a) a duly executed Release Notice in the form set out in Attachment 2;
(b) either (i) the relevant Pre-approved Budget or (ii) approval of the requested disbursement from the Escrow Account from Bondholders representing at least 35% of the Outstanding Bonds; and
(c) board approval from the Issuer of the Pre-approved Budget and the requested disbursement from the Escrow Account.
6.3 The Bond Trustee may, in its reasonable opinion, waive the deadline or requirements for documentation as set out in Clauses 6.1 and 6.2.
6.4 Disbursement of the net proceeds from the Bonds is subject to the Bond Trustee's written notice to the Issuer, the Manager and the Paying Agent that the documents have been controlled and that the required conditions precedent are fulfilled.
6.5 On the Issue Date, subject to receipt of confirmation from the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 6.4, the Manager shall transfer the net proceeds from the Bond Issue to the Escrow Account.
6.6 The Issuer is entitled to amend, modify, vary, restate or supplement the Pre-approved Budget from time to time with the prior written consent of Bondholders representing at least 35% of the Outstanding Bonds.
