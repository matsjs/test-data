10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in foil on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole, but not in part, at any time from and including the Issue Date and up until thirty-five (35) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date (the "Call Option").
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
