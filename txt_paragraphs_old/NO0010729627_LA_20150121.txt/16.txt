2.4 Purpose and utilization of the Initial Bond Issue
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue solely towards:
(i)
fees, costs and expenses incurred in connection with the establishment of
the Group and the issuance of the Bonds; and
(ii) funding the working capital requirements of the Obligors.
