10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole, but not in part, at any time from and including the Issue Date and up until thirty-five (35) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date (the "Call Option").
10.2.3
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.2.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.3 Mandatory Prepayment
10.3.1 Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall within 30 calendar days redeem 100% of the outstanding Bonds, subject to Clause 10.4 below.
10.3.2
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds, on a pro rata basis, the principal amount of each such Bond and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date, subject to Clause 10.4 below.
