2.1 Binding nature o f this Bond Agreement
2.1.1 By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 18.1.
2.1.2
This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of USD 20,000,000 (U.S. Dollar twenty million) plus Bonds issued as PIK interest. The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of USD 5,000,000 (the "Initial Bond Issue").
