8 Status of the Loan and Guarantee
8.1 The Loan shall rank pari passu with all other senior debt of the Borrower and shall rank ahead of subordinated capital.
8.2 The Loan is unsecured. However, the Loan is secured by the Guarantee until the shares in Primorsk Sakhalin Shuttle Tanker Limited, a company with registration number 138942 (Cyprus) currently owned 100% by a subsidiary of the Guarantor, are transferred to the Borrower, and will immediately thereafter be released.
8.3 The Guarantor shall issue the Guarantee in the favour of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), securing the proper fulfillment of the obligations of the Borrower under this Loan Agreement.
9 Interest
9.1 Interest accrues from and including the Disbursement Date. The NOK Tranche is based on NIBOR plus 3.60 percentage points, and the USD Tranche is based on LIBOR plus 3.60 percentage points. NIBOR and LIBOR are set on the first Interest Determination Date. Should NIBOR or LIBOR not be available, the interest rate will be set based on NIBOR - Reference Banks (for the NOK Tranche) or LIBOR - Reference Banks (for the USD Tranche) plus 3.60 percentage points.
