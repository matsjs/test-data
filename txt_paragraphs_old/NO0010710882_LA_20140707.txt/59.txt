Parent's special covenants (a) Dividend restrictions
(b) Maintenance of ownership of the Issuer
The Parent undertakes to maintain a 100% (direct or indirect) ownership over all the shares and control over all the voting rights of the Issuer.
(c) Maintenance of ownership of GEHCL
The Parent undertakes to maintain a 100% (direct or indirect) ownership over all the shares and control over all the voting rights of GEHCL other than C shares, which shall be acquired by the Parent upon exchange into Parent shares.
(d) Ownership of Restricted Subsidiaries
Except where it would qualify as a Mandatory Prepayment Event and the Bonds are redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.4 (Mandatory Prepayment), the Parent undertakes to maintain a Decisive Influence over each of the Restricted Subsidiaries.
(e) Single-purpose entity
The Parent shall ensure that the Issuer shall not have any business other than being the issuer of the Bonds or matters reasonably ancillary thereto (including on-lending to the Parent).
