(f) Subordinated loans
The Parent shall ensure that any loans to the Issuer from any Group Company shall be subordinated to the Bonds and subject to a subordination and turnover agreement acceptable to the Bond Trustee. For the avoidance of doubt, any such loan may be serviced only as long as no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing.
