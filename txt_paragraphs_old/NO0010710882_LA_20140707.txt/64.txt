Financial covenants The Parent undertakes to comply with the following financial covenants during the term of the Bond Issue:
The Parent shall ensure that the Group maintains an Equity Ratio of minimum 40%.
(b) Leverage Ratio
The Parent shall ensure that the Group maintains a Leverage Ratio not exceeding 3.Ox.
(c) Minimum Liquidity
The Parent shall ensure that the Group maintains a Liquidity of minimum USD 75,000,000 and increasing to USD 100,000,000 from and including 30 June 2018.
The Parent undertakes to comply with the above financial covenants at all times, such compliance to be measured by reference to:
(i) the consolidated Financial Statements of the Parent delivered in respect of each Quarter Date falling on 31 December,
(ii) the consolidated Interim Accounts of the Parent delivered in respect of each Quarter Date falling on 30 June, and
