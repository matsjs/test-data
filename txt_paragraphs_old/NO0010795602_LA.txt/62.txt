13.8 Financial support
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, grant or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any Financial Support, other than Permitted Financial Support.
13.9 Share issues
Save for any share issue as part of any IPO, the Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, issue any shares, except (i) by the Issuer to the Parent against cash consideration or set-off of debt, (ii) by a Group Company to another Group Company, (iii) to any existing minority shareholders pro rata to their existing ownership, or (iv) by a Group Company to the management of such Group Company in the form of an incentive scheme, provided that any additional issued shares in the Issuer or in a Material Group Company are included in the relevant share pledge under the Transaction Security, other than those shares issued under (iii) and (iv) above.
13.10
Continuation of business
The Issuer shall ensure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by it or by the Group as of the Issue Date.
13.11 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
