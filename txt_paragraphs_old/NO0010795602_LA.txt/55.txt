10.3 Voluntary Partial Repayment (a) In addition to redemption of Bonds pursuant to Clause 10.2, the Issuer may at any time in the period from (but excluding) the Issue Date and up to (but excluding) the First Call Date, repay an amount not exceeding 10 per cent. of the Bond Issue (the "Voluntary Partial Repayment Option") at a price equal to 104.125 per cent. of the Nominal Amount, provided that at least 50 per cent. of the Loan Amount remains outstanding after such redemption.
(b) Any amount for which the Voluntary Partial Repayment Option is exercised shall be used for pro rata payment to the Bondholders in accordance with the applicable regulations of the CSD.
(c) The repayment shall occur on an Interest Payment Date, and the Issuer shall give not less than twenty (20) Business Days' notice of the repayment to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders.
(d) For the avoidance of doubt, redemption in accordance with this Clause 10.4 may be exercised in addition to the Call Option in Clause 10.2 and the Equity Clawback pursuant to Clause 10.2.
