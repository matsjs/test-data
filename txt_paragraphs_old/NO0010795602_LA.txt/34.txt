2.2 Tenor of the Bonds
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
2.3 Use of proceeds
The Issuer will use the proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds (net of fees and legal cost of the Managers and the Bond Trustee and any other cost and expenses incurred in connection with the Bond Issue) towards:
(a) repayment in full of the Existing Debt;
(b) distribution of the Initial Dividend; and
(c) the general corporate purposes of the Group, including, without limitations, acquisitions (always to the extent permitted under the terms of the Finance Documents).
