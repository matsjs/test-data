4.
ADMISSION TO LISTING
The Issuer shall procure that the Bonds are listed on an Exchange within twelve (12) months o f the Issue Date and remain listed on the Exchange until the Bonds have been redeemed in full.
5.
REGISTRATION OF THE BONDS
5.1 Registration in the CSD
The Bonds shall be registered in dematerialised form in the CSD according to the relevant securities registration legislation and the requirements o f the CSD.
