10.4 Redemption following receipt of Tax Refund
10.4.2 In the event that receipt of any Tax Refund takes place prior to the Interest Payment Date in September 2011, the entire amount received shall be placed in an escrow account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank from the time of receipt and up until the Interest Payment Date in September 2011, whereby the Issuer shall redeem Bonds pursuant to Clause 10.4.1 above. The escrow account shall be blocked and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
10.4.3 The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercising of the redemption at least ten Business Days prior to the settlement date of such redemption exercise.

10.5 Change of Control
10.5.1 Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price of 105 % of par value (plus accrued interest).
