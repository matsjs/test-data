10.3 Redemption following a Preferred Senior Debt Increase
10.3.1 Notwithstanding Clause 10.2 the Issuer shall, immediately and in any event within five Business Days after receipt of funds following a Preferred Senior Debt Increase pursuant to Clause 13.3(g), redeem Bonds at a price equal to the Call Option price as set out in Clause 10. 2 .
10.3.2 The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercising of the redemption at least ten Business Days prior to the settlement date of such redemption exercise.
10.3.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds in accordance with the procedures of the Security Depository.
10.3.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.3 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.4 Redemption following receipt of Tax Refund
10.4.1 Notwithstanding Clause 10.2 the Issuer shall, immediately and in any event within five Business Days following receipt of any Tax Refund, apply the entire amount received to redeem Bonds at a price equal to Call Option price as set out in Clause 10.2.
