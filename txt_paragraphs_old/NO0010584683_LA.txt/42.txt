10.2.2 The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercising of the Call Option at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of such Call Option exercise.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds in accordance with the procedures of the Security Depository.
10.2.4 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each Bondholder, the aggregate principal amount of all Bonds held by such Bondholder (including any premium as stated above) to be redeemed and any unpaid interest accrued on such Bonds up to and including settlement date.
