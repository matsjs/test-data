2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum aggregate amount of NOK 310,000,000 (Norwegian kroner threehundredandtenmillions).
2.2.3 The Bond Issue will be described as " 15.00 per cent InterOil Exploration & Production ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2010/2014".
2.2.4 The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 0010584683.
2.2.5 The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date up to and including the Final Maturity Date.
2.2.6 The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account on the Issue Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization The net proceeds of the Bonds (net of fees, legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses) shall be employed to (i) firstly fund the Retention Account with an amount equal to three (3) months interest on the Bonds in accordance with Clauses 6.2.2 and 13,3(k), (ii) secondly to repay the Existing Bond Loans, including the costs related thereto and (ii) thirdly to strengthen the working capital of the Issuer. The employment of the net proceeds from the Bonds shall be satisfactory evidenced to the Trustee.
3. LISTING 3.1 The Issuer is under no obligation to list the Bonds on an Exchange, but shall have the right to
list the Bonds if it so desires.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
