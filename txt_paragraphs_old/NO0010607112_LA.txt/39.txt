10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall be repaid by the Issuers as follows:
(i) on each Interest Payment Date from and including the Interest Payment Date in July 2011 to and including the Interest Payment Date in January 2016, with an instalment of NOK 18,750,000; and
(ii) on the Maturity Date, with an instalment equal to the Outstanding Bonds.
Instalments shall be repaid at par value and must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
