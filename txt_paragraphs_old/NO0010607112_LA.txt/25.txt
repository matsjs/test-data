The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN BOA Deep C AS AND BOA Sub C AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2011/2016".
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization 2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied as follows:
(i) approximately NOK 700,000,000 to repay all existing debt in the Issuers currently provided by Eksportfinans ASA and Fokus Bank;
(ii) approximately NOK 100,000,000 for working capital puiposes for the Issuers; and
(iii) the remaining amount (however not to be more than NOK 400,000,000) to be provided as a loan to the Parent as part o f the refinancing of the Ultimate Parent Group.
