17.2 Liability and indemnity
17.2.1
The Bond Trustee is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders or the Issuers as a result of negligence or willful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set forth in this Bond Agreement. The Bond Trustee is not liable for the content of information provided to the Bondholders on behalf of the Issuers.
17.2.2
The Issuers are liable for, and shall indemnify the Bond Tiustee fully in respect of, all losses, expenses and liabilities incurred by the Bond Trustee as a result of negligence by any of the Issuers (including its directors, management, officers, employees, agents and representatives) to fulfil its obligations under the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Documents, including losses incurred by the Bond Trustee as a result of the Bond Tiustee's actions based on misrepresentations made by any of the Issuers in connection with the establishment and performance of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents.
