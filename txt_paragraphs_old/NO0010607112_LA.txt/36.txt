Status of the Bonds and security
8.1 The Bonds shall be senior debt of each of the Issuers. The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Issuers (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
8.2 The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest and expenses, shall be secured by the Security Interests.
