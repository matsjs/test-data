6.2 Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent
6.2.1 The amount in the Escrow Account shall only be: (i) used according to the purpose of the Bond Issue as set out in Clause 2.3.1 (Purpose and utilization) or, alternatively, (ii) returned to the Bondholders in the event that all of the conditions precedent set out in Clause 6.2.2 below are not satisfied for any reason no later than 90 days after the Issue Date at: (a) par value if such failure to comply with such conditions precedent is beyond the control of the Issuers, and (b) otherwise at the rate applicable as set out in Clause 10.3 (Mandatory Redemption).
6.2.2 After the Bond Trustee has received the following documents in form and substance satisfactory to it, the Bond Trustee shall instruct the Escrow Account bank to release the requested amount to the Issuers or at the Issuers' instruction:
(a) a copy of a duly executed release notice from the Issuers in form and substance as set out in Attachment 2 hereto;
(b) satisfactory documentation evidencing that the amount to be released will be applied in accordance with the purpose of the Bond Issue and that all existing debt in the Issuers currently provided by Eksportfinans ASA and Fokus Bank is or will be repaid and all securities thereunder are or will be released;
(c) a confirmation that no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing;
(d) satisfactory documentation evidencing that the Accounts are opened (other than the Escrow Account);
(e) copies of necessary corporate resolutions from the Issuers, the Parent, the Ultimate Parent and Boa Offshore (if applicable) to enter into the Bond Agreement and execute the relevant Security Documents (other than the Escrow Account Pledge) and any other documents related to or required under the Bond Agreement;
