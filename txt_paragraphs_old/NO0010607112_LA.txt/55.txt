13.6 Vessel Covenants
13.6.1 Each of the Issuers undertakes to comply with the following Vessel covenants:
(a) Insurance o f the Vessels
(i) Each of the Issuers shall ensure that its Vessel and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times shall be adequately insured with first class insurers against such risks, including but not limited to, Hull & Machinery, Hull Interest, Freight Interest, Protection & Indemnity as per industry standards and War Risk insurance, as well as other industry standard insurances. The Issuers shall also provide for any additional insurance required under any law or Charter Contract.
