10.2.3
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuers shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuers in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
