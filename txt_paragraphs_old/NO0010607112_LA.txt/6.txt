Interpretation
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses may be defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Accounts" means the following accounts in the name of any of the Issuers or Boa Offshore (as the case may be), in a bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee;
(i) the Escrow Accoimt; and
(ii) the Earnings Accounts; and
(iii) the Debt Service Account.
"Assignment of Charter Contracts" means a first priority assignment of the rights of Boa Offshore (provided Boa Offshore is a party to a Charter Contract) or the relevant Issuer (as the case may be) under any Charter Contracts, to the extent it is legally possible pursuant to the relevant Charter Contract and applicable law, and always provided that the Issuers and Boa Offshore (as the case may be) shall use reasonable efforts for any such Charter Contract to allow for an assignment, related to the Vessels, at any time, in the amount set out in Clause 2.2.1 plus interest and expenses, in favour o f the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders), securing the Issuers' obligations under this Bond Agreement. The Issuers shall give notice and use its best endeavours to obtain consent and acknowledgement of such assignment from any Charterer.
"Assignment of Earnings" means the assignment of the receivables of each of the Issuers or Boa Offshore under any Charter Contracts related to the Vessels, at any time, in the amount set out in Clause 2.2.1 plus interest and expenses, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders), securing the Issuers obligations under this Bond Agreement.
