13.5 Financial Covenants
(a) Asset Cover Ratio The Issuers shall ensure that the aggregate Market Value of the Vessels shall exceed 125 per cent of the outstanding amount under the Bond Agreement.
(b) Liquidity The Issuers shall in aggregate have Cash and Cash Equivalents in excess of NOK 50,000,000.
