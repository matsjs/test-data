"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to acts as its paying agent in the Securities Registry with respect to the Bonds, being the Oslo branch of Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken (publ) AB.
"PenSam Subordinated Loan" means the amount outstanding under the DKK 150,000,000 subordinated loan agreement between the Guarantor and PenSam Liv forsikringsaktieselskap, dated 5 July 2012. The Pensam Subordinated Loan is subordinated to all senior indebtedness of the Guarantor (including the Bonds), carries an 8% payment in kind interest, a 2% cash interest and an estimated 3% interest in the form of warrants issued by the Guarantor, and matures in full at 5 September 2018.
