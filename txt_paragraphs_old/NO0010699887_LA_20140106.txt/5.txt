1.1 Definitions
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Accounts" means the Issuer Accounts and the Guarantor Accounts.
"Asset Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of the latest Market Value to the principle amount from time to time outstanding under the Finance Documents less any amount standing to the credit o f the Issuer on the Collection Account.
"Assignment of Construction Insurances" means an assignment o f the Issuer's entitlements under the insurances related to WIND SERVER under construction (other than third party liability insurances).
"Attachment" means the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bareboat Charter" means the intra-group bareboat charter contract based on the Bimco Supplytime 2005 standard to be entered into by the Issuer and the Guarantor in respect o f WIND SERVER.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
