"Guarantor Earnings Account" means an account with the Paying Agent established in the name of the Guarantor and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf o f the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Guarantor Share Pledge" means a pledge granted by the Guarantor over 50% of the shares in the Issuer together with, inter alia, letters o f resignation (effective upon an Event of Default for which the Bond Trustee has issued a notice) (if legally possible) from the current board members and covenants to obtain such from future board members.
"Guarantor Subordinated Loans" means any loan or loan stock made or, as the context may require, to be made available by the direct shareholders o f the Guarantor to the Guarantor.
