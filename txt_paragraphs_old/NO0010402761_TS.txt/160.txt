14.2 The Trustee

 receive payments with respect to the Notes from the Company in terms of interest and principal on behalf of the Noteholders;
 upon a potential event of default or a default, instruct the Paying and Conversion Agent, the Registrar and the Company with respect to payments regarding the Notes;

 receive certain certificates from the Company;
