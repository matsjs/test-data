16.2 Procedural rulesfor Bondholders 'meetings 16.2.1 A Bondholders' Meeting shall be held at the request of:
(a) the Issuer, (b) Bondholders representing at least 1/10 of the aggregate principal amount of
Voting Bonds, (c) the Exchange, if the Bonds are listed, or (d) the Bond Trustee.
16.2.2 The Bondholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Bond Trustee. A request for a Bondholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Bond Trustee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
