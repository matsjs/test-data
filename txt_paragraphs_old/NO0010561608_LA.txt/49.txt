The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at an initial amount of USD 246,500,000 (Two Hundred Forty Six Million Five Hundred Thousand United States Dollars) which such amount may be increased in accordance with Clause 9.3, herein referred to as "the Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as Marine Subsea Cyprus Holding Limited Exchange Bond --Series I Bond Issue 2009/2019.
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 056160.8.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
The Borrower will not receive any additional proceeds in connection with the issuance of the Bonds. The Bonds shall be used as payment (payment-in-kind) to the holders of ISIN NO 001 035359.2 - FRN Marine Subsea AS (formerly known as Africa Offshore Services AS) Senior Secured Bond Issue 2007/2012 (the "February Bonds") and the holders of bonds in ISIN NO 001 037513.2 - FRN Marine Subsea AS (formerly known as Africa Offshore Services AS) Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012 (the "June Bonds") on the Issue Date. The minimum aggregate principal amount of June Bonds and/or February Bonds exchanged by any Bondholder on the Issue Date shall be the USD equivalent to Euro 50,000 or above; provided that where the Bondholder is acting as a custodian, this minimum principal amount shall apply to each beneficial owner of June Bonds and/or February Bonds.
