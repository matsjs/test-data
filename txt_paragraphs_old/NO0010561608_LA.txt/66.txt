11.1 Covenant to Pay
The Borrower will on any date when amounts under or in respect of the Loan become due for payment (whether such amounts are in respect of principal, premium, interest or otherwise), unconditionally pay to or to the order of the Loan Trustee all such amounts.
11.1.2 The covenant contained in Clause 11.1.1 shall be for the benefit of the Loan Trustee and each of the Bondholders.
11.2 Payment mechanics
11.2.1
If no specific order is made by the Loan Trustee under Clause 11.1.1, the Borrower shall pay all amounts due with respect to the Bonds under this Loan Agreement by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in connection with its securities account in the Securities Depository.
11.2.2
Payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank which holds the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, but if the paying bank and the receiving bank are the same, payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, see however Clauses 11.2.4 and 11.2.5.
11.2.3. Payment by the Borrower in accordance with this Clause 11.2. shall constitute good discharge of its obligations under Clause 11.1.1.
