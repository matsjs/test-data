22 Dispute resolution and legal venue
22.1 Disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement which are not resolved amicably, shall be resolved in accordance with Norwegian law and, except as otherwise permitted in this Clause 22, in the Norwegian courts.
22.2 For the benefit of the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders only: (a) the Borrower agrees that Oslo Tingrett shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any disputes which may arise out of or in connection with this Agreement and that, accordingly, any legal action or proceedings arising out of or in connection with this Agreement may be brought in such court and the Borrower irrevocably submits to the jurisdiction of Oslo tingrett; and (b) notiling in this Clause 22 shall limit the right of the Loan Trustee, or in case Clause 15.5 applies, the Bondholders, to take proceedings against the Borrower in any other court of competent jurisdiction. The Borrower hereby irrevocably submits to the jurisdiction of the Cypriot courts and agrees not to challenge jurisdiction of the Cypriot courts on grounds of inconvenient forum or otherwise.
22.3 The Borrower irrevocably and unconditionally undertakes that it shall not raise any claim, objection, counter-claim or argument in any action, proceeding or other litigation that the Loan Trustee does not have legal standing before a court to enforce its right or the rights of the Bondholders under or in connection with this Loan Agreement or the Bonds.
23 Appointment of process agent
23.1 The Borrower undertakes to appoint Marine Subsea AS of Oslo, Norway as its agent for service of process in respect of any legal action or proceedings in the courts of Norway in connection with this Agreement.
23.2 The Borrower agrees that failure by the process agent to notify it of any process will not invalidate the relevant proceedings.
23.3 This Clause does not affect any other method of service allowed by law.
