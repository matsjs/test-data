The Loan Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Loan Trustee or the Borrower. The Borrower shall ensure that the Loan Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Loan.
Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Banking Day:
a day when the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open, when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions and (if relevant) banks are open for business of the nature required in London and New York.
Bondholders:
the holders of the bonds.
Borrower's Bonds:
bonds in the Remaining Loan, owned by the Borrower or any party over whom the Borrower has decisive influence or any party who has decisive influence over the Borrower.
