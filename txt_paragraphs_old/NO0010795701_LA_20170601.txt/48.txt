12.3 Change of Control Event The Issuer shall inform the Bond Trustee in writing as soon as possible after becoming aware that a Change of Control Event has occurred.
(a) promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing of any Event of Default or any event or
circumstance which the Issuer understands or could reasonably be expected to
understand may lead to an Event o f Default and the steps, if any, being taken to
remedy it);
(b) at the request o f the Bond Trustee, report the balance of the Issuer's Bonds (to the best of its knowledge, having made due and appropriate enquiries);
(c) send the Bond Trustee copies of any statutory notifications o f the Issuer, including but not limited to in connection with mergers, de-mergers and reduction of the Issuer's share capital or equity;
