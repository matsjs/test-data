13.7 Disposals
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations, unless such sale, transfer or disposal is carried out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.8 Related party transactions
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that all other Group Companies will, conduct ail business transactions with any related party which is not a Group Company at market terms and otherwise on an arm's length basis.
