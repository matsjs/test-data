The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, carry outfa) any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving the
consolidation of assets and obligations of the Issuer or any other Group Company with any other person other than with a Group Company; or

if such merger, demerger, combination or reorganisation would have a Material Adverse Effect.
