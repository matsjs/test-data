2.1 Amount, denomination and ISIN of the Bonds
(a) The Bonds are denominated in Euro (EUR), being the single currency o f the participating member slates in accordance with the legislation of the European Community relating to Economic and Monetary Union.
(b) The Initial Nominal Amount of each Bond is EUR 100,000.
(c) The ISIN of the Bonds is NO 001 0795701. All Bonds issued under the same ISIN will have identical terms and conditions as set out in these Bond Terms.
2.2 Tenor of the Bonds The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
2.3 Use of proceeds The Issuer will apply the net proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds as follows:
(a) firstly, a minimum amount EUR 30,000,000 shall paid into the Escrow Account, which amount only to be used in accordance with Clause 6.2 (Disbursements), paragraph (b); and
(b) secondly, for the use o f general corporate purposes of the Group.
