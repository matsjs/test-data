8.2 The Company shall redeem the relevant Bonds subject to Early Redemption according to Condition 8.1 on the last Banking Day ("Early Redemption Date") which falls in the calendar month immediately following the calendar month in which the relevant notice for an Early Redemption call was received by the Company, provided however that the relevant Holder's Bonds have been blocked for further transfer not later than on the fifth Banking Day preceding the Early Redemption Date.
