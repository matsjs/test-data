10.5 Listing of the Bonds
The Company undertakes to apply for listing of the Bonds with NASDAQ OMX Stockholm and will use reasonable efforts to achieve and maintain such listing as long as any Bonds are outstanding, however not longer than up to and including the last day on which trading in the Bonds on the exchange reasonably can, under to the then applicable regulations by the exchange and the CSD, take place before the Redemption Date. The application for listing of the Bonds shall be filed with NASDAQ OMX Stockholm not later than 12 months from the Issue Date.
