10 Special undertakings
10.1 General undertakings
So long as any Bonds remain outstanding, the Company undertakes:
(a) to (i) not cease to operate its business and undertakes to ensure that the Group (taken as a whole) will not cease to operate its business, (ii) procure that no material change is made in the general nature of the business of the Group (taken as a whole) from that carried on as per the Issue Date, (iii) not to sell, transfer or otherwise dispose or permit any Group Company to sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any assets that are material for the general operations of the Group (taken as a whole) and (iv) procure that the Björn Borg Trade Mark is not entirely or in material parts transferred or assigned to a third party that is not a Group Company or an affiliate of the Company, and not cancelled or materially changed (for each of (i)-(iv) above, the undertaking made by the Company shall only apply in relation to actions that would be to the detriment of the Holders); and
(b) that it (and it will procure that each Group Company) will not provide or permit to subsist any security over any of its assets or guarantee for any Market Loan.
