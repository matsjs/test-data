14
Interest
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value o f the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at the relevant Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year-, the fust Interest Payment Date falls in 11. July 2011.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period Rom, and including, one Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche.
9.4 The day count Raction in respect of the calculation of the payable interest amount shall be "Actual/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in which payment being made is divided by 360.
9.5 The applicable Floating Rate on each Tranche is set/reset on each Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche by the Bond Trustee commencing on the Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche at the beginning of the relevant calculation period.
When the interest rate is set for the first time and on subsequent interest rate resets, the next Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche, the interest rate applicable up to the next Interest Payment Date for the relevant Tranche and the actual number of calendar days up to that date shall immediately be notified to the Bondholders, the Issuer, the Paying Agent, and if the Bonds are listed, the Exchange.
