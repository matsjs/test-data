"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bond Reference Rate" means: (i) in respect of the NOK Tranche, 3months NIBOR; and (ii) in respect of the USD Tranche, 3 months LIBOR.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Register, horn time to time.
"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set forth in Clause 16.
"Bonds" means the securities issued by the Issuer pursuant to this Bond Agreement, representing the Bondholders' claim on the Issuer.
"Business Day" means: (i) in respect of the NOIC Tranche, any day on which Norwegian commercial
banks are open for general business, and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions, being any day on which the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open; and (ii) in respect of the USD Tranche, any day on which commercial banks are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo, London and New York.
