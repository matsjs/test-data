10.3 If at any time prior to but not including the Maturity Date, any of the Vessels are sold or disposed of, or if the Conversion Contracts are sold, assigned, cancelled for any reason howsoever or disposed of, or if any of the Borrower's shares in the Guarantors controlling directly or indirectly the Vessels are sold, in each case including to an affiliate, or the Borrower or its subsidiaries, or in the event of a total loss or a constructive total loss of any of the Vessels, the Borrower shall redeem the entire Loan at a price equal to 101% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
Such mandatory redemption shall be notified by the Borrower in writing to the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders and at least five Banking Days prior to the settlement date of the mandatory redemption. The settlement date of the mandatory redemption shall be carried out on or about the fifth Banking Day after the occurrence of the event triggering the mandatory redemption.
