Conditions precedent - pre disbursement
Release of the net proceeds of the Initial Bonds from an Escrow Account denominated in USD to the Issuer will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received the documents listed below (unless such requirement is waived by the Bond Trustee, acting in its sole discretion), in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a) a duly executed release notice from the Issuer as set out in Schedule 2;
(b) satisfactory documentation evidencing that the amount to be released shall be applied in accordance with the purpose of the Bond Issue as set out in Clause 2.4;
(c) confirmation from the Issuer that the DSA Release has been applied in full;
'(d) copy of the loan agreement for any intercompany loan provided to the Norwegian Subsidiary from any other member of the Debtor Group;
(e) a form of intercompany loan agreement for any intercompany loan to be applied for any member of the Debtor Group;
