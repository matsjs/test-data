By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 21.1.
This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 2.2.1
The Bonds
Initial Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Initial Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 335,000,000 (U.S. Dollar three-hundred-and-thirty-six million), divided into (i) Tranche A Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 250,000,000 (U.S. Dollar twohundred-and-fifty-million) (plus the principal amount of any Tap Bonds and any Additional Bonds) and (ii) Tranche B Bonds in the principal maximum amount of USD 85,000,000 (U.S. Dollar eighty-five-million) (plus the principal amount of any Additional Bonds).The Initial Bonds shall be issued at a price of 93% of par value.
