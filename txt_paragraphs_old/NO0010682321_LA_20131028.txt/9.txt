1.1
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"5% Board Discretion" has the meaning as set out in the Master Restructuring Term Sheet.
"Acceptable Bank" means any reputable international bank selected by the Issuer with at least A- rating from Standard & Poor's Rating Services or Fitch Ratings Ltd or A3 rating from Moody's Investors Services Limited.
"Account" means each of the following accounts with an Acceptable Bank:
(a) the Escrow Accounts;
(b) the Earnings Accounts; and
(c) any other accounts at any time established by any member of the Debtor Group (other than the Excluded Bank Accounts).
"Account Bank" means each Acceptable Bank in which the relevant member of the Group holds an Account.
