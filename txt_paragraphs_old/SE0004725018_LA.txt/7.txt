"Bondholders' Meeting" has the meaning set forth in Clause 15 (Bondholders' Meeting).
"Business Day" means a day (other than a Saturday or Sunday) on which banks are open for general business in Stockholm and, in relation to any date for payment or purchase of euro, any day on which TARGET is open for settlement of payments in Euro.
"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant interest Payment Date falls on a day that is not a Business Day, that date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day.
"Capital Lease Obligation" means, at the time any determination thereof is to be made, the amount of the liability in respect of a capital lease that would at that time be required to be activated on a balance sheet in accordance with GAAP.
