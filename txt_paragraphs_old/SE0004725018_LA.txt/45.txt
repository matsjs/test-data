6. Listing
(a) The Issuer shall within four (4) months from the Issue Date apply for listing of the Bonds on the Exchange.
(b) The Issuer will use all efforts to obtain and maintain the listing on the Bonds on the Exchange as long as any Bond is outstanding, however no longer than up to and including the last day on which trading in the Bonds on the Exchange, according to the regulations applied by the Exchange and Euroclear Sweden at such date, can reasonably take place prior to the Redemption Date.
7. interest
(a) The Bonds will bear interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal Amount from the Issue Date up to and including the Redemption Date.
(b) The interest will be paid in arrears on each Interest Payment Date and shall be calculated on a 30/360-days basis.
