5. Conversion
As long as there are any EUR Bonds outstanding, each Bondholder (a "Converting Bondholder") holding such EUR Bonds shall have the right to convert its EUR Bonds into SEK Bonds, in the following manner:
(a) a Converting Bondholder shall notify the Issuer and the Issuing Agent at least twenty (20) Business Days prior to the designated Conversion Date (the "Conversion Notice");
(b) the Conversion Notice shall set out the Conversion Date, the amount and number of Conversion Bonds that shall be converted into New SEK Bonds;
(c) following receipt by the Issuing Agent and the issuer of the Conversion Notice, the Issuing Agent shall (A) request a debt register from Euroclear Sweden, detailing each Bondholder and VP Account, and (B) instruct Euroclear Sweden to issue New SEK Bonds corresponding to the amount of the Conversion Bonds (including the amount of any accrued but unpaid interest on such Conversion Bonds) at the Conversion Rate (as calculated by the Issuing Agent and instructed to Euroclear Sweden), which pending the Conversion shall be transferred to the Issuing Agent's VP Account;
