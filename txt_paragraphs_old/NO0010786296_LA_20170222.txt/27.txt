2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 850,000,000, which are settled either in cash or in kind by delivery of Existing Bonds.
2.2.2 The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000.
