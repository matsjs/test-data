10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature at and be repaid at Face Value (100%) on the Maturity Date to the extent the Bonds have not been prepaid in accordance with the provisions below.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1
The Issuer may redeem all, but not only some, of the Bonds at any time from and including the First Call Date up to (but not including) the Maturity Date, in which case the Bonds shall be redeemed at a price equal to the Call Option Amount plus accrued but unpaid interest up (to but not including) the settlement date for the Call Option. The Call Option Amount shall be
(a) 103.9% of Face Value if the Bonds are redeemed on or after the First Call Date up to (but not including) the date falling 36 months after the Issue Date;
