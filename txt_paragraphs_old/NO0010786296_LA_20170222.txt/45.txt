10.4 Equity Claw-Back
The Issuer may at one occasion, in connection with an Equity Listing Event, repay up to 35.00% of the aggregate principal amount of the Bonds (provided at least 65.00% of the aggregate principal amount of the Bond Issue remains outstanding after such repayment), in which case the relevant amount shall be repaid pro rata as provided for below and the Face Value per Bond be reduced accordingly.
10.4.2
The repayment must occur on an Interest Payment Date within 180 calendar days after such Equity Listing Event and be made with funds in an aggregate amount not exceeding the cash proceeds received by the Issuer as a result of such Equity Listing Event (net of fees, charges and commissions actually incurred in connection with such offering and net of taxes paid or payable as a result of such offering).
10.4.3
The repayment per Bond shall be at a price equal to the percentage applied for the Call Option for the relevant period applied to the relevant Face Value (such percentage to be equal to the percentage for the first Call Option upon Equity Claw Back before the First Call Date), together with accrued but unpaid interest up to (but not including) the settlement date for the claw-back.
