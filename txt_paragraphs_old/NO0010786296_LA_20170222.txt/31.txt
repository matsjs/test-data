Conditions Precedent
Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Escrow Account will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received no later than two (2) Business Days prior to the Issue Date the documents listed below, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by all parties hereto;
(b) the Escrow Account Pledge duly executed and perfected by all parties thereto, including relevant acknowledgement of notice from the bank operating the Escrow Account;
(c) a duly executed agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee related to expenses and fees;
