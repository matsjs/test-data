The Bond Trustee may reach other decisions than set out in Clauses 17.1.3 or 17.1.4 to amend or rectify decisions which due to spelling errors, calculation mistakes, misunderstandings or other obvious errors do not have the intended meaning.
17.1.7 The Issuer, the Bondholders and the Exchange shall be notified of decisions made by the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 17.1 unless such notice obviously is unnecessary.
17.1.8 The Bondholders' Meeting can decide to replace the Bond Trustee without the Issuer's approval, in accordance with the majority requirements in Clause 16.3.5.
17.1.9 The Bond Trustee may act as bond trustee and/or security agent for several bond issues relating to the Issuer notwithstanding potential conflicts of interest. The Bond Trustee may delegate exercise of its powers to other professional parties.
17.1.10 The Bond Trustee may instruct the Paying Agent to split the Bonds to a lower denomination in order to facilitate partial redemptions or restructuring of the Bonds or other situations.
17.1.11 The Bond Trustee may for the purpose of facilitating a refinancing of the Bond Issue:
(a) waive any notice period in relation to the exercise of the call option provided that the Issuer pays the interest which would have been payable by it in that period (if the Floating Rate has not been fixed in parts of that period, the Floating Rate for the relevant part thereof shall equal the interpolated NOK mid-swap rate for that part of the period plus the Margin); and
(b) notwithstanding any restriction in this Bond Agreement, accept the incurrence by the Issuer (or another Group Company) of debt for the purpose of refinancing the Bond Issue, and the granting of security and/or guarantees for such debt, provided that such debt is fully subordinated to amounts owing under or in connection with the Bond Issue and the relevant holder of such security and/or guarantee undertakes not to exercise any of its/their rights in respect thereof, unless and until the Bond Issue has been repaid in full.
