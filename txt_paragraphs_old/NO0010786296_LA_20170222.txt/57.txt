13.4 Special covenants
The Issuer shall not sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any of the Material Subsidiaries to any person not being a member of the Group, unless the transaction is carried out at fair market

value, on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and further provided that such transaction does not have a Material Adverse Effect. The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee of any such transaction, and upon request provide relevant details thereof.
13.4.2 Nomination of Material Subsidiary
The Issuer shall once every year (simultaneously with the delivery to the Bond Trustee of the annual Financial Reports) (in addition to those listed as "Guarantors" on the Issue Date) identify and nominate the Group Companies which qualify as Material Subsidiaries, and ensure that each such Material Subsidiaries no later than 60 days after its nomination provide Security in accordance with the Agreed Security Principles and accede to the Intercreditor Agreement.
13.4.3 Restrictions on Distributions
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will, make any Distribution other than Permitted Distributions.
