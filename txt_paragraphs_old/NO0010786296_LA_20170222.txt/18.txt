"Permitted Financial Support" means any guarantee or loan (financial support):
(b) made or granted to or for the benefit of another Group Company;
(c) arising by operation of law and in the ordinary course of trading and not as a result of any default or omission;
(d) arising in the ordinary course of business by way of normal trade credit granted to customers; or
(e) not otherwise permitted above but which in aggregate does not exceed NOK 5 million (or its equivalent in other currencies) at any time.
"Permitted Hedging Obligations" means any obligation of any Group Company under a derivative transaction entered into with one or more Hedging Counterparties.
"Permitted Security" means:
(a) the Security created in accordance with the Security Documents to secure the Secured Debt;
(b) Security granted in favour of any clearing organisation in connection with the clearing of derivatives transactions constituting Permitted Hedging Obligations;
