10.2 10.2.1
The Issuer may redeem parts o f the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue (Call Option) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in July 2011 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 106 % of par value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures o f the Security Depository).
10.2.4
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each o f the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
