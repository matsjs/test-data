2.2 The Bonds
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN DOF Subsea AS Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2009/2012".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) o f the Bond Issue will be NO 001 052662.7.
The tenor o f the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds o f the Bonds shall be employed for (i) refinancing of the existing DOFSUB02 bond (ISIN NO 001 045320.2) and (ii) general corporate purposes.
