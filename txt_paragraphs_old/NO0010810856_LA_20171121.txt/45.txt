14.9 Distributions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, make any Distribution other than a Permitted Distribution.
Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Obligor will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any asset which is subject to the Transaction Security, provided

however that a sale, transfer or other disposal shall be allowed if it constitutes a Permitted Disposal.
(b) The Bond Trustee shall be authorised to release existing Transaction Security of any Obligor in connection with a Permitted Disposal, and (to the extent applicable) re establish similar Transaction Security pursuant to these Bond Terms subject to such procedures and closing mechanisms as the Bond Trustee shall determine in its discretion.
14.11
Arm's lengths terms
Without limiting Clause 14.2 (Compliance with laws), the Issuer shall, and shall procure that each other Obligor will, conduct all business transactions with any Obligor, any Affiliate which is not an Obligor or any third party, at market terms and otherwise on an arm's length basis.
