14.6 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness or maintain or prolong any existing Financial Indebtedness.
14.7 Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any Security over any of its/their assets (whether present or future).
14.8 Financial support The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, be a creditor in respect of any Financial Support to or for the benefit of any person not being a Group Company.
14.9 Distributions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, make any Distribution other than a Permitted Distribution.
