14.15
Maintenance of Vessels
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of the Vessels and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times. During operation of the Vessels, the Issuer shall ensure that the Vessel Managers runs proper maintenance of the Vessels keeps the Vessels in good and safe condition and repair consistent with prudent ownership and industry standards.
