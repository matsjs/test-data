The following terms will have the following meanings:
"Affiliate" means, in relation to any specified person:
(a) any person which is a Subsidiary of the specified person;
(b) any person who has Decisive Influence over the specified person (directly or indirectly); and
(c) any person which is a Subsidiary of an entity who has Decisive Influence (directly or indirectly) over the specified person.
"Annual Financial Statements" means the audited unconsolidated and consolidated annual financial statements of the Issuer for any financial year, prepared in accordance with GAAP, such financial statements to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and report of the board o f directors.
"Approved Shipbroker" means any of Feamleys, Hesnes Shipping, and Clarkson Platou or such other independent reputable ship broker nominated by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee from time to time.
"Approved Ship Registry" means the Norwegian International Ship Registry, the Bahamas Ship Registry, the Marshall Islands Ship Registry, the Isle of Man Ship Registry, the Cayman Islands Ship Registry, the Antigua and Barbuda Ship Registry or any ship registry as approved by the Bond Trustee from time to time.
"Assignment Agreement" means an assignment agreement with respect to assignment of Earnings and Insurances, and pledge of Accounts, between the Obligors and the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Obligors' obligations under the Finance Documents, in form and content satisfactory to the Bond Trustee.
