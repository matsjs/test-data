14.17
Technical inspection of Vessels
The Issuer shall, upon request of the Bond Trustee, arrange for the Bond Trustee, and/or any person appointed by the Bond Trustee, to undertake a technical inspection of the Vessels without interference of the daily operation of the Vessels and at the expense of the Issuer, however limited to one yearly inspection per Vessel unless an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing.
14.18 Financial Covenants The Issuer shall procure that the Obligors will comply with the following:
(a) as of the date falling 15 month after the Interest Accrual Date, the Vessel LTV Ratio shall not exceed 75.0%; and
(b) The Issuer shall, at all times from and including the Issue Date, maintain a minimum Liquidity (on a consolidated basis) of no less than (i) USD 1,000,000 in the period from and including the Issue Date to but not including 28 November 2018, and (ii) USD 1,500,000 in the period from and including 28 November 2018 to the Bonds have been redeemed in full.
The Vessel LTV Ratio shall be measured each 30 June (first time 30 June 2019) and 31 December and certified by the Issuer in the Compliance Certificate to be delivered to the Bond Trustee together with each Interim Account. The Liquidity shall be measured and reported by the Issuer to the Bond Trustee on the 15thof every calendar month.
