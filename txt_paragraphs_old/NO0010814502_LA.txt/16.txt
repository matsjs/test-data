"Total Assets" means, on any date, the aggregate book value of those assets of the HoldCo Group (on a consolidated basis) which according to GAAP should be included as assets in the balance sheet.
"Total Debt" means the Issuer's debt or other financial instrument that has the commercial effect of a borrowing, including guarantees for loans but excluding Subordinated Loans. However, in the event the Issuer makes any repayment of principal or payment of interest in relation to any Subordinated Loan, such subordinated loan shall be included in the calculation of Total Debt from the date such repayment of principal or payment of interest takes place.
"Total Equity" means the booked equity of the Issuer in accordance with GAAP, but excluding booked goodwill acquired after the date of these Bond Terms (however, if a change in GAAP applicable for the treatment of pensions or derivative transactions occurs, any adverse effects on the accounts shall be excluded when determining Total Equity).
"Value Adjusted Total Assets" means Total Assets adjusted for any Excess Value.
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds less the Issuer's Bonds and a Voting Bond shall mean any single one of those Bonds
"Written Resolution" means a written (or electronic) solution for a decision making among the Bondholders, as set out in Clause 15.5 (Written Resolutions).
