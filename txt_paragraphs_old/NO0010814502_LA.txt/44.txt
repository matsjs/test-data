14.4 Calculation of claim The claim derived from the Outstanding Bonds due for payment as a result of the serving of a Default Notice shall be the Nominal Amount for each Outstanding Bond, including accrued interest and Default interest pursuant to Clause 8.2 (to the extent applicable), without any premium or other penalty.
