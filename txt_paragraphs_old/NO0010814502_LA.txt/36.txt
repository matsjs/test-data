13.7 Financial Covenants
For as long as the Issuer has not obtained Investment Grade Rating, the financial covenants set out in (a) and (b) below shall apply at all times and shall be certified by the Issuer in each Compliance Certificate:
(a) Total Debt to Total Equity
The Issuer undertakes that the ratio of Total Debt to Total Equity does not exceed 0.8 at any time.
(b) Group Loans The Issuer shall either maintain: (i) a ratio of Group Loans to Net Asset Value that is less than 0.50; or
(ii) outstanding Group Loans of less than NOK 10,000 million.
