The Senior Secured Notes are guaranteed for the full amount of the principal and interest or other payments on a senior secured basis by the Parent Guarantors and the Subsidiary Guarantors.
The Exchange Notes are guaranteed for the full amount of the principal and interest or other payments on a senior basis by the Parent Guarantors and on a senior subordinated basis by the Subsidiary Guarantors.
For the year ended December 31, 2014, the Guarantors generated 108% of the Group's consolidated EBITDA, and as of December 31, 2014, held 89% of the Group's total assets.
