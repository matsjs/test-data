The discount rate applied for the pension schemes in Norway for 2012 is based on the interest rate for covered bonds, and not the interest rate for government bonds as used previously. The impact of this change is an increase in the discount rate, since the interest rate for covered bonds is higher than the interest rate on government bonds.
Subsidiaries can deviate from these assumptions if local conditions require this. The discount rates applied vary from 2.5% to 4.5% . For Norske Skog Walsum Gm bH, the discount rate used is 4.5%.
