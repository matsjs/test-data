P ro visio ns
Provisions for environmental restoration, dismantling costs, restructuring activities and legal claims are recognised when the group has a present legal or constructive obligation as a result of past events; an outflow of resources is more likely than not to be required to settle the obligation and the amount can be reliably estimated.
Provisions for future environmental and dismantling liabilities are based on a number of assumptions made using management's best judgment.
See Note 2 in the annual financial statements for 2013 for a more thorough description of important accounting estimates and assumptions impacting the preparation of financial statements.
C o n tin g e n t lia b ilitie s
Norske Skog is an international company that, through its ongoing business operations, will be exposed to litigation and claims from public authorities and contracting parties.
Norske Skog has an ongoing process related to simplification of the group's corporate structure. This, in combination with changes in individual countries' tax laws, could increase the group's tax exposure.
