NORSKE SKOGINDUSTRIER ASA NOTES TO THE CONSOLIDATED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS
1. GENERAL INFORMATION
Norske Skogindustrier ASA (``the com pany'') and its subsidiaries (together ``the group'') manufacture, distribute and sell publication paper. This includes newsprint and magazine paper. The group has 11 fully and partly owned mills on four continents.
Norske Skogindustrier ASA is incorporated in Norway and has its head office at Lysaker outside of Oslo. The com pany is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange. The consolidated financial statements were authorised for issue by the board of directors in Norske Skogindustrier ASA on 1 March 2013.
