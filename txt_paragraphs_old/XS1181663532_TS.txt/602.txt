No Personal Liability of Directors, Officers, Employees and Stockholders
No director, officer, employee, incorporator or stockholder of the Exchange Notes Issuer or any of their shareholders or Affiliates, as such, will have any liability for any obligations of the Exchange Notes Issuer or the Guarantors under the Exchange Notes, the Exchange Notes Indenture, the Exchange Note Guarantees or for any claim based on, in respect of, or by reason of, such obligations or their creation. Each holder of Exchange Notes by accepting a Note waives and releases all such liability. The waiver and release are part of the consideration for issuance of the Exchange Notes. The waiver may not be effective to waive liabilities under applicable securities laws.
