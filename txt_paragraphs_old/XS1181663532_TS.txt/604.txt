Amendment, Supplement and Waiver
Except as provided otherwise in the succeeding paragraphs, the Exchange Notes Indenture, the Exchange Notes and any Exchange Note Guarantee may be amended or supplemented with the consent of the holders of at least a majority in aggregate principal amount of the Exchange Notes then outstanding (including, without limitation, consents obtained in connection with a purchase of, or tender offer or exchange offer for, Exchange Notes), and any existing Default or Event of Default or compliance with any provision of the Exchange Notes Indenture, the Exchange Notes and the Exchange Note Guarantees may be waived with the consent of the holders of a majority in aggregate principal amount of the then outstanding Exchange Notes (including, without limitation, consents obtained in connection with a purchase of, or tender offer or exchange offer for, Exchange Notes); p ro v id e d that, if any amendment, waiver or other modification will only affect one series of the Exchange Notes, only the consent of a majority in principal amount of the then outstanding Exchange Notes of such series shall be required.
