12. Maintenance Test
12.1 Maintenance Test
The Maintenance Test is satisfied if:
(i) the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 2.50x;
(ii) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than 3.75x; and
(iii) Issuer's Cash and Cash Equivalents amounts to at least SEK 20,000,000.
