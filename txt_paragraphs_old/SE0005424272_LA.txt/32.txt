PAYMENTS IN RESPECT OF THE NOTES
8.1
Any payment or repayment under the Terms and Conditions, or any amount due in respect
of a repurchase of any Notes, shall be made to such person who is registered as a
Noteholder on the Record Date prior to an Interest Payment Date or other relevant due
date, or to such other person who is registered with the CSD on such date as being entitled
to receive the relevant payment, repayment or repurchase amount.
