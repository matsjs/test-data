NO DIRECT ACTIONS BY NOTEHOLDERS
21.1
A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer to enforce or recover
any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Terms and Conditions, or to initiate, support
or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation
(foretagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other
jurisdiction) of the Issuer in relation to any of the liabilities of the Issuer under the Terms
and Conditions.
