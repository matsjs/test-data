15.3
The notice pursuant to Clause 15.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for the
meeting, (iii) agenda for the meeting (including each request for a decision by the Holders)
and (iv) a form of power of attorney. Only matters that have been included in the notice may
be resolved upon at the Holders' Meeting. Should prior notification by the Holders be
required in order to attend the Holders' Meeting, such requirement shall be included in the
notice.
