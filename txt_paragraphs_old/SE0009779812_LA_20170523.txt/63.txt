The following matters shall require consent of Holders representing at least two thirds (2/3)
which Holders reply in a Written Procedure in accordance with the instructions given
pursuant to Clause 16.3:
(a) waive a breach of or amend an undertaking set out in Clause 11 {Special undertakings)',
(b) a mandatory exchange of Bonds for other securities;
(c) reduce the principal amount, Interest Rate or Interest which shall be paid by the Issuer;
(d) amend any payment day for principal or Interest or waive any breach of a payment undertaking; or
(e) amend the provisions in this Clause 14.5.
