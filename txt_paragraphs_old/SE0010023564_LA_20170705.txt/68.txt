Clause 14 as soon as reasonably practicable.
If the Issuer or the Agent shall make any payment under this Clause 14, the Issuer or the
Agent, as applicable, shall notify the Noteholders of any such payment at least fifteen
(15) Business Days before the payment is made. Such notice shall specify the Record
Date, the payment date and the amount to be paid. Notwithstanding the foregoing, for
any Interest due but unpaid the Record Date specified in Clause 7.1 shall apply.
