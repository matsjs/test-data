"Subsequent Notes" means any Notes issued after the First Issue Date on one or more occasions.
"Swedish Kronor" and "SEK" means the lawful currency of Sweden.
"Swedish Government Bond Rate" means direct obligations of Sweden, acting through the Swedish National Debt Office (a Swedish Government Bond; Sw. statsobligation) with a fixed maturity most nearly equal to the period from the Redemption Date to the Final Maturity Date, provided that:
(a) if the period from the Redemption Date to the Final Maturity Date is not equal to the fixed maturity of a direct obligation of Sweden for which a weekly average yield is given, the Swedish Government Bond Rate shall be obtained by linear interpolation from the weekly average yields of direct obligations of Sweden for which such yields are given; and
(b) if the period from the Redemption Date to the Final Maturity Date is less than one (1) year, the weekly average yield on actually traded direct obligations of adjusted to a fixed maturity of one year shall be used.
