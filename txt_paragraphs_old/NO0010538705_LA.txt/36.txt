Should the Borrower exercise the Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed of this (the Bondholders in writing via the Securities Depository) no later than twenty (20) Banking Days before the date of redemption.
For the avoidance of doubt, each Bondholder may within the Exercise Period elect to exercise its Conversion Right after having received the Borrower's Call Option notice.
