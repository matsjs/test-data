17.3
The notice pursuant to Clause 17.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for the
meeting, (iii) agenda for the meeting (including each request for a decision by the
Noteholders) and (iv) a form of power of attorney. Only matters that have been included in
the notice may be resolved upon at the Noteholders' Meeting. Should prior notification by
the Noteholders be required in order to attend the Noteholders' Meeting, such requirement
shall be included in the notice.
