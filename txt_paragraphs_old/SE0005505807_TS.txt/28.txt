8.
INTEREST
8.1
Each Initial Note carries Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the First Issue
Date up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Note will carry
Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Interest Payment Date falling
immediately prior to its issuance up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date.
