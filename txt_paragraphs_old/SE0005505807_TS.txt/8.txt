"Interest" means the interest on the Notes calculated in accordance with Clauses 8.1 to 8.3.
"Interest Cover" means, in respect of any Reference Period, the ratio of (i) Consolidated EBITDA plus all financial income in each case for that Reference Period to (ii) Consolidated Financial Payable for that Reference Period.
"Interest Payment Date" means 30 June and 30 December of each year or, to the extent such day is not a Business Day, the Business Day following from an application of the Business Day Convention. The first Interest Payment Date for the Notes shall be 30 June 2014 and the last Interest Payment Date shall be the relevant Redemption Date.
