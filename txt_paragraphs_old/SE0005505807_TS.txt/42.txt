11.8 11.8.1
Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not acquire or invest, and shall procure that no Subsidiary acquires or invests, in shares or other ownership interests in any company or entity whose principal business operations do not comply with the terms of Clause 11.1.2.
Disposals
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of any shares or ownership interests in JSM Capital AB and notwithstanding Clause 11.7.2 shall procure that JSM Capital AB does not sell or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets.
Subject always to Clause 11.7.1, the Issuer may not sell or otherwise dispose of any shares or ownership interests in any Subsidiary and shall procure that no Subsidiary sells or otherwise disposes of all or substantially all of its assets unless:
(a) the purchaser or recipient is a Group Company; or
