Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than sixty (60) days following the Total Loss Event, redeem (a) 100% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related to both Vessels or (b) 60% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related one of the Vessels.
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.5, the amount in the Liquidity Account or any of the insurance proceeds which are available for prepayment pursuant to Clause 13.6(a) may be used as part payment in relation to the Total Loss prepayment, however so that if the Mandatory Prepayment Event relates to one of the Vessels only, only an amount exceeding USD 5,000,000 in the Liquidity Account may be used as part payment.
10.6 10.6.1
Miscellaneous
A Mandatory Prepayment Event or a Total Loss Event shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the expected settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or the Total Loss Event. The settlement date may however, upon further notice to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders, be reduced or prolonged, due to the settlement of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event, but not with shorter notice than five (5) Business Days before the settlement day of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event.
10.6.2
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall pay (if on or about the date upon which the sale or disposal of shares or a Vessel is completed, by transferring the redemption amount to an escrow account on the date upon which the sale or disposal of the relevant shares or Vessel is completed, for the redemption of Bonds to be carried out as soon as possible thereafter, as provided for in Clause 10.4.1) to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond and any unpaid cash interest and uncapitalised payment in kind interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
