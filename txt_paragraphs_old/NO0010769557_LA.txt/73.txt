(c) Title
(i) Each Vessel Owner shall hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in its Vessel and the insurances taken out in respect of the Vessel and assigned to the Bond Trustee, free of any and all Security except for that expressly permitted under Clause 13 (Covenants).
(ii) No Vessel Owner shall dispose of a Vessel unless the Bonds are redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.4 (Mandatory Prepayment).
(d) Class, flag, name, registry
From the delivery date, the Issuer and the Vessel Owners shall ensure that each Vessel maintains its class, flag and name, and that it remains registered in the Marshall Islands only, and that the Vessels are submitted to such periodic or other surveys as may be required for classification purposes and to ensure full compliance with regulations of the applicable ship registry.
(e) Operations in accordance with laws etc.
The Issuer and the Vessel Owners shall at all times ensure that the Vessels are operated in accordance with any relevant laws, regulations, administrative decisions and/or other public authorities as applicable from time to time and jurisdiction to jurisdiction.
