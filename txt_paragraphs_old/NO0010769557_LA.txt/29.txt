2.1 2.1.1
Binding natureof thisBond Agreement
By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 18.1.
This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in a USD amount equal to the amount outstanding under the Existing Bond Issue as of 4 August 2016, at the date of this Agreement estimated to be approximately USD 246,117,912.00 (USD two hundred and forty six million one hundred and seventeen thousand nine hundred and twelve). In addition, the Issuer may issue Additional Bonds in accordance with Clause 9 (Interest).
The Face Value is USD 1. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "8.40 per cent Nor Offshore SPV, Ltd. Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2016/2020".
