"Bonds" means the debt instruments issued by the Issuer pursuant to this Bond Agreement, including, for the avoidance of doubt, any Additional Bonds.
"Business Day" means any day when banks in Oslo and New York are open for general business, and when banks in Oslo and New York can settle foreign currency transactions.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the period end date occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments of interest will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day {No Adjustments of Business Day).
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2 {Call Option).
"Change of Control Event" means if the Parent ceases to own 100% of the issued share capital of the Issuer or otherwise ceases control 100% of the voting rights attached to the shares of the Issuer.
"Charter Contract" means any contract where a Vessel is utilised and performing services, including any bareboat or time charter contract, any other charter contract and any service contract(s), entered or to be entered into between a Vessel Owner and a Client or, if approved by the Bond Trustee, between a Related Charterer and a Client from time to time.
"Charter Contract Assignment" means an assignment of the rights (including step-in rights) of the relevant Vessel Owner or, as the case may be, any Related Charterer under any Charter Contract (including all earnings payable and security granted by the Client thereunder), and the relevant Vessel Owner or, as the case may be, any Related Charterer shall give notices and seek to obtain consents (if required thereunder) and acknowledgements of such assignments from the relevant Client, such assignment to be provided only to the extent permitted by applicable law and the terms of the relevant Charter Contract.
"Charter Free Market Value of the Vessels" means the sum of the fair market value of each of the Vessels in USD determined as the arithmetic mean of independent valuations of each of the relevant Vessels obtained from two independent and well-reputed sale and purchase brokers familiar with the market for such Vessels appointed by the Issuer and being selected from the list of Approved Shipbrokers. Such valuation shall be made on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms as between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts for employment.
