"Issue Date" means the date the Bonds are issued, expected to be on or about 29 July 2016.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
"LIBOR" means the London interbank offered rate administered by the British Bankers' Association (ot any other person which takes over the administration of that rate) for the relevant currency and period displayed on pages LIBOR01 or LIBOR02 of the Reuters screen (or any replacement Reuters page which displays that rate) on the appropriate page of such other information service which publishes that rate from time to time in place of Reuters, for USD and for a period of 3 months, and if the rate is less than zero, LIBOR shall be deemed to be zero.
"Liquidity Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer opened in accordance with and for the purpose of Clause 13.7(c).
"Management Agreement" means any agreement for the operational, technical and commercial management of each of the Vessels, such management agreement to be entered into between the Vessel Manager as the manager and the relevant Vessel Owners.
"Manager" means DNB Bank ASA.
"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means:
(a) except if it constitutes a Total Loss Event, the proceeds of a sale or other disposal of any of Vessels are distributed; or
(b) the Issuer ceases to own 100% of the issued share capital of any Vessel Owner or otherwise ceases control 100% of the voting rights attached to the shares of any Vessel Owner.
