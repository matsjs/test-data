10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 10.1.1
Maturity and instalments
The Bonds shall (if applicable, depending on the amount (if any) deposited on the Debt Service Account, and subject to Clause 10.1.2) be partially repaid by the Issuer in an amount equal to the Instalment Amount (if any) less the amount of accrued interest on the redeemed amount in quarterly instalments (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).The first instalment falls due on the first Interest Payment Date after the Issue Date, and each subsequent instalment falls due on each quarterly Interest Payment Date thereafter.
10.1.2
The Issuer may at its discretion elect to apply the relevant instalment amount to acquire Outstanding Bonds at par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) and pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository) as alternative to repayment of Bonds in accordance with Clause 10.1.1.
10.1.3 Any Outstanding Bonds shall be repaid in full on the Maturity Date at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.1.4 Payment of instalments must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
