10.2
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem the Bonds in whole but not in part at any time from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) plus any value of the Issuer up to the net value of the Vessel, cash balances on any bank accounts, any agreements relating to the Vessel, such as Insurances and Charter Contracts (if any), and any other equity.
10.3 10.3.1
Change of control
Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at a price of 100% of par plus accrued interest.
10.3.2 The Put Option must be exercised within sixty (60) calendar days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee of a Change of Control Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event has taken place.
