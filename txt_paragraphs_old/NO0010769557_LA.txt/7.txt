"Assignment of Charter Contracts" means, to the extent permitted under applicable law and the terms of the relevant Charter Contract, an assignment by the Vessel Owners (and/or the Related Charterer, if applicable) of their rights under the Charter Contracts (if any), including notices of, consents to (if required thereunder) and acknowledgements of such assignments from the Client.
"Assignment of Insurances" means an assignment by the Vessel Owners of their rights in respect of any insurances taken out in respect of any of the Vessels from time to time (other than third party liability insurances), governed by English law, including notices of assignments and loss payable clause to the insurers.
"Assignment of Internal Loans" means a pledge or an assignment (or such similar security under the relevant jurisdiction) granted by the Issuer in respect of the Internal Loans (present and future), governed by English law, including notices and acknowledgements of such assignments.
"Assignment of Management Agreements" means an assignment of any rights of the Vessel Owners under the Management Agreements, such assignment to include (to the extent legally permissible) direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights in case of default, and any payments under the Management Agreements shall be subordinated to the Bonds, however so that management fees that are not overdue may be payable to the Vessel Manager on a monthly basis, and the Vessel Owners shall use reasonable efforts to ensure that the Vessel Manager provides necessary or requested consents and acknowledgements to such assignment and provisions.
"Assignment of Vessel Owner Receivables" means assignments granted by the Vessel Owners in respect of the Vessel Owner Receivables (present and future) governed by English law, including notices and acknowledgements of such assignments.
