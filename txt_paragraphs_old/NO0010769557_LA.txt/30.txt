The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the 4 August 2016 to the Maturity Date.
The Issuer may, provided that no Event of Default is continuing and one or both of the Vessels have not been sold by the Issuer, elect to extend the Maturity Date by giving an extension request to the Bond Trustee not less than five (5) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The Bonds shall exclusively be employed to finance the purchase of the Vessels and the transfer of the Issue Date Cash to the Issuer pursuant to the Memorandum of Agreement and be issued to the Bondholders as settlement in full of the amounts outstanding (at par value) under the Existing Bond Issue, to allow for release of the existing mortgages over the Vessels.
