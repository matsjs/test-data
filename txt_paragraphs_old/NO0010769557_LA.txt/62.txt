13.4
Obligors' General Covenants (a) Pari passu ranking
The Obligors shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
(b) Mergers
No Obligor shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of any Obligor with any other companies or entities.
