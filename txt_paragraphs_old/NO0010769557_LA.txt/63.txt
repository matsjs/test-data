(c) De-mergers
No Obligor shall carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of any Obligor into two or more separate companies or entities.
(d) Continuation of business
No Obligor shall cease to carry on its business, and each Obligor shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
Each Obligor shall remain a single-purpose company (with no Subsidiaries other than the Vessel Owners), for the Vessel Owners owning and chartering the relevant Vessel.
(e) Disposal of business
No Obligor shall sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless the Bonds are redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.4 (Mandatory Prepayment).
