13.2 Information Covenants 13.2.1 The Issuer shall:
(a) without being requested to do so, promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing of any Event of Default, any event or circumstance which the Issuer understands or ought to understand may lead to an Event of Default and any other event which may have a Material Adverse Effect;
(b) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee in writing if the Issuer agrees to sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, or change the nature of its business;
(c) without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and make them available on its website in English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 120 days after the end of the financial year, provided always that such preparation and publication of reports are in accordance with applicable rules and regulations;
(d) without being requested to do so, prepare Interim Accounts and make them available on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication on Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 60 days after the end of the relevant quarter, provided always that such preparation and publication of reports are in accordance with applicable rules and regulations;
(e) upon the request of the Bond Trustee, at the Issuer's own cost, send to the Bond Trustee reports on the Charter Free Market Value of the Vessels not to be dated more than thirty (30) days prior to the date of submission to the Bond Trustee;
(f) without being requested to do so, at least quarterly, or following an Event of Default, upon the request of the Bond Trustee, at the Issuer's own cost, send to the Bond Trustee for publication on www.stamdata.no reports on the performance of the Vessels and the Issuer;
