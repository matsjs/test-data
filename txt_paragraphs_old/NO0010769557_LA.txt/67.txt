(f) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Obligors shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than (i) the Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue, (ii) Internal Loans and (iii) Vessel Owner Receivables.
(g) Financial support restrictions
The Obligors shall not grant any loans, guarantees, or other financial assistance to any party other than (i) the Guarantees, (ii) Internal Loans, (iii) Vessel Owner Receivables and (iv) the Existing Bonds.
(h) Project Documents - corporate documents
(i) The Obligors shall enforce any rights that they have to ensure that the relevant Vessel is to the best of its knowledge at all times operated, insured and maintained in accordance with the Project Documents.
(ii) The Obligors shall not agree to or permit any changes to the Project Documents, or the memorandum and articles of association of any of the Obligors.
(iii) The Obligors shall ensure that any payments under the Management Agreements shall be subordinated to the Bonds, however so that management fees that are not overdue may be payable to the Vessel Manager on a monthly basis.
