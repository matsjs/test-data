GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS
The Issuer shall comply, and shall procure that each other Issuer Group Company complies, in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject and its articles of association and other constitutional documents.
Mergers
The Issuer shall not enter into any merger unless where it is the surviving entity.
The Issuer shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries enters into any merger except in mergers with another Issuer Group Company, provided that if the shares in the transferor company but not the transferee company are pledged subject to the Transaction Security, the shares in the transferee company are pledged to the Secured Parties on terms satisfactory to the Agent in accordance with the Intercreditor Agreement.
Business of the Issuer Group
The Issuer shall not carry out any other business than financing the Issuer Group Companies, lending the proceeds from such financing to any or all of the Issuer Group Companies, owning the shares in the Issuer Group Companies other than the Issuer, and any business incidental thereto.
The Issuer shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries conducts any business other than:
