NOTEHOLDERS' COMMITTEE
16.1
The Noteholders may appoint a committee (a "Noteholders' Committee") to represent the
interests of the Noteholders. A Noteholders' Committee shall consist of no less than three
(3) natural persons. All members of a Noteholders' Committee shall be elected at a
Noteholders' Meeting.
