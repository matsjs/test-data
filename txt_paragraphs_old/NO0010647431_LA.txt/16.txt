2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of NOK 2,500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner twobillion). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 1,500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner onebillionfivehundredmillion).
The Bonds will be in denominations o f NOK 500 000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Aker Solutions ASA Senior Unsecured Open Bond Issue 2012/2017".
The International Securities Identification Number (1SIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0647431.
The tenor o f the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
