Interpretation
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses may be defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account M anager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Aker Oilfield Services" means the operations and services performed from time to time by the business area of the Group currently named "Aker Oilfield Services", which operations and services include subsea intervention, riserless and wireless light well intervention, light drilling services and certain other corresponding services to the oil service sector (and which operations and services are performed through, amongst others, the Group's vessel fleet programme described in Clause 13.3 (g) (xv) {Negative Pledge)).
"Attachm ent" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bank Facility" means the NOK 6,000,000,000 credit facility as amended from time to time for the Issuer as borrower and with DnB Bank ASA acting as agent.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bond Reference Rate" means 3 months NIBOR.
