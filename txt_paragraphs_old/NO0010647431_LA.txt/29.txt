10.1 Maturity
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from Interest Payment Date in June 2015 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in June 2016 at 105% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) any time from and included Interest Payment Date in June 2016 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 103% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (in relation to the Bondholders, via VPS) and at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
10.2.4
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date
