10.3 Change o f control or De-listing
10.3.1 Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event or a De-listing Event each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price of 101 % of par plus accrued interest.
10.3.2
The Put Option must be exercised within thirty (30) Business Days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders of a Change of Control Event or a De-listing Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event or a De-listing Event has taken place.
