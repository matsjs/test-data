13.5 Financial Covenants - Leverage Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Leverage Ratio shall not exceed 4.00:1.00, The Leverage Ratio shall be tested on each Accounting Date. The Issuer shall only be in non-compliance (and be obligated to notify the Bond Trustee in this respect) if the Leverage Ratio exceeds 4.00:1.00 during two (2) subsequent Accounting Dates (and so that the relevant exchange rates would be those used by the Issuer in, or in connection with, those financial statements). The Leverage Ratio shall be reported in connection with delivery o f financial accounts in accordance with Clause 13.2.1 (g) above.
For the purpose of this Clause 13.5:
"Accounting Date" means each of 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December in any financial year.

(a) the consolidated operating profits o f the Group (including the results from discontinued operations) before finance costs and tax for that Relevant Period;
