Events of Default
15.1 The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events (which shall be referred to as an "Event of Default") if:
(a) Non-payment The Issuer fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, it is obvious that such failure will be remedied, and payment in full is made, within five (5) Business Days following the original due date.
(b) Breach o f other obligations The Issuer fails to duly perform any other covenant or obligation pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any of the Finance Documents, unless, such failure is remedied within thirty (30) days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
