5. Transfer restrictions
(a) No Bondholder may offer, sell, pledge or otherwise transfer any Bond except:
(i) to the Issuer;
(ii) to a person who the seller reasonably believes is a QIB within the meaning of Rule 144A under the Securities Act purchasing for its own account or for the account or benefit of a QIB in a transaction meeting the requirements of Rule 144A;
