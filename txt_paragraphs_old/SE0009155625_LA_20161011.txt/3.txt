"Agent" means Nordic Trustee & Agency AB (publ), Swedish Reg. No. 556882-1879, or another party replacing it, as Agent, in accordance with these Terms and Conditions.

(a) the present value on the relevant record date of 103,625 per cent. of the Nominal Amount as if such payment originally should have taken place on the First Call Date; plus
