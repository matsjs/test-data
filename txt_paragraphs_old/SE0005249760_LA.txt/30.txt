16.2 Payments after an acceleration
16.2.1 All payments by the Issuer relating to the Bonds following an acceleration of the Bonds in accordance with Condition 12 (Acceleration of the Bonds) shall be distributed in the following order of priority, in accordance with the instructions of the Agent:
(a) first, in or towards payment pro rata of (i) all unpaid fees, costs, expenses and indemnities payable by the Issuer to the Agent (other than any indemnity given for liability against the Holders), (ii) other costs, expenses and indemnities relating to the acceleration of the Bonds or the protection of the Holders' rights as may have been incurred by the Agent and (iii) any costs and expenses incurred by the Agent in relation to a Holders' Meeting that have not been reimbursed by the Issuer in accordance with paragraph (j) of Condition 13.2 (Holders' meeting and procedure in writing)-,
(b) secondly, in or towards payment pro rata of accrued but unpaid Interest under the Bonds (Interest due on an earlier Interest Payment Date to be paid before any Interest due on a later Interest Payment Date);
(c) thirdly, in or towards payment pro rata of any unpaid principal under the Bonds; and
(d) fourthly, in or towards payment pro rata of any other costs or outstanding amounts unpaid under the term and Conditions.
16.1.2 If the Issuer or the Agent shall make any payment under this Condition 16.2 (Payments after an acceleration), the Issuer or the Agent, as applicable, shall notify the Holders of any such payment at least fifteen (15) Business Days before the payment is made. Such notice shall specify the Record Date, the payment date and the amount to be paid. Notwithstanding the foregoing, for any Interest due but unpaid the Record Date specified in Condition 7.2 (Payments of principal and interest) shall apply.
