10.6 Mandatory Prepayment - Bond Issue
Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring, the Issuer shall immediately (i) if the occurrence of such Mandatory Prepayment Event is beyond the control of the Issuer, redeem 100% of the Bonds at a price corresponding to 100% of Face Value (together with accrued interest), or (ii) otherwise redeem 100% of the Bonds at a price corresponding to 103.625% of Face Value (together with accrued interest).
