17 EVENTS OF DEFAULT
The Bond Trustee may declare the Bonds to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events:
17.1 Non-payment The Issuer fails to fulfill any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full within five Business Days following the original due date.
