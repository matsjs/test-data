INTEREST
9.1 Interest rate
The Issuer shall pay interest on the Face Value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of seven point twenty five per cent (7.25%) per annum.
9.2 Interest Payment Dates
Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Date semi-annually each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 15 December 2018.
