4 Subordination
4.1 In the event of liquidation or insolvency of the Company, the Bonds will not be paid out of the Company's assets until all of the Company's unsubordinated debts have been paid in full. However, the Bonds shall rank pari passu with other outstanding subordinated debts, which are not explicitly subordinated to the Bonds.
4.2 The Company reserves the right to issue additional subordinated debt and in doing so provide that such subordinated debt shall rank pari passu with the Bonds.
