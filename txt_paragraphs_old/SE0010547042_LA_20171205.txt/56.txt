12.11 CSD related undertakings
The Issuer shall keep the Bonds affiliated with a CSD and comply with all CSD regulations applicable to the Issuer from time to time.

CONDITIONS PRECEDENT TO THE FIRST ISSUE DATE
The Issuer shall provide to the Agent, prior to the First Issue Date, the following:
(a) copies of constitutional documents of the Issuer;
(b) copies of necessary corporate resolutions (including authorisations) of the Issuer; and
(c) executed copies of the Finance Documents.
