12.11.2
Undertakings relating to the Agency Agreement
The Issuer shall, in accordance with the Agency Agreement: (a) pay fees to the Agent; (b) indemnity the Agent for costs, losses and liabilities; (c) furnish to the Agent all information requested by or otherwise required to be
delivered to the Agent; and (d) not act in a way which would give the Agent a legal or contractual right to
terminate the Agency Agreement.
The Issuer and the Agent shall not agree to amend any provisions of the Agency Agreement without the prior consent of the Noteholders if the amendment would be detrimental to the interests of the Noteholders.
