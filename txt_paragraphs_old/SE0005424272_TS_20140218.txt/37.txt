Issuer's purchase of Notes
Provided that no Senior Notes Obligations are outstanding, or following a duly given consent from the Senior Noteholders, the Issuer may, subject to applicable law, at any time and at any price purchase Notes on the market or in any other way. The Notes held by the Issuer may at the Issuer's discretion be retained, sold or cancelled by the Issuer.
