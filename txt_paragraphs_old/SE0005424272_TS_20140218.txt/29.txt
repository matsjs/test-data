A Noteholder may issue one or several powers of attorney to third parties to represent it in

7.3
The Agent shall only have to examine the face of a power of attorney or other proof of
authorisation that has been provided to it pursuant to Clause 7.2 and may assume that it has
been duly authorised, is valid, has not been revoked or superseded and that it is in full force
and effect, unless otherwise is apparent from its face.
