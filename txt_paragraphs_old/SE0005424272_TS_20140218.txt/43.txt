Mergers
Business of the Issuer
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by it as of the Issue Date.
Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not incur or allow to subsist any Financial Indebtedness that is subordinated to the Senior Notes or to any Financial Indebtedness replacing the Senior Notes, unless such Financial Indebtedness incurred:
(a) is also subordinated to, or ranked pari passu with, the Notes;
(b) is not supported by any Security provided by a Group Company; and
(c) has a maturity date after the Final Maturity Date.
