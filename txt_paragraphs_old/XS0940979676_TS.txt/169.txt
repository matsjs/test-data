The EBITDA Metrics have limitations as analytical tools, and you should not consider them in isolation or as a substitute for analysis of our results or any performance measures under IFRS as set forth in our financial statements. Some of these limitations are:
 they do not reflect our cash expenditures or future requirements for capital expenditures;
 they do not reflect changes in, or cash requirements for, our working capital needs;
 they do not reflect the interest expense or cash requirements necessary to service interest or principal payments on our debt;
 they do not reflect any cash income taxes that we may be required to pay;
