12.6 Change of Business
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Group taken as a whole from that carried on at the Issue Date.
12.7 Pari Passu Ranking
The Issuer shall ensure that at all times its obligations under the Terms and Conditions rank at least pari passu with the claims of all its other unsubordinated and unsecured creditors, except those whose claims are mandatorily preferred by laws of general application.
