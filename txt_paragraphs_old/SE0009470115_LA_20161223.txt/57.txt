(b) the Issuer Completion Date.
12.10.2
Following the Issuer Completion Date and provided that no Issuer Acquisition Failure has occurred the Parent shall only be allowed to make Distributions provided that:
(a) the amount of such Distribution does not exceed fifty (50) per cent. of the Group's consolidated net profit for the previous Financial Year; and
(b) the Equity Ratio is not less than thirty (30) per cent. immediately following such Distribution.
