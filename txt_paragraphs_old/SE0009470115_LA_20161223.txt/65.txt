Creditors' process
13.9
Cross default
Any Financial Indebtedness of the Issuer is not paid when due, or is declared to be due as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this paragraph 13.9 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness is less than SEK 50,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency).
13.10
Cessation of business
Any Group Company suspends or ceases to carry on (or threatens to suspend or cease to carry on) all or a material part of its business.
