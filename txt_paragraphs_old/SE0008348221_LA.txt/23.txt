3. Use of Proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Bonds, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Bonds, to (i) repay the Vendor Loan by way of on-lending funds to the Target, (ii) finance the acquisition of the Target, (iii) pay Transaction Costs and (iv) to finance general corporate purposes.
