15. Events of Default and Acceleration of the Bonds
15.1 Non-Payment
The Issuer fails to pay an amount on the date it is due in accordance with the Finance Documents unless its failure to pay is caused by administrative or technical error and payment is made within five (5) Business Days of the due date.
15.2 Subordination
The Issuer's obligations under the Convertible Loan are no longer effectively fully subordinated to the Secured Obligations.
15.3 Convertible Loan
The terms of the Convertible Loan are amended, altered, replaced or in any way changed without the prior written consent of the Agent.
