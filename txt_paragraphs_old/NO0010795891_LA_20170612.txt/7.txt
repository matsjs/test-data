"Default Notice" means a written notice to the Issuer as described in Clause 14.2 (Acceleration o f the Bonds).
"Default Repayment Date" means the settlement date set out by the Bond Trustee in a Default Notice requesting early redemption of the Bonds.
"Disposal Account" means an account pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee.
"Distribution" means any:
(a) payment of dividend on shares,
(b) repurchase of own shares,
(c) redemption of share capital (repayment of Shareholder Loans) or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders, or
(d) other similar distribution or transfers of value to the direct and indirect shareholders of any Group Company or the Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders.
