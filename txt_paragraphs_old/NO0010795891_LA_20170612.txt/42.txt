6.2 Distribution (a) Disbursement of the proceeds from the issuance o f the Bonds to the Escrow Account is conditional on the Bond Trustee's confirmation to the Paying Agent that the conditions in Clause 6.1 (Conditions precedent fo r disbursement to the Issuer) have been either satisfied in the Bond Trustee's discretion or waived by the Bond Trustee pursuant to paragraph 6.1(e) above.

(b) The net proceeds from the Initial Issue Amount (or as the case may be, the net proceeds from a Tap Issue) in the Escrow Account shall only be released to the Issuer for utilization in accordance with the use of proceeds as set out in Clause 2.3 ( Use o f proceeds), subject to fulfilment of all relevant conditions precedent as set out in Clause 6.1 (Conditions precedent fo r disbursement to the Issuer). The net proceeds on the Escrow Account may be transferred to the Issuer as one or several partial disbursements (but USD 20 million for general corporate purposes shall be released to the Issuer in one disbursement once all conditions precedent set out in Clause 6.1 (Conditions precedentfo r disbursement to the Issuer) paragraph 6.1(c) is fulfilled);
(c) Any proceeds to be employed towards Additional Vessel Acquisitions shall remain on the Escrow Account until the settlement of the respective transactions;
(d) The Escrow Account shall remain pledged but unblocked from such time as the conditions set out in Clause 6.1(c) are fulfilled. For the avoidance of doubt, the funds on the Escrow Account may be applied for the financing of the purchase price for an Additional Vessel (acquired prior to the fulfilment of the conditions precedent in Clause 6.1(c)).
(e) Following a Tap Issue, the pledged Escrow Account may again be blocked until all conditions for release from the Escrow Account (in such Tap Issue) are again fulfilled unless a separate Escrow Account is established.
(f) Any remaining amount on the Escrow Account of less than USD 5 million, may freely be released to the Issuer and applied for general corporate purposes of the Group.
