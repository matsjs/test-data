"Permitted Transferee" means in relation to a disposal, or otherwise a transfer of shares in the Issuer (directly or indirectly), any other Person approved by a Bondholders' meeting as a transferee (prior to the actual Change of Control Event occurring) with a majority of at least half (50%) of the voting Bonds, and where the Issuer does not have the right to exercise its Change of Control Call Option. Any Bondholders who (as the case may be) have exercised their Change of Control Put Option prior to the Issuer's exercising its Change of Control Call Option shall be prepaid in accordance with the provisions of the Change of Control Call Option. The Issuer may (following such Bondholders' meeting) elect to discontinue and/or withdraw the relevant Change of Control Event, in which case the Issuer shall not have the right to exercise the Change of Control Call Option.
"Put Option" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 10.3 (.Mandatory repurchase due to a Put Option Event).
"Put Option Event" means a Listing Failure Event or a Change of Control Event.
"Put Option Repayment Date" means the settlement date for the Put Option Event pursuant to Clause 10.3 {Mandatory repurchase due to a Put Option Event).
"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
