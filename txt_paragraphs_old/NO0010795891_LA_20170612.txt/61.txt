13.12 Granting and maintenance of Security Documents The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each other Obligor will ensure that:
(a) each Transaction Security Document remains duly created, enforceable and perfected first priority, at the expense of the Issuer or the relevant security provider (as the case may be); and
(b) following an Additional Vessel Acquisition (or any other event resulting in the Group gaining ownership of an Additional Vessel), the relevant Group Company shall establish and maintain Security (as set out under Clause 2.5) as soon as practically possible, but in no event later than 10 business days after transfer o f ownership of such Additional Vessel(s) to the Group and procure that such Security is perfected promptly thereafter.
13.13
Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets (including shares of any Vessel Owning Companies, the Vessels, or other securities in any person) or operations, unless such sale, transfer or disposal would not have a Material Adverse Effect or constitutes a Pennitted Disposal.
(b) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Obligor will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any asset which is subject to the Transaction Security provided however that a sale, transfer or other disposal shall be allowed if it constitutes a Permitted Disposal.
