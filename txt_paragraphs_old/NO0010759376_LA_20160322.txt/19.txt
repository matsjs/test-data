2.1.2 This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds. The NOK Tranche consists of a scries of bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 65,375,000 (Norwegian kroner sixtyfive million three hundred and seventy-five thousand), with denominations of NOK 25.000. The SEK Tranche consists of a series of bonds at a maximum amount of SEK 39,625,000 (Swedish kroner thirty-nine million six hundred and twenty-five thousand), with a denominations of SEK 25,000.
The Face Value is NOK 25,000 for the NOK Tranche and SEK 25,000 for the SEK Tranche.
The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "12 % Hilleren Tomteselskap AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2016/2017".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be ISIN NO 001 0759376 (NOK Tranche) and ISIN NO 001 0759384 (SEK Tranche).
