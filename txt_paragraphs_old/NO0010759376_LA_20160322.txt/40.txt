10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole (all or nothing) as follows (a "Call Option"):
(i) with settlement date at any time from the date falling 1 year after the Issue Date to, but not included, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 % of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
The applicable call price above shall be determined on the basis of the settlement date of the Call Option.
