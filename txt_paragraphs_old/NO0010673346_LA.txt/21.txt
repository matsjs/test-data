The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied towards general corporate purposes of the Group.
LISTING
3.1
The Issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on Oslo Børs or, at the discretion of the
Issuer, on Oslo Børs ASA's Alternative Bond Market ("ABM").
3.2
If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have
been discharged in full.
