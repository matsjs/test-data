"Stamdata" means the web site www.stamdata.no, maintained by the Bond Trustee.
"STIBOR" means the interest rate which is published on Reuter's page "SIOR" (or such other system or on such other page which may replace the mentioned system and page, respectively) at approximately 11 a.m. CET on the relevant Interest Payment Date or, if no such quotation exists, in all cases as determined by the Bond Trustee, using the average of four Nordic commercial banks' (determined by the Bond Trustee) quoted interbank market interest rates in Stockholm, Sweden, or, if only one or no such quotation is available, the interest rate which according to the Bond Trustee's assessment is equal to the interest rate offered by Swedish commercial banks for lending SEK 100,000,000 for the relevant period of time on the interbank market in Stockholm, Sweden. If any such rate is below zero, STIBOR will be deemed to be zero.
"Subsidiary" means an entity over which another entity or person has a determining influence due to (i) direct and indirect ownership of shares or other ownership interests, and/or (ii) agreement, understanding or other arrangement. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of another entity or person if such entity or person has such number of shares or ownership interests so as to represent the majority of the votes in the entity, or has the right to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
"US Securities Act" means the U.S. Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds less the Issuer's Bonds.
