(c) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than:
(i) the Permitted Financial Indebtedness;
(ii) in the event of any acquisition of any company or business; any indebtedness existing in the acquired company at the time of the acquisition and any refinancing of the acquired company's indebtedness; and
(iii) any unsecured intra-group loans granted by any member of the Group.
