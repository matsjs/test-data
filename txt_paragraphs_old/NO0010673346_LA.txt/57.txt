The Issuer shall, and the Issuer shall procure that each Group Company will, maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters adequate insurance with respect to its aircraft, equipment and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as are consistent with prudent business practice.
13.5
Financial Covenants
The Issuer undertakes to comply with the following financial covenants during the term of the Bonds:
(i) Liquidity: The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains Liquidity of minimum SEK 100 million;
(ii) Book Equity to Capital Employed: The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Book Equity which constitutes more than 25 % of Capital Employed;
(iii) Interest Coverage Ratio: The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains an Interest Coverage Ratio equal or greater than 2.25:1.
