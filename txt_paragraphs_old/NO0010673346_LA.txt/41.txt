10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at 100% of par value (plus accrued interests on redeemed amount) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option The Issuer may redeem the Bonds (all or nothing) at any time from and including: (Call Option):
(a) the Interest Payment Date falling 3 years after the Settlement Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling 4 years after the Settlement Date at a price equal to 104 % of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount);
(b) the Interest Payment Date falling 4 years after the Settlement Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling 4 years and 6 months after the Settlement Date at a price equal to 102 % of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount); and
(c) the Interest Payment Date falling 4 years and 6 months after the Settlement Date to, but not including, the Final Maturity Date at a price equal to 101% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount).
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
