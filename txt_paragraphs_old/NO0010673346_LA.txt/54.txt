Special coven ants (a) Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare, make or pay any dividend, charge, fee or other distribution (or interest on any unpaid dividend, charge, fee or other distribution) (whether in cash or in kind) on or in respect of its share capital (or any class of its share capital) or make any payments of principal or interest on an Shareholder Loan if (i) an Event of Default is continuing or will occur as a consequence thereof, or (ii) such dividend, charge, fee, payment or other distribution in aggregate equals or exceeds 50% of Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year. Any un-utilized portion of the dividend permitted pursuant to the above may not be carried forward to any subsequent financial year.
(b) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, create or permit to exist any Security over any of its present or future assets or revenues, other than:
(i) any Security existing at Issue Date, provided that the amounts so secured shall never exceed the relevant amount outstanding at the Issue Date and as set out in Attachment A hereto:
(ii) any required Security granted for financing of new assets in relation to any Permitted Financial Indebtedness (unless otherwise provided in the definition of Permitted Financial Indebtedness) provided that such Permitted Financial Indebtedness is only secured by such new asset, including, for the avoidance of doubt, any share pledge over single purpose companies owning such new assets;
