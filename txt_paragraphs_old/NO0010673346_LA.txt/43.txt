10.3.2 The Put Option must be exercised within 60 days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee of a Change of Control Event (the "Exercise Period"). Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event has taken place.
The Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the redemption request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall occur 20 Business Days after the expiry of the Exercise Period.
