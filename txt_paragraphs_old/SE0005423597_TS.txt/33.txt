11.8 Listing of the Notes
(a) The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that the Notes are listed on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm or on another Regulated Market within two (2) months after the First Issue Date.
(b) Following the listing, the Issuer shall take all actions on its part to maintain the listing as long as any Notes are outstanding, but, however, no longer than up to and including the last day on which the listing reasonably can, pursuant to the then applicable regulations of the Regulated Market and the CSD, subsist.
