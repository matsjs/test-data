Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not engage in, or permit any other Group Company to engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except if such transaction (i) is in the ordinary course of the Issuer's or such Group Company's business, (ii) is a reasonable business requirement and (iii) is on fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to the Issuer or such Group Company, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
