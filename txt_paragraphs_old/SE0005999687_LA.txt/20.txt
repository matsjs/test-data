3.
STATUS OF THE BONDS
3.1
The Bonds constitute direct, general, unconditional, unsubordinated and
secured obligations of the Issuer and shall at all times rank (i) pari passu
and without any preference among them and (ii) at least pari passu with
the claims of its other unsubordinated creditors, except for obligations
which are mandatorily preferred by law.
