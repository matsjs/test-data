2.3 Use of proceeds (a) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue (net of fees and legal cost of the Managers and the Bond Trustee and any other cost and expenses incurred in connection with the Bond Issue) exclusively (i) to refinance the Existing Debt including acquisition of Existing NCG Bonds (and where proceeds may prior to being applied for full refinancing of the NCG Bond Issue, be deposited on a pledged and blocked bank account, as defeasance pledge, as security for the bondholders under the Existing NCG Bond Issue in, order to suspend negative covenants, which would otherwise apply thereunder) and (ii) to fully finance the Minority Acquisitions, and after which any remaining amounts shall be used (iii) for general corporate purposes of the Operating Group.
(b) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the issuance of any Additional Bonds for the general corporate purposes o f the Operating Group (within the limitations o f the terms of the Finance Documents).
