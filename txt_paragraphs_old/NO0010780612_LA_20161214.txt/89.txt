EXECUTION VERSION
Designation of Material Group Companies
(a) The Issuer shall nominate as Material Group Companies such Operating Group Companies as are necessary to ensure that the Material Group Companies in aggregate represent no less than 80% of the total EBITDA and total assets, respectively, of the Operating Group (excluding goodwill and intra-group transfers) on a consolidated basis, based on the preceding four financial quarters.
(b) The Issuer shall ensure that an Operating Group Company whose unconsolidated total assets or total EBITDA represent no less than 10% of the total EBITDA or total assets respectively of the Operating Group (excluding goodwill and intra-group transfers) on a consolidated basis, based on the preceding four financial quarters, shall be nominated as a Material Group Company.
(c) The Issuer shall nominate Material Group Companies once every year (simultaneously with the delivery to the Bond Trustee of the Annual Financial Statements) and upon acquisition of material assets by a Group Company (in addition to those listed as Initial Guarantors).
(d) The Issuer shall ensure no later than 60 days after the nomination of each Material Group Company (i) that such Material Group Company becomes subject to the Transaction Security (as applicable) and (ii) that each such Material Group Company provides the Guarantee and any other Security in accordance with, and accede to, the Intercreditor Agreement.
(e) The identity of the Material Group Companies nominated by the Issuer shall be listed in the Compliance Certificate.
(f) Notwithstanding paragraph (b) above , the Issuer may choose that a company which is holding real property and related Financial Indebtedness as set out in the definition of "Group Reorganisation" paragraph (b) and (c) shall not be a Material Group Company based on the 2016 accounts nomination (and where required calculated on a pro forma basis for the Operational Group as if paragraph (a) of the Group Reorganisation was completed prior to 1 January 2016). For the avoidance of doubt, this paragraph (f) shall not affect the requirement set out in paragraph (a) above.
(g) Furthermore, the Initial Material Group Companies shall be selected based on the Q3 Interim Accounts o f the Operating Group.
