13.14 Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.15
Share issues
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that (A) no other Operating Group Company will, issue any shares except (i) by the Issuer to its shareholder against cash consideration or set-off of debt, (ii) by an Operating Group Company to another Operating Group Company, provided (where relevant) that any additional issued shares in a Material Group Company are included
