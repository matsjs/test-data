"Minority Acquisitions" means the acquisition of shares and/or other transactions having the result that the Issuer becomes 100% owner, directly or indirectly, of each Initial Guarantor for an aggregate purchase price of NOK 674,500,000.
"Net Profit" means for an applicable financial period the Operating Group's net profit, or in respect of the financial year 2016 the Pro-forma Net Profit, (always excluding the Property Companies) after tax according to GAAP including Gain On Sale of Property, however excluding any GAAP accounting effects resulting from appreciation/write downs of fixed assets owned by the Operating Group.
