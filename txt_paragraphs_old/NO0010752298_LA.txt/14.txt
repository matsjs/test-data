"Non-Recourse Lenders" means such financial institutions that provide non recourse project financing to a Solar Park Holding Company for the turn key delivery of a solar power plant.
"Normalized Income Tax Expenses" means income tax expenses averaged over each Relevant Period.
"O&M" means operations and maintenance activities related to, wholly or partly, owned solar parks.
"Outstanding Bonds" means the Bonds not redeemed or otherwise discharged.
"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to act as its paying agent in the Securities Registry with respect to the Bonds.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment o f principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Permitted (EPC) Financing" means short term hade finance obligations or arrangements to that effect arising out of or in connection with either (i) an EPC Contract with an EPC Company (as debtor) and a Solar Park Holding Company (as creditor), and/or (ii) supply agreements with the EPC Company as purchaser and debtor and the suppliers as suppliers and creditors.
"Power Production" means the ownership o f (and capital income from) the Solar Park Holding Companies.
