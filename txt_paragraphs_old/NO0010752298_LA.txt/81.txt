The Issuer is liable for, and shall indemnify the Bond Tmstee fully in respect of, all losses, expenses and liabilities incurred by the Bond Tmstee as a result o f negligence by the Issuer (including its directors, management, officers, employees, agents and representatives) to fulfil its obligations under the terms o f this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including losses incurred by the Bond Trustee as a result of the Bond Trustee's actions based on misrepresentations made by the Issuer in connection with the establishment and performance of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document.
17.2.3
The Bond Trustee can as a condition for carrying out an instruction from the Bondholders (including, but not limited to, instructions set out in Clause 15.3(a) or 16.2.1(b), require satisfactory security and indemnities for any possible liability and anticipated costs and expenses, from those Bondholders who requested that instruction and/or those who voted in favour of the decision to instruct the Bond Tmstee. Any instructions from the Bondholders may be put forward to the Bondholders' Meeting by the Bond Tmstee before the Bond Trustee takes any action.
17.3 Change o f Bond Trustee
17.3.1 Change o f Bond Tmstee shall be carried out pursuant to the procedures set out in Clause 16. The Bond Tmstee shall continue to cany out its duties as bond trustee until such time that a new Bond Trustee is elected.
