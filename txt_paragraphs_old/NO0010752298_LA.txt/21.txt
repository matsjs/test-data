This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million).
The Face Value is NOK 500,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Scatec Solar ASA Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2015/2015".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 0 0 1 IX.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used for the establishment and operation of business based on technology for production of solar-electricity, hereunder investment in companies operating such business.
