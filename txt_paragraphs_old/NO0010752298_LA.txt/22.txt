Listing
3.1 The Issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on Oslo Børs within 12 months from the Issue Date.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full or until a De-listing Event.
