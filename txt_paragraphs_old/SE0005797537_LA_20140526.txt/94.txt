NO DIRECT ACTIONS BY HOLDERS
20.1
A Holder may not take any steps whatsoever against a Group Company or the Guarantor, or
any Subsidiary of the Guarantor, to enforce or recover any amount due or owing to it
pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate, support or procure the winding-up,
dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation (Sw. foretagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy
