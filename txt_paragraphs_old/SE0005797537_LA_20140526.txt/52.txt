11.4
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Material Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of all or some of the shares in any Material Group Company or of all or substantially all of its or a Material Group Company's assets or operations to any Person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries, unless such disposal is carried out at fair market value and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
