2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue the Loan in the maximum amount equivalent to NOK 1,000,000,000 divided into the NOK Tranche and the USD Tranche. Each Tranche may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first NOK Trance consists of a series of Bonds at an amount of NOK 300,000,000 (Norwegian krone three hundred million). The first USD Tranche consists of a series of Bonds at an amount of which will be issued as a subsequent tranch.
The Bonds in the NOK Tranche has denominations of NOK 500,000 and the Bonds in the USD Tranche with denominations of USD 100,000.
All the Bonds shall rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "FRN Primorsk International Shipping Limited Unsecured Open Bond Issue 2007/2011".
