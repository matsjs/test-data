12.2 Calculation of Equity Ratio
Equity Ratio shall be calculated as follows:
(a) the calculation of the Equity Ratio for the Incurrence Test shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than one month prior to the event relevant for the application of the Incurrence Test; and
(b) Equity Ratio shall be measured on the relevant testing date so determined, but shall take into account the relevant Distribution.
