Creditors' process
Any expropriation, attachment, sequestration, distress or execution, or any analogous process in any jurisdiction, which affects any asset of a Group Company having an aggregate value of SEK 20,000,000 and is not discharged within thirty (30) calendar days.
Cross default and cross acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness of a Group Company is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), or any commitment for a Financial Indebtedness of any Group Company is cancelled or suspended by a creditor as a result of an event of default however described, provided that no Event of Default will occur under this paragraph if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness referred to herein is less than SEK 20,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency).
