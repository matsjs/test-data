No Direct Actions by Noteholders
23.1
A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer to enforce or
recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate,
support or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation
(Sw. företagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (Sw. konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other
jurisdiction) of the Issuer in relation to any of the liabilities of the Issuer under the
Finance Documents.
