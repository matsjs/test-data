12.1 Maintenance Covenants (a) The Issuer shall at all times ensure that: (i) the Equity Ratio exceeds 30 per cent; (ii) the minimum amount of Cash and Cash Equivalent in the Group exceeds SEK 20,000,000; and (iii) the Bond to Super Senior Debt ratio exceeds 20 per cent. (b) The Maintenance Test shall be tested on 30 June and 31 December each year. The first test date for the Maintenance Test shall be 31 December 2016.
12.2 Incurrence Test (a) The Incurrence Test is met if the Equity Ratio exceeds 35 per cent. (b) The calculation of the Equity Ratio under this Clause 12.2 shall be made as per a testing date being the last date of the period covered by the most recent Financial Reports.
13. General Undertakings
13.1 General The Issuer undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that each other Group Company will) comply with the undertakings set out in this Clause 12 for as long as any Bonds remain outstanding.
13.2 Distributions (a) The Issuer shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will: (i) pay any dividend on its shares (other than to a Group Company); (ii) make any contribution (other than contributions to Group Companies); (iii) repurchase any of its own shares; (iv) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders; (v) make any prepayments or interest payments under any long-term debt ranking junior (including Shareholder Loans) to the Bonds (other than in relation to loans between Group Companies); or
