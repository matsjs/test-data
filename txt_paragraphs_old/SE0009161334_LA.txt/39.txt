13.11 Valuations
Until the Bonds are listed on Nasdaq Stockholm and IFRS has been implemented as the applied accounting standards of the Issuer, the Issuer shall:
(a) in connection with the publishing of the second and fourth Financial Report each year provide a Valuation of all Properties;
(b) if the Agent has good grounds to believe that a Maintenance Test may be breached, at the cost of the Issuer, obtain a Valuation from the Valuer in respect of all or any part of the Properties and promptly provide that Valuation to the Agent (provided that the Agent is not obliged to request such Valuation);
(c) promptly provide all necessary assistance to the Agent and the Valuer where the Agent wishes to obtain a new Valuation in respect of all or any of the Properties; and
(d) supply to the Agent a copy of any Valuation, promptly upon obtaining it.
14. Events of Default and Acceleration of the Bonds
Each of the events or circumstances set out in this Clause 14 (other than Clause 14.12 (Acceleration of the Bonds)) is an event of default.
