3 Listing
3.1 The Loan will initially not be listed on an Exchange. An application may be made for listing on Oslo Børs ASA's main bond market or Alternative Bond Market (ABM) at the discretion of the Borrower.
4 Registration in a Securities Depository
4.1 The Loan must prior to the Disbursement Date be registered in a Securities Depository in accordance with the Norwegian Securities Depository Act 2002 and the terms and conditions of the Securities Depository.
4.2 The Borrower shall promptly arrange for notification to the Securities Depository of any changes in the terms and conditions of the Loan. The Loan Trustee shall receive a copy of the notification.
4.3 The Borrower is responsible for correct registration of the Loan in the Securities Depository. The registration may be executed by an agent on behalf of the Borrower, provided that such agent is qualified in accordance with relevant regulation.
