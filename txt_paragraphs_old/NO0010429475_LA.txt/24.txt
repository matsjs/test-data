Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
This clause 10.2 shall be excluded from this Loan Agreement and shall not apply from and including the 16th Banking Day after the occurrence of a Change of Control Event. This means that if the Borrower decides to exercise Call Option after a Change of Control Event, the Borrower must inform the Loan Trustee about such decision no later than 15 Banking Days after the Change of Control Event has occurred.
