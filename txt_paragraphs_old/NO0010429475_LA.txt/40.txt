18 Repeated Bondholders' meeting
18.1 In the event that Bondholders holding less than 50% of the Outstanding Loan are represented, a valid decision may not be made at the first Bondholders' meeting at which the matter is discussed. After a new meeting has been called and the matter discussed again, a valid decision may be made pursuant to the voting rules set forth above; this also applies to cases in which less than 50% of the Outstanding Loan are represented.
19 Change of Loan Trustee
19.1 In the event that the Borrower or Bondholders in accordance with sub-clause 17.1 b) wish to replace the Loan Trustee, or a change of Loan Trustee is necessary according to law, regulation or ordinance, or the Loan Trustee has requested such change, the Loan Trustee shall immediately call a Bondholders' meeting to discuss the matter.
The Loan Trustee shall put before the Bondholders' meeting a proposal for a new loan trustee. The Bondholders, the Borrower and the Exchange - if the Loan is listed - may submit proposals.
