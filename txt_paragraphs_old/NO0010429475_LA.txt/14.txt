2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 400,000,000 (Norwegian kroner four hundred million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 1 each and rank pari passu with any other subordinated debt of the Borrower, and shall rank ahead of all amounts payable in respect of the share capital of the Borrower.
The Loan will be described as "FRN Roxar AS Subordinated Callable PIK Bond Issue 2008/2013".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 042947.5.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds of the Loan will be used for the refinancing of existing Bank Debt of the Borrower.
