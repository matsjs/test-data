Part I Condition Precedent to Issue Date
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall not be transferred to the Escrow Account before the following condition precedents, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, have been fulfilled:
(a) this Bond Agreement, duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Issuer to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents;
(c) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Parent to enter into and execute the relevant Finance Documents;
(d) power of attorneys from the Issuer and the Parent to relevant individuals for their execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant register or similar documentation evidencing such individuals' authorisation to execute the Finance Documents on behalf of the Issuer and the Parent;
(e) certified copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for the Issuer and the Parent, evidencing that it is validly registered and existing and (ii) the Articles of Association of the Issuer and the Parent;
