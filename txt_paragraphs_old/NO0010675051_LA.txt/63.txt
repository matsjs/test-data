The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(e) Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless:
(i) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
(f) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not directly or indirectly enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
