10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue (all or nothing) (the "Call Option") at any time from
and including:
(a) the date falling 3 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 4 years after the Issue Date at a price equal to 104.00 % of par value plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount, or
(b) the date falling 4 years after the Issue Date to, but not included, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 102,00 % of par value plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
10.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
