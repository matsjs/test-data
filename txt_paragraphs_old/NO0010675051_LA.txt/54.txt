12.
TOTAL LOSS REDEMPTION
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than 180 days following the Total Loss Event redeem (a) 100.00 % of the outstanding Bonds at 100.00 % of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related to both Vessels or (b) 60.00 % of the outstanding bonds at 100.00 % of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related to one (1) Vessel.
13.
PAYMENTS
13.1 Covenant to pay
13.1.1
The Issuer will on any Payment Date (or any other due date pursuant to any Finance Document) unconditionally pay to or to the order of the Bond Trustee all amounts due under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document.
