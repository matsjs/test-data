INTERPRETATION
1.1 Definitions In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account, the Earnings Account, the Debt Service Account and any other account opened and maintained by the Issuer.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) in the Accounts (excluding the Escrow Account Pledge) and in any other account opened and maintained by the Issuer, which shall remain unblocked until an Event of Default occurs (save for the Debt Service Account which shall remain blocked at all times).
"Assignment of Earnings and Charterparties" means a first priority assignment in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) o f } of any charter earnings payable under the Charters and any Charter (provided that such assignment is permitted pursuant to the terms of the relevant Charter and applicable law (it being understood that the Issuer shall use reasonable efforts for any such Charter to allow for an assignment thereof in favour of the Bond Trustee)), and shall give notice and use its best endeavours to obtain consent and acknowledgement of such assignment from any charterer and the Bond Trustee shall be obliged to issue a quiet enjoyment letter as set out in Clause 8.2 (Status of the Bonds and Security) if so required.
"Assignment of Insurances" means a first priority assignment in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the insurances related to the Vessels.
"Assignment of Management Agreement" means a first priority assignment in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) of the rights of the Issuer under the Management Agreement (to the extent legally permissible), including, inter alia, direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights in case of default (to the extent legally permissible).
