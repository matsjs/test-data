The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
The Bond Issue is a tap issue, under which Subsequent Bond Issues may take place after the Issue Date up to the maximum amount described in Clause 2.2.1, running from the Issue Date and to be closed no later than 5 Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
All Subsequent Bond Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects, except that additional Bonds may be issued at a price below or above the Face Value of the Bonds. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for Subsequent Bond Issues. The Bond Trustee will in connection with Subsequent Bond Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Subsequent Bond Issue.
