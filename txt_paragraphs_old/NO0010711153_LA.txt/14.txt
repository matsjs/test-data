"Leverage Ratio" means on a consolidated basis the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA for the Group.
"Managers" means the joint lead managers for the Bond Issue, being Pareto Securities AB and Swedbank Norway, branch o f Swedbank AB (publ).
"Margin" means 5 per cent. (5%) per annum.
"Material Adverse Effect" means an event or circumstance which has a material adverse effect on: (a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Issuer and/or the Group (taken as a whole), (b) the Issuer's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under any of the Finance Documents; or (c) the validity or enforceability o f any of the Finance Documents.
"Material Subsidiary" means Infratek AS and any Group Company whose EBITDA or Total Assets exceeds 10% of the Group's consolidated EBITDA or Total Assets, as applicable, for EBITDA as measured in the preceding four quarterly periods, together with other Group Companies as determined by the Issuer, provided that such Group Companies shall always account for at least 80% in aggregate o f the total EBITDA or Total Assets o f the Group on a consolidated basis.
"Maturity Date" means 20 May 2019. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means Total Interest Bearing Debt less Free Cash.
"Net Interest Expenses" means, on a consolidated basis, the Group's cash interest expenses less interest income in accordance with IFRS (for the avoidance of doubt excluding any interest element related to pension liabilities, and any interest element related to a lease or hire purchase contract which would, in accordance with IFRS in force as o f the Issue Date, not be treated as Financial Indebtedness).
