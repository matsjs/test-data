2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount o f NOK 1,300,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one billion and three hundred million). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 650,000,000 (Norwegian kroner six hundred and fifty million) (the "Initial Bond Issue").
The Face Value is NOK 1. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Infratek Group AS Senior Secured Callable Open Bond Issue 2014/2019".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0711153.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
