"Business Transfer" means the contemplated transfer of the security business division o f the Group to the parent o f the Issuer or an affiliated entity for an approximate consideration of NOK 70 million (enterprise value).
"Change of Control Event" means if and when:
(a) any person or group (as such term is defined in the Norwegian Limited Liability Companies Act § 1-3), other than the Sponsor and/or any subsidiary thereof, becoming the owner(s), directly or indirectly, of more than 50% of the outstanding voting shares of the Issuer prior to an Equity Listing Event, or
(b) following an Equity Listing Event, (i) the Sponsor directly or indirectly ceases to own more than 20% o f the outstanding voting shares of the Issuer, or (ii) any person or group (as such term is defined in the Norwegian Limited Liability Companies Act § 1-3), other than the Sponsor and/or any subsidiary thereof, acquires control of the Issuer.
For the purpose of this definition, "control" means (a) acquiring or controlling, directly or indirectly, more than 1/3 of the voting shares of the Issuer, or (b) the right to, directly or indirectly, appoint or remove the whole or a majority of the directors o f the board o f directors o f the Issuer.
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
When determining the relevant person's number o f voting rights in the other person or the right to elect and remove members o f the board o f directors, rights held by the parent company o f the relevant person and the parent company's Subsidiaries shall be included.
