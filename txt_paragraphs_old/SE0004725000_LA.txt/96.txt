20. Prescription
(a) The right to receive payment of the Nominal Amount shall be prescribed and become void ten (10) years from the Redemption Date. The right to receive payment of interest shall be prescribed (Sw. preskriberad) and become void three (3) years from the relevant Interest Payment Date. The Issuer is entitled to any funds set aside for payments in respect of which the Bondholders right to receive payment has been prescribed and has become void.
