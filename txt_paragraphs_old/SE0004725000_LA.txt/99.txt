Nominee Registration
In respect of Bonds registered with authorised nominees in accordance with the Swedish Financial instruments Accounts Act {1998:1479), the authorised nominee shall be deemed to be the Bondholder for the purpose of applying these Terms and Conditions (subject to the provisions about the voting right of the Bondholders in Clause 15(m) {Bondholders' Meeting)).
Limitation of Liability
(a) The Issuer, the Bond Trustee, the Security Agent, the Issuing Agent and Euroclear Sweden (each a "Relevant Party") shall have no liability for damage caused by Swedish or foreign enactment, action taken by a Swedish or foreign authority, war, strike, blockade, boycott, lockout or other similar circumstance. This limitation of liability in the case of a strike, blockade, boycott or lockout also applies if any of the Relevant Parties would itself initiate or become subject to such conflict.
(b) The Bond Trustee shall have no liability for damage caused by the Bond Trustee when acting as a representative for the Bondholders, pursuant to the decisions made in accordance with Clause 15 (Bondholders' Meeting).
(c) A Relevant Party shall have no liability (i) for damage caused provided that the relevant person has acted with normal care, or (ii) for indirect damage.
