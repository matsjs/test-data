17.5 Change of Bond Trustee
(a) The Bond Trustee and the Security Agent may retire from its assignment according to these Terms and Conditions provided that the Issuer has approved that a successor Bond Trustee or Security Agent (as applicable) accedes as new Bond Trustee or Security Agent at the same time as the current Bond Trustee or Security Agent retires.
(b) If the Bond Trustee or the Security Agent is subject to bankruptcy or financial reconstruction according to law or regulations from a supervising authority or if a resolution is passed at a Bondholders' Meeting to replace the Bond Trustee or Security Agent, the Issuer shall immediately appoint a new Bond Trustee or Security Agent (as applicable) who immediately shall replace the current Bond Trustee or Security Agent as Bond Trustee or Security Agent (as applicable) in accordance with these Terms and Conditions.
(c) The Bond Trustee shall continue to carry out its duties as bond trustee until such time that a new Bond Trustee is appointed.
(d) The Security Agent shall continue to carry out its duties as security agent until such time that a new Security Agent is appointed.
(e) The fees and expenses of a new bond trustee shall be covered by the Issuer pursuant to the terms set out in Clause 10 {Fees and expenses), but may be recovered wholly or partially from the Bond Trustee or Security Agent (as applicable) if the change is due to a breach by the Bond Trustee or Security Agent of its duties pursuant to these Terms and Conditions or other circumstances for which the Bond Trustee or Security Agent is liable.
(f) The Bond Trustee and the Security Agent undertakes to co-operate so that the new bond trustee or security agent receives without undue defay the documentation and information necessary to perform the functions as set forth under these Terms and Conditions.
