12.16 Payments for Consent
The Issuer will not, and the Issuer will not permit any of its Subsidiaries to, directly or indirectly, pay or cause to be paid any consideration to or for the benefit of any Bondholder for or as an inducement to any consent, waiver or amendment of any of the terms or provisions of these Terms and Conditions or the Bonds unless such consideration is offered to be paid and is paid to all Bondholders that consent, waive or agree to amend in the time frame set forth in the solicitation documents relating to such consent, waiver or agreement.
12.17 Permitted Activities and Amendment of Transaction Security Documents
(a) The Issuer shall not engage in any business activity other than, in the case of the Issuer, acting as a direct holding company of its Subsidiaries, including activities being undertaken on the Issue Date that are ancillary to such role. The Issuer shall not Incur any material liabilities not directly related to such activities other than the Incurrence of Indebtedness in accordance with t these Terms and Conditions.
(b) The Issuer will not, and will not permit any Guarantor to, amend any of the Finance Documents in a manner adverse to the interests of the Bondholders.
12.18 Advances to Subsidiaries
All advances to Restricted Subsidiaries made by the Issuer after the issue Date will be evidenced by intercompany notes or loan agreements in favour of the Issuer.
