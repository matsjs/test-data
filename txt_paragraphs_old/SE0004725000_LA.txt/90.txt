(a) The Bond Trustee shall monitor the compliance by the Issuer of its obligations under these Terms and Conditions and applicable laws and regulations which are relevant to the provisions in these Terms and Conditions, including supervision of timely and correct payment of principal or interest, (however, this shall not restrict the Bond Trustee from discussing matters of confidentiality with the Issuer), arrange Bondholders' Meetings, and make the decisions and implement the measures resolved pursuant to these Terms and Conditions.
