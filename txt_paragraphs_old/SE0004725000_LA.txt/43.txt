3. Status of the Bonds and Security
(a) The Bonds constitute unsubordinated, direct, unconditional and secured obligations of the Issuer and shall at all time rank at least pari passu with all other senior obligations of the issuer, other than the obligations which are mandatory preferred by law. The Bonds shall rank without any preference among them.
(b) The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest, fees and expenses, shall be secured with the Security created or expressed to be created under the Transaction Security.
4. Bonds in Book-entry Form
(a) The Bonds will be issued in electronic book-entry form and be registered in a debt register (Sw. skuldbok) kept by Eurociear Sweden. No physical notes will be issued. Accordingly, the Bonds will be registered in accordance with the Swedish Financial Instruments Accounts Act (1998:1479).
(b) Bondholders' registration requests relating to the Bonds shall be directed to an Account Operator.
(c) Those who, according to an assignment, pledge, the provisions of the Swedish Children and Parents Code (Sw. Fdr3ldrabalken), conditions of will or deed of gift or otherwise have acquired a right to receive payments in respect of a Bond shall be required to register their entitlement to receive such payment in accordance with the Swedish Financial Instruments Accounts Act (1998:1479).
(d) The issuer shall be entitled to obtain information from the debt register (Sw. Skuldbok) kept by Eurociear Sweden in respect of the Bonds. At the request of the Issuing Agent or the Bond Trustee, the Issuer shall request and provide such information to the Issuing Agent and/or the Bond Trustee (as applicable).
