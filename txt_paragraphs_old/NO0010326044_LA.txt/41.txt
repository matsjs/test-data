18 Repeated Bondholders' meeting
In the event that less than 2/20 of the Outstanding Loan are represented, a valid decision may not be made at the first Bondholders' meeting at which the matter is discussed. After a new meeting has been summoned and the matter discussed a second time, a valid decision may be made pursuant to the voting rules set forth above; this aIso applies to cases in which less than 2/10 of the Outstanding Loan are represented.
