(i) maintain existing security pledges on assets acquired by the Borrower or its subsidiaries, given that collateral is not established as part of the acquisition, and that the obligation secured by the collateral is not extended in any way either as part of the acquisition proceedings or later,
(ii) in connection with trading in securities and other financial instruments, provide the normal security for such transactions, and
(iii) provide normal collateral in connection with credit purchases of goods and services.
(c) cease to carry on its business, or
(d) sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its operations or change the nature of its business which might jeopardise the Borrower's ability to comply with its obligations under the Loan Agreement.
