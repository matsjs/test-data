15.3
The Agent may refrain from convening a Noteholders' Meeting or instigating a Written
Procedure if (i) the suggested decision must be approved by any person in addition to
the Noteholders and such person has informed the Agent that an approval will not be
given, or (ii) the suggested decision is not in accordance with applicable laws.
