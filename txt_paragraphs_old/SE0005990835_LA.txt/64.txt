Events of Default
Each of the events or circumstances set out in Clauses 13.1 to 13.9 is an Event of Default.
13.1
Non-Payment The Issuer does not pay on the due date any amount payable by it under the Finance Documents, unless the non-payment: (a) is caused by technical or administrative error; and (b) is remedied within three (3) Business Days from the due date.
