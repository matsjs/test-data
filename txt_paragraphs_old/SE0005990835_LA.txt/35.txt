Use of proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Notes, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Notes (the "Net Proceeds"), for (i) the financing of the Acquisition, (ii) the provision of one or more intragroup loans to Target for the purpose of enabling repayment of the Existing Debt, and (iii) general corporate purposes of the Group.
