"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means
(a) an event where any of the Security Vessels or Vessel Owners are sold or disposed of; or
(b) an event where a Security Vessel is subject to expropriation or act of piracy (to the extent not a Total Loss Event and in the case of an act of piracy, provided always that such act of piracy event shall have continued for a period of more than 180 calendar days).
