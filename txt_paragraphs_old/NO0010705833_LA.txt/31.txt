2.3
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bond Issue (net of legal costs, fees of the Manager and the Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall exclusively be employed to
(a) fund the Reserve Account by USD 10,000,000; and
(b) fund the Issuer's acquisition of the Original Vessels.
Any surplus funds following (i) and (ii), net of any further costs or expenses in relation to the Bond Issue and acquisitions, shall be transferred to the Reserve Account.
