13.8 Financial covenants (a) The Issuer shall maintain, at any time, on a consolidated basis, an Asset Cover Ratio of minimum 125%, and report its compliance with this covenant in each Compliance Certificate and, following an Event of Default or potential Event of Default, upon the request of the Bond Trustee.
(b) The Issuer shall ensure that the Parent maintains, at any time, on a consolidated basis, a Value Adjusted Equity Ratio of minimum 25%.
