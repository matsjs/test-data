"'Payment Date1' means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Pool Agreement" means the agreements (as amended from time to time) entered into between the agent and the pool company of the Pool and the respective Vessel Owner for the commercial operation of a Security Vessel.
"Pre-Settlement Security" means the Escrow Account Pledge, the Parent Guarantee and the Midco Guarantee.
"Project Documents" means the Management Agreement, the Pool Agreement, the Technical Management Agreement and any Charter Contract with a duration longer than 12 months.
"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 3D June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Redemption Amount" shall have the meaning ascribed to it in Clause 10.4.4 below.
"Reserve Account" means the reserve account in the name of the Issuer with the Account Bank (or an Acceptable Bank), pledged and blocked as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Reserve Account Pledge" means a pledge over the Issuer's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Reserve Account, where the bank has waived any set-off rights.
"Retention Account" means the retention account in the name of the Issuer with the Account Bank (or an Acceptable Bank), pledged and blocked as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
