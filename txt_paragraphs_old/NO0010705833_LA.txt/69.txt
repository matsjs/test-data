Issuer's negative covenants The Issuer shall not without the approval of the Trustee or, where necessary, the Bondholders' Meeting:
(a) Declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other capital distributions to its shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party);
