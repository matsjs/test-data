Any Cash Deposit shall be released (at the Issuer's cost) upon:
(a) Receipt by the Bond Trustee of a written request from the party providing the Cash Deposit to release the Cash Deposit; and
(b) The Bond Trustee receiving evidence satisfactory to it that there will be no Event of Default or potential Event of Default upon its release.
