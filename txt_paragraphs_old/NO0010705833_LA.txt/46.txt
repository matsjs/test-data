9.
INTEREST
9.1
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue
Date at a fixed rate of 7.625 per cent. (7.62536) per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2
Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the
first Interest Payment Date falling in September 2014.
