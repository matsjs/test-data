13.2.13 Disposal The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company, sell or dispose of the shares of a Vessel Owner, except for any disposals carried out as a Permitted Disposal.
13.2.14 Ownership of bonds
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, acquire and own Bonds, save in respect of Bonds acquired following a Change of Control or Tender Offer, provided, however that such Bonds shall be cancelled.
