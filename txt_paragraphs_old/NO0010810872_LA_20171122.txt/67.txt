13.2.7
Negative pledge
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its/their assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Security.
13.2.8
Financial support
The Issuer shall not grant any financial support such as loans, guarantees or other financial assistance to any party other than (a) in connection with the Finance Documents and/or (b) to or on behalf of the Vessel Owners or a company organised to acquire an Additional Vessel.
