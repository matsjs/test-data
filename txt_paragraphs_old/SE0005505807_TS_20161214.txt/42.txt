Financial Indebtedness
(a) the Initial Notes and any Subsequent Notes;
(b) Subordinated Debt and other Financial Indebtedness that is subordinated to, or ranked pari passu with, the Notes and with a maturity date falling on or after the Final Maturity Date;
(c) Financial Indebtedness owed to another Group Company incurred for the sole purpose of servicing the Notes;
(d) any indebtedness resulting from a dividend, interest payment, group contribution (koncernbidrag) or other distribution permitted pursuant to Clause 11.10.1 to 11.10.4; and
(e) any Financial Indebtedness not permitted by paragraphs (a) to (d) above, provided that the aggregate amount of such indebtedness does not at any time exceed SEK 3,000,000.
