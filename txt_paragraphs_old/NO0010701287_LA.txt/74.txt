10.2 Call Option
(a)
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or. in parts ("Call Option") at any time
from and including the first Interest Payment Date in January 2016 to, but not
including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 103.00% of par value (plus accrued
interest on red'eemed amount).
