15. FEES AND EXPENSES
15.2 Fee Agreement The fees, costs and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent) shall be paid by the Issuer and are set out in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent).
15.3 Payment deficiency
Fees, costs and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee (or the Security Agent) which, due to the
Issuer's insolvency or similar circumstances, are not reimbursed in any other way may be
covered by making an equivalent reduction in the proceeds to the Bondholders hereunder of
any costs and expenses incurred by the Bond Trustee (or the Security Agent) in connection with
the restructuring or default of the Bond Issue and the enforcement of any Finance Document.
