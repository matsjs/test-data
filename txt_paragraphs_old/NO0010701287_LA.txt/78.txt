(c)
The Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the
request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of
the redemption request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall be the fifteen
days after the end of the two month exercise period of the Put Option.
