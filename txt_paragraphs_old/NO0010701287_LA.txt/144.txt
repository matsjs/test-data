(a)
At the Bondholders' Meeting each Bondholder may cast one vote for each Voting Bond
owned at close of business on the day prior to the date of the Bondholders' Meeting in
accordance with the records registered in the Securities Depository. The Bond Trustee
may, at its sole discretion, accept other evidence of ownership. Whoever opens the
Bondholders' Meeting shall adjudicate any question concerning which Bonds shall
count as the Issuer's Bonds. The Issuer's Bonds shall not have any voting rights.
