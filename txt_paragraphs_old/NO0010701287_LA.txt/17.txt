"GAAP" means the generally accepted-accounting practice and principles in the country in which the Issuer is incorporated including, if applicable, the International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and guidelines and interpretations issued by the International Accounting Standards Board (or any predecessor and successor thereof), in force from time to time.
"Guarantee" means an unconditional on-demand guarantee on a joint and several basis from the Guarantors securing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Guarantors" means the Original Guarantors and any Additional Guarantors.
"Guarantor Share Pledge" means a first priority share pledge over all shares in the Guarantors (including for the avoidance of doubt, the Target).
"Hedging Counterparty" means one or more hedging counterparties providing Permitted Hedging.
"Intercompany Loan Pledge" means a first priority pledge over all claims (now and hereinafter existing) under the Acquisition Loan and any other intercompany loans between the Obligors.
"Intercreditor Agreement" means an agreement between the Issuer, the Guarantors (and any other relevant member of the Group having entered into any intra group loans), the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), the Bond Trustee as security agent for any Security over assets comprised by the Pre-Settlement Security (save for paragraph (f) therein), the RCF Lender as security agent for any Security over assets comprised by the RCF Security (save for paragraph (c) therein) and any Hedging Counterparty, in relation to inter alia ranking of Security and redistribution of the proceeds from any enforcement of Security.
"Interest Coverage Ratio" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 14.1.
"Interest Payment Date" means 31 January, 30 April, 31 July and 31 October each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
