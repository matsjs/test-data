11. PAYMENTS
(a)
The Issuer will on any Payment Date (or any other due date pursuant to any Finance
Document) unconditionally pay to or to the order of the Bond Trustee all amounts due
under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document.
(b)
The covenant contained in Clause 11.1 (a) shall be for the benefit of the Bond Trustee
and the Bondholders.
