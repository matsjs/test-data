1. INTERPRETATION 1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over relevant balances on the Issuer's and the Guarantors' bank accounts (excluding the Interest Retention Accounts and the Escrow Account) (subject to any netting or set-off arrangement under any Permitted Hedging or cash pooling arrangement). The Account Pledge shall not involve a blocking of such accounts unless an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Acquisition Loan" means each of (i) the intra group loan from the Issuer to StormGeo AS, and (ii) the intra group loan from StormGeo AS to StormGeo Inc. respectively, each in the amount of up to USD 50,000,000 (or the equivalent thereof in.any other currency) made for completion of the acquisition of the Target.
"Additional Guarantors" means any company within the Group, which after the date hereof is or becomes (alternatively is designated by the Issuer) a Material Subsidiary.
