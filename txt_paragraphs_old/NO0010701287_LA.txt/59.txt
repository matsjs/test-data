6.5 Waivers
The Bond Trustee may, in its reasonable opinion, waive the deadline or requirements for documentation as set out in Clause 6.1 and Clause 6.4.
6.6 Conditions Subsequent
The Issuer shall procure that the Target and (if applicable) its Subsidiaries shall as soon as possible and in no event later than sixty days after the closing of the acquisition of the Target (clean up period), (i) provide the relevant Guarantees and Security over their assets oh the same terms as other Guarantors and on terms and conditions acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and (ii) documentation acceptable to the Bond Trustee evidencing discharge of any existing Security and Financial Indebtedness (if any, and to the extent not permitted by the terms hereof) over any of the assets of the Target and its Subsidiaries (directly and/or indirectly owned).
