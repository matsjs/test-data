"Equity Ratio" means at the date of calculation (on a consolidated basis of the Group) the ratio of Book Equity to Total Assets.
"Leverage Ratio" means the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means, at the date of calculation (on a consolidated basis for the Group), the aggregate interest bearing financial indebtedness (excluding any Permitted Hedging and any shareholder loans that is fully subordinated and any interest bearing debt borrowed from any Group Company) less Cash.
