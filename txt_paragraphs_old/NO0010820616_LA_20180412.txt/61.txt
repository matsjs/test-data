13.12
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Guarantor will, incur or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.13
Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Guarantor will, create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets or enter into arrangement having a similar effect, except for Permitted Encumbrances.
13.14
Mergers - Issuer
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization, involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any other Group Company with any other company or entity not being a Group Company if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
