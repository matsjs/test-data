13.3 Nature of Business
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by the Group as of the First Issue Date if such change is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.4 Dealings with Related Parties
(a) The Issuer shall, and shall procure that its Subsidiaries, conduct all dealings with the direct and indirect shareholders of the Group Companies (excluding other Group Companies (other than U.S. Group Companies)) and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders at arm's length terms.
(b) No value transfers may be made from any Swedish Group Company to any U.S. Group Company.
