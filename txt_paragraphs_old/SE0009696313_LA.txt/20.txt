3. Use of Proceeds
The Net Proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds (the Initial Bonds and all Subsequent Bonds) shall be applied towards (i) financing general corporate purposes of the Group (including acquisitions of properties in Sweden and investments in the Properties and refinancing of any external debt) and (ii) financing Transactions Costs.
