"Business Day Convention" means the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day.
"Call Option" means the Issuer's right to redeem outstanding Bonds in full in accordance with Clause 10.3 (Voluntary Total Redemption).
"Call Option Amount" means:
(a) 103.00 per cent. of the Total Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid interest, if the Call Option is exercised on the date falling 36 months after the First Issue Date to, but not including, a date falling 42 months after the First Issue Date;
(b) 101.50 per cent. of the Total Nominal Amount, together with accrued but unpaid interest, if the Call Option is exercised on or after the date falling 42 months after the First Issue Date, to, but excluding, the Final maturity Date; and
(c) Notwithstanding paragraph (b) above, provided that the redemption is financed by way of one or several Market Loans issues, at any time from and including the date falling 42 months after the First Issue Date to, but excluding, the Final Maturity Date, at an amount equal to 100.00 per cent. of the Total Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid interest.
