2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 800,000,000 (Norwegian krone eight hundred million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Loan will be described as "FRN Oceanteam Power & Umbilical ASA Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 037324.4.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds of the Loan shall be used to finance (i) the fully redemption of the Borrower's existing NOK 370 million bond issue (with ISIN NO 001 033320.6, the "Existing Bond") including accrued interest and call premium and (ii) general corporate purposes of the Borrower related to existing business.
