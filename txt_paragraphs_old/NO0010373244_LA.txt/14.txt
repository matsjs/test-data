Market Adjusted Equity Ratio:
the ratio of Market Adjusted Equity to Market Adjusted Total Assets, and shall be calculated every Interest Payment Date in June and December each year.
Market Adjusted Liabilities: the aggregate book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's total liabilities in accordance with the Borrower's applicable accounting standards plus the discounted value of any financial leasing contracts to the extent such contracts are not recorded as liabilities in the consolidated balance sheet of the Group. If Vessels are partially owned and/or corresponding liabilities can be allocated to a company only partially owned by the Borrower, the Market Adjusted Liabilities shall be included with a fraction equal to the Borrower's ownership share, counted by issued shares, always subject to such partly owned company being member of the Group.
