11.2 Second Ranking Transaction Security
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to procure that all amounts outstanding under the Finance Documents, including but not limited to the Bonds, plus accrued interests and expenses shall be secured with second ranking security over the two New Vessels and all rights relating thereto, including but not limited to all security over which the financiers under the Vessel Financing obtains a first ranking security, but in any case including rights under the relevant construction contracts, construction insurance, mortgage security and insurance.
