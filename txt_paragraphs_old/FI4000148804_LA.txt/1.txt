1. Definitions and Construction
1.1 Definitions
In these terms and conditions (the "Terms and Conditions"):
"Accounting Principles" means generally accepted accounting principles in Finland, including International Financial Reporting Standard (IFRS).
"Adjusted Net Profit" means the net profit of the Guarantor in accordance with the Accounting Principles, excluding any realized gains or losses related to sale or other disposals of assets.
