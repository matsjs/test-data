15.2 Other Obligations
The Obligors do not comply with any provision under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out in Clause 15.1 [Non-Payment), provided that the Bonds Agent has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within fifteen (15) Business Days from such request (if the failure or violation is not capable of being remedied, the Bonds Agent may declare the Bonds payable without such prior written request).
15.3 Cross-Acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness of a Group Company is not paid when due as extended by any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this Clause 15.3 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness is less than SEK 1,000,000 and provided that it does not apply to any Financial Indebtedness owed to a Group Company.
