13. Financial Undertakings
13.1 Incurrence Covenants
The Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time:
(a) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA for the Relevant Period ending on the relevant testing date does not exceed 3.50; and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio for the Relevant Period ending on the last day of the period covered by the most recent Financial Report exceeds 3.00,
calculated in accordance with the calculation principles set out in Clause 13.2 (Calculation Adjustments).
