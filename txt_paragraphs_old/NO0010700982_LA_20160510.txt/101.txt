(g) Insolvency proceedings and dissolution

(i) the suspension of payments, a moratorium of any indebtedness, winding-up, dissolution, administration or reorganisation (by way of voluntary arrangement, provisional liquidation, judicial management, scheme of arrangement or otherwise) other than solvent liquidation or reorganisation;
