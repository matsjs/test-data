14.4.11 Area of operations
The Guarantor shall ensure that neither it nor any Rig shall operate in contradiction of any Sanctions and shall procure further that the Rigs shall not operate in Cuba, Iran, Libya, Venezuela, North Korea, Sudan, Syria.
