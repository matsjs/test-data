6.3
Enforcement of Transaction Security and Guarantees
(a) If the Bonds are declared due and payable according to Clause 15 (Termination o f the Bonds), or following the Final Redemption Date, the Trustee is, without first having to obtain the Holders' consent, entitled to enforce the Transaction Security and the Guarantees in such manner and under such conditions that the Trustee finds acceptable (if in accordance with the Security Documents, the Guarantee Agreement and the Intercreditor Agreement (if any)).
