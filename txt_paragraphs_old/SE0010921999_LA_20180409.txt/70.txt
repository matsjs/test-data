14.6 Clean down period
The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year there shall be a period of three (3) consecutive days during which the amount outstanding under the Working Capital Facility, less Cash and Cash Equivalents, equals zero (0) or less. Not less than six (6) months shall elapse between two such periods.
14.7 Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none ofthe Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
