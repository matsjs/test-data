12.8 Mandatory redemption due to failure to fulfil the Conditions Precedent or the Conditions Subsequent
12.8.1
If the Conditions Precedent or the Conditions Subsequent have not been fulfilled on or before sixty (60) Business Days following the First Issue Date, the Issuer shall redeem all Bonds at a price equal to one hundred (100.00) per cent, of the Nominal Amount together with any accrued but unpaid interest.
12.8.2
The redemption of the Bonds shall (i) be executed on the first Interest Payment Date (taking into account the rules and regulations of the CSD) and (ii) be made by the Issuer giving not less than ten (10) Business Days' notice to the Holders and the Trustee, where such notice shall state the relevant Interest Payment Date on which the redemption shall be made, the redemption amount and the relevant Record Date.
