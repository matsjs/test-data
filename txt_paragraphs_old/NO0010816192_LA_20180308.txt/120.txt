PRESCRIPTION
26.1
The right to receive repayment of the principal of the Notes shall be prescribed and become
void ten (10) years from the Redemption Date. The right to receive payment of interest
(excluding any capitalised interest) shall be prescribed and become void three (3) years
from the relevant due date for payment. The Issuer is entitled to any funds set aside for
payments in respect of which the Noteholders' right to receive payment has been
prescribed and has become void.
