"Securities Account" means the account for dematerialised securities maintained by the CSD pursuant to the Financial Instruments Accounts Act in which (i) an owner o f such security is directly registered or (ii) an owner's holding of securities is registered in the name o f a nominee.
"Securities Market Act" means the Swedish Securities Markets Act (lag (2007:582) om vardepappersmarknaden).
