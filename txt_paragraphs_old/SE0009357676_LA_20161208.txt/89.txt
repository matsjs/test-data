15.2
Mergers
The Parent shall:
(a) not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Parent with any other companies or entities if such transaction would (i) have a Material Adverse Effect or (ii) involve an Issuer Group Company; and
(b) ensure that no Issuer Group Company shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of any Issuer Group Company.
