9.
INTEREST
7.1
Each Bond carries Interest at the Interest Rate calculated on the Nominal Amount from
(but excluding) the Settlement Date up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date.
Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Bonds shall
be made to the Bondholders on each Interest Payment Date for the preceding Interest
Period.
