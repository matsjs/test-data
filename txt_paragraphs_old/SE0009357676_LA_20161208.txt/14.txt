"Make Whole Amount" means an amount equal to the sum of:
(a) the present value on the relevant Redemption Date of 101.50 per cent. of the Nominal Amount of the redeemed Bonds as if such payment originally had taken place on the First Call Date;
(b) the present value on the relevant Redemption Date of the remaining interest payments of the redeemed Bonds until the First Call Date (excluding accrued but unpaid Interest up to the relevant Redemption Date); and
(c) any accrued but unpaid Interest,
assuming for sub-paragraph (b) that the Interest Rate for the period from the relevant Redemption Date to the First Call Date will be equal to the Applicable Mid Swap Rate; and
both sub-paragraphs (a) and (b) shall be discounted using a discount rate equal to the yield to maturity on the Business Day immediately preceding the date on which the applicable notice of redemption is given of the direct obligations of Sweden (statsobligationer) with a maturity date on or about the First Call Date plus 0.50 per cent.
