14 12 Financial support
I 112 Management fee
The Issuer shall not (and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will) make or agree to make any payment of general and administrative expenses to the Parent or any other Issuer Group Company or any of their affiliates other than Permitted Management Fee Payments.
