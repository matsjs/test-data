De-mergers
The Issuer shall not (and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will) carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Issuer or any Issuer Group Company into two or more separate companies or entities.
Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not (and shall ensure that no Issuer Group Company) cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Issuer Group from that carried on at the Settlement Date, and/or as set out in the Finance Documents.
