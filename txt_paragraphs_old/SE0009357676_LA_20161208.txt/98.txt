Refurbishment and Refurbishment Plan The Issuer shall procure that the Refurbishment Proceeds are applied towards Refurbishment of the Properties in accordance with the Refurbishment Plan.
The Issuer shall hold all unspent Refurbishment Proceeds on a separate account in its own name until all or part of such proceeds are lent to a Property Company for the purpose of being applied towards Refurbishment o f the Properties in accordance with the Refurbishment Plan.
If the Initial Refurbishment Plan contains any material fault, material omission or becomes obsolete, the Issuer shall without undue delay notify the Bond Trustee thereofand provide the Bond Trustee with the Issuer's suggestion for a Revised Refurbishment Plan for the Bond Trustee's approval. Following the Bond Trustee's and the Property Advisor's approval of such Revised Refurbishment Plan, it shall become the Refurbishment Plan.
