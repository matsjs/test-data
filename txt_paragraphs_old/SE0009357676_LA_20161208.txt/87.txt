The Issuer shall from the Settlement Date keep the Bonds affiliated with a CSD and comply with all CSD Regulations applicable to the Issuer and the Bonds.
14.21.1
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that the Bonds are admitted to trading on an Exchange within thirty (30) calendar days following the Settlement Date, and that it remains admitted or, if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on another Exchange.
14.21.2
Following an admission to trading, the Issuer shall take all actions on its part to maintain the admission as long as any Bonds are outstanding, but not longer than up to and including the last day on which the admission reasonably can, pursuant to the then applicable regulations of the Exchange and the CSD, subsist.

The Issuer shall procure that the Parent cancel the Missing Share Certificates in accordance with Clause 15.11 (Missing Share Certificates undertaking).
