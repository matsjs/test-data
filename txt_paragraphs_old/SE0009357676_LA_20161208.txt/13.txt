"Lessee" means each of the lessees under the Lease Agreements (including the Hotel Lessees).
"Listing Failure Event" means that:
(a) the Bonds have not been admitted to listing on an Exchange within sixty (60) calendar days following the Settlement Date; or
(b) in the case of a successful admission to listing, that a period of sixty (60) calendar days has elapsed since the Bonds ceased to be admitted to listing on an Exchange.
"Loss Event" means that:
(a) there is a total or material loss of all of the Properties;
(b) any of the Properties (or a part of any Property) is damaged to the extent that hotel operations at the Property will not be feasible for a period exceeding six (6) months; or
(c) damages (and related repair costs) to any of the Properties exceed 30.00 per cent. of the Properties Market Value as set out in the Initial Valuation Report.
