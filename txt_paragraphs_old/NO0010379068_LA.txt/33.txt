Status of the Loan and security
8.1 The Loan shall rank pari passu with all other senior obligations o f the Borrower other than obligations which are mandatory preferred by law. The Loan shall rank ahead o f subordinated capital.
8.2 The Loan including interest and expenses shall be secured by the following which shall constitute the security for the Borrower's obligations under this Loan Agreement:
(i) the Escrow Account Pledge; and
(ii) a first priority pledge over all the Borrower's shares and convertible bonds issued by Altinex ASA by registration in the Pledged VPS Account as provided for in this Loan Agreement. Such security will be cancelled if and when a Merger takes place. The surviving entity will be subject to all obligations o f the Borrower under the Loan Agreement, and may discharge the Borrower's holdings of convertible bonds issued by Altinex ASA.
