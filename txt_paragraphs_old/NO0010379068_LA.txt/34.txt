Interest
9.1 The Fixed Tranche bears a fixed interest during the term of the Loan.
9.1.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Fixed Tranche from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 11.0 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made in semi annual arrears on 13 January and 13 July each year, the first interest Payment Date being in January 2008.
9.1.2 Interest on the Fixed Trance shall be calculated on the basis o f a year o f 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless:
a) the last day o f the period is the 31st day of a month but the first day o f the period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day o f a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or
b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month o f February, in which case the month o f February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
9.2 The FRN Tranche bears a floating interest during the term of the Loan.
