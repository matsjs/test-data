"Material Group Company" means the Issuer or a Subsidiary representing more than five (5.00) per cent, of either (i) the total assets of the Group on a consolidated basis (for the avoidance of doubt, excluding any intra-group transactions) or (ii) the EBITDA of the Group according to the latest consolidated Financial Report.

"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means the aggregate interest bearing debt (excluding any interest bearing debt borrowed from any Group Company), less the Free Cash of the Group according to the latest Financial Report, in accordance with the Accounting Principles, adjusted in accordance with the Operational Lease Freeze.
"Net Interest Expenses" means, in respect of the Relevant Period, the aggregate amount of accrued interest whether paid, payable or capitalised by any Group Company according to the latest Financial Report(s) (calculated on a consolidated basis), after deducting any interest payable for the Relevant Period to any Group Company and any interest income relating to cash or cash equivalent investments of the Group.
"Net Proceeds" means the proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue which, after deduction has been made for the transaction costs payable by the Issuer to the Issuing Agent and bookrunner for the services provided in relation to the placement and issuance of the Bonds.
"Nominal Amount" has the meaning set forth in Clause 2.1.
"Operational Lease Freeze" has the meaning set forth in item (b) of the definition "Financial Indebtedness" above.
