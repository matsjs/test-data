10. Transaction Security and Guarantee
(a) No later than the date of disbursement from the Proceeds Account in accordance with Clause 4.1(a), the Issuer shall:
(i) grant the Transaction Security in relation to the Security Documents referred to in paragraphs (a) and (b) of that definition; and
(ii) procure that each of the Guarantors grants the Guarantee.
(b) As continuing Security for the due and punctual fulfilment of the Secured Obligations, the Issuer shall grant or shall procure that the relevant Group Company will grant the Transaction Security referred to in paragraph (c) of the definition of Security Documents.
