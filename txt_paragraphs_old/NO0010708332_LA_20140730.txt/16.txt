"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees),
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement,
"Permitted Acquisition" means an acquisition by the Issuer or any of the Guarantors of assets constituting a business or a company or shares in a company (the "Acquired Company") but only if:
(a) no Event of Default is continuing on the closing date for the acquisition or will occur as a result of the acquisition;
(b) the Acquired Company is not registered or incorporated in a jurisdiction which is the subject of sanctions or measures imposed by the Department of Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC), the United Nations Security Council (UNSC) or the European Union (EU);
(c) the Acquired Company is engaged in the same line or similar line of business as the Issuer or any member of the Group;
(d) the Issuer is and immediately after the acquisition will be in compliance with the pro forma Financial Covenants (post the Permitted Acquistion) relating to Leverage Ratio and Interest Cover Ratio according to the principles described under "Calculation of Financial Covenants"; and
(e) the Acquired Company becomes a Guarantor to the extent legally permissible and practical.
