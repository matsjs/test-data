2.2
The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of SEK 650,000,000 (Swedish kroner 650,000,000). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of SEK 650,000,000 (Swedish kroner six hundred fifty million).
The Face Value is SEK 100,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
