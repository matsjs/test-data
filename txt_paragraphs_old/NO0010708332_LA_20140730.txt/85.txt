13.7
If any withholding tax is imposed due to subsequent changes in applicable law after the date of this Bond Agreement, the Issuer shall have the right to call all but not some of the Bonds at par value plus accrued interest. Such call shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty - 30 - Business Days prior to the settlement date of the call.
13.8
In the event the Obligors are required to withhold any withholding tax from any payment under any Finance Document and the Obligor as a result thereof increases the amount paid to ensure the Bondholders receive the amount they are required to receive in accordance with the terms of this Bond Agreement, the Bondholders and the Bond Trustee may assist the Obligor with exercising any right it may have to have such withholding tax repaid, refunded or in any other manner retransferred from the relevant tax authorities.
