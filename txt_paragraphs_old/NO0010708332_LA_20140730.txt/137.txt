GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION
(a) This Guarantee shall be governed by and construed in accordance with Norwegian law.
(b) The Guarantor hereby irrevocably submits to the jurisdiction of the Norwegian courts, the venue to be Oslo District Court.
(c) The submission to the jurisdiction of the Norwegian courts shall not limit the right of the Bond Trustee to take any legal action or proceedings against the Guarantor in any court which may otherwise exercise jurisdiction over the Guarantor or any of its assets.
