The Issuer shall procure that the Liquidity of the Group will not fall below USD 5 million at any time.
For the purpose of the Financial Covenants included herein, the following definitions shall apply:
"Leverage Ratio" means the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA.
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Finance Charges
"Liquidity" means the sum of any (i) amount available to the Group under any committed credit facility which has not been utilized or cancelled and (ii) Cash and Cash Equivalents.
"Cash and Cash Equivalents" means, at any time, (i) cash at hand or on deposit with a reputable bank; (ii) certificates of deposit or marketable debt securities maturing within one year after the relevant date of calculation issued by a reputable bank;(iii) certificates and bonds which have a debt rating of at least BBB- by Standard Et Poor's Rating Agencies or at least Baa3 by Moody's Investor Services) and (iiii) certificates and bonds approved by the Bond Trustee; in each case to which any member of the Group is beneficially entitled at that time and has free and unrestricted access and which is not subject to any Security. A reputable bank for this purpose is a commercial bank or trust company which has a short term debt rating of at least A1 by Standard fit Poor's Rating Agencies or at least P-1 by Moody's Investor Services.
"Finance Charges" means for any relevant period the aggregate amount of interest, fees and other finance payments in respect of (i) financial indebtedness payable by any member of the Group on a consolidated basis (excluding any upfront or other one off fees payable in connection with the issuance of the Bonds) and (ii) bank deposits, and all bonds and other securities held by the Group from time to time.
