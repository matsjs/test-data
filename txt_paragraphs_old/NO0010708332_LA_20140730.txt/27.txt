Tap Issues
The net proceeds of any subsequent Tap Issues (net of fees and legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses incurred by the Manager or Bond Trustee in connection with the Bond Issue or the Transaction) shall be used toward financing the future growth of the Group within its business strategy. The net proceeds from such subsequent Tap Issues shall be transferred to the Escrow Account and used for Permitted Acquisitions and Permitted Capital Expenditure.
