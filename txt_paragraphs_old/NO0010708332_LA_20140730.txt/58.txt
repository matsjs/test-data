9.2.2
Notwithstanding Clause 9.2.1 above, the Issuer may redeem Bonds of an amount of up to SEK 325,000,000 at any time from and including 15 June 2014 to, but not including the CutOff Date at a price of 102% of par value (plus accrued interest), provided that
(a) closing for a Permitted Acquisition has not occurred or a final purchase order for a Permitted Gapital Expenditure has not been executed (or is expected by the Issuer to close or be executed before the Cut-off Date); and
(b) an amount equal to the amount of the redeemed Bonds has not been disbursed from the Escrow Account.
