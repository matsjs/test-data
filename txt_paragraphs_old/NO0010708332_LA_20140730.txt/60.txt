9.3 9.3.1
Mandatory Redemption
If a Permitted Acquisition or Permitted Capital Expenditure has not completed (by using funds on the Escrow Account) within the Cut-off Date, 50% of the Bonds shall be redeemed (Mandatory Redemption) at a price of 102% of par plus accrued interest 10 Business Days following the Cut-Off Date. Funds on the Escrow Account shall be used in connection with such Mandatory Redemption.
9.3.2
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
9.3.3
Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
