"Net Finance Charges" means, for any relevant period, the Finance Charges according to the latest quarterly statement, after deducting any interest payable for that relevant period to any member of the Group and any interest income relating to Cash or Cash Equivalents (and excluding any payment-in-kind interest capitalized on Intra Group Loans).
"Interest Bearing Debt" means on a consolidated basis (i) the aggregate amount of all interest bearing obligations (including financial lease obligations which according to GAAP shall be treated as debt and obligations arising under earn out and deferred payments for acquisitions and excluding current and future lease obligations which as of the date hereof are treated as non-financial lease obligations).
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means Interest Bearing Debt less Cash and Cash Equivalents.
"EBITDA" means, on a consolidated basis of the Group, earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization.
(e) Calculation of Financial Covenants - general
The Leverage Ratio and the Interest Coverage Ratio shall be calculated on a rolling 12 months basis.
The Financial Covenants Will apply for the Group (on a consolidated basis) at any time and compliance shall be measured on each Quarter Date by reference to each annual financial statement and quarterly financial statement and certified by the Parent On the respective reporting date in a compliance certificate to be delivered to the Bond Trustee.
