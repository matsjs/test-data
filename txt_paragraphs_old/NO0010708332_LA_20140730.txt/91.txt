14.4
In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clause 14.1 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee may make direct payments to lease providers under any lease facilities to cover any overdue amount in order to prevent termination of such lease facility. Any such payment shall be made in consideration of the assignment by the lease provider of the corresponding claim against the lessee, and shall satisfy any overdue amount under such lease facility in full, without any discount.
