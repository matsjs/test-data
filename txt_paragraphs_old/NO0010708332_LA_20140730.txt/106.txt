Any payment, repayment, discharge or performance of any Finance Document Obligation shall, to that extent, satisfy the corresponding Parallel Debt and any payment, repayment, discharge or performance of a Parallel Debt to the Bond Trustee shall, to that extent, satisfy the corresponding Finance Document Obligation.
In the event of the appointment of a new Bond Trustee or a Security Agent other than the Bond Trustee, the Bond Trustee shall assign to such successor Bond Trustee or such Security Agent the obligations owed or to be owed to it pursuant to this paragraph 16.5. Any resignation by the Bond Trustee or any such appointment of a Security Agent other than the Bond Trustee is not effective with respect to its rights under the Parallel Debt until such rights are so assigned to the successor Bond Trustee or appointed Security Agent.
