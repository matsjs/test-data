Special covenants (a) Dividends and other distributions

in) Thereafter, the Issuer may declare or make dividend payments or other distributions, provided that such distributions are limited to 50% of the net income of the Group based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year and provided that the Liquidity of the Issuer, immediately after any such payment or transaction, exceeds USD 40 million.
