(c) Financial assistance
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting of Security) to any third party, other than loans, guarantees or other financial assistance granted in the ordinary course of business.
(d) Servians °f subordinated loans and shareholder loans
(i) Any existing and future subordinated loans other than shareholder loans to the Issuer, may be serviced as long as no Event of Default has occurred or is continuing.
(ii) Any existing and future shareholder loans to the Issuer may be serviced subject to paragraph (a) {Dividends and other distributions) of Clause 13.4 as long as no Event of Default has occurred or is continuing.
