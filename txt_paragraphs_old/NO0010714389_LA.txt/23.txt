Tap Issues
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects as set out in this Bond Agreement. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will in connection with the issuing of additional Bonds under any Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
3.
LISTING
3.1
The issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on Oslo Bers.
3.2
If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have
been discharged in full.
