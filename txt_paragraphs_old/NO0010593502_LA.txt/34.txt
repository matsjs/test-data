Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the Interest Accrual Date at a fixed rate of 13.15 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
However, the Fixed Rate is subject to possible reduction based on N et Interest Bearing Debt ("NIBB") to EBITDA ratio in the following manner. (i) f f NIBD/EBITDA is greater than 3.75, no reduction (ii) If NIBD/EBITDA is less than 3.75, but greater than 3.00 - reduction o f 2.0% p.a. (iii) If NIBD/EBITDA is less than 3.00 - a reduction o f 4.0% p.a.
Any such reduction of the Fixed Rate shall only be effective in the period from and including the first Interest Payment Date following the relevant actual reporting, which leads to a Fixed Rate reduction, and to the first Interest Payment date thereafter. For the avoidance o f doubt - any Fixed Rate reduction are only made with effect for one interest period (3 months) at a time.
9.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 19 February 2011.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one M erest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
