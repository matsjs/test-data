The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 100 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, provided that the First Priority Bond Loan have been, or on the same date as the settlement date o f the call of the Bond Issue are to be, redeemed in full,
(ii) at any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 102 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, if the First Priority Bond Loan have not been redeemed in full,
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to tire Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least twenty Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.
