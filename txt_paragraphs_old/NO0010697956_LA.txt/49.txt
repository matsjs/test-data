13.4 Special covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any Distribution (save for the Permitted Distribution) exceeding 50% of the Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes (net of minority interests) based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year. However, always provided that the Market Adjusted Equity Ratio is no less than 35% immediately following such Distribution, calculated on the basis of the Group's consolidated numbers as reported on the most recent Quarter Date and pro forma for such Distribution made or declared.
Any un-utilized portion of the permitted dividend pursuant to the above may not be carried forward to any subsequent financial year.
