(e) Insolvency
A Group Company is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall
due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason o f actual
or anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or
more of its creditors with a view to rescheduling any of its indebtedness.
(ii) The value of the assets o f any member of the Group is less than its liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities)
(iii) A moratorium is declared in respect of any indebtedness of any member of the Group
