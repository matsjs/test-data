"Change of Control Event'"means if and when any person or a group of persons acting in concert, directly or indirectly, other than Øgreid Eiendom AS or a related party, acquires Decisive Influence over the Issuer.
"Contractual Coverage" means for all Vessels within the Group the total number o f vessel-months on charter parties exceeding 30 days divided by the total number of vessel-months capacity (for the avoidance o f doubt, to be calculated excluding options). The Contractual Coverage ratio shall be calculated on a 12 months rolling basis (forward looking) on the relevant quarterly testing date. (For avoidance of doubt "exceeding 30 days" means that the contract have a duration o f more than 30 days from the calculation date).
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result o f an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a)
a majority o f the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority o f the members o f the board of directors of that other person.
