"Margin" means 8 percentage points per annum.
"Material Subsidiary" means:
(i) any Group Company (other than the Issuer) whose total consolidated assets represent at least 10 % of the total consolidated assets of the Group, or
(ii) any Group Company (other than the Issuer) whose total consolidated net sales represent at least 10 % of the total consolidated net sales of the Group, or
(iii) any other Group Company (other than the Issuer) to which is transferred either (A) all or substantially all of the assets of another Group Company which immediately prior to the transfer was a Material Subsidiary or (B) sufficient assets of the Issuer that such Group Company would have been Material Subsidiary had the transfer occurred on or before the relevant date,
(iv) each Guarantor, or (v) any other Group Company (other than the Issuer) designated as such by the
Issuer,
always provided that Group Companies not being a Material Subsidiary shall in aggregate not exceed 20 % of the consolidated turnover, gross assets or nets assets of the Group (as the case may be).
