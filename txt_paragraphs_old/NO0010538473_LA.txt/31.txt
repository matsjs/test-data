10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of theBond Issue in multiples of NOIC 25 million or
the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) any time from and included Interest Payment Date in September 2010 to, but not included, Interest Payment Date in September 2011 at 105 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
(ii) any time from and included Interest Payment Date in September 2011 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 103 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
