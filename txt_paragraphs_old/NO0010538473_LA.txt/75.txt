4. Swedish law - Parallel Debt
(a) The Issuer hereby irrevocably and unconditionally undertakes to pay to the Security Agent amounts equal to any amounts owing from time to time by the Issuer to any Bondholder under any Finance Document as and when those amounts are due.
(b) The Issuer and the Security Agent acknowledge that the obligations the Issuer under paragraph (a) above are separate and independent from, and shall not in any way limit or affect, the corresponding obligations of the Issuer to any Bondholder under any Finance Document (its "Corresponding Debt") nor shall the amounts for which the Issuer is liable under paragraph (a) above (its "Parallel Debt") be limited or affected in any way by its Corresponding Debt provided that:
(i) the Parallel Debt of the Issuer shall be decreased to the extent that its Corresponding Debt has been irrevocably paid or (in the case of guarantee obligations) discharged; and
