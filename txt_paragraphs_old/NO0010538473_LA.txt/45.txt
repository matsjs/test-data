Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall not within a calendar year declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares, any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity (but always distributions of bonus shares not affecting its equity) that constitute more than 50 per cent of (on a consolidated basis) the Issuer's net profit after taxes based on the accounts for the previous financial year for the Group on a consolidated basis. Any un-utili/ed portion of the permitted distribution may not be carried forward to any subsequent calendar year. For the purpose of this clause, net profit shall be adjusted for any positive effect resulting from sale of assets.
Gearing Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Gearing Ratio of maximum 3.50 on every Calculation Date. The Gearing Ratio as per each Calculation Date shall be reported on a consolidated basis for the Group within each Reporting Date. If the Issuer is in breach with the Gearing Ratio covenant, the Issuer will have the next 45 days after such breach being reported to restore the Gearing Ratio.
