BONDHOLDERS' MEETING
16.1 16.1. 1
Authority of the Bondholders' meeting
The Bondholders' Meeting represents the supreme authority o f the Bondholders community ill all matters relating to the Bonds. If a resolution by or ail approval of the Bondholders is required, resolution of such shall be passed at a Bondholders' Meeting. Resolutions passed at Bondholders' Meetings shall be binding upon and prevail for all the Bonds.
16.2 Procedural rules for Bondholders' meetings
16.2.1 A Bondholders' Meeting shall be held at the request of:
(i) tlie Issuer, (ii) Bondholders representing at least 1/10 of Voting Bonds, (iii) tlie Exchange, if the Bonds arc listed, or (iv) the Bond Trustee.
