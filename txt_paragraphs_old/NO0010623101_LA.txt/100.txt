17.3.3
Change of Rond Trustee
Change of Rond Trustee shall be carried out pursuant to the procedures set forth in Clause 16. The Rond Trustee shall continue to carry out its duties as bond trustee until such time that a new Rond Trustee is elected.
The fees and expenses of a new bond trustee shall be covered by the Issuer pursuant to the terms set out in Clause 14. but may be recovered wholly or partially from the Bond Trustee if tire change is due to a breach of tire Rond Trustee duties pursuant to the terms of this Rond Agreement or other circumstances for which the Bond Trustee is liable.
The Bond Trustee undertakes to co-operate so that the new bond trustee receives without undue delay following the Bondholders' Meeting the documentation and information necessary to perform the functions as set forth under the tarns o f this Rond Agreement.
