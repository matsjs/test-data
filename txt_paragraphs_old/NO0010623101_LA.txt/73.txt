(c) Ownership to Subsidiaries
The Issuer shall not sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any o f the Subsidiaries, and shall cause each Subsidiary not to issue or sell any new shares, treasury shares or other ownership interest, to any third party.
(d) Subsidiaries' distributions
The Issuer shall not permit any Subsidiaiy to create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or Encumbrance) restricting the light of any Subsidiary to (i) pity dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders, (ii) pay any Financial Indebtedness to the Issuer, make any loans to the Issuer or (iii) transfer any o f its assets and properties to the Issuer, except if provided in this Bond Agreement.
