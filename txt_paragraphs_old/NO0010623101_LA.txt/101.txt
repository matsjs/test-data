Appointment of Security Agent
The Rond Trustee may act as Security Agent or may appoint a bank or other institution to act as Security Agent for tire Rond Issue.
The main functions o f the Security Agent may include holding Security Interests on behalf of itself and the Bondholders and monitoring compliance by the Issuer and other relevant parlies of their respective obligations under this Rond Agreement and/or the Security Documents with respect to the Security Interests.
Before the appointment of a Security Agent other than the Rond Trustee, tire Issuer shall be given the opportunity to state its views on tlie proposed Security Agent, but tl*e final decision as to appointment shall lie exclusively with the Bond Trustee.
The functions, rights and obligations of the Security Agent may be determined by a Security Agent agreement to be entered into between the Bond Trustee and the Security Agent, which the Bond trustee shall have lire right to require any Obligor and any other parties to any Security Document to sign as a party, or, at the discretion of the Bond Trustee, to acknowledge.
Any changes to this Rond Agreement necessary or appropriate in connection with the appointment of u Security Agent shall be documented in an amendment to this Rond Agreement, signed by the Bond Trustee.
If so desired by the Bond Trustee and the Security Agent, any or all of the Security Documents shall be amended, assigned or re-issued, so that the Security Agent is the holder of the relevant Security Interest (on behalf o f itself and the Bondholders). The costs incurred in connection with such amendment, assignment or re-issue shall he for the account of the Issuer.
