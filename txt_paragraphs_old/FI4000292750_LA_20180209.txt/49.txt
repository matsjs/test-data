12. Financial Undertakings
12.1 Maintenance Covenants
(a) Interest Coverage Ratio: The Issuer shall ensure that the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 1.10:1 at all times.
(b) Asset Cover Ratio: The Issuer shall ensure that the Asset Cover Ratio exceeds 130 per cent. at all times.
(c) Minimum Cash: The Issuer shall ensure that the Cash and Cash Equivalents in the Issuer shall not, at any time, be less than the amount of interest to be paid on the next Interest Payment Date. For the purpose of this test, interest shall be calculated by using EURIBOR on the relevant Reference Date.
(d) Lease Payment Coverage: The Issuer shall ensure that the ratio of Operator EBITDA to Lease Payments (for the relevant Reference Period) shall at all times exceed 1.00:1.
12.2 Testing of the Maintenance Test
The Maintenance Test shall be tested on each Reference Date with respect to the Reference Period ending on such Reference Date and calculated based on the most recently delivered Valuation. The first test date shall be 30 June 2018.
