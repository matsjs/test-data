13.2 Distributions
None of the Issuer or the Operator shall, and each of them shall procure that no Group Company will,
(a) pay any dividend on its shares (other than by an Issuer Group Company to another Issuer Group Company or by an Operator Group Company to another Operator Group Company);

(d) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders;
(e) repay any Shareholder Loans;
