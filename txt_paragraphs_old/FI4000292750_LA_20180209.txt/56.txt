13.3 Nature of Business
Each of the Issuer and the Operator shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by any Group Company if such substantial change would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.4 Financial Indebtedness
None of the Issuer or the Operator shall, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, incur, prolong, renew or extend any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that a Group Company have a right to incur, prolong, renew or extend Financial Indebtedness that constitutes Permitted Debt.
