(c) A communication pursuant to Clause 21(a) shall include (i) each request for a decision by the Bondholders, (ii) a description of the reasons for each request, (iii) a specifica tion of the CSD Business Day on which a Person must be registered as a Bondholder in order to be entitled to exercise voting rights, (iv) instructions and directions on where to receive a form for replying to the request (such form to include an option to vote yes or no for each request) as well as a form of power of attorney, and (v) the stipulated time period within which the Bondholder must reply to the request (such time period to last at least fifteen (15) Business Days from the communication pursuant to Clause 21(a)).
