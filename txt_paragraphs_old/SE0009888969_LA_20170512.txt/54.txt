15.5 Insolvency Proceedings
Any corporate actions, legal proceedings or other procedures are taken (other than (A) proceedings which are vexatious or frivolous or are being disputed in good faith and are discharged within sixty (60) calendar days, (B) proceedings or petitions concerning a claim which is less than SEK 20,000,000 and (C), in relation to Subsidiaries of the Issuer, solvent liquidations) in relation to:
(a) the suspension of payments, winding-up, reorganisation (Sw. foretagsrekonstruktion) or similar (by way of voluntary arrangement or otherwise) of any Material Group Company; and
(b) the appointment of a liquidator, administrator, or other similar officer in respect of any Material Group Company or any of its assets or any analogous procedure.
