"Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement entered into between, amongst other, the Issuer, the lenders under the Super Senior RCF, the agent under the Super Senior RCF, the hedging counterparties to the Super Senior Hedges and the Agent (representing the Bondholders).
