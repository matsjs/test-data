7.13 No proceedings pending or threatened
(a)
No litigation, arbitration or administrative proceedings or investigations of, or before, any
court, arbitral body or agency which are reasonably likely to be adversely determined and, if
adversely determined, would have a Material Adverse Effect have been started or (to the best
of its knowledge or belief, having made due and careful enquiiy) threatened in writing
against it or any of its Subsidiaries.
