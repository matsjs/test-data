"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Payment Date originally falls on a day that is not a Business Day, an adjustment of the Payment Date will be made so that the relevant Payment Date will be the first following day that is a Business Day, unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that Payment Date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day.
"Call Date" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2 (Call Option).
"Call Option" means the call option described in paragraph (a) of Clause 10.2 (Call Option).
"Change of Control Event" means any event where a Person (or group of Persons acting in conceit) other than the Sponsor (directly or indirectly) acquires a majority of the share capital in the Issuer or gains Decisive Influence over the Issuer.
