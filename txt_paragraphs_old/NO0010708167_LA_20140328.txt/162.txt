Miscellaneous
7.17 Priority of ranking
Subject to the Reservations and Perfection Requirements, ranking of each Security Interest is in the priority as expressed in the relevant Security Document and the relevant security assets are not subject to any prior ranking or pari passu ranking Liens other than Permitted Security.
