18. THE BOND TRUSTEE
(a)
The Bond Trustee shall monitor the compliance by the Issuer of its obligations under this
Agreement and applicable laws and regulations which are relevant to the terms of this
Agreement, including supervision of timely and correct payment of principal or interest,
(however, this shall not restrict the Bond Trustee from discussing matters of confidentiality
with the Issuer), arrange Bondholders' Meetings, and make the decisions and implement the
measures resolved pursuant to this Agreement. The Bond Trustee is not obligated to assess
the Issuer's financial situation beyond what is directly set out in this Agreement.
