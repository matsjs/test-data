The Bond Trustee can as a condition for carrying out an instruction from the Bondholders
(Acceleration) or paragraph (b) of Clause 17.1 (Authority o f the Bondholders' Meeting),
require satisfactory security and indemnities for any possible liability and anticipated costs
and expenses, from those Bondholders who requested that instruction and/or those who
voted in favour of the decision to instruct the Bond Trustee. Any instructions from the
Bondholders may be put forward to the Bondholders' Meeting by the Bond Trustee before
the Bond Trustee takes any action.
