(d)
The functions, rights and obligations of the Security Agent may be determined by a Security
Agent agreement to be entered into between the Bond Trustee and the Security Agent, which
the Bond Trustee shall have the right to require the Issuer and any other parties to any
Security Document to sign as a party, or, at the discretion of the Bond Trustee, to
acknowledge. The Bond Trustee shall at all times retain the right to instruct the Security
Agent in all matters.
