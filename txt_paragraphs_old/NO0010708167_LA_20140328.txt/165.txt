34/60
Status of the Bonds
The Bonds shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer and will he:
(a)
secured by the Security Interests on the same priority as the other Secured Parties,
but will receive proceeds from an enforcement or a distressed disposal of any
Security Interest only after any obligations secured on a super priority basis have
been repaid in full (including obligations of the Group Companies under the
Revolving Credit Facility and any Permitted Hedging Obligations);
