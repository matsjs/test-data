11.2 Payment mechanics
(a)
If no specific order is made by the Bond Trustee under Clause 11.1 (Covenant to pay), the
Issuer shall pay all amounts due to the Bondholders under this Agreement or any other
Finance Document by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in
connection with its securities account in the Securities Depository.
