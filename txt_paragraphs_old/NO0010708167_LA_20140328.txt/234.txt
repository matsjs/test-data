16.
EVENTS OF DEFAULT
The Bond Trustee may declare the Bonds to be in default upon occurrence of any of the events set out in Clause 16.1 (Non-payment) through Clause 16.9 {Registered Security Interests).
16.1 Non-payment
The Issuer fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full within five (5) Business Days following the original due date.
16.2 Breach of other obligations
A Material Group Company does not comply with the covenants set out in Clause 13 (iCovenants) of this Agreement, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable of being remedied and is remedied within fifteen (15) Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
