"Initial Bonds" means all of the debt instruments issued or to be issued by the Issuer under this Agreement in accordance with paragraph (a) of Clause 2.2 (The Bonds).
"Intercompany Loan" means any loan made between any Group Companies (not including any Financial Indebtedness under any cash pooling arrangement of the Group), and which pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement, to the extent required to do so thereunder, shall be fully subordinated to the obligations of the relevant borrower under, inter alia, the Finance Documents.
"Intercompany Loan Assignments" means the Norwegian law assignment granted or to be granted by the Issuer, Pharmaq Holding and Pharmaq Analytic in respect of its monetary claims arising under or in respect of the Existing Intercompany Loans.
"Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement dated 28 March 2014 entered into between, among others, the Bond Trustee, the RCF Lenders and the Issuer.
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Finance Charges.
"Interest Payment Date" means, subject to the Business Day Convention, 28 February, 28 May, 28 August and 28 November each year and the Final Maturity Date.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited consolidated semi-annual financial statements of the Issuer for the semi-annual period ending on 30 June each financial year, prepared in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
"Investors" means the Permira Funds and ManCo.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
