"Shareholder Loan" means any shareholder loan granted or to be granted to the Issuer by the Parent (or any of its shareholders), with terms (including aggregate amount) and final structure acceptable to the Bond Trustee (acting in its sole discretion), inter alia, to ensure that:
such loan is fully subordinated to the obligations of the Issuer under, inter alia, the
Finance Documents;
(b)
such loan is considered as equity for rating purposes; and
(c)
any repayment of, or payment of interest under, any such loan is subject to all
present and future obligations and liabilities under, inter alia, the Finance
Documents having been discharged in full.
