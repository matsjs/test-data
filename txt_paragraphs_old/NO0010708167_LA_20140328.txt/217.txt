13.16 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, enter into any transaction with any Affiliate except on arm's length terms and for fair market value, unless it is a Permitted Affiliate Transaction.
13.17 Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies will, cany on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
