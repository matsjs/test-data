13.12 Continuation of business
The Group, taken as a whole, shall not cease to cany on its business, if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect. The Issuer will not, and will not permit any Group Company to, engage in any business other than a Related Business, except as would not have a Material Adverse Effect on the Group taken as a whole.
13.13 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
