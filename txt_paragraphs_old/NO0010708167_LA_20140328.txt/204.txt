13.5 De-mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out
any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of:
(i)
the Issuer into two or more separate entities, unless the Issuer remains in
existence as the primary obligor under the Bonds and:
(A)
any such separate companies are (directly or indirectly) wholly-
owned by the Issuer; and
