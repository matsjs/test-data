5.
ESCROW OF PROCEEDS
5.1
The Net Proceeds of the Initial Notes Issue shall be paid by the Issuing Agent into the
Escrow Account. The funds standing to the credit on the Escrow Account shall be secured
in favour of the Agent on behalf of the Noteholders.
