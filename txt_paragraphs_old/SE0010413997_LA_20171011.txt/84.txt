14.19.2
The Issuer and the Agent shall not agree to amend any provisions of the Agency Agreement without the prior consent of the Noteholders if the amendment would be detrimental to the interests of the Noteholders.
14.20 CSD related undertakings
The Issuer shall keep the Notes affiliated with a CSD and comply with all applicable CSD Regulations.
