INCURRENCE TEST
Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) no Event of Default is continuing or would result from the relevant incurrence;
(b) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA (the "Leverage Ratio") is equal to or less than 3.50:1 for the Relevant Period; and
(c) the Interest Coverage Ratio is greater than 2.00:1 for the Relevant Period.
