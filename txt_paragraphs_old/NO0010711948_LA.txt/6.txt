1.
1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Amount" means the total cash amount on the Accounts.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Accounts" means (i) the Escrow Account, (ii) the Issuer's Earnings Account, (iii) the Rig Manager's Earnings Account, (iv) the Rig Owners' Earnings Accounts and (v) the Debt Service Reserve Account, all of which shall be established with DNB Bank ASA or another Norwegian bank with minimum single A rating, except for the Rig Manager's Earnings Account.
"Account Pledges" means (i) the Escrow Account Pledge, (ii) the Issuer's Earnings Account Pledge, (iii) the Rig Manager's Earnings Account Pledge, (iv) the Rig Owners' Earnings Accounts Pledge and (v) the Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge.
"Assignment of Earnings" means a first priority assignment of any earnings related to any contract entered into in connection with the Rigs either by the Rig Owners or by the Rig Manager, including the Leasing Agreements and the Drilling Agreements, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Drilling Agreements" means the first priority assignment of any Drilling Agreement in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), to be created by a New York law security agreement and perfected by filing UCC financing statements in the District of Columbia and the State of Texas, subject to the consent of the counterparty. The Rig Manager shall give notices of such assignment to any counterparty to a Drilling Agreement and use its best endeavours to procure their acknowledgements and consents.
