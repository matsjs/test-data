"Cash Tranche" means the cash proceeds under the Bond Issue.
"Current Liabilities" means the aggregate book value of the Group's (on a consolidated basis) liabilities which are treated as current liabilities in accordance with GAAP, excluding the current portion of long term debt and accrued interest for which funds have been credited to the Debt Service Reserve Account, the current portion of amortized loan issuance costs, any payments for rigs under construction classified as current liabilities and liabilities to non-controlling interests, to be specified item by item in the accounts.
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the direct and/or indirect ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
4
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
When determining the relevant person's number of voting rights in the other person or the right to elect and remove members of the board of directors, rights held by the parent company of the relevant person and the parent company's Subsidiaries shall be included.
"Debt Service Reserve Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer held with the Paying Agent, to be used for interest payments under the Bond Issue, blocked (save for interest payments and amortization to be paid in accordance with the terms of the Bonds) and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, including interests, costs and expenses.
"Debt Service Reserve Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the issuer's claims against the account bank for the amount from time to time outstanding to the credit of the Issuer in the Debt Service Reserve Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the account bank has waived any set-off rights.
