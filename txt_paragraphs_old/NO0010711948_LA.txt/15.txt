"Insurances" means all policies and contracts of insurance which are from time to time taken out or entered into in respect of or in connection with the Rigs and their operation (and where the context permits ail benefits thereof, including all claims of any nature and returns of premium).
"Interest Payment Date" means 3 June and 3 December each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to GAAP, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means 3 June 2014.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer.
"Issuer's Earnings Account" means the Issuer's accounts with DNB Bank ASA, to which all proceeds on the Issuer's present and future money claims under any Subordinated Loans and any dividend payments or other distributions from the Guarantors shall be deposited, pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) but not blocked (unless there is an outstanding event of default pursuant to the Bond Agreement). No amount on the Issuer's Earnings Account shall be transferred to any bank account of the Group not being an Account.
"Issuer's Earnings Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge of the Issuer's Earnings Account by the Issuer in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
