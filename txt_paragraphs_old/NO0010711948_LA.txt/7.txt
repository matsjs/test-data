"Assignment of Earnings" means a first priority assignment of any earnings related to any contract entered into in connection with the Rigs either by the Rig Owners or by the Rig Manager, including the Leasing Agreements and the Drilling Agreements, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Insurances" means the first priority assignments over the Insurances and receipt of related consents and acknowledgments from the relevant insurers, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Leasing Agreements" means the first priority assignments of any Leasing Agreement by each of the Rig Owners in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), to be created by a New York law security agreement and perfected by filing UCC financing statements in the District of Columbia and the State of Texas. The Rig Manager shall give notices of such assignment to any counterparty to a Leasing Agreement and use its best endeavours to procure their acknowledgements and consents.
