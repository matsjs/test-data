calendar year, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase shares or grant loans or make other distributions to its shareholders (included but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party) exceeding in aggregate 40% of net profit after taxes (on a consolidated basis) based on the accounts for the previous calendar year. Any un utilized portion may not be carried forward.
(c) No investments
The Issuer shall not, without the prior written approval of the Bond Trustee or, where necessary, the Bondholders, invest or take part directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any party other than solely related to the construction, ownership and operation of the land rigs.
(d) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any Security over any of its present of future respective assets or revenues provided as security for the Bonds, or enter into arrangements having a similar effect, other than the Security Interest and the Permitted Security.
(e) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig Owners and the Rig Manager do not incur, create or permit to subsist, any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
