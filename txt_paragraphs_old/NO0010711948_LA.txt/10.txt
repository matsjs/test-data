"Debt Service Reserve Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer held with the Paying Agent, to be used for interest payments under the Bond Issue, blocked (save for interest payments and amortization to be paid in accordance with the terms of the Bonds) and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, including interests, costs and expenses.
"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Drilling Agreements" means any present and future leasing agreements between the Rig Manager and an operator/oil company where Rigs are leased by such end-client operating the relevant Rig.
"Equity Ratio" means the ratio of Book Equity to Total Assets.
"Escrow Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer held with the Paying Agent, blocked and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Escrow Account Bank" means DNB Bank ASA (Norwegian main branch).
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (for the benefit of the bondholders), over the Issuer's claims against the account bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means (i) a securities exchange or other reputable regulated market, or (ii) Oslo Alternative Bond Market, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
