The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 120,000,000 (U.S. Dollar One Hundred and Twenty Million).
The Face Value is USD 1.00 each with a minimum subscription and allotment amount of USD 200,000 and integral multiples of USD 100,000 in excess thereof. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "9 per cent Global Rig Company ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2014 / 2019".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 0010711948.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
