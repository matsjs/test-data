Each of the events or circumstances set out in this Clause 16 (other than Clause 16.10 (Acceleration of the Bonds)) is an Event of Default.
16.1 Non-Payment
The Issuer fails to pay an amount on the date it is due in accordance with the Finance Documents unless the non-payment:
(a) is caused by technical or administrative error; and
(b) is remedied within five (5) Business Days of the due date.
16.2 Other Obligations
The Obligors does not comply with any provision under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out in Clause 16.1 (Non-Payment), provided that the Agent has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within fifteen (15) Business Days from such request (if the failure or violation is not capable of being remedied, the Agent may declare the Bonds payable without such prior written request).
