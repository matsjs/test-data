"Change of Control Event" means the occurrence of an event or series of events whereby:
(a) the Principle Owners ceases to control and own, directly or indirectly, on a fully diluted basis at least fifty-one (51) per cent of the issued share capital, voting rights or the economic interest of the Parent;
(b) the Principle Owners ceases to have the ability to determine the composition of the majority of the board of directors of the Parent, or
(c) the Parent ceasing to control and own on a fully diluted basis one hundred (100) per cent of the issued share capital and voting rights of the Issuer.
