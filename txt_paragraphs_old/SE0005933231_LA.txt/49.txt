(a) The Issuer shall ensure that the relevant counterparties under the Price Agreement and the Operation and Maintenance Agreement (or any agreements replacing such agreements), at all times:
(A) SEK 25,000,000 during the period from the First Issue Date until the date falling one year after the First Issue Date;
(B) SEK 25,000,000 during the period from the date falling one year after the First Issue Date until the date falling two year after the First Issue Date;
(C) SEK 20,000,000 during the period from the date falling two year after the First Issue Date until the date falling three year after the First Issue Date;
(D) SEK 15,000,000 during the period from the date falling three year after the First Issue Date until the date falling four year after the First Issue Date; and
(E) SEK 10,000,000 during the period from the date falling four year after the First Issue Date until the Final Maturity Date; or
(ii) provides cash collateral, in an aggregate amount equal to the relevant amount set out in (A)-(E) above for such relevant period, to the Issuer,
as security for the timely fulfilment of their respective obligations under the Price Agreement and the Operation and Maintenance Agreement respectively.
(b) If at any time the Price Agreement and the Operation and Maintenance Agreement are made with two different counterparties the amount of the liability insurance or collateral as set out in paragraph (a)(i) and (ii) above shall be maintained to 50 per cent, by the counterparty under the Price Agreement and to 50 per cent by the counterparty under the Operation and Maintenance Agreement.
