3. Use of Proceeds
The Net Proceeds from the issuance of the Initial Bonds shall be applied against repayment in full of the Refinancing Debt, payment of the purchase price under the New Wind Turbines Acquisition Agreements and any remaining amount shall be used for general corporate purposes of the Group. Any proceeds from any issuance of Subsequent Bonds shall be used for acquisition of new Wind Turbines and general corporate purposes of the Group.
