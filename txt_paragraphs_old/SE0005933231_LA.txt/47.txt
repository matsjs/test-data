15.10 Insurances
In addition to 15.13 (Counterparty Insurance), the Issuer shall (and shall ensure that each member of the Group will) maintain full value insurances with reputable independent insurance companies on and in relation to its business and assets against those risks and to the extent as is usual for companies carrying on the same or substantially similar business.
