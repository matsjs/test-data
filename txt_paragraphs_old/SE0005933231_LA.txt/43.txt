The Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time the Equity Ratio is at least 30 per cent.
14.1 Equity Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Equity Ratio at all times shall be at least 20 per cent.
14.2 Minimum Cash
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a minimum Cash:
(a) of an amount equivalent to the Interest to be paid for two Interest Periods, calculated on STIBOR on the Quotation Date before the first Interest Period, during the period from the First Issue Date until the Final Maturity Date; and
(b) in addition to the amount set out in paragraph (a) above, an amount equivalent to 15 per cent of the Total Nominal Amount, during the period from the date falling four years after the First Issue Date until the Final Maturity Date.
