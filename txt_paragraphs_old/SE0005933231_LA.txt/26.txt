"Subsidiary" means a subsidiary of the Issuer according to Chapter 1 Section 11 of the Swedish Companies Act (or under such provision as may replace this provision).
"Swedish Government Note Rate" means the interpolated SGB rate between the SGB 12 July 2016 (series 1050) and the SGB 12 August 2017 (series 1051) (mid rates), as determined by the Issuing Agent on or about 11.00 am on the date of the notification of redemption. If a quote for any aforementioned SGB rate is unavailable on the relevant date, the Issuing Agent may select a SGB rate it deems appropriate for the purpose of the calculation set out in this definition (acting reasonably).
"Swedish Kronor" and "SEK" means the lawful currency of Sweden.
"Total Assets" means the consolidated book-value of all assets of all members of the Group calculated in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
"Total Nominal Amount" means the total aggregate Nominal Amount of the Bonds outstanding at the relevant time.
"Transaction Costs" means all fees, costs and expenses, stamp, registration and other taxes incurred by the Issuer or any other member of the Group in connection with the issuance of the Bonds and, in connection with the issuance of the Initial Bonds, the refinancing of the Refinancing Debt.
"Transaction Security" means the Security provided for the Secured Obligations pursuant to the Security Document.
