"Maintenance Test" is met if:
(i) from (and including) 31 December 2015 up to (and including) 30 December 2016 is not greater than 5.50;
(ii) from (and including) 31 December 2016 up to (and including) 30 December 2017 is not greater than 4.00;
(iii) from (and including) 31 December 2017 up to (and including) the Final Redemption Date is not greater than 3.00; and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio
(i) from (and including) 31 December 2015 up to (and including) 30 December 2016 exceeds 1.50;
(ii) from (and including) 31 December 2016 up to (and including) 30 December 2017 exceeds 1.75;
(iii) from (and including) 31 December 2017 up to (and including) the Final
Redemption Date exceeds 2.50.
