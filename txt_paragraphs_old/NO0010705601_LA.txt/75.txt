(a) Ownership The Parent shall remain the sole shareholder of the Issuer.
The Parent shall ensure that no Group Company (other than an Issuer Group Company) grants any loans to the Issuer and/or any o f the Guarantors other than Shareholder Loans.
(c) Disposal o f business
The Parent shall ensure that no Group Company sells or otherwise disposes o f all or a substantial part o f its assets or operations (save for any Mandatory Redemption Event), unless any such transaction is carried out at a fair market value and on customary terms, and provided that such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
(d) A rm 's length transactions
The Parent shall not engage in, or permit any Issuer Group Company to engage in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange o f assets or the rendering o f any service), except upon terms that are no less favourable to the Parent or such member of the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(e)
Co-investment
The Parent shall not, and shall procure that the Ultimate Parent does not, engage in any business arrangement that will (directly or indirectly) compete with the business o f the Issuer Group.
