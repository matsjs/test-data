"Interest Payment Date" means 19 March and 19 September each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Internal Loan Agreement" means a loan agreement (i) between the Issuer and a Guarantor, (ii) the Issuer and the Parent in respect o f the Parent Loan or (iii) between two Guarantors setting out the conditions for the Internal Loans, to be in form and substance acceptable to the Bond Trustee (on behalf o f the Bondholders).
"Internal Loans" means:
(i)
the loans from the Issuer to the Guarantors (as applicable) made in
connection with the purpose as described in Clause 2.3 (i) and (ii);
(ii) any other loan (if applicable) between companies in the Issuer Group; and
(iii) the Parent Loan.
"Internal Loans Assignment" means a first priority assignment o f the Internal Loans.
