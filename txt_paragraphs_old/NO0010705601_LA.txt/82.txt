14.5 The Issuer is responsible for withholding any withholding tax imposed by applicable law on any payments to the Bondholders.
(i)
the amount o f the payment due from the Issuer shall be increased to such
amount which is necessary to ensure that the Bondholders receive a net
amount which is (after making the required withholding) equal to the
payment which would have been due if no withholding had been required;
and
(ii) the Issuer shall at the request o f the Bond Trustee deliver to the Bond Trustee evidence that the required tax reduction or withholding has been made.
14.7 If any withholding tax is imposed due to subsequent changes in applicable law after the date of this Bond Agreement, the Issuer shall have the right to call all but not some o f the Bonds at par value plus accrued interest. Such call shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty - 30 Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the call.
