17.2 Liability and indemnity
17.2.1
The Bond Trustee is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders or the Issuer as a result o f gross negligence or wilful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set out in this Bond Agreement. Such liability is limited to the maximum amount set out in Clause 2.2. The Bond Trustee is not liable for the content o f information provided to the Bondholders on behalf o f the Issuer.
