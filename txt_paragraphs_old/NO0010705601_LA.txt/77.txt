Shareholder Loans
The Parent shall ensure that any Shareholder Loans are subordinated to the Bond Issue and documented by a loan agreement. The Parent shall furthermore ensure that any Shareholder Loans:
(i)
Are fully subordinated to the Bonds,
(ii) are non-amortising with a maturity date which falls after the Maturity Date and with no pre-payment before such date, and
(iii) earn interest by way of accumulation which is added to the principal o f such loan during the term o f the Bonds.
