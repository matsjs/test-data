(c) The- Parent Guarantor shall prepare a quarterly report of activities and cash flow report available on the Parent Guarantor's website as soon as they are available, and in any eyent no later than 90 days after the end of the relevant Quarter Date (for so long as and to the extent that the Parent Guarantor is required to make such reports available under the ASX Eisting Rules).
