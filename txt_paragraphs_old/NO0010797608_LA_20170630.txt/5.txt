14 Definitions The following terms will have the following meanings:
"Acceptable bank" means:
(a) a commercial bank, savings bank, treasury branch, trust company or other commercial lender with at least A- rating from Standard & Poor's Rating Services or Fitch Ratings Ltd or A3 rating from Moody's Investors Services Limited or a comparable rating from a nationally recognized credit ranking agency for its long-term debt obligations; Gl
ib) a bank or financial institution which is authorized to carry on banking business in Australia and/or the United States (or, in respect of the Escrow Account, Norway or such other jurisdiction as approved by the Bond Trustee).
"Account Banks" means the banks where each of the Accounts is established.
"Accounts" means together the following accounts:
(a) the Cost Overrun Account;
(b) the Earnings Accounts;
