13.8 Changes to the Capital Expenditure Budget (a) The Issuer shall promptly notify the Bond Trustee and the Independent Engineer of any changes to the Capital Expenditure Budget which, together with any previous increases (if any), results in an aggregate increase of the Capital Expenditure Budget by more than AUD 10,000,000. The notification shall be sent to the Independent Engineer as soon as the trend-register in the Master Control Budget shows that the AVD 10,000,000 contingency in the Capital Expenditure Budget will be fully utilized. The Issuer shall be permitted to implement such a change in the Master Control Budget provided that: (i) the change relates to the Pilgangoora Project; and
(ii) the Cost-to-Complete Test will continue to be satisfied,
(the "Permitted CapEx Increase").
(b) In the event such Permitted CapEx Increase is made to the Master Control Budget, the Issuer shall provide to the Bond Trustee and the Independent Engineer a Company Certificate describing in reasonable detail: (i) the natute of the budget increase;
(ii) the cost implication to the Master Control Budget of such change;
(iii) the time implication of the budget increase (if any); and
