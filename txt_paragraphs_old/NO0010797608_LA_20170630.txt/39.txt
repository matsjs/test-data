"Senior Secured Obligations" means all present and future obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents, the Permitted Working Capital Facility and the Permitted Hedging.
"Security Trustee" means the Bond Trustee or any successor Security Trustee, acting for and on behalf of the Secured Parties in accordance with the Security Trust Deed or any other Finance Document.
"Significant Future Documents" means the material future agreements, feasibility studies, environmental permits and other material documents that are required for the completion of the Pilgangoora Project set out in Schedule 4 [List o fSignificant Future Documents).
"Subordinated Loan" means a loan from the Parent Guarantor for the purpose of providing a Cure Amount, with no right of service (of principal nor interest) as long as any Bonds remain outstanding, and provided that creditor enter into a subordination and turnover agreement and in form, scope and substance acceptable to the Bond Trustee
"Subordinated Obligations" means all present and future obligations and liabilities of an Obligor owing to a Prepayment Creditor in respect of prepayments made under an offtake agreement in relation to the Pilgangoora Project, and Which pursuant to the Security Trust Deed shall be fully subordinated to the Senior Secured Obligations.
"Subsidiary" means a company over which another company has Decisive Influence.
"Summons" means the call for a Bondholders' Meeting or a Written Resolution as the case may be.
"Tax Event Repayment Date" means the date set out in a notice from the Issuer to the Bondholders pursuant to Clause 10.4 (Early redemption option due to a tax event).
"Total Assets" means the aggregate amount which would in accordance with IFRS be shown in the Parent Guarantor's financial statements as the total assets of the Group on a consolidated basis.
