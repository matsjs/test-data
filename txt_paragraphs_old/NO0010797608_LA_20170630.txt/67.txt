10.2 Voluntary early redemption - Call Option (a) The Issuer may redeem all or parts of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Call Option") on any Business Day from and including:
(ii) the First Call Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling in June 2021 at a price equal to 104.80 per cent of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond;
(iii) the Interest Payment Date falling in June 2021 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2021 at a price equal to 102.40 per cent, of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond; and
(iv) the Interest Payment Date falling in December 2021 to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 per cent, of the Nominal Amount for each redeemed Bond.
In addition to such amounts payable, under (i) - (iv) above^ the Issuer shall pay accrued and unpaid interest on redeemed Bonds.
(b) Any redemption of Bonds pursuant to Clause 10.2 (a) above shall be determined based upon the redemption prices applicable on the Gall Option Repayment Date.
(c) The Call Option may be exercised by the Issuer by written notice to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least ten (10), but not more than 20, Business Days prior to the proposed Call Option Repayment Date.
