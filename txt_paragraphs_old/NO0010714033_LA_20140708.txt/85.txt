9.1 Interest rate The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at the Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
9.3 Calculation of interest payments
(a)
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and
including, the Issue Date or one Interest Payment Date (as the case may be) to, but
excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
