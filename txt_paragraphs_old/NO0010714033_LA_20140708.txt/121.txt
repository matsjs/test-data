15. PARENT UNDERTAKINGS
15.1 Ownership to Issuer The Parent shall not sell or otherwise dispose of any shares or ownership interest in the Issuer.
15.2 Distributions The Parent shall not propose, vote in favour of, consent to or receive any dividend payment or distribution by the Issuer, whether in cash or in kind, or any other transactions implying a transfer of value from the Group to the Parent or any of its shareholders in breach of Clause 14.1 (Distributions).
15.3 Shareholder Loans The Parent shall (i) not consent to or receive any repayment of, or payment of interest under, any Shareholder Loan, (ii) not propose or consent to amendment of terms of any Shareholder Loan (unless a Permitted Distribution), and (iii) ensure that any Shareholder Loan remains fully subordinated to the Secured Obligations.
