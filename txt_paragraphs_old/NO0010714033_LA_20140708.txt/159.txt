19.4 Repeated Bondholders' Meeting
(a)
If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause 19.3(d), a
repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the same matters. The
attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders' Meeting shall be specified in
the summons for the repeated Bondholders' Meeting.
