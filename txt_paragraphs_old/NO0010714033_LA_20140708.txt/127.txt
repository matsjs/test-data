16.2 Incurrence Test The incurrence test (the "Incurrence Test") is met if the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than:
5.00, prior to the date falling 18 months after the Issue Date;
(b)
4.50, from and including the date falling 18 months after the Issue Date to, but not
including, the date falling 48 months after the Issue Date; or
(c)
4.00, from and including the date falling 48 months after the Issue Date to, but not
including, the Maturity Date.
