10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall be repaid by the Issuer in full on the Maturity Date at a price equivalent to the sum of 100% of par value.
10.2 Call Option
(a)
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue (in whole or in parts) (a "Call Option") at any
time from and including:
(i) the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling 2 years after Issue Date (the "Call Date") at a price equivalent to the sum of 100.00% of par value and the Applicable Premium (plus accrued and unpaid interest on redeemed Bonds);
