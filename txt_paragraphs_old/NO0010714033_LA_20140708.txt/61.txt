3. LISTING
3.1 The Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds are listed on the Exchange as soon as possible after the Issue Date.
3.2 In the event that such listing of the Bonds has not occurred by 10 July 2015 (the "Non-listing Put Option Event") each bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (put option) of the Bonds in accordance with Clause 10.4.
