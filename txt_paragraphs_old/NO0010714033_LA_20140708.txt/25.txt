"New Security" means Security in favour of the Secured Parties in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Intercreditor Agreement (subject to the Security Principles set out therein) to be granted over any new assets having been acquired.
"NGAAP" means the generally accepted accounting principles, practices and standards in Norway, in force from time to time.
"NIBOR" means the interest rate fixed for a defined period on Oslo Børs' webpage at approximately 12:15 hours (Oslo time) or, on days on which Oslo Børs has shorter opening hours (New Year's Eve and the Wednesday before Maundy Thursday), the data published at approximately 10:15 hours (Oslo time) shall be used. In the event that such page is not available, has been removed or changed such that the quoted interest rate no longer represents, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, a correct expression of the relevant interest rate, an alternative page or other electronic source which in the opinion of the Bond Trustee and the Issuer gives the same interest rate shall be used. If this is not possible, the Bond Trustee shall calculate the relevant interest rate based on comparable quotes from major banks in Oslo. If any such rate is below zero, NIBOR will be deemed to be zero.
"NOK" means Norwegian kroner, being the lawful currency of Norway.
"Non-listing Put Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.4.
"Non-listing Put Option Event" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 3.2.
"Norwegian Government Rate" means the rate interpolated from Norwegian Government rates available from Norges Bank on the 10th Business Day prior to redemption, as determined by the Bond Trustee. If the aforementioned rate is unavailable on the relevant date, the Bond Trustee may select a rate it deems appropriate for the purpose of the calculation set out in this definition (acting reasonably).
"Obligor" means the Issuer and any Guarantor.
