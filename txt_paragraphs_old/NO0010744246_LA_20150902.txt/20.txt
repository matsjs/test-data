The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
Purpose and utilization
The purpose of the Bond Issue is to procure settlement towards the holders of the Existing Bonds through the issuance and transfer of the Bonds in exchange and as settlement of the Existing Bonds upon completion of the Restructuring Transactions.
