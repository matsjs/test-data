9.1.3
The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, the Issue Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date, and thereafter from, and including, that Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
9.1.4
The Issuer shall, if it intends to pay any part of the interest by issuing Additional Bonds, as soon as practicable and in any event not less than ten (10) Business Days prior to the relevant Interest Payment Date, give notice to the Bond Trustee and the Paying Agent of the amount of interest that will be paid in cash and/or Additional Bonds, respectively, which notice shall be irrevocable.
9.1.5
If the Issuer elects to pay any interest by issuing Additional Bonds, the number of Additional Bonds allocated to a Bondholder holding Bonds shall be calculated as the product of (x) the aggregate amount of interest being paid in the form of Additional Bonds and (y) a fraction, the numerator of which is the aggregate number of Outstanding Bonds held by that Bondholder and the denominator of which is the aggregate amount of Outstanding Bonds, provided that the total number of Additional Bonds allocated to a Bondholder shall be rounded to the nearest USD 1.00. Following the increase in principal amount of Outstanding Bonds as a result of the issue of Additional Bonds, the Bonds will bear interest on such increased amount from and including the date of issue of such Additional Bonds.
9.1.6
The day count fraction ("Fixed Rate Day Count Fraction") in respect of the calculation of the payable interest amount shall be "30/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in respect of which payment is being made divided by 360 (the number of days to be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-days months (unless (i) the last day of the calculation period is the 31st day of a month but the first day of the calculation period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or (ii) the last day of the calculation period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month)).
