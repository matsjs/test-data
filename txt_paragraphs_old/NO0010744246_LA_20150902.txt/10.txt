" Manager" means either (i) AMA Capital Partners LLC pursuant to the terms of the Governance Agreement; or (ii) following termination of the Governance Agreement, another manager of equivalent standing (as agreed with the Bond Trustee) pursuant to the terms of a Replacement Governance Agreement
" Mandatory Prepayment" means repayment of any Outstanding Bonds due to a Mandatory Prepayment Event.
" Mandatory Prepayment Event" means the occurrence of any of the following events:
(a) the completion of a sale of the Vessel to the Company or Ocean Champion pursuant to the Company Purchase Option or the Ocean Champion Purchase Option;
(b) the completion of any transfer, disposal or other sale of the Vessel; and
(c) the occurrence of a Total Loss.
" Mandatory Prepayment Event Proceeds" means the aggregate cash proceeds received by the Issuer or Refectio in connection with any Mandatory Prepayment Event, after deduction of a sufficient amount (as determined in the Issuer's reasonable discretion) to cover the applicable taxes, related costs and expenses and liquidation costs for the Issuer and Refectio (if applicable).
