Advisory Committee Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Bond Agreement, the following provisions shall take priority and apply:
(b) Each member of the Advisory Committee shall serve until the Bondholders' Meeting has resolved to replace such member or such member has given written notice to the Issuer of his or her resignation from the Advisory Committee.
(c) The Issuer shall act in accordance with any instructions of the Advisory Committee in relation to clause 10.1.2 of this Bond Agreement; and in circumstances where the Issuer becomes aware of the occurrence of a Termination Event (as defined in the Charterparty) or where the Issuer considers there could be a Termination Event (as defined in the Charterparty), the Issuer shall (A) advise the Advisory Committee of such circumstances; (B) consult with the Advisory Committee prior to taking any steps in relation thereto; and (C) appoint any advisors recommended by the Advisory Committee.
(d) If the members of the Advisory Committee, at any time, collectively hold less than a majority of the Voting Bonds, the Advisory Committee shall inform the Bond Trustee of the same and any matter to be considered by the Advisory Committee will instead be determined by the Bond Trustee, acting in accordance with the terms of this Bond Agreement.
(e) The Issuer shall arrange and attend meetings (in person or by conference) with the members of the Advisory Committee (or their delegates) at the request of the Advisory Committee.
(f) Neither the Issuer, nor, for the avoidance of doubt, its directors or officers shall be liable for any actions taken in accordance with resolutions passed at a Bondholders' Meeting (or written Bondholders' resolutions pursuant to Clause 16.5) or as a result of advice received from the Advisory Committee provided in accordance with the terms of this Bond Agreement.
13.5
Maintenance and service of the Cash Sweep Account
The Cash Sweep Account shall be maintained with the Paying Agent and pledged (but not blocked) in favour of the Bond Trustee. The Issuer will ensure that (i) at no time the aggregate cash balances of the Issuer which are not held in Cash Sweep Account will exceed USD 10,000; and (ii) that it will not have any bank accounts other than the Cash Sweep Account.
