(h) Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all aspects and comply in all respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time. Breach of these obligations shall be regarded as non compliance only if such breach would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(i) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any financial indebtedness (including guarantees), other than: (i) any financial indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue and (ii) any financial indebtedness arising under the Super Senior Financing Facility.
(j) Single-purpose entity
The Issuer shall not have any business other than being the owner of the Vessel, entering into the Restructuring Transaction and matters reasonably ancillary thereto.
(k) Amendments and waivers
The Issuer shall not, without the prior written consent of the Bond Trustee, exercise any of its rights or make or permit any amendments or grant any waivers under (i) the Relevant Documents; (ii) the BOP Sale Agreement; (iii) the Governance Agreement; or (iv) the Issuer's memorandum and articles of association,.
