2. THE BONDS 2.1 Amount, denomination and ISIN of the Bonds
(a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of USD 100,000,000.
(b) The Bonds are denominated in US Dollars (USD), being the legal currency o f the United States o f America.
(c) The Initial Nominal Amount of each Bond is USD 200,000.
(d) The ISIN of the Bonds is NO 0010815053. All Bonds issued under the same ISIN will have identical terms and conditions as set out in these Bond Terms.
2.2 Tenor of the Bonds The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
2.3 Use of proceeds The Issuer may use the net proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds for general corporate purposes of the Group, including without limitation for partial repayment of existing debt.
