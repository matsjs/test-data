13.1.2
Compliance with laws
The Parent shall, and shall procure that each other Group Company (other than the Issuer) will, comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject from time to time, if failure so to comply would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.1.3 Continuation o f business
The Parent shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on by the Group at the Issue Date.
