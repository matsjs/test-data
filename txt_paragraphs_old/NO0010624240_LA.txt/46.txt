16.3.2 In all matters, the Issuer, the Bond Trustee and any Bondholder have the right to demand vote by ballot. In case o f parity o f votes, the chairman shall have the deciding vote, regardless of the chairman being a Bondholder or not.
In order to form a quorum, at least half (1/2) o f the Voting Bonds must be represented at the meeting, see however Clause 16.4. Even if less than half (1/2) of the Voting Bonds are represented, the Bondholders' Meeting shall be held and voting completed solely for the purpose o f providing this information to the Bondholders pursuant to Clause 16.4.
16.3.4 Resolutions shall be passed by simple majority o f the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting, unless otherwise set forth in Clause 16.3.5.
16.3.5 In the following matters, a majority o f at least 2/3 o f the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting is required:
(a) amendment of the terms o f this Bond Agreement regarding the interest rate, the tenor, redemption price and other terms and conditions affecting the cash flow o f the Bonds, as well as any provisions relating to the conversion rights;
(b) transfer of rights and obligations o f this Bond Agreement to another issuer (Issuer), or
