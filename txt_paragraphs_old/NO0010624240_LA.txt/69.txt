B.5 The Issuer is responsible for ensuring that:
(a) the share capital increase is registered in the company register as soon as possible,
(b) the Outstanding Bonds are written down within the Conversion Date, and
(c) Shares issued upon conversion are registered in the Securities Depository on the Conversion Date.
I f the share capital increase is not registered in the Company Register at the Conversion Date, the Issuer shall ensure that the shares following the conversion shall be temporarily registered in the Securities Depository (with temporary ISIN).
B. 6 Shares issued upon conversion give rights in the Issuer as o f the registration o f the share capital increase in the Company Register, unless the general meeting o f the Issuer has resolved differently.
