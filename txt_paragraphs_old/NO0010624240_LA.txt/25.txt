10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Outstanding Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may at any time redeem the Bond Issue at 100 % o f par plus accrued
