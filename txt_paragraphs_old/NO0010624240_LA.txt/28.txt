In the event o f conversion pursuant to this Clause, the Issuer shall as soon as possible, but in no event later than on the Bid for Shares Conversion Date/Delisting Conversion Date issue to and in the names of the Bondholder the number o f new Shares which are necessary in order to fulfil the Issuer's obligations to issue new Shares to the respective Bondholder.
The terms and conditions set out in Attachment 2 shall (to the extent applicable) apply for any conversion o f Bonds to Shares according to this Clause.
11 Payments
11.1 Payment mechanics
11.1.1 The Issuer shall pay all amounts due to the Bondholders under the Bonds and this Bond Agreements by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in connection with its securities account in the Securities Register.
11.1.2
Payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank which holds the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, but if the paying bank and the receiving bank are the same, payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, sec however Clause 11.2.
