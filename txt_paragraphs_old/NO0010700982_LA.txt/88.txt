10.5 Mandatory Redemption / Total Loss
10.5.2 Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than 60 (sixty) days following the Total Loss Event, redeem at 100% (one hundred per cent) o f Face Value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amounts (the "Total Loss Event Redemption Amount") (A) 100% (one hundred per cent) of the Outstanding Bonds if such Total Loss Event relates to all Rigs, or (B) 30% (thirty per cent) of the Outstanding Bonds pro rata for each Rig, if such Total Loss Event relates to 1 (one) or more, but not all, Rigs.
10.5.3 Upon the occurrence of several Mandatory Prepayment Events simultaneously, the Issuer shall only be obliged to pay 1 (one) redemption amount. For the avoidance of doubt, upon the simultaneous occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event and a Total Loss Event, the relevant redemption amount shall be the lower of the Mandatory Redemption Amount and the Total Loss Event Redemption Amount.
10.5.4 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.5, (i) the entire amount standing to the credit of the Escrow Account, the Rig Owner Earnings Accounts and the Issuer Debt Service Account, (ii) damages payments under any Construction Contract, (iii) other amounts received under the Project Documents, or (iv) any insurance proceeds, may be applied towards repayment o f the Bonds.
