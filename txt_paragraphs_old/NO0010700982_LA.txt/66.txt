6.3 Conditions Precedent for First Disbursement
6.3.1 In addition to the requirements set out in Clause 6.2 above, release of funds from the Escrow Account to the Issuer in respect of the First Disbursement is subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following, in form and substance reasonably satisfactory to it, and having issued a release notice to the relevant Account Bank:
(a)
satisfactory evidence that the Existing Bonds will be fully repaid and all Existing
Security in relation to the Existing Bonds will be released simultaneously with or, in
the Bond Trustee's discretion, as soon as practical after, the First Disbursement; and
(b) all legal opinions reasonably requested by the Bond Trustee required to be executed in accordance with this Clause 6.3.1 have been received in form and substance reasonably satisfactory to the Bond Trustee.
