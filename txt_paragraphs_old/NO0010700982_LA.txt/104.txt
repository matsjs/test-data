(d) Internal Loan
(i) Each Internal Loan shall be evidenced by an Internal Loan Agreement.
(ii) The rights of the Issuer under the Internal Loans shall be subject to the Assignments of Internal Loans.
(iii) Each Internal Loan shall stipulate that it shall (a) be repayable immediately and in full upon acceleration of the Bonds following an Event of Default, (b) bear interest at a rate at least equal to the interest rate described in Clause 9 above, and (c) shall have an amortisation profile at a minimum mirroring the amortisation profile of the Bonds, as described in Clause 10 above.
(e)
Parent Subordinated Loans
(i) Parent Subordinated Loans shall (i) be fully subordinated to the Bonds, (ii) have a maturity date later than the Maturity Date, and (iii) any fees and/or accrued interest shall be accumulated and added to the principal of such Parent Subordinated Loan during the term o f the Bonds.
