13.7 Financial Covenants The following financial covenants shall be complied with at any time:
Liquidity
The Issuer shall, on a consolidated basis, at all times have a minimum Liquidity of USD 5,000,000 (US Dollars five million) per Rig (as applicable).
(b)
Current Ratio
The Charterer shall at all times maintain a Current Ratio of at least 1:1.
(c)
Asset Coverage Ratio
The Issuer shall on a consolidated basis, at all times maintain a minimum Asset Coverage Ratio o f 120% (one hundred and twenty per cent).
