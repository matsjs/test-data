10.5 Mandatory Redemption / Total Loss
10.5.1 Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring, the Issuer shall (i) promptly in case an Event of Default has occurred and been notified in accordance with the provisions of Clause 15, and otherwise (ii) within 30 (thirty) days following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event, redeem (A) if such Mandatory Prepayment Event relates to all Rigs or in the case of an Event of Default, 100% (one hundred per cent) of the Outstanding Bonds, or (B) if such Mandatoiy Prepayment Event relates to 1 (one) or more, but not all, Rigs, 30% (thirty per cent) of the Outstanding Bonds pro rata for each Rig, in each case, at a price corresponding to the Call Option price at that time (the "Mandatory Redemption Amount"). For the avoidance of doubt, the redemption price shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date the redemption is carried out.
