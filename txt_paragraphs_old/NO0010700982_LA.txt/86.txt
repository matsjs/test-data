10.4 Change of Control
10.4.1 Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at a price of 101% (one hundred and one per cent) of Face Value (plus accrued interest).
