If the Bond Trustee or the Paying Agent receives a payment that is insufficient to discharge all the amounts then due and payable under the Finance Documents, that payment shall be applied in the following order:
Firstly, in or towards payment of any unpaid fees, costs, liabilities and expenses of the
Bond Trustee under the Finance Documents;
(b)
Secondly, in or towards payment of any accrued interest due but unpaid under the
Bond Agreement, pro rata and without any preference or priority of any kind; and
(c)
Thirdly, in or towards payment o f any principal due but unpaid under the Bond
Agreement, pro rata and without any preference or priority of any kind.
