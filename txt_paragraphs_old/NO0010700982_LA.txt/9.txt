"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks are open for general business, and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo and New York.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the Payment Date occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments o f interest and/or principal (as the case may be) will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day (No adjustments o f Business Day).
