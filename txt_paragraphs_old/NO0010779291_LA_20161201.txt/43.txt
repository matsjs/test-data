13.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Intra-group transactions
All transactions between any companies in the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
(b) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company, enter into any transaction with any related party except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(c) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
