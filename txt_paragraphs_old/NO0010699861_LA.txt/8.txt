"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 19.2.
"EBITDA" means, on a consolidated basis for the Group, on a trailing four quarter basis (the "Relevant Period"), earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortization, stock-based compensation, foreign exchange gains or losses, gains or losses on sale of equipment, and impairment of accounts, receivables or fixed assets; and
(a) including EBITDA of a member of the Group for the Relevant Period (or attributable to a business or assets acquired during the Relevant Period) prior to it becoming a member of the Group or, as the case may be, prior to the acquisition of the business or assets;
(b) excluding EBITDA attributable to any member of the Group (or to any business or assets) disposed of during the Relevant Period; and
(c) excluding any Exceptional Items.
"Escrow Account" means an account(s) in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 16.1.
