The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3.2
Purpose and utilization
First tranche
The net proceeds of the first tranche of Bonds (net of fees and legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses incurred by the Manager or Bond Trustee in connection with the Bond Issue or the Transaction) shall be used toward part financing of the Transaction.
Tap Issues
The net proceeds of any subsequent Tap Issues (net of fees and legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses incurred by the Manager or Bond Trustee in connection with the Bond Issue or the Transaction) shall be used toward financing the future growth of the Group within its business strategy.
