"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 18.4.
"Security and Covenant Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 19.2.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
"Security Interests" means:
(a) The Share Pledges;
(b) The Guarantees;
(c) the Intra-Group Assignments; and
(d) the Escrow Account Pledge.
"Shareholder Loan" means a loan from a shareholder of the Issuer, fully subordinated to the Bond Issue and with no rights of acceleration for as long as the Bonds are outstanding and with a tenor exceeding Maturity Date.
