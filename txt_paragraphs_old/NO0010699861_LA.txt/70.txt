16.3 In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clause 16.1 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee shall declare the Outstanding Bonds including accrued interest, costs and expenses to be in default and due for immediate payment if:
(a) the Bond Trustee receives a demand in writing that a default shall be declared from Bondholders representing at least 1/5 of the Voting Bonds, and the Bondholders' Meeting has not decided on other solutions, or
(b) the Bondholders' Meeting has with simple majority decided to declare the Outstanding Bonds in default and due for payment.

16.4 In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clause 16.1 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee shall, upon instruction of a simple majority of the Bondholders' Meeting, have the right to make direct payments to lease providers under any lease facility entered into by any Obligor to cover any overdue amount in order to prevent termination of such lease facility. Any such payment shall be made in consideration of the assignment by the lease provider of the corresponding claim against the lessee, and shall satisfy any overdue amount under such Lease Facility in full, without any discount.
