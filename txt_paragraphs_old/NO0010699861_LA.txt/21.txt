'Transaction" means the acquisition of the Initial Subsidiaries.
"Vendor Note" means a subordinated vendor note issued by the Issuer on behalf of the Bidco to be established upon completion of the Transaction.
"Vendor Note Undertaking" means an undertaking in writing provided by the CapMan Investors and CapMan NoA Holding AS to the Issuer and the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) stating that the CapMan Investors and/or CapMan NoA Holding AS will repay the Vendor Note on maturity and, upon repayment of the Vendor Note, either (i) convert its recourse claim against the Issuer into equity of the Issuer, (ii) discharge its recourse claim against the Issuer, or (iii) provide a subordination undertaking to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) on terms satisfactory to the Bond Trustee whereby the recourse claim is fully subordinated to the Bonds and with no right of repayment, prepayment or acceleration until the Bonds have been irrevocably repaid in full.
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds, less Bonds belonging to;
(a) the Issuer;
(b) either of the CapMan Investors;
(c) any person or entity with Decisive Influence over either (a) or (b) above;
(d) any person over which either (a) or (b) above has Decisive Influence; and
(e) any other entity in the CapMan Group.
