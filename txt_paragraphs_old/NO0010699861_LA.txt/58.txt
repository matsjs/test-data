The Issuer shall not, and it shall ensure that no Group Company shall, cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Group from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(e) Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not, and it shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its own or any Group Company's assets or operations to any person not being a member of the Group, unless:
(i) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
(f) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and it shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
