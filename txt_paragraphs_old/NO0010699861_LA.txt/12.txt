"Inter Creditor Agreement" means an inter creditor agreement between the Bond Trustee and a creditor under a Working Capital Facility (the 'W CF Creditor") containing a buy-out option for the benefit of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the bondholders), on terms reasonably satisfactory to the Bond Trustee, pursuant to which: (i) the WCF Creditor shall undertake to promptly notify the Bond Trustee of any payment default under the RCF; and (ii) in the event that an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing and subject to the instruction of a majority decision by the Bondholders' Meeting, the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the bondholders) shall have the option to, within 25 Business Days following receipt of such notice, pay the outstanding amount under the Working Capital Facility to the WCF Creditor against transfer and assignment of the WCF Creditor's rights under the Working Capital Facility (including, but not limited to, security) and assumption of the WCF Creditor's obligations under the Working Capital Facility (the "Buy-Out Option "). The Buy Out Option shall be governed by Norwegian law, and the Bond Trustee shall be authorized to approve the Buy-Out Option on behalf of the bondholders.
"Interest Cover Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Interest Expenses.
"Interest Payment Date" means 20 March, 20 June, 20 September and 20 December each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date in the second financial quarter of 2014 and later, drawn up according to GAAP.
"Intra-Group Assignment" means the first priority assignments over all claims under the Intra-Group Loan Agreement, in a form satisfactory to the Bond Trustee.
"Intra-Group Loan Agreement" means the loan agreement between the Issuer and other Group companies under which all intra-group borrowing of the Group is to be made.
