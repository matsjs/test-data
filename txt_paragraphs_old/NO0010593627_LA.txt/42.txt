(a) Dividends The Issuer shall not during the term of the Bond Issue, declare or make any dividend payments, repurchase of shares or other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer), repay any shareholder loans or grant any loans or other distributions to its shareholders exceeding 50 per cent of the Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year, however always provided that the Equity Ratio is, and remains immediately after such distribution, above 40 per cent for the Group on a consolidated basis, Any unutilized portion of the permitted dividend pursuant to the above may not be earned forward to any subsequent financial year, Notwithstanding the limitations above, the Issuer shall always be entitled to repurchase its own shares to cover its obligations under the employees' and board members' (of the Issuer) share option scheme,
The Issuer shall ensure that (unless the Bond Trustee or the Bondholders' meeting (as the case may be) has agreed otherwise in writing) the Issuer on a consolidated Group basis maintains an Equity Ratio of minimum 30% at all times, and that the Equity Ratio is measured on each Quarter Date.
