10.3.1 Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price of 100 per cent of par plus accrued interest thereon.
10.3.2
The Put Option must be exercised within 60 days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bondholders of a Change of Control, Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control has taken place and shall be deemed given when the Issuer has issued a press release to the effect and published such on its website, and simultaneously sent such notice to the Bond Trustee,
The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Paying Agent (via the Account Manager), The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the exercise of the Put Option. The Payment Date of the Put Option shall be - 75 ~ days after the Issuer's Change o f Control notice,
10.3.3
On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be pre-paid, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.3.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date,
