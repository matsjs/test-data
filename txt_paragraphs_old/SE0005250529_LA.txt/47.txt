22 Allocation of payments
22.1 Application of proceeds following an Event of Default
All payments by the Company or the Guarantor (as applicable) relating to the Bonds and the Security Documents following a termination of the Bonds in accordance with Section 16 and any proceeds received from an enforcement of the security under the Security Documents shall be distributed in the following order of priority, in accordance with the instructions of the Bond Trustee:
(a) first, in or towards payment pro rata of (i) unpaid fees, costs, expenses and indemnities payable by the Company to the Bond Trustee, (ii) other costs, expenses and indemnities relating to the acceleration of the Bonds or the protection of the Holders' rights, (iii) any non-reimbursed costs incurred by the Bond Trustee for
