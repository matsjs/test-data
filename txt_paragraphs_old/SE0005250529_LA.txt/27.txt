11.1 The Bonds will bear interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal Amount from, but excluding, the Issue Date up to and including the relevant redemption date.
11.2 Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of interest in respect of the Bonds shall be made quarterly in arrears to the Holders on each Interest Payment Date for the preceding Interest Period and shall be calculated on a 30/360-days basis.
12 Default interest
12.1 If the Company fails to pay any amount due under these Terms and Conditions, the Company shall pay default interest on such amount at an annual rate corresponding to the Interest Rate plus 2.00 per cent, from, but excluding, the date such payment was due, up to and including, the date of actual payment. Accrued default interest shall not be capitalised.
12.2 If the delay is due to a Force Majeure Event for the Company, the Bond Trustee, the CSD or the Issuing Agent, the default interest shall not exceed the Interest Rate.
13 The Group Companies' purchase of Bonds
13.1 Any Group Company may at any time purchase Bonds. Bonds held by a Group Company may at such Group Company's discretion be retained, sold or, if held by the Company, cancelled.
13.2 Bonds held by a Group Company, or any of its Affiliates, in accordance with Section 13.1 or 15 will, as long as they are held by a Group Company or any of its Affiliates, not carry the right to attend and vote at the Holders' meetings and will not be taken into account, inter alia, for the purposes of Section 17.4 or 18.
