(a) The Trustee shall represent the Bondholders in accordance with the Finance Documents, including, inter alia, holding the Transaction Security and the Guarantees pursuant to the Security Documents and the Guarantee Agreement on behalf of the Bondholders and, where relevant, enforcing the Transaction Security and/or the Guarantees on behalf of the Bondholders. The Trustee is not responsible for the content, valid execution, legal validity or enforceability of the Finance Documents.
