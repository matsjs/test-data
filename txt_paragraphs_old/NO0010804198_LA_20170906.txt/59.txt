13.19
(a) Liquidity
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains minimum Liquidity of not less than EUR 15,000,000.
(b) Leverage Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Leverage Ratio of maximum of:
(i) 5.50x up to and including 31 December 2017;
(ii) 5.00x up to and including 31 December 2018;
(iii) 4.25x up to and including 31 December 2019; and
(iv) 3.50x any time thereafter.
