13.9 Disposals of assets/business
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
(a) the transaction is carried out at a fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(b) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
