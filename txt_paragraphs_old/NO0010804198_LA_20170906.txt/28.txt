2.2 Tenor of the Bonds
2.3 The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.Use of proceeds (a) The net proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue shall be used to partly finance the Acquisition.
(b) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the issuance of any Additional Bonds for such purpose as determined in the term sheet for a Tap Issue.
