"Subordinated Loan" means subordinated loans provided by the shareholders of the Issuer to the Issuer. With the exception of any Permitted Shareholder Loans, any Subordinated Loan shall (i) be fully subordinated to the Bonds, (ii) have a maturity date later than the Maturity Date, and (iii) any fees and/or any accrued interest shall be accumulated and added to the principal of such loan facility during the term of the Bonds.
"Subsidiary" means a company over which another company has Decisive Influence.
"Total Assets" means the aggregate book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's total assets in accordance with SFRS, as illustrated in the then most recent audited consolidated annual financial statements or the latest quarterly financial reports (as the case may be).
"Total Loss Event" means an actual or constructive total loss of the Vessel.
