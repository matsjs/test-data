Interpretation
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"ABSA Amount" means the amount standing to the credit of the Additional Bond Security Account.
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account, the Debt Service Account and the Additional Bond Security Account.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Additional Bond Security Account" means a USD account established by the Issuer with the Paying Agent to be used for satisfying the Dan Swift Coverage Ratio, pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
