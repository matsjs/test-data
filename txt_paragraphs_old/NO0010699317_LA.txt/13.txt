"Group" means the Issuer and all its existing and future Subsidiaries (as may be incorporated from time to time).
"Group Company" means the Issuer or any of its existing and future Subsidiaries.
"Group Market Value" means the aggregate fair market value o f the Group Vessels in USD determined as the arithmetic mean of two (2) independent valuations of each o f the Group Vessels, on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms as between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts for employment, both obtained from independent and well-reputed sale and purchase brokers familiar with the market for the Group Vessels, appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee (such brokers as (a) are being used under the Senior Bank Facility or (b) the names listed in Attachment 3 to the Bond Agreement to be considered pre-approved). Such valuation shall be made semi-annually in connection with Reporting or upon request by the Bond Trustee. The cost of such valuation shall be for the account of the Issuer.
"Group Vessels" means the Vessel and any delivered Additional Unit owned by a Group Company.
"HI VI Invest Sierra Limited" means the fund named HI VI Invest Sierra Limited being 100% owned and controlled by Hitec Vision.
"Hitec Vision" means the Norwegian private equity fund HitecVision.
"Interest Payment Date" means 18 March, 18 June, 18 September and 18 December each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer and DS Singapore for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to SFRS.
