Conditions Precedent
Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Escrow Account will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received the documents listed below, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a)
this Bond Agreement, duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Issuer to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents;
(c)
a power of attorney from the Issuer to relevant individuals for their
execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant
register or similar documentation evidencing such individuals' authorisation
