10.4 Mandatory Prepayment
(a)
in the case of Mandatory Prepayment Event (i) or (ii), no later than upon
receipt of the proceeds of such sale or disposal; or
(b) in the case of Mandatory Prepayment Event (iii) or (iv), not later than 30 calendar days following such relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event (in each case unless there is an Event of Default in which case it will be promptly),
redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at a price a price equal to the prevailing call price outlined under the Issuer's Call Option set out in Clause 10.2.1.
For the avoidance of doubt, the aforesaid redemption prices shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date o f prepayment.
10.4.2
A Mandatory Prepayment Event shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders as soon as possible after it has occurred. The Issuer shall send a new notice at least five Business Days prior to when the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment is expected to place where it informs o f the settlement date.
