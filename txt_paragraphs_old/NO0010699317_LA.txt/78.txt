13.10 Vessel Covenants
(a) Maintenance o fInsurances
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of insurances of the Vessel and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times, hereunder to retain the Vessel in class. During operation of the Vessel, the Issuer shall ensure that the charterer runs proper maintenance of the Vessel according to planned maintenance system.
The Vessel shall also be adequately insured (including war risk) against Hull & Machinery risks for at least 120% of the aggregate of the Net Senior Bank Facility Amount and the outstanding amount under the Finance Documents (less the ABSA Amount), a third party liability insurance as per industry standards, mortgagee interest insurance, expropriation risk and any additional insurance required under any law.
The insurances shall be in accordance with the Nordic Marine Insurance Plan or other insurances with no less favourable terms.
