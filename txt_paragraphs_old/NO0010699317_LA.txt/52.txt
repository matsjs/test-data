10.4.3
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.4.4 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.4, the entire amount on the Accounts or any of the insurance proceeds may be used as part payment in relation to the Mandatory Prepayment.
10.4.5 Upon the occurrence of several Mandatory Prepayment Events, the Issuer shall only be obliged to pay one redemption amount.
10.4.6 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
