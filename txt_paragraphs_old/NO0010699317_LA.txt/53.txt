10.5.1
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than ninety (90) calendar days following the Total Loss Event, redeem 100% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% o f par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.5.2
A Total Loss Event shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders as soon as possible after it has occurred. The Issuer shall send a new notice at least five Business Days prior to when the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment is expected to place where it informs of the settlement date.
10.5.3
On the settlement date of the Total Loss Prepayment, the Issuer shall pay to each o f the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect o f each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.5.4 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.5, the entire amount on the Accounts or any o f the insurance proceeds may be used as part payment in relation to the Total Loss Prepayment.
10.5.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
