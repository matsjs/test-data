Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer together with accrued and uncapitalised interest thereon.
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem the Bonds in whole but not in part at any time from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at a price equal to 100% of par value (to include previously capitalized interest and together with accrued and uncapitalised interest on the redeemed amount). Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.3 10.3.1
Mandatory Prepayment Event Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall promptly redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at a price according to the Call Options as set out in Clause
10.2.
10.3.2
For the avoidance of doubt, the aforesaid redemption prices shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date of prepayment.
10.3.3 Upon the occurrence of several Mandatory Prepayment Events, the Issuer shall only be obliged to pay one redemption amount.
