2.3 2.3.1
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bonds (net of legal costs, taxes, fees to the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall be employed towards (i) funding the working capital requirements of the Issuer, including in relation to the day-today management and operation and the Vessel, and (ii) the Issuer's costs obligations to Norsk Tillitsmann ASA as bond trustee for the Existing Bondholders under the Existing Bonds, including, without limitation costs in relation to the judicial sale of the Vessel in Aruba in order to maximize sales proceeds recoverable by the Issuer.
2.3.2 The employment of the proceeds of the Bond Issue shall be evidenced to the satisfaction of the Bond Trustee.
