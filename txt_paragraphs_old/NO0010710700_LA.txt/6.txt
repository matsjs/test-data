"Banking Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the period end date occurs on a day that is not a Banking Day, and if such date is not a Banking Day, payments of interest will be made on the first following day that is a Banking Day (No Adjustments of Banking Day).
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 16.
"Bonds" means the debt instruments issued by the Issuer pursuant to this Bond Agreement.
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Change of Control Event" means circumstances in which any of the following occur or are subsisting, in each case without the consent of the Bond Trustee: (i) after the date of this Agreement, any person or group acquires a majority of the voting rights in the share capital of the Issuer or obtains the right to appoint a majority of the members of the board of directors of the Issuer or obtains the ability to direct the material affairs of the Issuer; (ii) the board of the Issuer is replaced; or (iii) the receiver currently appointed over the shares of the Issuer is removed from its position or any steps are taken to vary its status.
"Clause" means a clause of this Bond Agreement.
"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
