13.3 Issuer's negative covenants The Issuer shall not:
(a) declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, make any other distribution to its shareholder (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any share issued by any party);
(b) grant any loans, guarantees, or other financial assistance to any party other than the Security Interests;
(c) engage in any transaction with any party unless such transaction is on bona fide arms' length terms;
(d) create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets (including the Vessel) or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for (a) the Security Interests, (b) the Security Interests created under the Original Finance Documents (as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement), (c) any Security arising by operation of law or (c) any Security granted over an asset which has been provided on retention of title terms in the ordinary course of business (including collateral in connection with credit purchases of goods and services);
(e) incur or permit to remain outstanding, any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than the Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue and the Existing Bonds;
