11.2
If the Bonds are declared due and payable pursuant to Condition 11.1, the Company shall redeem the Bonds at a redemption amount equal to the Bonds' Nominal Amount plus a surcharge of three (3) per cent of the Nominal Amount plus the accrued interest, if any, pursuant to Condition 4.
