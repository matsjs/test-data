10 Special undertakings
10.1 So long as any Bonds remain outstanding, the Company undertakes:
(a) not to (i) make any dividend on any Shares, (ii) repurchase any Shares, (iii) redeem share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to the holders of Shares, (iv) other similar distribution to the holders of Shares and (v) to procure that each Group Company which is not wholly-owned, directly or indirectly, by the Company does not undertake any such actions.
