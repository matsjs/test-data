INTERPRETATION
Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account and the Debt Service Reserve Account.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Assignment of Insurances" means a Second Priority assignment in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) of any insurance related to the Vessels.
"Attachment" means the attachments and schedules to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
