Upon a Mandatory Redemption Event occurring, the Issuer shall (i) no later than on the closing date in respect of the relevant transaction constituting the relevant Mandatory Redemption Event, but (ii) in case of a Total Loss Event, on or about the day the relevant insurance proceeds are paid out, but in no event later than 180 days after the occurrence of the Total Loss Event, redeem:
(ii) NOK 110 million (i.e. 40.00 % of NOK 275 million) if related to a second Vessel;
(iii) the remaining Outstanding Bonds if related to the third Vessel;
(iv) 80.00 % of the Outstanding Bonds if related to two Vessels being sold; and
(v) 100.00 % of the Outstanding Bonds if related to all three Vessels being sold.
10.4.2
Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Redemption Event, other than a Total Loss Event as set out in Clause 10.4.4, the Outstanding Bonds shall be redeemed at the following price if occurring:
(i) anytime from the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling in May 2015, at a price equal to 108.00 % of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(ii) anytime from and including the Interest Payment Date in May 2015 to , but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling in May 2016, at a price equal to 106.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
