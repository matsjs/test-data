6.1
Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Issuer will be subject to the Bond
Trustee having received the documents in Schedule 3 Part I (Conditions Precedent to Issue
Date) and Part II (Conditions Precedent to Disbursement), in form and substance
satisfactory to it.
6.2
The documents in Schedule 3 Part I (Conditions Precedent to Issue Date) shall be delivered
prior to and at the latest on the Issue Date.
