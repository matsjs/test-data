Vessel covenants The Vessel covenants shall apply to the Issuer at all times during the term of the Bond Issue and the Issuer shall ensure and procure that: (a) the Vessels maintain their class free of overdue recommendations or notations;
(b) the Bond Trustee shall have the right to inspect the Vessels if requested;
(c) the Vessels maintain their flags, names and registry (however so that the flag and registry of the Vessels may be changed with the Bond Trustee's prior written approval (such approval not to be unreasonably withheld));
(d) the Vessels are kept in a good and safe condition and maintained and repaired consistently with prudent ownership and good industry standards;
