12.10 Mergers and demergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its subsidiaries, enter into any merger or demerger.
12.11 Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its subsidiaries, acquire any shares or other rights in any entity or a business or undertaking (or, in each case any interest in any of them). Further, the Issuer shall not make any other material investment or acquisition of any assets not being made in the ordinary course of trading.
