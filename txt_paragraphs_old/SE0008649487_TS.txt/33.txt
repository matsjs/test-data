13.2 Other Obligations
The Issuer and the Guarantors does not comply with any provision under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out in Clause 13.1 (Non-Payment), provided that the Agent have requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within ten (10) Business Days from such request (if the failure or violation is not capable of being remedied, the Agent may declare the Bonds payable without such prior written request).
13.3 Cross Default
Any Financial Indebtedness of a Group Company is not paid when due as extended by any originally applicable grace period, or any creditor or any Group Company is entitled to declare any financial indebtedness of any Group Company due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this Clause 13.3 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness, that has fallen due or may be declared due and payable is less than EUR 1,000,000.
