12.12 Slovenian Portfolio
The Issuer shall procure that:
(a) all proceeds from the Slovenian Portfolio is paid to the Bank Account(s) (for the avoidance of doubt, the Group may dispose of the Bank Account(s) and the account shall not be blocked); and
(b) reasonable endeavours are used to seek to maximise recovery from the Slovenian Portfolio and the relevant Group Company shall perform its obligations in respect of the services in relation to the Slovenian Portfolio with professionalism using reasonable skill and care expected of it as a diligent servicer.
