"Business Day Convention" means the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day.
"Calculation Principles" the calculation of the Incurrence Test shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than two (2) months prior to the incurrence of a Subsequent Bond Issue or a Restricted Payment (that requires that the Incurrence Test is met), and adjusted so that any assets acquired with the Financial Indebtedness (as applicable) shall be included calculated pro forma.
"Call Option Amount" means
(a) 103.00 per cent, of the Nominal Amount if the call option is exercised on or after the First Call Date up to (but not including) the date falling thirty-six (36) months after the First Issue Date;
(b) 101.875 per cent, of the Nominal Amount if the call option is exercised on or after the date falling thirty-six (36) months after the First Issue Date up to (but not including) the date falling forty-two (42) months after the First Issue Date;
(c) 100.75 per cent, of the Nominal Amount if the call option is exercised on or after the date falling forty-two (42) months after the First Issue Date up to (but not including) the Final Redemption Date.
