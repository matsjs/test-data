This agreement (the "Loan Agreement") has been entered into on 16 April 2007 between Petrojack ASA (Company No 987 358 920) as borrower (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (Company No 963 342 624) as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee").
The Loan Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Loan Trustee or the Borrower. The Borrower shall ensure that the Loan Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Loan.
All Bond transfers shall be subject to the terms of this Loan Agreement and the Trustee and all Bond transferees shall, in taking transfer of Bonds, be deemed to have accepted the terms of the Loan Agreement where by all such transferees shall automatically become parties to the Loan Agreement upon completed transfer having been registered in the VPS, without any further action required or formalities to be complied with.
1 Definitions
Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Assignment of Construction Contracts:
a third priority assignment in each of the Construction Contracts, in the maximum amount of the Loan plus accrued interest and expenses, in favour of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), ranking with priority after the Prior Security.
Banking Day:
a day when the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions.
