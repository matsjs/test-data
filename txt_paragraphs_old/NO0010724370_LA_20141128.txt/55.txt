The Issuer undertakes to maintain Decisive Influence of the Guarantor and the Material Subsidiaries.
The Issuer shall ensure that no Group Company shall novate, transfer, sell or otherwise dispose of any working interest in any PSC unless:
(a) the transaction is earned out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(b) such transaction does not have a Material Adverse Effect; and
(c) if applicable, the Bonds are partially or fully redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.4, above.
13.4.4 Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (including guarantees) other than the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.4.5 Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist Security over any of its present or future assets (including shares in Subsidiaries) or its revenues, other than the Permitted Security.
