The restrictions provided for in this Clause 13.4.7 shall not apply for obligations undertaken by a Group Company related to Permitted Financial Indebtedness (other than a Reserve Based Lending Facility). 13.4.8 No amendments to material agreements The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, make any amendments to the GSS PSC, the Framework Agreement, the Cooperation Agreement or any other material agreements if such amendments would have a Material Adverse Effect. 13.4.9 Hedging policy The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, enter into hedging arrangements for speculative purposes. 13.5 Financial covenants 13.5.1 Equity Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains an Equity Ratio of minimum 45%. 13.5.2 Interest Coverage Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains an Interest Coverage Ratio of minimum: (a) 2.00x as of 31 December 2015; (b) 3.00x as of 30 June 2016; and (c) 4.00x any time thereafter. 13.5.3 Leverage Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Leverage Ratio not exceeding: (a) 3.5Ox as of 31 December 2015; (b) 3.25x as of 30 June 2016; and

13.5.4 Minimum Liquidity
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Liquidity of minimum USD 20,000,000.
13.5.5 Calculations
The Issuer undertakes to, at all times, comply with the financial covenants in this Clause 13.5, and such compliance shall be measured in connection with the publication of its financial reports under Clause 13.2.1(c) and (d), and certified by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 13.2.3.
