2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 88,000,000 (U.S. Dollar Eighty Eight Million).
The Face Value is USD 100,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "10.00 per cent. Green Dragon Gas Ltd. Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2014/2017".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0724370.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied (i) to fund Production Development Costs related to the GSS Block, (ii) to repay the Mandolin Bonds (together with accrued interest and costs thereon), and (iii) to fund general corporate and working capital requirements of the Group.
