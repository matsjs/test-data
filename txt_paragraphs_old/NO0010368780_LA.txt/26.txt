(a) (i) for the period from Disbursement Date to, but not including interest Payment Date in May 2008, each Bondholder may have some or all of his Bonds redeemed by the Borrower (put option) at 150 % of their principal amount together with accrued interest; and (ii) for the period from and including interest Payment Date until (but not included) Maturity Date have the Bonds redeemed by the Borrower at 101 % of par together with accrued interest, ((i) and (ii) described as "Put Option")
The Put Option must be exercised within one month after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 16.1 (h).
The Put Option may be exercised within the Exercise Period by the Bondholders by giving written notice to the Paying Agent via its VPS account manager. The put date shall be 10 Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the pre-payment request.
(b) for a period of one month following a Change of Control Event (but within the Exercise Period) each Bondholder may require a Conversion of some or all of his Bonds at a conversion price calculated in accordance with the following formula:
COCCP = OCP / (1 + (CP x (c / t))), where: - COCCP means the Change of Control Conversion Price, - OCP means the Conversion Price in effect on the relevant Conversion Date
(disregarding the application of this provision), - CP means the conversion premium of 30 % (expressed as a fraction), - c means the number of days from and including the date of the Change of Control
Event to but excluding the Maturity Date, and - t means the number of days from and including the Disbursement Date to but
excluding the Maturity Date.
