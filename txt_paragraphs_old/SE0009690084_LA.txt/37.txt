12.1 Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than:
a. 3.25 from the First Issue Date until 31 December 2017;
b. 3.00 from 1 January 2018 until 31 December 2018;
c. 2.75 from 1 January 2019 until 31 December 2019;
d. 2.50 from 1 January 2020 until the Final Redemption Date; and
(b) no Event of Default is continuing or would occur upon the incurrence of new Financial Indebtedness or the making of a Restricted Payment.
