>The Notes will be the senior secured obligations of the Issuer and will rank equally in right of payment with all existing and future senior indebtedness of the Issuer that is not subordinated in right of payment to the Notes, will be senior in right of payment to all existing and future indebtedness of the Issuer that is subordinated in right of payment to the Notes and will be effectively senior to all of the Issuer's issued existing and future unsecured indebtedness to the extent of the assets securing the Notes.
>The Notes will be guaranteed on a senior secured basis by certain of the Issuer's subsidiaries (the "Guarantors"). The guarantee of the Notes by each Guarantor (each a "Guarantee" and, collectively, the "Guarantees") will rank equally in right of payment with all existing and future indebtedness of such Guarantor that is not subordinated in right of payment to such Guarantee and will be senior in right of payment to any and all of the existing and future indebtedness of such Guarantor that is subordinated in right of payment to such Guarantee.
