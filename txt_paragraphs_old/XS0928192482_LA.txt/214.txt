Option to purchase
The Noteholders and Pari Passu Creditors, which are holders of certain issued debt securities, may, after the acceleration event or the enforcement of any of the Collateral, and subject to various conditions set out in the Intercreditor Agreement (including the grant of an acceptable indemnity against clawback), exercise an option to purchase all (but not part only) of the Credit Facilities Liabilities and Priority Hedging Liabilities in full and at par.
