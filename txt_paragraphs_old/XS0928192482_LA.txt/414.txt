Basis of preparation
These consolidated financial statements are prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS), as adopted by the EU and SIC/IFRIC interpretations as applicable on 31 December, 2012.
The consolidated financial statements are prepared under historical cost convention except for the financial liabilities and assets (including derivative instruments) recognized at fair value through profit or loss, available-for-sale investments and assets and liabilities classified as held-for-sale in accordance with IFRS 5 at the closing date.
Items included in the financial statements of each of the Group's entity are measured using the currency of the primary economic environment in which that entity operates (the functional currency). The consolidated financial statements are presented in euro, which is the Parent Company's functional currency. The presentation is in millions of euro.
