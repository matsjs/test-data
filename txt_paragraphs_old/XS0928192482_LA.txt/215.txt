Release of Security and Guarantees--Distressed disposals
In circumstances where a distressed disposal is being effected, the Intercreditor Agreement will provide that the Security Agent is authorized:
(a) to release the Collateral or any other claim over the relevant asset;
(b) if the asset which is disposed of consists of shares in the capital of a Debtor, to release (a) that Debtor and any subsidiary of that Debtor from all or any part of its borrowing liabilities, guaranteeing liabilities (including in relation to the Notes) and certain other liabilities; (b) any Collateral granted over that Debtor's assets and the assets of any of its subsidiaries; and (c) any other claim of a subordinated creditor, Debtor or intra-group lender over that Debtor's assets or over the assets of any subsidiary of that Debtor;
(c) if the asset which is disposed of consists of shares in the capital of any holding company of a Debtor, to release (a) that holding company and any subsidiary of that holding company from all or any part of its borrowing liabilities, guaranteeing liabilities (including in relation to the Notes) and certain other liabilities; (b) any Collateral granted over the assets of any subsidiary of that holding company; and (c) any other claim of a subordinated creditor, Debtor or intra-group lender over the assets of any subsidiary of that holding company;
