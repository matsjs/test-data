10. Commitment Fee
(a) Notwithstanding Clause 9 (Interest) above, if the Transaction Lapse Event occurs, each Initial Bond shall carry Commitment Fee from, and including, the First Issue Date up to, but excluding, the date of the Transaction Lapse Event. The Issuer shall pay the accrued Commitment Fee to the Bondholders no later than the date falling ten Business Days after the Transaction Lapse Event.
