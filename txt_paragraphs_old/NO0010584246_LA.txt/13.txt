"Quarterly Financial Reports" means the unaudited unconsolidated management accounts of the Issuer and the Parent as of each Quarter Date, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary. For the Parent, such reports shall be both on an unconsolidated and consolidated basis.
"Rig Management Agreement" means the at all times applicable agreement(s) with the Rig Manager related to the marketing, technical management and operation o f the Rig.
"Rig Management Assignment" means the assignment of all earnings and rights of the Issuer under any relevant Rig Management Agreement (to the extent permitted by applicable law).
