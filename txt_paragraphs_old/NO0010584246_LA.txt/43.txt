Applicationfollowing an Event o f Default
On either (i) completion of sale o f the Rig, either by forced auction or private treaty, or (ii) receipt of monies from insurance proceeds or otherwise, as the case may be, such proceeds or monies shall be applied as follows and in the order mentioned:
(i) First; in respect of all costs and expenses whatsoever incurred in or about and incidental to the sale;
(ii) Second; in or towards satisfaction of all prior claims (being any claims, liabilities or debts owed or taking priority in respect of such proceeds over the security constituted by the Senior Security and the Security Interest) secured on the Rig;
(iii) Third; in or towards payment of all sums secured by the Senior Security;
(iv) Four; in or towards payment of all sums secured by the Security Interest; and
(v) Five; the balance, if any, shall be paid to the Issuer.
