10.3.4 Notwithstanding the above, the Put Option shall not apply in the event o f the Issuer's shares becoming listed on a securities exchange acceptable to the Bond Trustee (acting reasonably).
10.4.1 Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event other than a Total Loss Event occurring the Issuer shall redeem 100 % o f the Outstanding Bonds as on levels equal to the Issuer's Call Option at the payment date (however redemption level being 110% during the two first years after Issue Date when no Call Option applies) (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount), and provided that payment shall be made on or about the day the Issuer is receiving the proceeds following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event.
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this mandatory redemption clause, the entire amount on the Escrow Account may be used as partly payment for mandatory redemption.
10.4.2 The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory redemption, at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption.
10.4.3 On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
10.4.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
