1. INTERPRETATION 1.1 Definitions
"Accounts" means any of the Escrow Account, the Earnings Account, the Operating Account or the Bond Retention Account.
"Account Bank" means DnB NOR Bank ASA or any other first class international bank where the Bond Retention Account and the Escrow Account are held provided that the relevant security to be established over such accounts have been duly perfected.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Account Pledges" means the Escrow Account Pledge, the Bond Retention Account Pledge, the Operating Account Pledge and the Earnings Account Pledge.
"Assignment of Earnings" means the assignment of earnings payable to the Issuer and/or any intra-group charterer (as the case may be) (including earnings under any charters or bare-boat agreements for hire) related to the Rig, and the Issuer and/or any intra-group charterer (as the case may be) shall give notices and use its best endeavours to procure the acknowledgements of such assignment from its respective counterparties, including but not limited to under any employment contract with oil companies.
"Assignment of Rig Insurances" means the assignment of any relevant insurances related to the Rig and the equipment related thereto.
"Attachment" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
