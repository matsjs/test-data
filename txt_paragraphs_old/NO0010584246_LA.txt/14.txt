"Rig Mortgage" means the Bermudian mortgage over the Rig (including in all relevant equipment being legally part of the Rig under Bermudian law), to be supplemented with a separate deed of covenants if so required and as considered necessary by the Bond Trustee.
"Securities Register Act" means the Norwegian Act relating to Registration of Financial Instruments of 5 July 2002 No. 64.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee, unless any other legal entity is appointed as collateral agent pursuant to Clause 17.4.
"Security Documents" means any document establishing, recording, confirming or preserving any Security Interest relating to any Finance Document.
