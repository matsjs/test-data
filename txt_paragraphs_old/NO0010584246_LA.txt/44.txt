9. INTEREST
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate o f 13.00 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates, the first Interest Payment Date being 2 March 2011 (at which time the Bond Trustee shall release an amount equivalent to the first interest payment from the Bond Retention Account, which for the avoidance o f doubt, shall be funded at all times as set out in Clause 8.2(a))).
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
