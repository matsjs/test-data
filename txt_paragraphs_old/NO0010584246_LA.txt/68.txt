13.6 Rig Covenants
(a)
Maintenance and insurance
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance and insurance of the Rig and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times. The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig Manager (and any charterer if applicable) run proper maintenance of the Rig according to a pre-agreed planned maintenance systems.
The Rig shall be adequately insured (including war risk) against hull & machinery risks at least 120 % of the aggregate outstanding amounts under the Finance Documents and the Senior Debt, and a third party liability insurance as per industry standards, as well as any additional insurance required under any law or Charter Contract. The insurances, and any loss payee clause or similar provisions therein, shall be in accordance with the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan or other insurance plan with at least equally favourable terms (in the reasonable opinion of the Issuer).
(b)
Title
The Issuer will hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the Rig and the insurances taken out in respect o f the Rig, free of any and all Encumbrances except for those permitted under this Bond Agreement and the Senior Facility.
