28. Related party transactions
The parents Safari Finco 1 Oy and the ultimate parent Safari Luxco 2 S.A and Paroc Group Oy's subsidiaries are related parties to the Group. Further, members of the Board of Directors and key management, including the CEO, are also defined as related parties.
Transactions between the Company and its subsidiaries have been eliminated and are not therefore disclosed in this note. Details of the transactions carried out between the Group and other related parties are disclosed below.
The Group's parent company is Paroc Group Oy (incorporated in Finland). The immediate parent is Safari Finco 1 Oy (incorporated in Finland), which owns 100% of the Paroc Group Oy shares. The ultimate parent is Safari Luxco 1 S.A. (incorporated in Luxembourg).
