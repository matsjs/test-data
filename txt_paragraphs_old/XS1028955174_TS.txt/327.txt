The Intercreditor Agreement will contain provisions relating to future indebtedness that may be incurred by the members of the Issuer Group, which is permitted or not prohibited under the New Revolving Credit Facility, the Indenture, any existing Pari Passu Debt Document (as defined below) and any existing Senior Unsecured Notes Document (as defined below) to rank pari passu in right of payment with the liabilities under the New Revolving Credit Facility, the liabilities under the Notes, and any existing Pari Passu Liabilities (as defined below) and to be secured on the Collateral, subject to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement (such indebtedness being the ``Pari Passu Debt," the creditors in respect of such indebtedness being the ``Pari Passu Creditors", the liabilities of the Debtors in respect of such indebtedness being the ``Pari Passu Liabilities" and the documents creating or evidencing the Pari Passu Liabilities, the "Pari Passu Debt Documents").

The Intercreditor Agreement will also provide after the RCF Discharge Date, as defined below, for any credit facility constituting a "Credit Facility" under the Indenture, the creditors of which are entitled under the terms of the Indenture and any existing Pari Passu Debt Document to receive priority in respect of the proceeds of the enforcement against the Collateral (each such facility being a "Credit Facility" and, together with the New Revolving Credit Facility, the "Credit Facilities" and each finance document relating thereto, a "Credit Facility Document"). Each lender under a Credit Facility is a "Credit Facility Lender" and the liabilities of the Debtors to the Credit Facility Lenders are referred to as the "Credit Facility Lender Liabilities."
