There is no treaty between the United States and Lithuania providing for the reciprocal recognition and enforcement of judgments (other than arbitration awards) rendered in civil and commercial matters. Therefore, to judgments of U.S. courts, the rules of the Lithuanian Code of Civil Procedure shall apply (IX-743, Civilinio proceso kodeksas). Those rules provide for enforcement of foreign judgments.
