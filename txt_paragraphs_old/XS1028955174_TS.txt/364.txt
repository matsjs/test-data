Restricted Subsidiaries and Unrestricted Subsidiaries
Immediately after the issuance of the Notes, all the Issuer's Subsidiaries will be Restricted Subsidiaries. However, in the circumstances described below under ``--Certain Definitions--Unrestricted Subsidiary,'' the Issuer will be permitted to designate Restricted Subsidiaries as Unrestricted Subsidiaries. Unrestricted Subsidiaries will not be subject to any of the restrictive covenants in the Indenture.
