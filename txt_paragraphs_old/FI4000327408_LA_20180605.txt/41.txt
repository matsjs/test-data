The procedure for release by the Agent of the Transaction Security in accordance with the above Clauses 9.5 and 9.6 is set out in more detail in the relevant Security Document.
Information from the Issuer
The Issuer will make the following information available by email to the Agent:
(a) as soon as the same become available, but in any event within four (4) months after the end of each financial year, its audited consolidated financial statements for that financial year and annual report; and
(b) as soon as practicable following an acquisition or disposal of Notes by a Group Company, the aggregate Nominal Amount held by the Group Companies, or the amount of Notes cancelled by the Issuer.
The Issuer shall provide the above information also to the Noteholders upon request.
