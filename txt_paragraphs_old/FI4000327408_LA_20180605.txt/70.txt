15.
DECISIONS BY NOTEHOLDERS
15.1
A request by the Agent for a decision by the Noteholders on a matter relating to the Finance
Documents shall (at the option of the Agent) be dealt with at a Noteholders' Meeting or by
way of a Written Procedure.
