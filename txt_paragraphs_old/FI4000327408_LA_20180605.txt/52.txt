11.7
Disposals
The Issuer shall not (and shall procure that no other Group Company will) sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of its assets, except for a Permitted Disposal.
11.8
Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall (and shall procure that each other Group Company will) comply with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject from time to time, if a failure to do so would have Material Adverse Effect.
