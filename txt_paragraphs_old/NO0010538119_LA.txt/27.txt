10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from the Issue Date to, but not including the Interest Payment Date in November 2010 at 108% o f par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(ii) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in November 2010, to but not including the Interest Payment Date in November 2011 at 105% o f par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(iii) at any time thereafter at 104% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least twenty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
