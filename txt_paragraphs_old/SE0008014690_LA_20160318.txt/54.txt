The Trustee is entitled to, and shall following a demand in writing from a Holder (or
demand may only be validly made by a person who is a Holder on the second Business Day
following the day on which the demand is received by the Trustee and shall, if made by
several Holders, be made by them jointly) or following an instruction or decision pursuant to
Clause 12.6 or 12.7, on behalf of the Holders, terminate the Bonds and to declare all, but not
only some, of the Bonds due for payment immediately or at such later date as the Trustee
determines (such later date not falling later than twenty (20) Business Days from the date on
which the Trustee made such declaration), if:
