The Issuer will issue SEK 3,492,306,000 in aggregate principal amount of Notes on the Issue Date. Notes will mature on September 29, 2018. The Notes will be issued in minimum denominations of SEK 1,000,000 and in integral multiples of SEK 1,000 in excess thereof.
Interest
Interest on the Notes will accrue at the rate of 9V4% per annum. Interest on the Notes will:
 accrue from the date of original issuance or, if interest has already been paid, from the date it was most recently paid;
 be payable in cash semi-annually in arrears on April 5 and October 5, commencing on April 5, 2012;
 be payable to the holder of record of such Notes on March 15 and September 15 immediately preceding the related interest payment date; and
 be computed on the basis of a 360-day year comprised of twelve 30-day months.
Interest on overdue principal and interest, including Additional Amounts, if any, will accrue at a rate that is 1% higher than the interest rate on the Notes.
