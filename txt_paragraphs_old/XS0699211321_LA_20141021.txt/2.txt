The Guarantees
On the Issue Date, the Notes will be Guaranteed by HoldCo (the ``Parent Guarantor''). During the Post-Completion Period, the Issuer shall procure that the Notes, subject to the Agreed Security Principles, are Guaranteed by each of the Subsidiaries of the Issuer that Guarantees the Senior Secured Credit Facilities. As of the date of this Offering Memorandum, such Subsidiaries represent all of the direct and indirect Subsidiaries of the Issuer.
