14.3 Compliance Certificate The Issuer shall within a period o f 60 days from each Quarter Date confirm to the Bond Trustee in writing the Issuer's compliance with the covenants in this Clause 14 and 15, unless the Bond Trustee explicitly waives such requirement Such confirmation shall be undertaken in a certificate, substantially in the form set out in Attachment 2 hereto, signed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chief Financial Officer o f the Issuer (a "Compliance Certificate"). In the event of non-compliance, the Compliance Certificate shall describe the non-compliance, the reasons therefore as well as the steps which the Issuer has taken and will take in order to rectify the non-compliance.
