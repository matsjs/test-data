15. FINANCIAL COVENANTS 15.1 Definitions;
"EBITDA" means (on a consolidated basis) the Group's aggregate earnings before interest, taxes and depreciation and amortization (to be calculated on a 12-month rolling basis).
"Equity" means (on a consolidated basis) the aggregate book value of the Group's total equity treated as equity in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applicable to the Issuer).
"Equity Ratio" means the ration o f Equity to Total Assets.
"Liquidity" means the aggregate of
(a) the book value of the Group's Cash and Cash Equivalents (as defined below) (on a consolidated basis); and
(b) undrawn committed revolving credit lines available to the Group (but excluding committed revolving credit lines with less than three months to maturity).
