6 Redemption of the Bonds and payments
6.1 Redemption at maturity and Holders eligible for payments
Unless previously redeemed, or purchased and cancelled in accordance with these Terms and Conditions, the Bonds shall be redeemed at the Nominal Amount on the Maturity Date. Payment in relation to any redemption will be made to the person who is a Holder on the relevant Record Date.
6.2 Early redemption by the Company
The Company may elect to redeem all of the Bonds (but not less than all), on any Banking Day falling after 12 May 2012. The Company shall give the Holders at least 30 days but not more than 60 days' notice of such redemption in accordance with Clause 14. Such notice shall be irrevocable and state the Redemption Date and the relevant Record Date.
