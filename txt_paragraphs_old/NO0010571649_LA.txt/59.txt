(e) In the event o f a Total Loss, the Issuer shall, within 90 days after the Total Loss Date, obtain and present to the Bond Trustee a written confirmation from the relevant insurers that the claim relating to the Total Loss has been accepted in full, and the insurance proceeds shall, as soon as they are released, be paid to the Bond and applied in a partial or full redemption.
(f) The Bond Trustee shall be entitled to arrange, with such insurers it sees fit and for the account o f the Issuer, mortgagee interest insurance relating to its Mortgage over the Vessels.
(c) Maintenance, operation and management o f the Vessels The Issuer shall procure that the Vessels are kept in a good and safe condition and state o f repair consistent with standard ownership and management practice.
The Issuer shall procure compliance with all relevant environmental laws and regulations and all other laws and regulations relating to the Vessels, their ownership, operation and imanagement.
The Issuer shall ensure that any technical managers retained for the Vessels shall be reputable and professional.
