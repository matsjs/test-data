10.4 Change o f control
The respective Bondholder must notify the Paying Agent within the Change of Control Conversion Period o f its election of whether or not to (a) require early redemption, or (b) convert the Bonds to Shares,
20
In the event of an early redemption pursuant to this Clause, settlement shall be three (3) Banking Days after the Paying Agent has received such request.
In the event of conversion pursuant to this Clause, the Issuer shall as soon as possible, but in no event later than on the Change o f Control Conversion Date issue to and in the names o f the Bondholder the number of new Shares which are necessary in order to fulfil the Issuer's obligations to issue new Shares to the respective Bondholder.
The terms and conditions set out in Attachment 2 shall (to the extent applicable) apply for any conversion o f Bonds to Shares according to this Clause.
If a Change of Control is triggered by a statutory merger (amalgamation or other business combination) in which the Issuer is the acquired company, this Clause shall not apply and Clause D, 1 o f Attachment 2 shall apply.
