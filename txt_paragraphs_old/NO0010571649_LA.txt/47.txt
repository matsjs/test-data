10.5.2
Mandatory Redemption shall be settled by prepayment made on the earlier o f (i) the date falling ninety (90) days after the Total Loss Date, and (ii) the date of receipt by the Bond Trustee of the proceeds of insurance or requisition for title relating to such Total Loss.
For the avoidance o f doubt, each Bondholder may within the Exercise Period, and provided that the conversion conditions in Attachment 2 are otherwise fulfilled, elect to exercise its Conversion Right after having received the Issuer's notice of a Mandatory Redemption pursuant to Clause 10.5.3 below.
10.5.3 Mandatory Redemption shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least fifteen Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption.
10.5.4
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount o f such Bond (including any premium as stated above, if any) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date
