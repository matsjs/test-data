6.1 Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Issuer's Escrow Account will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it, on the Issue Date:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions necessary to (i) issue the Bonds, (ii) execute the Finance Documents, and (iii) issue the required number o f Shares to any Bondholder exercising any o f its Conversion Rights (as defined in Attachment 2 Clause A);
(c) if not included in the resolutions referred to in sub-paragraph (b) above a power of attorney from the Issuer and the Vessel Owners to its representatives for the execution of the relevant Finance Documents;
(d) certified copies o f (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for the Issuer, evidencing that it is validly existing and (ii) Articles o f Association of the Issuer;
(e) certified copies of the Construction Contracts;
(f) certified copies of the Refund Guarantees;
(g) in respect of the Security Documents: (i) The Escrow Account Pledge duly executed by the Issuer and perfected, and (ii) the notices of assignment or pledge and acknowledgements and consents required to be executed under the Escrow Account Pledge duly executed;
