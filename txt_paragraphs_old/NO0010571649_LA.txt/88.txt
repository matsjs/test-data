B.l Each Bondholder may exercise one or more o f his Conversion Right(s) at the Conversion Price at any time during the Exercise Period provided that the relevant Bondholder at the Conversion Date has the right to own Shares under the applicable law and that notification thereof is given pursuant to Clause B.4.
B.2 The Conversion Right cannot be separated from the Bond.
B.3 The Conversion Price shall be adjusted pursuant to Clauses C and D.
B.4 In order to exercise a Conversion Right, the Bondholder shall deliver to the Paying Agent (via its Account Manager) a duly completed, irrevocable and signed exercise notice (the "Exercise Notice").
Request for conversion takes place by the Bondholder notifying his Account Manager of the number of Bonds which shall be converted. The Account Manager shall then promptly forward the request to the Paying Agent. The Paying Agent shall carry the conversion into effect at the Conversion Date,
Conversion will be effected by a set-off o f the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted against the issuing o f the whole number o f Shares resulting from dividing the total nominal value o f the Bonds to be converted by the Conversion Price. Any excess amount beyond the whole number o f Shares converted by the Bonds shall fall to the Issuer.
