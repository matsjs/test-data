Status of the Bonds and security, new debt
8.1 The Bonds shall be senior debt of the Issuer. The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Issuer (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
8.2 Any amount that the Issuer is owing under the Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest and expenses under the Finance Documents, shall be secured on first priority by the Security Interests under the Security Documents and the Issuer and the Vessel Owners shall take any action necessary in order to establish and preserve the Security Interest (including but not limited to the execution and perfection of the Mortgages on or prior to the Delivery Date).
8.3 The Issuer shall have the right to issue new secured debt with ranking pari passu to the Bonds in the security relating to Vessel#3 and/or Vessel#4, provided however that such new debt shall (i) not be secured in Vessel#l and/or Vessel#2, (ii) be limited to a maximum of USD 11 million plus interest costs and expenses, (iii) have final maturity after the Maturity Date, and (iv) a lenders o f such new debt enters into a intercreditor agreement, in form and substance satisfactory for the Bond Trustee. For the avoidance of doubt, the Issuer's right to incur additional indebtedness for the purpose of financing the construction, delivery and operation of the Group's vessels and the Group is otherwise not restricted.
