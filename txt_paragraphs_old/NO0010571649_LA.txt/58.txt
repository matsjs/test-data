(b) Insurance o f the Vessels
(a) The Issuer shall procure that the Vessels from the time they are delivered from the Yard to the relevant Vessel Owner are fully insured in line with market practise in such amounts and on such terms as the Bond Trustee may approve (such approval not to be unreasonably withheld).
(b) The insured value of the Vessels shall at all times be equal to or greater than its Market Value and equal to or greater than 120 per cent of the aggregate principal value of the Outstanding Bonds.
