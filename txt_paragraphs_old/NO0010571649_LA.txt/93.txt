C.5 Should the Issuer make a dividend payment to shareholders of the Issuer, the Conversion Price shall be adjusted according to the principles of Clause C.3.
C.6 If (i) changes are made in the share capital other than those mentioned in Clauses C. 1- C.5 above, which are unfavourable to the Bondholders compared to the shareholders, and/or (ii) any o f the principles for the adjustment of the Conversion Price as set out in this Bond Agreement may not be enforced under applicable law, the Issuer will take such steps as shall be required by the Bond Trustee (including the execution of an agreement supplemental to or amending the Bond Agreement) to ensure that each Bond then outstanding shall retain its Conversion Right in full and the right for adjustment of the Conversion Price as set out herein. Any change made in accordance with this Clause shall to the extent possible be carried out by adjustments to the Conversion Price. The principles expressed in Clauses C, 1 to Clause C.5 above shall always be the basis for any adjustments pursuant to this Clause.
