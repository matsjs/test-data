The Call Option may be exercised for any amount, provided however that it must be exercised for (i) a total amount of minimum of 50% of the originally issued Bonds in order to obtain the release of Security Interest in respect one of the Vessel#3 or Vessel#4 or (ii) and a total amount equivalent to all the then Outstanding Bonds at the time the Call Option is exercised in order to obtain the release of Security Interest in respect o f both Vessel#3 and Vessel#4.
10.2.3 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option. The Call Option may be exercised several times,
10.2.4 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
10.2.5
On the settlement date o f the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each o f the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
