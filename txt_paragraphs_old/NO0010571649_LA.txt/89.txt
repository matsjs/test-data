B.5 The Issuer is responsible for ensuring that:
(a) on the Conversion Date the board of directors of the Issuer shall (i) approve the allotment of the relevant number o f Shares to Nordea Bank Norge ASA ("Nordea"), authorise the entry of Nordea's name in the Register of Members of the Issuer as holder of the said Shares and (ii) authorise the issue of the relevant Share Certificate in favour o f Nordea;
(b) a return of allotment o f shares is prepared and filed with the Registrar of Companies in Cyprus as soon as possible and, in any event, within a period of one month from the date of allotment as prescribed by the Cyprus Companies' Law, Cap. 113;
(c) the Outstanding Bonds is being written down within the Conversion Date, and
(d) Shares issued upon conversion are registered in the Securities Register on the Conversion Date.
If the allotment of shares is not registered in the Register of Members of the Issuer on the Conversion Date, the Issuer shall ensure that the shares following the conversion shall be temporarily registered in the Securities Register (with temporary ISIN).
B.6 Shares issued upon conversion give to the Bondholder rights in the Issuer as of the registration o f Nordea in the Register o f Members of the Issuer as registered holder o f such Shares and as of the registration o f such shares in the Securities Depository in favour of the Bondholder; both registrations should be effected on the Conversion Date.
B.7 Notwithstanding anything o f the foregoing provisions, the Issuer shall (i) no later than 10 Business Days after receipt of the Exercise Notice deliver to the relevant Bondholder exercising its Conversion Rights the relevant number Shares or depository receipts ("Depository Receipts") (in the event that the majority o f the traded Shares are represented by depository receipts registered in the Securities Register), representing the Shares resulting from the conversion of Bonds according to this Bond Agreement, and (ii) deliver to the Bond Trustee such documentation as it reasonably may require evidencing the proper issuance and allotment of the Shares and/or Depository Receipts.
