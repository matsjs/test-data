10.3 Clean-up Call
The Issuer may at any time during the term o f the Bond Issue, and provided that 90 per cent or more of the Bonds issued on the Issue Date shall have been redeemed or converted into Shares, call the Outstanding Bonds (the "Clean-up Call") at par value (100%) plus accrued interest.
Should the Issuer wish to exercise the Clean-up Call, it shall issue notice thereof to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (to the latter in writing via the Securities Register) no later than twenty (20) Business Days before the date of redemption.
For the avoidance of doubt, each Bondholder may within the Exercise Period, and provided that the conversion conditions in Attachment 2 are otherwise fulfilled, elect to exercise its Conversion Right after having received the Issuer's Clean-up Call notice.
10.4 Change o f control
If a Change of Control has occurred, each Bondholder shall at any time during the Change o f Control Conversion Period have the option to (at each Bondholder's discretion) require early redemption of its Bonds ("P ut Option") at 101 % o f par value plus accrued interest.
