13.19 Housing Cooperative
(a) The Group Companies shall be permitted to convert economic associations into housing cooperatives provided that all ownership interest in such housing cooperatives is pledged to the Trustee and the Bondholders (represented by the Trustee). Any housing cooperatives wholly-owned by the Group shall be permitted to enter into pre-agreements (Sw. Föravtal) but not to complete any sale of any housing rights (Sw. Bostadsrätter).
(b) Notwithstanding the above, the Issuer and/or the relevant housing cooperative shall be allowed to sell housing rights relating to the Fjärdingen 27:6 New Property under the following conditions:
(i) that the pre-agreements have been entered into in an aggregate amount that exceeds SEK 50,000,000 (the "Minimum Disposal Amount");
(ii) that the Minimum Disposal Amount is deposited on the Disposal Account;
