The Notes constitute direct, unconditional, unsubordinated and secured obligations of the

2.5
The Notes are freely transferable but the Noteholders may be subject to purchase or
transfer restrictions with regard to the Notes, as applicable, under local laws to which a
Noteholder may be subject (due to e.g. its nationality, residency, registered address or
place(s) of business). Each Noteholder must ensure compliance with such restrictions at its
own cost and expense.
