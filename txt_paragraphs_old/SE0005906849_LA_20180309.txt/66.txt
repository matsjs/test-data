13.13 Merger and de-mergers
13.13.1
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving consolidating assets and obligations, other than where the Issuer is the surviving entity.
13.13.2 The Issuer shall procure that no Restricted Company will (excluding as a result of a Permitted Partial Divestment):
(a) carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving consolidating assets and obligations, other than (1) with another Restricted Company or (2) a solvent reorganisation of any Restricted Company; or
(b) carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving splitting any of the Restricted Companies, provided in each case that the Parent is the surviving entity.
