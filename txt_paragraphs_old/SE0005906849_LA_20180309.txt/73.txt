The Agent shall, within twenty (20) Business Days of the date on which the Agent received actual knowledge of that an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing, decide if the Notes shall be so accelerated. If the Agent decides not to accelerate the Notes, the Agent shall promptly seek instructions from the Noteholders in accordance with Clause 17 (Decisions by Noteholders). The Agent shall always be entitled to take the time necessary to consider whether an occurred event constitutes an Event of Default.

If the Noteholders instruct the Agent to accelerate the Notes, the Agent shall promptly
declare the Notes due and payable and take such actions as may, in the opinion of the
Agent, be necessary or desirable to enforce the rights of the Noteholders under the Finance
Documents, unless the relevant Event of Default is no longer continuing.
