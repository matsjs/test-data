"Guarantee" means an unconditional and irrevocable on-demand guarantee from the Guarantors securing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Guarantor" means each o f the Original Guarantor and any Additional Guarantors.
"Incurrence Test" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 13.4(e).
"Initial Bond Issue" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 2.2.
"Initial Dividend" means the payment of dividend by the issuer to its shareholders of up to NOK 75,000,000 to be made at any time in the financial year 2014.
"Intercompany Loan Pledge" means a pledge over all present and future claims under any intercompany loan between the Issuer and any o f its directly owned Guarantors which exceeds NOK 5,000,000.
"Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement dated on our about the date of this Bond Agreement and made between the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and the lenders under the Revolving Credit Facility.
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio o f EBITDA to Net Interest Expense for any Relevant Period.
"Interest Payment Date" means 26 March, 26 June, 26 September and 26 December each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
