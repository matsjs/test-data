(b) Additional Guarantors
The Issuer shall, to the extent legally permitted, ensure that any Group Company which after the Issue Date becomes a Material Subsidiary is appointed as a Guarantor. A Guarantor shall be released from its obligations under the Guarantee provided by it to the extent it no longer qualifies as a Material Subsidiary.
(c) Financial Assistance
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Obligor shall, grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting Security) to or for the benefit of any third party, other than (i) in the ordinary course o f business, (ii) the Security Interests, (iii) any Security and guarantees granted as Security for the Revolving Credit Facility, (iv) intercompany loans (A) provided to any other Obligor, or (B) to Group Companies (not being Obligors) of up to NOK 3,000,000 in aggregate.
