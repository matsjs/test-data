"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 17.4.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
"Security Interests" means:
(a) the Escrow Account Pledge;
(b) the Guarantor Share Pledge;
(c) the Intercompany Loan Pledge;
(d) the Guarantee; and
(e) the Bank Account Pledge.
"Shareholder Loan" means any loan from Norvestor to the Issuer which is fully subordinated to the Bond Issue, have payment-in-kind interest and where no repayment or enforcement action is allowed until after the Maturity Date.
