1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Additional Guarantor" means a company within the Group which (after the date hereof) is or becomes a Guarantor in accordance with Clause 13.4(b).
"Affiliate" means, in relation to any person, a Subsidiary of that person or a holding company of that person or any other Subsidiary o f that holding company.
"Attachment" means any attachment to this Bond Agreement.
"Bank Account Pledge" means a pledge over the Issuer's monetary claims in respect o f the following bank accounts, which accounts will not be blocked until the occurrence of an Event o f Default: 1503 18 76602 and 1503 26 38691.
