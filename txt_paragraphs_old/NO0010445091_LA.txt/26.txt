The respective Bondholder must notify the Paying Agent within the Change of Control Conversion Period o f its election of whether or not to (a) require early redemption, or (b) convert the Bonds to Shares. In the event o f an early redemption pursuant to this Clause 10.3, settlement shall be three (3) Banking Days after the Paying Agent has received such request. In the event of conversion pursuant to this Clause 10.3, the Borrower shall as soon as possible, but in no event later than on the Change of Control Conversion Date issue to and in the names of the Bondholder the number of new Shares which are necessary in order to fulfil the Borrower's obligations to issue new Shares to the respective Bondholder.
The terms and conditions set out in Clauses 13-15 shall (to the extent applicable) apply for any conversion of Bonds to Shares according to this Clause 10.3.
If a Change of Control Event is triggered by a merger in which the Borrower is the acquired company, this Clause 10.3 and Clause 15.1 shall be alternative rights for the Bondholders.
