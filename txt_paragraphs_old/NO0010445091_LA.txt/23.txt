9 Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 12 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made in arrears on 30 June each year, the first interest Payment Date being 30 June 2009.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis o f a year o f 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless: (a) the last day of the period is the 3 1st day o f a month but the first day o f the period is a day other than the 30thor 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or (b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
