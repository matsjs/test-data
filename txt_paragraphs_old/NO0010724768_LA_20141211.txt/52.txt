13.3.6 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no Material Subsidiary will, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.3.7 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
