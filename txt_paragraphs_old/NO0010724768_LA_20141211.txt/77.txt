19.3.5
Save where all the Bonds are being held by one (1) Bondholder, a majority consisting o f at least two Bondholders jointly and representing at least 90% of the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting is required for:
(a) any waiver or amendment of any terms of this Bond Agreement (including but not limited to the replacement o f the Bond Trustee);
(b) consent to an amendment of any right of the holders of Shares;
(c) consent to issue of new equity (including convertible instruments, but excluding Permitted Equity Issue) in a form different from the existing Shares; and
(d) listing of the Bonds on an Exchange.
19.3.6 The Bondholders' Meeting may not adopt resolutions which may give certain Bondholders or others an unreasonable advantage at the expense of other Bondholders.
