10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
X0.2 Call Option
10.2.1
The Issuer may, at any time after 1 April 2016, redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part (but if in part, being in a minimum amount of NOK 10,000,000 (ten million)) with settlement date any time from and included 1 April 2016 to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 100% of par plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
10.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least forty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option. Each Bondholder may notify the Paying Agent (via its Account Manager) no later than twenty Business Days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders of the exercise of the Call Option that it will instead opt for exercising its Conversion Right with respect to the Bonds it holds and which the Issuer intends to redeem.
