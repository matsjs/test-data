18.1.7 Compliance with financial covenants
The Issuer or any Material Subsidiary is in breach of any financial covenant in its financing arrangements.
18.1.8 Creditors'process
The Issuer or any Material Subsidiary having any of its assets having an aggregate value o f USD 1,000,000 (one million) (or the equivalent thereof in other currencies) impounded, confiscated, attached or subject to distraint, or is subject to enforcement of Security.
18.1.9 Impossibility or illegality
It is or becomes impossible or unlawful for the Issuer or any of the Material Subsidiaries to fulfil or perform any of the material terms of any Finance Document to which it is a party.
