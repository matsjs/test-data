13.3.1 Pari passu ranking
The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
13.3.2 Mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that none of the Material Subsidiaries will, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation o f the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any other Material Subsidiary with any other companies or entities if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
