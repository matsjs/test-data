Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds (net of fees to the Bond Trustee, underwriting commission to underwriters in the Bond Issue and other agreed costs and expenses) shall be applied towards the general corporate purposes of the Group which will allow for further research and development, establishment of new production facilities and/or improvements to existing and new production facilities, and development of commercial activities in the Group's core markets.
