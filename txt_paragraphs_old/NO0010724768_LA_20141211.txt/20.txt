Listing
3.1
The Issuer may only apply for listing of the Bonds on an Exchange subject to
the prior consent of the Bondholders in accordance with Clause 19.3.5 and in
the period from 12 to 24 months after the Issue Date.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
