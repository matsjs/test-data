FINANCIAL UNDERTAKINGS
Equity Ratio
The Equity Ratio of the Group shall at anytime not be less than twenty (20) percent.
Interest Coverage Ratio
The Interest Coverage Ratio of the Group shall at any time not be less than 1.25 on a rolling twelve-month basis, calculated in accordance with the latest Financial Report.
