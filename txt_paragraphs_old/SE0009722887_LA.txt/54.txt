12.10 Operational leases
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will, incur any operational leasing debt, provided however that the Issuer and its Subsidiaries have a right to incur, prolong, renew or extend operational leasing debt existing on the First Issue Date plus a maximum of DKK 75,000,000 of additional operational leasing debt.
