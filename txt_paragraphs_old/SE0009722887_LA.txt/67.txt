15.6
In the event of an acceleration of the Bonds in accordance with this Clause 15, the
Issuer shall redeem all Bonds with an amount per Bond together with a premium on the
due and payable amount as set forth in the Call Option Amount for the relevant period
and, shall for the non-call period (until the First Call Date) be the price set out in
paragraph (i) of the Call Option Amount definition together with accrued but unpaid
interest.
