Interest
Each Initial Bond carries Interest at the Interest Rate during an Interest Period. Any Subsequent Bonds will carry Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to their issuance to (and including) the next succeeding Interest Payment Date.
Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Bonds shall be made to the Bondholders quarterly in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year.
