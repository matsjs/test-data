10 Maturity of the Loan
10.2 The Borrower may redeem the Remaining Loan or any portion of the Remaining Loan plus accrued interest ("Call Option") at Interest Payment date in June 2008 to a price of 106% of nominal value, and at Interest Payment date in June 2009 to a price of 104% of nominal value.
Should the Borrower exercise the Call Option, the Bondholders must be informed of this in writing via VPS no later than 30 - thirty - Banking Days before the date of redemption.
Partial Call Option must be carried out by drawing of lots between the bonds.
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this sub clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
10.3 Upon a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of the bonds at a price of 101 % of par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within two months after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 13.1 (h).
The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent of the Loan (the "Paying Agent") of the pre payment request. The Put Date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the pre-payment request.
10.4 If all the conditions set out in Clause 6.1 above are not fulfilled within 15 August 2006, the Remaining Loan shall be redeemed at par (plus accrued interest) within 10 Banking Days thereof. The Loan Trustee shall release amount from the Escrow Account for such redemption. The Loan Trustee may extend the time limit in this Clause due to public authorities delay connected to the establishment of the Contract Pledge, ref Clause 6.4.
