8 Status of the Loan, security and merger
8.1 The Loan shall rank pari passu with other senior debt of the Borrower other than obligations which are mandatory preferred by law, and shall rank ahead of subordinated capital.
8.2 The Loan shall be secured by the Loan Security.
9 Interest and interest calculations
9.1 Interest accrues from and including 28 June 2006 based on NIBOR plus 4.50 percentage points. The interest rate is set on the first Interest Determination Date. ShouldNIBOR not be available, the interest rate will be set based on NIBOR Reference Banks plus 4.50 percentage points.
The interest rate is reset in accordance with sub-clause 9.2.
The interest is paid in arrears on each Interest Payment Date, the first Interest Payment Date falling in September 2006.
9.2 The interest rate on the Loan is reset with effect from each Interest Payment Date. The new interest rate is reset on the Interest Determination Date based on NIBOR plus 4.50 percentage points. ShouldNIBOR not be available, the interest rate will be reset based on NIBOR Reference Banks plus 4.50 percentage points.
