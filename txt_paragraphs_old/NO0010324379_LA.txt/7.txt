The Loan Trustee has through the Bondholders' subscription been granted authority to act on behalf of the Bondholders to the extent provided for in the Loan Agreement.
1 Definitions
Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Acquisition:
The acquisition of the Borrower's shares by NewCo which closed on 21 April 2006.
Allowed Dividends
Dividends constituting less than 30 % of Free Cash Flow generated during the previous Period, provided the consolidated unencumbered cash position of the Borrower is minimum NOK 100 million following the dividend payment.
Asset Pledge:
first priority pledge over the Production Assets.
