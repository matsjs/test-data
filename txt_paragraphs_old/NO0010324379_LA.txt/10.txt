Free Cash Flow:
with respect to each Period, earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization less (i) interests payable (ii) capital expenditure and (iii) taxes payable, adjusted for changes in working capital (so that an increase in working capital shall be subtracted while a decrease shall be added to). Working capital means the aggregate of current assets (inventories, accounts receivables and other receivables) less the aggregate of current liabilities (including accounts payable and other current liabilities). Changes in working capital due to factoring arrangements (including changes in existing financial arrangement) and decreases not reflecting an improved financial position of the Borrower shall be disregarded.
