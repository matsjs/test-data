20.3
The Agent shall promptly notify the Noteholders of any amendments or waivers made in
accordance with Clause 20.1, setting out the date from which the amendment or waiver
will be effective, and ensure that any amendments to the Finance Documents are published
in the manner stipulated in Clause 12.3 (Publication o f Finance Documents). The Issuer
shall ensure that any amendments to the Finance Documents are duly registered with the
CSD and each other relevant organisation or authority.
