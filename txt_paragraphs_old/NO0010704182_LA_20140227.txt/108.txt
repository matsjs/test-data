(l) Excess funds with the Intra-Group Charterer
Any Intra-Group Charterer (if applicable) undertakes to lend any amount in the excess of USD 5,000,000 deposited on its operating account(s) to the Issuer as an Intra-Group Loan at each financial year-end (to the extent allowed by applicable laws).
(m) Subordination of intra-group loans
The Issuer, the Parent and the Intra-Group Charterer (if applicable) shall ensure that all Intra-Group Loans and the Convertible Loans are unsecured and fully subordinated to the Bonds and that no payment of interest or principal shall be made until the Bonds have been repaid in full and 12 months after the Maturity Date (to the extent legally and practically permissible), save for Permitted Intra-Group Loan Repayments (as defined below.)
(n) Negative pledge - Issuer and Intra-Group Charterer
The Issuer and the Intra-Group Charterer shall not create or permit to subsist any security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for (i) any security contemplated by the Bond Issue, (ii) the OMSL Security until the OMSL Indebtedness is repaid in relation to the delivery of the Vessel, (iii) the Vendor Loan Security and (iv) any security arising by operation of law;
