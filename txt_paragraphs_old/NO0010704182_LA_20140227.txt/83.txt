10.3 Call Option 10.3.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (Call Option):
(a) from the First Call Option Date to, but not including the Interest Payment Date in February 2018 at a price equal to 108% of par value plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount,
(b) from the Interest Payment Date in February 2018 to, but not including the Interest Payment Date in August 2018 a price equal to 106% of par value plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount; or
(c) from the Interest Payment Date in August 2018 to, but not including the Maturity Date at a price equal to 104% of par value plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount.
10.3.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.3.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
