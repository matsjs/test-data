The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall exclusively be applied as follows; (i) the deposit of 12 months of interest under the Bond Issue to the Retention Account, (ii) part repayment of the Yard Cost Loan (iii) other project costs related to the Vessel, including but not limited to variation orders, interest, cost of site team and other administration costs, (iv) part financing of the last yard instalment on the Vessel under the Shipbuilding Contract with such amounts exceeding the Vendor Loan, (v) the two last instalments under the Equipment Contract in the amount of NOK 56,000,000 (however estimated to constitute approximately USD 10,500,000), (vi) repayment of the OMSL Indebtedness and (vii) for general corporate purposes.
