13.5
Maintenance and service of Accounts covenants (a) Escrow Account
The Issuer shall prior to the issuance of the Bonds establish the Escrow Account, which shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee. The net proceeds from the Bond Issue deposited on the Escrow Account shall only be applied by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 2.3 (Purpose and utilization), or as set out in Clause 6.9 (if applicable). The amount of the Cash Collateral Loan shall be transferred to the Escrow Account prior to the Issue Date, and released from the Escrow Account in accordance with Clause 6.8.
(b) Earnings Account
The Issuer shall ensure that all earnings under any Employment Contract, or (to the extent the Employment Contract is held by an Intra-Group Charterer) all earnings under a Bareboat Charter, shall be paid to the Earnings Account and the following payments shall be made on a monthly basis within five days of receipt of payments of such earnings:
(i) Firstly, the Issuer shall transfer from the Earnings Account to the Retention Account an amount equal to 1/6 of the next interest payment (and 1/6 of the next scheduled amortization at any time when a repayment is about to take place within the next 6 months.
(ii) Secondly the Issuer shall transfer all remaining funds in excess of USD 10,000,000 on the Earnings Account to the Debt Service Account on each Quarter Date.
