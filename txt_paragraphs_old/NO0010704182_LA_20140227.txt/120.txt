If for any reason, funds on the Earnings Account are insufficient for the transfer to the Retention Account as set out above, such shortfall shall be covered from the Debt Service Account.

(c) Retention Account
The Retention Account shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee. Amounts standing on the Retention Account shall only be applied to pay scheduled interest and amortizations under the Bonds.
