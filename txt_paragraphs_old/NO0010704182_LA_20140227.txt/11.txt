"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 17.
"Business Day" means any day on which the commercial banks in Norway and New York are open for general business, and can settle foreign currency transactions.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the Payment Date occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments of interest and/or principal (as the case may be) will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day (No Adjustments of Business Day).
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.3.
"Cash Collateral Loan" means an intra-group loan between the Parent as lender and the Issuer as borrower in the amount of the Excess Amount.
"Cash Sweep Amount" means any amounts exceeding USD 10,000,000 deposited on the Debt Service Account on the tenth Business Day prior to each Interest Payment Date from and including the Interest Payment Date in February 2016.
