"Group" means the Parent and all its Subsidiaries from time to time.
"Guarantee" means an unconditional on-demand guarantee from the Guarantors, on a
joint and several basis, securing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any
other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses.
"Guarantors" means the Parent, the Vessel Manager and any Intra-Group Charterer (if applicable).
"Interest Payment Date" means 21 August and 21 February each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer and the Parent for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to GAAP.
"Intra-Group Charterer" means a wholly owned subsidiary of the Parent, other than the Issuer, entering into an Employment Contract for employment of the Vessel with an end user.
"Intra-Group Charterer Security" means the security to be entered into by an Intra-Group Charterer acceding to this Bond Agreement, including a share pledge over the Intra-Group Charterer, Account Pledge over its Earnings Account and Assignment of Employment Contracts, or such other documents as will be required in order to preserve the current security and cash waterfall under the Bond Agreement to the extent possible under applicable law.
