The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any financial indebtedness (including guarantees), other than: (i) any financial indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue, (ii) any Intra-Group Loans (subordinated both in respect of interest and principal), (iii) the OMSL Indebtedness until repaid in relation to the delivery of the Vessel, (iv) the Vendor Loan and (v) any financial indebtedness for the provision of guarantees (or recourse liability to any financial institution for provision of guarantees) not exceeding USD 5,000,000.
