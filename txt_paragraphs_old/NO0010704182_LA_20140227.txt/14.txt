"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
When determining the relevant person's number of voting rights in the other person or the right to elect and remove members of the board of directors, rights held by the parent company of the relevant person and the parent company's Subsidiaries shall be included.
"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 19.2.
"Earnings Account" means the Issuer's earnings account held with the Paying Agent, as well as any earnings account held of any Intra-Group Charterer (if applicable) pledged on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents..
"Escrow Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer, held with the Paying Agent, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 16.1.
"Employment Contract" means any contract of employment of the Vessel between the Issuer (or any Intra-Group Charterer if relevant) and the end-user, however not to include service or operation agreements related to the operation or performance of the Vessel if entered into between any third party agent or service provider unrelated to the Group and the end-user.
