(c) Changes to chartering structure
The Issuer and the Vessel Manager shall not make changes to the chartering structure set out in the Employment Contract, except that the Employment Contract may be entered into with an Intra-Group Charterer and that a different chartering structure may be applied if required for tax or operational purposes in the relevant jurisdiction, and always provided that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) will obtain a position with respect to covenants and Security which is no less favourable to the Bondholders than the initial terms and conditions of the Bond Agreement and the Security.
(d) The Vessel
The Issuer and the Vessel Manager (and any Intra-Group Charterer, if relevant) shall ensure that;
(i) the Vessel at all times to be registered in a jurisdiction acceptable to the Bond Trustee;
(ii) the Vessel is managed by a company within the Group or otherwise by commercial and technical managers (the "Operators") acceptable to the Bond Trustee and to procure that the rights of the Operators against the Issuer under the management agreements are subordinated to the rights of the Trustee and the Bondholders under the Bond Agreement in an Event of Default situation;
(iii) any fees payable to the Parent or any other company within the Group for services shall at all times be in accordance with the Issuer's transfer pricing policies which shall reflect internationally accepted transfer pricing principles;
(iv) the Issuer, the Operators and the Vessel are in the position of proper trading certificates and complying with all relevant regulations;
