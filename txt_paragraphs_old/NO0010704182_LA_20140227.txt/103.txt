(c) De-mergers
The Issuer and any Intra-Group Charterer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer or any of its Subsidiaries into two or more separate companies or entities.
(d) Continuation of business
No Obligor shall cease to carry on its business, and the Parent shall ensure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Parent and the Group from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, andfor as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(e) Disposal of business
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of such Group company's assets or operations, unless:i)(
(i) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
