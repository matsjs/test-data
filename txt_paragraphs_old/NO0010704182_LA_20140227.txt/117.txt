13.4
Financial covenants (a) Minimum free liquidity
The Issuer undertakes to comply with the following financial covenants during the term of the Bond Issue, from the First Drawdown onwards:
(i) Minimum Free Liquidity of USD 5,000,000. (For the avoidance of doubt, not including funds deposited on the Retention Account as the amount deposited on the Retention Account shall never fall below 12 months interest).
(ii) Asset Coverage Ratio of minimum 130%.
The Issuer undertakes to comply with the above Financial Covenants at all times, such compliance to be measured semi-annually and certified by the Issuer with each annual financial statement and semi-annual financial statement on the respective reporting date.
If at any time the Vessel has not been committed under any firm Employment Contract for a period of more than 5 out of tha last 12 months, Minimum Free Liquidity shall be increased to a minimum of USD 10,000,000 until such time as the Vessel has commenced operations under a new Employment Contract. The Free Liquidity may not be decreased to USD 5,000,000 until the Vessel has operated under a firm Employment Contract for 5 months out of the last 12 months. Vessel Manager's undertaking
