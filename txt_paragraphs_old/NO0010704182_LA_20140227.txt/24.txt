"Parallel Debt" means, in relation to an Underlying Debt, an obligation to pay to the Bond Trustee an amount equal to (and in the same currency as) the amount of that Underlying Debt.
"Paying Agent" means Nordea Bank Norge ASA.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Project Documents" means the Shipbuilding Contract, the Equipment Contract and any vessel management agreement (or any substitution thereof).
"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Restricted Country" means Iran, Sudan, Cuba, North-Korea, Syria, Burma, and any country or territory that becomes the subject of Sanctions similar to those in force at the date hereof against any of the countries referred to above, as well as Venezuela.

"Retained Escrow Amount" means USD 50,000,000 of the net proceeds from the Bond Issue which will be retained on the Escrow Account and be released when the conditions set out in clause 6.3 has been fulfilled.
"Sanctions" means the economic sanctions laws and/or other regulations imposed by any Sanctions Authority with respect to any Restricted Country.
