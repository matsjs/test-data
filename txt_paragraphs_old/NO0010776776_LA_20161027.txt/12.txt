2.3 Purpose and utilization
An amount equal to USD 300,000 from the proceeds of the first tranche of the Bonds will be retained by the Issuer (the "Running Costs") to meet (i) its own fees, costs and expenses; and (ii) the fees, costs and expenses of the Bond Trustee (the "Retained Amount"). The balance of the net proceeds from the first tranche of the Bonds shall be immediately onlent to the Existing Bond Trustee to be used: (i) to repay the Bridge Funding (as defined in the bond summons issued to the Existing Bondholders dated 8 July 2016 in accordance with its terms) including any increased amount which may have been provided subsequent to the provision of the initial Bridge Funding; and thereafter (ii) for the purpose of maximising recoveries under the Existing Bonds, as the Existing Bond Trustee may determine, together (the "Purpose").
