12.9 Guarantee Limitations
Notwithstanding the obligations of the Guarantors pursuant to this guarantee, the maximum guarantee liability of each Guarantor hereunder shall always be limited to USD 240,000,000 plus any interest, default interest or other costs, fees and expenses related to the liability of that Guarantor hereunder.
12.10
Norwegian Financial Agreements Act
Each Guarantor waives all its rights under the provisions of the Norwegian Financial Agreements Act of 25 June 1999 no. 46 (not being mandatory provisions), including (without limitation) the rights set out in Sections 62 through 74 of that act (to the extent that Guarantor is deemed to be a guarantor pursuant to the Norwegian Financial Agreements Act).
