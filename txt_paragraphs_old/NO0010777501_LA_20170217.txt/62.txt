14. GENERAL AND FINANCIAL UNDERTAKINGS 14.1 Obligor's general undertakings
Each Obligor undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that each of the Group Companies will) comply with the undertakings set forth in this Clause 14.1 (Obligor's general undertakings) at all times during the term of the Bonds (unless the Bond Trustee or the Bondholders' Meeting (as the case may be) in writing has agreed to otherwise).

(a) Pari passu ranking: Each Obligor shall, and shall procure that each other Obligor will, ensure that their obligations under these Bond Terms and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 2.4 (Status o f the Bonds and the Guarantees).
(b) Mergers: No Obligor shall, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of any of the Obligors or any other Group Company with any other companies or entities if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(c) De-mergers: No Obligor shall, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a splitting of any of the Obligors or any other Group Company into two or more separate companies or entities, if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
