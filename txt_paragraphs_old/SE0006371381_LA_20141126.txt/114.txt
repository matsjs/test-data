NOTEHOLDERS' MEETING
The Trustee shall convene a Noteholders' Meeting by sending a notice thereof to each
Noteholder no later than ten (10) Business Days after receipt of a request from the Issuer or
the Noteholder(s) (or such later date as may be necessary for technical or administrative
reasons).
