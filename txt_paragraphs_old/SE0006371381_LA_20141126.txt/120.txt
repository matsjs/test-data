18.4
When the requisite majority consents of the total Adjusted Nominal Amount pursuant to
Conditions 16.5 and 16.6 have been received in a Written Procedure, the relevant decision
shall be deemed to be adopted pursuant to Condition 16.5 or 16.6, as the case may be, even
if the time period for replies in the Written Procedure has not yet expired.
