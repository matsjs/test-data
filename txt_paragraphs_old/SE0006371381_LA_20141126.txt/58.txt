5.5
The Issuer shall issue any necessary power of attorney to such persons employed by the
Trustee, as notified by the Trustee, in order for such individuals to independently obtain
information directly from the debt register kept by the CSD in respect of the Notes. The
Issuer may not revoke any such power of attorney unless directed by the Trustee or unless
consent thereto is given by the Noteholders.
