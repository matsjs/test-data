TRANSACTION SECURITY
As continuing Security for the due and punctual fulfilment of the Secured Obligations, Holdco has granted the Transaction Security to the Security Agent and the Secured Parties on the terms set out in the Security Documents.
The Security Agent shall hold the Transaction Security on behalf of the Secured Parties in accordance with the Security Documents and the Intercreditor Agreement.
When all present and future moneys, debts and liabilities due, owing or incurred by the Debtors (as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement) under or in connection with any Senior Secured Notes (other than the Notes), any Additional Senior Secured Notes (other than the Notes), the High Yield Notes and any Additional High Yield Notes (in each case as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement) issued by a Group Company (in each case, whether alone or jointly, or jointly and severally, with any other person, whether actually or contingently, and whether as principal, surety or otherwise) have been fully and finally discharged, and each of the Senior Secured Creditors and the High Yield Creditors are under no further obligation to provide financial accommodation under the Senior Secured Notes (other than the Notes), any Additional Senior Secured Notes (other than the Notes), the High Yield Notes and any Additional High Yield Notes issued by a Group Company, the Trustee is entitled to and shall (without having to obtain the consent of the Noteholders) instruct the Security Agent, subject to Clause 18.1(e) of the Intercreditor Agreement, to release the Transaction Security promptly upon the instruction of the Senior Creditors (as defined under the Intercreditor Agreement) to release the Transaction Security. By acquiring the Notes, each Noteholder is deemed to agree to the release of the Transaction Security in relation to the Notes in the circumstances provided for in this Condition 11.3.
Upon the release of the Transaction Security as provided for in Condition 11.3, the Trustee is entitled to and shall at the request of the Issuer (without having to obtain the consent of the Noteholders) enter into such documentation as is necessary or desirable to terminate the Intercreditor Agreement provided that such documentation is entered into by all the other parties to the Intercreditor Agreement and becomes effective at the same time in respect of all the parties to the Intercreditor Agreement.
