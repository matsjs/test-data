RIGHT TO ACT ON BEHALF OF A NOTEHOLDER
6.1
If any person other than a Noteholder wishes to exercise the rights of a Noteholder under
the Finance Documents on behalf of such Noteholder, it must obtain a power of attorney.
or other proof of authorisation from the Noteholder or a successive, coherent chain of
powers of attorney or proofs of authorisation starting with the Noteholder and authorising
such person.
