(a) prepare or procure that Holdco prepares and make available the annual audited consolidated financial statements of Holdco by delivery to the Trustee and on the website of Holdco as soon as they are available but not later than four (4) months after the expiry o f each Financial Year;
(b) prepare or procure that Holdco prepares and make available the semi-annual consolidated financial statements of Holdco by delivery to the Trustee and on the website of Holdco as soon as they are available but not later than two (2) months after the expiry o f each half financial year;
