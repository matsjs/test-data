7.
GUARANTEE
7.1
In order to provide for the punctual fulfilment of the Guaranteed Obligations the
Guarantors grant the Guarantee to the Trustee on trust for the Noteholders on the terms set
out in the Trust Deed. The Trustee shall hold the Guarantee on behalf of the Noteholders in
accordance with the Trust Deed.
