9.2
Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Notes shall

9.3
Interest for the Notes shall be calculated on the basis of a 360-day year comprised of
twelve (12) months of thirty (30) days each and, in case of an incomplete month, the actual
number of days elapsed (30/360-days basis).
