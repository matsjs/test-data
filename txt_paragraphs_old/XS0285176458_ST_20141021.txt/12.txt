Effectiveness of covenants
(a) the Notes have an Investment Grade Rating from both of the Ratings Agencies; and
(b) no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing under the Indenture;
the Company and its Restricted Subsidiaries will not be subject to the provisions of the Indenture summarized under the subheadings "--Limitation on indebtedness" and "--Merger and consolidation" (collectively, the "Suspended Covenants").
