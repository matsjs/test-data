(d) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall not declare, make or pay any dividend, charge, fee or other distribution (or interest on any unpaid dividend, charge, fee or other distribution) (whether in cash or in kind) on or in respect of its share capital (or any class o f its share capital), except where:

iii. liquidity reserves (including unused bank overdraft commitments available to the Issuer) exceed (before and immediately after the declaration, making or payment thereof) NOK 45,000,000,
iv. no Default is continuing or will occur as a consequence thereof; and
v. such dividend, charge, fee or other distribution amount to less than 50% of the net profits for the Group for the previous financial year.
