(i) before deducting any interest, commission, fees, discounts, prepayment fees, premiums or charges and other finance payments whether paid, payable or capitalised by any member of the Group (calculated on a consolidated basis) in respect of that relevant period;
(ii) not including any accrued interest owing to any member of the Group;
(iii) after adding back any amount attributable to the amortisation, or depreciation o f assets o f members o f the Group;
(iv) before taking into account any exceptional items;
(v) before deducting any acquisition costs;
