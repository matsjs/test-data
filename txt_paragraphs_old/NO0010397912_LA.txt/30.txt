10.3 Upon a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of the Bonds at a price of 101 % o f par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within two months after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 13.1 (h).
The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice o f the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent o f the Loan (the "Paying Agent") o f the pre payment request. The put date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the repayment request.
