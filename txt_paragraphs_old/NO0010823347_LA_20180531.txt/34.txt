Bonds up to an amount equal to the net cash proceeds (after repayment of transaction costs (including taxes) and any relevant Financial Indebtedness pertaining directly to the financing of the Tawke PSC) received from the sale of ownership interest below the 50 per cent, ownership interest level (the "Put Amount").

(ii) publish a notice of such offer as soon as possible following the occurrence of a Put Option Event and no later than on the date when the proceeds are received; and such offer to be valid for the abovementioned thirty (30) calendar days period.
