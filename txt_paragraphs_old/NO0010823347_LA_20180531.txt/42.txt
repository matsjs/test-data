13.5 Financial Indebtedness The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.7 Financial support The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, provide any Financial Support, other than Financial Support:
(a) made in the ordinary course of business;
(b) made, granted or given by any Group Company to or for the benefit of any Group Company;
(c) in connection with Permitted Financial Indebtedness, or
(d) in connection with Permitted Security.
