Profit & Loss
The price for publication paper, i.e. newsprint and magazine paper, remained relatively stable throughout the fourth quarter of 2015 compared to the previous quarter. Seasonality and the restart of one machine at Skogn in Norway, following a prolonged summer stop, supported volumes in Europe to a certain degree. Volumes in Australasia were relatively flat. The weak Norwegian krone, which also continued to depreciate slightly in the fourth quarter of 2015,

Cashflow
Cash flow from operations benefited from a seasonal release of working capital, however this effect was largely offset by interest payments in the quarter. Capital expenditures in the fourth quarter of 2015 were somewhat higher than in in the previous quarter, reflecting progress on growth initiatives. The redemption of the remaining Senior Notes due 2015 in October 2015 lowered cash balances.
