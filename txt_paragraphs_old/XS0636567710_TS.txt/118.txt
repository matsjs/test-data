Notes Offered................................ Euro-denominated notes due 2026.
Notes Maturity Date..................... December 30, 2026.
Interest Rate.................................. The QSF Exchange Notes due 2026 will bear interest in cash at a rate of 6.0% and
in PIK interest at a rate of 6.0% (by increasing the principal amount of the QSF
Exchange Notes due 2026 or by issuing QSF Exchange Notes due 2026 in a
principal amount equal to such interest). The cash interest rate will be subject to
adjustment in the event that a specified loan-to-value (LTV) ratio is not satisfied.
Interest Payment Dates................ Interest on the Notes will be paid semi-annually in arrears on each April 15 and
October 15, commencing October 15, 2016, and will accrue from the Issue Date.
