Security Enforcement Principles
The following is a summary of the "security enforcement principles", which are set out in the Intercreditor Agreement:
(a) It shall be the primary and overriding aim of any enforcement of the Transaction Security to achieve the "Security Enforcement Objective". The "Security Enforcement Objective" means maximizing, so far as is consistent with a prompt and expeditious realization of value from an enforcement of the Transaction Security, recovery by the Primary Creditors.
(b) The security enforcement principles may be amended, varied or waived with the prior written consent of the Majority Super Senior Creditors and the Majority Senior Secured Creditors, provided that no additional obligations may be imposed on a member of the Group without the consent of the Parent.
