Cecilie Jonassen
Cecilie Jonassen has served as an employee representative on our Board of Directors since 2015. Ms. Jonassen has held the position of Assistant Line Manager Pulp at our Saugbrugs mill since 2014 and has been employed in various positions within the Group since 2005. Ms. Jonassen is also chairman of the TEKNA group at Norske Skog Saugbrugs and a member of the European Works Council (EWC). Ms. Jonassen graduated from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in 2005 with a Master of Science in Chemistry.
The business address of each director is our registered office, which is Karenslyst allé 49, 0279 Oslo.
General Meeting
The General Meeting is our highest corporate body. The General Meeting, among other functions, elects the shareholder elected members of the Board of Directors and the chairman of the Board of Directors. In addition, the General Meeting, among other functions, approves the annual accounts, the annual report and any distribution of dividends.
