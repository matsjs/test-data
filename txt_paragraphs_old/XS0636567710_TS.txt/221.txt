In certain jurisdictions in which security interests are being provided in favor of the QSF Exchange Notes, including France, due to the laws and jurisprudence governing the creation and perfection of security interests and enforceability of such security interests, the relevant security documents will provide for the creation of "parallel debt" obligations in favor of the Security Agent, and the security interests in such jurisdictions will secure the parallel debt only, and may not directly secure the obligations under the QSF Exchange Notes. The parallel debt construct has not been tested in court in certain of these jurisdictions and to the extent that the security interests in the QSF Exchange Notes Collateral created under the parallel debt construct are successfully challenged by other parties, holders of the QSF Exchange Notes will not receive any proceeds from an enforcement of the security interests in the QSF Exchange Notes Collateral.
