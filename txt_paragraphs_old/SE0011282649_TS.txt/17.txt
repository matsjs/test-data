Distribution Test, Incurrence Test and Maintenance Test:
The Distribution Test is met if the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to Pro Forma EBITDA is not greater than 3.00.
The Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to Pro Forma EBITDA is not greater than:
3.00 during the period from (and including) the First Issue Date up to (and including) the Final Redemption Date;
(b) Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds:
3.00 during the period from (and including) the First Issue Date up to (and including) the Final Redemption Date.
