Acceleration amount: In the event of an acceleration of the Bonds, the Issuer shall redeem all Bonds with an amount per Bond equal to the applicable Call Option Price together with accrued but unpaid interest, other than if the acceleration of the Bonds are caused by an Acquisition Failure Event in which case the Bonds shall be redeemed with an amount per Bond equal to 101% of the Nominal Amount together with accrued interest.

De-listing Event:
A De-listing Event occurs if (i) the shares in the Issuer are not listed and admitted to trading on Nasdaq Stockholm, Nasdaq First North Premier or any other recognised regulated or unregulated market place, (ii) trading in the shares in the Issuer on the relevant market is suspended for a period of 15 consecutive Business Days and such suspension is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect or (iii) the Bonds are de-listed from trading on Nasdaq Stockholm, Nasdaq First North Premier or any other recognised regulated or unregulated market place and such suspension is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
