13.2
In the event that IFRS changes, and no longer recognizes or requires fair value
adjustments in the calculation of the book value of properties, the equity and total assets
shall (for the calculation of Equity Ratio above) nevertheless be adjusted with reference
to such market values as if IFRS, as applied on the First Issue Date, would remain
applicable.
