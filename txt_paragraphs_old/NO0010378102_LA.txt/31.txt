10.4 Upon a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of the Bonds at a price according to the redemption table in Clause 10.3 (plus accrued interest).
The Put Option must be exercised within 60 calender days after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 16.1 (h), however no later than 10 Banking Days prior the Maturity Date.
The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the Loan of the pre-payment request. The Put Date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the pre-payment request, however no later that the Banking Day prior to the Maturity Date.
11 Interest in the event of late payment
11.1 In the event that payment of interest or principal is not made on the relevant Payment Date, the amount outstanding shall bear interest from the Payment Date at an interest rate equivalent to the interest rate according to Clause 9 plus 5.00 percentage points.
11.2 The outstanding amounts shall bear interest as mentioned above until payment is made, whether or not the Loan is declared to be in default pursuant to Clause 15.1 (a), cf. Clauses 15.2-15.4.
