(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Intercompany Loans.
13.8 Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose o f all or substantially all o f its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to a Group Company, the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries), unless such sale, transfer or disposal is carried out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
(b) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Obligor will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any asset which is subject to the Transaction Security.
