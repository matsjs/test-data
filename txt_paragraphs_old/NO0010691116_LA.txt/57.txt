10. 1.2
Maturity and Scheduled Instalments
The Bonds shall be partially repaid by the Issuer in 16 quarterly Scheduled Instalments (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount), the first Scheduled Instalment falling due on the Interest Payment Date in October 2014, and each subsequent Scheduled Instalment falling due on each quarterly Interest Payment Date thereafter.
On the Maturity Date, the remaining Outstanding Bonds shall be repaid in full at par value together with accrued interest thereon (the "Balloon Repayment").
10.1.3 Payment of instalments must be carried out pro rata in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository.
