"Issuer's Share Charge" means a share charge granted by the Parent over all of the shares of the Issuer, together with inter alia, letters of resignation (effective upon an Event of Default for which the Bond Trustee has issued a notice) (if legally possible) from the current board members of the Issuer and covenants to obtain such from future board members under the laws of Singapore.
"Liquidity Account" means an account with an Account Bank in the name of the issuer in which the Issuer shall deposit and at all times keep for credit of the Issuer a minimum amount of USD 7,500,000, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means, except if it constitutes a Total Loss Event, an event or circumstance where:
(a) the Vessel is sold or disposed of; or
(b) the Parent ceases to be the owner (directly or indirectly) of 100% of the shares in the Issuer or ceases to be the owner (directly) of 100% of the shares in any Sub-Charterer (for as tong as a Charter Contract entered into by such Sub-Charter remains in force).
