From the Delivery Date the Vessel Owner shall ensure that the Vessel maintains its class, flag and name, and that it remains registered in Panama.
(e) Operations in accordance with laws etc.
The Issuer shall at all times exercise its rights under the Project Documents to require that the Vessel is operated in accordance with any relevant laws, regulations, administrative decisions and/or other public authorities as applicable from time to time and jurisdiction to jurisdiction.
(f) Inspection of the Vessel
Upon the request of, and with thirty (30) days prior notice from, the Bond Trustee, the Vessel Owner shall allow for a technical adviser appointed by the Bond Trustee to undertake during normal business hours a technical inspection of the Vessel, provided that such inspections are without interference to the daily operation of the Vessel. Such inspections shall be limited to one per calendar year and shall be at the expense of the Parent, provided that on the occurrence and continuance of an Event of Default such inspections may be conducted at any time at the Parent's expense.
14. FEES AND EXPENSES 14.1 The Issuer shall cover all reasonable and documented costs and expenses incurred by them
or the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent) in connection with this Bond Agreement and the fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, including in connection with the negotiation, preparation, execution and enforcement of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), the listing of the Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository. The Bond Trustee may withhold funds from any escrow account (or similar arrangement) or from other funds received from the Issuer or any other person, irrespective of such funds being subject to Security under a Finance Documents, to set-off and cover any such costs and expenses.
