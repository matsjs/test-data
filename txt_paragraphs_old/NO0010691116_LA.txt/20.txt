"Outstanding Bonds" means the Bonds not redeemed or otherwise discharged.
"Parent Internal Loan" means the loan from the Issuer to the Parent, to be governed by the Parent Internal Loan Agreement, for the purpose of reducing the Parent's (or any of its subsidiaries') third party debt and/or liabilities. If the Parent Internal Loan triggers a guarantee obligation from the Issuer to the Parent as evidenced by a legal opinion acceptable to the Bond Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders and issued by counsel acceptable to the Bond Trustee, then the amount that would otherwise have been borrowed by the Parent pursuant to the Parent Internal Loan Agreement may instead be applied as dividend from the Issuer to the Parent ("Permitted Dividend").
"Parent Internal Loan Agreement" means the loan agreement between the Issuer as lender and the Parent as borrower in respect of the Parent Internal Loan, in form and substance acceptable to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and stipulating, inter alia, (i) that the Parent Internal Loan shall be immediately payable upon acceleration of the Bonds following an Event of Defautt; and (ii) an interest rate equal to the Fixed Rate and which may accrue and be added to the principal amount and payable in accordance with item (i).
"Parent Internal Loan Assignment" means a pledge or an assignment (or such similar Security under the relevant jurisdiction) of the rights of the Issuer as lender under the Parent Internal Loan (if any).
