"Escrow Account" means an account with an Account Bank in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the pledge over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means a securities exchange or other reputable regulated market (including the Euro MTF Market or the Luxembourg Stock Exchange).
"Extension Period" has the meaning given to that term in Clause 13.2.1 (s)(i).
"Face Value" means the denomination of each of the Bonds, as set out in Clause 2.2.3.
"Fiduciary Bank" means Banco Nacional de Mexico, S.A., integrante del Grupo Financiero Banamex, Division Fiduciaria, being the Mexican trustee (fiduciario) in relation to the Mexican Trust Agreement.
