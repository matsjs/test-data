Definitions
"Account Bank" means a first class Norwegian bank with a credit rating of at least an "A-" rating from Standard & Poor's Rating Services or Fitch Ratings Ltd or "A3" rating from Moody's Investors Services Limited, selected by the Issuer.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Amortisation(s)" means the Scheduled Instalments and the Balloon Repayment.
"Application of Earnings" means the order for application of earnings as set out in Clause 13.7.1.
"Assignment of Insurances" means a Singapore law assignment of any relevant insurances related to the Vessel (other than third party liability insurances).
"Balloon Repayment" has the meaning given to that term in Clause 10.1.2.
"Banking Day" means any day (other than a Saturday and Sunday) on which banks are open for business in Oslo and New York.
"Banking Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the period end date occurs on a day that is not a Banking Day, and if such date is not a Banking Day, payments of interest will be made on the first following day that is a Banking Day (No Adjustments of Banking Day).
