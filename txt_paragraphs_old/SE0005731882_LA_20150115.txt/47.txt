Redemption and repurchase of the Notes
Issuer's purchase of Notes
The Issuer may, subject to applicable law and approval of the SFSA, at any time and at any price purchase Notes on the market or in any other way. The Notes held by the Issuer may at the Issuer's discretion, subject to approval of the SFSA to the extent required, be retained, sold or cancelled by the Issuer.
