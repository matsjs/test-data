17.4
If the Issuer or the Agent shall make any payment under this Clause 17, the Issuer or the
Agent, as applicable, shall notify the Noteholders of any such payment not less than ten
(10) days and not more than twenty (20) days before the payment is made. Such notice
shall specify the Record Date, the payment date and the amount to be paid.
Notwithstanding the foregoing, for any Interest due but unpaid the Record Date
specified in Clause 8.1 shall apply.
