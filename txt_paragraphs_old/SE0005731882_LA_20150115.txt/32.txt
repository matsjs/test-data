Right to act on behalf of a Noteholder
7.1
If any person other than a Noteholder wishes to exercise any rights under the Finance
Documents, it must obtain a power of attorney or other proof of authorisation from the
Noteholder or a successive, coherent chain of powers of attorney or proofs o f
authorisation starting with the Noteholder and authorising such person.
7.2
A Noteholder may issue one or several powers of attorney to third parties to represent it
