The Issuer shall immediately notify the Noteholders and the Agent upon becoming aware of the occurrence of a Capital Disqualification Event, Rating Event and a Taxation Event.
Information from the Agent
The Agent is entitled to disclose to the Noteholders any event or circumstance directly or indirectly relating to the Issuer or the Notes. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Agent may if it considers it to be beneficial to the interests of the Noteholders delay disclosure or refrain from disclosing certain information other than in respect of a Capital Disqualification Event, Rating Event, Taxation Event and a Bankruptcy Event.
