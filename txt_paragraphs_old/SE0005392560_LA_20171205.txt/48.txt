12. Upstream Guarantee
(a) As continuing security for the due and punctual fulfillment of the Issuer's payment obligations towards the Secured Parties under the Bonds, the Issuer undertakes to deliver, on the Issue Date, the Upstream Guarantee duly executed by the Upstream Guarantors to the Secured Parties represented by the Agent.
(b) The Issuer shall procure that the Upstream Guarantee is perfected in accordance with its terms on or before the Issue Date.
