15.9 Derivative Transactions
The Issuer shall, within five (5) Business Days of the Issue Date, enter into foreign currency hedging arrangements with one or more reputable swap counterparties for the purposes of hedging the currency exposure to which it will be exposed through the issue of the NOK Bonds and the SEK Bonds.
15.10 Increase of interest under the Senior Unsecured Loan Agreement
The Issuer shall not agree to any increase of the applicable margin, which is added to the relevant interest base to determine the interest under Senior Unsecured Loans, or the fixed interest rate for any Senior Unsecured Loan, unless a corresponding increase of the Interest also is made with respect to the Bonds.
