8.2 Capitalisation of interest

(i) with respect to each SEK Bonds, from (but excluding) the Second Amendment Effective Date to (and including) the Interest Payment date falling on or about 31 March 2018, and
(ii) with respect to each NOK Bond from (and including) the Second Amendment Effective Date to (but excluding) the Interest Payment Date falling on 31 March 2018,
