8. Interest and capitalisation of interest
8.1 Interest
(a) As from the Second Amendment Effective Date, each SEK Bond carries Interest on its Capital Nominal Amount from time to time at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Second Amendment Effective Date up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date and the NOK Bonds carries Interest on their aggregate Capital Nominal Amount from time to time at the Interest Rate from (and including) the Second Amendment Effective Date up to (but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date.
(b) Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Bonds shall be made to the Bondholders on each Interest Payment Date
