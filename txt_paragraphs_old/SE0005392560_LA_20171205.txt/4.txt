"Business Day Norway" means a day other than a Saturday, Sunday or a public holiday in Norway on which the Norwegian Central Bank's settlement system is open and commercial banks in Norway are open for business.
"Cash" mean an amount, denominated in Rand, or any other currency (approved by the Senior Lenders under the Senior Unsecured Loan Agreement), of cash in hand, or credit balances or amounts on deposit with a bank to which a member of the Group is alone (or together with other members of the Group) beneficially entitled if:
(a) the cash is accessible on demand or within thirty days after the relevant date of calculation;
(b) access of that cash is not contingent on the prior discharge of any other indebtedness of any member of the Group or of any other person whatsoever or on the satisfaction of any other condition other than notice or demand thereof (but not exceeding the period of demand referred to in (a) above);
(c) there is no Security over that cash or over claims in respect thereof; and
