11. Cash Sweep
(a) If any cash is swept under the Senior Unsecured Loan Agreement to be applied to repay debt outstanding under the Senior Unsecured Loan Agreement, the Issuer shall redeem the Bonds in an amount equal to the Bonds Pro-rata Share.
(b) Any partial redemption of SEK Bonds in accordance with Clause 11(a) shall be made as partial prepayments of all outstanding SEK Bonds by way of reducing the Nominal Amount of each SEK Bond pro rata (rounded down to the nearest SEK 1.00).
