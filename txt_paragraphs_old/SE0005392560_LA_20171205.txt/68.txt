(vii) Insolvency:
(A) Any Material Group Company is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of its classes of creditors with a view to rescheduling any of its Financial Indebtedness;
(B) The value of the assets of any Material Group Company is less than its unsubordinated liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities); or
(C) A moratorium is declared in respect of any Financial Indebtedness of any Material Group Company.
