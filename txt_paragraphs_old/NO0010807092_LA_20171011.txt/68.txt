14.16
Dividends
The Issuer may declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make loans or other distributions ("Distributions") to its shareholders including servicing or repaying shareholder or other Subordinated Loans (each a "Dividend Payment") to the extent that:
(a) the Dividend Payment in any 12 month period does not exceed 50 per cent, of the Issuer's consolidated net profit according to the Issuer's most recent Financial Report; and
(b) that NIBD / EBITDA immediately following such Dividend Payment does not exceed 3.
