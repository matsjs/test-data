14.8 Negative pledge
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any Security over any of its/their assets (whether present or future).
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Security.
14.9 Financial support
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, make or grant any loans, grant any credit or give any guarantee or indemnity to or for the benefit of any person or group or otherwise voluntary assume any financial liability, whether actual or contingent, in respect of any other person or group, not being a member of the Group.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Financial Support.
