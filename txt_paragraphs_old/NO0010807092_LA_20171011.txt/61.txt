13. PARENT'S GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS As long as no IPO of the Issuer has been successfully completed, the Parent shall during the term of the Bonds comply with inter alia the undertakings set forth in this Clause 13 {Parent's general undertakings) at any time (unless the Trustee or the Bondholders' meeting (as the case may be) in writing has agreed otherwise).
13.1 Ownership The Parent shall remain the sole shareholder in the Issuer as o f the Issue Date, provided that it is not required to subscribe for any further shares in any share issue (such share issue to never dilute the Parent below 2/3 of the issued shares in the Issuer).
13.2 Disposal of business The Parent shall ensure that no Group Company sells or otherwise disposes of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless any such transaction is carried out at a fair market value and on customary terms, and provided that such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.3 Arm's length transactions The Parent shall not engage in, or permit any Group Company to engage in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except upon terms that are no less favorable to the Parent or such member of the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
13.4 Granting of loans The Parent shall not grant any loans to the Issuer and/or any of the Guarantors other than Subordinated Loans.
13.5 Co-investment The Parent shall not engage in any business arrangement that will (directly or indirectly) compete with the business o f the Group.
13.6 Maintenance of Licenses The Parent shall ensure that the Issuer and each of the Guarantors at all times carries out all reasonable effort to ensure compliance with all obligations under the Licenses.
