Undertaking in relation to rating
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to furnish, or procure that there is furnished, from time to time, any and all documents, instruments, information and undertakings (to the extent reasonably requested by the Rating Agency) that may be necessary in order to maintain the current rating of the Notes by the Rating Agency.
