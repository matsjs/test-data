12.7 Disposals The Issuer shall not sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to a Group Company), unless such sale, transfer or disposal is carried out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
12.8 Related party transactions Without limiting Clause 12.2 (Compliance with laws), the Issuer shall conduct all business transactions with any Affiliate at market terms and otherwise on an arm's length basis.
12.9 LTV Covenant The Issuer shall ensure that the Loan to Value Ratio does not at any time exceed the LTV Covenant, and shall, on an annual basis; (i) within fifty (50) Business Days following 31 December each year, by delivery of a separate certificate, confirm its compliance with the LTV Covenant as of 31 December; the LTV Covenant at each time to be measured based on a Market Value dated no earlier than three (3) months after the date of the previous Market Value. The first valuation shall be provided no later than fifty Business Days after 31 December 2018.
12.10 Distribution restrictions The Issuer may not make any dividend payment or any other distributions, whether in cash or in kind, if the Loan to Value Ratio exceeds the LTV according to the LTV Covenant before or after the intended distribution.
