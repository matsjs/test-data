Special undertakings of Guarantor
The Issuer shall procure that the Guarantor enters into the Guarantee and that the undertakings set out in this Clause 12 are made mutatis mutandis (as applicable) also by the Guarantor and are included in the Guarantee.
Agent Agreement
The Issuer shall, in accordance with the Agent Agreement:
(a) pay fees to the Bond Trustee;
(b) indemnify the Bond Trustee for costs, losses and liabilities;
(c) furnish to the Bond Trustee all information requested by or otherwise required to be delivered to the Bond Trustee; and
(d) not act in a way which would give the Bond Trustee a legal or contractual right to terminate the Agent Agreement.
