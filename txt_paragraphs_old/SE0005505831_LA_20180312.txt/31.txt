STATUS OF THE BONDS
4.
USE OF PROCEEDS
4.1
The Net Proceeds shall be transferred by the Issuing Agent to the Escrow Account. For the
purpose of securing that the Conditions Precedent for Disbursement have been fulfilled
before the disbursement of the Net Proceeds, the Escrow Account has been pledged in
favour of the Holders and the Bond Trustee under the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement
until the Conditions Precedent for Disbursement have been fulfilled.
