Project Documents
The Issuer shall be the only Group Company being party to a Project Document and the Issuer shall (i) perform all material obligations under the Project Documents to which it is a party, (ii) not amend, terminate or waive, or where relevant, vote in favour of any amendment, termination or waiver, in respect of any terms under any of the Project Documents, in each case which might have a Material Adverse Effect and (iii) promptly upon request provide the Bond Trustee with copies of any Project Document to the extent permitted under any applicable confidentiality restrictions.
