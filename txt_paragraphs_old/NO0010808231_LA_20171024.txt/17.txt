"Guarantor" means each of the Parent and the Ultimate Parent.
"IFRS" means International Financial Reporting Standards and guidelines and interpretations issued by the International Accounting Standards Board (or any predecessor and successor thereof), in force from time to time.
"Initial Distribution Transaction" means Distribution Transactions to the Parent and/or the Ultimate Parent in an aggregate amount of up to EUR 10,000,000.
"Initial Nominal Amount" means the nominal amount of each Bond as set out in Clause 2.1 (Amount, denomination and ISIN o f the Bonds).
"Insolvent" means that a person:
(a) is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due;
(b) suspends making payments on any of its debts generally; or
(c) is otherwise considered insolvent or bankrupt within the meaning of the relevant bankruptcy legislation of the jurisdiction which can be regarded as its center of main interest as such term is understood pursuant to Regulation (EU) no. 2015/848 on insolvency proceedings (as amended).
"Interest Cover Ratio" means, in respect of any Relevant Period, the ratio of EBITDA to Net Finance Charges.
