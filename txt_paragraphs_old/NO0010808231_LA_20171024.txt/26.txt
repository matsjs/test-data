"Overdue Amount" means any amount required to be paid by the Issuer under any of the Finance Documents but not made available to the Bondholders on the relevant Payment Date or otherwise not paid on its applicable due date.
"Parallel Debt" has the meaning given to it in Clause 12.11.
"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to act as its paying agent with respect to the Bonds in the CSD.
"Payment Date" means any Interest Payment Date or any Repayment Date.
"Permitted Account Bank Security" any Security entered into by the Issuer in the ordinary course of its banking arrangements over Dutch bank accounts in favour of the account holding bank and granted as part of that financial institution's standard terms and conditions, including without limitation, under general terms and conditions of banks, provided, in each case, that such Security only secures obligations and liabilities for which the Issuer is the principal debtor (and which shall not include guarantees or indemnities for the obligations or liabilities of others).
"Permitted Farm-out" means a farm-out of any Hydrocarbon Assets held by the Issuer, other than the Project Licence for the Q07 block, against a consideration to carry costs of further development of such Hydrocarbon Asset or cash (or a combination thereof), provided that any such transaction does not impair or reduce the Issuer's ownership interests in or economic benefits from the Q07 block.
