14.33 Distributions (a) The Ultimate Parent shall not declare or make any Distribution, unless:
(i) no Event of Default is continuing at such time;
(ii) the First Compliance Certificate Date has occurred; and
(iii) the amount of the Distribution in any calendar year, when taken together with any other Distribution made or declared in that calendar year, does not exceed an amount equal to 50.00 per cent, of the Ultimate Parent's consolidated profit after tax for the preceding calendar year.
(b) The Parent may only make Distributions to the Ultimate Parent.
