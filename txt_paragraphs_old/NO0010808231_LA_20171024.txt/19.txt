"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated (in respect of the Parent and the Issuer) and consolidated (in respect of the Ultimate Parent) semi-annual financial statements for the semi-annual period ending on a Calculation Date prepared in accordance with the Approved Accounting Principles.
"Issue Date" means 25 October 2017.
"Issuer" means the company designated as such in the preamble to these Bond Terms.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds which are owned by an Obligor or any Affiliate of an Obligor.
"Issuer Share Pledge" means a Dutch law governed share pledge over all of the shares in the Issuer, granted by the Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents.
"Leverage Ratio" means, in respect of any Relevant Period, the ratio of Total Net Debt on the last day of that Relevant Period to EBITDA in respect of that Relevant Period.
