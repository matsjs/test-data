14.13
The Issuer shall not take part in any petroleum activities or related activities in any geographical area other than the Dutch continental shelf. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Issuer is permitted to engage in limited activities outside the Dutch continental shelf, provided that such activities relates to Hydrocarbon Assets straddling other jurisdictions in addition to the Dutch continental shelf, and provided further in each case that any cash expenditures to petroleum activities or related activities outside the Dutch continental shelf does not exceed 20 per cent, of the Issuer's aggregated expenditures to exploration, development and production activities during any fiscal year.
14.14
Operations
The Issuer shall ensure that its operations are conducted in accordance with acknowledged practices related to the oil and gas industry in all material respect.
14.15
Pledged Accounts
(a) The Issuer shall ensure that each Pledged Account shall be pledged and perfected on in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the obligations and liabilities of the Obligors under the Finance Documents. No Pledged Account shall be blocked, unless an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing.
(b) The Issuer shall ensure that all Project Proceeds are paid directly into a Pledged Account.
