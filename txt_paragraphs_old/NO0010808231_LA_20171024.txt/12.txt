"Distribution Transaction" means any Financial Support by the Issuer, any Distribution made by the Issuer or any payment under a Subordinated Loan by the Issuer.
"EBITDA" means, in respect of any Relevant Period, the Issuer's earnings before interest, taxation, depreciation, value adjustments and amortisation of goodwill and capital gains/losses for that Relevant Period:
(a) excluding any items (positive or negative) of a one off, non-recurring, non-operational, extraordinary, unusual or exceptional nature;
(b) after adding back any losses to the extent covered by any insurance and (to the extent not already included) the proceeds of any business interruption insurance; and
(c) before taking into account any unrealised gains or losses on any derivative instrument (other than any derivative instruments which are accounted for on a hedge accounting basis).
"Equity Ratio" means a ratio, expressed as a percentage, of the Total Equity to Total Assets.
