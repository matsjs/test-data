"TON Subordinated Loans Pledge Agreement" means a Dutch law governed receivables pledge over all Subordinated Loans made by the Parent to the Issuer, granted by the Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents.
"Total Equity" means, at the relevant Calculation Date, the aggregate amount of the Total Assets less the Total Liabilities.
"Total Liabilities" means, at the relevant Calculation Date, the aggregate amount of the Issuer's long-term and short term debt and such other liabilities, which in accordance with the Approved Accounting Principles, shall be included as liabilities in the Issuer's balance sheet.
"Total Net Debt" means, at any time, the aggregate amount of all obligations of the Issuer for or in respect of Borrowings at that time (without double counting):
(a) including, in the case of finance leases, only the capitalised value thereof; and
(b) after deducting the aggregate amount of Cash and Cash Equivalent Investments.
"Transaction Security" means the Security created or expressed to be created in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Secured Parties) pursuant to the Transaction Security Documents.
"Transaction Security Documents" means, collectively, the Escrow Account Pledge, all of the documents which shall be executed or delivered pursuant to Clause 2.5 (Transaction Security) and all other documents expressed to create any Security by the relevant grantor thereof in respect of the Obligors' obligations under any of the Finance Documents.
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds less the Issuer's Bonds and a Voting Bond shall mean any single one of those Bonds.
