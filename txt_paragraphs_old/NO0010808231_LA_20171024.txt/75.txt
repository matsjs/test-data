(ii) at all times after the Interest Payment Date occurring in April 2019, is at least equal to the amount of the next upcoming interest payment.
(b) The Issuer shall ensure that the Debt Service Retention Account shall be blocked and pledged in favour of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to the Debt Service Retention Account Pledge, and the Account Bank shall waive any set-off rights to such account.
(c) The Issuer shall ensure that the amounts deposited on the Debt Service Retention Account shall only be released and applied for the due payment of interest payable in respect of the Bond Issue or towards the redemption of all the Bonds in full upon the exercise of a Call Option or a Mandatory Prepayment Event.
