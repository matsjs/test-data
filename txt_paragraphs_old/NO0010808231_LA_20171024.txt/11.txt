"Debt Service Retention Account" means an account opened as a client account on behalf of the Issuer, held with the Escrow Manager with a reputable Norwegian bank, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Debt Service Retention Account Pledge" means a Norwegian law security assignment over the Debt Service Retention Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Default Notice" means a written notice to the Issuer as described in Clause 15.2 (Acceleration o f the Bonds).
"Default Repayment Date" means the settlement date set out by the Bond Trustee in a Default Notice requesting early redemption of the Bonds.
"Distribution" means any dividend payment, repurchase, redemption or retirement of shares, making of any loans or other equity or capital distributions or payments (including group contributions) to direct or indirect shareholders (including servicing of shareholder loans), whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect.
"Distribution Threshold" means, with respect to any Distribution Transaction, a threshold equal to the sum of:
(a) an amount of EUR 10,000,000 during the term of the Bonds in as far as the same funds an Initial Distribution Transaction; plus
(b) after the occurrence of the First Compliance Certificate Date, an amount equal to 50.00 per cent, of the Issuer's profit after tax of the preceding calendar year, and when determining whether that threshold amount has been or will be exceeded with respect to any Distribution Transaction to be made or declared in that calendar year the aggregate amount of all Distribution Transactions made or declared in that calendar year shall be added to the amount of the Distribution Transaction for which the calculation is made.
