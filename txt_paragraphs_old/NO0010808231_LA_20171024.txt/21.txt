"Major Disposal Event" means that:
(a) the Ultimate Parent ceases to own and control 100 per cent, of the shares and/or voting rights in the Parent;
(b) the Parent ceases to own and control more than 50 per cent, of the shares and/or voting rights in the Issuer; or
(c) the Issuer ceases to hold an ownership/working interest in the Project Licence of at least 50 per cent.
"Major Total Loss Event" means an actual or constructive total loss of the Project Licence (including related assets such as production units, installations and infrastructure).
"Make Whole Amount" means an amount equal to the sum of:
(a) the present value on the Call Option Repayment Date of 105 per cent, of the Nominal Amount of the redeemed Bonds as if such payment originally had taken place on the First Call Date; and
(b) the present value on the Call Option Repayment Date of the remaining interest payments of the redeemed Bonds, less any accrued and unpaid interest on the redeemed Bonds as at the Call Option Repayment Date, to and including the First Call Date,
