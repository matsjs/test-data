Each Guarantor shall, and the Ultimate Parent shall procure that each other Group Company (other than the Issuer) will, in all material respects obtain, maintain and comply with the terms of any Authorisation required for the conduct of its business as carried out at the date of the Bond Terms if a failure to do so would have Material Adverse Effect.
14.24
Compliance with laws
Each Guarantor shall, and the Ultimate Parent shall procure that each other Group Company (other than the Issuer) will, comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject from time to time, if failure so to comply would have a Material Adverse Effect.
14.25
Continuation of business
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on by the Group at the Issue Date (for the avoidance of doubt, a sale, transfer, winding-up, liquidation or other disposal of any of the Group's onshore oil and gas business in the Netherlands and/or Germany will not constitute a material change of the general nature of business of the Group).
