"Affiliate" means, in relation to any specified person:
(a) any person which is a Subsidiary of the specified person;
(b) any person who has Decisive Influence over the specified person (directly or indirectly); and
(c) any person which is a Subsidiary of an entity who has Decisive Influence (directly or indirectly) over the specified person.
"Annual Financial Statements" means the audited unconsolidated (in respect of the Parent and the Issuer) and consolidated (in respect of the Ultimate Parent) annual financial statements for any financial year, prepared in accordance with the Approved Accounting Principles, such financial statements to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and report of the board of directors.
"Approved Accounting Principles" means the generally accepted accounting principles in the Netherlands (including, if relevant for a Group Company, IFRS).
