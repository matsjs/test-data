"TARGET Day" means any day on which the Trans-European Automated Real-time Gross Settlement Express Transfer payment system is open for the settlement of payments in euro.
"Tax Event Repayment Date" means the date set out in a notice from the Issuer to the Bondholders pursuant to Clause 10.5 (Early redemption option due to a tax event).
"TOH Intra-Group Loan Pledge Agreement" means a Dutch law governed receivables pledge over all intra-Group loans made by the Ultimate Parent to the Parent, granted by the Ultimate Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents.
"TON Share Pledge" means a Dutch law governed share pledge over all of the shares in the Parent, granted by the Ultimate Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents.
"TON Subordinated Loans Pledge Agreement" means a Dutch law governed receivables pledge over all Subordinated Loans made by the Parent to the Issuer, granted by the Parent in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents.
"Total Assets" means, at the relevant Calculation Date, the aggregate book value of the Issuer's assets, which in accordance with the Approved Accounting Principles, shall be included as assets in the Issuer's balance sheet.
