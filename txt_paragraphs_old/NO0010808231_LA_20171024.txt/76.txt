Distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any Distribution without the prior consent of the Bond Trustee or Bondholders' Meeting (as the case may be) with a simple majority resolution (as the case may be), unless:
(a) no Event of Default is continuing; and
(b) the amount of the Distribution does not cause the Distribution Threshold to be exceeded.
For the avoidance of doubt, no payment of ordinary fees for employee, management services or general services procured by the Issuer to any other Group Company shall be considered as "Distribution" for the purpose of the provisions in this clause.
