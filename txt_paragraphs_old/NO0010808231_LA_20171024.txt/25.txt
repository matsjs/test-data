"Omnibus Pledge" means a Dutch law governed omnibus pledge, granted by the Issuer in favour of the Bond Trustee on first priority, as security for the Obligor's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents, comprising:
(a) a receivables pledge of all of the Issuer's monetary claims under or with respect to any insurances required to be taken out hereunder;
(b) a receivables pledge over each of the Issuer's existing bank accounts held with Dutch banks (except for the Escrow Account and the Debt Service Retention Account) and the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer on such bank accounts;
(c) a receivables pledge over earnings from the sale of hydrocarbon produce under offtake agreements or gas and/or oil sale agreements; and
(d) a receivables pledge over monetary claims under or with respect to any loans granted by the Issuer to another Group Company.
"Outstanding Bonds" means any Bonds issued in accordance with these Bond Terms to the extent not redeemed or otherwise discharged.
