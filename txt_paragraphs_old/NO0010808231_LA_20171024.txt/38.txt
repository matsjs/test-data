2.2 Tenor of the Bonds The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
2.3 Use of proceeds The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the issuance of the Bonds (net of legal costs, fees payable to the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) to fund:
(i) the Debt Service Retention Account with an amount of EUR 11,092,500; (ii) the Initial Distribution Transactions;
(iii) the Project; and
(iv) the general corporate purposes of the Issuer.
2.4 Status of the Bonds The Bonds and each other payment obligation under or in relation to the Finance Documents will constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer and each relevant Obligor. The Bonds will rank pari passu between themselves and will rank at least pari passu with the claims of the Obligors' other unsubordinated creditors (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application).
