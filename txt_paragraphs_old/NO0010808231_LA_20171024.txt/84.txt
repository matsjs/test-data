14.30
Investments by the Sponsor
The Ultimate Parent shall ensure that the Sponsor is not (i) the direct shareholder in any Group Company other than the Ultimate Parent or (ii) the indirect shareholder in any Group Company other than through its shareholding in the Ultimate Parent.
14.31
Financial Indebtedness
The Ultimate Parent shall procure that no Group Company incurs or maintains any Financial Indebtedness (save for the Bonds) for which the Sponsor or any Affiliate of the Sponsor (other than a Group Company or a financial institution) is the creditor.
