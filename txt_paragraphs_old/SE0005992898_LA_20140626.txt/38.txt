10.6 Voluntary Redemption due to a Change of Control Event
If Bondholders representing more than 30 per cent. of the outstanding Bonds during the fifteen (15) Business Days' period referred to in Clause 10.5(a) (the "Put Exercise Period") has requested that their Bonds shall be repurchased, the Issuer shall have the right to redeem all, but not only some, of the remaining Bonds at a price equal to 102.00 per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest during a period of ten (10) Business Days following the end of the Put Exercise Period. The redemption date must fall no later than twenty (20) Business Days after the end of Put Exercise Period.
