15.3 Cross-Acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness of any Material Group Company is not paid when due as extended by any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this Clause 15.3 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness is less than SEK 10,000,000 and provided that it does not apply to any Financial Indebtedness owed to a Group Company.
