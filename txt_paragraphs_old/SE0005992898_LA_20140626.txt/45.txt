13. Incurrence Covenant
13.1 Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time:
(a) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA does not exceed 3.75:1; and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 3.00:1,
calculated in accordance with the calculation principles set out in Clause 13.2 (Calculation Adjustments), on a consolidated basis and based on the most recently delivered Financial Report.
