3. Use of Proceeds
(a) The Net Proceeds from the issuance of the Initial Bonds shall be applied (i) first, by granting the Bond Proceeds Loan and for repayment of the Refinancing Debt and (ii) secondly, against general corporate purposes of the Group.
(b) Any proceeds from any issuance of Subsequent Bonds shall be used for general corporate purposes of the Group, including acquisitions of the Group.
(c) For the avoidance of doubt, neither the Net Proceeds nor any other amount available to the Group or any Guarantor may be applied towards the direct or indirect repayment of any amount under the SEK 60,400,000 vendor loan agreement dated April 2013 (entered into between Ceratiidae I AB (formerly named Goldcup 8641 AB) and the parties named as Lenders therein) where such repayment, in the absence of this prohibition, would be required to be made because of the issue of the Initial Bonds and/or application of the Net Proceeds.
