14.9 Clean Down Period
The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year, there shall be a period of three (3) consecutive days during which the amount outstanding under the Working Capital Facility, less Cash and Cash Equivalent Investments, amounts to zero (0) or less. Not less than 6 months shall elapse between two such periods.
