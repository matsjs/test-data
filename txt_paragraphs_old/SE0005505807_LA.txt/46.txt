Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not create or allow to subsist any Security over any of its assets or revenues other than:
(a) any Security over cash paid into an escrow account pursuant to any deposit or retention of purchase price arrangements; or
(b) any Security or preferential arrangement not permitted by paragraph (a) above, securing indebtedness the principal amount of which does not in aggregate exceed SEK 3,000,000.
