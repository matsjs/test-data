A Noteholder may issue one or several powers of attorney to third parties to represent it in
relation to some or all of the Notes held by it. Any such representative may act
independently under these Terms and Conditions in relation to the Notes for which such
representative is entitled to represent the Noteholder and may further delegate its right to
represent the Noteholder by way of a further power of attorney.
