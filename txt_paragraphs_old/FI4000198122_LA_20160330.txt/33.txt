8.5
8.5.1
Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Noteholder shall have the right to
request that all, or only some, of its Notes be repurchased at a price per Note equal to
101 per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest, during a
period of twenty (20) Business Days following a notice from the Issuer of the Change of
Control Event pursuant to Clause 9.1.2 (after which time period such right shall lapse).
However, such period may not start earlier than upon the occurrence of the Change of
Control Event.
