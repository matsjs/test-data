STATUS OF THE BONDS
The Bonds constitute direct, unconditional, unsecured and unsubordinated debt obligations of the Issuer and shall at all times rank pari passu and without any preference among them, and at least pari passu with all other unsecured obligations of the Issuer other than those mandatorily preferred by law.
