9.1
Each Initial Bond carries Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Issue Date up
to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Bond will cany Interest at
the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to
its issuance up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date.
9.2
Interest accrues during an Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Bonds shall
be made to the Bondholders on each Interest Payment Date for the preceding Interest Period.
