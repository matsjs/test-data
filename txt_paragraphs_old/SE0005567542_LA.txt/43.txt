PARENT GUARANTEE
The Issuer's obligations under the Bonds shall be unconditionally and irrevocably guaranteed by the Parent and, as regards the Issuer's payment obligations, the Parent's guarantee shall be as for its own debt (Sw. proprieborgen) by way of the separately issued Parent Guarantee. The Parent Guarantee shall not in any way prevent a Permitted Merger and upon a Permitted Merger or an Issuer Listing Event the Parent Guarantee shall automatically expire.
