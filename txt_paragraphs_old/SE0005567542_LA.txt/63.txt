14.2 Interest Cover
The Issuer shall ensure that the Interest Cover Ratio as of the last day of each financial quarter shall not be less than 1.50:1.
14.3 Capital Cover
The Issuer shall ensure that the Capital Cover Ratio as of the last day of each financial quarter shall not be less than 1.10:1.
