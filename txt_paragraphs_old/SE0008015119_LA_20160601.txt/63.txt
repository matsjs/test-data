18.
AMENDMENTS AND WAIVERS
18.1
The Issuer and the Agent (acting on behalf of the Bondholders) may agree to amend the
Finance Documents or waive any provision in a Finance Document, provided that:
(a) such amendment or waiver is not detrimental to the interest of the Bondholders, or is made solely for the purpose of rectifying obvious errors and mistakes;
