14.3
Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the acceleration
of the Bonds constitute escrow funds (redovisningsmedel) and must be held on a separate
interest-bearing account on behalf of the Bondholders and the other interested parties.
The Agent shall arrange for payments of such funds in accordance with this Clause 14 as
soon as reasonably practicable.
