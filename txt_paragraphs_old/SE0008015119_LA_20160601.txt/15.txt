4.2
The Issuer shall provide to the Agent, prior to the issuance of any Subsequent Bonds the
following, in form and substance satisfactory to the Agent:
(a) a copy of a resolution from the board of directors of the Issuer approving the issue of the Subsequent Bonds and resolving to enter into documents necessary in connection therewith;
(b) the articles of association and certificate of incorporation (as at the date of (a)) of the Issuer; and
(c) a certificate from the Issuer confirming that no Event of Default is continuing or would result from the issue of the Subsequent Bonds and that no such issuance would cause a breach of any undertaking of the Issuer in accordance with Clause 12.
