10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (the "Call Option"):
(a)
With settlement date at any time from and including the Interest Payment
Date falling 2 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest
Payment Date falling 3 years after the Issue Date at a price equal to
103.875% o f par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount);
(b) With settlement date at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date falling 3 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date falling 3 years and 6 months after the Issue Date at a price equal to 101.9375% o f par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount); and
(c) With settlement date at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date falling 3 years and 6 months after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100.00% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount).
