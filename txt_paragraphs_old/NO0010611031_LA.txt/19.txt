10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the entire Bond Issue (all or nothing) as follows (Call Option):
(a) from and including the Interest Payment Date in May 2013 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2014, at a price equal to 106.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(b) from and including the Interest Payment Date in May 2014 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in May 2015, at a price equal to 104.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(c) from and including the Interest Payment Date in May 2015 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in November 2015, at a price equal to 102,00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount); and
(d) from and including the Interest Payment Date in November 2015 to, and including, the Maturity Date, at a price equal to 100,00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
10.2.2 Exercise ofthe Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option,
10.2.3 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date
10.2.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
