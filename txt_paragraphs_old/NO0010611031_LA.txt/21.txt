10.4.2 Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
10.4.3 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this clause 10.4, the entire amount on the Accounts may be used as partly payment for the redemption.
10.4.4 The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory redemption according to this clause 10.4, at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption.
