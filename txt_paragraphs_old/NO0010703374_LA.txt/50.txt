Mandatory prepayment
Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event (other than a Forced Sale) occurring, the Issuer shall not later than thirty (30) calendar days following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event (unless there is an Event of Default in which case it will be promptly at the prevailing call price in accordance with Clause 15.5, and Clause 10.2), redeem Bonds in accordance with the following principles:
(a) The Issuer shall use any and all Deep Sea Metro Distribution Amounts to pre-pay the Bonds with an aggregate principal amount equal to the Deep Sea Metro Distribution Amount (including any call premium and accrued unpaid interest on the redeemed amount) at the prevailing call price on the date of the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring. If the Issuer receives a Deep Sea Metro Distribution Amount prior to the Interest Payment Date on 15 August 2014, the price at which the Bonds are to be redeemed shall be as set out in 10.2.1 (a) above.
