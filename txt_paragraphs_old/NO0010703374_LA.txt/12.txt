"Deep Sea Metro Shareholders' Agreement" means a shareholders' agreement dated 10 October 2008 (as amended from time to time) entered into between the Issuer, Odfjell and Deep Sea Metro concerning the ownership to and governance of Deep Sea Metro.
"Deep Sea Metro Shareholders Loan" means a shareholder loan in the amount of USD 80,000,000 provided by Odfjell to Deep Sea Metro pursuant to the Sixth Amended and Restated Term Loan Agreement dated 12 December 2013 to be discharged as set out in Clause 2.3 (c).
"Deep Sea Metro Shareholders Loan Discharge" means the repayment and discharge of the Deep Sea Metro Shareholders Loan by (i) Deep Sea Metro's repayment of approximately 60 per cent of the amount outstanding under the Deep Sea Metro Shareholders Loan utilising approximately USD 49,500,000 of the proceeds from the Bonds Issue to be contributed to Deep Sea Metro as equity by the Issuer, and (ii) Odfjell converting the remaining 40 per cent of the Deep Sea Metro Shareholders Loan into equity in Deep Sea Metro.
"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Disbursement Date" means the date on which all conditions precedent pursuant to Clause 6.2.1 are fulfilled and the funds on the Escrow Account are released.
"Distribution" means the making of any dividend payments, loans or other distributions or other transactions implying a transfer of value from the Issuer to the Parent (or affiliates thereof), whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation repurchase of shares, total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in the Issuer's share capital or equity.
"Escrow Account" means an account with the Paying Agent in the name of the Issuer, into which the net proceeds from the Bond Issue will be paid, pledged and blocked on first priority as Security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, and the account bank shall waive any set-off rights against any balance thereon.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means a first priority Norwegian law pledge over the Issuer's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
