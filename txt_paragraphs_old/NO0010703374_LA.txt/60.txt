13.3.4 De-mergers The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
13.3.6
No other business
The Issuer shall remain a single purpose vehicle for the purpose of owning the Issuer's shares in Deep Sea Metro, and shall not acquire any additional assets (other than additional Deep Sea Metro shares) or initiate any additional operations (or assume any liabilities in respect thereto).
13.3.7 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
