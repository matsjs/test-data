13.3.8
The Issuer shall carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time. The Issuer shall ensure that it is not engaged in any conduct prohibited by any legal requirement of any program administered by OFAC of the U. S. Department of Treasury and the Issuer shall not engage in any conduct that would cause adverse consequences to the Issuer or the bondholders under any program or regulations administered by OFAC, UN, UK or EU/EEA authorities.
13.3.9
Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding any additional Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured or from affiliates or third parties) other than: (i) any Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Agreement; and (ii) the Intra-Group Loans.
