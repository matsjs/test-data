"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Commitment Fee" means a commitment fee of 1.5 per cent of the nominal amount of the issued Bond Issue on the Disbursement Date payable by the Issuer from the proceeds of the Bond Issue to the initial subscribers thereof.
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person (i) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or (ii) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person. When determining the relevant person's number of voting rights in the other person or the right to elect and remove members of the board of directors, rights held by the parent company of the relevant person and the parent company's Subsidiaries shall be included.
"Deed of Adherence" means a deed of adherence to be entered into by, the Issuer the Bond Trustee, Deep Sea Metro and Odfjell, whereby inter alia the Trustee as pledgee under the Deep Sea Metro Share Pledge undertakes to, in case of enforcement of the Deep Sea Metro Share Pledge, adhere to the Deep Sea Metro Shareholders' Agreement
"Deep Sea Metro" means Deep Sea Metro Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of Bermuda (registration no. 42348) and owned in part by the Issuer (60 per cent) and in part by Odfjell (40 per cent).
"Deep Sea Metro Distribution Amount" means any distributions or dividends received by either the Parent or the Issuer from Deep Sea Metro or any of its Subsidiaries.
"Deep Sea Metro Share Pledge" means a first priority share pledge granted by the Issuer over all of the shares owned by the Issuer in Deep Sea Metro, provided that such share pledge shall only extend to a maximum of 60 per cent of the issued and outstanding share capital of Deep Sea Metro from time to time, subject to the Deed of Adherence; together with, inter alia, letters of resignation (effective upon an Event of Default) from such current board members as are appointed by the Issuer and covenants to obtain such letters of resignation from future board members appointed by the Issuer.
"Deep Sea Metro Shareholders' Agreement" means a shareholders' agreement dated 10 October 2008 (as amended from time to time) entered into between the Issuer, Odfjell and Deep Sea Metro concerning the ownership to and governance of Deep Sea Metro.
