10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid by the Issuer at 101.00 per cent of Face Value (plus accrued but unpaid interest on the redeemed amount).
