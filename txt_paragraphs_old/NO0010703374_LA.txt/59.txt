13.3.1 Pari passu ranking The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
13.3.2
Security
The Issuer shall ensure that the Security Documents are fully preserved and maintained in good condition and repair, and do all acts which may be necessary to ensure that such Security is created as prescribed in this Bond Agreement and remains duly created, enforceable and perfected on its relevant priority, at the expense of the Issuer.
13.3.3
Mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities.
13.3.4 De-mergers The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
