1. INTERPRETATION 1.1 Definitions
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Application Agreement" means the application agreement entered into between the Manager and each Bondholder under which the Bondholders applied for Bonds in the Bond Issue.
"Additional Security" means all Security provided in accordance with the provisions of Clause 8.1.3.
"Attachments" means the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Book Equity" means the aggregate amount of book equity of Deep Sea Metro as is stated in Deep Sea Metro's consolidated financial statements as the shareholders' equity (in accordance with IFRS).
"Book Equity Ratio" means Book Equity to Total Assets.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
