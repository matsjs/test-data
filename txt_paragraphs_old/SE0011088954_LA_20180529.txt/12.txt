(a) before deducting any amount of tax on profits, gains or income paid or payable by any Group Company;
(b) before deducting any Net Finance Charges;
(c) minus the interest expenses in respect of any lease or hire purchase contract which would, in accordance with the accounting principles applicable on the Issue Date, have been treated as an operating lease but has subsequently been reclassified as a balance sheet liability;
