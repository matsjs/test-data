2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 150,000,000 (U.S. Dollar one hundred and fifty million).
The Face Value is USD 200,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "9.75 per cent Salamander Energy pic Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2020".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0697279.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
