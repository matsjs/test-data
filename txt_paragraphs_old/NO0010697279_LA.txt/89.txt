15.1.7 Creditors' process
Any Group Company has a substantial proportion of its assets impounded, confiscated, attached or made subject to a distraint, or is subject to enforcement of any Security over any of its assets, and such affected assets exceed USD 2,000,000 in market value.
15.1.8 Impossibility or illegality
It is or becomes impossible or unlawful for any Group Company to fulfil or perform any of the terms of any Finance Document to which it is a party.
