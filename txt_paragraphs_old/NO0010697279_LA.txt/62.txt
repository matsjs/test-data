Upon the occurrence of a Triggered Disposal Prepayment Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Triggered Disposal Put Option") of its Bonds from the Triggered Disposal Prepayment Amount at a price of 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds).
The Triggered Disposal Put Option must be exercised within sixty - 60 - calendar days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee of a Triggered Disposal Prepayment Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Triggered Disposal Repayment Event has taken place.

10.4.3
The Triggered Disposal Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. If the aggregate amount of exerc'sed Triggered Disposal Put Options exceeds the Put Disposal Repayment Amount, there shall be a pro rata reduction between the Bondholders exercising the put option. The Bonds shall on the basis of their current Face Value be split into several bonds with a Face Value of USD1 if required in order to facilitate such pro rata reduction. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the pre payment request. The settlement date of the Triggered Disposal Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end of the sixty - 60 - calendar days exercise period of the Triggered Disposal Put Option.
10.4.4
On the settlement date of the Triggered Disposal Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount o f each such Bond and any unpaid interest accrued on such Bonds up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.4.5 Any part of the Triggered Disposal Put Amount (or the other proceeds from the Triggered Disposal) not applied to redemption of any Bonds in accordance with this Clause 10.4 may be retained by the Group.
