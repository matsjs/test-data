"Adjusted Book Equity" means the aggregate book value of the Group's total equity treated as equity in accordance with IFRS, adjusted for any future write-offs of capitalized exploration expenses which are existing as of 30 June 2013 and made in accordance with IFRS.
"Attachments" means the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, includi g the Attachments, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue const'tuted by the Bonds.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
