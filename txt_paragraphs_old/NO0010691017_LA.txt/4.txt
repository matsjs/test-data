"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Financial Indebtedness" means any indebtedness incurred in respect of:
(a)
moneys borrowed, including acceptance credit;
(b) any bond, note, debenture, loan stock or other similar instrument;
(c)
the amount of any liability in respect of any lease, hire purchase contract
which would, in accordance with GAAP, be treated as a finance or capital
lease;
