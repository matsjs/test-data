The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum aggregate amount of NOK 30,000,000 (Norwegian kroner thirtymillion).
The Bond Issue will be described as "15 per cent Emerging Europe Land Development AS Callable Bond Issue 2013/2014".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be N00010691017.
The tenor o f the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used for repayment of the Issuer's current bank loan with Splitska Banka in Croatia and general operation of the Group.
