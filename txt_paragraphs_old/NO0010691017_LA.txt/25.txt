9
Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 15 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Payment of interest shall be made on the Maturity Date.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including the Issue Date to, but excluding, the Maturity Date.
9.4 The day count fraction in respect o f the calculation of the payable interest amount shall be "30/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in respect o f which payment is being made divided by 360 (the number of days to be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-days months (unless (i) the last day o f the calculation period is the 31st day of a month but the first day of the calculation period is a day other than the 30th or 31 st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30day month, or (ii) the last day of the calculation period is the last day o f the month of February, in which case the month o f February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month)).
