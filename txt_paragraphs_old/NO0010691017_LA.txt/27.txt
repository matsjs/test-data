10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of or the entire Bond Issue at par value plus accrued
interest at any time following the date falling 1 month after the Issue Date (Call Option).
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
