8.
8.1
Each Initial Note carries Interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal Amount from
(but excluding) the First Issue Date up to (and including) the relevant Redemption Date.
Any Subsequent Note will carry Interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal
Amount from (but excluding) the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to its
issuance (or the First Issue Date if there is no such Interest Payment Date) up to (and
including) the relevant Redemption Date.
