Issuer or the Noteholder(s) requesting a decision by the Noteholders may convene such
Noteholders' Meeting or instigate such Written Procedure, as the case may be, instead. The
Issuer or the Issuing Agent shall upon request provide the convening Noteholder with such
information available in the debt register (skuldbok) kept by the CSD in respect of the
Notes in order to convene and hold the Noteholders' Meeting or instigate and carry out the
Written Procedure, as the case may be.
