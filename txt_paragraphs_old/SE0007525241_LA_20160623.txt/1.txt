Definitions
In these terms and conditions (the "Terms and Conditions"):
"Account Operator" means a bank or other party duly authorised to operate as an account operator pursuant to the Financial Instruments Accounts Act and through which a Noteholder has opened a Securities Account in respect of its Notes.
"Accounting Principles" means (i) the international financial reporting standards (IFRS) within the meaning of Regulation 1606/2002/EC (or as otherwise adopted or amended from time to time), or (ii) other generally accepted accounting principles, standards and practices in Sweden (provided that, if the rules and regulations of the Regulated Market on which the Notes are admitted to trading requires such Regulated Market's acceptance of the application of such accounting principles, standards and practices, such acceptance has been obtained by the Issuer).
