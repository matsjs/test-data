In respect of loans under the Euro Term Loan Facility, the EURIBOR element of the interest calculation shall be determined as the higher of EURIBOR and 1.00% per annum. In respect of loans under the SEK Term Loan Facility, the STIBOR element of the interest calculation shall be determined as the higher of STIBOR and 1.00% per annum. If EURIBOR or where applicable LIBOR, STIBOR or NIBOR is less than zero, EURIBOR, LIBOR, STIBOR or NIBOR (as applicable) shall be deemed to be zero in respect of loans under the Revolving Credit Facility.
