Section 4.15. Change of Control.
(a) If a Change of Control occurs, subject to this Section 4.15, each Holder will have the right to require the Issuer to repurchase all or any part (equal to 100,000 or SEK 1,000,000, as the case may be, or integral multiples of 1 or SEK 1, as the case may be, in excess thereof; provided that Euro Senior Notes of 100,000 or less may only be redeemed in whole and not in part and SEK Senior Notes of SEK 1,000,000 or less may only be redeemed in whole and not in part) of such Holder's Notes at a purchase price in cash equal to 101% of the principal amount of the Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest and Additional Amounts, if any, to the date of purchase (subject to the right of Holders of record on the relevant record date to receive interest due on the relevant interest payment date); provided, however that the Issuer shall not be obliged to repurchase any series of Notes as described under this Section 4.15, in the event and to the extent that it has unconditionally exercised its right to redeem all of the Notes of such series as described under Section 5 of the Notes, or all conditions to such redemption have been satisfied or waived.
