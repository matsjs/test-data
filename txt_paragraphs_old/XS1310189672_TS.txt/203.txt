Attractive Industry Characteristics We believe the following key characteristics make the RHSB monitored alarm segment attractive.
Under-penetrated European Market
Despite recent growth, the European market for residential home alarms remains highly underpenetrated, particularly in comparison to the United States, where there is a significantly higher percentage of households with monitored alarms. According to third party reports, the average penetration by country in the European residential alarm monitoring segment in 2014 was estimated to be approximately 6%, compared to approximately 20% in the residential sector of the more developed U.S. market.
