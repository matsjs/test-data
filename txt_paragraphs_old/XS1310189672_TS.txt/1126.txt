2.2 Authentication. The Authenticating Agent shall authenticate and make available for delivery upon a written order of the Issuer signed by one of its Officers, the Notes (including Additional Notes). Such order shall (a) specify the amount of the Notes (including Additional Notes) to be authenticated, the date on which the original issue of such Notes (including Additional Notes) is to be authenticated, (b) direct the Authenticating Agent to authenticate such Notes (including Additional Notes) and (c) certify that all conditions precedent to the issuance of such Notes (including Additional Notes) have been complied with in accordance with the terms hereof.
