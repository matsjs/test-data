18.3
The Agent may refrain from convening a Holders' Meeting or instigating a Written Procedure
if (a) the suggested decision must be approved by any Person in addition to the Holders and
such Person has informed the Agent that an approval will not be given, or (b) the suggested
decision is not in accordance with applicable laws or regulations.
