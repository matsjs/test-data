The use of proceeds from the Bond Issue is to refinance the Refinancing Debt and to

3.2
The Net Proceeds shall be transferred to the Escrow Account. The Escrow Account will
be pledged in favour of the Trustee and the bondholders (represented by the Trustee)
subject to the terms of the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement. The pledge over the
Escrow Account shall be released when the Conditions Precedent for Disbursement
have been fulfilled.
