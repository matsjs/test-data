10.3 Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to repayment (the "Put Option") of the bonds at a price o f 101 % of par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within two calendar months after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 13.1 (h).
The Put Option may be exercised by the respective Bondholder giving written notice of its intention to require repayment to the Bondholder's VPS account manager. The Bondholder's VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent of the Loan (the "Paying Agent") o f a Bondholder's election to require early repayment. Settlement shall be made within fifteen - 1 5 - Banking Days from the date on which the Paying Agent received the repayment request.
