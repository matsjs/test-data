"Derivative Transaction" has the meaning set forth in the definition of Permitted Debt.
"Equity Cure" has the meaning set forth in Clause 11.7.2.
"Escrow Account" means the bank account of the Issuer held with the Bank, into which the Net Proceeds will be transferred and which has been pledged in favour of the Agent/Security Agent and the Holders (represented by the Agent/Security Agent) under the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement.
