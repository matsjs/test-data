The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (Call Option):
(i)
at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in June 2016 to,
but not including, the Interest Payment Date in June 2017 at a price equal to
103.00% of par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed Bonds);
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in June 2017 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2017 at a price equal to 102.00% o f par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed Bonds); and
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in December 2017 to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100.50% o f par value (plus accrued interests on the redeemed Bonds).
