The Issuer shall ensure that any existing and future shareholder loans or subordinated loans from any third party (not being a Group Company) to the Issuer shall be subordinated to the Bonds and have a maturity date after the Maturity Date. (f)
Financial support restrictions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting o f security) ("Financial Support") to or for the benefit of any third party or other Group Company other than:
(i)
Financial Support in connection with the Permitted Financial Indebtedness;
and
(ii) Financial Support in the ordinary course o f business.
