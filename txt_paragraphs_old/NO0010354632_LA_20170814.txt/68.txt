17.10 Written Resolution
17.10.1
Subject to the provisions in this Loan Agreement, anything which may be resolved by the Bondholders in a Bondholders' meeting pursuant to Clause 16 may also be resolved by way of a Written Resolution. A Written Resolution passed with the relevant majority is as valid as if it had been passed by the Bondholders in a Bondholders' meeting, and any reference in any Finance Document to a Bondholders' meeting shall be construed accordingly.
