10.4 10.4.1
Total Loss Prepayment
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than sixty (60) days following the Total Loss Event redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.4.2 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.4, the amount in the Liquidity Account or any of the Insurance proceeds may be applied as part payment in relation to the Total Loss Event.
