"Instalment Amount" has the meaning given to that term in Clause 13.4.1(a).
Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement dated on or about the date hereof, entered into between the Issuer, the Bond Trustee as bond trustee for the Second Bondholders and the Bond Trustee as bond trustee for the Bondholders, whereby the parties thereto agree, inter alia, that any proceeds received by the Bond Trustee in connection with the Finance Documents and the Second Finance Documents (as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement) shall be applied in the following order: first, towards the Issuer's obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents and secondly, towards the Second Bond Obligations.
"Interest Payment Date" means the dates falling at three-monthly intervals after the First Issue Date and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Banking Day Convention.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means the First Issue Date and any subsequent dates when a Tap Issue is made.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
