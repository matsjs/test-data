Tap Issues
The Issuer may issue subsequent tap issues after the First Issue Date up to the maximum amount described in Clause 2.2 (as such maximum amount may be subsequently increased as approved on a Bondholders' Meeting). Such Tap Issues shall be settled no later than five (5) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
