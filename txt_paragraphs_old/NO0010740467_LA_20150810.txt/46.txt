10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 10.1.1
Maturity
The Bonds shall (if applicable, depending on the amount (if any) deposited on the Debt Service Account) be partially repaid by the Issuer in an amount equal to the Instalment Amount (if any) less the amount of accrued interest on the redeemed amount in quarterly instalments (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount). The first instalment falls due on the first Interest Payment Date after the First Issue Date, and each subsequent instalment falls due on each quarterly Interest Payment Date thereafter.
10.1.2
The remaining Outstanding Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer together with accrued and uncapitalised interest thereon.
