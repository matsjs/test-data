13.4.3 Value remaining in the Issuer
Unless, following a bondholders' meeting approving such conversion, any amount of Bonds and/or Second Bonds have been converted to equity and the shareholder structure of the Issuer has been affected as a consequence, any value remaining in the Issuer after all Bonds and Second Bonds have been redeemed in full, shall be distributed to the Second Bondholders.
