The Issuer may, provided that no Event of Default is continuing and the Vessel has not been sold by the Issuer, elect to extend the Maturity Date by giving an extension request to the Bond Trustee not less than five (5) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date. The Issuer shall prior to any extension of the Maturity Date being effective (i) inform the bond trustee for the Second Bonds about the extension of the Maturity Date and (ii) ensure that any amendments required to be made to the Mortgage as a consequence of extending the Maturity Date is made (unless waived by the Bond Trustee).

Purpose and utilization
The first tranche of Bonds shall be employed to partially finance the purchase of the Vessel and transfer of any amounts remaining on the OSA Escrow Account pursuant to the Memorandum of Agreement, and the first tranche of Bonds will be issued to the Bondholders as settlement in full of the amounts outstanding under the OSA Liquidity Bond Issue to allow for release of the existing mortgage over the Vessel.
