17.2 Liability and indemnity
17.2.1
The Bond Trustee is liable and shall fully indemnify the Issuer in respect of direct losses incurred by Bondholders or die Issuer as a result of negligence or misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set forth in this Bond Agreement. The Bond Trustee is not liable for the content of information provided to the Bondholders on behalf of the Issuer.
