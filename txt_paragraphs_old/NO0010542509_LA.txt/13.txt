2.1 Binding nature o f the Bond Agreement
2.1.1 The Bondholders are, through their subscription, purchase or other transfer of Bonds, bound by the terms o f the Bond Agreement and other Finance Documents, as the initial Bondholders in the subscription agreements who. granted authority to the Bond Trustee to finalize and execute the Bond Agreement on their behalf, while all Bond transfers are subject to die terms o f this Bond Agreement and all Bond transferees are, in taking transfer o f Bonds, deemed to have accepted the terms o f the Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and will automatically become parties to the Bond Agreement upon a completed transfer having been registered in the Securities Register, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 18.1.
2.1.2
The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer throughout the entire term of the Bonds.
