(b) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer and the Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (including guarantees) other than:
(i) Financial Indebtedness arising under this Bond Issue; and
(ii) Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
(c) Dividend restrictions
The Issuer and the Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions or payments to its shareholders (including servicing of shareholder loans), whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect during the lifetime of the Bonds.
