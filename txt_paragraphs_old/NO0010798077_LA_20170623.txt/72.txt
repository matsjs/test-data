(h) Creditors' process
Any Designated Shipowning Subsidiary has a substantial proportion of its assets impounded, confiscated, attached or subject to distraint, and such process is not stopped or lifted by the provision of security to such creditor within 14 days.
(i) Impossibility or illegality
It is or becomes impossible or unlawful for any Obligor or the Parent to fulfil or perform any of the terms of any Finance Document to which it is a party.
