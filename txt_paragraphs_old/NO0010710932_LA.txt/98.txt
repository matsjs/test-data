Issuer Group General Covenants (a) Mergers
No Issuer Group Company shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of it or any of the Subsidiaries with any other companies.
(b) De-mergers
No Issuer Group Company shall carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of it or any of the Subsidiaries into two or more separate companies or entities.
