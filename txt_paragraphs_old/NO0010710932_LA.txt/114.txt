13.13 Bank Accounts (a) General
The Issuer shall open and maintain the Escrow Account and the Bond DSA in Norway with DNB Bank ASA or any other first class international bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
(b) Escrow Account
(i) The Issuer shall establish the Escrow Account prior to the Issue Date, and the net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account on the Issue Date. The Escrow Account shall be pledged in favor of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) under Norwegian law and be blocked in favor of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
(ii) The funds in the Escrow Account shall only be (i) used by the Issuer according to Clause 2.3 (Purpose and utilization) (including paying any fees and costs related to the Bond Issue as approved by the Issuer) as described above or, alternatively, (ii) in the event that the conditions precedent in Clause 6.3 and Clause 6.4 for any reason are not satisfied prior to 30 August 2014, (A) used in connection with a mandatory prepayment of the entire Bond Issue at (a) par value (together with accrued interest) if such failure to comply with the pre disbursement conditions precedent is beyond the control of the Issuer (except in the event of a Total Loss Event) and (b) otherwise at the rate applicable as set out under Clause 10.2 (Call Option) or (B) in the event of a Total Loss Event, used in connection with a total loss prepayment in accordance with Clause 10.5 (Total Loss Prepayment).
(iii) In connection with the release of funds from the Escrow Account taking place, all applicable Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent (as described below) shall be complied with or otherwise waived by the Bond Trustee.
