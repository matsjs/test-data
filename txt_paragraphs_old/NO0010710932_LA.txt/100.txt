13.8 Issuer's covenants (a) Ownership to Obligors
(b) Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other equity distributions to its shareholders, including but not limited to total return swaps and debt service of Parent Loans, other than the Issuer Loan, for the first twelve (12) months after the date of the Rig 1 Refinance, thereafter distributions may be made available subject to:
(i) the minimum Issuer Group Liquidity being equivalent to USD 7,500,000 per Rig after payment of such distribution;
(ii) no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing;
(iii) the Bank DSRA being fully funded; and
(iv) the Bond DSA being funded in accordance with the terms of this Bond Agreement.
(c) Financial support restrictions
The Issuer shall not grant any loans, guarantees, security or other financial assistance to any party other than any Intra-Group Loan.
