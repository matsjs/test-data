8.
STATUS OF THE BONDS AND SECURITY
8.1
The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other claims of the Issuer other than
obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law and all obligations under the Senior
Bank Facility and the Permitted Hedging. The Bonds shall rank ahead of Parent Loans.
8.2 The Bonds, accrued but unpaid interest, costs and expenses, and any other obligations under the Finance Documents, shall be secured by the Security Interest in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) on second priority behind the security established to secure the Senior Bank Facility and the Permitted Hedging, except for the Issuer Bond Account Pledges which shall rank on first priority.
