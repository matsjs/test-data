(e) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than (i) the obligations under the Finance Documents, (ii) the Senior Bank Facility, (iii) the Permitted Hedging, (iv) any Subordinated Loan and (v) any Intra-Group Loan.
(f) Investment restrictions
The Issuer may not make any investments or capital expenditures, other than solely related the ownership of the Charterers, Rig 5 Owner, Rig 1 Parent and the Rigs.
