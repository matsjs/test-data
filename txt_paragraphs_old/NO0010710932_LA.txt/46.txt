Conditions precedent for disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Escrow
Account.
6.1.1
Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Escrow Account will be subject to certain conditions precedent as customary for these types of transactions, including (but not limited to):
(a) confirmation from the Issuer that no potential or actual Event of Default exists;
(b) this Bond Agreement, duly executed by all parties thereto;
(c) the Guarantees from the Ultimate Parent, the Cyprus Interco and theParent duly executed;
