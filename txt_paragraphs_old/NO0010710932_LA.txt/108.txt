(d) Negative pledge
The Rig 1 Parent shall not create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect, including not agree to or permit the assignment of any of their rights under the Project Documents, except for (i) any Security granted as security for the Senior Bank Facility or Permitted Hedging, (ii) any security granted as security for the Bond Issue and (iii) prior to Rig 1 Refinance, the any Security granted as security for Existing Rig 1 Facility.
(e) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Rig 1 Parent shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding, any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than (i) prior to the Rig 1 Refinance, the Existing Rig 1 Facility, (ii) the Existing Rig 1 Parent Loan, (ii) any Intra-Group Loan and (iii) any financial support permitted by paragraph (c) above.
