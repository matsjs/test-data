(g) Project Documents
The Obligors shall, to the extent it is a party to such Project Document:
(i) Not agree to any material changes to any Project Document, except for changes as a consequence of Alternative Charter Arrangements and the Rig 1 Parent Restructuring (as applicable).
(ii) Not give any consent under any Project Document or exercise any material option thereunder, except for amendments not having any negative impact on the value of the Rigs or value of the relevant charter contract as contemplated pursuant to the Project Documents.
(iii) Enforce any rights that it has to ensure that the relevant Rig is to the best of its knowledge at all times operated and maintained in accordance with the Project Documents.
(iv) Perform and observe all of its covenants and agreements contained in any of the Project Documents, take all reasonable action to prevent the termination of any such Project Document in accordance with the terms thereof or otherwise, and take any and all action as may be reasonably necessary to promptly enforce its rights and to collect any and all sums due to it under the Project Documents.
(h) Rig 1 Parent Restructuring
Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Clause 13, following completion of the Rig 1 Refinance, Rig 1 Parent may transfer 100% of the shares in Rig 1 Owner to the Issuer and the Group may decide to dissolve Rig 1 Parent provided that the Bondholders will, in the reasonable opinion of the Bond Trustee, continue to have Security over 100% of the shares in the Rig 1 Owner and the Bondholders otherwise retain the security position contemplated under this Bond Agreement (the "Rig 1 Parent Restructuring").
