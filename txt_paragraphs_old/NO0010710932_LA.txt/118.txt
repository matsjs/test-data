(b) Maintenance, class
(ii) The Rig Owners and the Charterers shall ensure that the Rigs maintain their highest class with a classification society acceptable to the Bond Trustee, free of any overdue recommendations or adverse notations, and that the Rigs are submitted to such periodic or other surveys as may be required for classification purposes and to ensure full compliance with regulations of the applicable ship registry.
(c) Inspection of the Rigs
Upon the request of the Bond Trustee, the Charterers shall arrange for the Bond Trustee and/or any person including technical adviser appointed by the Bond Trustee to undertake technical inspections of the Rigs without interference of the daily operation of the Rigs and at the expense of the Charterer, however limited to one yearly inspection unless an Event of Default has occurred.
(d) Flag, name, registry
The Guarantor shall ensure that the Rigs maintain their flag and name, and that they remain registered in the Vanuatu only, unless otherwise approved by the Bond Trustee, such approval not to be unreasonably withheld, always provided that such approval will not be required if a change is required and permitted pursuant to the Project Documents and the Security Documents are not impaired.
(e) Title
The Rig Owners shall hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the relevant Rig and the insurances taken out in respect of the relevant Rig and assigned to the Bond Trustee, free of any and all Security except for that expressly permitted under Clause 13.9(c) above.
