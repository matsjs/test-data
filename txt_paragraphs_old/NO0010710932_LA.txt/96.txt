13.6 Parent and Cyprus Interco Covenants (a) Mergers
Neither the Parent nor the Cyprus Interco shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Parent, the Cyprus Interco or any of their respective Subsidiaries with any other companies if such reorganisation might have a Material Adverse Effect.
