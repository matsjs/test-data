2.3 2.3.1
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of legal costs, fees of the Manager and the Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall be used as follows:
(a) on the Rig 5 Delivery Disbursement, an amount of up to USD 50,000,000 for (i) an Intra-Group Loan from the Issuer to Rig 5 Owner to finance in part the final instalment to be paid to the Yard at the delivery of PROS 5 from the Yard and (ii) funding of the Bond DSA in an amount equal to 7 months Interest Rate; and
(b) on the Rig 1 Refinance Disbursement, (i) an amount of approximately USD 25,000,000 to be made available to the Rig Owners and/or the Charterers as working capital in form of Intra-Group Loans from the Issuer and (ii) the remaining as a loan from the Issuer to the Parent for general corporate purposes of the Group (the "Issuer Loan").
