(c) Ownership to Obligors
(i) The Parent shall maintain 100% (direct or indirect) ownership over all the shares and control over all voting rights of the Cyprus Interco, the Issuer, the Rig Owners, the Charterers and, subject to the Rig 1 Parent Restructuring, Rig 1 Parent.
(ii) The Cyprus Interco shall maintain 100% (direct or indirect) ownership over all the shares and control over all voting rights of the Issuer, the Rig Owners, the Charterers and, subject to the Rig 1 Parent Restructuring, Rig 1 Parent.
(d) Additional rig projects
The Parent and the Cyprus Interco shall ensure that any existing and additional construction contract (for Prospector 6, Prospector 7, Prospector 8 and any future rig) entered into by any Group Company is entered into on a non-recourse basis to any Group Company so that the construction contracts shall be entered into by special purpose companies (not being any of the Obligors) and no Group Company shall grant any guarantee or other financial assistance for any obligations under any such construction contract (however so that intra-group loans without available commitments may be provided to the relevant special purpose company by any Group Company which is not an Issuer Group Company).
(e) Negative Pledge
Neither the Parent nor the Cyprus Interco shall create or permit to subsist any additional Security over any of the assets subject to Security for the Bond Issue other than the Security for the Senior Bank Facility, the Permitted Hedging and the Bond Issue.
