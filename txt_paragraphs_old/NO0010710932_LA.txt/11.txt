"Bond DSA" means the account described in paragraph (c) (Bond DSA) of Clause 13.13 (Bank Accounts).
"Book Equity" means the aggregate nominal book value of total equity treated as equity in accordance with IFRS on a consolidated basis for the Group, as set out in the then most recent financial statements (annual or quarterly (as the case may be)) of the Ultimate Parent.
"Business Day" means any day when banks in Oslo and New York are open for general business, and when banks in Oslo and New York can settle foreign currency transactions.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment will be made, notwithstanding the Payment Date occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments of interest and/or principal (as the case may be) will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day (No Adjustments of Business Day).
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Change of Control Event" means if and when any person or a group of persons acting in concert, other than any international reputable company operating in the oil ft gas industry holding minimum an investment grade rating by a reputable investment grade agency, becomes the owner, directly or indirectly, of more than 1/3 of the shares and/or voting rights of the Ultimate Parent.
"Charterers" means the Original Charterers and any New Charterer (if any).
"Client" means an entity unrelated to the Group being party to a Drilling Contract.
"Cyprus Interco" means Prospector Offshore Drilling Limited, a company incorporated under the laws of Cyprus with registration no. HE 268531 and being the 100% direct owner of the Parent.
