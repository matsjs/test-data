(i) The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu with all other claims of the Issuer, other than obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law and all obligations under the Senior Bank Facility and the Permitted Hedging as set out in Clause 8.1.
(ii) Each Guarantor shall ensure that its obligations under the Guarantees and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu with all other claims of that Guarantor other than obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law and all obligations under the Senior Bank Facility and the Permitted Hedging. The Guarantors' obligations under the Guarantees shall rank ahead of Intra-Group Loans.
(b) Continuation of business
The Obligors shall not cease to carry on its business or make any substantial change to the general nature of its business, from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(c) Arm's length transactions
The Obligors shall not enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(d) Corporate status
The Obligors shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
