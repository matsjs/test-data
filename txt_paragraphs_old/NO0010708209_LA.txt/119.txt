16.2 Procedural rules for Bondholders' meetings 16.2.1 A Bondholders' Meeting shall be held at the written request of:
(a) the Issuer;
(b) Bondholders representing at least 1/10 of the Voting Bonds; (c) the Exchange, if the Bonds are listed; or (d) the Bond Trustee.
