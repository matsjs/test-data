17.1.7 The Bond Trustee may not adapt resolutions which may give certain Bondholders: or others an unreasonable advantage at the expense of other Bondholders.
17.1.8: The Issuer, the Bondholders and the Exchange shall be notified of decisions made by the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 17.1 unless such notice obviously is unnecessary.

17.2 17.2.1
Liability and indemnity
The Bond Trustee is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders or the Issuer as a result of gross negligence or wilful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in performing its functions and duties as set out in this Bond Agreement. Such liability is limited to the maximum amount set out in Clause 2,2. The Bond Trustee is not liable fo r the content of information provided to the Bondholders on behalf of the Issuer.
