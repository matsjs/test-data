(d) Issuer's Debt Service Account
(i) Commencing from the Issue Date, the Issuer shall on a monthly basis ensure that an amount equivalent to 1/6 of the next coupon payment under the Bond Issue is transferred to the Issuer Debt Service Account. Furthermore, the Issuer shall, on a monthly basis commencing from 28 September 2014 ensure that art amount equivalent to 1/6 of the next Scheduled Instalments (as defined above) is transferred to the Issuer Debt Service Account.
(ij) The Issuer Debt Service Account shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee. The deposited amount shall only be released and applied for payments of interest and Scheduled Instalments in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.
(e) Liquidity Account: The Issuer shall prior to the release from the Escrow Account open a Liquidity Account with Nordea Bank Norge ASA in Norway, In connection with the draw-down from the Escrow Account, the Issuer shall deposit an amount equal to the Minimum Liquidity into the Liquidity Account. The Liquidity Account shall be held With Nordea Bank Norge ASA in Norway, pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee and blocked.
(f) Vessel Owners' Earnings Accounts: Each Vessel Owner shall procure that all its First Bareboat Charter Hire, all of that Vessel Owner's other net earnings relating to its Vessel and any insurance or sale proceeds shall be paid to the relevant Vessel Owners' Earnings Account.
