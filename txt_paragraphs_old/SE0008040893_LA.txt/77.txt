16.3
Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the termination of
the Bonds constitute escrow funds according to the Escrow Funds Act and must be held on a
separate interest-bearing account on behalf of the Holders and the other interested parties.
The Agent shall arrange for payments of such funds in accordance with this Clause 16 as
soon as reasonably practicable.
