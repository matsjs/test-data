DEFINITIONS AND CONSTRUCTION
Definitions
In these terms and conditions (the "Terms and Conditions"):
"Account Operator" means a bank or other party duly authorised to operate as an account operator pursuant to the Financial Instruments Accounts Act and through which a Noteholder has opened a Securities Account in respect of its Notes.
"Accounting Principles" means (i) the K3 standard adopted by the Swedish Accounting Standards Board on the date of these Terms and Conditions and until the earlier to occur of (a) the date (in this case excluding such date) the Notes are admitted to trading on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm or (b) the end of the financial year 2013/2014 and (ii) international financial reporting standards (IFRS) within the meaning of Regulation 1606/2002/EC (or as otherwise adopted or amended from time to time) from and including the earlier to occur of (x) the date the Notes are admitted to trading on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm or (y) the first financial quarter of the financial year 2014/2015.
"Adjusted Cash" means, at any relevant time, the aggregate amount of all cash in hand (including in transit) or at bank and cash equivalents held by a Group Company reduced by (i) the aggregate amount of any and all Restricted Payments made since the end date of the latest financial statements delivered to the Agent in accordance with 10.1.1 and (ii) the aggregate amount of the Restricted Payment proposed to be made by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 11.2.2.
"Adjusted Consolidated Net Debt" means, at any relevant time, (without double counting) the aggregate amount of all obligations of any Group Company for or in respect of Financial Indebtedness at that time, but excluding any such obligations to any other Group Company, less Adjusted Cash. If the aggregate amount of Adjusted Cash is negative, Financial Indebtedness hereunder shall be deemed to be increased with a positive amount equal to that negative number of Adjusted Cash.
"Adjusted Equity" means, at any relevant time, the sum of restricted equity (bundet eget kapital) and non-restricted equity (fritt eget kapital) of the Group reduced by (i) the aggregate amount of any and all Restricted Payments made since the end date of the latest financial statements delivered to the Agent in accordance with 10.1.1 and (ii) the aggregate amount of the Restricted Payment proposed to be made by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 11.2.2.
"Adjusted Nominal Amount" means the Total Nominal Amount less the Nominal Amount of all Notes owned by a Group Company, irrespective of whether such person is directly registered as owner of such Notes.
