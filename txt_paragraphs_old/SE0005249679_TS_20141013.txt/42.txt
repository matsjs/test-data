Financial undertakings
The Issuer shall ensure that on any Relevant Date the aggregate of cash, cash equivalent investments and the undrawn available commitments under any working capital facility of the Group shall not be less than the higher of (i) SEK 20,000,000 or (ii) an amount equal to 5 % of the total outstanding amount as per the Relevant Date of Financial Indebtedness permitted under Clauses 11.1.3 i-ii, iv-vi, viii and x.
Until and including 30 April 2016, the Issuer shall not declare, make, pay, repay or distribute any dividends on or in respect of its share capital or any similar distribution (including share repurchase) or transfer of value (each a "Restricted Payment") to its shareholders. After 30 April 2016, the Issuer may make, declare, pay, repay or distribute any Restricted Payment to its shareholders, provided that (i) the aggregate amount of any and all Restricted Payments in any fiscal year of the Issuer does not exceed the Distribution Threshold, (ii) the Leverage Test will be met following each Restricted Payment and (iii) the Equity Test will be met following each Restricted Payment.
Calculations in relation to the Leverage Test shall be made taking into account that the ratio of Adjusted Consolidated Net Debt to EBITDA will be measured as per the end date of the latest financial statement of the Issuer, which has been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1, and the EBITDA figure for the Relevant Period will be based upon up to four consolidated financial statements of the Issuer, which have been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1.
Calculations in relation to the Equity Test shall be made taking into account that Adjusted Equity will be measured as per the end date of the latest financial statement of the Issuer, which has been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1, and the Adjusted Equity figure for the Relevant Period will be based upon the latest consolidated financial statement of the Issuer, which has been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1
Admission to trading
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that the loan constituted by these Terms and Conditions and evidenced by the Notes is admitted to trading on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm no later than 15 December 2014, and that it remains admitted or, if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on another Regulated Market.
