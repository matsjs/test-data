14.4
The Noteholders' Committee may agree with the Issuer not to disclose information
received from the Issuer provided that it, in the reasonable opinion of the Noteholders'
Committee, is beneficial to the interests of the Noteholders. The Agent shall be a party to
such agreement and receive the same information from the Issuer as the Noteholders'
Committee.
