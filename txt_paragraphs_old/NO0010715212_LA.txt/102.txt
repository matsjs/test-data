14.6 If the Issuer is required by law to withhold any withholding tax from any payment under any Finance Document:
the amount of the payment due from the Issuer shall be increased to Such

which is (after making the required withholding) equal to the payment which
would have been due if no withholding had been required; and

the Issuer shall at the request o f the Bond Trustee deliver to the Bond Trustee
evidence that the required tax reduction or withholding has been made.
