The Issuer shall ensure that.its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Qause 8.1.
(b) Mergers
The Issuer shall not carry put any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation o f the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities, and the Issuer shall procure that no such merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation o f the assets and obligations of any of Modular Holdco or Rigco with any other companies or entities shall occur, if such a transaction would cause a Material Adverse Effect.
(c) De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities, and the Issuer shall procure that no such de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split o f any of Modular Holdco or the Rigco shall occur, if it would have a Material Adverse Effect.:
(d) Continuation o fbusiness
The Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither the Modular Holdco nor the Rigco shall cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no changes are made to the general nature o f the business from that carried on at the date of the Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in the Bond Agreement.
