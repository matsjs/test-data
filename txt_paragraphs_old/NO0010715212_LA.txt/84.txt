(g) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither the Modular Holdco nor the Rigeo shall enter ,into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(h) No other business
The Issuer shall remain a single purpose holding vehicle for the purpose o f owning the shares in Latina Offshore Limited and Modular Holdco, and shall not acquire or initiate any additional assets (other than additional shares through permitted equity increases) or operations (or assume any liabilities in respect thereto).
(i) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither the Modular Holdco nor the Rigeo shall change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
