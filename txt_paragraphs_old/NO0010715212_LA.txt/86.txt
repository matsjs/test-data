(n) No Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not and shall procure that neither the Modular Holdco nor the Rigco or Second Rigco (if applicable) shall incur or permit to remain outstanding, any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured or from affiliates or third parties) other than (i) the Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue and (ii) any Subordinated Loans from the Parent,. provided however, that said covenant shall not prohibit (i) Modular Holdco and/or the Rigco from obtaining the Permitted First Rig Financing, and (ii) Modular Holdco and/or the Second Rigco from obtaining the Permitted Second Rig Financing, always provided compliance with the Debt Level set out in Clause 13.4(b).
(o) Second Rigco
The Issuer undertakes to ensure that, if the Parent (or any other relevant group company) . decides to undertake th e :contemplated acquisition of the Second Rig that (i) the construction contract with respect to the Second Rig is entered into by or novated to the v Second Rigco, (ii) the Second Rig shall be owned by the Second Rigco, such Second Rigco to be a sister company of the Rigco, 100% owned by the Modular Holdco and (iii) the financing of the Second Rig shall be obtained in accordance with the terms set out in Clause 13.3 (r).
(p) Intercompany Loans
Any forwarding o f proceeds from the Bond Issue by the Issuer to the Rigco (whether made directly or through Modular Holdco), shall be carried out by way of Intercompany Loans.
(q) Subordination o f Intercompany Loans and Permitted First Rig financing
The Issuer shall ensure that any Intercompany Loan shall be subordinated to any Permitted First Rig Financing. For the avoidance of doubt, no covenants set out in Clause 13.3 shall restrict the Rigco's (and Modular Holdco's, if relevant) ability to service the interest and principal amount of any Intercompany Loan.
