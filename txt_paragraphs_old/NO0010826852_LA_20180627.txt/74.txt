12.4 Information: Miscellaneous The Issuer shall:
(a) not later than 120 days after the end of each financial year, deliver an updated reserves report for each of the Hydrocarbon Assets of the Group;
(b) promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing if a company is becoming Group Company.
(c) promptly inform the Bond Tmstee of any event which could reasonably be expected to result in the revocation, withdrawal, cancellation, termination, suspension, forfeiture or variation of any of the Existing Licences, and/or any other Hydrocarbon Asset;
(d) promptly inform the Bond Tmstee of any failure (the "Relevant Payment Default") by any Group Company to make a payment under anyjoint operating agreement, unitization agreement or similar agreement or document with respect to any Hydrocarbon Assets (the "Relevant Hydrocarbon Asset"), such notice to include specific details of the Relevant Payment Default (including the original maturity date thereof and the amount of the Relevant Payment Default), whether the Issuer has or shortly will have sufficient funds to remedy the Relevant Payment Default and details of the Relevant Hydrocarbon Asset.
(e) promptly inform the Bond Tmstee in writing of any Event of Default or any event or circumstance which the Issuer understands or could reasonably be expected to understand may lead to an Event of Default and the steps, if any, being taken to remedy it);
(f) at the request of the Bond Tmstee, report the balance of the Issuer's Bonds (to the best of its knowledge, having made due and appropriate enquiries);
