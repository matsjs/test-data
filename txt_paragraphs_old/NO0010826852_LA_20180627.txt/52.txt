4.
ADMISSION TO LISTING
The Issuer has applied, or shall within 6 months of the Issue Date apply, for the Bonds to be admitted to listing on the Exchange and the Issuer shall use its best efforts in ensuring the Bonds are listed on the Exchange within 3 months after the first application was made.
5.
REGISTRATION OF THE BONDS
5.1 Registration in the CSD The Bonds shall be registered in dematerialised form in the CSD according to the relevant securities registration legislation and the requirements of the CSD.
