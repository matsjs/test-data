"Group" means the Issuer and its Subsidiaries from time to time.
"Group Company" means any person which is a member of the Group.
"Guarantee" means the Norwegian law on-demand guarantee granted by a Guarantor in relation to the Finance Documents.
"Guarantor" means and any Group Company which subsequently becomes a New Group Company.
"Hydrocarbon Asset" means, from time to time, each hydrocarbon licence and block in which any Group Company holds an ownership interest (either directly or through interests in production sharing contracts or similar) (including the Existing Licences).
"Hydrocarbon Asset Development Costs" means the Issuer's part of the costs and expenses for the development of any Hydrocarbon Assets.
"Hydrocarbon Assets Acquisition" means the direct or indirect acquisition of Hydrocarbon Assets.
"IFRS" means International Financial Reporting Standards and guidelines and interpretations issued by the International Accounting Standards Board (or any predecessor and successor thereof), in force from time to time.
