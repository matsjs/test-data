The International Securities Identification Number (ISEN) o f the Bond Issue will be NO 001 064940.3.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bonds shall be employed for refinancing the Issuer's existing bond issue with ISIN NO 001037241.0 (where USD 47.5 million is outstanding) and for general corporate puiposes.
