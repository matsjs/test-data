17.9 Surveys The Issuer and each Guarantor being owner of a Unit shall submit to or cause the Units to be submitted to such periodic or other surveys as may be required for classification purposes and to ensure full compliance with regulations of the flag state of the Units and to supply or to cause to be supplied to the Bond Trustee copies of all survey reports and confirmations of class issued in respect thereof no later than 30 days after receipt of the relevant reports and/or confirmation.
