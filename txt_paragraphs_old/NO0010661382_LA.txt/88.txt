8.2 Security The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest and expenses, shall be secured by the Security Interests.
8.4 Quiet Enjoyment Letter The Bond Trustee shall issue a quiet enjoyment letter if so required by a charterer of an Existing Accommodation Unit under any Employment Contract, with a wording as reasonably requested by such charterer. The quiet enjoyment letter may only be issued if it provides for the Bond Trustee (subject to the terms and conditions of the InterCreditor Agreements) to (a) be capable of remedying insolvency and (b) to assign the Employment Contract to a nominee of the Bond Trustee being reasonably satisfactory to the charterer.
9. INTEREST 9.1 Interest rate
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 8.00 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Interest Payment Dates Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falls in January 2013.
