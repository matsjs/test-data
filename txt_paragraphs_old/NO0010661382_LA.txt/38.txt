"Retention Account" means an account denominated in USD in the name of the Issuer, with an Acceptable Bank, to be blocked and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee pursuant to the Retention Account Pledge, and in which the bank has waived any set-off rights.
"Retention Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Retention Account.
"Securities Register Act" means the Norwegian Act relating to Registration of Financial Instruments of 5 July 2002 No. 64.
