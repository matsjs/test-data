The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events (which shall be referred to as an "Event of Default") if:
19.1 Non-payment Any of the Obligors fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full, within 5 Business Days following the original due date.
19.2 Breach of other obligations Any Obligor does not comply with any provision pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable of being remedied and is remedied within 15 Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee. For the avoidance of doubt, such 15 Business Days' remedy period shall not apply in respect of Clause 15.4.
