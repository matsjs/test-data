16.4 Disposal of business The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets, subsidiaries or operations to any person not being a member of the Group, unless:
(a)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions
customary for such transactions; and
(b)
such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
