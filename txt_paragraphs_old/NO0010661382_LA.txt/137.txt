14.3 Financial statements The Issuer shall without being requested to do so, produce Financial Statements and Quarterly Financial Reports in the English language and deliver them to the Bond Trustee as soon as they become available, and not later than 150 days after the end of the financial year (for Financial Statements) and 60 days after the end of the relevant quarter (for Quarterly Financial Reports) or, subject to listing of the Issuer, via the distribution system at such stock exchange and at the Issuer's website);
14.4 Progression reports The Issuer shall up to and including the month of the Victory Delivery Date, provide to the Bond Trustee quarterly updates on the progression of the construction of Floatel Victory, in a reporting form and content reasonably satisfactory to the Bond Trustee.
