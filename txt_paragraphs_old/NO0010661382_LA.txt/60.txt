2.5 Temporary Bonds
(a)
As part of the closing procedure, and until the establishment of the Security
Interests, some Bonds under the Bond Agreement will be issued as Temporary
Bonds. The Temporary Bonds will be allotted to those Bondholders having
exercised the Existing Bondholders' Roll-Over and delivered Settlement
Bonds.
