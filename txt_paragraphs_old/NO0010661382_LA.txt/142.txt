15.3 Financial covenants The Issuer shall ensure that the Group on a consolidated basis at all times maintains a ratio of Total Book Equity to Total Assets:
(a)
in respect of the financial quarter in which delivery of Floatel Victory takes
place and the first financial quarter thereafter, of no less than 25%; and
(b)
in respect of any other Reference Period than set out in paragraph (a) above,
of no less than 30%.
