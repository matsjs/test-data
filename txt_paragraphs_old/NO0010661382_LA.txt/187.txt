17.11 Ownership No Obligor being owner of a Unit shall sell or dispose of the respective Units or the companies holding such Units, or the Victory Building Contract, in whole or in part, unless the Bonds are redeemed in accordance with the mandatory redemption provisions at set out Clause 10.4 or the Bondholders are given opportunity to exercise the put option in Clause 10.5 (as applicable).
17.12 Environmental compliance The Issuer and each Guarantor being owner of a Unit shall and shall procure that the charterer shall, comply in all respects with all applicable law, regulation, convention or treaty in any jurisdiction in which the Issuer and/or any relevant Guarantor and/or any rig operator and/or any charterer conduct business which relates to the relevant Unit and the pollution or protection of the environment or to the carriage of material which is capable of polluting the environment relevant to the relevant Unit, including without limitation, requirements relating to manning and establishment of financial responsibility and to obtain and comply with all approvals applicable to the relevant Unit.
