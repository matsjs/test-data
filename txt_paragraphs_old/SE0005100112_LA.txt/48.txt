If the voting shall be made electronically, instructions for such voting shall be included in the communication.
(d) Quorum for a Written Procedure only exists if a Bondholder (or Bondholders) representing at least twenty (20) per cent, of the Adjusted Nominal Amount replies in the Written Procedure.
(e) If a quorum is not achieved in a Written Procedure through received answers at the end of the time for replies, the Agent or the Issuer may instigate a new Written Procedure. The quorum requirement in paragraph (d) above shall not apply to such second Written Procedure.
