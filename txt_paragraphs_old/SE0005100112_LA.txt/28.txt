10.2 Incurrence Covenants
The Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time:
(a) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA for the Relevant Period ending on the relevant testing date does not exceed 4.0:1; and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio for the Relevant Period ending on the last day of the period covered by the most recent Financial Report exceeds 2.25:1,
calculated in accordance with the calculation principles set out in Clause 10.3 (Calculation Adjustments).
