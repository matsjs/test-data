Mandatory Repurchase due to a Change of Control Event
(a) Upon a Change of Control Event occurring, each Bondholder shall have the right to request that all, or some of, its Bonds be repurchased at a price per Bond equal to 101 percent, of the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest, during a period of sixty (60) Business Days following a notice from the Issuer of the Change of Control Event pursuant to paragraph (b) of Clause 9.1 (Information from the Issuer) (after which time period such rights lapse).
(b) The notice from the Issuer pursuant to paragraph (b) of Clause 9.1 (Information from the Issuer) shall include instructions about the actions that a Bondholder needs to take if it wants the Bonds held by it to be repurchased.
