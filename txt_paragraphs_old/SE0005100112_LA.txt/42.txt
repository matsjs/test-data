14. Decisions by Bondholders
(a) Any decision by the Bondholders on a matter relating to the Finance Documents shall (at the option of the Agent) be dealt with at a Bondholders' Meeting or by way of a Written Procedure.
(b) Any request from the Issuer or a Bondholder (or Bondholders) representing at least ten (10) percent, of the Adjusted Nominal Amount for a decision by the Bondholders on a matter relating to the Finance Documents shall be directed to the Agent and dealt with at a Bondholders' Meeting. If in the Agent's opinion it is not detrimental to the interests of the Bondholders, the Agent may decide that the relevant matter shall be dealt with by way of a Written Procedure.
