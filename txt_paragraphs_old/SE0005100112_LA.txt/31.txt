11.2 Distributions
(i)
pay any dividend on its shares (other than loans and group
contributions);
(ii) repurchase any of its own shares;
(iii) redeem its share capital or other restricted or unrestricted equity with payment to shareholders;
(iv) grant any loans (other than to the Issuer or a wholly-owned Subsidiary of the Issuer);
(v) repay (or prepay) principal under the Promissory Notes; or
(vi) make any other similar distribution or transfers of value to the direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, or any Affiliates of the Issuer (other than the Issuer or another Subsidiary of the Issuer).
