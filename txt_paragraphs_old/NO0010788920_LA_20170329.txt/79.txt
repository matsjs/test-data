Existing Facilities
13.16
Arm's length transactions
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value, other than intra-group loans which constitute Permitted Financial Support.
13.17
Security
The Issuer undertakes to ensure to at all times provide Security over all Intercompany Loans within the Group (provided that no such Security shall be granted by a Restricted Company) and to provide and maintain guarantees from and pledge over shares in all of the Operating Companies (except that no guarantee shall be provided by any Restricted Companies).
