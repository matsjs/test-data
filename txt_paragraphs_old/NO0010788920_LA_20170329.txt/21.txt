"Liquidity" means the sum of any unrestricted (a) amount available to the Group under any committed or uncommitted credit facility or any other financing arrangement which has not been utilized or cancelled in full and (b) Cash and Cash Equivalents.
"Listing Failure Event" means:
(a) that the Bonds have not been admitted to listing on an Exchange within nine (9) months following the Issue Date, or
(b) in the case of a successful admission to listing, that a period of six (6) months has elapsed since the Bonds ceased to be admitted to listing on an Exchange.
"Longstop Date" means the date that falls ninety (90) Business Days after the Issue Date, or, in respect of any Tap Issue, after the issue date in respect of that Tap Issue.
"Make-Whole Amount" means an amount equal to the sum of:
(a) the present value on the Call Option Repayment Date of 102 per cent. of the Nominal Amount of the redeemed Bonds as if such payment originally had taken place on the First Call Date; and
