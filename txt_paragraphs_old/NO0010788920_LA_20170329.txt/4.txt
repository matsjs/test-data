"Bond Trustee Agreement" means the agreement entered into between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee relating among other things to the fees to be paid by the Issuer to the Bond Trustee for its obligations relating to the Bonds.
"Bondholder" means a Person who is registered in the CSD as directly registered owner or nominee holder of a Bond, subject however to Clause 3.3 (Bondholders 'rights).
"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders as set out in Clause 15 (Bondholders' decisions).
"Bond Escrow Account" means a blocked CSD escrow account in the name of the Issuer, to which the Roll-Over Bonds will be credited. The Bond Escrow Account shall be pledged to the Bond Trustee on behalf of the holders of Temporary Bonds under the Bond Escrow Account Pledge.
"Bond Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Bond Escrow Account and any securities credited to the Bond Escrow Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the holders of the Temporary Bonds).
