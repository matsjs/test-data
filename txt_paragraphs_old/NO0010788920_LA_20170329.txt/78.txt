Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect
13.15
Existing Facilities
The Parent shall ensure that no material amendments are made to the Existing Facilities which would be detrimental to the interests of the Bondholders. For the avoidance of doubt, this undertaking does not restrict a refinancing or replacement of any amount outstanding under the Existing Facilities by way of a New Bank Facility.
