6.2 Settlement in cash or in kind (a) The Bonds issued under the Initial Bond Issue shall be settled either in (i) cash, or (ii) in kind by delivery of Roll-Over Bonds (upon receipt of subscriptions from Existing Bondholders in accordance with the Existing Bondholders' Roll-Over). The Settlement process shall be further specified in a separate application form.
