(e) If the Security Agent is Insolvent, or otherwise is permanently unable to fulfil its obligations under these Bond Terms, the Security Agent shall be deemed to have resigned and a successor Security Agent shall be appointed in accordance with this Clause 16.6 (Security Agent).
(f) The provisions set out in Clause 16.4 (Expenses, liability and indemnity) shall apply mutatis mutandis to any expenses and liabilities of the Security Agent in connection with the Finance Documents.
16.7 Parallel Debt (a) Notwithstanding any other provision o f these Bond Terms, the Issuer hereby irrevocably and unconditionally undertakes to pay to the Security Agent as creditor in its own right and not as representative o f the Bondholders, sums equal to and in the currency of each amount payable by the Issuer to each of the Bondholders under each o f the Finance Documents as and when that amount falls due for payment under the relevant Finance Document or would have fallen due but for any discharge resulting from failure o f another Bondholder to take appropriate steps, in insolvency proceedings affecting the Issuer, to preserve its entitlement to be paid that amount.
(b) The Security Agent shall have its own independent right to demand payment o f the amounts payable by the Issuer under this Clause 16.7, irrespective of any discharge of the obligation o f the Issuer to pay those amounts to the Bondholders resulting from failure by them to take appropriate steps, in insolvency proceedings affecting the Issuer, to preserve their entitlement to be paid those amounts.
(c) Any amount due and payable by the Issuer to the Security Agent under this Clause 16.7 shall be applied in accordance with the terms of these Bond Terms and shall be decreased to the extent that the Bondholders have received (and are able to retain) payment in full of the corresponding amount under the other provisions of the Finance Documents and any amount due and payable by the Issuer to the Bondholders under those provisions shall be decreased to the extent that the Security Agent has received (and is able to retain) payment in full of the corresponding amount under this Clause 16.7.
(d) The aggregate amount which the Issuer is liable to pay to the Security Agent under this Clause 16.7 shall never exceed the aggregate amount which the Issuer is liable to pay to the Bondholders under the Finance Documents.
(e) In the event of the appointment of a new Security Agent other than the Bond Trustee, the Bond Trustee shall assign to such successor Bond Trustee or such Security Agent the obligations owed or to be owed to it pursuant to this Clause 16.7. Any resignation by the Security Agent or any such appointment o f a Security Agent other than the Bond Trustee is not effective with respect to its rights under this Clause 16.7 until such rights are so assigned to the successor Security Agent.
