The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no member of the Group shall (other than to the Issuer), declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase o f shares or make any loans or other distributions or payments to its shareholders (including servicing of shareholder loans), whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect.
13.5.5 Financial indebtedness restrictions
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, incur create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.5.6 Negative pledge
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance or security interest over any of its present or future assets, revenues or shares (or rights related to such shares), other than Permitted Security (provided always that if such Permitted Security is created over any assets, rights, revenues or shares that are subject to the Security Interest, such additional security is permitted over assets that are subject to a floating charge only).
