15.1.5 Insolvency
(a)
A Group Company, is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due or
suspends making payments on any of its debts.
(b)
The value of the assets of any Group Company is less than its liabilities (taking
into account contingent and prospective liabilities).
