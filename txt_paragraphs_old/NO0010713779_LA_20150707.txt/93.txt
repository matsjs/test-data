INTEREST
The Issuer shall pay interest on the Face Value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue
Date at the aggregate of the Fixed Rate and the PIK Interest,
9.2
The Fixed Rate interest payments shall be made quarterly in arrears on the Interest Payment
Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 30 September 2014.
The PIK Interest shall be paid in kind or in cash at the Issuer's discretion on the Interest Payment Date in June each year. PIK Interest to accrue from the Issue Date and be compounded annually. Bonds issued as PIK Interest shall have the same rights as the original Bonds.
