The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving consolidating the assets and obligations of the Issuer or such Group Company with any other company or entity not being a member of the Group but may (in respect of any Group Company other than the Parent and the Issuer) if such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.3.3 De-mergers
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any de merger or other corporate reorganization involving splitting the Issuer or such Group Company into two or more separate companies or entities, but may (in respect of any Group Company other than the Parent and the Issuer) if such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.3.4 Continuation o f business
The Parent shall not change the general nature of its business (and shall not become the direct owner of any working interest in any field or hydrocarbon assets) and shall not cease to carry on its business.
