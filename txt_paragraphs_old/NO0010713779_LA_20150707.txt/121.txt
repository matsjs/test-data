13.4.3 Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (including guarantees) other than the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.4.4 Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance or security interest over any of its present or future assets, revenue or shares (or rights related to such shares) other than the Permitted Security (provided always that if such Permitted Security, other than as permitted under item (k) of the definition of "Permitted Security", is created over any assets, rights, revenues or shares that are subject to the Security Interest, such additional security is permitted over assets that are subject to a floating charge only, excluding the Bentley Interest, where applicable).
13.4.5 Financial support restrictions
The Issuer shall not grant any Financial Support, other than as permitted by Permitted Financial Indebtedness and Permitted Security.
