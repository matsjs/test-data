13.3.5 Disposal o f business
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part o f the Group's assets and operations (provided always that the Issuer and any other Group Company having an ownership interest in any Licence may dispose o f all or part of the Bentley Interest in accordance with Clause 13.4.2 below), unless the transaction is carried out at Fair Market Value and such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect. The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of an ownership in the Bentley Licence.
13.3.6 A rm 's length transactions
The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for Fair Market Value.
13.3.7 Corporate status
None o f the Issuer or the Parent shall change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation, except for conversion o f the Issuer to a public company.
13.3.8 Compliance with laws
The Parent shall, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies shall, carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all aspects and comply in all respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time. Breach of these obligations shall be regarded as non-compliance only if such breach would have a Material Adverse Effect.
