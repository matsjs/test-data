14.9
If a quorum does not exist at a Holders' Meeting or in respect of a Written Procedure, the
Agent or the Issuer shall convene a second Holders' Meeting (in accordance with
Clause 15.1) or initiate a second Written Procedure (in accordance with Clause 16.1), as the
case may be, provided that the relevant proposal has not been withdrawn by the Person(s)
who initiated the procedure for Holders' consent. The quorum requirement in Clause 14.8
shall not apply to such second Holders' Meeting or Written Procedure.
