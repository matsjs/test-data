17.1.2
The Bond Trustee may take any step necessary to ensure the rights of the Bondholders in all matters related to the terms of this Bond Agreement. The Bond Trustee may postpone talcing action until such matter has been put forward to the Bondholders' Meeting.
17.1.3
Except as provided for in Clause 17.1.5 the Bond Trustee may reach decisions binding for all Bondholders concerning this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents, including amendments to and consents under the Finance Documents and waivers or modifications of certain provisions thereof, which, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, do not have a material adverse effect on the rights or interests of the Bondholders under the relevant Finance Documents.
17.1.4 Except as provided for in Clause 17.1.5, the Bond Trustee may reach decisions binding for all Bondholders in circumstances other than those mentioned in Clause 17.1.3 provided prior notification has been made to the Bondholders. Such notice
