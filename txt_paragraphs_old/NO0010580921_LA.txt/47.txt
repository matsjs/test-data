15 Events of Default
15.1 The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events or circumstances (each of which shall be referred to as an "Event of Default") if:
(a) Non-payment Any Obligor fails to fulfil any payment obligation under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure can be remedied, and payment in full is made, within 5 five - Business Days following the original due date.
(b) Breach o f other obligations Any Obligor or Material Subsidiary fails to duly perform any covenant, undertaking or obligation to which is it subject pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any of the Finance Documents (other than those referred to in Clause 15.1(a)), unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure can be remedied and is remedied within 10 - ten - Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
(c) Cross default In relation to the Issuer or any Subsidiary:
(i) any Financial Indebtedness or guarantee is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period,
