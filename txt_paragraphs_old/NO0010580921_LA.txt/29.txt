10 Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1
The Issuer may, at any time prior to the Maturity Date, redeem all or part of the Outstanding Bonds at par (100%) plus accrued interest thereon (the "Call Option"). The Issuer shall be entitled under the Call Option to redeem all of the Bonds held by Swedbanlc AB (publ) at par (100%) plus accrued interest thereon in circumstances where only some or none of the Bonds held by the other Bondholders are being redeemed.
