14.4 In addition to the fees due to the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 14.2 and normal expenses pursuant to Clauses 14.1 and 14.3, the Issuer shall, on demand, cover extraordinary expenses incurred by the Bond Trustee in connection with the Bonds (including, without limitation, costs, fees and expenses incurred in connection with any enforcement proceedings), as determined in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee.
Where the introduction of, or any change in, or any change in the interpretation, administration or application of, any applicable law or compliance with any law or regulation made after the Issue Date requires the Issuer to withhold or deduct amounts on any payment to the Bondholders, as soon as the Issuer becomes aware of the same, it must promptly notify the Bond Trustee, and the Bond Trustee must then promptly notify the Bondholders.
Within 30 days of making such a withholding or deduction or a payment required in connection with such a withholding or deduction, the Issuer must deliver to the Bond Trustee for the relevant Bondholder evidence satisfactory to the Bond Trustee (acting reasonably) that such withholding or deduction has been made and (if applicable) the appropriate payment has been paid to the relevant taxing authority.
