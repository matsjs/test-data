17.1.9
If the Bond Trustee receives information pursuant to a Finance Document other than this Bond Agreement, the Bond Trustee shall not (and shall not be required to) disclose such information to the Bondholders if the Bond Trustee reasonably believes that disclosure of such information would be prejudicial to the commercial interests of the Group and that withholding such information would not prejudice the Bondholders' rights or interests.
17.1.10 If the Bond Trustee receives information pursuant to a Finance Document other than this Bond Agreement and (i) the Bond Trustee reasonably believes that disclosure of such information would be prejudicial to the commercial interests of the Group or (ii) the Issuer has notified the Bond Trustee that, in the Issuer's opinion, disclosure of such information would be prejudicial to the commercial interests of the Group, if the Bond Trustee reasonably believes that withholding such information would prejudice the Bondholders' rights or interests, the Bond Trustee may disclose such information to the Bondholders but shall consult with the Issuer before disclosing such information as to the form and manner in which such information is to be disclosed.
