"Retiring Bond Trustee" is defined in Clause 17.3.1.
"Securities Register" means the securities register in which the Bond Issue is registered.
"Security Trustee" means the Bond Trustee, unless any other legal entity is appointed as collateral agent pursuant to Clause 17.4.
"Security Documents" means any document creating, establishing, recording, confirming or preserving any security interest over any Security Interest relating to any Finance Document.
"Security Interest" means the Guarantees or any Encumbrance or other security created (or to be created) by the Security Documents securing the obligations of the Obligors under this Bond Agreement.
"Subsidiary" means an entity over which another entity or person has a determining influence due to (i) direct and indirect ownership of shares or other ownership interests, and/or (ii) agreement, understanding or other arrangement. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of another entity or person if such entity or person has such number of shares or ownership interests or other rights so as to represent or control the majority of the votes in the entity, or has the right or control to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
"Successor Bond Trustee" is defined in Clause 17.3.1.
"Taxes" means all present and future taxes, levies, imposts, duties, charges, fees, deductions and withholdings, and any restrictions and or conditions resulting in a charge together with interest thereon and penalties in respect thereof and "Tax" and "Taxation" shall be construed accordingly.
