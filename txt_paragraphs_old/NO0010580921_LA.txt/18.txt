2.3 Purpose and utilization
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
2.3.1 The consideration for the initial Bondholders' subscription for the Bonds shall consist of the transfer to the Issuer (i) from the initial subscribers for the Bonds of the bonds issued by Remedial (Cyprus) Public Company Limited in the US$210 million FRN Remedial (Cyprus) Public Company Limited Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012, ISIN NO 001 036034.0, and (ii) from Swedbank Ab (publ) of a debt claim arising under, and its rights, title and interest under, three interest rate swap confirmations dated 20 November 2008, 16 January 2009 and 13 February 2009 and governed by an ISDA master agreement dated 20 November 2008.
2.3.2
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed for financing of the Vessels, fees, contingencies, supervision related to the Vessels, cost of equipment to be placed on the Vessels, general corporate purposes such as administration, payments and remuneration to employees and similar expenses or for the financing by way of financial indebtedness of Remedial (Cyprus) Public Company Limited (for so long as such entity has title to ESV1 and/or the Yantai Contract).
3
Listing
3.1 The Issuer is under no obligation to list the Bonds on an Exchange, but shall have the right to list the Bonds if it so desires with the approval of a Bondholder meeting.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
