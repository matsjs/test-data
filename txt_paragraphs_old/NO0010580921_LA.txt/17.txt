2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the principal amount of USD 164,500,000 (U.S. Dollar one hundred and sixty-four million and five hundred thousand). Additional Bonds may be issued in accordance with the provisions set out in Clause 9.
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1.00 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "10.00 per cent Remedial (Cayman) Limited Callable Bond Issue 2010/2012".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 058092.1.
The term of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
