Whoever opens the Bondholders' Meeting shall adjudicate any question concerning which Bonds shall count as the Issuer's Bonds. The Issuer's Bonds shall not have any voting rights and shall be deemed not to be outstanding for purposes of determining quorum or for purposes of any resolution, waiver, approval, demand, declaration or consent, whether taken at a Bondholders' Meeting or otherwise.
16.3.3
In order to form a quorum, at least half (1/2) of the Voting Bonds must be represented at the meeting, subject to Clauses 15.3 and 16.4. Even if less than half (1/2) of the Voting Bonds are represented, for purposes of Clauses 15.3 and 16.4 the Bondholders' Meeting shall be held and voting completed.
16.3.4 Resolutions shall be passed by simple majority of the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting, unless otherwise set forth in Clause 16.3.5.
16.3.5 In the following matters, a majority of at least 2/3 of the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting is required:
(a) any amendment of the terms of this Bond Agreement regarding the interest rate, the tenor, redemption price and other terms and conditions affecting the cash flow of the Bonds;
(b) any transfer of rights and obligations of this Bond Agreement to another issuer (Issuer);
(c) any release of a Guarantor from its obligations under its Guarantee; (d) any release of any Security Interest other than in connection with a disposal of
the asset subject to that Security Interest where that sale is expressly permitted by the terms of the Finance Documents; (e) any amendment or waiver the effect of which is to modify this Clause 16.3.5; (1) any change to the terms of the Finance Documents that might reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect on the rights or interests of the Bondholders under the relevant Finance Documents; or (g) any change of Bond Trustee or Security Trustee.
