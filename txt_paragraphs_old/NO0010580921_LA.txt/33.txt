11.4 Interest in the event o f late payment
11.4.1
In the event that payment of interest or principal is not made on the relevant Payment Date, the overdue amount shall bear interest from the relevant Payment Date at an interest rate equivalent to the interest rate according to Clause 9 plus 5.00 percentage points (the "Default Rate").
11.4.2
Any interest charged at the Default Rate shall be payable upon demand by the Bond Trustee. Any interest that is accrued and unpaid as of an Interest Payment Date shall be deemed an overdue amount and shall accrue interest at the Default Rate from such Interest Payment Date until paid in full.
11.4.3 Overdue amounts shall bear interest at the Default Rate as stated above until payment is made, whether or not the Bonds are declared to be in default by virtue of the fact that the existence of such overdue amounts constitute a Default under Clause 15.1(a).
