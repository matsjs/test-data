10.3 Change o f Control Put Option and mandatoryprepayment events
10.3.1 Immediately following the occurrence of a Change of Control Event (and in any event within five Business Days of such occurrence), the Issuer shall give written notice of such fact (the "Issuer Notice") to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders. The Issuer Notice shall (i) describe the facts and circumstances of such Change of Control in reasonable detail, (ii) refer to this Clause 10.3.1 and the rights of the Bondholders hereunder and state that a Change of Control Event has occurred, (iii) contain an offer by the Issuer to redeem all of the outstanding Bonds at par, together with interest thereon to the settlement date selected by the Issuer, which settlement date shall be specified in the Issuer Notice and which date shall be a Business Day not less than 90 days and not more than 120 days after such Issuer Notice is given, and (iv) request each Bondholder to notify the Bond Trustee in writing by the date that is 60 days after the date the Issuer Notice is delivered to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (the "Change of Control Response Date"), of its acceptance or rejection of such offer to redeem. If a Bondholder does not notify the Bond Trustee as provided above, then such Bondholder shall be deemed to have accepted such offer.
