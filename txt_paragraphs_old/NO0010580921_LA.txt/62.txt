17.3 Change o fBond Trustee
Change of Bond Trustee shall be carried out pursuant to the procedures set forth in Clause 16. The retiring Bond Trustee (the "Retiring Bond Trustee") shall continue to carry out its duties as bond trustee until such time that a new Bond Trustee (a "Successor Bond Trustee") is elected.
17.3.2
The fees and expenses of a Successor Bond Trustee shall be covered by the Issuer pursuant to the terms set out in Clause 14, but may be recovered wholly or partially from the Bond Trustee if the change is due to a breach of the Bond Trustee duties pursuant to the terms of this Bond Agreement or other circumstances for which the Bond Trustee is liable.
17.3.3 The Retiring Bond Trustee undertakes to co-operate so that the Successor Bond Trustee receives without undue delay the documentation and information necessary to perform the functions as set forth under the terms of this Bond Agreement.
17.3.4 Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in the foregoing, the replacement of a Retiring Bond Trustee and the appointment of any Successor Bond Trustee will both become effective only when the Successor Bond Trustee:
(i) notifies the Bondholders that it accepts its appointment; and
(ii) accedes to each of the Finance Documents to which the Bond Trustee is party in accordance with the respective terms thereof; and
(iii) confirms that it is satisfied that the rights of the Bond Trustee under the relevant Finance Documents have been assigned or transferred to it.
