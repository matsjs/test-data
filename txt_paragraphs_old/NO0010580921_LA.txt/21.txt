6.1 The consummation of the Bondholders' subscription for the Bonds contemplated hereby will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it, at least two Business Days prior to the Issue Date where possible, and otherwise on the Issue Date:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by all Parties;
(b) certified copies of corporate resolutions of each Obligor authorising the execution, delivery and performance of each of the Finance Documents to which it is a party and, in the case of the Issuer, the issuance of the Bonds;
(c) a power of attorney from each Obligor to relevant individuals for their execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant corporate register or similar documentation evidencing the individuals' authority to sign the relevant Finance Documents on behalf of that Obligor;
(d) certified copies of (i) the certificate of incorporation or other similar constitutional document for each Obligor, evidencing that it is validly existing and (ii) the articles of association of each Obligor;
(e) to the extent necessary, evidence of any public authorisations required for the Bond Issue;
(f) confirmation from the Paying Agent that the Bonds have been registered in the Securities Register;
(g) written confirmation from each Obligor in accordance with Clause 7.3 (if required);
