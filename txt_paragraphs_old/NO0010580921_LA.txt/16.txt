2
The Bonds
2.1 Binding nature o f the Bond Agreement and Finance Documents
2.1.1 The Bondholders are, through their subscription, purchase or other acquisition of Bonds, bound by the terms of the Bond Agreement and other Finance Documents. The initial Bondholders through their subscription in the Bonds granted authority to the Bond Trustee to finalize and execute the Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents to which the Bond Trustee is a party on their behalf while all Bond transfers are subject to the terms of this Bond Agreement and all Bond transferees are, in taking transfer of Bonds, deemed to have accepted the terms of the Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and will automatically become parties to the Bond Agreement upon completed transfer, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with; see also Clause 18.1.
2.1.2
The Bond Agreement is available to any Bondholders from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds.
