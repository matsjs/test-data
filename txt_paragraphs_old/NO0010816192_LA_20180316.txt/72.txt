De-mergers
Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations other than to the Issuer or any of its whollyowned Subsidiaries, unless such sale, transfer or disposal constitutes a Permitted Disposal and that no Event of Default is continuing or would follow from such disposal.
