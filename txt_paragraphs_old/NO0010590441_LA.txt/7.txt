"Earnings Account" means an account(s) in the name of the Ship Owning Company, pledged on first priority in favour of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to the Earnings Account Pledge, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights. Upon an Event of Default, the account shall be blocked.
"EBITDA" means for any Relevant Period earnings before Interest, Taxes, Depreciations and Amotization, on a consolidated basis for the Group.
"Encumbrance" means any encumbrance, mortgage, pledge, lien, charge (whether fixed or floating), assignment by way o f security, finance lease, sale and repurchase or sale and leaseback arrangement, sale o f receivables on a recourse basis or security interest or any other agreement or arrangement having the effect of conferring security.
"Escrow Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer, pledged on first priority and blocked in favour o f the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to the Escrow Account Pledge, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Issuer's claims against the relevant bank for the amounts from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Excess Value" means the difference (positive or negative) between the value of the Vessel and any other vessel owned by any member of the Group in the latest Financial Statements or Quarterly Financial Reports (as the case may be) and its current Market Value.
