2.2 The Bonds
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 1 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Havila Shipping ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2010/2016".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 059044.1.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date up to the Maturity Date.
