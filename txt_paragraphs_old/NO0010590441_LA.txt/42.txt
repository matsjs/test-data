10.4.2 Mandatory Redemption Event shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the relevant settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption Event.
10.4.3
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the relevant settlement date.
10.4.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
