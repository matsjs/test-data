Upon an Event of Default, the account shall be blocked.
"Earnings Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Ship Owning Company's claims against the relevant bank(s) for the amounts from time to time standing to the credit of the Ship Owning Company in the Earnings Account, securing a maximum principal amount of NOK 225,000,000 plus interest and expenses in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for (i) the Ship Owning Company's obligations under the Intercompany Loan and (ii) the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
