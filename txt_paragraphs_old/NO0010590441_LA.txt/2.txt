1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses m aybe defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Assignment of Earnings" means the first priority assignment of the Earnings o f the Ship Owning Company, securing a maximum principal amount of NOK 225,000,000 plus interest and expenses in favour o f the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), securing (i) the Ship Owning Company's obligations under the Intercompany Loan and (ii) the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
"Assignment of Insurances" means the first priority assignment of the insurance policies taken out by the Ship Owning Company relating to the Vessel, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), securing a maximum principal amount of NOK 225,000,000 plus interest and expenses, securing (i) the Ship Owning Company's obligations under the Intercompany Loan and (ii) the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
"Assignment of the Intercompany Loan" means the first priority assignment o f the Intercompany Loan, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), securing the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement.
"Attachment" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
