13.6 Vessel Covenants
(b) Insurance o f the Vessel
(a) The Issuer shall procure that the Vessel is fully insured against such risks (including, but not limited to Hull and Machinery, Hull Interest, Freight Interest, Protection & Indemnity (including a maximum club cover for oil pollution liability for the Vessel) and War Risk (including terrorism), in such amounts, on such terms (always applying Norwegian law and including the terms of the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan o f 1996, version 2003 (as amended from time to time) or such other terms as the Bond Trustee may approve (such approval not to be unreasonably withheld) in relation to losses payable thereunder) and with such insurance brokers and insurers as the Bond Trustee may approve or require.
(b) The insured value o f the Vessel shall at all times be equal to or greater than its Market Value and equal to or greater than 120 per cent of the aggregate principal value of the Outstanding Bonds. Furthermore, the Hull and Machinery insured value of the Vessel shall at all times cover 80 per cent of its Market Value, while the remaining cover may be taken out by way o f Hull and Freight Interest insurances.
(c) Not later than 7 days before the expiry date of the relevant insurances, the Issuer shall deliver to the Bond Trustee a certificate from the insurance brolcer(s) through whom the insurances relevant to the Vessel have been placed, evidencing that all insurances referred to in paragraph (a) above have been renewed and taken out in respect of the Vessel with insurance values as required by paragraph (b), that such insurances are in full force and effect and

that the interests o f the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) therein have been noted by the relevant insurers. (e) The Issuers shall procure that the Vessel is always employed in conformity with the terms of the instruments of insurance (including any expressed or implied warranties) and shall comply with such requirements as to extra premium or otherwise as the insurers may prescribe. (I) In the event of a Total Loss, the Issuer shall, within 120 days after the Total Loss Date, obtain and present to the Bond Trustee a written confirmation from the relevant insurers that the claim relating to the Total Loss has been accepted in full, and the insurance proceeds shall, as soon as they are released, be paid to the Bond Trustee and applied in a Mandatory Redemption pursuant to Clause 10.4.
(c) Classification and international regulations The Issuer shall procure that the Vessel is classified and maintained in the highest class for vessels such as the Vessel, free of any overdue recommendations, with Det Norske Veritas or another classification society acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and at all times complies with the applicable rules and regulations of the relevant classification society. Furthermore, the Issuer shall at all times ensure compliance with all applicable international conventions and regulations, including SOLAS conventions and the International Management Code for the Safe Operation of Ships and for Pollution Prevention adopted by the International Maritime Organisation and the International Ship and Port Security Code adopted by the International Maritime Organisation. In particular, the Issuer shall ensure compliance with the ISM Code and the ISPS Code and shall ensure that any charterer of the Vessel and any company performing management services for the Vessel comply with said conventions and regulations.
