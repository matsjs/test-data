13.5 Preservation o f equity, Security Interests and Financial Covenants
(a) Consolidated Value Adjusted Equity Ratio The Issuer undertakes to ensure that the Group maintains a consolidated Value Adjusted Equity Ratio o f at least:
(i) 25 per cent from and including Issue Date until but not including 30 June 2011,
(ii) 26.50 per cent from and including 30 June 2011 until but not including 30 June 2012,
(iii) 28.00 per cent from and including 30 June 2012 and until Maturity Date.
(b) Liquidity Reserve From the earlier of (i) the release to the Issuer of the funds in the Escrow Account and (ii) the delivery of the Vessel to the Ship Owning Company, the Issuer undertakes to ensure that the Group maintains Cash and Cash Equivalent of no less thanNOK 150,000,000.
