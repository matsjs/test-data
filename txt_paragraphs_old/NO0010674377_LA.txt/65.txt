13.5 Mergers The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of fire assets and obligations of the Issuer or any of the Subsidiaries with any other companies or entities, except for any change resulting from a Permitted Reorganisation.
13.7 Continuation of business (a) The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business.

(b) The Issuer shall remain as a single-purpose company leasing out the Properties and owning such Properties through its 100% directly owned Subsidiaries and Additional Subsidiaries.
(c) The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature or scope of the business of the Group from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
13.8 Disposal of business The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of (i) all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations except Permitted Reorganisation or (ii) a Property, unless the Bonds are redeemed in full in accordance with the Mandatory Prepayment provisions.
13.9 Aim's length transactions The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.10 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
13.11 Constitutional documents The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, amend its constitutional documents in such a way which would have a Material Adverse Effect (it being understood that any amendment relating to issuance of new shares will be permitted).
