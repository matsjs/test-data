13.18 Financial Covenants The Issuer shall ensure that:
(a) the aggregate of (i) the Market Value of the Properties and (ii) the amount standing to the credit of the Issuer at the Escrow Account and the Earnings Account, at all times exceeds 120% of the total Financial Indebtedness of the Group (on a consolidated basis) (the "LTV requirement"); and
(b) the amount on die Earnings Account is at all times no less than minimum NOK 5 million (the "Minimum Cash Requirement").
(c) No breach of a financial covenant set out in paragraphs 13.18 (a) or (b) above will occur if the Issuer deposits a cash amount in NOK (the "Cash Amount") in the Earnings Account within 15 Business Days from the earlier of (i) the Issuer becoming aware of the failure to comply with any financial covenant and (ii) delivery of a Compliance Certificate evidencing such failure to comply, always provided that:
(i) the relevant financial covenant(s) would be complied with if tested for the same testing period on the basis that such Cash Amount was deemed to have been made immediately prior to the relevant testing date; and
(ii) the Cash Amount shall remain deposited on the Earnings Account for as long as it is required to comply with the financial covenant(s).
14. FEES AND EXPENSES 14.1 Expenses
The Issuer shall cover all costs and expenses incurred by it or the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent) in connection with this Bond Agreement and the fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, including in connection with the negotiation preparation, execution and enforcement of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), the listing of the Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository. The Bond Trustee may withhold funds from any escrow account (or similar arrangement) or from other funds received from the Issuer or any other person), irrespective of such funds being subject to Security under a Finance Documents, set-off cover any such costs and expenses.
