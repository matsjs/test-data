13.14 Maintenance of Properties The Issuer shall exercise its rights under the Project Documents to procure that the Lessee fulfils its maintenance obligations, hereunder that the Properties and all inventory are kept in a state of good and safe condition and state of repair consistent with good industry standard, and if necessary, replaced with items of similar quality and value, all according to a pre-agreed planned maintenance system. Such maintenance shall be paid directly by the Lessee.
13.15 Insurances The Issuer shall ensure that reasonable and satisfactory insurance cover of the Properties and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times (the "Insurances"), and such Insurances shall be taken out with reputable insurance companies and be paid directly by the Lessee. The Issuer shall ensure that the Insurances for each Property covers its foil value (No: 'fullverdiforsikret" - including, for the avoidance of doubt, coverage to rebuild the property to meet the then current public requirements and cleaning up any damage on the laird), rental loss for 36 months (No: "driftsavbruddsforsikring") and all such other risks as are customary for companies engaged in real estate generally and preschooling specifically to take out insurance. The Issuer shall provide documentation evidencing that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) is also covered by the Insurances.
