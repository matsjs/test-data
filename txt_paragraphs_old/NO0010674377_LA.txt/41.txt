6.2.2 Pre-disbursement of Residual Amount
The Residual Amount on the Escrow Account shall only be used according to the Purpose of the Bond Issue, and any release (the "Release") from the Escrow Account shall be subject to certain conditions precedent customary for these types of transactions to have been met prior to or in connection with the Release, including but not limited to and in form satisfactory to the Bond Trustee:
(a) satisfactory documentation evidencing that the Issuer, for the purpose of financing the equity portion of the acquisition of the Additional Properties, has issued additional new shares and equity equivalent to no less than 30% of the Acquisition Price for the Additional Properties has been paid in to the Issuer in full;
(b) a duly executed release notice from the Issuer;
