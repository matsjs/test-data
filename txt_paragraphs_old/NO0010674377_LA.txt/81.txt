15.5 Insolvency
(a)
A Group Company, is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall
due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or
anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of
its creditors with a view to rescheduling any of its indebtedness.
(b) The value of the assets of any member of the Group is less than its liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities).
(c) A moratorium is declared in respect of any indebtedness of any member of the Group.
