"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
"Permitted Reorganisation" means internal restructuring within the Group on a solvent and arm's length basis, provided that (i) the position of the Bondholders, in Trustee's reasonable opinion, are not in any way jeopardized, (ii) the LTV Requirement is complied with after the internal reorganization and - (iii) such restructuring would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Pledge of Claims Internal Leases" means a pledge over the Issuer's rights under the Internal Leases (being registered in the land register).
"Pledge of Claims M aster Lease" means a pledge of Issuer's claims under the Master Lease Agreement.
"Pledge of Claims against Bank under Cash Pooling System" means, to the extent permitted by applicable law; a pledge over the Issuer's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the top account under the Cash Pooling System (such account to be unblocked).
"Pledge of Claims against Subsidiaries under Cash Pooling System" means a pledge over the Issuer's intercompany receivables, from time to time, against the Subsidiaries under the Cash Pooling System.
"Properties" means the Initial Properties and the Additional Properties.
"Property Owning Company" means each of the companies which own a Property.
