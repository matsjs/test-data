13.17 Special covenants
Ownership to Property Owning Companies
The Issuer shall procure that it remains the owner of 100% of the shares and voting rights in the Property Owning Companies (directly or indirectly); and that each Property Owning Company remains the sole owner and holds the title of each respective Property.
(b)
Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not allow itself, to declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other capital distributions to its shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party and not make any payments under the Subordinated Loan whether in cash or in kind other than conversion to shares during the term of the Bonds).
(c)
Earnings Account
The Issuer shall procure that all earnings under the Master Lease shall be paid into the Earnings Account.
