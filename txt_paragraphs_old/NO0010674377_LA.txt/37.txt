The Initial Disbursement Amount on the Escrow Account shall only be used according to the Purpose of the Bond Issue, and any release from the Escrow Account shall be subject to certain conditions precedent customary for these types of transactions, including hut not limited to (in form satisfactory to the Bond Trustee):
