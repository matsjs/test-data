(f)
A majority of at least 2/3 of the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders'
Meeting is required for any waiver or amendment of any terms of this Bond
Agreement.
(g) The Bondholders' Meeting may not adopt resolutions which may give certain Bondholders or others an unreasonable advantage at the expense of other Bondholders.
