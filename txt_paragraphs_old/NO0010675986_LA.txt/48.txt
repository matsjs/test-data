13.3.1.2 Mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any of such Group Company with any other companies or entities not being a member of the Group if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
