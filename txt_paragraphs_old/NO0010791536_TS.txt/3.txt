"Business Day" means a day other than a Saturday, Sunday or a public holiday in Norway on which the Norwegian Central Bank's and the CSD's settlement systems are open and commercial banks in Norway are open for business.
"Business Day Convention" means that no adjustment of interest will be made, notwithstanding the date of payment occurs on a day that is not a Business Day, and if such date is not a Business Day, payments of interest and/or principal (as the case may be) will be made on the first following day that is a Business Day.
"Change of Control Event" means a person or group of persons acting in concert gaining Decisive Influence over the Group.
