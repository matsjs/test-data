Payments in respect of the Notes
Any payment or repayment under the Finance Documents, or any amount due in respect
of a repurchase of any Notes requested by a Noteholder pursuant to these Terms and
Conditions, shall be made to such person who is registered as a Noteholder on the Record
Date prior to an Interest Payment Date or other relevant due date, or to such other person
who is registered with the CSD on such date as being entitled to receive the relevant
payment, repayment or repurchase amount.
