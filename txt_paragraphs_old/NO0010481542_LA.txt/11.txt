2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of bonds at a maximum amount of USD 45,000,000 (US dollar forty-five million), herein referred to as the "Loan". The Loan may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranch will be in the amount of USD 8,800,000 (US dollar eightmillion and eighthundered).
The Loan is an open (tap) issue, running from the Disbursement Date and will be closed no later than 5 Banking Days prior to the Maturity Date.
