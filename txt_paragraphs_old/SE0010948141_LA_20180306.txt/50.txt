Other Obligations
The Issuer does not comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out under Clause 13.1 (Non-Payment) above, provided that the Trustee has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within ten (10) Business Days from such request ( if the failure or violation is not capable o f being remedied, the Trustee may declare the Bonds due and payable without such prior written request).
