12.6 Disposal of A sse ts
I he Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Subsidiary, sell or otherwise dispose o f shares in any Subsidiary or o f all or substantially all of its or that Subsidiary's assets, or operations to any person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries, unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.

The Issuer shall not. and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any security (including for the avoidance of doubt guarantees) over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure Financial Indebtedness, prov ided however that the Group Companies have a right to (i) prov ide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security, and (ii) retain, but not prolong or renew, any existing security in relation to indebtedness held by an entity acquired, provided it constitutes Permitted Security.
