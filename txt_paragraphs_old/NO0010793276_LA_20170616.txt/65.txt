(k) Subordination
The Issuer shall ensure that all loans to the Issuer or any Subsidiary of the Issuer from any of the Issuer's direct or indirect shareholders or any other third party not being a member of the Group shall be fully subordinated to the Bonds, with maturity after the Maturity Date and with no cash pay interest and that the parties thereto enters into a turnover agreement.
(l) Ownership
The Issuer shall:
(i) maintain 100% direct ownership of the Rig except as set out in Clause 13.8(b) (Rig Transfer), or otherwise as approved by the Bond Trustee or, where necessary, the Bondholders' Meeting; and
(ii) maintain a 100% ownership interest (save for any minority ownership interest necessary for operations under any legal framework where the Rig is chartered) and control over each Subsidiary (if any).
