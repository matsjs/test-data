The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Subsidiary shall:

Accounts (a) Maintenance of the Accounts
The Issuer shall ensure that the Escrow Account and the other Accounts are maintained with DNB Bank ASA or with any first class international bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
