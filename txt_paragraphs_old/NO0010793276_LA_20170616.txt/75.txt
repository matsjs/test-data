13.8 Charter Transfer and Rig Transfer (a) Charter Transfer
The Charter may be entered into by a Subsidiary of the Issuer provided that the relevant Subsidiary delivers (i) the relevant charter transfer documents in compliance with the terms of the Existing Bonds, and (ii) any other relevant documentation reasonably required by the Bond Trustee.
(b) Rig Transfer
The Issuer may transfer ownership of the Rig to a directly wholly-owned Subsidiary established in Malta or Luxembourg provided that it delivers (i) the relevant charter transfer documents in compliance with the terms of the Existing Bonds and (ii) any other relevant documentation reasonably required by the Bond Trustee, both to the satisfaction of the Bond Trustee.
