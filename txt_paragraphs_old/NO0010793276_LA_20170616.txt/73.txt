(b) Insurance
(ii) The Issuer shall procure during the operations of the Rig that the Rig Manager runs proper maintenance of the Rig according to a planned maintenance system.
(iii) The Issuer shall ensure that the Insurances, and any loss payable clause issued in respect of the Insurances or similar provisions therein, shall be in accordance with the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan or other insurance plan with at least equally favourable terms.
(iv) The Issuer shall maintain or ensure that the Rig is adequately insured against such risks, including but not limited to, Hull and Machinery/Hull Interest risks, Protection & Indemnity, Mortgage Interest Insurance and War Risk Insurances, as well as any additional insurances required under any other contracts.
(v) The Issuer shall keep a Mortgagee's Interest Insurance on standard market terms in respect of rigs of this type in the waters it operates.
(vi) The Issuer shall ensure that the insurable value of the Rig shall be for at least the full market value of the Rig as determined by a valuation performed by an independent third party expert and at least equal to or the higher of 120 % of the Outstanding Bonds.
(vii) The Issuer shall ensure that all the Insurances shall be maintained with reputable insurers of good financial standing.
(c) Title
The Issuer shall hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the Rig and the Insurances taken out in respect of the Rig, other than as set out in Clause 2.3 (Alternate Issuer) or 13.8 (Charter Transfer and Rig Transfer), and assigned to the Bond Trustee, free of any and all Security except for those expressly permitted under this Bond Agreement.
