(c) Breach of Liquidity financial covenant
The Issuer does not comply with Clause 13.6(a) (Liquidity) and such breach is not remedied within thirty (30) Business Days of the earlier of (i) the date on which the Issuer sent notice of non-compliance to the Bond Trustee or (ii) the date on which the Issuer became aware of such breach and therefore should have sent such notice. Remedy shall be made by providing the required funds through equity or subordinated loans to the Issuer (on terms acceptable to the Bond Trustee) which shall be deposited (for the required period, which in any event shall not expire prior to the next reporting date of the Financial Covenants) on a separate account to be pledged but not blocked to the Bond Trustee and where the account bank has waived its set-off rights.
