10.1.3 Extended maturity If at any time prior to the Maturity Date the Issuer issues a Tap Issue in an amount of USD 1,000,000 or more, or the Issuer issues Manager Rig Bonds, the Issuer will in addition have the option (in its sole discretion) to unilaterally extend the maturity date of the Bonds for one (1) additional year, until 16 June 2020, three (3) years after the Issue Date.
The Issuer shall promptly notify the Bond Trustee in writing of its resolution to so extend the Maturity Date.
10.1.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.1 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
