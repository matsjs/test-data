10.3 10.3.1
Mandatory Redemption
In the event that (i) the Rig is sold or disposed of, or (ii) there is an expropriation or hijack of the Rig (in either case, to the extent not a Total Loss Event), then a mandatory prepayment event (each a "Mandatory Prepayment Event") will occur and the Issuer shall, on or about the day the relevant Group Company receives the proceeds following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event, redeem all the Outstanding Bonds at a price equal to 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.3.2
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly as soon as the insurance proceeds are available to the relevant Group Company, but no later than ninety (90) days following the Total Loss Event, redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.3.3 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.3, the amounts on the Accounts and the Subsidiary Earnings Account and Subsidiary Operating Account (if any), or any insurance proceeds may be used as partial payment for the redemption.
10.3.4 The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing (i) immediately about any Mandatory Prepayment Event or any Total Loss Event and (ii) at least ten (10) Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption
