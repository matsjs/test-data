13.5.3
The Issuer shall be entitled to make payments from the Reserve Account to the Operating Account where (i) the funds on the Operating Account fall below the minimum amount of USD 400,000; or (ii) as otherwise required by the Rig Manager for the management of the Rig, in accordance with the terms of the Management Agreement.
13.5.4
At any time after 1 September 2018, if the Rig is not earning hire under a contract for offshore services, and the total cash balance of the Issuer is less than USD 5,000,000, the Issuer shall have the option of making payment of the fixed management fee referred to in 13.5.1 (a)(ii) above through the issue of Rig Manager Bonds.
