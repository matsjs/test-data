"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to acts as its paying agent in the Securities Registry with respect to the Bonds.
"Permitted Disposal" means:
(a) a sale to a purchaser that is not in any way directly or indirectly related to any of the shareholders in the Parent, the Bondholders or the Original Shareholders of (i) all the shares in the capital of the Issuer or (ii) all or substantially all the business and/or assets of the Issuer; or
(b) the admission to trading of Parent's or the Issuer's shares on an Exchange.
"Permitted Financial Indebtedness" means:
(a) any Financial Indebtedness arising under the BondIssue (including the Tap Issue);
(b) any Financial Indebtedness arising under the Existing Bonds;
(c) any subordinated loans; and
(d) any Intercompany Loans.
