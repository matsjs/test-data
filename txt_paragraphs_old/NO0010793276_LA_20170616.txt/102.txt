18.5
Amendments
All amendments of this Bond Agreement shall be made in writing, and shall unless otherwise provided for by this Bond Agreement, only be made with the approval of all parties hereto.
With effect from the Rig Charter Contract Date, the terms of this Bond Agreement in particular with respect to the Maturity Date, interest, cash sweep and covenants shall be amended on terms approved by a Bondholders' Meeting or a Written Resolution, provided that the interest rate payable under the amended terms of the Bond Agreement shall not exceed 10.00 %.
