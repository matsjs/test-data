"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 20.
"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks in Norway are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo.
"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Interest Payment Date falls on a day that is not a Business Day, that date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day (Modified Following Business Day Convention).
"Call Date" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.2.
"Change of Control Event" means any event where a Person or group of Persons acting in concert, other than Altor Fund III, directly or indirectly, acquire a majority of the share capital in the Issuer or gain Decisive Influence over the Issuer.
