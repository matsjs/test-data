20.4 Repeated Bondholders' meeting
(a)
If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause
20.3(d), a repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the
same matters. The attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders'
Meeting shall be specified in the summons for the repeated Bondholders'
Meeting.
(b) A valid resolution may be passed at a repeated Bondholders' meeting even though less than half (1/2) o f the Voting Bonds are represented.
