15. MANDATORY PREPAYMENT OF THE BONDS
15.1.1
The Issuer shall ensure that an amount equal to any Excess Cashflow is paid into the Mandatory Prepayment Account within three (3) Business Days of delivery of a Financial Report pursuant to paragraph (a) or (b) of Clause 18.15.1.
15.1.2 Provided that any funds stand to the credit of the Mandatory Prepayment Account on:
(a) on the date falling twenty-four (24) months after the First Issue Date; and
(b) on the date falling thirty (30) months after the First Issue Date,
the Issuer shall prepay Bonds in an amount equal to seventy-five (75) per cent, of the funds standing to the credit of the Mandatory Prepayment Account on such dates no later than the date falling twenty (20) Business Days after each such date.
