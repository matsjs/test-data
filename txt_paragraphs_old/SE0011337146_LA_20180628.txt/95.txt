18.16 Agent Agreement
18.16.1 The Issuer shall, in accordance with the Agent Agreement:
(a) pay fees to the Agent; (b) indemnify the Agent for costs, losses and liabilities; (c) furnish to the Agent all information reasonably requested by or otherwise required to
be delivered to the Agent; and
(d) not act in a way which would give the Agent a legal or contractual right to terminate the Agent Agreement.
