The Issuer may, subject to applicable law, at any time and at any price purchase Notes on the market or in any other way. Notes held by the Issuer shall be promptly cancelled by the Issuer.
14.3.1
Subject to Clause 14.3.2, the Issuer may prepay Bonds, in whole or in part, on any Business Day before the Final Redemption Date (together with accrued but unpaid Interest) at the applicable Call Option Price plus the Preference Return.
14.3.2
A redemption pursuant to Clause 14.3.1 shall be made in an amount which reduces the Total Nominal Amount by a minimum amount of SEK 10,000,000 and, in case of a partial prepayment, shall reduce the Nominal Amount of each bond pro rata (rounded down to the nearest SEK 1.00).
14.3.3
Redemption in accordance with Clause 14.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than fifteen (15) Business Days' notice to the Holders and the Agent. Any such notice shall state the Redemption Date and the relevant Record Date and is irrevocable but may, at the Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent. Upon expiry of such notice and the fulfilment of the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is bound to redeem the Bonds in full at the applicable amounts.
