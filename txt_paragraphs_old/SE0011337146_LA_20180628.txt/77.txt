15.2 Mandatory Redemption due to Mandatory Prepayment Event
Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall redeem all Bonds at a price equal to the applicable Call Option Price together with any accrued but unpaid interest at the times contemplated by Clause 15.2.2.
