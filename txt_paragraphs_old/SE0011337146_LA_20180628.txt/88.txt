The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business as carried out by the Group on the Issue Date if such change is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness, other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
18.4 Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Restricted Group Company, create or allow to subsist any Security or guarantee over any of its assets, other than any Permitted Security.
18.5 Leasehold Rights
18.5.1
All Wind Turbines over which the Restricted Group has any Ownership Interests must be located on real property which is subject to Leasehold Rights in favour of a Restricted Group Company that has Ownership Interests over the relevant Wind Turbine.
18.5.2
The terms and conditions of the Leasehold Agreements shall not be materially amended and any new Lease Agreements entered into with respect to new Leasehold Rights shall be on terms and conditions materially corresponding to the terms and conditions of the Lease Agreements existing on the Issue Date.
