The aggregate amount of the bond loan will be an amount of up to SEK 62,000,000 which will
be represented by Bonds, each in the relevant Initial Nominal Amount. The total nominal
amount of the Bonds issued on the First Issue Date is SEK 50,000,000 (the "Initial Bond
Issue"). All Initial Bonds are issued with an original issue discount of twelve point five
(12.50) per cent, at an issue price of eighty seven point five (87.50) per cent, of the Initial
Nominal Amount.
