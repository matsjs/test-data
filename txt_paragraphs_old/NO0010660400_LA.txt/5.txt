"Change of Control Event" means if and when the Parents cease to hold in aggregate, directly or indirectly, more than 50% of the outstanding shares and voting rights of the Issuer.
"Charge over Inventory" means a charge over TTI's inventory (including unfinished and finished goods, fuels and other consumables) (No: varelagerpant).
"Charge over Machinery and Plant" means a first priority pledge/charge over TTI's machinery and plant (No: driftstilbehørspant).
"Charge over Trade Receivables" means a first priority charge over TTI's trade receivables (No: factoringpant).
"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person:
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
