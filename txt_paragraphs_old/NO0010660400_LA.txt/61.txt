13.5 Financial covenants
(a) Equity Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains, on a consolidated basis, an Equity Ratio of a minimum of 35%.
(b) Liquidity The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains, on a consolidated basis, Liquidity of a minimum of USD 15 million.
(c) Interest Coverage Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains, on a consolidated basis, an Interest Coverage Ratio of not less than 2.5.
(d) Financial testing The Issuer undertakes to comply with the above financial covenants at all times, such compliance to be measured on each Reporting Date by reference to the Financial Statements or Interim Accounts delivered pursuant to Clause 13.2.1(d) and (e).
14 Fees and expenses
14.1 The Issuer shall cover all costs and expenses incurred by it and all documented costs and expenses incurred by the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent) in connection with this Bond Agreement and the fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, including in connection with the negotiation, preparation, execution and enforcement of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), the listing of the Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository. The Bond Trustee may withhold funds from any escrow account (or similar arrangement) or from other funds received from the Issuer or any other person, irrespective of such funds being subject to Security under a Finance Documents, to set-off and cover any such costs and expenses.
