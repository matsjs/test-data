"Reporting Date" means 30 June and 31 December each year.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 17.4.
"Security" means any encumbrance, mortgage, charge, pledge, lien or other security interest securing any obligation of any person or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security and Covenant Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
"Security Interests" means:
(a) the Share Pledge; and
(b) the Assignment of Intercompany Loans.
"Share Pledge" means a first priority pledge over all the outstanding shares (100%) in TTI.
