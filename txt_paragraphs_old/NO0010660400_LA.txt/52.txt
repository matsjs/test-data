(a) Restrictions on dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any divided payment, repurchase of shares or make any other distributions to its shareholders (including, but not limited to, loans) before the Grande Cote Mineral Sands Project has been completed to the extent that commercial operations have been commenced with costs no longer being capitalised. Further, and at any time thereafter, the Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make any other distributions to its shareholders (including, but not limited to, loans) exceeding 50% of the Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the Financial Statements for the previous financial year, provided, however, that:
(i)
subsequent to the incurrence of any Permitted Indebtedness, the Leverage
Ratio is less than 1.5 on the Reporting Date prior to the date such dividend
transaction takes place, and
(ii) that no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing under any Finance Document.
