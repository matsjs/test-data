(e) Subordinated loans
The Issuer shall ensure that any subordinated loan or shareholder loan to the
Issuer or any other Group Company from any of the Parents or any other
person: (A) shall be unsecured; (B) shall be expressly subordinated to the
prior payment in full in cash of the Bonds; (C) may not have any default
provisions or provide to the holder any acceleration rights that are
exercisable so long as the Bonds are outstanding; (D) may not be repaid or
redeemed so long as the Bonds are outstanding; (E) may not obligate the
Issuer to pay interest unless the payment thereof is subject to compliance
