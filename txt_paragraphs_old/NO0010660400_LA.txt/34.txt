10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part as follows (a "Call Option"):
(a) with settlement date at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2015, to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2016 at a price equal to 104% of the Face Value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(b) with settlement date at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2016, to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 102% of the Face Value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least 30 Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
