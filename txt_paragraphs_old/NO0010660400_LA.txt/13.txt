"Maintenance Period" means a period of no longer than 4 calendar months within which the Phase 1 Upgrade shall be completed.
"Mandatory Prepayment Offer" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.3.
"Mandatory Prepayment Offer Event" means if:
(a) the Tyssedal Facility is sold or otherwise transferred, other than to a Group Company; or
(b) any o f the shares in TTI, GCO, TML, or any Group Company to which the Tyssedal Facility has been transferred are sold or otherwise transferred, or any new shares in any such Group Company is issued, to any person other than a Group Company.
"Material Adverse Effect" means an event or circumstance which has a material adverse effect on: (a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Issuer and/or the Group (taken as a whole), (b) the Issuer's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents; or (c) the validity or enforceability of the Finance Documents.
"Maturity Date" means 28 September 2017. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Net Interest Cost" means, in respect of any Relevant Period, the aggregate gross cash interest costs of the Group related to the Group's interest-bearing debt less the aggregate gross cash interest income of the Group for that Relevant Period, provided, however, that realized or unrealized gains or losses on interest rate, currency or derivative transactions shall not be considered as interest income or cost.
"NOK" means Norwegian kroner, being the lawful currency of Norway.
