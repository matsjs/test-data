2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 150,000,000 (one hundred and fifty million U.S. Dollars).
The Face Value is USD 100,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "9.00 per cent. TiZir Limited Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2012/2017".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 066040.0.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds (after deduction of fees, legal costs of the Managers and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses) shall be applied towards the partial funding of the construction of the Grande Cote Mineral Sands Project.
