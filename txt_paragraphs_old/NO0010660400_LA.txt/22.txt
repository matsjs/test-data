2.3 Purpose and utilization
3
Listing
3.1 The Issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on an Exchange to be elected by the Issuer in consultation with the Managers.
3.2 The Issuer shall use its best endeavours to ensure that the Bonds are listed on an Exchange no later than the date which falls six months from the Issue Date, and if the Bonds are listed, that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
4
Registration in the Securities Depository
4.1 The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall prior to disbursement be registered in the Securities Depository according to the Norwegian Securities Depository Act (Act 2002/64) and the terms and conditions of the Securities Depository.
