"Leverage Ratio" means the ratio of Total Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA.
"Liquidity" means the sum of (i) the aggregate book value of the Group's Cash and Cash Equivalents; and (ii) undrawn committed revolving credit lines available to the Group (but excluding committed revolving credit lines with less than six months to maturity).
