"Permitted Indebtedness" means any additional Financial Indebtedness incurred by the Issuer with the purpose of financing the Phase 2 Upgrade up to a maximum amount of the lower of (i) USD 300 million and (ii) 75% of the total cost of the expansion as estimated by the Tyssedal Feasibility Study.
"Phase 1 Upgrade" means the planned shutdown of the Tyssedal Facility in 2014 for a maintenance programme and capacity upgrade of the existing furnace.
"Phase 2 Upgrade" means the expansion of the capacity of the Tyssedal Facility with a second furnace and other related equipment.
"Put Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.4.
"Real Estate Mortgages" means first priority real estate mortgages (No: pant ifast eiendom) by TTI over each property owned by TTI.
"Relevant Period" means each period of twelve months ending on a Reporting Date.
"Reporting Date" means 30 June and 31 December each year.
"Securities Depository" means the securities depository in which the Bond Issue is registered, being Verdipapirsentralen ASA (VPS) in Norway.
