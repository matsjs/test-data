APPOINTMENT AND DUTIES OF THE AGENT
Appointment of Agent
8.1.1
By subscribing for Notes, each initial Noteholder appoints the Agent to act as its agent in all
matters relating to the Notes and the Finance Documents, and authorises the Agent to act on
its behalf (without first having to obtain its consent) in any legal or arbitration proceedings
relating to the Notes held by such Noteholder. By acquiring Notes, each subsequent
Noteholder confirms such appointment and authorisation for the Agent to act on its behalf.
