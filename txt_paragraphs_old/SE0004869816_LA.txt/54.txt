"Material Group Company" means a Group Company which together with its Subsidiaries (on a consolidated basis) has net sales or gross assets (excluding intra-group items) representing 5 per cent. or more of the total consolidated net sales or gross assets (excluding intra-group items) of the Group, in each case calculated by reference to the then latest audited accounts of such Group Company (consolidated, if it has Subsidiaries) and the then latest consolidated audited accounts of the Issuer.

provided that the total amount of reductions under paragraphs (b) and (c) above may not exceed an amount equal to the proceeds received by the Issuer from the issue of the Notes which is not used to refinance the Existing Financial Indebtedness.
For the avoidance of doubt, the Net Debt shall not include the marked to market value of any derivative transaction referred to in paragraph (e) of the definition of Financial Indebtedness (unless entered into for speculative purposes). To the extent a Group Company has provided cash collateral for debt owed by itself or another Group Company, the amount of such debt and the corresponding cash collateral shall be excluded from the calculation of the Net Debt.
"Net Leverage Ratio" means the ratio of total Net Debt to EBITDA.
