Upon the appointment of a successor Agent, the retiring Agent shall be discharged from any
further obligation in respect of the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement but shall
remain entitled to the benefit of the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement in
respect of any action which it took or failed to take whilst acting as Agent. Its successor, the
Issuer and each of the Noteholders shall have the same rights and obligations amongst
themselves under the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement as they would have
had if such successor had been the original Agent.
