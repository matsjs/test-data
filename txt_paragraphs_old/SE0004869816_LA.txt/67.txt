5.1
The Initial Notes carry Interest from (but excluding) the First Issue Date up to (and
including) the Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Notes carry Interest from (but excluding)
the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to their issuance up to (and including)
the Redemption Date. Interest accrues during an Interest Period.
5.2
The Notes will carry interest at the Interest Rate.
