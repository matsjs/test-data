10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS, CALL OPTION AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall be repaid by the Issuer at the Final Maturity Date at 100 % of par value plus accrued and unpaid interest.
10.2 Call Option 10,2.1 The Issuer may redeem the entire Bond Issue (all or nothing) as follows:
(a) from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2012 at a price equivalent to the sum of:
(i) the present value on the Relevant Record Date of 106 % of the par value; and
