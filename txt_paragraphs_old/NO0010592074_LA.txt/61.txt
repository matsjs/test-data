10.5 Cash Sweep Prepayment
10.5.1 The Issuer shall, in the event that the amounts standing to credit in the DSRA reaches an amount equal to or higher than the Cash Sweep Amount (a "Cash Sweep Event"), apply the entire amount on the DSRA to redeem Bonds at a price of 100 % of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount). Any amount on the DSRA which does not relate to gross earnings from the Drillship transferred to the DSRA pursuant to Clause 8.3.1 or 8.3.2 (as the case may be) may only be applied provided that Clause 10.2 (Call Option) is complied with.
