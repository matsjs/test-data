2.2 The Bond Issue
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum aggregate amount of USD 460,000,000 (US Dollars four hundred and sixty million).
2.2.2 The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and rank pari passu between each other,
2.2.3 The Bond Issue will be described as " 11.0 per cent Golden Close Maritime Corp. Ltd Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2010/2015".
2.2.4 The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 059207.4.
2.2.5 The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date up to and including the Final Maturity Date.
2.2.6 The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account on the Issue Date.
