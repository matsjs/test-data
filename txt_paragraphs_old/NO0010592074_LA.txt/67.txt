13.3 General Covenants
(a) Ranking and subordination
(i) The Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu with the claims of all the Issuer's other unsubordinated creditors, save for those whose claims are preferred solely by any bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application.
(ii) The Issuer shall procure that all of the Issuer's intra-group debt (if any) and all payments to the Drillship Manager and the Project Manager (save for such fees as are unsubordinated pursuant to the Assignment of Management Agreements) are fully subordinated to the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
