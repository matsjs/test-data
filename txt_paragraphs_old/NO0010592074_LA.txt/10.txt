"DSRA" means the debt service reserve account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank, to be funded in accordance with Clause 8,3 (Applications o f proceeds prior to an Event o f Default) and utilised in accordance with the provisions of Clause 13.4(b) only. The account shall be blocked and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"DSRA Pledge" means the pledge over the DSRA, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Earnings Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank, into which all earnings of the Issuer and all net earnings related to the Drillship shall be paid directly from the contracting party (unless a Subsidiary enters into the Drilling Contract pursuant to Clause 8.2). The account shall be pledged (but not blocked) in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Earnings Account Pledge" means the pledge over the Earnings Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Encumbrance" means any encumbrance, mortgage, pledge, lien, charge (whether fixed or floating), assignment by way of security, finance lease, sale and repurchase or sale and leaseback arrangement, sale of receivables on a recourse basis or security interest or any other agreement or arrangement having the effect of conferring security.
"Equity Escrow Account" means an account held by the Issuer with the Account Bank, hi which the Additional Equity shall be kept and utilized only pursuant to Clause 6.2.1, The account shall be blocked and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), and the Account Bank shall have waived any set-off rights.
"Equity Escrow Account Pledge" means the pledge over the Equity Escrow Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
