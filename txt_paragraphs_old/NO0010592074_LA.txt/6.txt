"Assignment of Earnings" means the assignment of all earnings payable to the Issuer (including earnings under any Drilling Contract, charters and bare-boat agreements for hire) related to the Drillship, and the Issuer shall give notices and use its best endeavours to procure the acknowledgements of such assigmnent from its respective counterparties (including but not limited to under any Drilling Contract), and the Bond Trustee shall be authorized to issue a quiet enjoyment letter on customary terms and reasonably acceptable to the Bond Trustee if so required by the Client under the Drilling Contract.
"Assignment of Insurances" means the assigmnent of any relevant insurances related to the Drillship and the equipment related thereto.
"Assignment of Management Agreements" means the assignment of all earnings and rights of the Issuer under the Management Agreements. The assignment shall include a declaration of subordination of amounts payable by the Issuer to the claims of the Bondholders (to the effect that payments to the Drillship Manager and the Project Manager may only be made while there is no Default), except for (a) unpaid management fees constituting three months or less of management services (but due with no more than two months) under the Project Management Agreement, and (b) unpaid fees and reimbursements costs constituting two months or less of management services (but due with no more than two months) under the Drillship Management Agreement. The assignment shall also include direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights in case of a Default, and the Issuer shall give notice and obtain consent and acknowledgment from the Drillship Manager and the Project Manager.
"Assignment of Receivables" means an assignment by way of security from the Issuer of all present and future intra-group receivables, including a right for the Bond Trustee to release those receivables on enforcement, and the Issuer shall give notices and obtain acknowledgements of such assignment from its respective counterparties.
"Attachment" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Register, from time to time.
