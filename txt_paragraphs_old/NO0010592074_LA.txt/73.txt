(e) Disposal o f business
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets (including but not limited to the Drillship) or operations (in connection with a sale to a third party or an internal reorganization), unless the Bonds are redeemed in full in accordance with 10,4 (Mandatoiy Redemption).

Subsidiary
(i) The Issuer shall maintain 100 % direct ownership and voting rights of any Issuer Subsidiary.
(ii) The Issuer shall procure that any Issuer Subsidiaiy is performing all its obligations under the Project Documents and Security Documents to which it is a party.
(g) Inter-group transactions
All transactions between any companies within the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Acts 1997.
