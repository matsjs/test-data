(h) Transactions with shareholders, directors or affiliated companies
The Issuer shall not engage directly or indirectly in any transaction between the Issuer and (i) any direct or indirect shareholder thereof, (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business, and on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Issuer or any other company within the Group and/or the Issuer Group than would have prevailed in an arms' length transaction with a third party.
