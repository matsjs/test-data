3.
THE BONDHOLDERS
3.1
Bond Terms binding on all Bondholders
(a) By virtue o f being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the CSD, the Bondholders are bound by these Bond Terms and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with by the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders, the Issuer or any other party.
(b) The Bond Trustee is always acting with binding effect on behalf of all the Bondholders.
