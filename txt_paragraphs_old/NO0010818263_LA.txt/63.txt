13.10
Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), except acquisitions of kindergartens, institutions related to care services, licences to operate kindergartens or institutions related to care services, properties on which a kindergarten or an institution related to care services is operated, and such other acquisitions which are carried out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.11
Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to a Group Company), unless such sale, transfer or disposal is carried out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
