Tax Redemption
The notes are redeemable at the option of the company in whole, but not in part, at any time at the principal amounts thereof plus accrued and unpaid interest in the event of certain tax law changes requiring the payment of additional amounts.

Denomination
The notes are in denominations of $1,000 and integral multiples of $1,000 in excess thereof.
