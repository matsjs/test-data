13.5 Preservation o f equity and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall not within a calendar year declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares, any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity (but always distributions of bonus shares not affecting its equity) that constitute more than 40 per cent of (on a consolidated basis) the Issuer's net profit after write downs and taxes for the previous financial year.
