3. Use of Proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Bonds, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Bonds, for refinancing the Refinancing Debt and for general corporate purposes, including distributions up to a maximum aggregate amount of EUR 1,250,000.
