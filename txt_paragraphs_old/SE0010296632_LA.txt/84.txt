A matter decided at a duly convened and held Bondholders' Meeting or by way
of Written Procedure is binding on all Bondholders, irrespective of them being
present or represented at the Bondholders' Meeting or responding in the
Written Procedure. The Bondholders that have not adopted or voted for a
decision shall not be liable for any damages that this may cause other
Bondholders.
(m) All costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer or the Agent for the purpose of convening a Bondholders' Meeting or for the purpose of carrying out a Written Procedure, including reasonable fees to the Agent, shall be paid by the Issuer.
