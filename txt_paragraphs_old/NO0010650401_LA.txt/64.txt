Financial arrangements
13,6 Vessel Covenants (a) Maintenance
(i) The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of the Vessels and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times, hereunder to retain the Vessels in class.
(ii) During operation of the Vessels, the Issuer shall ensure that the Vessels Managers runs proper maintenance o f the Vessels according to a pre-agreed plamted maintenance system,
(b) Insurance
(i) The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory insurance of the Vessels and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times.
(ii) The Vessels shall be adequately insured (including war risk) against (i) Hull & Machinery risks, (ii) a third party liability insurance as per industry standards; and (iii) any additional insurance required under any other contracts,
(iii) The insurances and Loss Payee Clause shall be in accordance with the `Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan, latest version as amended from time to time or other insurances with at least similar terms.
