18.7 Dispute resolution and legal venue
18.7.2 The Finance Documents (not including the Bond Agreement) and all disputes arising out of, or in connection with the Finance Documents (not including the Bond Agreement) between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and the Issuer, shall be governed by the appropriate law.
18.7.3 All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Bond Agreement between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and the Issuer, shall be exclusively resolved by the courts of Norway, with the District Court o f Oslo as sole legal venue.
18.7.4 All disputes arising out of, or in connection with the Finance Documents (not including the Bond Agreement) between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholder's and the Issuer, shall be exclusively resolved by the appropriate courts.
