13.5 Preservation of equity, Security Interests and Financial Covenants
(a)
Capital distribution
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payments, repurchase of shares or make other capital distributions to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind including without limitation any total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party.
(b) Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Subsidiary shall, incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than:
(i) any Financial Indebtedness arising under' the Bond Issue and the Existing Bonds;
