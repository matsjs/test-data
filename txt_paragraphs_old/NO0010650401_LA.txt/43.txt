9. INTEREST
9.1.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value of the Bonds from, and including, the relevant Settlement Date at a fixed rate of 20.00 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.1.2 Interest shall be compounded on a quarterly basis and payable annually in an ear on the Interest Payment Date.
9.1.3 The day count fraction in respect of the calculation of the payable interest amount shall be "30/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in respect o f which payment is being made divided by 360 (the number of days to be calculated on the basis o f a year of 360 days with twelve 30-days months, unless (i) the last day of the calculation period is the 31st day of a month but the first day o f the calculation period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or (ii) the last day o f the calculation period is the last day of the month o f February, in which case the month o f February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month).
