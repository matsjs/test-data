10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS, CALL OPTION AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall be repaid by the Issuer in full together with accrued unpaid Interest at the Final Maturity Date. Repayments will be at 100 % of par value plus accrued and unpaid interest on the redeemed amount.
