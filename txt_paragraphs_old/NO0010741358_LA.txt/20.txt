All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for later Tap Issues. 2.3 2.3.1
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds (net of legal costs, fees of the Managers and the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses) shall be used towards:
(a) repayment of the 2010 Bond Issue; and
(b) the general corporate purposes of the Group.
2.3.2 The net proceeds of any subsequent Tap Issue shall be used towards the general corporate purposes of the Group.
