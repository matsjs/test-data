(k) Account Payments
(i) The Issuer shall from and including the Interest Payment Date falling three years after the Settlement Date and on a monthly basis thereafter until 1 month before the Maturity Date, transfer an amount equal to 1/12 of the annual interest under the Bond Issue to the Debt Service Reserve Account.
(ii) All payments shall be deposited on the Debt Service Reserve Account on the calculation date above. The deposited amounts shall be released for interest payments as they fall due.
13.4 Corporate matters (a) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(b) Transactions with shareholders, directors or affiliated companies
The Issuer shall cause all transactions between the Issuer and (i) any direct or indirect shareholder thereof , (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) or any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, to be entered into in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business, and on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Group Company than would have prevailed in an arms' length transaction with a third party.
