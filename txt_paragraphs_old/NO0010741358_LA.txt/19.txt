The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the aggregate amount of the equivalent of NOK 400,000,000, excluding any additional Bonds issued pursuant to Clause 9.2. The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 213,751,200.
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 1 each. The Bonds will rank pari passu between each other.
The Bond Issue will be described as "8.5 per cent. NBT AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2015/2020".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue is N00010741358.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date up to and including the Final Maturity Date.
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account on the Issue Date.
