(f) Dividend restrictions
The Issuer shall not pay or declare any dividend, reduction of share capital, repurchase of shares (including any transaction with a similar effect, i.a. total return swap) or declare or make any other distribution to its shareholders without the prior written consent of the Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
14. 14.1.1
FEES AND EXPENSES
The Issuer shall cover all its own expenses in connection with this Bond Agreement and fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement, including preparation of this Bond Agreement, preparation of the Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto, listing of the Bonds on the Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository.
14.1.2
The expenses and fees payable to the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent, as the case may be) shall be paid by the Issuer and are set forth in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee. Fees and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee which, due to the Issuer's insolvency or similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the payments to the Bondholders.
