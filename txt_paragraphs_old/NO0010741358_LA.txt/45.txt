Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring the Issuer shall, redeem 100 % of the Outstanding Bonds according to the terms of the Call Option.
10.4.2 If the Bonds are redeemed according to this clause 10.4, the entire amount on the Debt Service Reserve Account may be used as partial payment for mandatory redemption.
10.4.3
The Bond Trustee and the Bondholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory redemption, at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory redemption. On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued.
10.4.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
