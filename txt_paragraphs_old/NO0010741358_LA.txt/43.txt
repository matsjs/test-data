Call Option
The Issuer may redeem all or parts of the Bond Issue at any time up to but not including the Final Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 % of par value plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount.
10. 2.2
The Issuer shall notify the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (through the Bond Trustee) in writing about any exercising of the Call Option at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of such Call Option exercise. Partial redemption shall be made pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures in the Security Register).
10.2.3 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay the aggregate principal amount of all Bonds to be redeemed and any unpaid interest accrued on such Bonds.
10.2.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
