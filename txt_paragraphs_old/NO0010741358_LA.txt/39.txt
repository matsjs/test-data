INTEREST
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue
Date at a fixed rate of 8.50 per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2
Interest on the Outstanding Bonds shall start to accrue from and including the Issue Date,
and interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year.
Until and including the Interest Payment Date falling three years after the Issue Date,
interest will be paid by way of issuing additional Bonds (Payment-In-Kind). From and
including the Interest Payment Date in December 2018 interest shall be paid by cash.
