(b)
The Issuer shall maintain class, flag, name and registry of the Vessels. The Issuer may reflag the Vessels to any generally recognized first class flag state, provided that such reflagging does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
(c)
Maintenance of condition
The Issuer shall ensure that the Vessels are kept in a good and safe condition and that necessary repairs are carried out consistent with prudent ownership and industry standards.
(d)
Operation in accordance with applicable laws and regulations
The Issuer shall ensure that the Vessels at all times are operated in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.
