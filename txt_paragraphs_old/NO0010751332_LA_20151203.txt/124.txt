13.7 Vessel covenants
Maintenance of insurances
The Issuer shall provide for reasonable and satisfactory maintenance of insurances for the Vessels and all relevant equipment related thereto at all times, hereunder to retain the Vessels in class. During operation of the Vessels, the Issuer shall ensure that it runs proper maintenance of the Vessels according to planned maintenance system. The Vessels shall also be adequately insured (including war risk) against risks related to hull & machinery and hull & freight
