16.4 Repeated Bondholders' Meeting
If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause 16.3.4, a repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the same matters. The attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders' Meeting shall be specified in the summons for the repeated Bondholders' Meeting.
16.4.2
The procedures and resolutions as set out in 16.2 and 16.3 above also apply for a repeated Bondholders' meeting, however, a valid resolution may be passed at a repeated Bondholders' Meeting even though less than 20% of the Voting Bonds are represented.

The Bond Trustee
17.1 Appointment of the Bond Trustee
17.1.1
The Issuer appoints the Bond Trustee to act as Bond Trustee of the Bondholders pursuant to Chapter 2.a. of the Danish Securities Trading Act. The Bond Trustee accepts such appointment. By acquiring Bonds, each Bondholder confirms such appointment and authorisation for the Bond Trustee to act on its behalf.
