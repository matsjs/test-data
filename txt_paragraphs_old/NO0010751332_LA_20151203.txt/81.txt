8.5
The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest, costs and expenses, shall be
secured by the Security Interests, but will receive proceeds from the
enforcement (including distressed dispasals pursuant to the terms of the
Intercreditor Agreement) of the Security only after the Super Senior Obligations
have been repaid in full. The Security Interests shall rank on a first priority basis.
