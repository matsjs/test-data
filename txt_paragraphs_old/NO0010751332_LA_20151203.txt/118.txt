13.4.3 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will, incur any Financial Indebtedness except for Financial Indebtedness that constitutes Permitted Debt.

The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year (commencing in 2016) there shall be a period of three (3) consecutive days during which the amount outstanding under the Super Senior Working Capital Facility (excluding any guarantees issued thereunder), less cash and cash equivalents of the Group, amounts to zero (O) or less. Not less than six (6) months shall elapse between two such periods.
