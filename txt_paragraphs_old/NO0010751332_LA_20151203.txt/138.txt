15.12
In the event that the Bond Trustee pursuant to the terms of Clauses 15.10 or 15.11 declares the Outstanding Bonds to be in default and due for payment, the Bond Trustee shall immediately deliver to the Issuer a notice demanding payment of interest and principal due to the Bondholders under the Outstanding Bonds including accrued interest and interest on overdue amounts and expenses. The claim derived from the Outstanding Bonds due for payment as a result of an Event of Default shall be calculated at the prices set out in Clause 10.2.1, and for the non-call period until the First Call Date be the price set out in Clause 10.2.1(b).
