13.5.2 Continuation of business
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall not cease to carry on its business, and that no substantial change is made to the general nature or scope of the business of the Guarantor from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
13.5.3 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall ensure that no Guarantor shall enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms.
13.6 Financial Covenant - Subordinated Capital Ratio
The Issuer shall at all times maintain a Subordinated Capital Ratio of minimum 32% in the first 12 months from the Issue Date and increase with 1 % each subsequent year, to be tested quarterly on the basis of the Interim Accounts for such period and reported by delivery of a Compliance Certificate to the Bond Trustee no later than 60 calendar days following the last day of the relevant financial quarter.
