"Regulated Market" means any regulated market (as defined in Directive 2004/39/EC on markets in financial instruments).
"Remaining Amount" has the meaning set forth in Clause 12.7 {Disposals o f assets).
"Restricted Payment" has the meaning set forth in Clause 12.1 {Distributions).
"Securities Account" means the account for dematerialised securities maintained by the CSD pursuant to the Financial Instruments Accounts Act in which (a) an owner of such security is directly registered or (b) an owner's holding of securities is registered in the name of a nominee.
"Security Documents" means the Share Pledge Agreements, together with any other documents delivered to the Agent under the terms of the Share Pledge Agreements or requested by the Agent in relation to the perfection of the security thereunder.
"SEK" means the lawful currency of Sweden.
"Shareholder Loans" means any shareholder loans of the Issuer or any of its Subsidiaries, where the Issuer or the relevant Subsidiary is the debtor, if such shareholder loans (a) are provided by any direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, (b) according to its terms are subordinated to the obligations of the Issuer under these Terms and Conditions, (c) according to its terms can be repaid only after the payment obligations under these Terms and Conditions have been discharged and (d) according to its terms yield payment-in-kind interest or prescribes that interest can only be paid after the Issuer's payment obligations under these Terms and Conditions have been discharged.
"Share Pledge Agreements" means each of the share pledge agreements entered into between the Issuer on the one hand and the Agent (on behalf of the Holders) on the other hand on or about the Issue Date or at a later date (as applicable) regarding a first priority pledge over all shares in the Pledged Group Companies.
"Subsequent Bond Issue" has the meaning set forth in Clause 2.2.
