WRITTEN PROCEDURE
19.1
The Agent shall instigate a Written Procedure no later than five (5) Banking Days after
receipt of a request from the Issuer or the Holder(s) (or such later date as may be necessary
for technical or administrative reasons) by sending a communication to each such person
who is registered as a Holder on the Banking Day prior to the date on which the
communication is sent.
