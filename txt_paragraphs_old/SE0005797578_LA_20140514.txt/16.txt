(f) incurred as a result of any Group Company acquiring another entity which has indebtedness, provided that such indebtedness shall be cleaned-up within thirty (30) calendar days;
(g) incurred in the ordinary course of business under Advance Purchase Agreements;
(h) which constitutes Existing Bonds, until the prepayment of such Existing Bonds in accordance with the Purpose of the Bond Issue; or
(i) not permitted by (a)-(h) above, in an aggregate maximum amount not, at any time, exceeding SEK 400,000,000, provided that no more than SEK 100,000,000 may relate to Financial Indebtedness (including Financial Leases) other than Working Capital Indebtedness.
