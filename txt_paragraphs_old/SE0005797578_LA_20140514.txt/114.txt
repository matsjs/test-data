27.
LISTING
The Issuer intends to list the Bonds within thirty (30) calendar days after the Issue Date on the corporate bond list of NASDAQ OMX Stockholm (or any other Regulated Market, as applicable). The Issuer has undertaken to list the Bonds on the corporate bond list of NASDAQ OMX Stockholm (or any other Regulated Market, as applicable) within sixty (60) calendar days after the Issue Date and to maintain such listing for as long as any Bond remains outstanding in accordance with Clause 12.6 {Listing o fBonds).
