Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the termination of
Documents constitute escrow funds (Sw. redovisningsmedel) according to the Escrow Funds
Act (Sw. lag (1944:181) om redovisningsmedel) and must be held on a separate interest-
bearing account on behalf of the Holders and the other interested parties. The Agent shall
arrange for payments of such funds in accordance with this Clause 16 as soon as reasonably
practicable.
