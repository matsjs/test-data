"Material Group Company" means the Issuer and any other Group Company representing more than 5.00 per cent, of either (a) the Total Assets or (b) the EBITDA of the Group on a consolidated basis according to the latest Financial Report.
"Net Finance Charges" means, for the Relevant Period, the Finance Charges according to the latest Financial Report(s), after deducting any interest payable for that Relevant Period to any member of the Group and any interest income relating to cash or cash equivalent investment (and excluding any interest capitalised on Shareholder Loans or other subordinated loans subject to Intercreditor Agreements).
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means, in accordance with Original IFRS, the aggregate interest bearing debt less cash and cash equivalents of the Group per the relevant testing date, excluding any:
(a) leases treated as operating leases under Original IFRS;
(b) Shareholder Loans; and
(c) interest bearing debt owed to any Group Company.
"Net Proceeds" means the cash proceeds from the Bond Issue after deduction has been made for the transaction costs payable by the Issuer to the Joint Bookrunners and Issuing Agent for their services provided in relation to the placement and issuance of the Bonds.
"Nobina BusCo" means Nobina BusCo AB (reg. no. 556583-0527, Armégatan 38, SE171 04 Solna, Sweden) and all of its Subsidiaries from time to time.
"Nobina Fleet Denmark" means Nobina Fleet Danmark ApS (reg. no. 31586429), as of the Issue Date, an indirect Subsidiary of the Issuer's indirect shareholder Nobina AB (publ).
