12.3
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future), provided however that the Group Companies have a right to retain, provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
12.4
Clean down period
The Issuer shall procure that during each calendar year, there shall be a period of five (5) consecutive calendar days during which the Working Capital Indebtedness (excluding Cash Pool Indebtedness in an aggregate maximum amount of SEK 50,000,000) or any amount outstanding thereunder shall amount to zero. Not less than three (3) months shall elapse between two clean down periods.
12.5
Loans out etcetera
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, except for Permitted Loans, be the creditor of any Financial Indebtedness (other than Financial Indebtedness owed to the Issuer or to a wholly owned Group Company) or guarantee the obligations of any persons outside of the Group.
