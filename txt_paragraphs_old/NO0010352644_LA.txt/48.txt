13 Covenants
(a) immediately inform the Loan Trustee of any Event of Default as well as o f any circumstances which the Borrower understands or should understand may lead to an Event o f Default,
(b) o f its own accord, make annual and semi-annual reports available on the Borrower's website (alternatively by sending them to the Loan Trustee for public distribution) as soon they are available, and not later than 100 days alter the end o f the financial year and not later than 60 days after the end o f the relevant interim period,

(f) within a reasonable time limit provide information about the Borrower's financial condition as the Loan Trustee may reasonably request in writing,
(g) without undue delay notify the Bondholders (via Securities Depository), the Loan Trustee and the Exchange (if listed) if a Change o f Control Event takes place,
