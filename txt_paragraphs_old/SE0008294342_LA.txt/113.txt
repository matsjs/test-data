TIME-BAR
The right to receive repayment of the principal of the Bonds shall be time-barred and
become void ten (10) years from the relevant Redemption Date. The right to receive
payment of Interest (excluding any capitalised Interest) shall be time-barred and become
void three (3) years from the relevant due date for payment. The Issuer is entitled to any
funds set aside for payments in respect of which the Holders' right to receive payment has
been time-barred and has become void.
