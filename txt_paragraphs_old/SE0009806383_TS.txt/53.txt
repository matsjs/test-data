11.1 Financial covenants:
(a) the Loan to Value in the Group does not exceed 75 per cent.; and
(b) the Equity Ratio of the Group is not less than 20 per cent.
11.1.2 At all times when the Interest Coverage Ratio is less than 1.3:1 the Issuer shall hold the Security Amount on a bank account in the Issuer's name (the "Bank Account") which shall be pledged in favour of the Agent (acting on behalf of itself and the Bondholders).
11.1.3 The Loan to Value and the Equity Ratio shall be tested quarterly on each Reference Date and the Interest Coverage Ratio shall be tested quarterly for the preceding 12 months ending on each Reference Date, and shall be included in the Compliance Certificate delivered in connection therewith. The first test date for the Loan to Value ratio, the Interest Coverage Ratio and the Equity Ratio shall be 30 September 2017, and with respect to the Interest Coverage Ratio, the calculation period shall for the first test date be the period from the Issue Date until 30 September 2017.
11.1.4 If the Interest Coverage Ratio set out in a Compliance Certificate is below 1.3:1 and the Security Amount is not held in the Bank Account, the Issuer shall ensure that the Security Amount is transferred to the Bank Account within twenty (20) Business Days.

11.2 Listing of Bonds
The Issuer shall ensure (i) that the Bonds are listed on the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Stockholm within sixty (60) days after the Issue Date (and with an intention to complete such listing within thirty (30) days after the Issue Date), and that it remains admitted or, if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on another Regulated Market. Following an admission to trading, the Issuer shall take all actions on its part to maintain the admission as long as any Bonds are outstanding, but not longer than up to and including the last day on which the admission to trading reasonably can, pursuant to the then applicable regulations of the Regulated Market and the CSD, subsist.
