15.5 The following matters shall require consent of Bondholders representing at least three quarters (3/4) of the Adjusted Nominal Amount for which Bondholders are voting at a Bondholders' Meeting or for which Bondholders reply in a Written Procedure in accordance with the instructions given pursuant to Clause 17.3:
(a) waive a breach of or amend an undertaking set out in Clause 11 (Special undertakings);
(b) reduce the principal amount, Interest Rate or Interest which shall be paid by the Issuer;
(c) amend any payment day for principal or Interest or waive any breach of a payment undertaking; or
(d) amend the provisions in this Clause 15.5 and Clause 15.6.
15.6 Any matter not covered by Clause 15.5 shall require the consent of Bondholders representing more than 50 per cent, of the Adjusted Nominal Amount for which Bondholders are voting at a Bondholders' Meeting or for which Bondholders reply in a Written Procedure in accordance with the instructions given pursuant to Clause 17.3. This includes, but is not limited to, any amendment to or waiver of these Terms and Conditions that does not require a higher majority (other than an amendment or waiver permitted pursuant to Clause 18.1 (a), (b) or (c)), a termination of the Bonds.
