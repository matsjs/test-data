11.7 Disposals of assets
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of shares in any Subsidiary or of all or substantially all of its or that Subsidiary's assets, or operations to any person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries, unless the transaction is carried out on market terms and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction.
11.8 Dividends
The Issuer may, during each financial year, pay dividend up to a portion of 50 per cent, of the Group's operating profits less tax for the previous financial year as derived from its audited annual report, provided that the Incurrence Test is met. Notwithstanding what is stated in this clause, the Issuer is not restricted to pay dividend in respect of preference shares.
