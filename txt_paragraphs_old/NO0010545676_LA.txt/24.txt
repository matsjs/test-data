Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at 103 % par by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from Disbursement Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in 2010 at 101 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) at any time from Interest Payment Date in 2010 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in 2011 at 102 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(iii) at any time from Interest Payment Date in 2011 to and including the Maturity Date at 103 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
