13.5.2
Negative Pledge The Issuer shall procure that no new Encumbrances are created, incurred or permitted to subsist on any asset of the Issuer or any Group Company, except for an aggregate amount of, on a consolidated basis, USD 2,500,000 unless otherwise provided for in this Bond Agreement.
13.5.3
Debt restriction The Issuer shall ensure that (i) the Group shall not (on a consolidated basis) incur more than maximum NOK 100 million in additional financial indebtedness (the "Permitted Additional Indebtedness"), and (ii) any such the Permitted Additional Indebtedness shall not mature on or before the Maturity Date. Short-term liabilities incurred under the ordinary course of business shall not be considered as "additional financial indebtedness" in connection with this sub-clause.
14
Fees and expenses
14.1 The Issuer shall cover all its own expenses in connection with this Bond Agreement and fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement, including preparation of this Bond Agreement, preparation o f the Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto, listing of the Bonds on the Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Register.
14.2 The expenses and fees payable to the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent, as the case may be) shall be paid by the Issuer and are set forth in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee. Fees and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee which, due to Issuer's insolvency or similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the payments to the Bondholders.
