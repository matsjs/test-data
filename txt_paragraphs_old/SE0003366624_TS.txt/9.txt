3
Interest
The Bonds will bear interest at the Interest Rate from, but excluding, the Issue Date up to and including the Maturity Date (or any earlier Redemption Date). Interest shall be paid annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates, commencing on the first Interest Payment Date falling after the Issue Date. Interest shall be calculated on a 30E/360-days basis.
