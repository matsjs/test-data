The redemption price shall depend on when the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurs, and shall be as follows:
(i) if occurring at any time from and including the Issue Date until (but not including) 29 September 2011, at 110.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) if occurring at any time from and including 29 September 2011 until (but not including) 29 December 2011, at 105.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(iii) if occurring any time thereafter, at 102.50 % o f par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
provided, however, that if the Mandatory Prepayment Event is due to a total loss of the Rig, the Issuer shall redeem 100% o f the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par (plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount) immediately upon receipt of the insurance proceeds from that loss.
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Mandatory Prepayment clause, the entire amount on the Escrow Account may be used as partly payment for Mandatory Prepayment.
