(d) Permitted debt The Issuer shall not incur financial indebtedness, in addition to the Bonds, except for the Permitted Debt and any guarantee liability incurred in the ordinary course of business (including from guarantees relating to employment of the Rig).
(e) Permission to other security The Issuer shall not create or permit to subsist any security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for (i) any security contemplated by this Bond Agreement, or (ii) any security arising by operation of law or in the ordinary course of business customary (including collateral in connection with credit purchases o f goods and services), (iii) the security rights granted to the Yard under the Construction Contract with respect to the Rig and equipment, to expire upon the full payment of the Final Installment or (iv) security rights for the Permitted Debt.
(f) Insurance o f the Rig
(i) The Issuer shall procure that the Rig following delivery is adequately insured against such risks (including, but not limited to, hull and machinery, hull interest, freight interest, protection & indemnity (including a maximum club cover for oil pollution liability for the Rig) and war risk (including terrorism)), in such amounts and on terms in accordance with the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan of 1996, version 2003 (as amended from time to time) or such other terms as the Bond Trustee may approve (such approval not to be unreasonably withheld) in relation to losses payable there under) and with such insurance brokers and insurers, as is industry standard or as required by law or drilling contracts.
