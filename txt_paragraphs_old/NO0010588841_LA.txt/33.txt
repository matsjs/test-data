10.2 Call Option 10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including 29 September 2011 until (but not including) 29 December 2011 at 105.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) any time thereafter at 102.50 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.4
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
