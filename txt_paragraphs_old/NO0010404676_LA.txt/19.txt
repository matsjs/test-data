10.1 The Loan will run without installments and mature in whole on the Maturity Date at par (100%).
Should the Borrower exercise its Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed of this (the Bondholders in writing via VPS) no later than 30 - thirty - Banking Days before the date of redemption.
Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
10.3 If a Change of Control Event takes place, the Borrower shall immediately inform the Bondholder in writing via VPS and the Bondholders shall have the right to exercise either (a) or (b) below:
(a) (i) for the period from the Disbursement Date to, but not including the interest Payment Date in July 2008, each Bondholder may have some or all of his Bonds redeemed by the Borrower (put option) at 150 % of their principal amount together with accrued interest; and (ii) for the period from and including the interest Payment Date in July 2008 until (but not included) the Maturity Date have the Bonds redeemed by the Borrower at 101 % of par together with accrued interest, ((i) and (ii) described as the "Put Option")
The Put Option must be exercised within one month after the Borrower has given notification that a Change of Control Event has occurred (h).
The Put Option may be exercised within the Exercise Period by the Bondholders by giving written notice to the Paying Agent via its Account Manager. The put date shall be 10 Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the pre-payment request.
