2. The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a bond loan in the amount of USD 20,000,000 (US dollar twenty million), herein after called the "Loan" :
The Bonds has denominations of USD 100,000. All the Bonds shall rank pari passu among themselves.
The Loan will be described as "6.00 per cent Malka Oil AB Senior Convertible Bond Issue 2008/2011" .
Issue price of the Loan is 95 per cent of par value.
The international securities identification number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 040467.6.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date, or earlier if all of the Bonds are redeemed prior to the Maturity Date following (i) the Bondholders' exercise of their Conversion Right, (ii) the Borrower's exercise of its call option, and/or (iii) otherwise pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth herein.
