9. Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 6.00 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made quarterly on 10 January, 10 April, 10 July, and 10 October each year, the first interest Payment Date being 10 April 2008.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless:
(a) the last day of the period is the 31st day of a month but the first day of the period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or
(b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
10. Maturity of the Loan and prepayment
10.1 The Loan will run without installments and mature in whole on the Maturity Date at par (100%).
10.2 The Borrower may redeem parts of the Loan or the entire Loan any time from Disbursement Date to and included interest Payment Date in January 2009 at 150 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount (Call Option).
