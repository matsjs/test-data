14.7 Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company, create or allow to subsist any Security over any of its assets, other than any Permitted Security.
14.8 Listing
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that:
(a) the Initial Bonds are listed on the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Stockholm or, if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on another Regulated Market, not later than five months after the First Issue Date;
