14.9 Change of control of the Issuer
The Issuer ceases to be wholly-owned by the Originator.
14.10 Taxes
(a) The Swedish Tax Authority requests payment from the Issuer of value added tax payable with respect to a VAT group that includes the Issuer; and
(b) the Originator does not pay or compensate the Issuer for taxes imposed on the Issuer as set out in Clause 13.15 (Taxes).
14.11 Creditors' Process
Any expropriation, attachment or any analogous process in any jurisdiction affects any asset or assets of the Issuer having an aggregate value of an amount equal to or exceeding SEK 3,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency) and is not discharged within thirty (30) days.
