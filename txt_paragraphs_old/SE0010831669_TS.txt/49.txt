13.15 Taxes
13.16 Bank Accounts
(a) The Issuer shall maintain the following bank Accounts in the name of the Issuer:
(i) Escrow Account;
(ii) Collection Account;
(iii) Interest Reserve Account;
(iv) Excess Liquidity Account; and
(v) Cost Account.
(b) Subject to paragraphs (c) and (d) below, each Account must be held with the Account Bank.
