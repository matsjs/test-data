9. Redemption and Repurchase of the Bonds
9.1 Redemption at maturity
The Issuer shall redeem all, but not some only, of the outstanding Bonds in full on the Final Redemption Date with an amount per Bond equal to the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest. If the Final Redemption Date is not a Business Day, then the redemption shall occur on the first following Business Day.
9.2 Issuer's purchase of Bonds
The Issuer may, subject to applicablelaw, at any time purchase Bonds in accordance with paragraphs (a) and (b) of Clause 4.4 (Excess Liquidity Account). The Issuer may not cancel repurchased Bonds.
