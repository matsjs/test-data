Publication of Finance Documents
The latest version of these Terms and Conditions (including any document amending these Terms and Conditions) shall be available on the website of the Group and the Agent.
The latest versions of the Finance Documents shall be available to the Noteholders at the office of the Agent during normal business hours.
Listing of Notes
The Issuer intends to apply for and procure the listing o f the Notes on NASDAQ OMX Stockholm within sixty (60) days from the Issue Date.
Absence of acceleration event
The Notes are perpetual subordinated and unsecured and the Issuer has no obligation to redeem or prepay the Notes.
The Noteholders have no right to accelerate the Notes or otherwise request prepayment or redemption of the principal amount o f the Notes, except upon a Bankruptcy Event.
