17.3
Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the acceleration
o f the Notes constitute escrow funds (Sw. redovisningsmedel) and must be held on a
separate interest-bearing account on behalf o f the Noteholders and the other interested
parties. The Agent shall arrange for payments of such funds in accordance with this
Clause 17 as soon as reasonably practicable.
