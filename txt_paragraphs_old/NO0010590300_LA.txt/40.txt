10.4.2
If the Mandatory Prepayment Event applies to (i) one Vessel or company only, the aggregate face value of Bonds to be redeemed as described in clause 10.4.1 shall be 60 per cent of the Outstanding Bonds, and (ii) both Vessels or companies (either in a single transaction/incident or a series of transactions/incidents), the entire Bond Issue shall be redeemed (the "Redemption Amount").
10.4.3 In the event that the New Equity is not in place as described in clause 6.3.2, all Outstanding Bonds shall be redeemed at a price equal to 101 per cent of par plus accrued interest on the par value of the redeemed amount.
10.4.4 If all Outstanding Bonds are redeemed according to this clause 10.4, the Issuer may apply any amount debited on the Escrow Account and the Bond Retention Account for mandatory prepayment hereunder.
