13 Covenants
13.1 General
13.1.1 The Obligors have undertaken the covenants in this clause 13 to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), as further stated below.
13.1.2
The covenants in this clause 13 shall remain in force from the date of this Bond Agreement and until such time that no amounts are outstanding under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, unless the Bond Trustee (or the Bondholders Meeting, as the case may be), has agreed in writing to waive any covenant, and then only to the extent of such waiver, and on the terms and conditions set forth in such waiver.
13.1.3
The Bond Trustee acknowledges and agrees that the information covenants in clause 13.2 may require that the Bond Trustee in certain cases would have to undertake confidentiality and other obligations in accordance with the Norwegian Securities Trading Act prior to receiving and in respect of information that is subject to delayed disclosure under the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
