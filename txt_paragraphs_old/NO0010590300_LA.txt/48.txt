(d) Continuation o f business The Issuer, the Parent or Polarcus 6 shall not cease to carry out its business, save for Polarcus 6 which may file for a solvent liquidation after the sale of M/V Alima to the Issuer and settlement of any of its obligations.
(e) Disposal o f assets The Issuer, the Parent or Polarcus 6 shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of their respective assets or operations or nature of their respective business other than that Polarcus 6 may sell M/V Alima to the Issuer immediately following the delivery of M/V Alima from the Yard and that the Issuer may sell the shares of Polarcus 6 after the acquisition of M/V Alima (for the avoidance of doubt, please also see clause 10.4 "Mandatory Prepayment"). The Issuer, the Parent or Polarcus 6 shall not carry out any sale or disposal of their respective assets or operations if the sole purpose of same is to trigger a Mandatory Prepayment Event.
Notwithstanding clause 10.4 "Mandatory Prepayment", any sale or disposal of assets between Group Companies shall be subject to the prior written consent of the Bond Trustee (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld) and subject to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) being provided with security equivalent to that held by the Bond Trustee prior to such sale or disposal.
13.2.3 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Intra-group transactions All transactions between any companies in the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
(b) Transactions with shareholders, directors and affiliated companies The Issuer shall procure that all transactions between any Group Company and (i) any shareholder thereof not part of the Group, (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, to be entered on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Group Company than would have prevailed in arms' length transaction with a third party.
