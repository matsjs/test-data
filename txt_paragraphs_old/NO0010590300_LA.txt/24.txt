6.1 Conditions precedent to Bond Issue
Norsk Tillitsmann ASA
6.1.1 Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Issuer will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it, at least two (2) Business Days prior to the Issue Date:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Obligors to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents to which they respectively are a party;
(c) if not included in the resolutions referred to in sub-paragraph (b) above, a power of attorney from each Obligor to relevant individual(s) for his/their execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant register or similar documentation evidencing the individuals' authorization to sign on behalf of the Obligors;
(d) certified copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for each of the Obligors evidencing that it is validly existing and (ii) Articles of Association or by-laws of each of the Obligors;
(e) evidence that the Parent owns and controls 100 per cent of the shares and voting rights in the Issuer, that Polarcus UK Ltd owns and controls 100 per cent of the shares and voting rights in the Parent and that the Ultimate Parent owns and controls 100 per cent of the shares and voting rights in Polarcus UK Ltd;
(f) the latest Financial Statements and Semi-annual Financial Report of the Ultimate Parent;
