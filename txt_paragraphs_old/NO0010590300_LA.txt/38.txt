10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty (30) days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Securities Register).
10.2.4
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.3 Change of Control - Put Option
10.3.1
If a Change of Control Event has occurred, each Bondholder shall at any time during the Change of Control Period have the option to (at each Bondholder's discretion) request early redemption of its Bonds ("Put Option") at 102 per cent of par value plus accrued interest on the par value of the redeemed amount.
