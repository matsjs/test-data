2.2 The Bonds
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and shall rank pari passu amongst themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "12.5per cent Polarcus Alima AS Senior Secured Bond Issue 2010/2015".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 059030.0
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
