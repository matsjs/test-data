13.2.5 Covenants related to the Vessels
(a) The Issuer, Parent and Polarcus 6 (as applicable) shall:
(i) upon request of the Bond Trustee, arrange for the Bond Trustee and/or any persons appointed by the Bond Trustee, to inspect either of the Vessels at the expense of the Issuer, however, no more than once every 12 months period and without interference to the daily operation of the relevant Vessel;
(ii) procure that any Group Company performs all its obligations under any employment contracts (intra-group charter) for the Vessels to which such Group Company is a party;
(iii) ensure that the relevant Notices of Assignment of Earnings are given to any charterers of the Vessels in respect of charters with a duration of 12 months or more;
(iv) (a) perform and observe all of its covenants and agreements contained in any of the Project Documents to which it is or becomes a party, (b) take all necessary action to prevent the termination of any such Project Documents in accordance with the terms thereof or otherwise other than when such termination takes place by the Parent, the Issuer or Polarcus 6 and alternative equipment is ordered, and (c) take any and all action as may be reasonably necessary promptly to enforce its rights and to collect any and all sums due to it under the Project Documents;
(v) ensure that the Vessels at all times to be registered in a jurisdiction acceptable to the Senior Lenders after consultation with the Bond Trustee;
