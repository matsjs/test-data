13.2.4 Preservation o f equity, Security Interests, Accounts and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer, the Parent and Polarcus 6 shall not make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, service any Subordinated Loans, or make any loans or other distribution to its shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party), except for (i) a maximum amount of USD 5 million (in aggregate for Issuer and Parent) per year in servicing of interest on Subordinated Loans and/or to contribute for operational expenses of the Ultimate Parent, and (ii) from the date falling three years after Issue Date make payment of dividend constituting no more than 35 per cent of net profit after tax for previous fiscal year, such payments always being subject to that the Issuer and the Parent (on a consolidated basis) are in compliance with the following financial covenants after such payment has been made (calculated on the basis of the most recent Financial Statements or Semi-annual Financial Reports):
minimum Equity Ratio being 30 per cent, and;
minimum Book Equity of USD 70 million.
For the avoidance of doubt, payments to members within the Group for services in accordance with the transfer pricing policies established for the Group shall always be allowed, however, always in accordance with applicable law.
