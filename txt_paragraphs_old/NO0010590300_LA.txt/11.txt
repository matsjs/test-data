"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means (a) a sale or disposal of any of the Vessels to a party who is not a Group Company (b) a sale or transfer of all or a portion of the shares in the Parent or the Issuer to a party who is not a Group Company, or (d) Total Loss of any of the Vessels.

"Maturity Date" means 29 October 2015 or an earlier maturity date as provided for in this Bond Agreement. Any further adjustment may be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Mortgage" means the cross collateralized second priority mortgages over the Vessels ranking after the relevant First Security and including all relevant equipment being legally part of the Vessels under applicable law as follows:
(i) In respect of M/V Asima, to be established no later than on the Disbursement Date; and
(ii) In respect of M/V Alima, to be established on or about the Alima Delivery Date.
