(b) Dividends and other distributions The Ultimate Parent shall not:
(i) demand any dividend payments or other distributions or repayments or service of Subordinated Loans from the Parent, the Issuer or Polarcus 6 other than such dividend payments or other distributions which is permitted by the terms and conditions of this Bond Agreement; or
(ii) make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, service any intragroup debt to shareholders or make any other distribution to its shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party).
(c) Ownership to Equipment The Ultimate Parent shall maintain the Parent, the Issuer or Polarcus 6 (as the case may be) as owners of the owner furnished equipment and all other equipment related to each of the Vessels unless such equipment is financed by other companies within the Group than the Parent and the Issuer and such equipment from time to time is leased out to the Vessels in order for these to perform particular seismic survey projects. Such equipment shall then be clearly identified in the leasing agreement between the parties and be clearly marked with the same serial number or other identification number.
(d) Disposal of assets The Ultimate Parent shall not sell or dispose of a substantial part of its assets or operations or change the nature of its business, if that would result in a Material Adverse Effect.
