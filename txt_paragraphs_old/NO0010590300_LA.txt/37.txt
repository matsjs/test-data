10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue (Call Option) at any time from and including:
(i) the Interest Payment Date in October 2012 to, but not including the Interest Payment Date in October 2013 at 106 per cent of par plus accrued interests on the par value of the redeemed amount;
(ii) the Interest Payment date in October 2013 to, but not including the Interest Payment Date in October 2014 at 104 per cent of par plus accrued interests on the par value of the redeemed amount; and
(iii) the Interest Payment Date in October 2014 to, but not including the Maturity Date at 102 per cent of par plus accrued interests on the par value of the redeemed amount.
