13.2 Covenants undertaken by the Issuer, Parent and Polarcus 6
13.2.1 Information Covenants
(a) The Issuer, Polarcus Asima and Polarcus 6 (as applicable) shall:
(i) without being requested to do so, immediately inform the Bond Trustee of any Event of Default as well as of any circumstances which it understands or should understand may lead to an Event of Default;
(ii) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee of any other event known to them which may have a Material Adverse Effect;
(iii) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee if it intends to sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, or change the nature of its business other than Polarcus 6 selling M/V Alima to the Issuer upon delivery of the vessel from the Yard;
(iv) without being requested to do so, produce Financial Statements and Semi-annual Financial Reports (by sending them to the Bond Trustee) as soon as they become available, and not later than 120 days after the end of the financial year and 60 days after the end of the relevant interim period.
