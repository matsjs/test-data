If the interest rate in the market is reduced, the Group will profit from receiving fixed interest and paying floating interest. Market values are calculated by using market rate curves as of 31 December 2002. The Group uses interest rate swaps for interest rate hedging. Unrealised market to market re-valuation are not booked in the Profit & Loss. This is in line with the underlying debt being kept at cost.
