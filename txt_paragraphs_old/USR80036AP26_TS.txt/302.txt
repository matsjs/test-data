4. Pension costs and pension obligations
Norske Skog have various pension schemes. The contributions to the pension schemes are according to local agreements. In total 8 685 persons are covered through pension schemes, of these are 7 119 persons covered by defined benefit plans and 1 566 persons covered through defined contribution plans.
