8 Status of the Loan and security

(i) from the Disbursement Date and thereafter:

(ii) from the first release of funds from the Escrow Account and thereafter:
(a) subject to a letter of quiet enjoyment to Maersk Oil & Gas, the Second Mortgages;
(b) subject to Maersk Oil & Gas, the Second Assignment of Insurance;
(c) the Second Share Pledge; and
(d) the Second Assignment of Earnings.
