Payment Date:
in relation to the Loan the dates specified for payment of interest or principal. If Payment Date is not a Banking Day payments should be made the following Banking Day.
Put Option:
each Bondholder's right to demand an early redemption of its Bonds as set out in Clause 10.2.
Remaining Loan:
the aggregate principal amount of all Bonds outstanding in the Loan less the principal amount of the Bonds redeemed by the Borrower and discharged through the Securities Depository.
