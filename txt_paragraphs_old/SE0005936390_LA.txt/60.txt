14.5
The following matters shall require consent of Holders representing at the following
proportions, respectively, of the Adjusted Nominal Amount for which Holders are voting at
a Holders' Meeting or for which Holders reply in a Written Procedure in accordance with the
instructions given pursuant to Clause 16.3:
(a) two thirds (2/3) to (i) waive a breach of an undertaking set out in Clause 11 (Special undertakings) or (ii), subject to item (b) below, amend a provision in these Terms and Conditions;
(b) three quarters (3/4) to (i) reduce the principal amount, Interest Rate or Interest which shall be paid by the Issuer, (ii) amend any payment day for principal or Interest or waive any breach of a payment undertaking or (iii) amend the provisions in this Clause 14.5.
