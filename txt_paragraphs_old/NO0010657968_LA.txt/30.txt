Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the Face Value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 1.00 per cent, per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 12 March 2013.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, the Issue Date or one Interest Payment Date (as the case may be) to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
