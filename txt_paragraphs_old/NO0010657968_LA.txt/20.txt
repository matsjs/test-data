2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of USD 445,000,000 (U.S. Dollar Fourhundredandfortyfivemillion).
Face Value is USD 200,000. The Bonds rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "1.00 per cent. Siem Industries Inc. USD 445,000,000 Senior Secured Exchangeable Bonds 2012/2019".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 0010657968.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied towards the Issuer's general corporate purposes.
