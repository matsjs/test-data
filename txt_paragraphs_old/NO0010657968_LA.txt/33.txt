Unless previously purchased and cancelled, redeemed, or exchanged pursuant to the provisions set out in Attachment 1, the Bonds shall run without instalments and mature in whole at the Maturity Date at par (100%). The Bonds may be redeemed at the option of the Issuer prior to the Maturity Date only in accordance with Clause 10.2 below and Clauses 10.1 (Redemption at the option of the Issuer) and 10.2 (Redemption for tax reasons) of Attachment 1.
The Issuer may elect to satisfy its obligation to redeem the Bonds by exercising its Share Settlement Option with respect to all, but not some only, of the Bonds as described in Clause 11 (Share Settlement Option) of Attachment 1. To exercise its Share Settlement Option, the Issuer shall give a notice to such effect (the "Share Settlement Option Notice") to the Trustee and to the Bondholders in accordance with Clause 18.5 not less than 35 nor more than 60 days prior to the Maturity Date.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1
The Bonds may be redeemed at the option of the Issuer in whole (but not in part only) at the Face Value, together with accrued and unpaid interest, if exchanges and/or purchases and cancellations and/or redemptions have occurred in respect of 90 per cent, or more of the Bonds originally issued (Call Option), as further set out in Attachment 1.
10.3 Put Option
10.3.1
On 12 September 2016 (the 4th anniversary of the Issue Date) (the "Optional Put Date") each Bondholder shall, for one day only, be entitled to require the Bonds redeemed at the Face Value, together with accrued and unpaid interest (Put Option), as further set out in Attachment 1.
