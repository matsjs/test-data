10 Maturity of the Bonds and Change of Control
10.1 Maturity
10.1.1
Unless previously purchased and cancelled, redeemed, or exchanged pursuant to the provisions set out in Attachment 1, the Bonds shall run without instalments and mature in whole at the Maturity Date at par (100%). The Bonds may be redeemed at the option of the Issuer prior to the Maturity Date only in accordance with Clause 10.2 below and Clauses 10.1 (Redemption at the option of the Issuer) and 10.2 (Redemption for tax reasons) of Attachment 1.
