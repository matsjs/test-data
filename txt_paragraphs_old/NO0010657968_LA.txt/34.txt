10.4 Exchange Right
10.4.1 Unless previously purchased and cancelled, redeemed or exchanged, each Bond may be exchanged, at the option of the holder, into fully paid Subsea Shares at any time during the Exchange Period, as further set out in Attachment 1.
10.5 Relevant Event
10.5.1 If a Relevant Event has occurred, each Bondholder shall for a period of 60 days following the notification of such Relevant Event be entitled, at its option to:
(a) require the Bonds redeemed at the Face Value, together with accrued and unpaid interest (put option); or
(b) exchange its Bonds pursuant to the provisions set out in Attachment 1.
