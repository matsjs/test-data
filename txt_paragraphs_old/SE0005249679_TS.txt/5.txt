"Debt Instruments" means bonds, notes or other debt securities (however defined), which are or are intended to be quoted, listed, traded or otherwise admitted to trading on a Regulated Market.
"EBITDA" means, in respect of the Relevant Period, the consolidated operating profit of the Group:
a) before deducting any interest, commission, fees, discounts, prepayment fees, premiums or charges and other finance payments whether paid, payable or capitalised by any member of the Group (calculated on a consolidated basis) in respect of that Relevant Period;
b) before taxation;
c) before taking into account any exceptional or extraordinary items of an unusual or non-recurring nature in accordance with the Accounting Principles;

d) before taking into account any unrealised gains or losses on any derivative instrument (other than any derivative instrument which is accounted for on a hedge accounting basis);
e) after adding back or deducting, as the case may be, the amount of any loss or gain against book value arising on a disposal of any asset (other than in the ordinary course of trading) and any loss or gain arising from an upward or downward revaluation of any asset; and
f) after adding back any amount attributable to the amortisation, depreciation or impairment of any asset of any Group Company.
