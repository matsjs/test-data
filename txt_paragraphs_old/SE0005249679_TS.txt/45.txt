Financial undertakings
Until and including 30 April 2016, the Issuer shall not declare, make, pay, repay or distribute any dividends on or in respect of its share capital or any similar distribution (including share repurchase) or transfer of value (each a "Restricted Payment") to its shareholders. After 30 April 2016, the Issuer may make, declare, pay, repay or distribute any Restricted Payment to its shareholders, provided that (i) the aggregate amount of any and all Restricted Payments in any fiscal year of the Issuer does not exceed the Distribution Threshold, (ii) the Leverage Test will be met following each Restricted Payment and (iii) the Equity Test will be met following each Restricted Payment.
Calculations in relation to the Leverage Test shall be made taking into account that the ratio of Adjusted Consolidated Net Debt to EBITDA will be measured as per the end date of the latest financial statement of the Issuer, which has been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1, and the EBITDA figure for the Relevant Period will be based upon up to four consolidated financial statements of the Issuer, which have been delivered to the Agent in accordance with Clause 10.1.1.
