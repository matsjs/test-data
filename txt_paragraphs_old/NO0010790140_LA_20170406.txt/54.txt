13.9 Designation of Material Group Companies The Issuer shall ensure that a Group Company whose assets or EBITDA constitutes more than 10 per cent of the Group's total assets on a consolidated basis or the Group's EBITDA on a consolidated basis according to the latest Financial Report, shall be nominated as a Material Group Company.
