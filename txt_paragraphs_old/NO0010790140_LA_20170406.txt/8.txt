"Exchange" means:
(a) Oslo Børs; or

"Existing Debt" means bank and junior debt that is wholly or partially refinanced in connection with the disbursement of Net Proceeds from the Escrow Account in the approximate amount of NOK 710,000,000.
"Extraordinary Distribution" means the repurchase of shares by the Issuer from members of the board of directors and management and employees leaving their position with Group in an aggregate amount that does not exceed NOK 4,000,000 (or its equivalent) in total during the term of the Bonds and does not exceed NOK 2,000,000 (or its equivalent) in any financial year of the Issuer.
