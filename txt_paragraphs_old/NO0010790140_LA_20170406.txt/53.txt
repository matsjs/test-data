13.6 Financial support (a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Material Group Company will, extend any loans, grant any guarantees or extend any other financial assistance other than to Group Companies.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Advance Purchase Agreement.
13.7 Disposals (a) The Issuer shall not sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of the shares in VAG or any interest in any VAG Loan, except for a conversion of any liability owing by VAG to the Issuer under a VAG Loan into shares in VAG, provided that those shares are subject to the Transaction Security immediately upon issue.
(b) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of any shares in any Group Company or of any substantial assets (including but not limited to material intellectual property rights) or operations to any person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Group Companies, unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.8 Related party transactions Without limiting Clause 13.2 (Compliance with laws), the Issuer shall, and shall procure that its Subsidiaries will, conduct all dealings with their direct and indirect shareholders and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders on arm's length terms (other than contributions from the Issuer to wholly owned subsidiaries).
13.9 Designation of Material Group Companies The Issuer shall ensure that a Group Company whose assets or EBITDA constitutes more than 10 per cent of the Group's total assets on a consolidated basis or the Group's EBITDA on a consolidated basis according to the latest Financial Report, shall be nominated as a Material Group Company.
