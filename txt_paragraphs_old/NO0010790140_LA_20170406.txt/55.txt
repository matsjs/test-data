13.11
Distributions
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, (i) pay any dividend on its shares, (ii) repurchase any of its own shares, (iii) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders, (iv) grant any loans, (v) repay any Shareholder Loans or capitalized or accrued interest thereunder, or (vi) make any other similar distribution or transfers of value to the direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, or any Affiliates of the Issuer, in each case other than to the Issuer, a Subsidiary of the Issuer, or minority shareholders in a Subsidiary where the dividend, distribution or other payment attributable to its minority shareholders shall be proportionate to their shareholding (i) to (vi) each being a "Restricted Payment").
(b) Paragraph (a) above shall not prohibit any Group Company to make any Permitted Payment.
