13.16
Shareholders Loans and VAG Loans
(a) If the Issuer incurs any Shareholder Loan after the Issue Date which is not subject to Transaction Security, the Issuer will promptly, and in any event within ten (10) Business Days, ensure that the lender without undue delay enters into a subordination agreement with the Bond Trustee, in form and content satisfactory to the Bond Trustee or accedes to the Intercreditor Agreement.
(b) If the Issuer extend any VAG Loan after the Issue Date to VAG which is not subject to Transaction Security, the Issuer will promptly, and in any event within ten (10) Business Days, grant and deliver to the Bond Trustee Security over such VAG Loan in favour of the Bond Trustee and take all such action as is available to it for the purpose of the creation, perfection and protection or maintenance of any such Security conferred or intended to be conferred on the Bond Trustee.
