13.1 Incurrence Test
The Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) the Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is not greater than:
(i) 4.00x from the First Issue Date until (and including) the date falling 12 months of the First Issue Date;
(ii) 3.75x from the date falling 12 months of the First Issue Date until (and including) the date falling 24 months of the First Issue Date;
(iii) 3.50x from the date falling 24 months of the First Issue Date until (and including) the date falling 36 months of the First Issue Date;
(iv) 3.25x from the date falling 36 months of the First Issue Date until (and including) the Final Maturity Date; and
