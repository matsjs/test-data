3. Use of Proceeds
(a) The Issuer shall use the Net Proceeds from the issue of the Initial Bonds, towards financing (i) the acquisition of the Target, and (ii) general corporate purposes.
(b) The Issuer shall use the Net Proceeds from the issue of any Subsequent Bonds, towards financing (i) general corporate purposes including acquisitions and investments, and/or (ii) a Vendor Note Repayment.
