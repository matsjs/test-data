"Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement which will, if requested by the Issuer, be entered into between, inter alios, the Issuer, the Guarantor, the Bonds Agent (as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement), the Security Agent, the Vendors and any Super Senior RCF Creditor (as defined in the Intercreditor Agreement).
