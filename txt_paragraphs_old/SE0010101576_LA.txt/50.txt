15.6 Mergers and Demergers
A decision is made that any Group Company shall be demerged or merged if such merger or demerger is likely to have a Material Adverse Effect, provided that:
(a) a merger subject to existing security between Subsidiaries shall not be an Event of Default; and
(b) a merger involving the Issuer, where the Issuer is not the surviving entity, shall always be considered an Event of Default; and
(c) the Issuer may not be demerged.
