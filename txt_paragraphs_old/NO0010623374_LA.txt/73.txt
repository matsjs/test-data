B.4 In order to exercise a Conversion Right, the Bondholder shall deliver to the Paying Agent (via its Account Manager) a duly completed, irrevocable and signed exercise notice.
Request for conversion takes place by the Bondholder notifying his Account Manager o f the number o f Bonds which shall be converted, The Account Manager shall then promptly forward the request to the Paying Agent. The Paying Agent shall cany the conversion into effect at the Conversion Date.
Conversion will be effected by a set-off o f the total nominal value o f the Bonds including accrued unpaid interest to be converted against the issuing o f the whole number o f shares resulting from dividing the total nominal value of the Bonds to be converted by the Conversion Price. Any excess amount beyond the whole number o f shares converted by the Bonds shall fall to the Issuer.
