"Conversion Event" means the discretionary decision o f a Bondholder to convert the Bonds into Shares.
"Conversion Date" means a date being ten (10) Banking Days after the Bondholder has given a notice o f a Conversion Event.
Conversion Period" means the thirty (30) calendar days period following the notification o f a Conversion Event.
"Costs" means all costs, expenses, disbursements, payments, charges, losses, demands, claims, liabilities, penalties, fines, damages, judgments, orders, sanctions, fees (including travel expenses, VAT, court fees and legal fees) and any other outgoings o f whatever nature.
"Default Conversion" means the conversion o f the Outstanding Bonds that will take place following a Default Conversion Event.
"Default Conversion Event" means the event where the Issuer in the Issuer's opinion as made on or about Maturity Date is not able to repay the Outstanding Bonds including accrued interest on or before the Maturity Date.
"Default Conversion Mechanism" means the mechanism under which the Issuer and the Bondholders have the right and duty to convert all the Outstanding Bonds, included accrued interest, to shares in the Issuer at the Default Conversion Price which is planned approved by the Issuer's general meeting o f shareholders prior to 24 October 2012.
