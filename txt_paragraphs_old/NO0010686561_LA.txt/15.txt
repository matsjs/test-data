"EBITDA" means the Group's aggregate earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (to be calculated on a 12-month rolling basis).
"Escrow Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 16.1.
"Excess Value" means the difference (positive or negative) between the most recent Group Market Value and the book value of the Group Vessels in the most recent consolidated Financial Statements.
"Exchange" means (i) a securities exchange or other reputable regulated market, or (ii) the Nordic Alternative Bond Market, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
"Existing Internal Loan" means each and every existing loan within the Group where the Issuer, the Issuer's Subsidiary, the Parent and/or Seisranger AS is the lender.
"Existing Intra-Group Loan" means each and every existing loan within the Group where the Issuer, the Issuer's Subsidiary, the Parent and/or Seisranger AS is the borrower, other than any Existing Internal Loan, and which is governed by a Subordinated Loan Agreement.
