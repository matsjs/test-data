14.5 Maintenance and service of Accounts covenants 14.5.1 Escrow Account
(a) The Issuer shall prior to the issuance of the Bonds establish the Escrow Account (with an Acceptable Bank).
(b) The amount on the Escrow Account shall only be applied by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 2.3 (Purpose and utilization) or, if the conditions precedent to Disbursementhave not been fulfilled, as set out in Clause 6.8 (Conditions Precedent).

14.5.2 Issuer Earnings Accounts
(a) The Issuer shall, prior to the Disbursement Date, open and thereafter maintain the Issuer Earnings Account (with an Acceptable Bank), into which all earnings related to FUgro Saltire payable to the Issuer shall be paid directly from the Client.
(b) For the avoidance of doubt, if such earnings related to Fugro Saltire are received in more than one currency, the Issuer shall open additional Issuer Earnings Accounts in such other currencies under the same basis as described in (a) immediately above.
