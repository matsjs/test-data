Part I Condition Precedent to Issue Date
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall not be transferred to the Escrow Account before the Bond Trustee has received, the following condition precedents, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a) this Bond Agreement, duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions of the Obligors (save for the Internal Bareboat Charterer) to issue the Bonds and execute the Finance Documents;
(c) a power of attorney from the Obligors (save for the Internal Bareboat Charterer) to relevant individuals for their execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant register or similar documentation evidencing such individuals' authorisation to execute the Finance Documents on behalf of the Obligors (save for the Internal Bareboat Charterer);
(d) certified copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for the Obligors (save for the Internal Bareboat Charterer), evidencing that it is validly registered and existing and (ii) the Articles of Association of the Obligors (save for the Internal Bareboat Charterer);
