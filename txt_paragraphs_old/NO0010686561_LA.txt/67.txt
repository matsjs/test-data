11.4 Mandatory redemption
Upon a Mandatory Redemption Event occurring in relation to Fugro Saltire, the Issuer and/or the Internal Bareboat Charterer, the Issuer shall (a) no later than thirty (30) days following the relevant Mandatory Redemption Event, but (b) in case of a sale of Fugro Saltire or transfer of any of the shares in the Issuer, no later than upon closing of the relevant transaction, redeem 100 % of the Outstanding Bonds at a price according to the following prices, if occurring anytime from
(a) the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2014, at a price equal to 110% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(b) and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2014 to, butnot including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2015, at a price equal to 108% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(c) and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2015 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2016, at a price equal to 106% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(d) and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2016 to, but hot including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2017, at a price equal to 104% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
(e) and including the Interest Payment Date in September 2017 to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at a price equal to 102% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
11.4.2
Provided, however, that if an Event of Default is outstanding such redemption as set Out immediately above shall be redeemed promptly upon the occurrence of the relevant Mandatory Redemption Event.
