PURCHASE AND TRANSFER OF BONDS
Bondholders may be subject to purchase or transfer restrictions with regard to the Bonds,
as applicable from time to time under local laws to which a Bondholder may be subject
(due e.g. to its nationality, its residency, its registered address, it's place(s) for doing
business). Each Bondholder must ensure compliance with applicable local laws and
regulations at its own cost and expense.
