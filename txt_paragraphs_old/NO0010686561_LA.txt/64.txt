11,2 Call Option
11.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue (all or nothing) (the "Call Option")at any time from and including the:
(a) Interest Payment Date in September 2016 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in September 2017, at 104% of par plus accrued interests on the redeemed amount, and
(b) Interest Payment Date in September 2017 to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at 102% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
11.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
11.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
