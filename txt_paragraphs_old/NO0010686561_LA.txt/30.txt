Binding nature of this Bond Agreement By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 19.1. This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
The Bonds The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 525,000,000 (Norwegian kroner Five-Hundred-Twenty-Five-Million).
The Face Value is NOK 1. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN E FORLAND AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2018".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 068656.1.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
