14.1 14.1.1
From the date of this Bond Agreement and until such time that no amounts are outstanding under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, each relevant Obligor makes the undertakings to the Bond Trustee, as further set out in this Clause 14.
14.2 Information Covenants 14.2.1 Each Obligor shall:
(a) without being requested to do so, promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing of any Event of Default, any event or circumstance which any Obligor understands or ought to understand may lead to an Event of Default and arty other event which may have a Material Adverse Effect;
(b) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee in writing if any Obligor agrees to sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, or change the nature of its business;
(c) without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements (on a consolidated and/or unconsolidated basis, as applicable) and make them available to the Bond Trustee and on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 120 days after the end of the financial year;
(d) without being requested to do so, prepare Interim Accounts (on a consolidated and/or unconsolidated basis, as applicable) and make them available the Bond Trustee and on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication on Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 60 days after the end of the relevant quarter;
(e) without being requested to do so, send the Bond Trustee copies of any statutory notifications of any Obligor, including but not limited to in connection with mergers, de-mergers and reduction of any Obligor's share capital or equity; and
(f) within a reasonable time, provide such information about the Obligors' business, assets and financial condition as the Bond Trustee may reasonably request.
