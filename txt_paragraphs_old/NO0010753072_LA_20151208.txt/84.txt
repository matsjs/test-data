1 O Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem all but not some only of the Outstanding Bonds (Call Option) on any Business Day from and including:
(i) the Issue Date to, but not including, the First Call Date at the Make-Whole Amount,
(ii) the First Call Date to, but not including, the date falling 48 months after the Issue Date at a price equal to 104.5 per cent. of the Face Value for each redeemed Bond (plus accrued and unpaid interest);
