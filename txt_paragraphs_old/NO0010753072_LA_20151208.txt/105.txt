13.5 Financial covenants
During the term of the Bonds, the Group on a consolidated basis shall comply with the fol1owingfinancial covenants at all times:
(i) Interest Cover Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain an Interest Cover Ratio of minimum 4.0x.
(ii) Leverage Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain a Leverage Ratio of maximum 4.0x.
(iii) Total Loan to Value Ratio: The Issuer shall maintain a Total Loan to Value Ratio of maximum 75%.
14 Fees and expenses
14.1 The Issuer shal1 cover all costs and expenses incurred by it or the Bond Trustee in connection with this Bond Agreement and the fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, including in connection with the negotiation, preparation, execution and enforcement of this Bond Agreement and the other Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), the listing of the Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository. The Bond Trustee may withhold funds from any escrow account (or similar arrangement) or from other funds received from the Issuer or any other person, irrespective of such funds being subject to Security under a Finance Documents, to set-off and cover any such costs and expenses.
