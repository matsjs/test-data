2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 50,000,000 (Norwegian krone fifty million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "13.00 per cent Aladdin Oil & Gas Company ASA Senior Secured Bond Issue 2007/2009 with warrants".
The international security identification number (ISIN) of the Loan/Bonds will be NO 001 040032.8.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
