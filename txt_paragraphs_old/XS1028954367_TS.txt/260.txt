Passenger Vehicles
European passenger car production increased by approximately 4% through 2013, indicating that recovery is underway following a decline in the European passenger car market in 2012. Improving economic conditions and pent-up demand are drivers for current growth with a positive outlook for the next several years. At the same time, production growth in the United States and China provide opportunities for select high value applications in these regions.
