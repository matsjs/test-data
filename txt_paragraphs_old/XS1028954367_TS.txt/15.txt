Non IFRS financial measures
Throughout this offering memorandum, we present financial measures and adjustments that are not presented in accordance with IFRS or any other internationally accepted accounting principles, including Contribution, Contribution margin, EBITDA, EBITDA margin, Pro forma Adjusted EBITDA, EBITDA per ton, Adjusted EBITDA, Adjusted EBITDA margin, Trade working capital, Capital expenditure, expansion capital expenditure, maintenance capital expenditure, Free cash flow, as well as certain leverage and coverage ratios derived from these financial measures.

 "Adjusted EBITDA" is defined as EBITDA after adding back restructuring costs incurred during the period;
