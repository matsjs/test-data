Amendment
The Intercreditor Agreement may be amended with the consent of more than 66 2/3% by value of each of, the Credit Facility Lenders and the PIL Lenders, the required percentage of Noteholders (as set out in the Indenture), the required percentage of Pari Passu Creditors in respect of each tranche of Pari Passu Debt (as set out in the relevant Pari Passu Debt documentation), the Parent Company and the Security Agent unless the amendment relates to certain specified matters such as ranking, priority, redistribution, turnover, enforcement, non distressed disposals, distressed disposals or the payments waterfall. Such amendments require consent from all Credit Facility Lenders and PIL Lenders, 90% by value of Noteholders, either 90% by value or all of the Pari Passu Creditors, depending on the type of debt each Hedge Counterparty (to the extent such amendments adversely affect it), the Parent Company and the Security Agent.
No amendment or waiver of the Intercreditor Agreement may impose new or additional obligations on or withdraw or reduce the rights of any party (other than in a way which affects creditors of that party's class generally) under the Intercreditor Agreement without the prior consent of that party (or, in the case of the Noteholders, the Trustee, and in the case of Pari Passu Creditors which are holders of debt securities, the relevant trustee in respect thereof). The Intercreditor Agreement may be amended without the consent of the Noteholders in certain circumstances set out further in " Description o f Notes--Certain covenants--Additional intercreditor agreements" below.
If the Debtors wish to enter into Pari Passu Debt or other additional or replacement indebtedness ("Additional Indebtedness") which is permitted to share in the Collateral pursuant to the Revolving Credit Facility Agreement (and in certain circumstances, facilities that refinance the Revolving Credit Facility), the Pension Insurance Line Agreement, the Indenture and any then existing Pari Passu Debt documentation, the parties to the Intercreditor Agreement may be required to enter into a replacement intercreditor agreement on substantially the same terms as the Intercreditor Agreement or to amend the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement, provided that such documentation does not otherwise adversely affect the interests of the Credit Facility Lenders, the PIL Lenders the Hedge Counterparties, the Noteholders and any other Pari Passu Creditors. The Intercreditor Agreement will also permit the Security Agent to enter into new or supplemental security and/or release and retake Collateral if certain conditions are met, set out further in "Description o f Notes--Certain covenants--Impairment o f security interest" below.
