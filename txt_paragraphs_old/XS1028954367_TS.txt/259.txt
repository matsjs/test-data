Competitive landscape
The European competitive landscape includes approximately ten different regional steel makers including Acciaierie Venete, Danieli (ABS), GMH, Ovako, Schmolz & Bickenbach, Saarstahl and others, as well as select subsidiaries of Gerdau and Tata Steel Europe. Significant global competitors include Daido Steel, Sanyo, Timken and OEMK, of which only OEMK has meaningful imports into Europe at present.
