The Notes
 will be general senior obligations of the Issuer;
 will be secured by first-ranking Liens over the Collateral, but will receive proceeds from enforcement of security over the Collateral or otherwise from the Collateral, only after any obligations secured on a super priority basis have been paid in full. Such obligations include obligations under the Pension Insurance Line, the Revolving Credit Facility and certain Hedging Obligations;
 will be effectively subordinated to any existing and future Indebtedness of the Issuer that is secured by Liens senior to the Liens securing the Notes, or secured by property or assets that do not secure the Notes, to the extent of the value of property and assets securing such Indebtedness;
 will be pari passu in right of payment with all existing and future Indebtedness of the Issuer that is not subordinated in right of payment to the Notes, including Indebtedness incurred under the Pension Insurance Line, the Revolving Credit Facility and certain Hedging Obligations;
 will rank senior in right of payment to all existing and future Indebtedness of the Issuer that is subordinated in right of payment to the Notes; and
 will be fully and unconditionally guaranteed by the Guarantors.
