Committees
Audit Committee. The Audit Committee is a subcommittee of the Board of Directors, established primarily for the purpose of overseeing the accounting and financial reporting processes as well as the audits of the financial statements of Ovako Group AB and the Group. The Committee is responsible for assisting the Board of Directors in overseeing the quality and integrity of Ovako Group AB's financial statements and related disclosure, Ovako Group AB's compliance with legal and regulatory requirements that have a material impact on the financial statements, the reports from and relationship with the external auditors, including the safeguarding of their impartiality and independence, and related-party transactions.
