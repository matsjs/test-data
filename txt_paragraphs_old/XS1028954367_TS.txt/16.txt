 "Capital expenditure" is defined as purchases of property, plant, equipment and intangible assets;
 "Contribution margin" is defined as Contribution shown as a percentage of net sales for the same period;
 "EBITDA" is defined as operating profit (loss) for the period before depreciation, amortization and impairments;
 "EBITDA margin" is defined as EBITDA as a percentage of net sales;
 "EBITDA per ton" is defined as EBITDA divided by sales volume (tons of our final product shipped to customers);
 "Free cash flow" is defined as cash flows before financing activities after adding back income tax paid and net cash interest paid;
 "Pro forma Adjusted EBITDA" is defined as Adjusted EBITDA, further adjusted to reflect headcount reductions, certain transportation cost savings and energy cost savings that occurred in the twelve months ended March 31, 2014, and headcount reductions, energy cost savings, electricity cost reductions as a result of new hedging policy and electricity certificate savings due to changes in legal structure at Hofors that are planned for 2014;
 "Pro forma net financial debt" is defined as total third party debt excluding finance lease liabilities, net of cash and cash equivalents of the Group, on a consolidated basis as of March 31, 2014 after giving effect to the Refinancing, including the issuance of the Notes and the use of proceeds thereof as contemplated herein as if it occurred on March 31, 2014;
 "Pro forma interest expense" is defined as interest expense for the twelve months ended March 31, 2014, adjusted to give effect to the Refinancing, including the issuance of the Notes and the use of proceeds thereof as contemplated herein, as if it occurred on April 1, 2013 and assuming that the Revolving Credit Facility was undrawn and excluding the commitment fees under that facility and imputed interest expense on finance lease liabilities and financial income for such period;
