Option to purchase
The Noteholders and Pari Passu Creditors may, after the commencement of a consultation period referred to in "--Limitations on enforcement" above, and subject to various conditions set out in the Intercreditor Agreement (including the grant of an acceptable indemnity against clawback to the Credit Facility Lenders, the PIL Lenders and the Hedge Counterparties), exercise an option to purchase the Credit Facility Liabilities, the Pension Insurance Line Liabilities and the Hedging Liabilities in full and at par (in the case of Credit Facility Liabilities or the Pension Insurance Line) or the close-out amount owing to them (in the case of Hedging Liabilities).
The Super Senior Creditors may, after the commencement of a consultation period referred to in "--Limitations on enforcement" above, and subject to various conditions set out in the Intercreditor Agreement (including the grant of an acceptable indemnity against clawback to the PIL Lenders), exercise an option to purchase the Pension Insurance Line Liabilities in full and at par.
Release of security and guarantees--non-distressed disposals
