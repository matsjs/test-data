Presentation of financial and other data
Unless otherwise indicated, the financial information included in this offering memorandum relates to the Parent Company and not to the Issuer.
The Issuer and certain Guarantors are holding companies with no independent business operations or significant assets other than investments in its subsidiaries. The Issuer is a direct, wholly-owned subsidiary of the Parent Company, and the results of operations and financial condition of the Issuer are included in the Parent Company's consolidated financial statements.
In this offering memorandum, financial information has been derived from the following information of the Parent Company:
· the audited consolidated financial statements as of and for the fiscal years ended December 31, 2013, 2012 and 2011, including the notes thereto, which have been prepared in accordance with IFRS and audited by our independent auditors, Ernst & Young AB, as indicated in their audit reports included elsewhere in this offering memorandum (the "Audited Consolidated Financial Statements");
· the unaudited interim condensed consolidated financial statements as of and for the three months ended March 31, 2014 (that include comparative information for the three months ended March 31, 2013), which have been prepared in accordance with IAS 34 on the same basis as our Audited Consolidated Financial Statements and which have been reviewed by our independent auditors, Ernst &Young AB, as indicated in their review report included elsewhere in this offering memorandum (the "Unaudited Interim Consolidated Financial Statements" and together with the Audited Consolidated Financial Statements the "Consolidated Financial Statements"); and
· our internal accounting system.
The Audited Consolidated Financial Statements and Ernst & Young AB's audit reports thereon as well as the Unaudited Interim Consolidated Financial Statements and Ernst & Young AB's review report thereon are included in this offering memorandum beginning on page F-1.
In addition, this offering memorandum includes certain unaudited financial information for the twelve months ended March 31, 2014. The unaudited consolidated statement of income information and the other financial and operating information presented in this offering memorandum for the twelve months ended March 31, 2014 were derived by subtracting from the audited consolidated financial statements as of and for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2013 of the Parent Company the comparative information from unaudited interim condensed consolidated financial statements as of and for the three months ended March 31, 2013 and adding the information from the unaudited interim condensed consolidated financial statements for the three months ended March 31, 2014, as well as from the Parent Company's internal accounting system. The unaudited consolidated statement of income information and the other financial and operating information as of and for the twelve months ended March 31, 2014 has been prepared solely for the purposes of this offering memorandum and is for illustrative purposes only and is not necessarily representative of our results of operations for any future period or our financial condition at any future date and is not prepared in the ordinary course of our financial reporting.
