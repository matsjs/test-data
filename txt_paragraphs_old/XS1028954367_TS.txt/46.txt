For the twelve months ended December 31, 2013, our Guarantors collectively represented 88.9% of our net sales and 103.9% of our consolidated EBITDA and, as of December 31, 2013, they represented 89.6% of our total assets. These figures represent net sales, EBITDA and total assets, as applicable, after intercompany eliminations. Certain of our non-Guarantor subsidiaries have negative EBITDA and total assets.
