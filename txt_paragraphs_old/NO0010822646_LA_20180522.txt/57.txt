14.3 General Covenants
(a) Pari passu ranking The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at leastpari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
(b) Mergers The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company shall, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any other Group Company with any other companies or entities if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
