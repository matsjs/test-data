11.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at no less than thirty (30) nor more than sixty (60) calendar days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option (such notice to be irrevocable and specify the settlement date of the Call Option).
11.2.3
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued to the settlement date.
