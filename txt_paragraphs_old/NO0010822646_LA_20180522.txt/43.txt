10 Interest
10.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at the Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
10.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falling on 23 August 2018 (3 months after the Issue Date).
10.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period (each an "Interest Period") from, and including, the Issue Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date, and thereafter from and including, that Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
