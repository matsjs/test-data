17.1
Each o f the events or circumstances set out in this Clause 17.1 is an Event o f Default:
(a) Non-payment
the Issuer fails to pay an amount on the date it is due in accordance with the Finance Documents unless its failure to pay is caused by administrative or technical error and payment is made within five (5) Business Days o f the due date;
(a) Financial covenants
the Issuer breaches any financial covenant set out in Clause 16.1 (Financial Undertakings), unless such breach has been cured in accordance with the provisions set out therein;
(b) Other obligations
the Issuer or any other Group Company does not comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out under (a) and (b) above, provided that the Issuer has not remedied, or has not procured that the relevant party has remedied, the failure within twenty (20) Business Days from the earlier o f the Issuer becoming aware o f the failure and the Agent has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure (if the failure or violation is not capable o f being remedied, the Agent may declare the Notes due and payable without such prior written request);
