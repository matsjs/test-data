The Agent's resignation or dismissal shall only take effect upon the appointment o f a successor Agent and acceptance by such successor Agent o f such appointment and the execution o f all necessary documentation to effectively substitute the retiring Agent.
Upon the appointment o f a successor, the retiring Agent shall be discharged from any further obligation in respect o f the Finance Documents but shall remain entitled to the benefit of the Finance Documents and remain liable under the Finance Documents in respect of any action which it took or failed to take whilst acting as Agent. Its successor, the Issuer and each o f the Noteholders shall have the same rights and obligations amongst themselves under the Finance Documents as they would have had if such successor had been the original Agent.
