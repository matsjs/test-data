Ownership of the Target
The Issuer shall at all times retain direct ownership o f 100 per cent. o f the issued share capital o f the Target.
Listing
The Issuer shall (i) ensure that the Notes are admitted to trading on the regulated market o f Nasdaq Stockholm within nine (9) months after the Issue Date, and (ii) ensure that the Notes once admitted to trading on Nasdaq Stockholm, continue to be listed thereon but no longer than up to and including the last day on which the admission to trading reasonably can subsist, pursuant to the then applicable regulations o f Nasdaq Stockholm and the CSD.
