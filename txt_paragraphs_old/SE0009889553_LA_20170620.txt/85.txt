18.4
If the Issuer or the Agent shall make any payment under this Clause 18, the Issuer or the
Agent, as applicable, shall notify the Holders of any such payment at least fifteen (15)
Business Days before the payment is made. Such notice shall specify the Record Date, the
payment date and the amount to be paid. Notwithstanding the foregoing, for any Interest due
but unpaid the Record Date specified in Clause 9.1 shall apply.
