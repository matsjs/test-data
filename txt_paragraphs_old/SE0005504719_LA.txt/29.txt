5.2
The Issuer shall ensure that the Share Pledge Agreement and all documents relating thereto
are duly executed in favour of the Agent and the Holders (as represented by the Agent) and
that such documents are legally valid, perfected, enforceable and in full force and effect
according to their terms. The Issuer shall execute and/or procure the execution of such
further documentation by the Pledgor as the Agent may reasonably require in order for the
Holders and the Agent to at all times maintain the security position envisaged hereunder.
