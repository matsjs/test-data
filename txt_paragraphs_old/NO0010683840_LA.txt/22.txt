The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN GasLog Ltd. Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2013/2018".
