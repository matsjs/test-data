(ii) Maximum Leverage: The Issuer shall maintain a Maximum Leverage of 75%;
(iii) Market Adjusted Net Worth: The Issuer shall maintain Market Adjusted Net Worth of not less than USD 350,000,000;
(iv) Minimum Free Liquidity. The Issuer shall maintain cash and cash equivalents of minimum the greater of (a) USD 20,000,000 and (b) three per cent of Total Indebtedness.

Fees and expenses
