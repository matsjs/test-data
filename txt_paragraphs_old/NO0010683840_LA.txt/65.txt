13.4.1.3 Listing
During the term of the Bonds, the Issuer shall ensure that the Issuer's shares remain listed on the New York Stock Exchange or another recognized regulated stock exchange.
13.4.1.4 MLP
In the event that the Issuer establishes an MLP, the Issuer shall maintain full control over such MLP through directly or indirectly controlling 100% of the voting rights and equity of the General Partner of the MLP. The Issuer shall not, and it shall ensure that no Subsidiary shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance over any of the Issuer's shares in the MLP and in the General Partner.
