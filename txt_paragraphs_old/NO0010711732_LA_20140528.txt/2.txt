Interpretation
1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Bank" means Sparebank 1 SMN.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account, the Earnings Account and the Debt Service Account.
"Accounts Pledge" means a first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee over the Issuer's claim against the Account Bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Accounts.
"Assignment of Insurances" means a first priority assignment in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf o f the Bondholders) o f any insurance related to the Vessels.
