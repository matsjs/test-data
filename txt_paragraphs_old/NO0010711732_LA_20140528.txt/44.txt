10.4.3 For the avoidance of doubt, the redemption price shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date the repayment is carried out.
10.4.4 Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available for the Issuer, however no later than 180 calendar days after the occurrence of the Total Loss Event, redeem:
(a)
100% of the Outstanding Bonds at par value (plus accrued and unpaid
interest on redeemed Bonds) if all Vessels suffer a Total Loss Event; or
(b) X% of the Outstanding Bonds at par value (plus accrued and unpaid interest on the redeemed Bonds) if either of the Vessels suffers a total loss, where X% shall be calculated as the Market Value of the relevant Vessel divided by the Market Value o f all the Vessels.
