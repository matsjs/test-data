2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 370,000,000 (Norwegian kroner three hundred seventy million).
The Face Value is NOK 1. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Golden Energy Offshore Services AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2014/2017".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0711732.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
