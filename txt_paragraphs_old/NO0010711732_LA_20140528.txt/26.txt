The disbursement from the Escrow Account to the Issuer is subject to the Bond Trustee having received the documents set out in Part II (Conditions precedent to Disbursement Date) of Schedule 3 {Conditions precedent documents) delivered prior to and at the latest on the Disbursement Date.
Subject to the delivery by the Issuer of the documents set out in Part II (Conditions precedent to Disbursement Date) of Schedule 3 (Conditions precedent documents) in form and substance satisfactory to the Bond Trustee, the Bond Trustee shall release the net proceeds from the Escrow Account to the Issuer. The amount on the Escrow Account shall only be applied by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 2.3.
6.2.3
In the event that the documents set out in Part II (Conditions precedent to Disbursement Date) of Schedule 3 (Conditions precedent documents) are not delivered or are not satisfactory to the Bond Trustee before the first Interest Payment Date after the Issue Date, the amount on the Escrow Account shall be applied as set out in Clause 10.5.
