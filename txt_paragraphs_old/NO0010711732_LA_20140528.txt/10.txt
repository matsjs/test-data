"Group" means the Issuer and its Subsidiaries from time to time (each a "Group Company").
"Intercreditor Agreement" means an agreement between the Issuer, the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and any subordinated mortgagee providing security for any currency and/or hedging agreements on terms acceptable to the Bondholders.
"Interest Payment Date" means 28 February, 28 May, 28 August and 28 November each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to GAAP.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number o f the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means 28 May 2014.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
