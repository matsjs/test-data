The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied towards (in the following order of priority):
(a)
financing the Issuer's acquisition of the Vessels; and
(b) the general corporate purposes of the Issuer.
3
Listing
3.1 The Issuer is under no obligation to list the Bonds on a regulated market or on Oslo Bors ASA's Nordic ABM, but shall have the right to list the Bonds if it so desires.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
