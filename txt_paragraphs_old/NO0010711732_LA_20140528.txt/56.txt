(f)
A rm 's length transactions
The Issuer shall not engage directly or indirectly in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer than those which might be obtained in an arms' length transaction at the time.
(g) Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
(h) Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all aspects and comply in all respects with all laws and regulations it may be subject to from time to time. Breach of these obligations shall be regarded as non-compliance only if such breach would have a Material Adverse Effect.
