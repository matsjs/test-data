(d) Maintenance o fAccounts
The Issuer shall open and maintain the Accounts in the name of the relevant party with a bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
(e) Payment o f earnings to the Earnings Account
The Issuer shall ensure that all earnings related to the Vessels shall be paid directly to the Earnings Account. For the avoidance of doubt, if such earnings related to the Vessels are received in more than one currency, the relevant parties shall open additional Earnings Accounts in such other currencies, subject to those Earnings Accounts being pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
