"Defeasance Security" has the meaning given to that term in Clause 18.2.1.
"Disbursement Date" means the date when all conditions precedent documents set out in Part I (Conditions precedent to Issue Date) and Part II (Conditions precedent to Disbursement Date) o f Schedule 3 (Conditions precedent documents) have been delivered, and the cash proceeds from the Bond Issue are released from the Escrow Account.
"Earnings Account" means one or more accounts opened and maintained in the name of the Issuer for each Vessel or collectively for the Vessels with the Account Bank, which shall be pledged, but not blocked, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) pursuant to the Earnings Account Pledge, in which the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights, and to which all earnings related to the Vessels shall be paid directly.
"Earnings Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the Account Bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Earnings Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer with the Account Bank, pledged on first priority basis and blocked as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the Account Bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
