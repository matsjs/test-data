13.4 Special covenants
(a) Ownership to assets
The Issuer shall not own any material assets (included herein any shares in any subsidiary of the Parent or in any other company) other than (A) the Vessels and (B) any assets related to the Vessels in the ordinary course of business of the Issuer.
(b) Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer may not within any financial year, during the term of the Bonds, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions to its shareholders.

(c) Financial assistance
The Issuer shall not grant any loans, any guarantees or any other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting security) to or for the benefit o f any third party except for (i) normal trade credit or advance payments granted or made in the ordinary course of business and (ii) any guarantee and security granted to secure any currency and/or interest hedging liability entered into in relation to the Bond Issue, provided that such guarantee and security is subject to the Intercreditor Agreement.
