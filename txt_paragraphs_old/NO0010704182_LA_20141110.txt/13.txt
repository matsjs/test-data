"Escrow Account" means the account in the name of the Issuer, held with the Paying Agent, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 16.1.
"Employment Contract" means any contract of employment of the Vessel between the Issuer (or any Intra-Group Charterer if relevant) and the end-user, however not to include service or operation agreements related to the operation or performance of the Vessel if entered into between any third party agent or service provider unrelated to the Group and the end-user.
"Exchange" means (i) a securities exchange or other reputable regulated market, or (ii) Oslo Børs ASA' Nordic ABM, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
"Excess Amount" means USD 11,000,000 which is the aggregate of (i) 2% of the Bond Issue, (ii) estimated interest from the Issue Date to the Back Stop date in the amount of USD 3,000,000 and (iii) USD 5,000,000 to cover fees and costs in connection with the Bond Issue.
"Equipment Contract" means the contract and its appendixes dated 30 November 2009 (as amended) between the Issuer and National Oilwell Varco Norway AS for the design works and supply of equipment, materials and machinery, supervision of the installation and commission of provided equipment, materials and machinery and/or other services which National Oilwell Varco Norway AS is required to provide under the contract.
"Face Value" means the denomination o f each of the Bonds, as set out in Clause 2.2.
