"Issue Date" means 21 February 2014.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
"Manager" means the managers for the Bond Issue, being ABG Sundal Collier Norge ASA and Arctic Securities ASA.
"Management Contract" means the contract between the Vessel Manager and the Issuer dated 14 February 2014 in relation to the operational and technical management of the Vessel.
"Mandatory Prepayment Event" means if (i) the Vessel becomes a Total Loss, or (ii) the Vessel is no longer 100% directly owned by the Issuer, or (iii) the Parent ceases to own 100% (directly or indirectly) to the shares of the Issuer.
"Market Value" means the fair market value of the Vessel in USD determined as the arithmetic mean of independent valuations provided by two independent and reputable shipbrokers, appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee. Such valuation shall be made on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms as between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts fo r employment. The cost of such determination shall be for the account of the Issuer. The valuation shall be made at least annually, or following an Event of Default, upon the request of the Bond Trustee.
"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on: (a) the financial condition or operations of the Issuer or any of the Guarantors, or the Group (taken as a whole), (b) the Issuer's or any of the Guarantors' ability to perform and comply with its obligations under any of the Finance Documents; or (c) the validity or enforceability of any of the Finance Documents.
"Maturity Date" means 21 December 2018. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
