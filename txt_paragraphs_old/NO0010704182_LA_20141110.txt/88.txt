(c) De-mergers
The Issuer and any Intra-Group Charterer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer or any of its Subsidiaries into two or more separate companies or entities.

No Obligor shall cease to carry on its business, and the Parent shall ensure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Parent and the Group from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
