(c) Retention Account
The Retention Account shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee. Amounts standing on the Retention Account shall only be applied to pay scheduled interest and amortizations under the Bonds.
(d) Debt Service Account
The Debt Service Account shall be pledged on a first priority basis in favour of the Bond Trustee and blocked. Cash deposited on the Debt Service Account shall be utilised to cover any shortfall as described aboye under (b) and in such case under the consent of the Bond Trustee.
