Disbursement of the Retained Escrow Amount from the Escrow Account to the Issuer for the
repayment of the Vendor Loan will be subject to the Bond Trustee having received
evidence as listed below, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(a) USD 35,000,000 shall be released from the Escrow Account once an Employment Contract of at least 75 days duration and with a day rate of at least USD 200,000 has been entered into with either ConocoPhilliphs, Shell, BP, Chevron, Exxon Mobile, Total, Statoil or another reputable oil major with investment grade rating and that is involved in the exploration, development and production of oil and gas reserves internationally.
(b) USD 15,000,000 shall be released when the Vessel has been operating on Employment Contract(s) for at least six months with a day rate of at least USD 185,000 and has proven a utilization (meaning an up-time of the Vessel under the relevant Employment Contract(s) at full rate and where payment has been made or with a binding confirmation from the oil company that there are no disputes regarding the payment) above 85%.
