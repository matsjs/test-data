Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account, the Earnings Account, the Operating Account, the Retention Account and the Reserve Account.
"Accounts Pledge" means a first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over each of the Accounts, other than the Escrow Account, and where the Retention Account is blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee.
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Alternate Issuer" means an entity incorporated in Malta or Luxembourg, having assumed all rights and obligations of the Issuer and having the identical activity as the Issuer.
"Asset Coverage Ratio" means the Market Value of the Rig relative to the aggregate Face Value of the Outstanding Bonds.
