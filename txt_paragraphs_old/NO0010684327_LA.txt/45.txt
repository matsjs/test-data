10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 10. 1.1
Maturity, instalments and cash sweep Instalments
The Bonds shall be repaid by the Issuer in instalments of USD 1,500,000 on each Interest Payment Date. The remaining Outstanding Bonds shall be repaid at the Maturity Date. All instalments shall be carried out at par value (100%).
Payment of instalments must be carried out pro rata in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository.
