"Share Pledge" means a pledge granted by the shareholders of the Issuer over all of the shares (100%) issued by the Issuer in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), together with inter alia, letters of resignation (effective upon a default) from current board members and covenants to obtain such from future board members
"Subsidiary" means a company over which another company has Decisive Influence.
"Subsidiary Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over the Subsidiary Earnings Account and the Subsidiary Operating Account (if any).
"Subsidiary Earnings Account" means an account in the name of the relevant Subsidiary of the Issuer held with an account bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee to hold any earnings related to the Rig (if applicable).
"Subsidiary Share Pledge" means a pledge granted by the Issuer or a Group Company (as the case may be), and any other shareholders (as long as reasonably practical), in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) over all of the shares (100%) in each Subsidiary (if any), together with (to the extent legally possible), inter alia, letters of resignation (effective upon a default) from current board members and covenants to obtain such from future board members.
"Subsidiary Operating Account" means an account in the name of the relevant Subsidiary of the Issuer held with an account bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee.
