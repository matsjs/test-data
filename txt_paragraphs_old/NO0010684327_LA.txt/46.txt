10. 1.2 Cash Sweep
All cash exceeding USD 7.5 million, however not including the part of the Initial Dividend not paid within thirty (30) days after the Issue Date, deposited on the Reserve Account in accordance with Clause 13.5 (Application of Earnings) shall be used to repay the Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount). Such redemption shall take place on every Interest Payment Date subject to the credit balance on the Reserve Account, on the tenth banking day prior to the relevant Interest Payment Date, being in an amount of minimum USD 7.5 million, however not including the part of the Initial Dividend not paid within thirty (30) days after the Issue Date. The cash sweep shall only be carried out if the Asset Coverage Ratio was below 160% on the previous Quarter Date.
10.1.3 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.1 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
