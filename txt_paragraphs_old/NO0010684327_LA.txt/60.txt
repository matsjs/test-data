(e) Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Group from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, other than as set out in this Bond Agreement.
(f) Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations other than as set out in Clause 2.3 (Alternate Issuer) or 13.8 (Charter and Rig Transfer).
(g) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any related person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(h) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that any other Group Company shall not, change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation or otherwise amend its constitutional documents other than (i) as set out in Clause 2.3 (Alternate Issuer) and (ii) as related to the issuance of new shares to the shareholders in connection with a conversion of debt to equity or otherwise, provided that such shares are subject to the Share Pledge.
