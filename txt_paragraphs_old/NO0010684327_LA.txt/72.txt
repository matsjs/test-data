(c) Title
The Issuer shall hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the Rig and the Insurances taken out in respect of the Rig, other than as set out in Clause 2.3 (Alternate Issuer) or 13.8 (Charter Transfer and Rig Transfer), and assigned to the Bond Trustee, free of any and all Security except for those expressly permitted under this Bond Agreement and, until disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bond Issue pursuant to Clause 6.2 (Conditions Precedent to Disbursement), established under the Natixis Facility.
(d) Class, flag, name, registry
The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig maintains its class, flag and name, and that it remains registered in Panama, unless otherwise approved by the Bond Trustee. Such approval not to be unreasonably withheld; provided, always that such approval will not be required if a change is required and permitted pursuant to the Project Documents and the Security Interests are not impaired.
(e) Operations in accordance with laws etc.
The Issuer shall at all times exercise its rights under the Project Documents to require that the Rig is operated in accordance with any laws, regulations, administrative decisions and/or other public authorities as applicable from time to time and jurisdiction to jurisdiction.
