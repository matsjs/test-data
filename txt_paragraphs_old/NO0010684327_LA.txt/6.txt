"Alternate Issuer" means an entity incorporated in Malta or Luxembourg, having assumed all rights and obligations of the Issuer and having the identical activity as the Issuer.
"Assignment of Earnings" means to the extent possible, an assignment of any earnings payable to the Issuer under the Total Charter Contract in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and the Issuer shall give notice and use its best endeavors to procure the acknowledgement of such assignment from the Charterer.
"Assignment of Insurances" means an assignment of all relevant insurances related to the Rig and the equipment related thereto, on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Assignment of Intercompany Loans" means a first priority assignment of any intercompany loans or receivables between two Group Companies in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) (if any).
"Assignment of Management Agreements" means an assignment of any and all Management Agreements in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders). The assignment to contain direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights in case of default, and the Issuer or any other relevant Group Company (as the case may be) shall give notice and obtain consent and acknowledgement from the Rig Manager.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Schedules to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
