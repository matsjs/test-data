10.3.2
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly as soon as the insurance proceeds are available to the relevant Group Company, but no later than ninety (90) days following the Total Loss Event, redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
10.3.3 The aforesaid redemption prices shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date of the repayment.
10.3.4
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.3 (Mandatory Redemption), the amounts on the Accounts and the Subsidiary Earnings Account and Subsidiary Operating Account (if any), or any insurance proceeds may be used as partial payment for the redemption.
10.3.5
Upon the occurrence of several Mandatory Prepayment Events, the Issuer shall only be obliged to pay one redemption amount. The redemption price shall be determined based on the Mandatory Prepayment Event that occurred earliest.
