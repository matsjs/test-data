This agreement (the "Loan Agreement") has been entered into on 19 June 2013 between Petrolia SE ( a company incorporated in Cyprus with Company No SE14) as borrower (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (Norwegian Company No 963 342 624) as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee").
The Bondholders are bound by the terms of the Loan Agreement as the Loan Trustee through the Bondholders' subscription was granted authority to finalise and execute the Loan Agreement on behalf o f the Bondholders.
All Bond transfers shall be subject to the terms of this Loan Agreement and the Trustee and all Bond transferees shall, in taking transfer of Bonds, be deemed to have accepted the terms of the Loan Agreement where after any and all such transferees shall automatically become parties to the Loan Agreement upon completed transfer having been registered in the VPS, without any further action required or formalities to be complied with.
