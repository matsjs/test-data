2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of NOK 600,000,000 (Norwegian kroner six hundred million), The Bond Issue:may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates, The first tranoh will be ;in the amount of NOK 400,000,000 (Norwegian kroner four hundred million),
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA Senior Unsecured Open Bond Issue 2009/2012".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 056091.5.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.2.2
The Bond Issue is a Tap Issue, under which subsequent issues may take place after Issue Date up to the maximum amount described in Clause 2.2,1, miming from the Issue Date and to be closed no later than 5 Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parlies to the Bond Agreement also apply for later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will on the issuing of additional Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
