"Summons" means the call for a Bondholders' Meeting or a Written Resolution as the case may be.
"Super Senior Credit Facility" means a revolving credit facility or facilities to be provided to the Issuer (and/or Group Companies) as borrower, having the following terms in relation to the Bonds:
(a) the Issuer and any other Group Companies may apply amounts borrowed by the Issuer under the Super Senior Credit Facility towards general corporate and working capital or acquisition purposes of the Group;
(b) the commitment under such facility may, subject to compliance with the Incurrence Test be up to a maximum amount of the lower of (i) NOK 150,000,000 (or the equivalent in any other currency), and (ii) lxEBITDA for the Group (calculated on a pro forma basis for the Group for the preceding 12 months);
(c) all amounts outstanding under the Super Senior Finance Documents shall be secured on first priority over the same security assets as covered by the Pre-Disbursement Security (which shall constitute senior obligations of the Issuer);
(d) the Super Senior Credit Facility shall (together with any Permitted Hedging Obligations) rank super senior to the Bonds (including Bonds issued under a Tap Issue) with respect to any Enforcement Proceeds, pursuant to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement;
(e) the Super Senior Credit Facility may consist of one or several facilities (also guarantee, letter of credit or any other ancillary facilities) from one or more lenders, which shall rank pari passu between each other; and
(f) all facility commitments under the Super Senior Credit Facility are subject to simultaneous clean down once every 12 months. A minimum period of 6 months shall elapse between each such clean down.
