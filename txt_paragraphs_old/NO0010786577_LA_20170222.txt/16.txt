"Intercompany Loan" means any loan made by a Group Company to a Material Group Company (not including any Financial Indebtedness under any cash pooling arrangement of the Group) where (i) the term of the loan is over one year (the term being determined at the sole discretion of the Issuer) and (ii) the amount is in excess of EUR 1,000,000 and which pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement shall be fully subordinated to the Secured Obligations.
