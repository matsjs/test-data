"Equity Ratio" means the ratio of Equity to Adjusted Book Assets.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means securities exchange or other reputable marketplace for securities, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
"Finance Documents" means (i) this Bond Agreement, (ii) the agreement between the Bond Trustee and the Issuer referred to in Clause 14.2, (iii) the Guarantee (iv) any documents executed in relation to the Bond Issue to the Bond Trustee, and (v) any other document (whether creating a security interest or not) which is executed at any time by the Issuer or the Guarantor in relation to any amount payable under this Bond Agreement.
