"Contract Company" means all and any Group Company that at any time is the counterparty in the contract for use of any of the Rigs with a third party. Such Group Company may be a Rig Owner or any other Subsidiary of the Issuer. The Issuer may, in its own discretion, alter with which Group Company shall be the "Contract Company".
"Current Assets" means on any date, the aggregate value of the assets which are treated (on a consolidated basis) as the Group's current assets in accordance with GAAP less the aggregated value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's restricted cash deposit securing the Eksportfinans Loan.
"Current Liabilities" means on any date, the aggregate amount of all liabilities which are treated (on a consolidated bases) as the Group's current liabilities in accordance with GAAP, less any scheduled instalments, treated as current liabilities in accordance with GAAP, of the Bank Loan Facilities, the Eksportfinans Loan and the Bonds.
"Bank Loan Facilities" means the loan facilities provided by a syndicate of banks with DnB NOR Bank ASA as agent and with Alfa and Beta as borrowers.
"Eksportfinans Loan" means the loan facility provided by Eksportfinans ASA as lender and with the Issuer as borrowers.
