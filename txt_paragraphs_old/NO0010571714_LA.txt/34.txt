10 Maturity of the Bonds, Call Option, Change of Control and Mandatory Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in April 2011 at 104.5 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
