8. Interest
(a) Each Initial Bond carries Interest at the Applicable Interest Rate from (and including) the First Issue Date up to (but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Bonds will carry Interest at the Applicable Interest Rate from (and including) the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to its Issue Date up to (but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date.
