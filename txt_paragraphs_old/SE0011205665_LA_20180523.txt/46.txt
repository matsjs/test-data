The Group Companies' purchase of Bonds
For as long as any Existing Bonds remain outstanding no Group Company may purchase Bonds except if the Issuer has an obligation to do so pursuant to these Terms and Conditions. Once no Existing Bonds remain outstanding each Group Company may, subject to applicable law, at any time and at any price purchase Bonds. Bonds held by a Group Company may at such Group Company's discretion be retained or sold, but not cancelled, except in connection with a full redemption o f the Bonds.
