If a Holder has registered, through an Account Operator, that principal, Interest and any
certain bank account; such deposits will be effectuated by the CSD on the relevant payment
date. In other cases, payments will be transferred by the CSD to the Holder at the address
registered with the CSD on the Record Date. Should the CSD, due to a delay on behalf of the
Issuer or some other obstacle, not be able to effectuate payments as aforesaid, the Issuer
shall procure that such amounts are paid to the Persons who are registered as Holders on the
relevant Record Date as soon as possible after such obstacle has been removed.
