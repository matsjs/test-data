(i) the Issuer notifies the Agent of any such transaction at least five (5) Business Days before the relevant Permitted Divestment is made;
(ii) the Issuer commits to the Agent that the Divestment Proceeds, together with any other available funding, will suffice to carry out a partial repayment of the Bonds and the Issuer undertakes to carry out such partial repayment of the Bonds, in each case, in accordance with Clause 11.6 (Mandatory partial repayment due to a Permitted Divestment); and
