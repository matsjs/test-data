2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of subordinated convertible Bonds at a maximum amount of USD 21,000,000 (U.S. Dollar twenty one million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be issued in denominations of USD 100,000 each, and rank pari passu among each other.
The Loan will be described as "7.0 per cent Mosvold Supply Pic. Callable Subordinated Convertible Bond Issue 2007/2012".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 038936.4.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
