The respective Bondholder must notify the Paying Agent within the Change of Control Conversion Period of its election of whether or not to (a) require early redemption, or (b) convert the Bonds to Shares. In the event of an early redemption pursuant to this Clause 10.3, settlement shall be three (3) Banking Days after the Paying Agent has received such request.
