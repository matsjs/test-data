The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole, but not in parts, as follows (Call Option):
(a) with settlement date at any time from Issue Date to and including 31 December 2015, at 110 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, and
(b) with settlement date at any time from and including 1 January 2016 to, but not included, the Maturity Date, at 105 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
