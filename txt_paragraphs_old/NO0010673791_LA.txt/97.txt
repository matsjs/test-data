18.4.4 Security Agent's Discretions
The Security Agent may:
(a) assume (unless it has received actual notice to the contrary from the Bond Trustee) that (i) no Event of Default has occurred and no Obligor is in breach of or default under its obligations under any of the Finance Documents and (ii) any right, power, authority or discretion vested by any Finance Document in any person has not been exercised;
