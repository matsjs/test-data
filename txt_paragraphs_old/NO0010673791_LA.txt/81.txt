16.1.5 Insolvency
(a) Any Obligor is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of its creditors with a view to rescheduling any o f its indebtedness.
(b) A moratorium is declared in respect of any indebtedness of Obligor.
