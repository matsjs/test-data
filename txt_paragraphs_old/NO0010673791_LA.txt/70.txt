14.5 Special covenants
14.5.1 Ownership o fSubsidiaries
The Issuer shall not sell, or otherwise dispose of any of its equity ownership in the Guarantors, other than by a Permitted Disposal.
14.5.2 Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not make any dividend payments, repurchase of shares or make other distributions to its shareholders.
14.5.3 Subsidiaries 'distributions
The Issuer shall ensure that no Subsidiary creates or permits to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right of any Subsidiary to pay dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders.
