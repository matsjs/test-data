(a) the Security for the Bonds;
(b) any lien arising by operation of law or in the Ordinary Course of Business;
(c) any netting or set-off arrangement entered into by a member o f the Group in the ordinary course of its banking arrangement for the purpose of netting debit and credit balances o f members o f the Group;
