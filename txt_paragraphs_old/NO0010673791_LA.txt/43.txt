10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue (in full or in part) as follows (such right being the "Call Option"):
(a)
at any time from the Interest Payment Date in March 2016 to, but not
included, the Interest Payment Date in March 2017 at 105% o f par plus
accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(b) at any time from the Interest Payment Date in March 2017 to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 103% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise o f the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date o f the Call Option.
