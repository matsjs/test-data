10 M aturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in March 2011 at 112 % o f par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) any time from and included the Interest Payment Date in March 2011 to, but not included, Maturity Date at 107 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
