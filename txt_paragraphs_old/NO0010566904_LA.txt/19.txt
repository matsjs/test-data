Listing
3.1 The Issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on Oslo Bars.
3.2 Prior to listing, the Issuer shall obtain a confirmation from the Oslo Stock Exchange or the Norwegian Financial Supervisory Authority that the prospectus prepared in connection with the listing o f the Bonds has been approved under the requirements set forth in Chapter 7 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act (implementing the EU prospectus directive (2003/71 EC).
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full. No market-maker agreement has been entered into with respect to the Bonds.
