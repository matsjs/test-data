When the Interest Cover Ratio is measured under the Incurrence Test, as applicable, the calculation of the Interest Cover Ratio shall be made for the Relevant Period ending on the last day of the period covered by the most recent Financial Report available.
The figures for EBITDA, Finance Charges and Net Finance Charges, shall be used for the Incurrence Test, but adjusted so that:
(a) entities acquired or disposed of by the Group during the Relevant Period, or after the end of the Relevant Period but before the relevant Testing Date, shall be included or excluded (as applicable), pro forma, for the entire Relevant Period; and
(b) any entity to be acquired with the proceeds from new Financial Indebtedness shall be included, pro forma, for the entire Relevant Period.
