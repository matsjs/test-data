6.2
A Bondholder may issue one or several powers of attorney to third parties to represent it in
relation to some or all of the Bonds held by it. Any such representative may act
independently under the Finance Documents in relation to the Bonds for which such
representative is entitled to represent the Bondholder and may further delegate its right to
represent the Bondholder by way of a further power of attorney.
