11.9 Financial Covenants
11.9.1 The Interest Coverage Ratio shall not be less than 2.0:1.
11.9.2 Equity Ratio may not be less than 20% at all times.
11.9.3 The Loan to Value may not exceed 80% at any time.
11.9.4
The financial covenants in Clauses 11.9.1, 11.9.2 and 11.9.3 above will be measured on each Test Date, and in respect of the Interest Coverage Ratio, for the relevant Test Period.
