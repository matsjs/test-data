13.13 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
13.14
Holding company
The Issuer shall not trade, carry on any business or own any material assets, except for (i) the provision of administrative services to other Group Companies of a type customarily provided by a holding company, (ii) ownership of shares in any company, cash and cash equivalents, and (iii) any Intercompany Loans.
