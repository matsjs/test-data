4.
ADMISSION TO LISTING
The Issuer shall use its reasonable endeavours to ensure that the Bonds are listed on the Exchange within 12 months of the Issue Date and remain listed on such Exchange until the Bonds have been redeemed in full. For the avoidance of doubt, a Listing Failure Event shall not constitute an Event of Default.
5.
REGISTRATION OF THE BONDS
5.1 Registration in the CSD
The Bonds shall be registered in dematerialised form in the CSD according to the relevant securities registration legislation and the requirements of the CSD.
