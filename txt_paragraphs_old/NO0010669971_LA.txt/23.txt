"Subsidiary" means an entity over which the Issuer has a Decisive Influence due to (i) direct and indirect ownership of shares or other ownership interests, and/or (ii) an agreement, understanding or other'arrangement. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of the Issuer if the Issuer owns such number of shares or ownership interests therein so as to represent the majority (more than 50 %) of the votes in the entity, or has the right to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
"Total Interest-Bearing Debt" means the book value of the Group's total Interest Bearing Debt in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer).
"US Securities Act" means the U.S. Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
