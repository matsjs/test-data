6.3 Waivers The Bond Trustee may, in its reasonable opinion, waive the deadline or requirements for documentation as set out in Clause 6.1 and 6.2.
6.4 Bond Trustee's confirmation of conditions precedent Settlement and Disbursement of the net proceeds from the Bonds is subject to the Bond Trustee's written notice to the Issuer, the Manager and the Paying Agent that the documents have been controlled and that the required conditions precedent are fulfilled.
6.5 Transfer / Release On the Issue Date, subject to receipt of confirmation from the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 6.4, the Manager shall transfer the net proceeds from the Bond Issue to the Escrow Account.
On Disbursement, subject to receipt of confirmation from the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 6.4 and subject further to the Retention Account Funding is earned out in accordance with Clause 6.6, the Cetus Financing or the Insula Financing (as the case may be) may be made available to the Issuer from the Escrow Account.
6.6 Retention Account The Issuer shall in connection with the first Disbursement from the Escrow Account deposit the Retention Account Funding in a retention account (the "Retention Account") from which interest on the Bonds may be paid (unless an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing) provided that the Issuer shall procure that an amount corresponding to minimum 6 months' interest on the Bonds shall at all times be deposited in the Retention Account.
