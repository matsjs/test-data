"IFRS" means international accounting standards within the meaning of IAS Regulation 1606/2002.
"Intercompany Loan Pledges" means 1st priority pledges in any intercompany loans from the Issuer (as lender) to any of its Subsidiaries (as borrower) and, if permitted under the Senior Facilities, any intercompany loans from any Subsidiary (as lender) to any Group Company (as borrower).
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Interest Cost.
"Interest Payment Date" means 15 January, 15 April, 15 July and 15 October each year
and the Maturity Date. The first Interest Payment Date is 15 April 2013. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to GAAP.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means 15 January 2013.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
