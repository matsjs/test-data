"Current Liabilities" means the aggregate book value of the Group's liabilities (on a consolidated basis) which are treated as current liabilities in accordance with NGAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer), excluding the current portion of long term debt.
"Disbursement" means release and disbursement of the net proceeds from the Bond issue from the Escrow Account to the Issuer.
"EBITDA" means the Group's aggregate earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (to be calculated on a quarterly basis or a 12-month rolling basis (as die case may be) unless otherwise expressly stated herein) determined in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer).
"Employment Contract" means any charter agreement or other employment contracts in respect of any of the Existing PSVs.
"Equity" means the aggregate book value of the Group's total equity treated as equity in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer).
"Equity Ratio" means the ratio of Equity to Total Assets.
"Escrow Account" means an account(s) denominated in NOK with a bank as may be agreed between the Issuer and the Trustee in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority in favor of the Trustee as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the 1st priority pledge over the Escrow Account (according to Norwegian law), where the bank operating the account has waived any set off rights.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clauses 15.1 -15.9.
