2.3 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of
NOK 150,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one hundred and fifty million).
(b)
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between
themselves.
(c)
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Vestland Offshore Invest AS
Callable Bond Issue 2013/2016".
