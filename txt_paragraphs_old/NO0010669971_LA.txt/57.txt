11.5 Interest in the event of late payment
(a)
In the event that any amount due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance
Document is not made on the relevant due date, the unpaid amount shall bear
interest from the due date at an interest rate equivalent to the interest rate
according to Clause 9 plus five per cent, per annum.
(b) The interest charged under this Clause 11.5 shall be added to the defaulted amount on each respective Interest Payment Date relating thereto until the defaulted amount has been repaid in full.
