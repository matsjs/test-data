The Bondholders, the Bond Trustee and - provided the Bonds are listed -
representatives of the Exchange, have the right to attend the Bondholders'
Meeting. The chairman may grant access to the meeting to other parties, unless
the Bondholders' Meeting decides otherwise. Bondholders may attend by a
representative holding proxy. Bondholders have the right to be assisted by an
advisor. In case of dispute the chairman shall decide who may attend the
Bondholders' Meeting and vote for the Bonds.
