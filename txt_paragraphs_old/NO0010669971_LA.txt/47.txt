10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.2 Call Option
(a)
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part at any time (Call
Option) with settlement date at any time from and including the date falling 2
years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at 103% of
par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
(b) Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
