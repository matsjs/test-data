13.17 Financial Support Restriction The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Subsidiary will, grant or permit to subsist any loans, make any investment or contribution, give any guarantees or otherwise voluntarily assume any financial liability, whether actual or contingent, ("Financial Support"), to or for the benefit of any Group Company or any third party (not being a

13.18 Dividend Restrictions The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind, repurchase of shares, provide loans or make other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer and repayment of shareholders loans), or other distributions or transactions implying a transfer of value to its shareholders.
