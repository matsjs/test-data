13.9 Transactions
The Issuer shall not engage, and procure that no member of the Group engage, directly o f indirectly in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets o r the rendering o f any service), except pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Group's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
