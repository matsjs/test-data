EVENTS OF DEFAULT
17.1 Non-payment
The Issuer fails to fulfill any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full within five Business Days following the original due date.
17.2 Breach of other obligations
Any Obligor does not comply with any provision pursuant to this Bond Agreement o r any other Finance Oocument, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable of being remedied and is remedied within ten Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
