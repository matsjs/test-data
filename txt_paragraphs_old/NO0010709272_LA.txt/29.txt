"Security" means any encumbrance, mortgage, charge, pledge, lien or other security interest securing any obligation of any person o r any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security and Covenant Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 20.2.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
