18.4 (a)
Repeated Bondholders' Meeting
If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause 18.3(d), a repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the same matters. The attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders' Meeting shall be specified in the summons for the repeated Bondholders' Meeting.
(b)
The procedures and resolutions as set out in 18.2 and 18.3 above also apply for a repeated Bondholders'
meeting, however, a valid resolution may be passed at a repeated Bondholders' Meeting even though
less than half (1/2) of the Voting Bonds are represented.
