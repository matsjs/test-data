The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in June 2011 (the "Call Period") to and including the Interest Payment Date in December 2011 at a redemption price of 115% of par value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount (the "Call Option").
Each Bondholder may exercise its Conversion Right after a Call Option has been exercised, provided that such exercise of Conversion Right has been exercised no later than 10 Business Days following the Issuer's notice of exercise pursuant to Clause 10.2.3 below.
10.2.2
The Call Option may be exercised for any amount, provided however that it must be exercised for (i) a total amount of minimum of 50% of the originally issued Bonds in order to obtain the release of Security Interest in respect one of the Vessel#3 or VesselM or (ii) and a total amount equivalent to all the then Outstanding Bonds at the time the Call Option is exercised in order to obtain the release of Security Interest in respect of both Vessel#3 and Vessel#4.
