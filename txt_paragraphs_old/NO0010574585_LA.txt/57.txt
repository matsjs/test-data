(c) Maintenance, operation and management o f the Vessels The Issuer shall procure that the Vessels are kept in a good and safe condition and state of repair consistent with standard ownership and management practice.
The Issuer shall procure compliance with all relevant environmental laws and regulations and all other laws and regulations relating to the Vessels, their ownership, operation and management.
The Issuer shall ensure that any technical managers retained for the Vessels shall be reputable and professional.
