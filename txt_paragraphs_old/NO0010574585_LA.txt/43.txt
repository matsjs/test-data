The Issuer may at any time during the term of the Bond Issue, and provided that 90 per cent or more of the Bonds issued on the Issue Date shall have been redeemed or converted into Shares, call the Outstanding Bonds (the "Clean-up Call") at par value (100%) plus accrued interest.

For the avoidance of doubt, each Bondholder may within the Exercise Period, and provided that the conversion conditions in Attachment 2 are otherwise fulfilled, elect to exercise its Conversion Right after having received the Issuer's Clean-up Call notice.
10.4 Change o f control
If a Change of Control has occurred, each Bondholder shall at any time during the Change of Control Conversion Period have the option to (at each Bondholder's discretion) require early redemption of its Bonds ("Put Option") at 101 % of par value plus accrued interest.
The respective Bondholder must notify the Paying Agent within the Change of Control Conversion Period of its election of whether or not to (a) require early redemption, or (b) convert the Bonds to Shares.
