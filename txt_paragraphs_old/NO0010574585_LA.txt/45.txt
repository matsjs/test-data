10.5.2
Mandatory Redemption shall be settled by prepayment made on the earlier of (i) the date falling ninety (90) days after the Total Loss Date, and (ii) the date of receipt by the Bond Trustee of the proceeds of insurance or requisition for title relating to such Total Loss.

10.5.3
Mandatory Redemption shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least fifteen Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption.
