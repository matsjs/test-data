Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Bonds to the Issuer's Escrow Account will be subject to the
Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it, on
the Issue Date:
(a) this Bond Agreement duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary coiporate resolutions necessary to (i) issue the Bonds, (ii) execute the Finance Documents, and (iii) issue the required number of Shares to any Bondholder exercising any of its Conversion Rights (as defined in Attachment 2 Clause A);
(c) if not included in the resolutions referred to in sub-paragraph (b) above a power of attorney from the Issuer and the Vessel Owners to its representatives for the execution of the relevant Finance Documents;
(d) certified copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for the Issuer, evidencing that it is validly existing and (ii) Articles of Association of the Issuer;
