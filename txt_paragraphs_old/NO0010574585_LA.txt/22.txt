The Bond Issue will be described as "14 per cent Mosvold Supply Pic Senior Secured Convertible Callable Bond Issue 2010/2012".
The tenor o f the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed for the financing of the second installments on Vessel#4 and operating cost and finance costs (including among other things supervision, management and interest costs).
Convertible provisions

Listing The Issuer is under no obligations to list the Bonds on an Exchange, but shall have the right to list the Bonds if it so desires.
If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
