Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to die settlement date of the Call Option. The Call Option may be exercised several times.
10.2.4 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
10.2.5
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date.
10.2.6
Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds. The Bond Trustee shall procure the release of all Security Interest in relation to a Vessel in accordance with clause 10.2.2 above on the settlement date for the Call Option.
