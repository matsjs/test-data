14.2
The expenses and fees payable to the Bond Trustee (and/or the Security Agent, as the case may be)
shall be paid by the Issuer and are set forth in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond
Trustee. Fees and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee which, due to the Issuer's insolvency or
similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the
payments to the Bondholders.
14.3 The Issuer shall cover all public fees in connection with the Bonds and the Finance Documents. Any public fees levied on the trade of Bonds in the secondary market shall be paid by the Bondholders, unless otherwise decided by law or regulation, and the Issuer is not responsible for reimbursing any such fees.
