The Issuer shall not carry out any merger where the Issuer is not the surviving entity or any liquidation of the Issuer.
a)
any merger (or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving the
consolidation of assets and obligations) of the Issuer or such other Group Company with
any other Person (other than a Group Company); or
b)
any demerger (or a corporate reorganisation having the same or equivalent effect) of
the Issuer or such other Group Company;
if an Event of Default is continuing or would occur as a result of such merger, business combination, corporate reorganisation, demerger (or a corporate reorganisation having the same or equivalent effect).
