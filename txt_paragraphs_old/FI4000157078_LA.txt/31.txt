8.3.3
Redemption in accordance with Clause 8.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than
fifteen (15) Business Days' notice to the Noteholders and the Agent. Any such notice is
irrevocable but may, at the Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent.
Upon expiry of such notice and the fulfilment of the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is
bound to redeem the Notes in full at the applicable amounts.
