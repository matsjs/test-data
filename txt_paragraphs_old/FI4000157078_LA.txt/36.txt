9.1.2
The Issuer shall immediately notify the Noteholders and the Agent upon becoming aware of the occurrence of a Change of Control Event. Such notice may be given in advance of the occurrence of a Change of Control Event and be conditional upon the occurrence of such Change of Control Event if a definitive agreement is in place providing for a Change of Control Event.
9.1.3
When the financial statements and other information are made available to the Noteholders
pursuant to Clause 9.1.1, the Issuer shall send copies of such financial statements and other
information to the Agent.
9.1.4
The Issuer shall together with the financial statements submit to the Agent a compliance certificate in the form of Appendix 1 hereto (i) setting out calculations and figures as to compliance with Clause 10.6 (Financial undertakings), (ii) containing a confirmation that no Event of Default has occurred (or if an Event of Default has occurred, what steps have been taken to remedy it, and (iii) attaching copies of any notices sent to the Relevant Market.
