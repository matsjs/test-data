Listing
The Loan will be applied for listing on Oslo Børs or Oslo Børs ASA's Alternative Bond Market (ABM.). If the Loan is listed on Oslo Børs, the Loan may be transferred to ABM without a mandatory Bondholders' meeting and without a mandatory repurchase offer of the Loan to the Bondholders.
