2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 100,000,000 (Norwegian Kroner one hundred millions) through a private placement directed to a limited number of Norwegian investors and international institutional investors.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN European Opportunity AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2011/2013".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001061424.1.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed for the repurchase of the Citibank Loan Facility.
