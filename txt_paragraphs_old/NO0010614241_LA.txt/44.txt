10. Maturity of the Bonds and Call Option 10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, aud shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(a)
at any time from Issue Date to and included the Interest Payment Date in June 2012 at
102% of par value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(b) at any time thereafter at 100% of par value plus accrued interest on redeemed amount.
