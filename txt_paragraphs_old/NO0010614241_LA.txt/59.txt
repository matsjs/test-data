15. Events of Default 15.1 The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the
(a) Non-payment
The Issuer fails to fulfill any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure will be remedied, and payment in full is made, within 5 - five - Business Days following the original due date.
