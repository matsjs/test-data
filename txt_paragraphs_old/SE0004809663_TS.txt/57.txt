NOTEHOLDERS' MEETING
Each of (i) the Issuer, (ii) the Agent, and (iii) a Noteholder (or Noteholders) representing at
least ten (10) per cent, of the Adjusted Nominal Amount, may at any time call for a
meeting among the Noteholders, the Issuer and the Agent (a "Noteholders' Meeting").
Such request shall be made in writing to the Agent.
14.2
The Noteholders' Meeting may, subject to the requirements set out in Clause 13
{Noteholders' consent), decide upon any issue or matter in relation to the Finance
Documents.
