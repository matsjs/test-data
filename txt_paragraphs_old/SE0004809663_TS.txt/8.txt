"EBITDA" means operational earnings of the Group, before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortisation (without double counting) for any 12 months period ending on the last day of the period covered by the financial statements delivered pursuant to Clause 10.1 (a) or (b), calculated in accordance with the Accounting Principles. For the purpose of calculating EBITDA, entities acquired or disposed of during a measurement period will be included or excluded (as applicable) for the entirety of that measurement period.
"Final Maturity Date" means the date falling five (5) years after the First Issue Date.
"Finance Documents" means these Terms and Conditions and any other document designated by the Issuer and the Agent (on behalf of itself and the Noteholders) as a Finance Document.
"Financial Indebtedness" means:
(a) moneys borrowed (including under bank financing);
(b) the amount of any liability under any finance leases (a lease which in the accounts for the Group is treated as an asset and a corresponding liability, however not including current or future leases, which as of the date hereof are considered as not being financial leases);
