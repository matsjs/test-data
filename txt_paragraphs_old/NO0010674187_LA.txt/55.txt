The Issuer shall not to create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for:
any Security contemplated by the Finance Documents;
(ii) any Security contemplated by the Senior Bank Facility;
(iii) any Security arising by operation of law or in the ordinary course of business or operation of the Rig (including collateral in connection with credit purchases of goods and services);
(iv) any ship repairer's or outfitter's possessory lien in respect of the Rig;
(v) any Security on the Rig for master's, officer's or crew's wages outstanding in the ordinary course of its trading which are not overdue;
(vi) any Security on the Rig for salvage;
(vii) any contractor or subcontractor's possessory lien; and
(viii) any Security arising by way of set off or other standard netting, cash management or account-zeroing arrangements in connection with the Issuer's banking arrangements.
