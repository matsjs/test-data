Insurance
(i) The Issuer shall procure that the Rigisinsured againstsuch risk, including but limited to, hull and machinery, hull interest,freight interest, Loss of Hire, war risks (including acts of terrorism, war risks, P&I and piracy) and protection & indemnity (including maximum cover for pollution liability as normally adopted by the industry for similar rigs), in such amounts as set out in paragraph (ii) below, and placed or entered with such reputable insurers, brokers or P&I clubs and on such terms and conditions as the Issuer from time to time may approve.

(ii) The hull and machinery insurance shall be at least equal to 125% of the aggregate of the Bond Issue and the Senior Bank Facility, Further, the aggregate insurable value of the Rig shall be at least equal to the higher of the Rig's market value and 120% of the aggregate of the outstanding amounts under the Finance Documents and the Senior Bank Facility, and the hull and machinery insurance shall at all times cover at least 80% of the insurable value.
(iii) The Issuer shall procure that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) is noted as second priority mortgagee in the insurance contracts, together with the confirmation from the underwriters to the Bond Trustee thereof that the notice of assignment with regards to the insurances and the loss payable clauses are noted in the insurance contracts and that standard letters of undertaking are executed by the insurers.
(iv) Within reasonable time prior to the expiry date of the relevant insurances, the Issuer shall procure the delivery to the Bond Trustee of a certificate from the insurance broker(s) through whom the Insurances referred to in (i) have been renewed and taken out in respect of the Rig with insurance values as required by b), that such Insurances are hi full force and effect and that the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) has been noted by the relevant insurers.
(v) If any of the insurances referred to in (i) have been taken out on conditions other than the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan of 1996 version 2010 or Nordic Marine Insurance Plan of 2013 (as any of them may be amended from time to time) and/or form part of a fleet cover, the Issuer shall procure that the insurers shall undertake to the Bond Trustee that they shall neither set-off against any claims in respect of the Rig any premiums due in respect of other rig under such fleet cover or any premiums due for other insurances, nor cancel this insurance for reason of non-payment of premiums for other rig under such fleet cover or of premiums for such other insurances, and shall undertake to issue a separate policy in respect of the Rig if and when so requested by the Bond Trustee.
(vi) The Issuer shall procure that the Rig always is employed in conformity with the terms of the instruments of insurances (including any warranties expressed or implied therein) and comply with such requirements as to extra premium or otherwise as the insurers may prescribe.
