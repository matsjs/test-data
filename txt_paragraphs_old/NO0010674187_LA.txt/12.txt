"Mortgage" means a Norwegian ship mortgage on second priority, ranking behind the mortgage granted as Security for the obligations under the Senior Bank Facility, over the Rig in the amount of USD 170 million.
"NOR" means the Norwegian ordinary ship registry.
"Outstanding Bonds" means the Bonds not redeemed or otherwise discharged.
"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to acts as its paying agent in the Securities Registry with respect to the Bonds.
"Payment Date" means a date for payment of principal or interest under this Bond Agreement.
"Pledge of Machinery and Plant" means a pledge on second priority, ranking behind the pledge granted as Security for obligations under the Senior Bank Facility, of the Issuers machinery and plant (No.: "driftstilbehør").
"Pre-Utilisation Security" means (i) the Mortgage, (ii) the Assignment of Earnings, (iii) the Assignment of Insurances, (iv) the Account Pledges, (v) the Charter Assignments, (vi) the Assignment of Receivables, and (vii) the Pledge of Machinery and Plant.
"Pre-Issue Security" means the Escrow Account Pledge and the Interest Retention Account Pledge.
