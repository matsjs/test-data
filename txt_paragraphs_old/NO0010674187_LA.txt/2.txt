1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder''s account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Account Pledges" means a pledge on second priority, ranking behind the pledge granted as Security for the obligations under the Senior Bank Facility, over the Earnings Account and the Equity Escrow Account, and any other bank accounts of the Issuer from time to time in respect of a pledge on first priority in favour of the Senior Agent shall be granted.
"Accounts" means the Escrow Account, the Interest Retention Account, the Earnings Account, the Equity Escrow Account and any accounts for operating expenses or other accounts related to the operations of the Rig held by the Issuer and/or a Charterer from tune to time.
"Additional Equity" means a minimum of NOK 285 million in new cash equity.
"Additional Security" means all Security provided in accordance with the provisions of Clause 8.2.
"Assignment of Earnings" means an assignment on second priority behind the obligations under the Senior Bank Facility of all present and future earnings and monies due in respect of the Rig and the operation thereof and services rendered in relation thereto to the Issuer or any member of the Group, including under any such earnings deriving from the Charter Contract.
