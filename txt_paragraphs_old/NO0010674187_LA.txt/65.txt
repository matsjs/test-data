d.
Class
The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig's class is maintained at the highest applicable class with Det Norske Veritas, Lloyds Register of Shipping, Bureau Veritas, American Bureau of Shipping or another first class, internationally recognised classification society, acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and not change the class nor classification society for the Rig and upon request provide the Bond Trustee with all information regarding the Rig's class position (including but not limited to information on extensions and conditions of class).
e.
Operations in accordance with laws etc.
The Issuer shall at all times ensure that the Rig is operated in accordance with any laws, regulations (environmental or otherwise, including but not limited to the ISM Code and the ISPS Code, where applicable), administrative decisions and/or other public authorities as applicable from time to time and jurisdiction to jurisdiction.
