"Group" means the Issuer and its Subsidiaries, and a "Group Company" means the Issuer or any of its Subsidiaries.
"Interest Retention Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer, into which the Issuer shall deposit certain funds for use of payment of interest under the Bond Issue.

"Interest Retention Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Interest Retention Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Interest Payment Date" means 3 April and 3 October each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and (if relevant) consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to GAAP, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means 3 April 2013.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
