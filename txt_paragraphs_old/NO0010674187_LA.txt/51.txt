Continuation o f business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
f.
Disposal o f business
The Issuer shall not, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets or operations, unless:
(i) the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions customary for such transactions; and
(ii) such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
