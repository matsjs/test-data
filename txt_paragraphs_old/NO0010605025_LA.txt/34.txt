6.2 Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent
6.2.1 The amount in the Escrow Account shall only be (i) used according to the Purpose of the Bond Issue as set out in Clause 2.3 (Purpose and utilization) or, alternatively, (ii) returned to the Bondholders in the event that the Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent are not satisfied for any reason at (a) par value if such failure to comply with the Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent is beyond the control of the Issuer, and (b) otherwise at the rate applicable as set out in Clause 10.4 (Mandatory Redemption).
