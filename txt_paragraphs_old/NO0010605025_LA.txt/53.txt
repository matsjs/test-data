10.4.2 Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount) on the earlier of (i) the date falling one hundred and eighty (180) days after the Total Loss Date, and (ii) the date the insurance proceeds are available to the Issuer.
10.4.4 On the settlement date of a mandatory redemption, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest acciued up to and including the settlement date of the Mandatory Redemption.
10.4.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
11. PAYMENTS
11.1 Payment mechanics
11.1.1 The Issuer shall pay all amounts due to the Bondholders under the Bonds and this Bond Agreement by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in connection with its securities account in the Securities Register.
11.1.2 Payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank which holds the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, but if the paying bank and the receiving bank are the same, payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, see however Clause 11.2 (Currency).
