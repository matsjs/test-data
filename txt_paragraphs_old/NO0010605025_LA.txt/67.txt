(f)
Amendments to the Charter and the Management Agreement
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that the Owning Company or Bareboat Charterer (if party to a Charter) shall not, pass any amendments, supplements, variations or waiver of any material terms of any Charter with a duration of more than six (6) months or the Management Agreement to be made if any such amendment, supplement, variation or waiver would have a Material Adverse Effect.
(g) Inspections The Issuer shall permit and shall procure that the Owning Company or Bareboat Charterer (if party to a Charter) or any charterer shall permit one person appointed by the Bond Trustee to inspect the Vessel for the account of the Issuer upon the Bond Trustee giving prior written notice. The Bond Trustee may request such inspections (i) once a year; and (ii) hi any event upon the occurrence of an Event of Default. The cost of such inspection shall be covered by the Issuer.
