Restrictions on indebtedness
The Bareboat Charterer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than (i) as is incurred under the Finance Documents; (ii) counter indemnities in respect of guarantees provided under the ordinary operations of the Bareboat Charterer; (iii) derivative transactions as referred to in paragraph (g) of the definition of Financial Indebtedness entered into in the ordinaiy course of business of the Bareboat Charterer; or (iv) as approved by the Bond Trustee.
(k) Intercompany Loan Agreement
(i) The Issuer shall ensure and shall procure that the Owing Company ensures that the Intercompany Loan Agreement is in place at the time of the disbursement of the funds from the Escrow Account pursuant to Clause 6.2 (Pre-Disbursement Conditions Precedent) and in accordance with the Purpose of the Bond Issue.
(ii) The interest on and amortization of the Intercompany Loan shall be equal to the interest and amortization on the Bond Issue. Any repayments and/or prepayments of the Intercompany Loans shall be used by the Issuer to repay or prepay (as the case may be) the Bonds and so that the outstanding amount under the Intercompany Loan shall be equal to the outstanding amount in respect of the Bonds from time to time.
