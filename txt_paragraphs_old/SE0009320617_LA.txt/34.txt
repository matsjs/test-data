12.2 Other Obligations
The Issuer does not comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out under Clause 12.1 (Non-Payment) above, unless the non compliance is capable of remedy and the Issuer has remedied the failure within fifteen (15) Business Days of the earlier of (i) a request by the Agent and (ii) the Issuer becoming aware of the non-compliance.
12.3 Cross-Acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness of a Group Company is not paid when due as extended by any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this Clause 12.3 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness that has fallen due is less than SEK 10,000,000 and provided that it does not apply to any Financial Indebtedness owed to a Group Company.
