"Decisive Influence" means a person having, as a result of an agreement or through the ownership of shares or interests in another person (directly or indirectly):
(a) a majority of the voting rights in that other person; or
(b) a right to elect or remove a majority of the members of the board of directors of that other person.
"Default Notice" means a written notice to the Issuer as described in Clause 14.2 (Acceleration o f the Bonds).
"Default Repayment Date" means the settlement date set out by the Bond Trustee in a Default Notice requesting early redemption of the Bonds.
"Defeasance Account" means the account in a financial institution acceptable to the Bond Trustee to which the net cash proceeds from the Bond Issue will be transferred upon release of funds from the Escrow Account, where the Issuer will deposit an amount sufficient for the payment of principal and interest on the Existing Bond Loan to its maturity date (including, to the extent relevant, any premium payable upon exercise of a call option), which the bond trustee (under the Existing Bond Loan) may apply towards any amount payable by the Issuer under any finance document (under the Existing Bond Loan) on the due date for the relevant payment until all obligations of the Issuer and all amounts outstanding under such finance documents are repaid and discharged in full.
