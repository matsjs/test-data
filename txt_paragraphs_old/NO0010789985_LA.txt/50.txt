6.2 Settlement in cash or in kind
(a) The Bonds shall be settled in cash, and/or in kind by delivery of Existing Bonds (when receipt of subscriptions from the bondholders under the Existing Bonds in accordance with the Existing Bondholders' Roll-Over).
(b) Applicants delivering Existing Bonds will receive the accrued interest on the Existing Bonds up until the Issue Date and the applicable buy back premium of 2.0% (the "Buy Back Premium") on the terms set out in the terms of the Existing Bonds, payable in cash at the Issue Date (subject to reimbursement of such premium in the event of Mandatory Redemption).
(c) Bonds issued in cash will be issued with ISIN 0010789985, which will be the surviving ISIN for the Bond Issue. Bonds issued in kind will be issued with a temporary ISIN NO 0010789993 ("Temporary Bonds"). The Temporary Bonds will be merged with the Bonds in connection with disbursement of funds (and release of the Existing Bonds) from the Escrow Accounts. The VPS and the Bond Trustee are authorised to carry out the aforesaid in the best practical way.
