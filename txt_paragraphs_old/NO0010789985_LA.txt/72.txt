13.14 Dividends The Issuer shall not make any Distributions.
13.15 Incurrence Test (a) The Incurrence Test is met if:
(i) Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA is less than 3.0; and
(ii) Interest Cover Ratio is above 3.0.
(b) The calculation of the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to EBITDA shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no earlier than one month prior to the event relevant for the application of the Incurrence Test. The Net Interest Bearing Debt shall be measured on the relevant testing date so determined, but include the undrawn commitment of any existing Financial Indebtedness which is included in the basis for determining Net Interest Bearing Debt (other than the Revolving Credit Facility) and the new Financial Indebtedness and the Finance Charges related thereto, provided it is an interest bearing obligation (however, any cash balance resulting from the incurrence of such new Financial Indebtedness shall not reduce the Net Interest Bearing Debt) in respect of which the Incurrence Test is applied. The EBITDA shall be calculated as set out below.
(c) The calculation of the Interest Coverage Ratio shall be made for the Relevant Period ending on the last day of the period covered by the most recent Financial Report, but adjusted as set out below.
