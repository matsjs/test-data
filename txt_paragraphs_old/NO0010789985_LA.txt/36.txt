"Temporary Bonds" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 6.2 (Settlement in cash or in kind) paragraph (c).
"Transaction Security Documents" means, collectively, the Escrow Pledges and all of the documents which shall be executed or delivered pursuant to Clause 2.5 (Transaction Security) expressed to create any Security by the relevant grantor thereof in respect of the Issuer's obligations under any of the Finance Documents.
"Voluntary Partial Prepayment" means a voluntary partial prepayment as set out in Clause 10.6 (Voluntary Partial Prepayment).
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds less the Issuer's Bonds and a Voting Bond shall mean any single one of those Bonds.
"Written Resolution" means a written (or electronic) solution for a decision making among the Bondholders, as set out in Clause 15.5 (Written Resolutions).
