13.10
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will sell or otherwise dispose of assets or operations in any Group Company, outside the ordinary course of business, to any Person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries or where the Issuer (directly or indirectly) maintains the same pro rata ownership share in that Group Company before or after such disposal, unless such transaction constitutes a Permitted Disposal.
13.11
Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.12 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
13.13
Designation of Material Group Companies
(a) The Issuer shall nominate as Material Group Companies such Group Companies as are necessary to ensure that the Issuer and the Material Group Companies in aggregate account for at least 80% of the Group's EBITDA.
