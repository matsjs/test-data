"Security Agent Agreement" means any agreement whereby the Security Agent is appointed to act as such in the interest of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders).
(a) an agreement between the Security Agent, the Issuer and any other Obligor, subordinating the claims between each of the Obligors to the Secured Obligations on terms acceptable to the Security Trustee (the "Subordination Terms"), or
(b) the Intercreditor Agreement, if the Subordination Terms are included therein and it is entered into by the relevant Obligors.
"Subsidiary" means a company over which another company has Decisive Influence.
"Summons" means the call for a Bondholders' Meeting or a Written Resolution as the case may be.
"Structural Intercompany Loan" means any loan or receivable granted by the Issuer to
(i) any of the other Obligors; or
(ii) to any other Group Company where the shares of such Group Company are subject to a share pledge in favour of the Secured Parties,
provided for both (i) and (ii) above that: (A) such loan or receivable has an amount exceeding NOK 10,000,000 (or equivalent in other currencies) per loan; or (B) such loan or receivable will, when taken together with all the other loans granted by the Issuer to that Group Company, exceed an amount of NOK 25,000,000 (or equivalent) in aggregate to that Group Company, however so that no claims arising out of any cash pool arrangement shall fall within the definition of Structural Intercompany Loan.
