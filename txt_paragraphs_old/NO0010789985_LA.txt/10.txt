"Escrow Account" means an account (with a Norwegian bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee) in the name of the Issuer where the bank has waived any set-off rights, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means the pledge over the Escrow Account, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Accounts" means together the Bond Escrow Account and the Escrow Account.
"Escrow Pledges" means together the Escrow Account Pledge and the Bond Escrow Account Pledge.
