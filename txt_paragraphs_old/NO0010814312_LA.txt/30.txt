"USD" means United States Dollars, being the legal currency of the United States of America.
"Working Capital Facility" means a working capital facility a maximum commitment of USD 5,000,000 (or its equivalent in other currencies) consisting of one or several facilities (which also may be utilised for the purpose of guarantees, letters of credit, leasing or any other ancillary facilities) from one or more lender (ranking pari passu between each other) to the Issuer as borrower, and where:
(a) the Issuer and any other Group Companies may apply amounts borrowed by the Issuer under the Working Capital Facility towards general corporate and working capital purposes of the Group;
(b) all revolving facility commitments under the Working Capital Facility shall are subject to simultaneous clean down once every 12 months. A minimum period of 6 months shall elapse between each such clean downs, and the duration of the clean downs shall be no less than three (3) consecutive Business Days.
(c) all amounts outstanding under the Working Capital Finance Documents may be secured on first priority over the same security assets as covered by the Pre-Disbursement Security (which shall constitute senior obligations of the Issuer); and
(d) all amounts outstanding under the Working Capital Finance Documents may (together with any Permitted Hedging Obligations) rank super senior to the Bonds with respect to any Enforcement Proceeds, pursuant to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement.
"Working Capital Facility Creditors" means the finance parties under the Working Capital Finance Documents.
"Working Capital Finance Documents" means the agreement(s) for the Working Capital Credit Facility, and any guarantees, letter of credits, leasing agreements or other ancillary facilities entered into in relation thereto.
"Written Resolution" means a written (or electronic) solution for a decision making among the Bondholders, as set out in Clause 16.5 (Written Resolutions).
