Subsidiary Distributions
The Issuer shall not permit any of its Subsidiaries to create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right of any Subsidiary to pay dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders, other than permitting to subsist such contractual obligations which are not reasonably likely to prevent the Issuer from complying with its payment obligations under the Bond Terms.

Minimum Liquidity
(a) The Issuer shall procure that the Group shall from (and including) 31 March 2019 at all times retain Minimum Liquidity in excess of NOK 20,000,000.
(b) The Minimum Liquidity shall be tested at the last day of each calendar quarter ("Testing Date") (the first time on 31 March 2019). Compliance with the Minimum Liquidity shall be certified in a Compliance Certificate with reference to the preceding quarter, to be delivered to the Bond Trustee simultaneously with each Financial Report.
(c) Upon a breach o f the Minimum Liquidity, the Group shall have a 60 days period (commencing on the Testing Date) to cure the Minimum Liquidity shortfall, before such breach shall constitute an Event of Default. A breach of the Minimum Liquidity covenant may be cured either through receipt by the Issuer o f net cash proceeds from any person (other than a Group Company) in the form o f new equity, a Shareholder Loan or as later generated in the Group's ordinary course o f business.
