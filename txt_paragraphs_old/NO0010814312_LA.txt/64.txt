13.13
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.14
Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of
(i) shares in any Guarantor; or
(ii) all or substantial part of its assets or operations if such disposal may have a Material Adverse Effect.
