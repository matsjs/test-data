"Intercompany Loan" means any loan made by a Group Company to another Group Company (not including any Financial Indebtedness under any cash pooling arrangement of the Group) where (i) the term of the loan is over six months (the term being determined at the sole discretion of the Issuer) and (ii) the amount is in excess of USD 100,000 (or the equivalent in other currencies), and which pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement shall be fully subordinated to the Secured Obligations.
"Insolvent" means that a person:
(a) is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due;
(b) suspends making payments on any of its debts generally; or
(c) is otherwise considered insolvent or bankrupt within the meaning of the relevant bankruptcy legislation of the jurisdiction which can be regarded as its center of main interest as such term is understood pursuant to Council Regulation (EC) no. 1346/2000 on insolvency proceedings (as amended).
"Intercreditor Agreement" means an intercreditor agreement acceptable to the Bond Trustee entered (or acceded) into by, inter alia, the Bond Trustee (on the behalf of the Bondholders), the Issuer, each of the Guarantors, each creditors for Intercompany Loans and Shareholder Loans and each Working Capital Facility Creditor (when relevant).
"Interest Payment Date" means the last day of each Interest Period, the first Interest Payment Date being 30 April 2018 and the last Interest Payment Date being the Maturity Date.
"Interest Period" means, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Business Day Convention, each period between 31 January, 30 April, 31 July and 31 October each year, provided however that an Interest Period shall not extend beyond the Maturity Date.
