3. Purpose of the Bonds
(a) payment of Transaction Costs (including, for the avoidance of doubt, any stamp tax payable in relation to the issuance of new mortgage certificates in the Properties);
(b) towards refinancing of the Existing Bridge Loan which may be made either (i) in cash or (ii) by way of conversion of existing loans under the Existing Bridge Loan to Bonds; and
(c) the remainder, for general corporate purposes of the Issuer.
