2. Status of the Bonds
(a) The NOK Bonds are denominated in Norwegian Kroner, the SEK Bonds are denominated in Swedish Kronor and the USD Bonds are denominated in United States Dollar and each Bond is constituted by these Terms and Conditions. The Issuer undertakes to make payments in relation to the Bonds and to comply with these Terms and Conditions.
(b) By subscribing for Bonds, each initial Bondholder agrees that the Bonds shall benefit from and be subject to the Finance Documents and by acquiring Bonds, each subsequent Bondholder confirms such agreement.
