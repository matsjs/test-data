13.9 Dealings with Related Parties
13.10 Environmental compliance and claims
(a) The Issuer shall:
(i) comply with all Environmental Laws, including in relation to human health and conditions on workplace;
(ii) obtain, maintain and ensure compliance with all requisite Environmental Permits; and
(iii) implement procedures to monitor compliance with and to prevent liability under any Environmental Law, in each case where failure to do so has or is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
(b) The Issuer shall, promptly upon becoming aware of the same, inform the Trustee of any claim, proceeding or investigation in respect of any such Environmental Law against any member of the Group which is current, pending or threatened where which, if determined against that member of the Group, has or is reasonably likely to have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.11 Enforcement Restrictions Agreement
The Issuer shall prior to incurring any Permitted Debt or granting any Permitted Security that are subject to the Enforcement Action Restrictions, deliver a copy of a duly executed Enforcement Restriction Agreement to the Trustee, together with relevant corporate authorization documents.
