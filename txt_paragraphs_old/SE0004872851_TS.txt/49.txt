Mergers and de-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving consolidating its assets and obligations with any company or entity.
None of Promyzer or Orc shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving consolidating its assets and obligations with any company or entity, except for a merger with a wholly-owned Group Company with either Promyzer or Orc as surviving entity.
None of the Issuer, Promyzer or Orc shall carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving splitting it into two or more separate companies or entities.
