Notwithstanding Clauses 14.3 to 14.5, any decision that adversely affects some but not all of the Noteholders (in their capacity as Noteholders) shall require the consent of all Noteholders that are adversely affected.
Notwithstanding Clauses 14.3 to 14.6, if a WCF Provider is a beneficiary of the Share Pledge and any Loan Pledge together with the Secured Parties, any action to be taken by the Agent under the Share Pledge Agreements or the Loan Pledge Agreements (including, but not limited to, an enforcement of the Share Pledge or any Loan Pledge) may require the consent of the WCF Provider, as stipulated in the relevant Share Pledge Agreement or Loan Pledge Agreement.
Notes held by a Group Company or an Affiliate shall not entitle to participation in decisions in respect of matters requiring Noteholders consent or any voting rights at a Noteholders' Meeting.
A Noteholder holding more than one Note need not use all its votes or cast all the votes to which it is entitled in the same way.
