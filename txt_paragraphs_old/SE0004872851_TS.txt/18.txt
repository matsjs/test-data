2.5
Each Subsequent Note issued in accordance with Clause 2.4 shall entitle its respective
holder to Interest only from the end of the previous Interest Period for which Interest has
been paid, but shall otherwise have the same rights as the Initial Notes.
2.6
The Notes constitute direct and unconditional obligations of the Issuer and shall at all times
rank pari passu and without any preference among them.
