"Redemption Date" means the date on which the relevant Notes are to be repaid or repurchased in accordance with Clause 10 (Repayment and Repurchase o f the Notes).
"Regulated Market" means any regulated market (reglerad marknad) (as defined in the Securities Market Act (lag (2007:528) om vardepappersmarknaden)).
"Repaid Percentage" means the percentage of the Nominal Amount of each Note repaid pursuant to Clause 10.4 (Voluntary partial repayment).
"Secured Obligations" means all present and future obligations and liabilities of the Issuer to the Secured Parties under the Finance Documents (including, for the avoidance of doubt, all obligations and liabilities under the Notes) and the Agency Agreement.
"Secured Parties" means the Noteholders and the Agent.
"Security" means a mortgage, charge, pledge, lien, security assignment or other security interest securing any obligation of any person, or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Shareholder Debt" means any Financial Indebtedness owed by the Issuer to the Parent.
"Shareholder Loan" means (i) the shareholder loan in the maximum amount of SEK 200,000,000 (excluding interest) granted by the Parent to the Issuer on the First Issue Date, in connection with the Parent's assumption of all liabilities under the Swedbank Bridge Facility, and (ii) the subordinated shareholder loan agreement dated 30 January 2012 in a maximum amount of SEK 620,000,000 (exluding interest) granted by the Parent to the Issuer, in each case on the terms set out in the relevant Shareholder Loan Agreement .
