Quorum at a Noteholders' Meeting only exists if a Noteholder (or Noteholders)
representing at least twenty (20) per cent. of the Adjusted Nominal Amount attend the
meeting in person or by telephone conference (or appear through duly authorised
representatives).
15.6
If a quorum does not exist at one Noteholders' Meeting, the Agent shall convene a second
Noteholders' Meeting (in accordance with Clause 15.3). The quorum requirement in
Clause 15.5 shall not apply to such second Noteholders' Meeting.
