16.2
A communication from the Agent or the Issuer in accordance with Clause 16.1 shall
include (i) the request by the Issuer or the Agent for consent, approval, instructions or
agreement by the Noteholders, (ii) a description of the reasons for the request, (iii) a form
for replying to the request (with an option to vote yes or no for each request) and (iv) the
stipulated time period within which the Noteholder must reply to the request (such time
period to be at least ten (10) Business Days after the date on which the communication was
dispatched).
