For so long as any Note is outstanding, the Agent shall act with regard only to the interests of the Noteholders and shall not be required to have regard to the interests or to act upon or comply with any direction or request of any other person, other than as explicitly stated in the Finance Documents. However, with respect to the Share Pledge and any Loan Pledge, the Agent shall also represent the WCF Provider(s) in accordance with Clause 9 (Transaction Security) and act with regard only to the interests of the WCF Provider(s) and the Noteholders. The Agent's duties and obligations in relation to the WCF Provider(s) are further set out in the Share Pledge Agreements and any Loan Pledge Agreement.
Duties of the Agent
The Agent shall represent each of the Noteholders in accordance with the Finance Documents, including, inter alia, holding the Share Pledge and any Loan Pledge on behalf of the Noteholders and enforcing the Share Pledge and any Loan Pledge on behalf of the Noteholders. The Agent is always acting with binding effect on behalf of the Noteholders.
The Agent shall carry out its duties under the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement in a reasonable, proficient and professional manner, with reasonable care and skill.
