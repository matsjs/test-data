12.5.2 12.5.3
The restriction set out in Clause 12.5.1 shall not apply to:
(a) Financial Indebtedness in respect of which the Noteholders have given their prior consent;
(b) Financial Indebtedness incurred under the Working Capital Financing for the sole purpose of financing short term working capital needs and guarantees and not to facilitate long term financing needs;
(c) Shareholder Debt (subordinated pursuant to the Subordination Agreement);
(d) derivative transactions (unless for speculative purposes);
(e) Financial Indebtedness between Group Companies;
