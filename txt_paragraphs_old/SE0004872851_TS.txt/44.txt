by way of press release and by publication on the website of the Group:

(b) as soon as the same become available, but in any event within two (2) months after the end of each quarter of its financial year, its unaudited consolidated financial statements and the year-end report (bokslutskommuniké) (when applicable) for such period, except that its unaudited consolidated financial statements for the third quarter in the financial year 2012 shall be made available on or before 19 December 2012;
