Distributions
Subject to Clause 12.7.2, the Issuer shall not, if and for as long as (i) the Consolidated Net Leverage Ratio (calculated as set out in Clause 12.5.3 (applied mutatis mutandis for a distribution), adjusted on a pro forma basis to take into account any proposed distribution) is 3.0:1 or above, (ii) the Interest Cover Ratio (calculated as set out in Clause 12.5.4) is below 2.0:1, or (iii) an Event of Default is continuing make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other similar transactions (including, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer), repay or pay interest under any Shareholder Debt, grant any loans or make other distributions or transfers of value (värdeöverföringar) to the Parent, any Affiliate or other third parties (including management) other than Permitted Costs Payments, the Permitted Distribution and the Permitted Shareholder Loan Repayments which shall at all times be allowed provided that in the case of each Permitted Shareholder Loan Repayment only, (A) such repayment shall not be allowed if an Event of Default is continuing or would be caused by such repayment and (B) such repayment is either (i) funded with proceeds received from an issue or issues of Subsequent Notes before the first anniversary of the First Issue Date or (ii) made by way of set-off as payment of the subscription price for newly issued shares in the Issuer in connection with a new issue against set-off (kvittningsemission).
Notwithstanding Clause 12.7.1, distributions shall not for any financial year exceed 50 per cent of the Issuer's consolidated net profit after tax based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year.
Notwithstanding Clause 12.7.1, the Issuer shall be entitled to give group contributions (koncernbidrag), provided that no cash or other funds are transferred from the Issuer as a result thereof (i.e. the group contributions are merely accounting measures) and provided that such distribution is subsequently converted into a shareholder's contribution to the Issuer as soon as practically possible.
