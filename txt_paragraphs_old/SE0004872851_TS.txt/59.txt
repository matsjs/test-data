12.10 Pari passu ranking
Subject to the WCF Obligations (which rank senior to the payment obligations under the Notes), the Issuer must ensure that its payment obligations under the Finance Documents rank at least pari passu with all its other present and future unsecured payment obligations, except for obligations mandatorily preferred by law applying to companies generally.
12.11 Dealings between Group Companies and related parties
12.11.1
The Issuer shall (and shall ensure that each Group Company will) conduct all dealings with the shareholders of any Group Company (excluding other Group Companies) and/or any Affiliates at arm's-lengths terms.
12.11.2
Notwithstanding Clause 12.5 (Financial Indebtedness), none of Promyzer or Orc shall make any loans or grant any credit to or for the benefit of the Issuer, other than (i) any loans made in order to serve the payments under the Notes, (ii) the inter-company loan already granted by Orc to the Issuer in an amount of SEK 325,000,000 and (iii) the inter company loan already granted by Orc to the Issuer in an amount of SEK 3,611,976. Any loans made in order to serve the payments under the Notes shall as soon as practically possible be netted out against dividends from Promyzer or Orc to the Issuer.
