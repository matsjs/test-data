Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than sixty (60) days following the Total Loss Event redeem 100% of the outstanding bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued but unpaid interest on redeemed amount).
10.4.4
Upon the occurrence of several Mandatory Prepayment Events, the Issuer shall only be obliged to pay one redemption amount. Upon the occurrence of (i) a Total Loss Event and (ii) a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall only pay the lowest applicable redemption amount.
