6.2 Conditions precedent for release from the Escrow Account and/or Additional Escrow Account and release of the relevant part o f the Initial Equity from the Earnings Account.
6.2.1 The net proceeds from the Bond Issue shall only be used in accordance with the purpose set out in Clause 2,3, and any release from the Escrow Account and/or the Additional Escrow Account (and/or release of the relevant part of the Initial Equity from the Earnings Account) (as the case may be) to the Issuer in accordance with Clause 6.2.3, will be subject to customary closing mechanisms as agreed between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee and the Bond Trustee receiving prior to or (as the case may be) no later than on the date of release from the Escrow Account and/or the Additional Escrow Account (and/or release of the relevant part of the Initial Equity from the Earnings Account (as the case may be)) the documents listed below, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
(i)
a duly executed release notice from the Issuer (including a statement
regarding use of funds in accordance with the purpose set out in Clause 2.3
and confirmation of no potential or actual Event of Default);
(ii) copies of necessary corporate resolutions from the Parent, the Guarantor and the Issuer to execute the Pre-Utilisation Security (unless delivered under Clause 6.1);
