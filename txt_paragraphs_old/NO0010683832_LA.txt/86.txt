13.7.5 Mergers
13.7.6 De-mergers
The Guarantor shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Guarantor into two or more separate companies or entities, if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.7.7 Continuation of business
The Guarantor not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
13.7.8 No other business and investments
The Guarantor shall not invest or take part in any other activity than solely related to the ownership of the Issuer and Rig 2 Owner.
13.7.9 Disposal of business
The Guarantor shall not sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless (i) such sale or disposal is on arm's length terms and for fair market value and (ii) the Bonds can be and are redeemed in full in accordance with the mandatory prepayment provisions set out in Clause 10.4 in relation to sale or disposal of the shares in the Issuer only.
