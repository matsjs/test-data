13.7.3 Pari passu ranking
The Guarantor shall ensure that its obligations under any Finance Document to which it is a party shall all time rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Guarantor (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and that such obligations rank ahead of subordinated debt.

The Guarantor shall maintain 100% direct ownership and control over the shares in the Issuer, except as set out in Clause 13.7.9 below.
13.7.5 Mergers
The Guarantor shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Guarantor with any other companies or entities if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
