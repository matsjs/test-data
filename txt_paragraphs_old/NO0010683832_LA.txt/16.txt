"Operational Management Agreement" means an agreement to be entered into by and between the Parent (or, as the case may be, another third party reputable rig operator reasonably acceptable to the Bond Trustee) and the Issuer or (as the case may be) the Issuer Subsidiary pursuant to Clause 13.3.2(iv) for the provision of all marketing, operation, crewing, supplying, technical management and any other required management services under the relevant Charter Contract.
"Parent" means Contructora y Perforadora Latina S.A., a company existing under the laws of Mexico with registration number CPL801111PS2, being the legal and beneficial owner of 100% of the outstanding shares in the Guarantor.
"Parent Assignment of Subordinated Loans" means an assignment (or such similar Security under the relevant jurisdiction) on first priority of all monetary claims and other rights of the Parent under any Subordinated Loan.
"Parent Earnings Account" means an account in the name of the Parent, into which all its earnings under any Charter Contract to which it is a party and all its other net earnings relating to the Rig shall be paid.
"Parent Earnings Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Parent's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Parent in the Parent Earnings Account.
"Parent Undertaking" means an undertaking from the Parent dated on or about the date of the Bond Agreement including inter alia subordination statements for any claims due to the Parent from the Issuer under any Management Agreement and any Subordinated Loans, relevant representations and warranties, the Parent special covenants as set out in Clause 13.8 and certain events of default provisions.
"Party" means a party to this Bond Agreement (including its successors and permitted transferees).
