Listing
The Issuer shall apply for listing ofthe Bonds on Oslo Bms.
3.2 If the Bonds are listed, the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
4
Registration in the Securities Depository
4.1
The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall prior to disbursement be registered in the
Securities Depository according to the Norwegian Securities Depository Act (Act
2002/64) and the terms and conditions of the Securities Depository.
