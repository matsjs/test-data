(e) Disposalo/business
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Material Subsidiary shall, sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations to any person not being a member of the Group, unless:
(i)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions
customary for such transactions; and
