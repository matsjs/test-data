12.11
Refinancing
The Issuer shall procure that the Noteholders shall have the right to replace or refinance any current or future debt provided by a third party to any of Bodarne 11, Hedvig or Sutaren 14, provided, that the Noteholders offer the same, or more favourable, terms than such third party. The debtor shall in such case provide the same, or equivalent, security for such loan.
