1 Definitions
Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Assignment of Construction Contracts:
second priority assignment over the Construction Contracts, in the amount of NOK 185,000,000 plus expenses and accrued interest over each of the Construction Contracts, with priority after the Preferred Senior Mortgage, in favour of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
Banking Day:
a day when the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions.
