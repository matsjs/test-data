The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to the Bondholders' VPS account manager. The Bondholders' VPS account manager shall notify the paying agent of the Loan (the "Paying Agent") of the pre payment request. The Put Date shall be fifteen - 15 - Banking Days following the date when the Paying Agent received the repayment request.

(i) If occurring at any time from and including Disbursement Date to, but not included, the interest Payment Date in June 2008, the Borrower shall redeem as described above at a price equal to 107% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount),
(ii) If occurring at any time from and including the interest Payment Date in June 2008 to, but not included, the interest Payment Date in June 2010, the Borrower shall redeem as described above at a price equal to 106% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount),
(iii) If occurring at any time from and including the interest Payment Date in June 2010 to, but not included, the interest Payment Date in June 2011 the Borrower shall redeem as described above at a price equal to 105.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount),
