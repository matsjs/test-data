Preferred Senior Mortgage:
cross collateralized first priority security over the Loan Security (except the Escrow Account), in the maximium amount of USD 112,000,000 (plus accrued interest and expenses and any foreign exchange and/or interest hedging transactions entered into in connection with the debt under the Preferred Senior Mortgage), however this amount shall be maximum USD 56,000,000 (plus accrued interest and expenses and any foreign exchange and/or interest hedging transactions entered into in connection with that remaining part of the debt under the Preferred Senior Mortgage) if one of the Vessels or Construction Contracts are sold or disposed of, or if any of the Borrower's shares in one of the Subsidiaries are sold or disposed of.
