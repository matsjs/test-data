8 Status of the Loan and security
8.1 The Loan shall rank pari passu with all other senior obligations of the Borrower and shall rank ahead of subordinated capital.
8.2 The Loan including interest and expenses shall be secured by the Loan Security. From Disbursement Date and until the establishment of the Vessels Mortgage, the Loan shall be secured by the pledge over the Escrow Account, and, in addition, within 20 Banking Days after Disbursement Date and until the establishment of the Vessels Mortgage, the Assignment of Construction Contracts and the Share Pledge.
