9 Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 11.00 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made in semi annual arrears on 29 June and 29 December each year, the first interest Payment Date being 29 December 2007.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless a) the last day of the period is the 31st day of a month but the first day of the period is a day other than the 30th or 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
10 Maturity of the Loan and Call Option
10.1 The Loan will run without installments and mature in whole on the Maturity Date at par (100%).
10.2 The Borrower may redeem parts of the Loan or the entire Loan as follows ("Call Option"): (i) at the interest payment day in June 2010 at 105% of par value plus accrued interest on redeemed amount, or (i) at the interest payment day in June 2011 at 102.50% of par value plus accrued interest on redeemed amount.
