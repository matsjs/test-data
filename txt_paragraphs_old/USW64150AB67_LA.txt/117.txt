The Indenture will provide that each holder of a Note, by accepting such Note, will be deemed to have agreed to and accepted the terms and conditions of each of the Parallel Intercreditor Agreement and Additional Intercreditor Agreement and any amendment referred to in the preceding paragraph and none of the Company, the Trustee or the Security Agent will be required to seek the consent of any holders of Notes to perform its obligations under and in accordance with this covenant. At the request of the Company, the Trustee and the Security Agent will execute any Additional Intercreditor Agreement or amendment or amendment and restatement of the Parallel Intercreditor Agreement that complies with the provisions of this covenant.
