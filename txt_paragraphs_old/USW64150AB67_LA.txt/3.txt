 will be a general obligation of such Guarantor and have the following features under the Parallel Intercreditor Agreement:
 following the occurrence of certain types of Enforcement Action (as defined in the Parallel Intercreditor Agreement), will be subject to turnover and a requirement to direct the person responsible for collection of distributions (including by way of set-off) to pay that distribution to the Security Agent until the first-priority senior secured liabilities have been paid in full;
 following the turnover described above, creditors in respect of which will receive distributions from the Security Agent in accordance with the application of proceeds waterfall for proceeds of enforcement of Collateral, which provides for the payment in full of the first-priority senior secured liabilities prior to any application toward the Second Lien Notes and any future second lien liabilities; and
 will be subject to a limited standstill on enforcement of Guarantors and their subsidiaries that are being sold by the first-lien secured creditors pursuant to a Distressed Disposal (as defined in the Parallel Intercreditor Agreement).
