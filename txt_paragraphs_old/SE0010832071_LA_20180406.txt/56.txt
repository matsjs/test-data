14.4 Acquisitions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), if such acquisition would have a Material Adverse Effect.
14.5 Disposals
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to the Issuer or any of its Subsidiaries which are owned and controlled to no less than 90 per cent. by the Issuer), unless such sale, transfer or disposal is carried out in the ordinary course of business, constitutes a Permitted Distribution or is carried out in accordance with the overall strategy of the Group and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
