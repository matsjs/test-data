"Incurrence Test" means the test pursuant to Clause 15.1 (Incurrence Test).

"Intercreditor Agreement" means the intercreditor agreement made between, among others, the Parent, the Issuer, the Security Agent, the Notes Agent, the Super Senior RCF Agent and the Original Hedge Counterparties (as defined therein).
