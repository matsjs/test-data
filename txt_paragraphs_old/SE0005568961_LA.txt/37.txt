The latest versions of the Finance Documents shall be available to the Noteholders at the office of the Agent during normal business hours.
Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each Subsidiary, comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject.
Change of business
The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by the Group as of the Issue Date.
Mergers
The Issuer shall not enter into any merger unless where the Issuer is the surviving entity.
