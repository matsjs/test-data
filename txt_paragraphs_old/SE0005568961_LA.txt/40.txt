FINANCIAL UNDERTAKINGS
Equity to Total Assets
Equity to Total Assets shall on each Reference Date not be less than 0.15:1.
The calculation of Equity to Total Assets shall be adjusted for market values, regardless of if the Accounting Principles would cease to accept or demand such adjustment.
Interest Cover
Interest Cover shall on each Reference Date not be less than 1.20:1.
