Distribution
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, make any Distribution other than any Permitted Distribution.
13.15
Share issues Save for any share issue as part of any IPO, the Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, issue any shares, except:
(a) by the Issuer to MidCo against cash consideration or set-off of Subordinated Loans made by MidCo to the Issuer;
(b) by a Group Company (other than the Issuer) to another Group Company; or
(c) by a Group Company (other than the Issuer) to any existing minority shareholders pro rata to their existing ownership,
provided (in each case) that any such issued shares in the Issuer or a Material Group Company upon issue are pledged in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Secured Parties) on the same terms as the existing shares in such company (other than any shares referred to in paragraph (c) above).
