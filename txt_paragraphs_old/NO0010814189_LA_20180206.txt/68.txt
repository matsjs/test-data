13.3 Continuation of business The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on by it or the Group at the Issue Date.
13.5 Holding company The Issuer shall not trade, carry on business or own any material assets, except for:
(a) the provision of administrative services to other Group Companies of a type customarily provided by a holding company;
(b) ownership of shares in any company, cash and cash equivalents; and

(c) any Intercompany Loans.
13.6 Mergers and de-mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any other Group Company with any other companies or entities, unless such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect (and, in case of any such transaction involving the Issuer, (i) it does not involve MidCo and (ii) the Issuer is the sole surviving entity in respect thereof).
