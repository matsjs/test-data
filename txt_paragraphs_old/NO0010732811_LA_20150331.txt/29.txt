"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Restricted Payment" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 13.4(a).
"Sale Event" means the sale of all, or substantially all, of the assets of the Group (taken as a whole) whether in a single transaction or a series of related transactions, to a person who is not a member of the Group.
"Secured Parties" means the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itseif and the Bondholders), the Security Agent (in its capacity as security agent for itseif and for the other Secured Parties and as creditor of the Security Agent Liabilities), each Super Senior Creditor and any Pari Passu Creditor.
"Securities Depository" means the securities depository in which the Bond Issue is registered, being Verdipapirsentralen ASA (VPS) in Norway.
"Security" means an encumbrance, mortgage, charge, pledge, hen or other security interest securing any obligation of any person or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee in its capacity as security agent and/or security trustee pursuant to Clause 17,4.
"Security Agent Liabilities" means all the liabilities owing by the Issuer or any other Group Company to the Security Agent (in its capacity as security agent for the Secured Parties) under or in respect of the Finance Documents.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, each of the documents entered into by a Group Company creating or expressed to create any Transaction Security or any other Security over all or any part of its assets in respect of the obligations of the Obligors under the Finance Documents.
