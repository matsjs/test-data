"Group" means the Issuer, and, following the completion of the Merger, the Target and all oftheir respective Subsidiaries from time to time (each a "Group Company").
"Guarantors" means BidCo and any Additional Guarantor.
"Holding Company" means, in relation to a person, any other person in respect of which it is a Subsidiary.
"IFRS" means the international financial reporting standards within the meaning of Regulation i 606/2002/EC (or as otherwise adopted or amended from time to time).
"Incurrence Test" means a test which is met if:
(a) the ratio of Total Net Debt to Consolidated EBITDA for the four most recently ended full fiscal quarters is:
(i) for the period from (and including) the Issue Date to (but excluding) the date that falls 18 months after the Issue Date, not greater than 3.50 to 1.00; and
(ii) for the period from (and including) the date that falls 18 months after the Issue Date to (but excluding) the Maturity Date, not greater than 3.25 to 1.00, and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio exceeds 2.00 to 1.00.
