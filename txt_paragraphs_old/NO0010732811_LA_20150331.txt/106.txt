18.8 Process Agent
The Issuer shall ensure that any Obligor or other Group Company being party to a Finance Document not being incorporated in Norway, no later than upon becoming an Obligor or entering into such Finance Document, nominate a process agent in Norway for the purpose of serving a writ of summons and/or any other act of process in respect of the courts in Norway, or any notices as set out in this Bond Agreement.
