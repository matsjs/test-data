10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.2.4 On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.3 Voluntary partial prepayment
10.3.1 The Issuer may redeem Bonds with aggregate Face Value not exceeding 10% of the sum ofUSD 113,300,000 plus the total aggregate Face Value of any Bonds issued pursuant to a Tap Issue, on one Interest Payment Date per 12 month period from (but excluding) the Issue Date to (but excluding) the first Interest Payment Date falling after 36 months after the Issue Date (without carry-back or carry forward) at a price equal to 103.00% of the Face Value of the Bonds redeemed.
10.3.2 Exercise of the voluntary partial prepayment shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least twenty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the prepayment.
