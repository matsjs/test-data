Events of Default
15.1 The Bond Trustee may deciare the Bonds to be in default upon occurrence of any of the foliowing events:
(a) Non-payment
The Issuer fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full within five Business Days foliowing the original due date.
(b) Breach of other obligations
The Parent or any Group Company does not comply with any provision pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable of being remedied and is remedied within fifteen Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
