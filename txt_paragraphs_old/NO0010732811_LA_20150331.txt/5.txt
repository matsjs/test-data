"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Payment Date originally falls on a day that is not a Business Day, an adjustment of the Payment Date will be made so that the relevant Payment Date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day (Modified Foliowing Business Day Convention).
"Change of Control Event" means
(i) prior to a Flotation, the occurrence of either of the foliowing:
(a) the Sponsors, collectively, cease to own a combined shareholding, directly or indirectly, of more than 50% of the aggregate of the shares in the Issuer; and/or
(b) the Sponsors, collectively, cease to control, directly or indirectly, more than 50% of the voting rights in respect of the Issuer; and
(ii) after a Flotation, an event whereby:
(c) the Sponsors collectively, cease to have a Decisive Influence in the Issuer,
provided that a change of ownership as a result of a Flotation shall not constitute a Change of Control Event pursuant to Clause (a) or (b) above.
