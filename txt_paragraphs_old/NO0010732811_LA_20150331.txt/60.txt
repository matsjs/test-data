10.3.1 The Issuer may redeem Bonds with aggregate Face Value not exceeding 10% of the sum ofUSD 113,300,000 plus the total aggregate Face Value of any Bonds issued pursuant to a Tap Issue, on one Interest Payment Date per 12 month period from (but excluding) the Issue Date to (but excluding) the first Interest Payment Date falling after 36 months after the Issue Date (without carry-back or carry forward) at a price equal to 103.00% of the Face Value of the Bonds redeemed.
10.3.3 On the settlement date of the voluntary partial prepayment, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including the premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.3.4 Voluntary partial prepayments must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.3.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.3 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.4 Equity Clawback
10.4.1 The Issuer may at any time prior to the first Interest Payment Date falling after 36 months after the Issue Date, by using the net cash proceeds received by the Group from an Equity Offering, repay at 107.395% of par, Bonds with a total aggregate Face Value not exceeding 40% of the aggregate ofUSD 113,300,000 plus the total aggregate Face Value of any Bonds issued pursuant to a Tap Issue.
