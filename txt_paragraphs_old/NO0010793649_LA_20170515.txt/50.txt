13.7
Financial support
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not be a creditor in respect of any Financial Support to or for the benefit of any person not being a Group Company.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Financial Support in relation to (any Permitted Financial Indebtedness or any Permitted Security.
13.8
Disposals
The Issuer shall not, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets to a person not being a member of the Group, unless such sale, transfer or disposal (i) is carried out at fair market value on terms and conditions customary for such transactions and (ii) would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
