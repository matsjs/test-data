7. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES The Issuer makes the representations and warranties set out in this Clause 7 (Representations and warranties), in respect of itself to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) at the following times and with reference to the facts and circumstances then existing:
(a) at the Issue Date;
(b) on each date of disbursement of proceeds from the Escrow Account;
(c) at the date of issuance of any Additional Bonds.
7.1 Information All information which has been presented to the Bond Trustee or the Bondholders in relation to the Bonds is, to the best knowledge of the Issuer, having taken all reasonable measures to ensure the same:
(a) true and accurate in all material respects as at the date the relevant information is expressed to be given; and
(b) does not omit any material information likely to affect the accuracy of the information as regards the evaluation of the Bonds in any material respects unless subsequently disclosed to the Bond Trustee in writing or otherwise made publicly known.
