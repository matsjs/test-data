13.9
Arm's lenght transactions
The Issuer shall not enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.10 Distributions The Issuer shall not pay, make or declare any Distribution, whether in cash or in kind, on its ordinary shares and/or preference shares.
13.11 Group Companies'distributions
The Issuer shall ensure that the Company does not create or permit to subsist any contractual obligation or encumbrance restricting the right of the Company to pay, make or declare any Distribution if the creation of such contractual obligation is reasonably likely to prevent the Issuer form complying with any of its obligations under the Finance Documents.
13.12 Ownership to the Target Shares
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose of the Target Shares except in the Event of an Exit.
