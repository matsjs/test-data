10.1
Redemption of Bonds
(a) The Outstanding Bonds will mature in full on the Maturity Date and shall be redeemed by the Issuer on the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 per cent, of the Nominal Amount.
(b) The amount standing to the credit of the Debt Service Reserve Account on the Maturity Date may be applied towards redemption of the Bonds in accordance with paragraph (a) above, subject to delivery by the Issuer to Bond Trustee of a Release Notice in the form set out in Schedule 3.
