13.18 Pari passu ranking The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under the Finance Documents at all times rank at least pari passu with other senior debt of the Issuer, except for obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law, and rank head of subordinated capital.
13.19 Financial Covenants and Incurrence Test
(a) The Issuer shall, until the date falling eighteen (18) months prior to the Maturity Date, at all times maintain an amount equal to the Minimum Interest Reserve on the Debt Service Reserve Account.
(b) The Incurrence Test only needs to be met in the event of a Tap Issue, cf. Clause 6.3 (Tap Issues). The Incurrence Test is met if Net LTV is below 50%.
