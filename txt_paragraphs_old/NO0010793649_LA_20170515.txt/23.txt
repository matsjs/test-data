2.3 Use of proceeds (a) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue for;
(i) funding the Debt Service Reserve Account with an amount equal to twenty-four (24) months' interest payment on the Bonds;
(ii) financing the acquisition of the Target Shares; and
(iii) the balance (if any), for general corporate purposes.
(b) The Issuer will use the net proceeds from the issuance of any Additional Bonds for;
(i) if required, to top-up the Debt Service Reserve Account to comply with the Minimum Interest Reserve;
(ii) acquisition of additional shares in the Company (for the avoidance of doubt, such additional shares to be pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) in the same manner as the Target Shares); and
(iii) general corporate purposes.
