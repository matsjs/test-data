13.13 Subordinated loans
The Issuer shall not (i) make any payment or discharge of any principal or interest under any Subordinated Loan or (ii) propose or consent to any amendment of terms of any Subordinated Loans, and (iii) shall ensure that any Subordinated Loan remains fully subordinated to the Bonds.
