13.3.4 Continuation o f business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business. The Issuer shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature o f the business of the Group from that carried on at the date o f this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement. The Issuer shall remain as a single-purpose company owning the Properties through its 100% directly owned Subsidiaries and being the Lessee's counterparty under the Master Leases.
13.3.5 Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell or otherwise dispose of any Property, directly or indirectly through sale o f shares in a Subsidiary, unless the disposal constitutes a Permitted Disposal.
13.3.6 Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value. The Issuer shall thus not allow itself or any other Group Company to engage directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering o f any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement o f the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favorable to the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
13.3.7 Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
