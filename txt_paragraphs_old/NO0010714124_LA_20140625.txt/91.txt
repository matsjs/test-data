13.3.12 Maintenance o f Properties
The Issuer shall exercise its rights under the Project Documents to procure that the Lessee fulfils its maintenance obligations in accordance with the Project Documents, hereunder that the Properties and all inventory are kept in a state of good and safe condition and state o f repair consistent with good industry standard, and if necessary, replaced with items o f similar quality and value, all according to a pre-agreed planned maintenance system. Such maintenance shall be paid directly by the Lessee.
