13.3.11 Environmental matters
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that all other Group Companies will:
(a) comply in all material respects with all Environmental Laws relevant to its business and operations;
(b) promptly upon becoming aware, notify the Bond Trustee of any Environmental Claim initiated, or to its knowledge, threatened;
(c)
promptly upon becoming aware, notify the Bond Trustee of any
circumstances reasonably likely to result in an Environmental Clain or any
suspension, revocation or notification o f any Environmental Permit;
