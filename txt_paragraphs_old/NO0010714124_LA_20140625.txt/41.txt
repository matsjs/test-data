2.2.3
The Bond Issue is an open (tap) issue and will be closed no later than five Business Days prior to Maturity Date at which time no further tap issues may be made.
2.2.4
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects, save for adjustment to (i) issue amount and (ii) issue price for the Tap Issue. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will on the issuing of additional Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
2.2.5
The Face Value is NOK 1,000 (ONE THOUSAND NORWEGIAN KRONER). The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves. The minimum subscription and allotment amount shall be NOK 1,000,000, and integral multiples o f NOK 500,000 thereof.
