10.5.2
Under the Disposal Put Option, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems Bonds on a pro rata basis for an amount equalling the net proceeds remaining after the sale of the asset at a price of 101% of par value plus accrued interest.
10.5.3
The Disposal Put Option must be exercised within 30 days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bond Trustee o f any amount not being invested within 90 days. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after the expiration o f such 90 days period.
10.5.4
The Disposal Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice o f the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the redemption request. The settlement date of the Disposal Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end o f the 30 day exercise period of the Disposal Put Option.
