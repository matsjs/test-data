15.2
Each Noteholder is entitled to nominate candidates to the Noteholders' Committee by
notice to Agent no later than two (2) Business Days prior to the Noteholders' Meeting. At
the Noteholders Meeting all candidates so nominated shall be presented to the Noteholders.
Each Noteholder that is entitled to vote shall for such election have the same number of
votes to cast for each Note as the total number of persons to be elected. A Noteholder may
