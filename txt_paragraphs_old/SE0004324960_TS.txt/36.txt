The Company shall ensure that the Bonds are listed on OMX not later than 29 December 2012 and shall take all measures required to ensure that the Bonds continue being listed on OMX for as long as any Bonds are outstanding (however, taking into account the rules and regulations of OMX and the CSD (as amended from time to time) preventing trading in the Bonds in close connection to the redemption of the Bonds).
