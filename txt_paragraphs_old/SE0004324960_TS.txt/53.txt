18 Prescription
18.1 The right to receive payment of the Nominal Amount shall be prescribed and become void ten (10) years from the relevant Redemption Date. The right to receive payment of interest shall be prescribed and become void three (3) years from the relevant due date for payment. The Company is entitled to any funds set aside for payments in respect of which the Holders right to receive payment have been prescribed and void.
18.2 If such periods of prescription periods are duly interrupted, in accordance with the Swedish Act on Limitations (Sw. preskriptionslag (1981:130)), a new prescription period of ten (10) years with respect to the Nominal Amount, and of three (3) years with respect to interest payments will commence, in both cases calculated from the date of interruption of the prescription period as such date is determined pursuant with the provisions of the Swedish Act on Limitations.
19 Allocation of payments
If both the Nominal Amount and interest are due for payment and if the available funds are insufficient to discharge all the amounts due and payable, the available funds shall first be applied towards payment of interest and secondly towards payment of the Nominal Amount.
20 Taxation and Tax gross-up
The Company shall pay any stamp duty and other public fees accruing in connection with the issuance of the Bonds, but not in respect of trading in the secondary market (except to the extent required by applicable laws).
