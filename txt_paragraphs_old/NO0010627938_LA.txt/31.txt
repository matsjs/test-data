10. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem parts of the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue (Call Option) at any time from and included the Issue Date to, but not included, Maturity Date at 101% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
