13.5 Preservation of equity
The Issuer shall not, during the term of the Bond Issue, declare or make any dividend payment or distribution, whether in cash or in kind, repurchase of shares or make other similar transactions (included, but not limited to total return swaps related to shares in the Issuer), grant any loans or other distributions or transactions implying a transfer of value to its shareholders.
13.6 Financial Indebtedness and negative pledge
13.6.1 Negative Pledge on the Issuer
The Issuer shall not create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance over any of its present or future respective assets (including, but not limited to, the shares in the Subsidiaries) or its revenues, other than the encumbrances already granted in favor of the First Bond Loan and the Second Bond Loan.
