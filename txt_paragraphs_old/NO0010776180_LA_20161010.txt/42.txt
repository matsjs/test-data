(b) the Fee Agreement duly executed;
(c) copies of the constitutional documents of the Issuer;
(d) certified copies of all corporate documents of the Issuer and necessary corporate resolutions to issue the Bonds and execute the Bond Agreement, the Security Documents and the other Finance Documents to which it is to become a party;
(e) confirmation that the relevant individuals are authorised to sign on behalf of the Issuer the Bond Agreement, the relevant Security Documents and any other relevant documents in relation thereto;
(f) copies of constitutional documents of the Property Company and necessary corporate resolutions to execute the Bond Agreement, the Mortgage and any other Finance Documents to which it is to become a party;
(g) confirmation that the relevant individuals are authorised to sign the Bond Agreement and the Mortgage on behalf of the Property Company and any other relevant documents in relation thereto; and
(h) establishment of the Escrow Account Charge, duly executed by all parties (including relevant notice to and acknowledgement from the Account Bank, stating that it has waived all set-off rights with respect to the Escrow Account).
