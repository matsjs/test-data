3.9 Change of Control (a) Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price of 101% of Face Value (plus accrued but unpaid interest).
(b) The Put Option must be exercised within sixty (60) days after the Issuer has given notification to the Bondholders ("Notification Period"), through the Securities Depository, of the Change of Control Event.
