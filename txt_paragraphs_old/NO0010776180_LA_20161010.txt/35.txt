Payments Interest (a) On each Coupon Date, the Issuer shall in arrear pay to the Bondholders the
accrued Coupon amount comprising the Cash Interest and the PIK Interest.
(b) The Issuer shall pay the Cash Interest on the Face Value of the Bonds from, and including the Coupon Accrual Date (including, for the avoidance of doubt, any Additional Bonds) to (but excluding) the Maturity Date.
(c) The Issuer shall pay the PIK Interest on the Face Value of the Bonds from, and including the Coupon Accrual Date (including, for the avoidance of doubt, any Additional Bonds) to (but excluding) the Maturity Date.
