3.9 Change of Control (a) Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of pre-payment (a "Put Option") of its Bonds at a price of 101% of Face Value (plus accrued but unpaid interest).
(c) The Put Option may be exercised by the Bondholders by giving written notice of the request to its account manager. The account manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the pre-payment request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall be fifteen Business Days following the end of the Notification Period.
(d) On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each Bondholder holding Bonds to be pre-paid, the principal amount of each such Bond (at the price stipulated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to and including the settlement date of the Put Option.
(e) The Issuer has no obligation to discharge Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 3.9.

3.10
Mandatory prepayment
(a) Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall within ten Business Days redeem the Outstanding Bonds at a price corresponding to the applicable Call Option price (plus accrued and unpaid interest). The Bond Trustee is however under no obligation to release its Security before all outstanding amounts pursuant to the Bond Agreement is repaid in full.
