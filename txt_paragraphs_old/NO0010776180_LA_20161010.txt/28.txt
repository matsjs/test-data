3.2 Listing and prospectus
3.2.1
The Issuer is under no obligation to list the Bond on any exchange. In the event that the Bonds are listed on an exchange, matters concerning the listing requiring the approval of the Bondholders shall be resolved pursuant to the terms of this Bond Agreement.
3.2.2 In the event that the Bonds are listed on an exchange, the Issuer shall submit the documents and the information necessary to maintain the listing.
3.2.3 The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement shall be incorporated in any prospectus and other subscription or information materials related to the Bonds.
3.3 3.3.1
Status
The Issuer's payment obligations under this Bond Agreement shall rank as senior debt obligations of the Issuer, secured by the Security created under the Security Documents, ahead of all subordinated payment obligations of the Issuer and shall rank at least pari passu with all the Issuer's other unsecured obligations, save for obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law.
