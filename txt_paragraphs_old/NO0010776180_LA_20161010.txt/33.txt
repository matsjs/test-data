The Issuer shall deliver, on a semi-annual basis, a compliance certificate confirming compliance with the LTV Covenant. The first valuation of Market Value shall be provided no later than 6 months after the Issue Date. Each subsequent valuation shall be made 6 months after the date of the previous valuation.
In case of breach of the LTV Covenant, the Issuer shall be entitled to post additional collateral in cash or cash equivalents into an account blocked and pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee in an amount sufficient to bring the Issuer in compliance with the LTV Covenant within 45 days after becoming aware of the breach of the LTV Covenant (and so that the amount so deposited shall then be deducted from the Total Financial Indebtedness).
3.6.5
The Issuer shall not, and ensure that no member of the Group will, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make any loans or other distributions or payments to the direct or indirect shareholders of the Issuer (including servicing of any Subordinated Loans) of any kind.
3.6.6
The Issuer shall not permit any member of the Group to create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right of any member of the Group to make any distributions (including dividend) to the Issuer.
