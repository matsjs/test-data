Listing of Bonds
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to have the Bonds listed on the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Stockholm, or if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on any other Regulated Market, within thirty (30) days after the Issue Date, but in no case later than sixty (60) days after the Issue Date, and shall take all measures required to ensure that the Bonds, once listed on the relevant Regulated Market, continue being listed thereon for as long as any Bond is outstanding (however, subject to and taking into account the rules and regulations of the relevant Regulated Market and the CSD (as amended from time to time) preventing trading in the Bonds in close connection to the redemption of the Bonds).
