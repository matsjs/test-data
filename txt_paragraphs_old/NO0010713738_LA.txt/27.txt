"Secured Debt" means the payment obligations of the Obligors under the Finance Documents and the Super Senior Finance Documents.
"Sponsor" means Segulah IV L.P.
"SSRCF" means a MNOK 300 super senior revolving credit and guarantee facility to be provided to Beerenberg Holding AS and each of its Subsidiaries which becomes a party thereto as a borrower by one or several bank lenders, with a limit on cash drawings of MNOK 150, and any refinancing or replacements thereof.
"SSRCF Agreement" means the agreement for the SSRCF.
"Super Senior Finance Documents" means the SSRCF Agreement and any other document defined as a "Finance Document" therein, and any Hedging Agreements entered into with any Hedging Counterparty that is a party to the Intercreditor Agreement.
"Super Senior Creditors" means the finance parties under the Super Senior Finance Documents.
"Super Senior Obligations" means the payment obligations towards the Super Senior Creditors under the Super Senior Finance Documents.
"Stamdata" means the web site www.stamdata.no, maintained by the Bond Trustee.
"Subsidiary" means an entity over which another entity or person has a Decisive Influence. An entity shall always be considered to be the subsidiary of another entity or person if such entity or person has such number of shares or ownership interests so as to represent the majority of the votes in the entity, or has the right to vote in or vote out a majority of the directors in the entity.
