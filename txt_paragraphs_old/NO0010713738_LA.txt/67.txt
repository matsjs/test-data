The Issuer shall, and the Issuer shall procure that each Material Subsidiary will, maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters adequate insurance or captive arrangements with respect to its properties and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as are consistent with prudent business practice.
13.5.1 The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.5.2 The Incurrence Test shall be applied upon the incurrence or the acquisition of new Financial Indebtedness to the extent required in the definition of "Permitted Financial Indebtedness".
13.5.3 The Incurrence Test is met if the Leverage Ratio for the Group (on a consolidated basis) does not exceed 3.00:1.00 and the Interest Coverage Ratio for the Group (on a consolidated basis) exceeds 3.00:1.00.
The calculation of the Leverage Ratio shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no earlier than the date of the last financial quarter prior to the event relevant for the application of the Incurrence Test,
The Net Interest Bearing Debt shall be measured on the relevant testing date however so determined that the full commitment of the new Financial Indebtedness, provided it is an interest bearing obligation (however, any cash balance resulting from the incurrence of such new Financial Indebtedness shall not reduce the Net Interest Bearing Debt in respect of which the Incurrence Test is applied) shall be taken into account.
The calculation of the Interest Coverage Ratio shall be made for the Relevant Period ending on the last day of the period covered by the most recent Financial Report.
