"Guarantee" means an irrevocable and unconditional on-demand guarantee on a joint and several basis from the Guarantors securing inter alia the Issuer's obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other of the Finance Documents, as established by the Guarantee Agreement.
"Guarantee Agreement" means an agreement between each Guarantor, the Issuer and the Secured Creditors establishing the on-demand guarantees from the Guarantors for the Secured Debt on the terms set out therein.
"Guarantors" mean Beerenberg Holding AS, Beerenberg Corp. AS and any other Material Subsidiary, each a "Guarantor".
"Hedging Agreements" mean foreign exchange transactions for spot or forward delivery protection against fluctuation in currency rates where that foreign exchange exposure arises in the ordinary course of business or in respect of payments to be made under the Bond Agreement and/or the SSRCF Agreement, and interest rate derivatives agreements for interest rate hedging purposes with respect to the Secured Debt, in each case not for investment or speculative purposes.
"Hedging Counterparty" means a counterparty to any Hedging Agreements.
"Interest Payment Date" means the 27th of September, December, March and June each year. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" mean the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements of the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to IFRS.
