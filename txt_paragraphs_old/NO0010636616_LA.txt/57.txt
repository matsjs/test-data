13.5 Preservation o f equity, Security Interests and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall during the term of the Bonds not declare or make any dividend payments, repurchase of shares or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity. In the event that the Subordinated Loans are converted in full into equity, dividend payments under this clause 13.5 (a) are permitted provided that the Issuer has complied with the Cash Sweep Redemption.
(b) Minimum Cash Amount The Issuer shall at all times from the date falling no later than the Interest Payment Date in November 2012, maintain unrestricted cash of no less than the Minimum Cash Amount, to be tested on a quarterly basis thereafter.
