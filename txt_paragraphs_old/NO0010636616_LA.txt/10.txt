"DOC" means in relation to the Vessel Manager a valid document of compliance issued pursuant to paragraph 13.2 of the ISM Code.
"Earnings Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer held with a reputable Norwegian or international bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), but not blocked unless an Event of Default has occurred and is continuing, where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Earnings Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the account bank in respect of any amounts from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer on the Earnings Account.
"Encumbrance" means any encumbrance, mortgage, pledge, lien, charge (whether fixed or floating), assignment by way of security, finance lease, sale and repurchase or sale and leaseback arrangement, sale of receivables on a recourse basis or security interest or any other agreement or arrangement having the effect of conferring security.
"Escrow Account" means an account in the name of the Issuer held with a reputable Norwegian or international bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and pledged on first priority and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the Escrow Account, governed by Norwegian law, blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means securities exchange or other reputable marketplace for securities, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
