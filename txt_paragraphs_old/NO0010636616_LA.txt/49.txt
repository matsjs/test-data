13.2 Information Covenants
13.2.1 The Obligors shall
(a) without being requested to do so, immediately inform the Bond Trustee of any Event of Default as well as of any circumstances which the Obligor understands or should understand may lead to as an Event of Default;
(b) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee of any other event which may have a Material Adverse Effect;
(c) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee if the Obligors intend to sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of their respective assets or operations, or change the nature of their respective business;
(d) without being requested to do so, produce Financial Statements annually and, in respect of the Issuer only, Quarterly Financial Report quarterly and each Obligors shall make the relevant of the Financial Statements and/or Quarterly Financial Reports available on its website in the English language (alternatively by sending them to the Bond Trustee for further distribution to anyone requesting such) as soon as they become available, and not later than 150 days after the end of the financial year and 60 days after the end of each Quarter Date;
(e) at the request of the Bond Trustee, report the balance of the Issuer's Bonds;
