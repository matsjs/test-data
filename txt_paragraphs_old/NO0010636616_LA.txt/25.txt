2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series o f Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 100,000,000 (U.S. Dollar onehundredmillion).
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 1 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as " 10.00 per cent SinOceanic II AS Callable Bond Issue 2012/2015".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 063661.6.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
