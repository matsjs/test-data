10.4 Mandatory prepayment
10.4.1 Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment E v en t, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the proceeds is received by it, or as the case may be, the Guarantor, following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event, redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds as follows:
(a) if occurring anytime from the Issue Date to (but not including) the Interest Payment Date in February 2013, at a price equal to 108% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(b) if occurring anytime from the Interest Payment Date in February 2013 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in February 2014 at a price equal to 106% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount); or
(c) if occurring anytime from the Interest Payment Date in February 2013 to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 103% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount.
10.4.2 Upon a Total Loss Event related to the Vessel, the Issuer shall as soon as insurance proceeds are available to it redeem 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at 100.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
10.4.3 For the avoidance of doubt, the redemption price in respect of the Outstanding Bonds shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Redemption Event occurred, and not based on the date the repayment is carried out.
