If the condition precedents set out in clause 6.2 below have not been fulfilled to the satisfaction of the Bond Trustee by 30 March 2012, the Issuer shall, on this date, repay the Bondholders their investment in the Bonds at an amount equal to the subscription price, and the net proceeds from the Bond Issue deposited in the Escrow Amount may be utilized for this purpose.
6.2 Conditions precedent in respect o f the payment from the Escrow Account into the Sale and Purchase Joint Account and conditions precedent in respect o f the payment from the Sale and Purchase Joint Account
6.2.1 The amount on the Escrow Account shall only be used in accordance with the purpose of the Bond Issue. The Bond Trustee shall only be obligated to release the net proceeds of the Bonds from the Escrow Account into the Sale and Purchase Joint Account subject to the Bond Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it:
a) a duly executed request for release notice from the Issuer;
b) evidence that the Sale and Purchase Joint Account has been opened in the joint names of the Issuer and the Seller, and that the Bond Trustee is the irrevocable sole authorized signatory in respect of the Issuer's rights to instruct the release and transfer of funds deposited thereon.
