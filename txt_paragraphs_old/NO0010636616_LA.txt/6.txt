Interpretation
1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement the following terms shall have the following meanings (certain terms relevant for Clauses 13 and 18.2 and other Clauses may be defined in the relevant Clause):
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Assignment of Earnings" means a first priority assignment of any earnings payable to the Issuer (included earnings under any charters) related to the Vessel granted by the Issuer in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), governed by Norwegian law, to be perfected by the giving of notices of such assignment to any relevant third parties and in respect of which the Issuer shall use its best endeavours to procure the acknowledgements of such notices from the respective charterer.
"Assignment of Insurances" means a first priority assignment over the insurances related to the Vessel granted by the Issuer in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders, governed by Norwegian law.
"Assignment of Management Agreement" means a first priority assignment of the rights of the Issuer under the Management Agreement (to the extent legally permissible), governed by applicable law and made in favour of the Bond Trustee (in favour of the Bondholder, such assignment to contain (to the extent legally permissible) direct cure and step-in rights and termination rights in case of default or in any event by giving maximum 30 days' notice, to be perfected by the giving of notice to the Vessel Manager and in respect of which the Issuer shall use its reasonable endeavours to obtain an acknowledgement of from the Vessel Manager.
