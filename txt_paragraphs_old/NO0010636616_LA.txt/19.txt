"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Registry" means the Liberian International Ship and Corporate Registry.
"Sale and Purchase Joint Account" means a joint account in the name of the Issuer and the Seller, to which the Purchase Price shall be deposited on, and in respect of which the Bond Trustee shall be the sole authorized signatory as concerns the Issuer's rights to instruct the release and transfer of funds deposited thereon.
"Second Priority Security Documents" means i) a second priority mortgage over the Vessel including all relevant equipment being legally part of the Vessel under applicable law, including, if applicable, a deed of covenants supplemental to the Mortgage and to the security thereby created between the Issuer and the Subordinated Loan Providers, made in favour of the Subordinated Loan Provider(s), ii) a second priority assignment of any Earnings granted by the Issuer in favour of the Subordinated Loan Provider(s), governed by Norwegian law, and iii) a second priority assignment over the insurances related to the Vessel granted by the Issuer in favour of the Subordinated Loan Provider(s), governed by Norwegian law.
"Securities Register Act" means the Norwegian Act relating to Registration of Financial Instruments of 5 July 2002 No. 64.
"Securities Register" means the securities register in which the Bond Issue is registered.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee, unless any other legal entity is appointed as collateral agent pursuant to Clause 17.4.
