(f) Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured), other than:
(i) any Financial Indebtedness under the Finance Documents; (ii) any hedging transactions entered into as part o f the Issuer's ordinary
course of business; (iii) any Financial Indebtedness under the Subordinated Loans with a maturity
later than the Maturity Date.
(g) Financial support The Issuer shall not grant any new loans, any guarantees or any other financial assistance (including but not limited to granting security) to any party (including any member of the Parent Group).
(h) Investments The Issuer shall not make any investments or capital expenditures, other than solely related to the ownership in and operation of the Vessel, provided such capital expenditures are fully funded.
