13.4 Corporate and operational matters
(a) Inter-group transactions
All transactions between any companies within the Group shall be on commercial terms, and shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-9 of the Private or Public Limited Companies Acts 1997.
(b) Transactions with shareholders, directors or affiliated companies
The Issuer shall cause all transactions between the Issuer and (i) any direct or indirect shareholder thereof, (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company holds more than 10 per cent (10%) of the shares, or (iv) or any company, person or entity controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, to be entered into in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business, and on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Group Company than would have prevailed in an arm's length transaction with a third party.
