2.1 Binding nature o f this Bond Agreement
2.1.2
This Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that this Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. This Bond Agreement may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of USD 32.000. 000 (United States Dollars thirty two million) plus Bonds issued as PIK interest.
The Face Value is USD 1.00. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "6.00 per cent Interoil Exploration and Production ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2015/2020".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0729908.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
