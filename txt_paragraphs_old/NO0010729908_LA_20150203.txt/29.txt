10 Maturity of the Bonds andRedemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer plus accrued and unpaid interest.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds in whole or in part (Call Option) at any time to, but not included, the Maturity Date, at 105 per cent (105%) of Face Value plus accrued unpaid interest on the redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
