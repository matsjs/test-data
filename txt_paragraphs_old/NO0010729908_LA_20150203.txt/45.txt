13.7 Subsidiary Covenants
(a) The Issuer shall procure that the following provisions of Clauses 13.3 - 13.5 also applies for each of the Bond Issue Subsidiaries so that all references to the Issuer are replaced with references to the respective Bond Issue Subsidiary: 13.3 (except 13.3(d)), 13.4 and 13.5 (except 13.5(a)).
(b) Notwithstanding the limitations in Clause 13.5(d) above, the Bond Issue Subsidiaries shall also be entitled to incur indebtedness arising in the ordinary course of business for working capital purposes and as part of the daily operation of the Group.
