Interpretation
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Andes Energia" means Andes Energia pic, company number 5083946 of 2nd Floor Berkeley Square House, W1J 6BD - London, UK.
"Assignment over Intercompany Loans" means a 1st priority security interest over all of the Issuer's rights and receivables under any permitted loans made by the Issuer to any of the Subsidiaries, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders).
"Attachment" means the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.1.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
