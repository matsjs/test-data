16 Bondholders' meeting
16.1 Authority o f the Bondholders ' meeting
16.1.1
The Bondholders' Meeting represents the supreme authority of the Bondholders community in all matters relating to die Bonds. If a resolution by or an approval of the Bondholders is required, resolution of such shall be passed at a Bondholders' Meeting. Resolutions passed at Bondholders' Meetings shall be binding upon and prevail for all Bonds.
16.2 Procedural rulesfo r BondholdersJmeetings
16.2.1 A Bondholders' Meeting shall be held at the request of:
(a) the Issuer, (b) Bondholders representing at least 1/10 of Outstanding Bonds, (c) the Exchange, if the Bonds are listed, or (d) the Bond Tmstee.
16.2.2 The Bondholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Bond Tmstee. A request for a Bondholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Bond Tmstee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
