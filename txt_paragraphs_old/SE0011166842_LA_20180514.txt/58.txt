FINANCIAL UNDERTAKINGS
11.1.1 The Issuer shall at all times ensure that the Maintenance Test is met.
11.1.2 The Maintenance Test is met if:
a) the Equity Ratio is at least 25 per cent. on the relevant Reference Date; and
b) the Interest Cover Ratio is at least 2.00:1 on the relevant Reference Date.
11.1.3
The Maintenance Test shall be tested quarterly on the basis of the Financial Report for the period ending on the relevant Reference Date and be included in the Compliance Certificate delivered in connection therewith. The first test date for the Maintenance Test shall be the Reference Date occurring on 30 June 2018.
