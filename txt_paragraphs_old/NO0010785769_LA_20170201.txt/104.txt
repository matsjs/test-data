2 5.5
Any notice or other communication made by one person to another under or in connection
with the Finance Documents shall be sent by way of courier, personal delivery or letter, or,
if between the Issuer and the Agent, by email, and will only be effective, in case of courier
or personal delivery, when it has been left at the address specified in Clause 25.1, in case
of letter, three (3) Business Days after being deposited postage prepaid in an envelope
addressed to the address specified in Clause 25.1, or, in case of email, when received in
readable form by the email recipient.
