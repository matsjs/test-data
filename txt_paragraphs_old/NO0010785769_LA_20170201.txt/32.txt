If, due to any obstacle for the CSD, the Issuer cannot make a payment or repayment, such

7. S
If payment or repayment is made in accordance with this Clause 7, the Issuer shall be
deemed to have fulfilled its obligation to pay, irrespective of whether such payment was
made to a person not entitled to receive such amount.
