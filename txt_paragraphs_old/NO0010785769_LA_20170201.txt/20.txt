The Arranger shall pay the proceeds from the issuance of any Subsequent Notes to the
Issuer on the later of (i) the date of the issue of such Subsequent Notes and (ii) the date on
which the Agent notifies the Arranger that it is satisfied that it has received the following
documents and evidence:
(a) a certificate from the Issuer confirming that no Event of Default is continuing or would result from the issue of the Subsequent Notes;
(b) confirmation from the Paying Agent that such Subsequent Notes have been registered in the CSD; and
(c) such other documents and information as is agreed between the Agent and the Issuer.
