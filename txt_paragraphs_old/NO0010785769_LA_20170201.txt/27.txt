7.1
Any payment or repayment under the Finance Documents, or any amount due in respect of
a repurchase of any Notes requested by a Noteholder pursuant to these Terms and
Conditions, shall be made to such person who is registered as a Noteholder on a Securities
Account on the Relevant Record Date immediately preceding the relevant due date, by way
of (if no specific order is made by the Agent) crediting the relevant amount to the bank
account nominated by such Noteholder in connection with its Securities Account with the
CSD.
