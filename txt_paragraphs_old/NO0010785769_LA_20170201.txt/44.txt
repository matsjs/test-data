Continuing Guarantee
The Guarantee shall extend to the ultimate balance of the Guaranteed Obligations and shall continue in force notwithstanding (i) any intermediate payment or discharge in whole or in part of the Guaranteed Obligations or (ii) the Agent enforcing any other Security granted in respect of the Guaranteed Obligations.
If any payment by the Issuer of the Guaranteed Obligations is avoided or reduced as a result of insolvency or any similar event, the liability of the Parent under the Guarantee shall continue as if the payment, discharge, avoidance or reduction had not occurred.
