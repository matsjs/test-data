The Parent and the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company will, sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets (including shares or other securities in any person) or operations (other than to a Group Company), unless such sale, transfer or disposal is earned out in the ordinary course of business and would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.9 Related party transactions
Without limiting Clause 13.2 (Compliance with laws), the Parent and the Issuer shall, and shall procure that all other Group Companies will, conduct all business transactions with any Affiliate which is not a Group Company at market terms and otherwise on an arm's length basis.
13.10
Dividends
The Parent may declare or make any Dividend Payment, provided that the Dividend Payment does not exceed the Permitted Dividend Amount in any calendar year, and where no unutilised portion of such amount may be carried forward.
