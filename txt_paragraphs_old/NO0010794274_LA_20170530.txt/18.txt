"Paying Agent" means the legal entity appointed by the Issuer to act as its paying agent with respect to the Bonds in the CSD.
"Permitted Dividend Amount" means a Dividend Payment that does not exceed NOK 15 million.
"Permitted Financial Indebtedness"
(a) any Financial Indebtedness incurred pursuant to the Finance Documents;
(b) Financial Indebtedness incurred pursuant to any Shareholder Loan agreement; .
(c) any Financial Indebtedness incurred under the Existing Facilities, and Financial Indebtedness incurred with an Acceptable Bank as lender at similar terms as the Existing Facilities;
(d) Financial Indebtedness related to hedging of interest rates or currency fluctuations in the ordinary course of business and on a non-speculative basis;
(e) Financial Indebtedness arising out of any Permitted Loan, Permitted Guarantee or Permitted Security;
(f) Financial Indebtedness incurred under any pension or tax liabilities in the ordinary course of business;
