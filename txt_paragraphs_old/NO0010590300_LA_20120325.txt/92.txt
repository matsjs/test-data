15
Events of Default
15.1 The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any o f the following events (which shall be referred to as an "Event of Default"):
(a) Non-payment The Obligors fail to fulfill any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or the Intercreditor Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion o f the Bond Trustee, it is obvious that such failure will be remedied, and payment in full is made within five (5) Business Days following the original due date.
(b) Breach o ffinancial covenants The Issuer or the Ultimate Parent fails to fulfill any o f the financial covenants set forth in clause 13.3.4 (a) and 13.4.2 (a), and such failure is not remedied within ten (10) Business Days after the relevant Reporting Date.
(c) Breach o f other obligations Any Obligor fails to duly perform or satisfy any other covenant or obligation pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any o f the Finance Documents, unless, in the opinion ofthe Bond Trustee, it is likely that such failure will be remedied and is remedied within ten (10) Business Days after written notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
(d) Cross default - USD 410M Facility and/or the GSH2 Junior Loan The USD 410M Facility and/or the GSH2 Junior Loan is declared due and payable or is capable o f being declared due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result o f an event o f default (however described).
