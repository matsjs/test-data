1. Terms defined in the Intercreditor Agreement shall, unless otherwise defined in this Agreement, bear the same meaning when used in this Agreement.
2. As o f [the date hereof / [ ]] the Acceding Borrower shall become a party to the Intercreditor Agreement and agrees that it shall be bound by all the provisions of the Intercreditor Agreement as if it had been an original party to the Intercreditor Agreement.

4. [The Acceding Borrower irrevocably appoints [ ] as its agent for service of process relating to any proceedings before the Norwegian courts in connection with this Agreement. The Acceding Borrower agrees that failure by its process agent to notify it of the process will not invalidate the proceedings concerned, and consents to the service of process to any such proceedings before the Norwegian courts by posting a copy of the process to above mentioned process agent.]
