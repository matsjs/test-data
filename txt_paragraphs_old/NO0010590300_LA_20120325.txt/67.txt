For the avoidance o f doubt, the redemption price shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date the repayment is carried out.
If the Mandatory Prepayment Event applies to (i) one Vessel or company only, the aggregate face value o f Bonds to be redeemed as described in clause 10.4.1 shall be 25 per cent of the Outstanding Bonds, (ii) two Vessels or companies only (either in a single transaction/incident or a series o f transactions/incidents), the aggregate face value of Bonds to be redeemed as described in clause 10.4.1 shall be 50 per cent o f the Outstanding Bonds (for the avoidance o f doubt, in case o f a series o f transactions or incidents, Outstanding Bonds means the amount o f Outstanding Bonds on the day immediately before the first transaction/incident), and (iii) three or more Vessels or companies (either in a single transaction/incident or a series o f transactions/incidents), the entire Bond Issue shall be redeemed (the "Redemption Amount").

Payments
11.1 Payment mechantes
11.1.1 The Issuer shall pay all amounts due to the Bondholders under the Bonds and this Bond Agreements by crediting the bank account nominated by each Bondholder in connection with its securities account in the Securities Register.
11.1.2
Payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank which holds the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, but if the paying bank and the receiving bank are the same, payment shall be considered to have been made once the amount has been credited to the bank account nominated by the Bondholder in question, see however clause 11.2.
