13.4 Covenants undertaken by the Ultimate Parent
(a) Financial Statements The Ultimate Parent shall, without being requested to do so, produce Financial Statements and Semi-annual Financial Reports o f itself as parent company and o f the Group on a consolidated basis, which shall be made available on its website (alternatively, by sending them to the Bond Trustee) as soon as they become available, and not later than 120 days after the end o f the financial year and 60 days after the end of the relevant interim period.
(b) Compliance Certificate The Ultimate Parent shall in connection with the issue o f its Semi-annual Financial Reports under clause 13.4.1 (a) (each a "Reporting Date"), confirm to the Bond Trustee in writing the Ultimate Parent's compliance with the covenants in this clause 13. Such confirmation shall be undertaken in a compliance certificate, substantially in the format set out in Attachment 3 hereto, signed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chief Financial Officer o f the Ultimate Parent. In the event of non-compliance, the compliance certificate shall describe the non-compliance, the reasons therefore as well as the steps which the Ultimate Parent has taken and will take in order to rectify the non-compliance.
(c) Change o f Control Event The Ultimate Parent shall notify the Bond Trustee in writing o f any Change o f Control Event immediately after it has become aware o f the occurrence o f same.
