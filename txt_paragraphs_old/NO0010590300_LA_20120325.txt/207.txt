BORROWERS' UNDERTAKINGS
The Borrowers, as security providers for the GSH2 Junior Loan under the First Mortgages and the First Assignments of Insurances, undertake with the Junior Mortgagee for as long as the Junior Indebtedness is outstanding or capable of becoming outstanding that:
a)
in the event that any of the First Mortgages and/or First Assignments of Insurances is
enforced in order to satisfy any claim under the GSH2 Junior Loan, any monies the relevant
Borrower receives due to its claim for reimbursement from GSH2, shall forthwith be paid to
the Junior Mortgagee until all sums due under the Bond Loan have been irrevocably paid in
full; and.
