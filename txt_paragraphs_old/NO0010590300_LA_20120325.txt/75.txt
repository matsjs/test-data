13.3.2 General Covenants
(b) Mergers No Vessel Owning Company nor Company Guarantor shall carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving consolidating their respective assets and obligations with any other company or entity if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect. Solvent intragroup reorganization shall be permitted. The relevant Vessel Owning Company or Company Guarantor shall notify the Bond Trustee o f any such transaction other than solvent intra-group reorganization, providing relevant details thereof, as well as, if applicable, its reasons for believing that the proposed transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
