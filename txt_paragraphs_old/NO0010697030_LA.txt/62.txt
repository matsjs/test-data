13.4.7
No additional security
Notwithstanding the other provisions in this Clause 13, no Obligor shall and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance or security interest over any of its present or future assets or its revenues which are subject to the Security created by the security documents relating to the New Bonds from time to time. 13.4.8 Accounts and payments
Each of the relevant Obligors shall maintain the Earnings Accounts with Account Bank(s) and shall procure that all Project Proceeds are paid directly into the relevant Earnings Account from the relevant contract party, provided however, that each such Obligor shall be entitled to change the Account Bank (and thus the relevant Earnings Account(s)) provided that adequate Security (Earnings Account Pledge) may be established over the new Earnings Account.
