2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 1,399,050,000 (Norwegian kroner one billion three hundred and ninety nine million andfifty thousand).
2.2.2 The Face Value is NOK 1. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
2.2.3 The Bond Issue will be described as "6.00 per cent Norwegian Energy Company ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2016".
2.2.4 The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 069703.0.
2.2.5 The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
