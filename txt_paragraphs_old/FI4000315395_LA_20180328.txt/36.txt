If Notes representing more than seventy-five (75) per cent of the aggregate nominal
principal amount of the Notes have been repurchased pursuant to this Clause 8.5, the Issuer
is entitled to repurchase all the remaining outstanding Notes at the price stated in Clause
8.5.1 above by notifying the remaining Noteholders of its intention to do so no later than
twenty (20) Business Days after the latest possible repurchase date pursuant to Clause
8.5.2. Such prepayment may occur at the earliest on the tenth CSD Business Day following
the date of such notice.
