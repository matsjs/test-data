INTEREST
7.1
Each Note carries Interest at the Interest Rate from (and including) the Issue Date up to
(but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date.
7.2
Interest accrues during each Interest Period. Payment of Interest in respect of the Notes
shall be made to the Noteholders on each Interest Payment Date for the preceding Interest
Period.
