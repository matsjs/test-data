Financial Indebtedness
Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any indebtedness or liability to a (direct or indirect) shareholder of the Issuer (or any Affiliates thereof).
Financial Support
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, grant any loans, guarantees, security or other financial assistance ("Financial Support") to or for the benefit of any Person not being a Group Company, except to the extent such Financial Support is provided within the ordinary course of a Group Company's business.
