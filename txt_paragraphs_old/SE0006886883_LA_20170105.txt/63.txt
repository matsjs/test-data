25.
LISTING
The Issuer intends to list the Bonds within thirty (30) calendar days after the Issue Date on First North Bond Market. Further, the Issuer has undertaken to list the Bonds on First North Bond Market within one hundred and eighty (180) calendar days after the Issue Date and to maintain such listing for as long as any Bond remains outstanding in accordance with Clause 12.2 (.Listing o fBonds).
