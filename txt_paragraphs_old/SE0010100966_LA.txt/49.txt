13.1 General
13.2 Distributions
(a) The Issuer shall not and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will:
(i) pay any dividend on its shares;
(ii) repurchase any of its own shares;
(iii) redeem its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders;
(iv) repay any Subordinated Loans or capitalised or accrued interest thereunder; or
(v) make any other similar distribution or transfers of value to the direct or indirect shareholder of the Issuer, or any Affiliates of the Issuer,
(subparagraphs (i)-(v) above each being a "Restricted Payment") provided that a Restricted Payment may be made if such Restricted Payment constitutes a Permitted Restricted Payment.
