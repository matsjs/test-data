"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds which are owned by the Issuer or any Affiliate of the Issuer.
"Issuer Share Pledge" means a first priority Norwegian law pledge granted by the Parent over all of the shares (100%) in the Issuer.
"Kirberg Partnership Pledge" a first priority pledge granted by SPV 2 and the Issuer over their partnership interests (Nw. KS andeler) in Kirberg.
"Kirberg" means Kirberg Shipping KS, a kommandittselskap with registration number 985 792 151, being the sole legal and beneficial owner of MS Nordlys, which shall be jointly owned by SPV 2 as general partner (Nw. komplementar) and the Issuer as owner of the partnership shares (Nw. kommandittist).
"Longstop Date" means the date falling 60 calendar days after the Issue Date.
