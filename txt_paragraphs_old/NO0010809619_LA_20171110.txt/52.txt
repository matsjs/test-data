10.6 Mandatory Redemption - sale or disposal (a) Upon a sale or disposal of a Vessel, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer receives the proceeds from the relevant sale or disposal, redeem a principal amount of Bonds equivalent to the Redemption Amount at the redemption prices equal to the applicable call price at that time pursuant to paragraph (a) o f Clause 10.2 ( Voluntary early redemption - Call Option) (determined based on the date the sale or disposal occurred and not based on the date the repayment is carried out).
(b) After a sale or disposal has occurred and prepayment of Bonds has been canned out, the Bond Trustee shall upon the request and cost of the Issuer, release the Security relating solely to the Vessel sold or otherwise disposed of.
(c) The net proceeds received from a sale or disposal after deducting (i) the amount payable in accordance with paragraph (a) of this Clause 10.6 and (ii) any fees or expenses related to the sale or other disposal, shall be transferred to the Issuer.
