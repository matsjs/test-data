15.5 Bareboat Charter Contracts The Issuer shall ensure that:
(a) each of the Bareboat Charter Contracts are entered into on arm's length terms with a minimum term of five (5) years; and
(b) no amendments, supplements, variations or waiver of the terms of the Bareboat Charter Contracts are made if any such amendment, supplement, variation or waiver would have a Material Adverse Effect.
