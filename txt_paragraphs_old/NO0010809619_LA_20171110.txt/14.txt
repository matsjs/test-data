"Intercompany Loans" means any subordinated loans granted or to be granted from one Group Company to another Group Company.
"Intercompany Loan Pledge" means a first priority pledge over monetary claims under any Intercompany Loan (other than Intercompany Loans from Kirberg).
"Interest Payment Date" means the last day of each Interest Period, the first Interest Payment Date being 16 February 2018 and the last Interest Payment Date being the Maturity Date.
"Interest Period" means, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Business Day Convention, the periods between 16 February, 16 May, 16 August and 16 November each year, provided however that an Interest Period shall not extend beyond the Maturity Date.
