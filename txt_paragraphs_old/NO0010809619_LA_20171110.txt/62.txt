14.13
Dividends
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, servicing of shareholder loans or other capital distributions to its shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party).
14.14
Sale- and leaseback
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company will, enter into any sale- and leaseback transactions other than any sale- and leaseback transactions entered into in connection with the acquisition of the Vessels as provided for in Clause 2.3 {Use o f Proceeds).
