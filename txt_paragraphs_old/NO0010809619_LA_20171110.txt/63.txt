The Issuer shall procure that all payments under each Bareboat Charter Contract is made to the relevant Earnings Account and that payments from any Earnings Account in the Issuer are only made (i) to a Subsidiary, (ii) for servicing Financial Indebtedness under the Finance Documents and/or (iii) in the ordinary course of business of the Issuer.
