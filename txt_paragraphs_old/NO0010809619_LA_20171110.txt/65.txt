The Parent shall procure that:
(a) the Vessels are operated in all material respects in accordance with applicable laws and regulations and good industry standards;
(b) the Vessels maintain flag and class and remain registered in the Norwegian ship register (NIS or NOR) or another ship registry acceptable to the Bond Trustee and in consistency with prudent ownership and good industry standards.
15.2 Maintenance of Vessels (a) The Parent shall procure that the Vessels and all relevant equipment related thereto are reasonably and satisfactorily maintained at all times.
(b) During operation of the Vessels, the Issuer shall ensure that the Vessels are properly maintained and kept in good and safe condition and that repairs are consistent with prudent ownership and industiy standards.
15.3 Insurances The Parent shall procure that:
(a) insurance of the Vessels are taken out and maintained with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters, including adequate insurance arrangements with respect to its assets, equipment and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as are consistent with prudent business practice in their relevant jurisdiction; and
(b) the aggregate value of the hull and machineiy insurance, hull interest insurance and freight interest insurance shall be at least equal to the higher of 100 per cent, of the Market Value and 120 per cent, of the principal amount of Bonds outstanding, less any amount on the Escrow Account, at any time.2
