2.1 Amount, denomination and ISIN of the Bonds (a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 400,000,000.
(c) The Initial Nominal Amount of each Bond is NOK 1.
(d) The ISIN of the Bonds is NO 001 0809619. All Bonds issued under the same ISIN will have identical terms and conditions as set out in these Bond Terms.
2.2 Tenor of the Bonds The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to but excluding the Maturity Date.
2.3 Use of proceeds
The Issuer will use the net proceeds (net of legal costs, fees of the Managers and the Bond Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) from the issuance of the Bonds for (i) funding of the Group's acquisition of MS Richard With, (ii) funding of the Group's acquisition of Kirberg and (iii) partly funding o f the upgrades of MS Richard With and MS Nordlys.
