(c) Financial assistance

The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to granting security) to or on behalf of any third party, except for (i) financial assistance to or for the benefit of a member of the Group (ii) financial assistance made or allowed to subsist in its ordinary course of business, (iii) financial assistance as part of customary long term financing of current and future vessels of the Group or to Material Investment Companies (iv) the Guarantee and Security granted or to be granted as security for the Bonds and/or (v) any guarantee and security granted to secure any currency and/or hedging liability provided that such guarantee and security is subject to the Intercreditor Agreement.
(d) Transactions on arm's length terms
The Parent shall not engage in, directly or indirectly and ensure that no Group Company engages in, any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of that Group Company's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to that Group Company than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(e) Material Adverse Effect
The Parent shall not and shall ensure that no Obligor does anything that might have a Material Adverse Effect, including but not limited to (i) cease to carry on its business, (ii) sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its business or (iii) change the general nature of its business from that carried out on the date of this Bond Agreement.
(f) Restructuring
The Parent undertakes, that by 1 September 2013, it shall have completed the restructuring of the Group which includes the establishment of two new companies to hold any intra-group charterparties and external charterparties that Volstad Seismic currently holds, save for the Charters.
