No Vessel Owner shall change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
Each Vessel Owner shall carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
(h) Maintenance of ownership
Each Vessel Owner shall remain the sole and direct owner of the relevant Vessel(s) (subject to mandatory prepayment provisions).
(i) Capital expenditures
No Vessel Owner shall agree to or make any acquisitions of other companies or the assets of other companies, nor make or commit to any other capital expenditures exceeding NOK 20,000,000 in aggregate during one calendar year (save for normal operating expenses like dry docking, periodical maintenance/break-downs, etc. relating to the Vessels) unless such additional capital is provided by the Parent by way of Subordinated Loans.
(j) Negative Pledge
No Vessel Owner shall incur or allow to exist any Security on the Security Interest , except for Permitted Encumbrances.
