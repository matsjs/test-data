14. COVENANTS
14.2 Information Covenants 14.2.1 Each Vessel Owner and the Parent shall:
(a) without being requested to do so, promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing of any Event of Default, any event or circumstance which the Issuer or the Parent understands or ought to understand may lead to an Event of Default and any other event which may have a Material Adverse Effect;
(b) without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and make them available on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata), in addition to sending them to the Bond Trustee, as soon as they become available, and not later than 120 days after the end of the financial year; and
(c) without being requested to do so, prepare Interim Accounts and make them available on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication on Stamdata), in addition to sending them to the Bond Trustee, as soon as they become available, and not later than 60 days after the end of the relevant quarter.

(a) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee in writing if the Issuer or the Parent agrees to sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, or change the nature of its business;
