(d) Continuation of business
No Vessel Owner shall cease to carry on its business. Each Vessel Owner shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of the Bond Agreement, or as contemplated by the Bond Agreement.
(e) Arm's length transactions
No Vessel Owner shall engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of that Vessel Owner's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to that Vessel Owner than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(f) Corporate status
No Vessel Owner shall change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
