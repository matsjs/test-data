"Margin" means 6.50 per cent per annum.
"Market Adjusted Equity Ratio" means the ratio of Market Adjusted Equity to Market Adjusted Total Assets.
"Market Adjusted Total Assets" means the Total Assets, adjusted for any Excess Value.
"Market Value" means the fair market value of a vessel determined as the arithmetic mean of independent valuations of the vessel obtained from two independent and wellreputed sale and purchase brokers familiar with the market for the vessel appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee. Such valuation shall be made on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms as between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts for employment, and shall be for the cost of the Issuer.
"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on;
(a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Issuer and/or any of the Guarantors' and/or the Group (taken as a whole);
(b) any of the Obligors' ability to perform and comply with its obligations under any of the Finance Documents; or
(c) the validity or enforceability of any of the Finance Documents.
"Material Investment Company" means any company or companies in which any member of the Group has an ownership interest, and which are considered material investments of the Group.
