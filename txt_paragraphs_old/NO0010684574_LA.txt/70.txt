14.3.2 Earnings Account (a) The Parent shall;
(i) ensure that each of the Vessel Owners and Volstad Seismic opens and maintains an Earnings Account (with a bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee) held in the name of the Issuer, Volstad Offshore and Volstad Seismic respectively; and
(ii) procure that all earnings related to each Vessel shall be paid directly to the relevant (and final) Earnings Account.
(b) For the avoidance of doubt, if such earnings related to the Vessels are received in more than one currency, the relevant parties shall open additional Earnings Accounts in such other currencies on the same basis as described in paragraph (a) above.
(c) The Earnings Accounts shall be pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee, but not blocked (until an Event of Default which is continuing has been declared pursuant to Clause16.2 and 16.3) as further set out in the Earnings Account Pledge.
14.3.3
Debt Service Account
Commencing one calendar month after the Issue Date, the Issuer shall and the Parent shall ensure that the Issuer transfer monthly from its Earnings Account into an account (the "Debt Service Account") an amount equal to the aggregate of (i) 1/6 of the next payable scheduled instalment (excluding the final repayment) and (ii) 1/3 of the Margin payable on the next Interest Payment Day. If the Issuer does not have sufficient means to credit the earnings account as set out above, Volstad Offshore shall transfer necessary funds to cover the shortage. The Debt Service Account shall be pledged and blocked in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders). The deposited amount shall only be released for the interest and amortization payments as and when they fall due.
