11.2 11.2.1
Mandatory Prepayment Event
Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring, the Issuer shall on the day the relevant Obligor receives the proceeds from the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event (however no later than the date that the relevant asset is transferred to the new owner, subject to a closing procedure and to an escrow account as agreed with the Bond Trustee), redeem;
(a) If the Vessel M/V Bibby Topaz is sold or the 100% direct ownership of the Issuer changes, 100% of the Outstanding Bonds at a price set out in Clause 11.2.2 (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount).
(b) In relation to the sale or disposal of the Vessels Geco Tau and/or Geco Bluefin and/or any of the Parent's shares in Volstad Offshore to be applied towards the Outstanding Bonds at a price set out immediately below with an amount of Bonds with aggregate Face Value equal to the net proceeds (sales price less any potential sales expenses).
(c) In relation to a dilution of Parent's ownership interest in Volstad Offshore by issuance of new shares of Volstad Offshore, an amount of Bonds with aggregate Face Value equal to the calculated market value of the diluted ownership interest. Such market value shall be calculated by first calculating the implied market value of Volstad Offshore based on such transaction, and then calculate the market value of diluted share of Volstad Offshore.
