The Parent may not within any financial year, during the term of the Bonds, declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions or loans to its shareholders exceeding NOK 10,000,000 in aggregate. Such dividend payment shall further (i) not exceed 50% of the net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year and (ii) be subject to the Vessel Owners and the Parent being in compliance with their respective covenants prior to and subsequent of such permitted dividends being made. Any unutilized portion of the permitted dividends may not be carried forward.
The Parent shall procure that neither it nor any of the Obligors;
(i) ceases to carry on its business;
(ii) sells or disposes of all or a substantial part of its operations;
(iii) changes the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of the Bond Agreement, other than expansion of the business into related activities; or
(iv) de-merges, merges or carries out any other corporate restructuring,
if such transaction in item (i) through (iv) would have a Material Adverse Effect.
