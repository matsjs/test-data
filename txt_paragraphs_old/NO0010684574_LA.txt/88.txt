(e) Insolvency/
(i) A Group Company or a Material Investment Company is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of its creditors with a view to rescheduling any of its indebtedness;
(ii) The value of the assets of any Group Company or of a Material Investment Company is less than its liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities); or
(iii) A moratorium is declared in respect of any indebtedness of any Group Company or any Material Investment Company,
always provided that no default shall be deemed to have occurred if a situation set out in (i) to (iii) above occurs in respect of a Group Company or a Material Investment Company which is not an Obligor and which has aggregate liabilities and indebtedness of less than NOK 20,000,000.
