"Subordinated Loan" means any loan or loans provided by the Parent to the Vessel Owners or any other Guarantor subject to any such loan being subordinated in rank, and subject to a Subordinated Loan Agreement. The rights of the Parent under any Subordinated Loan shall further be subject to the Assignment of Subordinated Loans.
(a) in relation to any Subordinated Loans, such loan(s) shall (i) be fully subordinated to the Bonds, (ii) be non-amortizing and have a maturity date which falls after the Maturity Date, and (iii) earn interest by way of accumulation and be added to the principal of such loan during the term of the Bonds and
(b) in general, the Parent shall (i) refrain from taking any enforcement or other formal action against the Guarantors in respect of any amount outstanding and shall, after the occurrence of an Event of Default and as long as it is continuing, accept that any and all amounts which may be outstanding against the Guarantors shall be subordinated in all respects to the claims of the Bondholders, and (ii) procure that

"Subordinated Mortgage" means any mortgage securing any currency and/or hedging liability, provided that such security is provided on second priority and always to be subject to the Intercreditor Agreement.
"Subsidiary" means a company over which another company has Decisive Influence.
