"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
"Bonds" means the debt instruments issued by the Issuer pursuant to this Bond Agreement.
"Book Equity" means the aggregate book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's total equity treated as equity in accordance with GAAP.
"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo.
"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Interest Payment Date falls on a day that is not a Business Day, that date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day (the "Modified Following Business Day Convention'').

(c) cash in hand or amounts standing to the credit of any current and/or on deposit accounts with an Acceptable Bank; and
(d) time deposits with acceptable banks and certificates of deposits issued, and bills of exchange accepted, by an Acceptable Bank,
in each case, to which any Group Company is beneficially entitled at that time and to which is available to any such Group Company. Any amount standing to the credit of any pledged earnings and/or debt service accounts of a Group Company shall be regarded as Cash and Cash Equivalents as long as no event of default has occurred under the finance documents relating to any such encumbrance.
