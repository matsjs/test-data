9.5
Immediate recourse
Each Guarantor waives any right it may have of first requiring Bond Trustee (or any trustee or agent on its behalf) to proceed against or enforce any other rights or security or claim payment from any person before claiming from that Guarantor under this Clause 9. This waiver applies irrespective of any law or any provision of a Finance Document to the contrary.
