"Maximum Issue Amount" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 2.1 (Amount, denomination and ISIN o f the Bonds).
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means, at any time, the sum of the:
(a) consolidated, aggregate interest bearing Financial Indebtedness of the wholly owned members of the Group but after excluding Subordinated Capital and any Bonds owned by the Issuer and after deducting cash and cash equivalents of the Group; and
(b) the pro rata part of any interest bearing Financial Indebtedness of (i) any other real estate owning entity in which the Issuer (directly or indirectly) has an ownership interest and/or (ii) any intermediate holding company of any entity referred to in paragraph (i), but, in each case, after deducting a pro rata part of any cash and cash equivalents o f that entity.
"Net LTV" means the Net Interest Bearing Debt divided by the Market Value of the Portfolio, based on valuations that are no older than 270 days.
"Nominal Amount" means the Initial Nominal Amount less the aggregate amount by which each Bond has been partially redeemed pursuant to Clause 10 (Redemption and repurchase of Bonds).
"Outstanding Bonds" means any Bonds issued in accordance with these Bond Terms to the extent not redeemed or otherwise discharged.
"Overdue Amount" means any amount required to be paid by the Issuer under any of the Finance Documents but not made available to the Bondholders on the relevant Payment Date or otherwise not paid on its applicable due date.
"Overdraft Facility" means the overdraft facility provided by Pareto Bank ASA of NOK 45,000,000 which matures in April 2020.
