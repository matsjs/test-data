The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 1,500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one billion fivehundred millions).
The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The Initial Issue will be in the amount of NOK 750,000,000 (Norwegian kroner seven hundred and fifty million).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN GasLog Ltd. Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2016/2021".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001076785.8.
