15.1.1.5 Insolvency
(i)
The Issuer or a Material Group Company is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they
fall due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or anticipated
financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of its creditors with a view to
rescheduling any of its indebtedness.
(ii)
The value of the assets of the Issuer or any Material Group Company is less than its liabilities
