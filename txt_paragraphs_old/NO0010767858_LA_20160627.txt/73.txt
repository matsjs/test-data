13.4.1.3 Listing During the term of the Bonds, the Issuer shall ensure that the Issuer's shares remain listed on the New York Stock Exchange or another recognized stock exchange.
13.4.1.4 MLP The Issuer shall maintain full control over the MLP through directly or indirectly controlling 100% of the voting rights and equity of the General Partner of the MLP.
13.4.1.5 Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and it shall ensure that no Group Companies shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrances over any of the Issuer's shares in the General Partner or the MLP.
