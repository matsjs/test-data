18.5 The Independent Engineer The engagement of the Independent Engineer will last until the Independent Engineer issues the Project Completion Confirmation, and the Independent Engineer's scope of work will be governed by a separate agreement. The Independent Engineer shall, inter alia, monitor the Project Construction Plan, the Master Control Budget, countersign the Issuer's requests for drawdown from the Escrow Accounts, review any construction reports from the Issuer and verify that Project Completion has been achieved. The Bond Trustee may, after consultation with the Issuer, replace the Independent Engineer with another independent engineer to act in such capacity.
