13.12
Accounts (a) General
(i) The Issuer shall establish and maintain the Accounts, which may be denominated in one or more of the Relevant Currencies.
(ii) All of the Accounts shall be maintained with an Account Bank, however the Escrow Account(s) shall be established through the Bond Trustee (or any of its Affiliates) with a bank located in Norway or such other jurisdiction as approved by the Bond Trustee. No accounts other than the Accounts shall be established or maintained by any Obligor.
