10. REDEMPTION AND REPURCHASE OF BONDS
10.1 Redemption of Bonds The Outstanding Bonds will mature in full on the Maturity Date and shall be redeemed by the Issuer on the Maturity Date at a price equal to one hundred per cent. (100%) of the Nominal Amount.
10.2 Voluntary early redemption - Call Option
(a) The Issuer may, subject to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement, redeem all but not only some of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Call Option") on any Business Day from and including:
(i) the Issue Date to, but not including, the First Call Date at a price equal to the Make Whole Amount;
