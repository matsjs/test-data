Payment of interest Tranche 1: The Issuer shall pay interest at a fixed rate of 7,5% per annum, where (i) 3.75% per annum is being payable on each Interest Payment Date and the Repayment Date, and (ii) the remaining Interest Rate of 3.75% shall be PIK Interest and be payable in the form of Additional Bonds to be issued on each Interest Payment Date based on the Initial Nominal Amount of the total number of Tranche 1 Bonds at each Interest Payment Date.
Tranche 2: The Issuer shall pay interests at a fixed rate of 8% per annum, where (i) 4% per annum is being payable on each Interest Payment Date and the Repayment Date, and (ii) the remaining Interest Rate of 4% shall be PIK Interest and be payable in the form of Additional Bonds to be issued on each Interest Payment Date based on the Initial Nominal Amount of the total number of Tranche 2 Bonds at each Interest Payment Date.
REDEMPTION AND REPURCHASE OF BONDS
Redemption of Bonds The Outstanding Bonds will mature in full on the Maturity Date and shall be redeemed by the Issuer on the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 per cent of the Nominal Amount.
The Issuer is entitled to a 12 month extension of the Maturity Date, subject to written notice to the Bond Trustee at least 90 (ninety) Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
