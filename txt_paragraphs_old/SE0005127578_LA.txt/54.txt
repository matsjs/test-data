PROPERTY UNDERTAKINGS
Disposal of assets
The Issuer shall not (and shall procure that no Restricted Company will) directly or indirectly sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of (i) the Structural Loans, (ii) the shares or other ownership interest in the Restricted Companies (in whole or in part) or (iii) permit any amalgamation, demerger, merger or corporate reconstruction of the Restricted Companies.
The Issuer shall not (and shall procure that no Restricted Company will) by way of a single transaction or a series of transactions (whether related or not) sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any Property (in whole or in part).
