"Equity" shall mean the total consolidated equity of the Group in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
"Total Assets" shall mean the total assets of the Group in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
"Value" shall mean (in each case as of the relevant Reference Date, save for the Cure Amount that should be determined with reference to Clause 14.2.3):
(a) the total aggregate Market Value of all Properties (other than the Property held by Klövern Tomadon, which shall be the lower of (i) its Market Value, and (ii) its Allocated Loan Amount);
(b) plus any amount deposited on an account following a disposal of a Property over which Security has been granted in accordance with Clause 13.1.3(b); and
(c) plus any Cure Amount injected pursuant to Clause 14.2.2.
Loan to Value
Loan to Value on each Reference Date shall not be higher than 0.75:1.
The Issuer may cure a breach of the Loan to Value covenant set out in Clause 14.2.1 by depositing Cure Amounts.
