9.5
9.5.1
Upon a Change of Control Event occurring, each Bondholder shall have the right to request
that all, or some of, its Bonds be repurchased at a price per Bond equal to 101 per cent, of
the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest, during a period of twenty
(20) Business Days following a notice from the Issuer of the Change of Control Event
pursuant to Clause 11.1.2 (after which time period such rights lapse). However, such
period may not start earlier than upon the occurrence of the Change of Control Event.
