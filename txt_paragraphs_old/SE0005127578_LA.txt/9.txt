"Market Value" means the market value for each Property pursuant to the most recent Valuation.
"Property" means each of the Properties listed in Schedule 1{Property information), excluding any Property that has been disposed of in accordance with Clause 13.1.3 and including any Replacement Property.
"Quotation Day" means, in relation to any period for which an interest rate is to be determined, two (2) Business Days before the first day of that period.
"Record Date" means the fifth (5) Business Day prior to an Interest Payment Date, a Redemption Date, a date on which a payment to the Bondholders is to be made under Clause 16 {Distribution o fproceeds), the date of a Bondholders' Meeting or another relevant date (or such other Business Day falling prior to a relevant date, if generally applicable on the Swedish bond market).
"Redemption Date" means the date on which the relevant Bonds are to be redeemed or repurchased in accordance with Clause 9 {Redemption and repurchase o f the Bonds).
"Reference Date" means each of 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December in each year during the term of the Bonds.
"Reference Period" means a twelve month period ending on a Reference Date or such shorter period as the context may require.
"Regulated Market" means any regulated market {reglerad marknad) (as defined in the Swedish Securities Market Act {lag (2007:528) om värdepappersmarknaden)).
"Replacement Property" has the meaning set forth in Clause 13.1.3(a).
