BONDHOLDERS' COMMITTEE
The Bondholders may appoint a committee (a "Bondholders' Committee") to represent
the interests of the Bondholders. A Bondholders' Committee shall consist of no less than
three (3) natural persons. All members of a Bondholders' Committee shall be elected at a
Bondholders' Meeting.
