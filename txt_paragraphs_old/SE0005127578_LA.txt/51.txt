Structure Loans
(a) documented in the form of negotiable promissory notes (löpande skuldebrev);
(b) disbursed in full on the Issue Date;
(c) as regards the Klövern Ett Loan, disbursed in an aggregate Allocated Loan Amount equal to the Nominal Amount, and, as regards the Klövern Tornadon Loan, disbursed in an Allocated Loan Amount equal to approximately 64 per cent, of the Market Value of the Property held by Klövern Tornadon;
(d) payable on demand;
(e) non-amortising and containing a provision that no payment of interest may be made following an Event of Default; and
(f) governed by Swedish law.
It is noted that Security is granted over the Structure Loans under the Security Documents and that the Restricted Companies may not make any repayment of principal under such loans (other than to the Agent) until the Bonds have been duly repaid in full and the Security has been released.
