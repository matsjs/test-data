Compliance with laws
Mergers
The Issuer shall not enter into any merger unless where the Issuer is the surviving entity.
Business of the Group
The Issuer shall not make any substantial change to the general nature of the business carried out by the Group on the Issue Date.
Negative pledge
The Issuer shall procure that the Restricted Companies do not create or allow to subsist any Security over the Properties other than the Security granted (directly or indirectly) to the Secured Parties pursuant to the Finance Documents.
