9 Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the Nominal Amount o f the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date to, but excluding, the Maturity Date, unless previously redeemed, at a fixed rate of 11.75% per cent per annum (the "Fixed Rate"),
9.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falls in September 2011.
9.3 The relevant amount of interest payable shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
9.4 The day count fraction in respect of the calculation o f the payable interest amount shall be "30/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in respect of which payment is being made divided by 360 (the number of days to be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months (unless (i) the last day of the calculation period is the 31 st day of a month but the first day of the calculation period is a day other than the 30th or 31 st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30day month, or (ii) the last day of the calculation period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month)).
9.5 The payable interest amount per Bond for a relevant calculation period shall be calculated as follows:
