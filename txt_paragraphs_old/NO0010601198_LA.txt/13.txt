"Financial Statements" means the audited unconsolidated and consolidated annual accounts and financial statements of the Issuer for any financial year, drawn up according to GAAP; such consolidated accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and report from the Board of Directors; and such unconsolidated accounts to include a profit and loss account and a balance sheet.
" Fixed Assets" means all real estate properties (together with any pertaining fixtures (Sw. fastighetsUllbehör)) owned by any Group Company, and any equipment, machinery, buildings, plant, production facilities or other tangible asset related to the Mine held for business use and owned by any Group Company. For the avoidance of doubt, equipment, machinery and other inventory not owned by the Issuer or any Group Company (e.g. leased equipment) shall not be included in the definition of Fixed Assets.
"Fixed Assets Pledge" means each pledge governed by Swedish law over the following:
(i) mortgages over the real estate properties Östhammar Harvik 4:28, Östhammar Films-Österby 3:5 and Östhammar Harvik 4:12 and pertaining fixtures which will be second in ranking until the Östhammar Municipality Loan has been finally discharged (from which point in time the mortgages will be first ranking);
(ii) first ranking mortgages over any future real estate and pertaining fixtures acquired by the Issuer and/or the Guarantors related to the Mine; and
(iii) 1st priority pledge over Fixed Assets owned or acquired (related to the Mine) by the Issuer and/or the Guarantors other than real estate properties.
