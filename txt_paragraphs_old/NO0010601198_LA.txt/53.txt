13.5 Presentation o f equity, Security Interests and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares or any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity. Notwithstanding the foregoing limitations, the Issuer shall however be entitled to repurchase its own shares to cover its potential obligations under any employees' and board members' (of the Issuer) bonus share program.
(b) Security Interests
Notwithstanding any other provision of this Bond Agreement, to the extent there exists Fixed Assets where granting security over such assets is not legally feasible under Swedish law or justifiable considering the value and benefit for the secured party and the costs and expenses of the legal entity providing that security, these assets shall be excluded from the granting of such security in which case, the Issuer and Guarantors undertakes not to create any security interest, lien, retention or other encumbrance over such assets (other than Permitted Encumbrance).
(c) Equity Ratio The Issuer undertakes to ensure that the Group has an Equity Ratio (on a consolidated basis) o f minimum 1/3 on the first reporting date following the Issue Date with audited accounts, not later than 30 June 2011.
