2.2 The Bonds
The Bonds will be in denominations o f USD 1,000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as " 11.75 per cent Dannemora Mineral AB Callable Senior Secured Bond Issue 2011/2016",
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001060119.8.
The term of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2 3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed firstly to complete the financing of the Dannemora Mine iron ore project and secondly for general corporate purposes.
