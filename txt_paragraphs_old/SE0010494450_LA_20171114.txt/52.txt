10.6 Mandatory redemption due to failure to fulfil the Conditions Precedent for First Disbursement
10.6.1
If the Conditions Precedent for First Disbursement have not been fulfilled on or before sixty (60) calendar days following the First Issue Date, the Issuer shall redeem all Bonds at a price equal to one hundred (100.00) per cent. of the Nominal Amount together with any accrued but unpaid interest.
10.6.2 The Agent may partly fund the redemption with the amounts standing to the credit on the Escrow Account.
10.6.3
The redemption of the Bonds shall (i) be executed on the first Interest Payment Date (taking into account the rules and regulations of the CSD) and (ii) be made by the Issuer giving not less than ten (10) Business Days' notice to the Bondholders and the Agent, where such notice shall state the relevant Interest Payment Date on which the redemption shall be made, the redemption amount and the relevant Record Date.
