CONDITION SUBSEQUENT
15.1 The Issuer shall provide evidence to the Agent, showing that the Existing Bonds have been repaid in full or rolled-over into Bonds. Such evidence to be provided as soon as possible after the Conditions Precedent for First Disbursement have been fulfilled and the payments from the Escrow Account have been made.
15.2 The Agent shall, after the Condition Subsequent has been fulfilled, instruct the Escrow Bank to transfer any residual funds from the Escrow Account to a bank account specified by the Issuer, to be used for general corporate purposes and subsequently release the Security created pursuant to the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement.
