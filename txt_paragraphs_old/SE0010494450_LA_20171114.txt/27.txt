USE OF PROCEEDS
The Net Proceeds from the Initial Bond Issue shall be transferred to the Escrow Account by
the Issuing Agent. For the purpose of securing that the Conditions Precedent for First
Disbursement have been fulfilled before any disbursement of the Net Proceeds is made, the
Escrow Account will pledged in favour of the Agent and the Bondholders (represented by
the Agent) in accordance with the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement. The pledge over the
Escrow Account shall be released when the Condition Subsequent set out in Clause 15
(Condition Subsequent) has been fulfilled and all payments from the Escrow Account have
been made.
