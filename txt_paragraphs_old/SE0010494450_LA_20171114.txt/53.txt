11.1.1 The Issuer shall procure that Cash and Cash Equivalents of the Group at all times is at least SEK 50,000,000.
11.1.2
The Maintenance Test shall be tested quarterly on the basis of the Financial Report for the period covered by the relevant Reference Date and set out in the Compliance Certificate delivered in connection therewith.
11.1.3 The first test date for the maintenance Test shall be 31 December 2017.
