"Sandsberg Projects" means the construction of 12 (Stage 1), 10 (Stage 2) and 18 (Stage 3) condominium apartments (Sw. bostadsrattslagenheter) in Sandsberg, Falun.
Events of Default:
Customary Events of Default for Swedish bond issues, including:
(a) Non-payment;
(b) Breach of other obligations in the Terms and Conditions;
(c) Cross-acceleration (subject to a SEK 5,000,000 threshold);
(d) Insolvency and Insolvency proceeding;
(e) Mergers and demergers (subject to Material Adverse Effect qualification);
(f) Creditors' process (subject to a SEK 5,000,000 threshold and not discharged within 30 days);
