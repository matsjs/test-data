Interest shall be payable semi-annually in arrears on each Interest Payment Date during the term of the Bonds.
Interest on the NOK Bonds will accrue from, and including, the Issue Date to, but excluding, the first Interest Payment Date. Interest on the SEK Bonds and EUR Bonds will accrue from, but excluding, the Issue Date to, and including, the first Interest Payment Date. In respect of subsequent interest periods, the period from, but excluding, an Interest Payment Date to, and including, the next succeeding Interest Payment Date (or a shorter period if relevant).
Day-count convention for the interest calculation is 30/360.
