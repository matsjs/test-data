Default interest:
If the Issuer fails to pay any amount due under the Terms and Conditions, the Issuer shall pay default interest on such amount at a rate corresponding to the Interest Rate plus five (5) percentage units, from and including, the date such payment was due up to and excluding the date of actual payment. Change of Control:
The occurrence of an event or series of events whereby the Parent, directly or indirectly, ceases to control (a) 100% of the shares or votes of the Issuer, or (b) the right to, directly or indirectly, appoint or remove the whole or a majority of the directors of the board of directors of the Issuer.
