Nominal value: Status of the loan:
The bonds will have a nominal value of USD 100,000.
The bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other senior obligations of the Issuer other than obligations which are mandatory preferred by law. The bonds shall rank ahead of subordinated capital.
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed for financing the planned acquisition of the Rig for USD 225 million, such acquisition to be finally approved by the general assembly of the Issuer and Petrolia Drilling ASA and for financing the construction program for rig(s) owned by subsidiaries of PetroMena ASA by financing payments under the relevant Construction Contract(s) and management cost related to the construction of rig(s) owned by subsidiaries of PetroMena ASA. This may include the establishement of performance bonds towards charterers (the "Guarantees") to secure employment of the Rig and the three rigs under construction. In addition, the proceeds may be used for servicing of the Bond described herein if not already used as described above.
