14 Fees and expenses
14.1 The Issuer shall cover all its own expenses in connection with this Note Agreement and fulfdment of its obligations under this Note Agreement, including preparation of this Note Agreement, preparation of the Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto, listing of the Notes on the Exchange (if applicable), and the registration and administration of the Notes in the Securities Register.
14.2 The expenses and fees payable to the Note Trustee (and/or the Security Agent, as the case may be) shall be paid by the Issuer and are set forth in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Note Trustee. Fees and expenses payable to the Note Trustee which, due to the Issuer's insolvency or similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the payments to the Noteholders.

14.3 The Issuer shall cover all public fees in connection with the Notes and the Finance Documents. Any public fees levied on the trade of Notes in the secondary market shall be paid by the Noteholders, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation, and the Issuer is not responsible for reimbursing any such fees.
14.4 In addition to the fee due to the Note Trustee pursuant to Clause 14.2 and normal expenses pursuant to Clauses 14.1 and 14.3, the Issuer shall, on demand, cover extraordinary expenses incurred by the Note Trustee in connection with the Notes, as determined in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Note Trustee.
