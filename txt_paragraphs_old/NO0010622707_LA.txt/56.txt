The Noteholders ' Meeting represents the supreme authority of the Noteholders community in all matters relating to the Notes. If a resolution by or an approval of the Noteholders is required, resolution of such shall be passed at a Noteholders' Meeting. Resolutions passed at Noteholders' Meetings shall be binding upon and prevail for all the Notes.
16.2.1 A Noteholders' Meeting shall be held at the request of:
(a) the Issuer, (b) Noteholders representing at least 1/10 of Voting Notes,

(c) the Exchange, if the Notes are listed, or (d) the Note Trustee.
16.2.2 The Noteholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Note Trustee. A request for a Noteholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Note Trustee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
16.2.3 If the Note Trustee has not summoned a Noteholders' Meeting within 10 - ten - Business Days after having received such a request, then the requesting party may summons the Noteholders' Meeting itself.
