10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts of the Note Issue or the entire Note Issue as follows (Call Option) at any time from Issue Date to, but not included, the Maturity Date at 100 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Note Trustee and the Noteholders and at least 10 calendar days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Notes (according to the procedures of the Security Depository).
