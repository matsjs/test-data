15.06
This Agreement may be executed in several counterparts and evidenced
by a facsimile copy of an original execution page bearing the signature of each party,
each of which when so executed shall be deemed to be an original, and such
counterparts or facsimile copies thereof together shall comprise one and the same
instrument and, notwithstanding their date of execution, shall be deemed to bear the
date as of the date first above written.
