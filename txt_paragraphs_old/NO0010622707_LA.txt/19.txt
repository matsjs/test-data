2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1
The net proceeds o f the Notes shall be employed partly to pay some of the outstanding trade creditors in the Ukraine and partly to engage contractors for the upcoming fracturing program o f the Karl 101 well in the Ukraine. The net proceeds shall serve as interim financing until the contemplated rights issue has been executed, which is expected to close mid September but at the latest by the end of November 2011.
