(i) Negative Pledge
Unless the Noteholders or the Note Trustee has given its written consent thereof, the Issuer shall not and shall ensure that none of its Subsidiaries with Sub-Subsidiaries shall create, permit to subsist or allow to exist over any of its present or future assets or revenues, any mortgage, pledge, lien or any other encumbrance other than the encumbrances granted for:
(a) any derivative transaction related to the Issuer's hedging policy;
(b) obligations incurred by any member of the Group in the ordinary course of business and as part of the daily operation by any such member of the Group; or
(c) any lien arising by operation of law.
