18.3 Limitation o f claims
18.3.1 All claims under the Notes and this Note Agreement for payment, including interest and principal, shall be subject to the time-bar provisions of the Norwegian Limitation Act of May 18, 1979 No. 18.
18.4 Access to information
18.4.1
The Note Agreement is available to anyone and copies may be obtained from the Note Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Note Agreement is available in copy form to the general public until all the Notes have been fully discharged.
18.4.2 The Note Trustee shall, in order to carry out its functions and obligations under the Note Agreement, have access to the Securities Register for the purposes of reviewing ownership of the Notes registered in the Securities Register.
