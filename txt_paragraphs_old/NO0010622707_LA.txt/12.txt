(iii) any other Subsidiary to which is transferred either (A) all or substantially all of the assets of another Subsidiary which immediately prior to the transfer was a Material Subsidiaiy or (B) sufficient assets of the Issuer that such Subsidiary would have been a Material Subsidiaiy had the transfer occurred on or before the relevant date,
