16.2.2 The Noteholders' Meeting shall be summoned by the Note Trustee. A request for a Noteholders' Meeting shall be made in writing to the Note Trustee, and shall clearly state the matters to be discussed.
16.2.4
Summons to a Noteholders Meeting shall be dispatched no later than 10 - ten - Business Days prior to the Noteholders' Meeting. The summons and a confirmation of each Noteholder's holdings of Notes shall be sent to all Noteholders registered in the Securities Register at the time of distribution. The summons shall also be sent to the Exchange for publication.
16.2.5
The summons shall specify the agenda of the Noteholders' Meeting. The Note Trustee may in the summons also set forth other matters on the agenda than those requested. If amendments to this Note Agreement have been proposed, the main content of the proposal shall be stated in the summons.
16.2.6 The Note Trustee may restrict the Issuer to make any changes of Voting Notes in the period from distribution of the summons until the Noteholders' Meeting, by serving notice to it to such effect.
16.2.7 Matters that have not been reported to the Noteholders in accordance with the procedural rules for summoning of a Noteholders' Meeting may only be adopted with the approval of all Voting Notes.
16.2.8
The Noteholders' Meeting shall be held on premises designated by the Note Trustee. The Noteholders' Meeting shall be opened and shall, unless otherwise decided by the Noteholders' Meeting, be chaired by the Note Trustee. If the Note Trustee is not present, the Noteholders' Meeting shall be opened by a Noteholder, and be chaired by a representative elected by the Noteholders' Meeting.
