16.2.11 Representatives of the Issuer have the right to attend the Noteholders' Meeting. The Noteholders' Meeting may resolve that the Issuer's representatives may not participate in particular matters. The Issuer has the right to be present under the voting.
16.3.1
At the Noteholders' Meeting each Noteholder may cast one vote for each Voting Note owned at close of business on the day prior to the date o f the Noteholders' Meeting in accordance with the records registered in the Securities Register. Whoever opens the Noteholders' Meeting shall adjudicate any question concerning which Notes shall count as the Issuer's Notes. The Issuer's Notes shall not have any voting rights.
16.3.2 In all matters, the Issuer, the Note Trustee and any Noteholder have the right to demand vote by ballot. In case of parity of votes, the chairman shall have the deciding vote, regardless o f the chairman being a Noteholder or not.
16.3.3 In order to form a quorum, at least half (1/2) of the Voting Notes must be represented at the meeting, see however Clause 16.4. Even if less than half (1/2) of the Voting Notes are represented, the Noteholders' Meeting shall be held and voting completed.
16.3.4 Resolutions shall be passed by simple majority of the Voting Notes represented at the Noteholders' Meeting, unless otherwise set forth in Clause 16.3.5.
