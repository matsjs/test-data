The Put Option must be exercised within two months after the Issuer has given notification to the Note Trustee and the Noteholders of a Change of Control Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event has taken place.
10.3.3
On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Noteholders holding Notes to be pre-paid, the principal amount of each such Note (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.3.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.4 Mandatory prepayment
10.4.1 Upon the occurrence of the Mandatory Prepayment, the Notes shall be redeemed at 100.00 % of redeemed amount plus accrued interests within 5 business days after the Contemplated Rights Issue has been completed and settled.
10.4.2
The Notes shall be redeemed using the proceeds of the Contemplated Rights Issue in accordance with a Rights Agency Agreement to be entered into between the Issuer and Computershare Trust Company of Canada, substantially in the form attached to this Note Agreement as "Schedule A".
10.4.3
The Note Trustee and the Noteholders shall be notified by the Issuer in writing about any mandatory prepayment according to this Clause 10.4 (Mandatory Prepayment), at least 10 calendar days prior to the settlement date of any such mandatory prepayment. On the settlement date of a mandatory prepayment, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Noteholders holding Notes to be redeemed, in respect of each such Note, the principal amount of such Note and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date of such mandatory prepayment.
