Conditions Precedent
6.1 Disbursement of the net proceeds of the Notes to the Issuer will be subject to the Note Trustee having received the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to it, at least two Business Days prior to the Issue Date:
(a) this Note Agreement duly executed by all parties thereto;
(b) certified copies of all necessary corporate resolutions (both from the Issuer and Mattson) to issue the Notes and execute the Finance Documents;
(c) a power of attorney from the Issuer and Mattson to relevant individuals for their execution of the relevant Finance Documents, or extracts from the relevant register or similar documentation evidencing the individuals authorized to sign on behalf of the Issuer;
(d) certified copies of (i) the Certificate of Incorporation or other similar official document for the Issuer and Mattson, evidencing that it is validly existing and (ii) Articles of Association of the Issuer and Mattson;
(e) the latest Financial Statements and Quarterly Financial Report;
