18.5 Amendments
18.5.1 All amendments of this Note Agreement shall be made in writing, and shall unless otherwise provided for by this Note Agreement, only be made with the approval of all parties hereto.
18.6 Notices, contact information
18.6.1
Written notices, warnings, summons etc to the Noteholders made by the Note Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Register with a copy to the Issuer and the Exchange. Information to the Noteholders may also be published at the web site www.stamdata.no.
18.6.2 The Issuer's written notifications to the Noteholders shall be sent via the Note Trustee, alternatively through the Securities Register with a copy to the Note Trustee and the Exchange.
