Minutes o f the Noteholders' Meeting shall be kept. The minutes shall state the numbers of Noteholders represented at the Noteholders' Meeting, the resolutions passed at the meeting, and the result of the voting. The minutes shall be signed by the chairman and at least one other person elected by the Noteholders' Meeting. The minutes shall be deposited with the Note Trustee and shall be available to the Noteholders.
16.2.10 The Noteholders, the Note Trustee and - provided the Notes are listed - representatives of the Exchange, have the right to attend the Noteholders' Meeting. The chairman may grant access to the meeting to other parties, unless the Noteholders' Meeting decides otherwise. Noteholders may attend by a representative holding proxy. Noteholders have the right to be assisted by an advisor. In case of dispute the chairman shall decide who may attend the Noteholders' Meeting and vote for the Notes.

16.2.11 Representatives of the Issuer have the right to attend the Noteholders' Meeting. The Noteholders' Meeting may resolve that the Issuer's representatives may not participate in particular matters. The Issuer has the right to be present under the voting.
