Admission to trading
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that the Note Loan is admitted to trading on a Regulated Market within six (6) months after issuance, and that it remains admitted or, if such admission to trading is not possible to obtain or maintain, admitted to trading on another Regulated Market.
Following an admission to trading, the Issuer shall take all actions on its part to maintain the admission as long as any Notes are outstanding, but not longer than up to and including the last day on which the admission reasonably can, pursuant to the then applicable regulations o f the Regulated Market and the CSD, subsist.
