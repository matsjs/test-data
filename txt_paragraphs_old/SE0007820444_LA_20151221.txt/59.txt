Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not (and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company will) create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets.
The Issuer shall not (and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company will):
(a) sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of its assets on terms whereby they are or may be leased to or re-acquired by any Group Company;
(b) sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of its receivables on recourse terms;
