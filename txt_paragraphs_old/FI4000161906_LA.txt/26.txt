7. Transfer restrictions
(a) No Bondholder may offer, sell, pledge or otherwise transfer any Bond except:
(i) to the Issuer;
(ii) outside the United States in compliance with Rule 903 or Rule 904, as applicable, of Regulation S under the Securities Act;
(iii) pursuant to an exemption from registration under the Securities Act provided by Rule 144 thereunder (if available);
