Cross acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness of the Issuer or a Group Company is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be or otherwise becomes due and payable prior to its specified maturity, as a result of an event of default (however described), provided that no Event of Default will occur under this paragraph 12.9 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness is less than SEK 100,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency).
