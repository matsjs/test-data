11.15 Undertakings relating to the CSD
The Issuer shall at all times keep the Notes affiliated with a CSD and comply with all applicable CSD Regulations.
12
Events of Default
Each of the events or circumstances set out in Clauses 12.1 to 12.10 is an Event of Default.
12.1
Non-Payment The Issuer does not pay on the due date any amount payable by it under the Finance Documents, unless the non-payment: (a) is caused by technical or administrative error; and (b) is remedied within five (5) Business Days from the due date.
