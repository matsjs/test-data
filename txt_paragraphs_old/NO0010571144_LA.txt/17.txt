"Permitted Security" means the Existing Security (or any replacement thereof, including any Encumbrance created as security for any Financial Indebtedness incurred to repay or refinance any Existing Financial Indebtedness) together with any Encumbrance:
(ii) granted or created by Nera Telecommunications;
(iii) arising by operation of law and in the ordinary course of trading and not arising as a result of any default or omission on the part of the Issuer or any other member of the Group and securing obligations of not more than thirty days' maturity, unless being contested in good faith;
(iv) arising under any retention of title arrangements entered into in the ordinary course of trading which are required by any supplier of any goods to any member of the Group in the normal course of such supplier's business;
(v) arising on accounts pursuant to standard netting, cash management or account zeroing arrangement;
(vi) over or affecting any asset of any company which becomes a Subsidiary after the date of this Agreement, where the Encumbrance is created prior to the date on which that company becomes a Subsidiary, if (A) the Encumbrance was not created in contemplation of the acquisition of that company, (B) the principal amount secured has not increased in contemplation of or since the acquisition of that company and (C) the Encumbrance is removed or discharged within six (6) months of the date of acquisition of that company; and
(vii) granted or created as security for any Permitted Financial Indebtedness not covered by sub-paragraph (i) through (vi) above up to an aggregate amount of NOK 1,000,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency or currencies).
"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Quarterly Accounts" means the unaudited consolidated accounts of the Issuer as of each Quarter Date, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary.
