"Permitted Financial Indebtedness" means the Existing Financial Indebtedness (or any Financial Indebtedness incurred to repay or refinance the Existing Financial Indebtedness) together with any Financial Indebtedness:
(ii) incurred by any Subsidiaries of the Issuer in an aggregate amount not at any time exceeding NOK 280,000,000 (or its equivalent in any currency or currencies);
(iii) incurred in the form of guarantees, indemnities, bonds, standby or documentary letters of credit or other instruments issued by a bank or financial institution on behalf of a member of the Group in the ordinary course of such member of the Group's trading and in an aggregate amount outstanding at any time not exceeding NOK 300,000,000 (or its equivalent in other currencies);
(iv) incurred by a member of the Group from another member of the Group;
(v) incurred through any derivative transaction entered into in the ordinary course of business in connection with protection against or benefit from fluctuation in any rate or price;
(vi) of any company which becomes a Subsidiary after the date of this Agreement, where the Financial Indebtedness is created prior to the date on which that company becomes a Subsidiary, if (A) the Financial Indebtedness was not created in contemplation of the acquisition of that company, (B) the principal amount secured has not increased in contemplation of or since the acquisition of that company and (C) the Financial Indebtedness is repaid or discharged within six (6) months of the date of acquisition of that company; and
(vii) not otherwise permitted by sub-paragraphs (i) through (vi) above in an aggregate amount outstanding at any time not exceeding NOK 700,000,000 (or its equivalent in any other currency or currencies).
