"EBITDA" means, in respect of any relevant period, (on a consolidated basis for the Group) the earnings on ordinary activities before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation. EBITDA to be calculated on a 12-month rolling basis.
"Equity Adjusted for Goodwill" means the Book Equity less the book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's goodwill treated as goodwill in accordance with GAAP, as set out in the most recent Financial Statements (or, as applicable, the most recent Quarterly Accounts) of the Issuer.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means securities exchange or other reputable marketplace for securities, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
