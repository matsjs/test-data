as set out in the Group's latest available Financial Statements or Quarterly Accounts (whether audited or unaudited, as the case may be) and, in each case, unencumbered by any security interest (other than pursuant to the Finance Documents, and in such case provided that the relevant member of the Group's use of such cash, deposit or cash equivalents is unrestricted).

"Material Adverse Effect" means an event or circumstance which has a material adverse effect on: (a) the business, financial condition or operations of the Group taken as a whole, or (b) the Issuer's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under this Bond Agreement.
