Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts o f the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including Interest Payment Date in January 2016 to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in January 2017 at 106 % o f par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount, and
