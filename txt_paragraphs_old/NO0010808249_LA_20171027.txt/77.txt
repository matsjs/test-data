13.8 Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist any security over any of its assets other than Permitted Security.
13.9 Financial support
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, grant or allow to subsist any loans or guarantees, or otherwise voluntarily assume any financial liability (whether actual or contingent) other than Permitted Financial Support.
