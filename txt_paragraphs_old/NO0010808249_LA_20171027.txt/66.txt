10.1 Redemption of Bonds The Outstanding Bonds will mature in full on the Maturity Date and shall he redeemed by the Issuer on the Maturity Date at a price equal to 100 per cent, of the Nominal Amount.
(a) The Issuer may redeem the Outstanding Bonds (in whole or in part) (the "Call Option") on any Business Day from and including:
