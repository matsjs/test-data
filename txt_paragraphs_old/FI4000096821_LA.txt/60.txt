21.2 Duties of the Bonds Agent
(a) The Bonds Agent shall represent the Bondholders in accordance with the Finance Documents, and, where relevant, in relation to instructions to the Security Agent to enforce the Transaction Security on behalf of the Bondholders.
(b) When acting in accordance with the Finance Documents, the Bonds Agent is always acting with binding effect on behalf of the Bondholders. The Bonds Agent shall carry out its duties under the Finance Documents in a reasonable, proficient and professional manner, with reasonable care and skill.
