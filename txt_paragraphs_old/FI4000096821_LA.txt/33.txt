(a) The Issuer may redeem the Bonds in whole, but not in part, at any time from and including:
(A) the present value on the relevant record date of 105.0 per cent, of the Outstanding Nominal Amount as if such payment originally should have taken place on the Interest Payment Date falling 24 months after the Issue Date; and
(B) the present value on the relevant record date of the remaining coupon payments (less any accrued but unpaid interest) through and including the date falling 24 months after the Issue Date,
both calculated by using a discount rate of 50 basis points over the comparable German government bond rate (i.e. comparable to the remaining duration of the Bonds until the mentioned date falling 30 months after the Issue Date) (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount) and where "relevant record date" shall mean a date agreed upon between the Bonds Agent, the CSD, the Paying Agent and the Issuer in connection with such repayment;
(ii) the date falling 24 months after the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 30 months after the Issue Date at a price equal to 105.0 per cent, of the Outstanding Nominal Amount (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount);
