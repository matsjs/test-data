10.4 Upon the occurrence of a Cash Sweep Amount, the Borrower shall prepay the Loan with any such Cash Sweep Amount as set out below. The Cash Sweep Amount shall be prepaid within 31 March 2009 for the Cash Sweep Amount accumulated during 2008.

"Cash Sweep Amount" means, in relation to 2008, the Cash Flow of the Borrower on a consolidated basis, less liabilities for accrued but unpaid taxes for 2008, and less debt service during 2008, with the addition of any voluntary prepayments of any financial debt during 2008, The cash sweep amount shall be limited upwards to NOK 75,000,000,-, and;
"Cash Flow" means EBITDA less the aggregate of change in working capital, maintenance capital expenditure and taxes paid.
The Bondholders shall be informed of the prepayment of any Cash Sweep Amount no later than 15 Banking Days before prepayment takes place, such prepayment to take place no later than 31 March 2008. The prepayment prise for each Bond is 100 % of par plus accrued interest.
Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out pro rata in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Register..
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
10.5 Upon a Mandatory Redemption Event, each Bondholder shall have a right of repayment (a "Put Option") of the Bonds at a price o f 108 % of par plus accrued interest.
The Put Option must be exercised within two months after the Borrower has given notification as set out in Clause 13.1 (h).
