The Loan will be described as "FRN Bergen Oilfield Sendees AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2008/2009".
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds of the Loan will be employed for the general financing of the Borrower.
The purpose of the Loan is to finance (i) the conversion of Vessels and for the purchase and installment of seismic equipment onboard the Vessels and (ii) purchase and installment of seismic equipment on board the "Stad Angler" (including the re installment, being 2D and/or 3D seismic equipment onboard "Stad Angler" expected to take place during fourth quarter 2008, in order to equip "Stad Angler" for seismic activities as a part of the Groups ordinary business).
