12.1 The Borrower has the right to acquire and own Borrower's Bonds. Borrower's Bonds may at the Borrower's discretion be retained by the Borrower, sold or used for partial redemption of the Remaining Loan.
13.1 During the term of the Loan the Borrower shall comply with the following information covenants:
(a) immediately inform the Loan Trustee of any default of the Loan pursuant to Clause 15 as well as of any circumstances which the Borrower understands or should understand may lead to default,
(b) submit to the Loan Trustee; (i) annually audited accounts and quarterly management accounts, (ii) annually audited accounts and annually management and board reports for any partnerships that the Borrower or the Group have ownership holdings in, (i) and (ii) to be published by the Loan Trustee to the Bondholders at the web site www.stamdata.no.
(c) submit to the Loan Trustee semi - annually limited audited accounts and management reports confirming that the Borrower is in compliance with the financial covenants set out in Clause 13.4 and a confirmation that it complies with the pledging of intra-group claims as set out under the definition of "Intra-Group Claim Pledge",
(d) at the request of the Loan Trustee send a report outlining the balance o f Borrower's Bonds,
(e) forward to the Loan Trustee copies of any creditors' notifications of the Borrower, including but not limited to; mergers, demergers and reduction of shareholders capital,
(f) at the request of the Loan Trustee provide the documents and information necessary to maintain the listing and quotation of the Loan on Exchange (if applicable) and to otherwise enable the Loan Trustee to carry out its rights and duties pursuant to the Loan Agreement, laws and regulations,
(g) within a reasonable time limit provide information about the Borrower's financial condition as the Loan Trustee may reasonably request,
