"EBITDA" means, in respect of the Relevant Period, the consolidated profit of the Group from ordinary activities according to the latest Financial Report(s):
(b) before deducting any Net Finance Charges;
(c) before taking into account any exceptional items which are not in line with the ordinary course of business;
(d) not including any accrued interest owing to any Group Company;
(e) before taking into account any unrealised gains or losses on any derivative instrument (other than any derivative instruments which is accounted for on a hedge account basis);
(f) after adding back or deducting, as the case may be, the amount of any loss or gain against book value arising on a disposal of any asset (other than in the ordinary course of trading) and any loss or gain arising from an upward or downward revaluation of any asset;
(g) after deducting the amount of any profit (or adding back the amount of any loss) of any Group Company which is attributable to minority interests;
(h) after adding back or deducting, as the case may be, the Group's share of the profits or losses of entities which are not part of the Group; and
(i) after adding back any amount attributable to the amortisation, depreciation or depletion of assets of Group Companies.
