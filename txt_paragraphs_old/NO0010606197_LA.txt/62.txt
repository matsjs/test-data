18.7 Dispute resolution and legal venue
All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Bond Agreement between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders and the Issuer, shall be exclusively resolved by the courts of Noiway, with the District Court of Oslo as sole legal venue.
