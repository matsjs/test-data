10.2.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option, such settlement date to be specified in the notice (with the minimum period).
10.2.3
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to he redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date
10.2.4 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.3 Put Option
10.3.1 Upon a Change of Control Event occurring, each Bondholder shall have a right of prepayment (the "Put Option") of the Bonds at a price of 100% of par value (plus accrued interest).
