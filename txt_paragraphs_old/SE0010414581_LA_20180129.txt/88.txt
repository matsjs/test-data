17.3
When consents from Noteholders representing the requisite majority of the total
Adjusted Nominal Amount pursuant to Clauses 15.7 and 15.8 have been received in a
Written Procedure, the relevant decision shall be deemed to be adopted pursuant to
Clause 15.7 and 15.8, as the case may be, even if the time period for replies in the
Written Procedure has not yet expired.
