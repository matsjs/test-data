Use of proceeds
The Issuer shall apply the net proceeds from the issue of the Notes, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Notes, firstly, towards general corporate purposes, which include but is in no way limited to the financing of property acquisitions, and secondly (if relevant and to the extent necessary), towards the refinancing of the 2018 Notes.
