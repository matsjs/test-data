22.1
A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer or any Group
Company to enforce or recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance
Documents, or to initiate, support or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation,
company reorganisation (Sw. foretagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (Sw. konkurs) (or
its equivalent in any other jurisdiction) of the Issuer in relation to any of the obligations
and liabilities of the Issuer or any such Group Company under the Finance Documents.
Such steps may only be taken by the Agent.
