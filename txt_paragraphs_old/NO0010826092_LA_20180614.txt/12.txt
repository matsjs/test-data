"Listing Failure Event" means:
(a) that the Bonds have not been admitted to listing on Oslo Børs within 12 months following the Issue Date, or
(b) in the case of a successful admission to listing, that a period of 6 months has elapsed since the Bonds ceased to be admitted to listing on Oslo Børs.
