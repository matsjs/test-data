(a) During the first two years after Disbursement Date the Guarantor shall not:
(i) make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares, or (ii) make distributions to its shareholders or enter into instruments with
similar effect to the foregoing.
(b) Thereafter, during the remaining term of the Loan, the Guarantor shall not
make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions to its shareholders that constitutes more than, on a consolidated basis, 25% of the Guarantor's net profit after taxes for the previous financial year. Any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or other distributions to its shareholders according to the above shall also be conditional on that the FPSO is operating on contract with minimum remaining duration equal the remaining duration of the loan. For the avoidance of doubt, any un-utilized portion o f the permitted dividend pursuant to the above may not be carried forward and be distributed in any subsequent calendar year. The above dividend restrictions will also apply to the Borrower if the Guarantor reduces its ownership share in the Borrower to below 100 %.
