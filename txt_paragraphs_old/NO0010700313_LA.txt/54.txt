The Issuer shall cover all its own expenses in connection with this Bond Agreement and fulfilment of its obligations under this Bond Agreement, including preparation of this Bond Agreement, preparation of the Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto and the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Register.
The Issuer shall pay any stamp duty and other public fees accruing in connection with the Bonds and the Finance Documents. Any public fees levied on the trade of Bonds in the secondary market shall be paid by the trading Bondholder, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation.
17.4 Extraordinary expenses
In addition to the Costs, fees and expenses due to the Bond Trustee and the Security Agent pursuant to Clause 17.1 and normal expenses pursuant to Clauses 17.2 and 17.3, the Issuer shall, on demand, pay extraordinary expenses incurred by the Bond Trustee and the Security Agent in connection with the Bonds, as determined in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee.
17.5 Withholding tax
If the Issuer is required by law to make a tax deduction or withholding from any payment under any Finance Document:
(a) the amount of the payment due from the Issuer shall be increased to such amount that is necessary to ensure that the Bondholders receive a net amount which is (after making the required tax deduction or withholding) equal to the payment which would have been due if no tax deduction or withholding had been required; and
(b) the Issuer shall at the request of the Bond Trustee deliver to the Bond Trustee evidence that the required Tax reduction or withholding has been made.
