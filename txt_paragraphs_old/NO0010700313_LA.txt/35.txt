8.17 Repetition The representations and warranties set out in Clause 8 are made on the execution date of this Bond Agreement, and shall be deemed to be repeated on the Settlement Date.
8.19 Indemnity Each Obligor shall indemnify the Bond Trustee for any economic losses suffered, both prior to the disbursement of the Bonds, and during the term of the Bonds, as a result of its reliance on the representations and warranties provided by that Obligor herein.
9. STATUS OF THE BONDS AND SECURITY 9.1 Status
The Bonds shall be super senior secured debt of the Issuer. The Bonds shall rank ahead of all other obligations of the Issuer (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application).
9.2 Security The Bonds, including accrued but unpaid interest and expenses, shall be secured by the Security Interests.
10. INTEREST 10.1 Interest rate
No interest shall be paid on the Bonds save where required under Clause 12.4.
10.2 Default Interest Payment Dates Interest payments required to be made as a result of Clause 12.4 shall be made in arrears on the relevant Default Interest Payment Dates.
10.3 Calculation of default interest payments (a) The relevant amount of default interest payable shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Default Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Default Interest Payment Date.
