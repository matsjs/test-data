2.4 Purpose and utilisation The proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of fees and legal costs of the Bond Trustee and any other costs and expenses) shall be applied towards the working capital needs of the Parent Guarantor and its Subsidiaries and may only be utilised in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Bond Agreement.
3. LISTING The Bonds will not be listed on an exchange.
4. REGISTRATION IN THE SECURITIES REGISTER 4.1 Registration
The Bond Issue and the Bonds shall prior to disbursement be registered in the Securities Register according to the Securities Register Act and the conditions of the Securities Register. Principal and accrued interest will be credited the Bondholders through the Securities Register.
4.2 Notifications The Issuer shall promptly arrange for notification to the Securities Register of any changes in the terms and conditions of this Bond Agreement. The Bond Trustee shall receive a copy of the notification.
