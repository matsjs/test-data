1. INTERPRETATION 1.1 Definitions
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Additional Existing Bonds Security" means
(a) the German law-governed security assignment of any receivables and proceeds resulting from the off-take contracts with (i) Salzgitter Flachstahl GmbH entered into on 4 January 2011 and (ii) Thyssen Rrupp Steel Europe AG entered into on 20 September 2011;
(b) the English law-governed security assignment of any receivables and proceeds resulting from any current and future iron ore sale and purchase contracts entered into with Stemcor UK Ltd.;
"Approved Liquidity Budget" means a liquidity budget and cash flow statement showing the costs and amounts to be payable during the period starting on 17 December 2013 and ending on 28 February 2014, approved by the Bond Trustee.
"Attachment" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including its Attachments, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bond Issue" means the Bonds in the principal nominal amount of SEK 21,000,000 to be issued on the Settlement Date.
