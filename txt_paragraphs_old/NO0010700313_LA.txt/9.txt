"Financial Statements" means the Issuer's and/or the Parent Guarantor's (as the case may be) audited consolidated (except the Issuer's annual accounts for 2011 which may be unconsolidated) annual accounts and financial statements for any financial year, drawn up according to GAAP, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and report from the Board of Directors.
"Group" means the Issuer and its Subsidiaries and direct and indirect holding companies from time to time and the Parent Guarantor and a "Group Company" means any of them.
"Guarantee" means an unconditional on-demand guarantee (No: pakravsgaranti) given by each Guarantor as set out in Clause 14.
"Guarantor" means each of the Parent Guarantor and the Sister Guarantor.
"Intercompany Loan" means any intercompany loan (which shall include cross-default provisions in respect of an Event of Default) between any member of the Group as lender and any other member of the Group as borrower.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Numbering system - the identification number of the Bonds.
"Issuer's Bonds" means Bonds owned by the Issuer, any party or parties who controls the Issuer, or any party or parties over whom the Issuer has control.
"Long-Term Financing" means the Group raising any new debt and/or equity capital in an amount exceeding USD 65,000,000 (or its equivalent in other currencies).
"Manager" means Swedbank as manager for the Bond Issue.
