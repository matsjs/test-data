14.4 Limitations
15. INFORMATION COVENANTS
The information covenants in this Clause 15 are given by each relevant Obligor to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), as further stated below.
The covenants in this Clause 15 shall remain in force from the date of this Bond Agreement and until such time that no amounts are outstanding under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, unless the Bond Trustee (or the Bondholders Meeting, as the case may be), has agreed in writing to waive any covenant, and then only to the extent of such waiver, and on the terms and conditions set forth in such waiver.
15.1 Notification of default The Issuer shall, without being requested to do so, immediately inform the Bond Trustee of any Event of Default as well as of any circumstances which the Issuer understands or should understand may lead to an Event of Default;
15.2 Miscellaneous information
The Issuer shall:
(a) promptly make available to the Bond Trustee (for further distribution to the Bondholders) all information about its business and financial situation as the Bond Trustee may reasonably require;
(b) without being requested to do so, inform the Bond Trustee of any other event which may result in a Material Adverse Change; or which may cause an Event of Default or a Drawdown Block Event;
