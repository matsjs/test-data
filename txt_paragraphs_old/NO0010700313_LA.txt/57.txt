18.5 Milestones
Any Milestone Condition is not satisfied by its applicable date unless such failure is remedied within 3 Business Days from such applicable date.
18.6 Restructuring Failure Event
At any time, it is or becomes evident that an implementation of a Restructuring (whether or not documented and agreed in a Restructuring Term Sheet at that time) that will leave the Group commercially and financially viable on a medium-term basis will fail (a "Restructuring Failure Event of Default").
