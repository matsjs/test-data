(a) In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clauses 18.1 18.13 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee can, in order to protect the interests of the Bondholders, declare the Outstanding Bonds including any default interest payable under Clause 12.4 and Costs to be in default and due for payment.
(c) In the event that one or more of the circumstances mentioned in Clauses 18.1 -- 18.13 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee shall declare the Outstanding Bonds including any default interest payable under Clause 12.4 and Costs to be in default and due for payment if:
(i) the Bond Trustee receives a demand in writing with respect to the above from Bondholders representing at least 1/5 of the Voting Bonds, and the Bondholders' Meeting has not determined otherwise; or
(ii) the Bondholders' Meeting has decided to declare the Outstanding Bonds in default and due for payment.
(d) In either case, the Bond Trustee shall on behalf of the Bondholders take every measure necessary to recover the amounts due under the Outstanding Bonds. The Bond Trustee can request satisfactory security for any possible liability and anticipated expenses, from those Bondholders who requested that the declaration of default be made pursuant to sub clause (c)(i) above and/or those who voted in favour o f the decision pursuant to sub clause (c)(ii) above.
(e) In the event that the Bond Trustee pursuant to the terms of Clause 18.14(a) or (c) declares the Outstanding Bonds to be in default and due for payment, the Bond Trustee shall immediately deliver to the Issuer a notice demanding payment of principal and any default interest payable under Clause 12.4 due to the Bondholders under the Outstanding Bonds. Declaration of default shall be deemed as a mandatory prepayment situation and the Outstanding Loan shall be repaid at the rates as set out in Clause 11.3.
(f) In the event of partial payments of any amount pursuant to this Bond Agreement, such payments shall first be allocated to pay the Bond Trustee for incurred fees and expenses, and thereafter to settle any other outstanding amounts under the Finance Documents. In the event that the Issuer docs not pay the Bond Trustee the accumulated fees and expenses, the Bond Trustee shall be entitled to seek funding for such fees from other sources. In such case, the providers of such funding shall have the right of subrogation into the position of the Bond Trustee but shall be subordinated to any further expenses and fees of the Bond Trustee.
