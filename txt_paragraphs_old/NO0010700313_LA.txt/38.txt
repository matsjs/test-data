11. MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
11.2 Call Option
(a) The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole or in part at any time at 115% of the principal face amount of the Outstanding Bonds (the "Call Option").
(b) Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least 3 Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
(c) Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata between the Bonds (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Register)
(d) On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of all such Bonds (including any premium as stated above).
(e) Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 11.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
11.3 Mandatory prepayment
(a) Upon the occurrence of a Mandatory Prepayment Event, the Issuer shall redeem the Outstanding Bonds (in whole and not in part) at 115% of the principal face amount of the Outstanding Bonds.
