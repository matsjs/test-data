"Business Day" means any day on which Norwegian commercial banks are open for general business, and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions, being any day on which the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open.
"Call Option" shall have the meaning given to that term in Clause 11.2.
"Cash" means, at any time, cash denominated in USD or SEK in hand or held in a bank.
"Convertible Bonds" means the Dannemora Mineral AB Convertible Bonds 2012/2015.
"Convertible Bondholders" means a holder of Convertible Bond(s), as registered with Euroclear Sweden AB, from time to time.
"Costs" means all costs, expenses, disbursements, payments, charges, losses, demands, claims, liabilities, penalties, fines, damages, judgments, orders, sanctions, fees (including travel expenses, VAT, court fees and legal fees) and any other outgoings of whatever nature.
"Default Interest" shall have the meaning given to that term in Clause 12.4.
"Default Interest Payment Date" means in respect of Default Interest the 15th of every month.
"Drawdown Block Event" means any of the events specified in (c) of the definition of Milestone Condition not having occurred by the date specified in respect of such event.
