2.2 Availability The Bond Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds.
2.3 The Bonds (a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the aggregate amount of SEK 21,000,000. The Bonds shall be issued at a price of 95% of par value.
(b) The Face Value is SEK 1.00 each and the Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves and senior in right of payment to all other indebtedness of the Issuer (whether secured or unsecured). The minimum subscription amount and allotment amount is SEK 1,000,000 and integral multiples of SEK 100,000 thereof.
(c) The Bond Issue will be described as "Dannemora Magnetit AB Super Senior Secured Bond Issue due 19 February 2014".
(d) The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bonds will be N00010700313.
(e) The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Settlement Date to the Maturity Date.
(I) The Bonds shall be subscribed for in Cash only.
