Status of the Bonds
8.1 Ranking and priority
The Bonds shall be unsecured and subordinated obligations o f the Issuer. The Bonds shall be subordinated to the Senior Debts, pursuant to the terms of the Inter-creditor Agreement, and rank pari passu with all other subordinated obligations o f the Issuer which are not mandatorily preferred by law. The Bonds shall rank ahead o f the Issuer's equity capital.
