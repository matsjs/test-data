23.
23.1
A Holder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer or a Subsidiary to enforce or
recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate,
support or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation
(Sw.foretagsrekonsti·uktion) or bankruptcy (Sw. konkurs) (or its equivalent in any other
jurisdiction) of the Issuer or a Subsidiary in relation to any of the liabilities of the Issuer
under the Finance Documents.
