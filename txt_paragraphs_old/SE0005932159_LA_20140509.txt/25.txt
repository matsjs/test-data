Provided that the Incurrence Test is met, the Issuer may, at one or more occasions, issue
Subsequent Bonds amounting to in total maximum SEK 500,000,000. Subsequent Bonds
shall benefit from and be subject to the Finance Documents and, for the avoidance of doubt,
the ISIN, the Interest Rate, the Nominal Amount, the Final Redemption Date applicable to
the Initial Bonds and otherwise have the same rights as the Initial Bonds. The price of
Subsequent Bonds may be set at a discount or at a higher price than the Nominal Amount.
