CONDITIONS PRECEDENT FOR DISBURSEMENT
The Agent's approval of disbursement from the Escrow Account of the Net Proceeds is subject to the following documents having been received by the Agent, in form and substance satisfactory to the Agent (acting reasonably):
(a) evidence that the amount set out in Clause 4.1 has been transferred to the Debt Service Account and that the pledge under the Debt Service Account Pledge Agreement has been perfected;
(b) copy of duly executed release notice from the lender under the Senior Debt confirming that all Senior Debt Security (except for security which constitutes Permitted Security) will be released upon repayment of the Senior Debt; and
(c) copy of duly executed Funds Flow Statement evidencing that the amounts to be released from the Escrow Account partly shall be used towards repayment of the Existing Debt in full.
