26.1
Neither the Agent/Security Agent nor the Issuing Agent shall be held responsible for any
damage arising out of any legal enactment, or any measure taken by a public authority, or
war, strike, lockout, boycott, blockade or any other similar circumstance (a "Force Majeure
Event"). The reservation in respect of strikes, lockouts, boycotts and blockades applies even
if the Agent/Security Agent or the Issuing Agent itself takes such measures, or is subject to
such measures.
