The Issuer shall not engage in, or permit any Subsidiary to engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party not being a Group Company (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange o f assets or the rendering o f any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's or such Group Member's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to the Issuer or such Group Company, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(g) Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
(h) Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies shall, carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
