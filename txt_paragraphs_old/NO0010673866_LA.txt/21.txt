The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount o f up to NOK 700,000,000 (Norwegian kroner seven hundred million). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million)
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Island Offshore Shipholding L.P. Senior Unsecured Open Callable Bond Issue 2013/2016".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 067386.6.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.

The net proceeds from the Bonds shall be used for (i) repayment in full of die Existing Shareholder Loans, and (ii) general corporate purposes.
