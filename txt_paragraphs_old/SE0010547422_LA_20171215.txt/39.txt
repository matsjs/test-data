13.7 Negative Pledge
None of the Issuer or any Guarantor shall, and each of them shall procure that none of its respective Subsidiaries will, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer, the Guarantors and the other Group Companies have a right to provide, retain, prolong or renew, any Permitted Security.
