2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner five hundred million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "12.0 per cent Petrolia Drilling ASA Callable Bond Issue 2008/2012".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 044025.8.
