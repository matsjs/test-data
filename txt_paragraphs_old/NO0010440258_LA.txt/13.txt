The Loan will be described as "12.0 per cent Petrolia Drilling ASA Callable Bond Issue 2008/2012".
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds of the Loan shall be used for (i) refinancing of the existing outstanding bond of NOK 250 million (ISIN NO 001 030199.7) and (ii) for general corporate purposes which may include investments in other companies/assets within the oil/offshore sector.
