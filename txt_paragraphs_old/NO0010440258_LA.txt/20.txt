8 Status of the Loan and security
8.1 The Loan shall rank pari passu with all other senior obligations of the Borrower and shall rank ahead of subordinated capital.
8.2 The Loan including interest and expenses shall be secured by the DSR Account Pledge. The DSR Accounts shall at all times be in an amount sufficient to employ interest payment for the next 9 months period of the Loan.
9 Interest
9.1 The Borrower shall pay interest on the Loan from and including the Disbursement Date at a fixed rate of 12.0 per cent per annum. Interest payments shall be made semi annually in arrears on 20 June and 20 December each year, the first interest Payment Date being 20 December 2008.
9.2 Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 360 days with twelve 30-day months, unless a) the last day of the period is the 31st day of a month but the first day of the period is a day other than the 30thor 31st day of a month, in which case the month that includes that last day shall not be considered to be shortened to a 30-day month, or b) the last day of the period is the last day of the month of February, in which case the month of February shall not be considered to be lengthened to a 30-day month.
