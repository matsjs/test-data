This agreement (the "Loan Agreement") has been entered into on 13 June 2008 between Petrolia Drilling ASA (Norwegian Company No 977 321 484) as borrower (the "Borrower") and Norsk Tillitsmann ASA (Norwegian Company No 963 342 624) as loan trustee (the "Loan Trustee").
The Bondholders are bound by the terms of the Loan Agreement as the Loan Trustee through the Bondholders' subscription was granted authority to finalise and execute the Loan Agreement on behalf of the Bondholders.
All Bond transfers shall be subject to the terms of this Loan Agreement and the Trustee and all Bond transferees shall, in taking transfer of Bonds, be deemed to have accepted the terms of the Loan Agreement where after any and all such transferees shall automatically become parties to the Loan Agreement upon completed transfer having been registered in the VPS, without any further action required or formalities to be complied with.
The Loan Agreement is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Loan Trustee or the Borrower. The Borrower shall ensure that the Loan Agreement is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Loan.
