13.3 During the term of the Loan, the Borrower shall (unless the Loan Trustee or the Bondholders' meeting (as the case may be) in writing has agreed to otherwise) on each Balance Sheet Reporting Date ensure that the Borrower maintenance a Coverage Ratio of 2.Ox or higher.
Notwithstanding the aforesaid, if the Borrower raises further indebtedness of more than NOK 100 million, sell assets for net proceeds of more than NOK 100 million or acquires new assets for more than NOK 100 million (all amount per transaction), the Borrower shall within 30 Banking Days after such transaction report that it is in compliance with the Coverage Ratio.
The Coverage Ratio shall be reported to the Loan Trustee and to the Oslo Stock Exchange within 30 Banking Days after the relevant Balance Sheet Reporting Date.
