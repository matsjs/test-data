"Obligors" means the Issuer, the Oselvar Subsidiary and the Guarantors (to the extent the Guarantees have become valid and enforceable with respect to the Bond Issue).
"Oselvar Disposal" means a transaction following which all or any part of the Oselvar Licence (directly or indirectly) is being sold or disposed of.
"Oselvar Earnings Account" means the account in the name of the Oselvar Subsidiary charged in favour of the Bond Trustee pursuant to the Oselvar Earnings Account Pledge and into which all Oselvar Project Proceeds shall be paid.
"Oselvar Earnings Account Pledge" means the first priority pledge over the Oselvar Subsidiary's claims against the relevant Account Bank(s) for the amounts from time to time standing to the credit of the Oselvar Subsidiary in the Oselvar Earnings Account, in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the Account Bank operating the Oselvar Earnings Account has waived any set-off rights.
"Oselvar Guarantee" means an unconditional and irrevocable Norwegian law on demand guarantees from the Oselvar Subsidiary securing the Oselvar Obligors' obligations when they have become due under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, including interest, costs and expenses, with payment by the Oselvar Subsidiary to be made within 10 Business Days of any demand,
"Oselvar Intragroup Loan" means any loan or credit that (i) the Oselvar Subsidiary may provide to the Issuer from time to time, or (ii) the Issuer or another Group Company may provide to the Oselvar Subsidiary from time to time.
"Oselvar Mandatory Prepayment Event" means the occurrence of an Oselvar Disposal.
"Oselvar Mandatory Prepayment" means any payment to be made by the Issuer in accordance with Clause 10.4.
"Oselvar Licence" means the Oselvar Subsidiary's 15% working interest in the Norwegian production licences 274 and 274 CS.
