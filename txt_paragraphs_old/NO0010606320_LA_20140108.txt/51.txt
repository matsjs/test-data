Status of the Bonds and Security
8.1 Status o f the Bonds
The Bonds (including any interest accrued thereon) shall constitute senior debt obligations of the Issuer. The Bonds shall rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Issuer, save for such claims which are mandatorily preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application. The Bonds shall rank ahead of any subordinated capital (equity or debt).
