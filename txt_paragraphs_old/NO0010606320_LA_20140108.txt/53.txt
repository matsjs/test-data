Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of six and a half per cent (6.50%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made semi-annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date being 9 June 2014.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
