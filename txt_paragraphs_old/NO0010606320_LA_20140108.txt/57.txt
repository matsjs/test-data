10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 The Issuer may redeem the Bonds in whole or in parts as follows (Call Option):
(a) any time from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2014, at a price equal to 105.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds);
(b) any time from, and including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2014 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2015, at a price equal to 103.50% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds);
(c) any time from, and including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2015 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2016, at a price equal to 102.50% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds);
(d) any time from, and including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2016, to, but not included, Maturity Date at 101.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed Bonds).
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.2.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
