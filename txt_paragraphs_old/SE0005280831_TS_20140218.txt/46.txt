GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS
Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall comply, and shall procure that DDM Invest XX complies, in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject and its articles of association and other constitutional documents.
Mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that DDM Invest XX does not, enter into any merger unless where it is the surviving entity.
