Security restriction
The Issuer shall not make any other financial or other arrangements concerning any of the Security Interests other than provided for in the Finance Documents.
(g) Financial Assistance restrictions
The Issuer shall not grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance to any Group Company and/or any third party, except (i) guarantees issued for the benefit of third parties in the ordinary course of business, Group Loans from the Parent to the Issuer and (ii) guarantees required under the Project Documents.
(h) Related party transactions
The Issuer shall not engage directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
