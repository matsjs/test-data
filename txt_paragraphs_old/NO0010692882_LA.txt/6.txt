"Calculation Date" means 30 June and 31 December each year.
"Change of Control Event" means:
(i)
If persons or entities related to the Lundin family cease to own directly or
indirectly 19.50% or more of the outstanding shares and/or voting capital of
the Ultimate Parent, save for dilutions resulting directly from mergers or
acquisition activities and exercise o f incentive options; or
(ii) a group or entity acquiring control over more than 50.00% of the voting share capital in the Ultimate Parent; or
(iii) a de-listing of the Ultimate Parent's shares from a stock exchange.
