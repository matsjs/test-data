(c) De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
(d) Continuation o fbusiness
The Issuer shall remain a single-purpose company owning a working interest in the Atrush Block under the Atrush PSC, and not cease to carry on its business or change the general nature of its business.
(e) Disposal o f business
The Issuer shall not sell or otherwise dispose o f all or a substantial part of its assets or operations, unless the Bonds are redeemed in accordance with the Mandatory Prepayment provisions.
