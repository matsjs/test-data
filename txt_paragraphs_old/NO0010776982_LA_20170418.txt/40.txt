14.2 Other Obligations
The Issuer does not comply with its obligations under the Finance Documents, in any other way than as set out in Clause 14.1 (Non-Payment), provided that the Agent has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within 15 Business Days from such request (if the failure or violation is not capable of being remedied, the Agent may declare the Bonds due and payable without such prior written request).
14.3 Cross-Acceleration
