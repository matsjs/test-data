Outstanding Loan:
Remaining Loan less Borrower's Bonds.
Payment Date:
in relation to the Loan the dates specified for payment of interest or principal. If Payment Date is not a Banking Day payments should be made the following Banking Day.
Period:
means interest payment period as defined in the loan agreement for the FRN Loan (the interest period from one Interest Payment Date to the next).
Project Company:
a company (whether or not a member of the Group) whose principal assets and business are constituted by a project or series of projects and whose liabilities in respect of the financing of such projects are not directly or indirectly the subject of a guarantee, indemnity or any other form of assurance, undertaking or support from any member of the Group except as expressly referred to in paragraph (b)(iii) in the definition of Project Finance Borrowing below.
