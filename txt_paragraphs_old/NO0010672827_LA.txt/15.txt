2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the total aggregate amount of NOK 1,500,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one thousand five hundred million). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 1,250,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one thousand two hundred and fifty million).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Marine Harvest ASA Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2013/2018".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0672827.
