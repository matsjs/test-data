The notice pursuant to Clause 16.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for the
Bondholders) and (iv) a form of power of attorney. Only matters that have been included
in the notice may be resolved upon at the Bondholders' Meeting. Should prior notification
by the Bondholders be required in order to attend the Bondholders' Meeting, such
requirement shall be included in the notice.
16.4
The Bondholders' Meeting shall be held no earlier than ten (10) Business Days and no
later than twenty (20) Business Days from the notice.
