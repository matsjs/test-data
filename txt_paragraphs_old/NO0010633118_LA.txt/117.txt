16.14 Acquisitions and investments No Guarantor shall make any acquisition of other companies or assets (either in a single transaction or in as series of transactions), nor make any investments (which shall include any expenditure which is treated as capital expenditure in accordance with GAAP), other than solely related to the ownership in and operation of the Vessels.
16.15 Insurances The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each Group Company will, maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters adequate insurance or captive arrangements with respect to its vessels, equipment and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as are consistent with prudent business practice.
