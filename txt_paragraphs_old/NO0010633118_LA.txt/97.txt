in connection with the issue of its Financial Statements and Quarterly Financial
Report pursuant to Clause 14.3, confirm compliance with the covenants in
Clauses 14, 15, 16 and 17; and

within 45 days after the end of each month, confirm compliance with the
covenants in paragraphs (a) and (c) of Clause 15.3,
in each case in writing undertaken in a compliance certificate from the Issuer to the Bond Trustee, substantially in the format set out in Appendix 1 and Appendix 2 hereto, respectively, signed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chief Financial Officer of the Issuer.
