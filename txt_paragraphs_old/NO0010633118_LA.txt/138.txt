17.12 Total Loss In the event that a Vessel shall suffer a Total Loss, the Issuer shall, within a period of 90 days after the Total Loss Date, obtain and present to the Bond Trustee, a written confirmation from the relevant insurers that the claim relating to the Total Loss has been accepted in full, and the insurance proceeds shall be applied in prepayment of the Bonds in accordance with Clause 10.5.
17.13 Environmental compliance The Issuer and each Guarantor being owner of a Vessel shall and shall procure that the Charterer shall, comply in all respects with all applicable law, regulation, convention or treaty in any jurisdiction in which the Issuer and/or any relevant Guarantor and/or any Vessel Manager and/or any Charterer conduct business which relates to the pollution or protection of the environment or to the carriage of material which is capable of polluting the environment relevant to any of them or the Vessels, including without limitation, requirements relating to manning and establishment of financial responsibility and to obtain and comply with all approvals applicable to any of them and/or the Vessels.
18. FEES AND EXPENSES

Fees and expenses payable to the Bond Trustee which, due to the Issuer's insolvency or similar, are not reimbursed in any other way may be covered by making an equivalent reduction in the payments to the Bondholders.
