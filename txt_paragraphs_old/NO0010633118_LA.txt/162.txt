20.4 Repeated Bondholders' meeting
(a)
If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause 20.3(c),
a repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the same
matters. The attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders' Meeting
shall be specified in the summons for the repeated Bondholders' Meeting.
