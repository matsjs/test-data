At the Bondholders' Meeting each Bondholder may cast one vote for each
Bondholders' Meeting in accordance with the records registered in the Securities
Register. Whoever opens the Bondholders' Meeting shall adjudicate any
question concerning which Bonds shall count as the Issuer's Bonds. The Issuer's
Bonds shall not have any voting rights.
(b)
In all matters, the Issuer, the Bond Trustee and any Bondholder have the right to
demand vote by ballot. In case of parity of votes, the chairman shall have the
deciding vote, regardless of the chairman being a Bondholder or not.
