16.8 Negative pledge
(a)
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, create
or permit to subsist any Encumbrance over any of its assets or revenues, present
or in the future.

Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Encumbrance which is:
