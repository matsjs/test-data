16.12 Listing The Issuer shall ensure that its shares remain listed on Oslo Børs or another recognized stock exchange.
(a)
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company, make or
allow to subsist any Financial Support to any third party not being a member of
the Group.
(b)
Paragraph (a) above does not apply to Financial Support:
(i) made or allowed to subsist in its ordinary course of trading; or
(ii) made or allowed to subsist in relation to Financial Indebtedness permitted pursuant to paragraph (b) of Clause 16.9.
