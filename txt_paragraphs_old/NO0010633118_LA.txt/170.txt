21.2 Liability and indemnity
(a)
The Bond Trustee is liable only for direct losses incurred by Bondholders or the
Issuer as a result of negligence or wilful misconduct by the Bond Trustee in
performing its functions and duties as set forth in this Bond Agreement. The
Bond Trustee is not liable for the content of information provided to the
Bondholders on behalf of the Issuer.
