8.1 The Issuer's payment obligations under this Bond Agreement shall rank ahead of all subordinated payment obligations o f the Issuer and shall rank at least pari passu with all the Issuer's other obligations, save for (i) secured obligations to the extent they are secured and (ii) obligations which are mandatorily preferred by law.
8.2 The Bonds are unsecured.
9
Interest
9.1 The Issuer shall pay interest on the face value o f the Bonds from, and including, the Issue Date at the Bond Reference Rate plus the Margin (together the "Floating Rate").
9.2 Interest payments shall be made in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates each year, the first Interest Payment Date falls in July 2012.
9.3 The relevant interest payable amount shall be calculated based on a period from, and including, one Interest Payment Date to, but excluding, the next following applicable Interest Payment Date.
9.4 The day count fraction in respect of the calculation o f the payable interest amount shall be "Actual/360", which means that the number of days in the calculation period in which payment being made divided by 360.
