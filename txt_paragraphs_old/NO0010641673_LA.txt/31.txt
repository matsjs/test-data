Maturity of the Bonds and Redemption
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 10.2.1
Call Option
The Issuer may redeem parts o f the Bond Issue or the entire Bond Issue as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including the date falling 3 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the date falling 4 years after the Issue Date at a price equal to 105 % o f par value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
i
(ii) at any time from and including the date falling 4 years after the Issue Date to, but not including, the Maturity Date at a price equal to 102,5 % o f par value plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.2.2 Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders and at least thirty Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
