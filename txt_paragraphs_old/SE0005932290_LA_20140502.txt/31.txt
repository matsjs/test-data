3. Use of Proceeds
The Net Proceeds from the issuance of the Initial Bonds shall be applied against onlending to the Guarantor by way of the Initial Bond Proceeds Loan and further to the Subsidiaries of the Guarantor for the purposes of financing Projects. Any proceeds from any issuance of Subsequent Bonds shall be used for (i) refinancing of the Existing Indebtedness of the Group, and (ii) on-lending to the Guarantor by way of the Future Bond Proceeds Loans to be applied by the Guarantor Group to finance Projects.
4. Conditions Precedent
(a) The payment of the Net Proceeds to the Escrow Account is subject to the Agent having received documents and evidence of the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement being duly executed and perfected.

(i) copies of constitutional documents of the Issuer and the Guarantor;
