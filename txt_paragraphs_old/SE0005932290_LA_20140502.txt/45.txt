13. Maintenance Covenants
13.2 Leverage (a) The Issuer shall ensure that Leverage of the Guarantor Group shall not at any time: (i) during the period from the First Issue Date until and including 30 June 2014 exceed 80 per cent; and (ii) at any time after the period set out in paragraph (a) above, exceed 75 per cent. (b) For the avoidance of doubt, for the purpose of the Maintenance Test referred to in paragraph (m) of the definition of "Permitted Debt" in Clause 1.1 (Definitions), the calculation of Leverage under this Clause 13.2 shall be tested as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than one month prior to the incurrence of the new Financial Indebtedness.
14. Incurrence Covenants
14.1 Issuer Incurrence Covenant The Issuer Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time, the Equity Ratio is at least 25 per cent.
14.2 Guarantor Incurrence Covenant The Guarantor Incurrence Test is met if, at the relevant time, Leverage of the Guarantor Group does not exceed 70 per cent.
14.3 Calculation Adjustments (a) The calculation of the Equity Ratio under Clause 14.1 above shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than one month prior to the incurrence of the new Financial Indebtedness or a distribution in accordance with Clause 15.2(b). (b) The calculation of Leverage under Clause 14.2 (Guarantor Incurrence Covenant) above shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than one month prior to the incurrence of a distribution in accordance with Clause 15.2(d) below.
