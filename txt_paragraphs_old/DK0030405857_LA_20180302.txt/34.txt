6.2 Conditions subsequent
(a) As soon as reasonably possible after Completion, the Issuer shall provide the Bond Trustee with each of the following Transaction Security Documents duly signed by all parties thereto, in form and substance satisfactory to the Bond Trustee a second priority pledge over the shares in the Property Company.
(b) The proceeds from the issue of the Bonds shall among others be applied by the Issuer to acquire the Property Company. Accordingly, the Issuer will not get title to the shares in the Property Company and thereby be able to provide the Bondholders, represented by the Security Agent, with the security interests listed in this Clause 6.2 (Conditions Subsequent) until after Completion i.e. after the proceeds from the Bonds have been made available to the Issuer.
