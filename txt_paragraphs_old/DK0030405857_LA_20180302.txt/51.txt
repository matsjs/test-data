Any expropriation, attachment, sequestration, distress or execution affects any asset or assets of the Issuer or the Property Company having an aggregate value exceeding the threshold amount set out in paragraph 14.1 (d) (Cross acceleration) above and is not discharged within twenty (20) Business Days.
(g) Unlawfulness
It is or becomes unlawful for the Issuer or the Property Company to perform or comply with any of its obligations under the Finance Documents to the extent this may materially impair:
(i) the ability of any of the Issuer or the Property Company to perform its obligations under these Bond Terms; or
(ii) the ability of any of the Bond Trustee or the Security Agent to exercise any material right or power vested to it under the Finance Documents.
