13.2 During the term of the Loan, the Borrower shall (unless the Loan Trustee or the Bondholders' meeting (as the case may be) in writing has agreed otherwise) comply with the following general covenants:
(a) not cease to carry on its business,
(b) not demerge or merge,
(c) not in any way restructure its business, in a manner which is likely to materially adversely affect the Borrower's fulfilment of its obligations under the Loan Agreement,
(d) not sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its assets or operations or change the nature of its business in a manner which is likely to materially adversely affect the Borrower's fulfilment of its obligations under the Loan Agreement,
