3. WAIVER
The Loan Trustee hereby waives any right it may have under Clause 15.1(a) of the Loan Agreement to declare an Event of Default on the basis of the Borrower's failure to repay the Bonds on the Original Maturity Date, subject to the continuing satisfaction by the Borrower of the following conditions:
(a) provision by the Borrower of cost cover to the Loan Trustee for its and Counsel's reasonable fees, costs and disbursements; and
(b) co-operation by the Borrower with the Loan Trustee and Counsel through ongoing consultation and provision of access to such information and documentation as the Loan Trustee may reasonably require.
