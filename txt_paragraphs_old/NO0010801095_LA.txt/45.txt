9.
INTEREST
9.1 Calculation of interest
(a) Each Outstanding Bond (including the Temporary Bonds) will accrue interest at the Interest Rate on the Nominal Amount for each Interest Period, commencing on and including the first date of the Interest Period, and ending on but excluding the last date of the Interest Period.
(b) Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a 360-day year comprised of twelve, months of 30 days each and, in case of an incomplete month, the actual number of days elapsed (30/360-days basis).
