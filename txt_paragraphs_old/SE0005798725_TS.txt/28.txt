10.5 Minimum outstanding Bond amount
(a) If the aggregate Nominal Amount under all outstanding Bonds (less any Bonds held or retained by the Issuer or an Affiliate to the Issuer) would fall below 30 % of the initial aggregate Nominal Amount on the Issue Date, the Issuer must redeem all remaining outstanding Bonds at 101.00 % of the Nominal Amount.
(b) Prepayment in accordance with Clause 10.5(a) shall be made by the Issuer without undue delay (but in any case within 60 Business Days) following the event set out in Clause 10.5(a) and giving not less than 20 Business Days' notice prior to the relevant Redemption Date to the Holders and the Agent and in accordance with the instructions of the Issuer or the Issuing Agent, as applicable.
