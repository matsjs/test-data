2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series o f Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 2,500,000,000 (Norwegian krone twobillionfive hundred million), herein referred to as "the Loan", however such that the total borrowing for the Loan together with FIX Loan shall be NOK 2,500,000,000 (Norwegian krone twobillionfive hundred million). The Loan may comprise of one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche in the Loan will be in the amount of maximum NOK 187,000,000 (Norwegian krone one hundred and eighty seven million).
The Loan will be described as "FRN Aker Solutions ASA Bond Issue 2009/2014".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 050460.8.
The term o f the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds o f the Loan will be employed for general corporate purposes..
2.2 All tranches under the Loan will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Loan will also apply for later tranches issued within the maximum amount of the Loan.
The Loan Trustee will when issuing additional tranches keep an addendum to the Loan Agreement regulating the conditions precedent to disbursement of the relevant tranche.
3 Listing
3.1 The Loan will initially not be listed on an Exchange. An application may be made for listing on Oslo Børs or Oslo Børs ASA's Alternative Bond Market (ABM) at the discretion of the Borrower.
