2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series o f Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 2,500,000,000 (Norwegian krone twobillionfive hundred million), herein referred to as "the Loan", however such that the total borrowing for the Loan together with FIX Loan shall be NOK 2,500,000,000 (Norwegian krone twobillionfive hundred million). The first tranche in the Loan will be in the amount of maximum NOK 187,000,000 (Norwegian krone one hundred and eighty seven million).
The Bonds will be in denominations ofNOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
