Equivalent Licence
means a UKCS licence(s) in which Sussex Energy Ltd or any affiliate acquires a net interest(s) equal to, or greater in aggregate value than, the value of a 100% interest in the Licence.
Escrow Account:
a USD account established in a Bermuda bank reasonably satisfactory to the Loan Trustee in the name of the Borrower, pledged on first priority and blocked in favour of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
Escrow Account Pledge:
Bermuda law first priority pledge over the Borrower's claim against the bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Borrower in the Escrow Account in favor of the Loan Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Borrower's fulfilment of its payment obligations (included payment of principal, interest and expenses) under this Loan Agreement.
