10.2 Any Excess Cash Flow shall be transferred monthly in arrears to the Cash Sweep Account and shall (together with any interest accrued on the Cash Sweep Account) on each Interest Payment Date be used to redeem a number of outstanding Bonds, such number to be calculated (by reference to par value) by dividing the balance at the Cash Sweep Account at the Interest Payment Date by an amount equal to the sum of the par value of one Bond and any accrued unpaid interest on such Bond as of the Interest Payment Date.
The actual amount of the Loan to be repaid pursuant to this Clause 10.2 shall be determined by the Loan Trustee 10 Banking Days before the relevant Interest Payment Date. The Borrower shall submit a draft calculation of such amount to the Loan Trustee no later than 20 Banking Days before the relevant Interest Payment Date. Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
In the event of any disagreement as the amount to be paid into the Cash Sweep Account, the Borrower may request its independent auditors to express its opinion as to the correct amount. If the Borrower makes no such request or if the Loan Trustee does not accept the opinion of the Borrower's auditor, a reputable accounting firm independent of the Borrower shall be retained by the Loan Trustee to determine the correct amount, acting as expert and not as arbitrator, and whose decision shall be final and binding on the parties to this Loan Agreement. Each of the Loan Trustee, the Borrower and each Bondholder shall be given the opportunity to present its views in writing to the auditors.
