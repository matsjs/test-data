16.1 During the term of the Loan the Borrower shall comply with the following information covenants:
(a) immediately inform the Loan Trustee of any default of the Loan pursuant to Clause 18 as well as of any circumstances which the Borrower knows or would be reasonably expected to know will lead to default,
(b) of its own accord, make audited annual and unaudited interim reports available on the Borrower's website (alternatively, the Borrower may comply by sending them to the Loan Trustee) as soon they are available, and not later than 180 days after the end o f the financial year and not later than 80 days after the end of the relevant interim report period,
(c) at the request of the Loan Trustee send a report outlining the balance of the Borrower's Bonds,
(d) forward to the Loan Trustee copies of any creditors' notifications regarding the Borrower, including but not limited to; mergers, demergers and reduction of shareholders capital,
(e) at the request of the Loan Trustee provide the documents and information necessary to maintain the listing and quotation of the Loan on Exchange (if applicable) and to otherwise enable the Loan Trustee to carry out its rights and duties pursuant to the Loan Agreement, laws and regulations,
(f) within a reasonable time limit provide information about the Borrower's financial condition as the Loan Trustee may reasonably request,
(g) in connection with reporting under 16.1(b) confirm to the Loan Trustee the Borrower's compliance with Clause 16,
