18 Events of Default
18.1 The Loan may be declared to be in default by the Loan Trustee upon the occurrence of any of the following events (Events of Default), provided always that reasonably prompt written notice of the same has been delivered to the Borrower,:
(a) the Borrower shall on any Payment Date fail to pay any interest or principal due or any other amount payable under the Finance Documents; provided, however, that such failure shall not include failures which are remedied within 5 (five) Banking Days after the Payment Date, and it is obvious the Borrower will remedy the failure within this time.
