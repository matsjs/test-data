Should the Borrower exercise its Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed (the Bondholders to be notified in writing via VPS) no later than 30 (thirty) Banking Days before the date of redemption.
Partial redemption of the Loan must be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
Bonds redeemed by the Borrower in accordance with this clause shall be discharged against the Remaining Loan.
If the Borrower redeems the entire Loan pursuant to this Clause 10.3, the remaining funds on the Escrow Account may be used as partial payment for such redemption.
In the event of an early redemption of the Loan pursuant to this Clause 10.3, settlement shall be made five (5) Banking Days after the Paying Agent has received such request.
10.4 If a Change of Control Event or an event as described in Clause 16.1 (i) has occurred, each Bondholder shall at any time in the Change of Control Put Period have the option to (at each Bondholder's discretion) require early redemption of its Bonds (put option) at the price of 101% per cent of par value.
The respective Bondholder must notify the Paying Agent within the Change of Control Put Period of its election to require early redemption.
