19.1 To the extent creditor's approval is required according to law, regulation or this Loan Agreement, the Bondholders' meeting may make decisions in all questions concerning the Loan, such as:
(a) change of Loan Trustee,
(b) change of Borrower,
(c) changes to the Loan Agreement regarding interest, payment, maturity or other conditions,
(d) changes in the Borrower's corporate structure, such as mergers, demergers, capital reduction or conversion,
(e) approving the sale o f or other transactions concerning the Borrower's assets or security for the Loan, and
(f) declaring the Loan to be in default.
