2 The Loan and the Warrants
The Bonds will be in denominations of USD 100,000 each and will rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "11.00 per cent Enovation Resources Limited Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012 with Warrants".
The international security identification number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 0401680.
The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date.
The net proceeds of the Loan shall be used solely for capital expenditures in connection with the Borrower's present and future exploration and development activities in the Gulf of Mexico (GoM), North Western Europe, Africa and Asia Pacific. For the avoidance of doubt this also includes general corporate purposes such as administration costs, payments to and remuneration of employees, interest payments, instalments and principal on Loan and other expenditures as required in the normal course of business
