PAYMENTS IN RESPECT OF THE BONDS
8.1
Any payment or repayment under these Terms and Conditions, or any amount due in respect
of a repurchase of any Bonds, shall be made to such Person who is registered as a Holder on
the Record Date prior to the relevant payment date, or to such other Person who is registered
with the CSD on such date as being entitled to receive the relevant payment, repayment or
repurchase amount.
