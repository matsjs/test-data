NO DIRECT ACTIONS BY NOTEHOLDERS
A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer or with respect to the
Transaction Security to enforce or recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the
Finance Documents, or to initiate, support or procure the winding-up, dissolution,
liquidation, company reorganisation (företagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy (konkurs) (or
its equivalent in any other jurisdiction) of the Issuer in relation to any of the liabilities of
the Issuer under the Finance Documents.
