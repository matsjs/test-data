"Calculation Principles" the calculation of the Incurrence Test shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than two (2) months prior to the payment of the Restricted Payment that requires that the Incurrence Test is met. When calculating the Incurrence Test, the Restricted Payment shall for the calculation of Loan to Value be subtracted from the Cash and Cash Equivalents employed in the calculation of Net Interest Bearing Debt.
