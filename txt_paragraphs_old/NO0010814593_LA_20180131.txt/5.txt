"Adjusted Net Profit" means for any accounting period, the consolidated net income of the Issuer on a consolidated basis for that accounting period, after adding back or deducting, as the case may be, the amount of:
(a) any loss or gain against book value arising from any Permitted Disposal or Permitted Divestment;
(b) any non-recurring exceptional items included in the Financial Reports;
(c) any expenses, fees, charges or losses that are directly incurred or required to be reimbursed in connection with the issuance of the Bonds (including any amendments thereto);
(d) any loss or gain against book value arising from any Total Loss Event including insurance proceeds resulting from such Total Loss Event; and
(e) any appreciation made on the Vessels.
