"Earnings Account" means an account opened by a Charter Company with Bank of America or another Acceptable Bank where Earnings related to the Vessels and payable to a member of the Group shall be paid directly to the Earnings Account(s) of the Charter Company (where the Earnings Account(s) may be a sub-account in the Cash Pooling Top Account). The Earnings Account(s) shall, unless consisting of a sub-account of the Cash Pooling Top Account, be pledged on a first priority basis in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders), but not blocked (unless there is an outstanding Event of Default).
"Equity Clawback" shall have the meaning ascribed to such term in Clause 10.3.
"Escrow Account" means an account with the Account Bank in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
