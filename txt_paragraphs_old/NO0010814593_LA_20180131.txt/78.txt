13. PARENT UNDERTAKINGS The Parent undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that the other Group Companies will) comply with the undertakings set forth in this Clause 13 (Parent Undertakings).
13.2 Corporate status The Parent shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of formation.
13.3 Continuation of business The Parent shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on by the Group at the Issue Date.
13.4 Arm's length transactions The Parent shall not enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
13.5 Issuer ownership The Parent shall maintain direct or indirect ownership of 100% of the equity interests and voting rights of the Issuer.
14. ISSUER UNDERTAKINGS The Issuer undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that the other Group Companies will) comply with the undertakings set forth in this Clause 14 (Issuer Undertakings).
14.1 Distributions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, make any Distribution other than a Distribution in favour of another Group Company or a Permitted Distribution.
14.2 Investments The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will make any investments or capital expenditures, other than Additional Vessel Acquisitions or otherwise solely related to the direct or indirect ownership in and operation, maintenance and improvement of the Vessels.
