Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, engage, directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Vessel Owning Companies' business and

14.17
Jurisdiction of incorporation
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
