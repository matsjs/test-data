14.5
If the Issuer or the Agent shall make any payment under this Clause 14, the Issuer or the
Agent, as applicable, shall notify the Noteholders of any such payment at least fifteen (15)
Business Days before the payment is made. Such notice shall specify the Record Time, the
payment date and the amount to be paid. Notwithstanding the foregoing, for any Interest
due but unpaid the Record Time specified in Clause 6.1 shall apply and for any partial
redemption in accordance with Clause 8.4 (Voluntary/Mandatory partial redemption (call
option)) due but not made, the Record Time specified in Clause 8.4.2 shall apply.
