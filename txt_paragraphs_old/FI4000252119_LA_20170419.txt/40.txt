2.7
Each Note is freely transferable after it has been registered into the respective book-entry
account of a Noteholder but the Noteholders may be subject to purchase or transfer
restrictions with regard to the Notes, as applicable, under local laws to which a Noteholder
may be subject. Each Noteholder must ensure compliance with such restrictions at its own
cost and expense.
