INTEREST
7.1
Each Initial Note carries Interest at the Interest Rate from (and including) the First Issue
Date up to (but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date. Any Subsequent Note will carry
Interest at the Interest Rate from (and including) the Interest Payment Date falling
immediately prior to its issuance up to (but excluding) the relevant Redemption Date.
