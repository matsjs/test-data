11.4
The figures for EBITDA set out in the financial statements as of the most recent Quarter Date (including when necessary, financial statements published before the First Issue Date), shall be used, but adjusted so that:
(a) entities acquired or disposed (i) during a Reference Period or (ii) after the end of the Reference Period but before the relevant testing date, will be included or excluded (as applicable) pro forma for the entire Reference Period; and
(b) the pro forma calculation of EBITDA shall be adjusted to take into account the net cost savings realisable for the Group during the next twelve (12) months as a result of acquisitions and/or disposals of entities referred to in (a) above, provided that (i) such net cost savings do not exceed an aggregate maximum amount of ten (10) per cent. of EBITDA for the Reference Period, and (ii) such savings are confirmed in writing by a reputable accounting firm.
