21.4.10
As long as the Super Senior Lenders has not ceased to be a party under the Security Documents, the Security Agent may not assign or transfer any part of its rights, benefits or obligations under the Security Documents to any assignee or successor unless the Super Senior Lenders (or their successors) has given its prior written consent. If the Security Agent is insolvent, the Security Agent shall be deemed to resign as Security Agent and the Super Senior Lenders shall, after have taken due consideration to the interests of the Holders, within ten (10) Banking Days appoint a successor Security Agent which shall be an independent financial institution or other reputable company which regularly acts as security agent under debt issuances and credit facilities, as further set out in the Security Documents.
