"Incurrence Test" the Incurrence Test is met if:
(a) the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to Group EBITDA calculated in accordance with the Incurrence Test Accounting Principles is not greater than 3.00; and
(b) the Interest Coverage Ratio calculated in accordance with the Incurrence Test Accounting Principles exceeds 3.00.
"Incurrence Test Accounting Principles" means:
(a) that (i) the calculation of the ratio of Net Interest Bearing Debt to Group EBITDA shall be made as per a testing date determined by the Issuer, falling no more than one month prior to the event relevant for the application of the Incurrence Test, (ii) the Net Interest Bearing Debt shall be measured on the relevant testing date so determined, but include the new Financial Indebtedness provided it is an interest bearing obligation (however, any cash balance resulting from the incurrence of the new Financial Indebtedness shall not reduce the Net Interest Bearing Debt), (iii) and the Group EBITDA shall be calculated as set out in Clauses (b)-(c) below;
