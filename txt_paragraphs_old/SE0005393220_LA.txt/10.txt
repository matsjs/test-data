"Existing Junior Debt" means the loans outstanding under the Junior Loan Facility Agreement dated 26 July 2010 entered into between, inter alios, ETIGH as borrower, and a certain mezzanine lender as lender, as of 30 June 2013 in an aggregate outstanding principal amount of approximately SEK 152,200,000, which shall be refinanced through the Bond Issue and constitutes all junior financing of the Group at the Issue Date.
"Existing Senior Debt" means the loans outstanding under the Senior Multicurrency and Revolving Facilities Agreement dated 26 July 2010 entered into between, inter alios, ETIGH as borrower, and Nordea Bank AB (publ) as lender, as of 30 June 2013 in an aggregate outstanding principal amount of approximately SEK 169,300,000, which shall be refinanced through the Bond Issue and constitutes all senior bank financing of the Group at the Issue Date.
"Existing Shareholder Loans" means the shareholder loans existing at the Issue Date amounting to approximately SEK 408,600,000.
"Final Redemption Date" means 27 September 2017.

"Finance Documents" means the Security Documents, the Intercreditor Agreement and the Terms and Conditions.
