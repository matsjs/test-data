21.2.7
Duties of the Bond Trustee/Security Agent
The Bond Trustee/Security Agent shall represent the Holders in accordance with the Finance Documents. However, the Bond Trustee/Security Agent is not responsible for the execution or enforceability of the Finance Documents. The Bond Trustee/Security Agent shall keep the latest version of these Terms and Conditions (including any document amending these Terms and Conditions) available on the website of the Bond Trustee/Security Agent.
When acting in accordance with the Finance Documents, the Bond Trustee/Security Agent is always acting with binding effect on behalf of the Holders. The Bond Trustee/Security Agent shall carry out its duties under the Finance Documents in a reasonable, proficient and professional manner, with reasonable care and skill.
The Bond Trustee/Security Agent is entitled to delegate its duties to other professional parties, but the Bond Trustee/Security Agent shall remain liable for the actions of such parties under the Finance Documents.
The Bond Trustee/Security Agent shall treat all Holders equally and, when acting pursuant to the Finance Documents, act with regard only to the interests of the Holders (except in relation to the Security Documents where the Security Agent shall also act in the interest of the Super Senior Lenders) and shall not be required to have regard to the interests or to act upon or comply with any direction or request of any other person, other than as explicitly stated in the Finance Documents and the Security Agent Agreement.
