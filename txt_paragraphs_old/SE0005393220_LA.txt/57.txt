The Bond Trustee's approval of the disbursement from the Escrow Account of the Net Proceeds is subject to the following documents being received by the Bond Trustee, in form and substance satisfactory to it (acting reasonably), and that the following actions have been taken or that the following events have occurred:
(a) duly executed release notice(s) from the lender(s) under the Existing Senior Debt and Existing Junior Debt confirming that all Existing Security will be released upon repayment;
(b) evidence that the amounts to be released from the Escrow Account shall be applied towards repayment in full of the Existing Senior Debt and Existing Junior Debt in accordance with the Purpose of the Bond Issue;
(c) duly executed copies of documents evidencing the Intercompany Loans;
(d) duly executed copies of the Security Documents;
(e) duly executed copy of the Intercreditor Agreement;
(f) duly executed copies of documents evidencing that the Resolutions have been duly carried out; and
(g) that all shares in ETIGH have been transferred to the Issuer and that the Issuer is recorded in the share register of ETIGH as the owner of all shares in ETIGH.
