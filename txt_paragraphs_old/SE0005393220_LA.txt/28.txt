2.6
Provided that the Incurrence Test is met, the Issuer may, at one or several occasions, issue
Subsequent Bonds. Subsequent Bonds shall benefit from and be subject to the Finance
Documents, and, for the avoidance of doubt, the ISIN, the Interest, the Nominal Amount and
the Final Redemption Date applicable to the Initial Bonds shall apply to Subsequent Bonds.
