13.5 Financial Covenants
The Issuer shall (on an unconsolidated basis) maintain a Market Adjusted Equity Ratio of minimum 50%.
13.5.2 Market Adjusted Equity
The Issuer shall (on an unconsolidated basis) maintain a Market Adjusted Equity of no less than NOK 2,500,000,000.
13.5.3 Liquidity
The Issuer shall (on an unconsolidated basis) maintain free and unrestricted Cash and Cash Equivalents of minimum NOK 50 million.
13.5.4 Testing
The financial covenant set out in Clauses 13.5.1 to 13.5.3 will apply at all times and shall be tested on a semi-annual basis, to be calculated on 30 June and 31 December each year and reported in connection with the corresponding Financial Statements and Semi-Annually Financial Reports, cf. Clause 13.2.1(c) and (d).
