17. THE BOND TRUSTEE
17.1 The role and authority o f the Bond Trustee
17.1.1
The Bond Trustee shall monitor the compliance by the Issuer of its obligations under these Bond Terms and applicable laws and regulations which are relevant to the terms of these Bond Terms, including supervision of timely and correct payment of principal or interest, (however, this shall not restrict the Bond Trustee from discussing matters of confidentiality with the Issuer), arrange Bondholders' Meetings, and make the decisions and implement the measures resolved pursuant to these Bond Teims. The Bond Trustee is not obligated to assess the Issuer's financial situation beyond what is directly set out in these Bond Teims.
17.1.2 The Bond Trustee may take any step it in its sole discretion considers necessary or advisable to ensure the rights of the Bondholders in all matters pursuant to the teims of these Bond Teims and is entitled to rely on advice from professional advisors. The
