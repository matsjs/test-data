(e) all other provisions of the Bond Terms (except (a) - (d) above) shall remain fully in force without any modifications, or as otherwise agreed.
18.2.3
All amounts owed by the Issuer hereunder covered by the Defeasance Pledge shall be applied by the Bond Trustee, in accordance with the provisions of these Bond Terms, against payment to the Bondholders of all sums due to them under these Bond Terms on the due date thereof.
Any excess funds not required for the payment of principal, premium and interest to the Bondholders (including any expenses, fees etc. due to the Bond Trustee hereunder) shall be returned to the Issuer.
18.3 Limitation o f claims
18.3.1 All claims under the Bonds and these Bond Terms for payment, including interest and principal, shall be subject to the time-bar provisions of the Norwegian Limitation Act of May 18, 1979 No. 18.
