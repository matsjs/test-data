THE BONDS
Binding nature o f the Bond Terms
By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of these Bond Terms and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 18.1.
These Bond Terms is available to anyone and may be obtained from the Bond Trustee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that these Bond Terms is available to the general public throughout the entire term of the Bonds. These Bond Terms may be published on Stamdata or such other venues as decided by the Bond Trustee.
The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 1,000 million (One thousand million Norwegian kroner). The Bond Issue may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 600,000,000 (Norwegian kroner six hundred million).
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and the minimum subscription and allocation shall be NOK 1,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one million).
The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
