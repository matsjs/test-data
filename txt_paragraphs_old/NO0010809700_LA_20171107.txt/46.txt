(h) Financial Indebtedness and guarantees
The issuer shall not incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness or grant guarantees ("Guarantees") to any third party other than (1) Financial Indebtedness ranking pari passu with this Bond Issue, (2) Financial Indebtedness which is subordinated debt of the Issuer, (3) Guarantees granted in favour of any creditor of Financial Indebtedness (permitted under the Bond Issue) incurred by any Subsidiary, or (4) other loans, guarantees or other financial assistance to third parties in the ordinary course of business of the Issuer on bona fide commercial terms, limited to a total amount of NOK 200 million.
