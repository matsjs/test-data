The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and the minimum subscription and allocation shall be NOK 1,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one million).
The Bond Issue will be described as "Kistefos AS Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2017/2021".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0809700.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
The Bond Issue is a Tap Issue, under which subsequent issues may take place after Issue Date up to the maximum amount described in Clause 2.2.1, running from the Issue Date and to be closed no later than 5 Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Terms also apply for later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will on the issuing of additional Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Terms regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
