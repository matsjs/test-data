17.3 Change o f Bond Trustee
17.3.1 Change of Bond Trustee shall be carried out pursuant to the procedures set out in Clause 16. The Bond Trustee shall continue to carry out its duties as bond trustee until such time that a new Bond Trustee is elected.
17.3.2
The fees and expenses of a new bond trustee shall be covered by the Issuer pursuant to the terms set out in Clause 16, but may be recovered wholly or partially from the Bond Trustee if the change is due to a breach by the Bond Trustee of its duties pursuant to the terms of these Bond Terms or other circumstances for which the Bond Trustee is liable.
17.3.3
The Bond Trustee undertakes to co-operate so that the new bond trustee receives without undue delay following the Bondholders' Meeting the documentation and information necessary to perform the functions as set out under the terms of these Bond Terms.
