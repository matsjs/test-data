"Permitted Security" means any security:
(a) in respect of the Existing Security, to be discharged on the first release of funds from the Escrow Account;
(b) created under the Finance Documents or the RCF Finance Documents;
(c)
created in respect of any Permitted Hedging Obligation or any New Debt, provided
that such security is extended to and shared between the Secured Parties on a pari
passu and pro rata basis pursuant to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement;
(d) arising by operation of law or in the ordinary course of trading and not as a result of any default or omission;
