Right to purchase Bond Liabilities
The RCF Creditors may, within 30 days after the Initial Enforcement Notice, by giving not less than 10 days' notice to the Bond Trustee, require the transfer to them (or to a nominee or nominees) of all, but not part, of the rights, benefits and obligations in respect of Secured Obligations owed to the Bond Trustee and the bondholders (the "Bond Liabilities") against payment to the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the bondholders) of any amount equal to the Bond Liabilities (plus costs and expenses in relation to such transfer). Such transfer shall be without recourse to the Bond Trustee or the bondholders or any representation or warranty from the Bond Trustee or the bondholders as to the validity, enforceability or recoverability of the Bond Liabilities or any Security, and shall otherwise be carried out in accordance with the terms and conditions set out in the Intercreditor Agreement.
Voting rights:
Subject to certain exceptions, each relevant Party shall cast its vote in any proposal put to the vote by or under the supervision of any judicial or supervisory authority in respect of any insolvency, pre-insolvency or rehabilitation or similar proceedings relating to any Group Company as instructed by the Bond Trustee (in its capacity as security agent for the Secured Parties). The Bond Trustee (in its capacity as security agent for the Secured Parties) shall give such instructions as directed by the relevant instructing group pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement.
