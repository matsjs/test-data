13.4 Distribution The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, make any Distributions, provided that the Group Companies shall have a right to make any Permitted Distribution.
13.6 De-mergers The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of:
(a) the Issuer;
(b) any Guarantor into two or more separate companies, unless (A) such separate companies are (directly or indirectly) wholly-owned by the Issuer, (B) the shares of such companies are pledged as security in favour of the Secured Parties according to the terms of the Intercreditor Agreement, and (C) following the de-merger, corporate restructuring or split, the Guarantee and Security Interest provided by any such company remains in full force and effect; or
