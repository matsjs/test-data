"Finance Charges" means, for the Relevant Period, the aggregate amount of the accrued interest, commission, fees (excluding arrangement fees in respect of any of the Initial Loan Amount under the Bond Issue (including Tap Issues) and the Revolving Credit Facility), discounts, payment fees, premiums or charges and other finance payments in respect of Financial Indebtedness whether paid, payable or capitalised by any member of the Group according to the latest Financial Report(s) (calculated on a consolidated basis), without taking into account any capitalised interest in respect of any Shareholder Loan or other subordinated indebtedness, or gains or losses (whether realised or unrealised) on any derivative instrument (including but not limited to in respect of any foreign exchange fluctuations).
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Finance Charges.

"Net Finance Charges" means, for the Relevant Period, the Finance Charges according to the latest Financial Report(s), after deducting any interest payable for that Relevant Period to any member of the Group and any interest income relating to cash or cash equivalent investment.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means the aggregate consolidated interest bearing Financial Indebtedness (excluding any Permitted Hedging Obligation, any Shareholder Loans and interest bearing debt borrowed from and between any Group Company) (including but not limited to any Intercompany Loans)) less cash and cash equivalents, including funds held on the Escrow Account, of the Group in accordance with GAAP.
"Relevant Period" means each period of twelve (12) months (on a rolling basis) ending on or about the last day of each financial quarter.
"Total Assets" means at the date of calculation the aggregate book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's total assets which are treated as assets in accordance with GAAP, as illustrated in the then most recent Financial Reports.
