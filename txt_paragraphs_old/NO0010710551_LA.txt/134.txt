Subject to certain exceptions, each relevant Party shall cast its vote in any proposal put to the vote by or under the supervision of any judicial or supervisory authority in respect of any insolvency, pre-insolvency or rehabilitation or similar proceedings relating to any Group Company as instructed by the Bond Trustee (in its capacity as security agent for the Secured Parties). The Bond Trustee (in its capacity as security agent for the Secured Parties) shall give such instructions as directed by the relevant instructing group pursuant to the Intercreditor Agreement.
The bondholders will appoint and authorise the Bond Trustee to hold and to act as their agent with respect to the Post-Settlement Security, to the extent permitted by applicable law.
The RCF Creditors and Hedging Counterparties will appoint and authorise the Bond Trustee as security agent to hold and to act as their agent with respect to the Post-Settlement Security, to the extent permitted by applicable law.
Any change of security agents shall require the consent of the Bond Trustee and the RCF Creditors. (The Bond Trustee shall be authorised (in its sole discretion) to grant such consent without any approval or consent from the bondholders.)
Governing law:
Norwegian.
Jurisdiction:
Non-exclusive jurisdiction of the Oslo City Court (in Norwegian: Oslo tingrett).
