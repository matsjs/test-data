Ranking of liabilities:
Each of the Parties agrees that (i) the Secured Obligations shall rank in right and priority of payment pari passu and without preference between them, and (ii) the Obligors' liabilities in respect of any Intercompany Loans and Shareholder Loans are postponed and subordinated to the Secured Obligations.
The Intercreditor Agreement shall not purport to rank any of the Shareholder Loans and Intercompany Loans as between themselves.
