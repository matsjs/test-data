In the event that any assets over which Security is granted under the Finance Documents are sold or otherwise disposed of by any Group Company to either the Issuer or any of the Issuer's wholly-owned Subsidiaries, the acquirer shall pledge such assets as Security in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Secured Parties according to the terms and conditions of the Intercreditor Agreement).
For the avoidance of doubt, the restrictions in this Clause 13.8 paragraph (a) above shall not restrict any Group Company from entering into negotiations and/or any sales agreement in respect of the shares in any Guarantor as long as the legal ownership over such shares are not being transferred to any third party (under such sales agreement) prior to the Bonds having been redeemed and discharged in full or a cash amount accepted by the Bond Trustee has been deposited in accordance with Clause 20.2 (Bond Defeasance).
Any Security granted pursuant to this Clause 13.8 shall be deemed as a "Pre-Disbursement Security" and any documents executed in relation hereto shall be deemed as "Security Documents".
13.9 Financial Indebtedness The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, incur any new Financial Indebtedness, provided that the Group Companies shall have a right to incur and maintain Financial Indebtedness that constitute Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
13.10 Negative pledge The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any loan or other indebtedness, provided that the Group Companies have a right to allow to create, subsist, retain, provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
13.11 Financial support The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, grant or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any loans or guarantees, or otherwise voluntarily assume any financial liability (whether actual or contingent), provided that the Group Companies have a right to grant, allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Financial Support.
