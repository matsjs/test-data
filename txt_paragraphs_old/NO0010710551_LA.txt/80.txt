13.21 Financial Covenants
During the term of the Bonds, the Issuer shall ensure that the Group (on a
consolidated basis), at all times, maintains an Equity Ratio of minimum 25.0%.
(b)
The financial covenant set out in (a) above will apply for the Group (on a consolidated
basis) at any time and compliance shall be measured on a quarterly basis (the first time
30 June 2014) by reference to each Financial Statement and Interim Account and
certified by the Issuer on the respective reporting date in a Compliance Certificate to
be delivered to the Bond Trustee.
