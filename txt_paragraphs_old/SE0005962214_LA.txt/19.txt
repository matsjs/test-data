USE OF PROCEEDS
4.
CONDITIONS FOR DISBURSEMENT
4.1
The Issuer shall provide to the Agent, prior to the issuance of the Initial Notes the
following, in form and substance satisfactory to the Agent:
(a) the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement duly executed by the Issuer and the Agent;
(b) a copy of a resolution from the board of directors of the Issuer approving the issue of the Initial Notes, the terms o f the Finance Documents and the Agency Agreement, and resolving to enter into such documents and any other documents necessary in connection therewith; and
(c) evidence that the person who has signed the Finance Documents, the Agency Agreement and any other documents in connection therewith on behalf of the Issuer is duly authorised to do so.
