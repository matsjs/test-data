9.4 Voluntary partial redemption
(a) The Issuer may on one occasion, being no earlier than the date occurring 21 months after the Issue Date, make a partial repayment of Bonds in an amount of maximum EUR 60,000,000. Such partial repayment shall reduce the Nominal Amount of each Bond pro rata and shall be made together with accrued but unpaid interest and a premium of 2 percent, of the repaid amount.
(b) Partial redemption in accordance with Clause 9.4(a) shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than twenty (20) Business Days' notice to the Bondholders and the Agent. Any such notice is irrevocable and, upon expiry of
