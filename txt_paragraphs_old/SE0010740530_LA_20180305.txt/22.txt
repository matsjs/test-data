3. Use of Proceeds
The Issuer shall use the Net Proceeds, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Bonds, for (i) financing the acquisitions of the Targets, and (ii) financing general corporate purposes of the Group including refinancing of any Existing Debt.
