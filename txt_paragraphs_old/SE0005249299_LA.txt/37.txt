Redemption in accordance with Clause 9.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less
than 15 Banking Days' notice to the Holders and the Trustee. Any such notice shall state
the Redemption Date and the relevant Record Date and is irrevocable but may, at the
Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent. Upon expiry of such notice
and the fulfilment of the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is bound to redeem the
Certificates in full at the applicable amounts.
