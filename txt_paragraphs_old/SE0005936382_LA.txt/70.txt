15.3
Funds that the Agent receives (directly or indirectly) in connection with the acceleration of
the Notes or the enforcement of the Transaction Security constitute escrow funds
(redovisningsmedel) and must be held on a separate interest-bearing account on behalf of
the Noteholders and the other interested parties. The Agent shall arrange for payments of
such funds in accordance with this Clause 15 as soon as reasonably practicable.
