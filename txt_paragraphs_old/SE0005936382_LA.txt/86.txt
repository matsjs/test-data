18.3
A communication pursuant to Clause 18.1 shall include (i) each request for a decision by
the Noteholders, (ii) a description of the reasons for each request, (iii) a specification of the
Business Day on which a person must be registered as a Noteholder in order to be entitled
to exercise voting rights, (iv) instructions and directions on where to receive a form for
replying to the request (such form to include an option to vote yes or no for each request)
