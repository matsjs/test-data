17.2
Should the Issuer want to replace the Agent, it may convene a Noteholders' Meeting in
accordance with Clause 17.1 with a copy to the Agent. After a request from the
Noteholders pursuant to Clause 20.4.3, the Issuer shall no later than five (5) Business Days
after receipt of such request (or such later date as may be necessary for technical or
administrative reasons) convene a Noteholders' Meeting in accordance with Clause 17.1.
