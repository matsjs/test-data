13.3
Financial Covenants The following financial covenants shall be complied with at any time:
(a) Minimum Liquidity: The Issuer undertakes to ensure that the Issuer has at any time minimum USD 5 million per Vessel on the Liquidity Account.
(b) Asset Coverage Ratio: The aggregate Charter Free Market Value of the Vessels shall at all time exceed 125% of the Outstanding Bonds.
