13.2.2
In connection with the publication of the Issuer's financial reports under Clause 13.2.1(c) and (d), the Issuer shall confirm to the Bond Trustee in writing its and the Vessel Owners' compliance with the covenants in this Clause 13, unless the Bond Trustee explicitly waives such requirement. Such confirmation shall be undertaken in a certificate, substantially in the form set out in Schedule 2 (Compliance Certificate) hereto, including relevant confirmations under the Covenant Agreement, signed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chief Financial Officer of the Issuer and the Covenant Providers (other than the Ultimate Parent) (a "Compliance Certificate").
