(e) Misrepresentations

(f) Insolvency
(i) Any Obligor, any Covenant Provider, the Vessel Manager, any other member of the Charterer Group being party to a Bareboat Charter, Alternative Charter Arrangement or Sub Charter, or any Material Charterer Subsidiary is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due, suspends making payments on any of its debts or, by reason of actual or anticipated financial difficulties, commences negotiations with one or more of its creditors with a view to rescheduling any of its indebtedness.
(ii) The value of the assets of any Obligor, any Covenant Provider, the Vessel Manager, any other member of the Charterer Group being party to a Bareboat Charter, Alternative Charter Arrangement or Sub Charter, or any Material Charterer Subsidiary is less than its liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities)
