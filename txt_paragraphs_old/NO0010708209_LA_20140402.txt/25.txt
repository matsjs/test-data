"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on (a) the financial condition or operations of any of the Obligors, (b) the Issuer's ability to perform or comply with its obligations under any of the Finance Documents, (c) any of the security providers' ability to perform and comply with its obligations under the Security Documents, (d) any of the Interco Subsidiaries', Charterer's or other member of the Charter Group's ability to perform and comply with its obligations under the Bareboat Charters, if applicable, any Alternative Charter Arrangement and any guarantees provided in respect of payments thereunder or (e) the validity or enforceability of any of the Finance Documents.
"Material Charterer Subsidiary" means a Subsidiary of the Charterer which, at the occurrence of the Event of Default, has either (i) assets in the aggregate amount of a total of USD five (5) million (or the equivalent thereof in other currencies) or (ii) Financial Indebtedness and commitments for Financial Indebtedness in the aggregate amount of a total of USD five (5) million (or the equivalent thereof in other currencies). When calculating the Financial Indebtedness and commitments for Financial Indebtedness in (ii) above, any Financial Indebtedness commitments for Financial Indebtedness of the Charterer Group falling within sub-paragraphs (i) - (iv) of paragraph (d) (Cross default) of Clause 15 (Events of Default) shall be taken into account.
