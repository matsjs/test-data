INTEREST
The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from, and including, the Issue
Date at a fixed rate of Seven point five per cent. (7.50%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate").
9.2
Interest on the Bonds will accrue from (and including) the Issue Date and shall be payable
semi-annually in arrears on the Interest Payment Dates, the first Interest Payment Date
falls in September 2014 and the last Interest Payment Date being the Maturity Date.
