If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.7, the amount in the Liquidity Account may, subject to the Issuer being in compliance with Financial Covenants immediately thereafter, be used as part payment in relation to the mandatory prepayment.

10.8 10.8.1
Total Loss Prepayment
Upon a Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall promptly once insurance proceeds are available, but in any event no later than sixty (60) days following the Total Loss Event, redeem (a) 100% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related to both Vessels or (b) 60% of the outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) if related one of the Vessels.
10.8.2
If the Bonds are redeemed according to this Clause 10.8, the amount in the Liquidity Account or any of the insurance proceeds, subject to the Issuer being in compliance with Financial Covenants immediately thereafter, may be used as part payment in relation to the Total Loss prepayment.
