On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the prinripal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.6.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.7 10.7.1
Mandatory Prepayment
Upon a Mandatory Prepayment Event occurring, the Issuer shall not later than 30 days following the relevant Mandatory Prepayment Event, or, in the event of sale or other disposal of any of the Vessels, or sale of shares in the Issuer Parent, the Issuer, any of the Vessel Owners, the Interco Parent, the Interco Subsidiaries or the Charterer, on or about the date upon which the sale or disposal of such shares or Vessel is completed (by transferring the redemption amount to an escrow account on the date upon which the sale or disposal of the relevant shares or Vessel is completed, for the redemption of Bonds to be carried out as soon as possible thereafter), unless there is an Event of Default in which case it will be promptly, redeem (a) 100% of the Outstanding Bonds if related to both Vessels or shares, other than shares in one of the Vessel Owners, or (b) 60% of the Outstanding Bonds if related to one of the Vessels or one of the Vessel Owners, in both instances at a price according to the Call Options as set out in Clause 10.5 (Call Option).
10.7.2
For the avoidance of doubt, the aforesaid redemption prices shall be determined based on the date the Mandatory Prepayment Event occurred and not based on the date of repayment.
