10.5.2
Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the settlement date of the Call Option.
10.5.3 Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.5.4
On the settlement date of the Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.5.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.5 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
