CHARTERER COVENANTS
(a) The Charterer undertakes from the date of this Agreement and until such time that no amounts are outstanding under the Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, to the Bond Trustee, as further set out in this Clause 24 and Clause 21 (Financial Covenants).
(b) The Charterer undertakes to:
(i) remain responsible under any Bareboat Charter (and any replacement thereof) for all operating costs relating to the relevant Vessel including insurance, operational and maintenance costs relating to the relevant Vessel;
(ii) ensure that it and any of its Subsidiaries complies with its obligations under the Second Bareboat Charters, the Charter Contracts and any Alternative Charter Arrangements (in all material respects);
(iii) procure that the Second Bareboat Charter Hire is in compliance with paragraph (c) (Second Bareboat Charter Hire) of Clause 13,8 (Intra-Group charter contract covenants and application of earnings) of the Bond Agreement;
(iv) for the purposes of The Council o f the European Union Regulation No. 1346/2000 on Insolvency Proceedings (the Regulation), have its centre of main interest (as that term is used in Article 3(1) of the Regulation) situated in England and Wales and have no "establishment" (as that term is used in Article 2(h) of the Regulation) in any other jurisdiction;
(v) ensure that any payments under the Management Agreements and the Business Service Agreements shall be subordinated to the Bonds, however so that management fees and service fees that are not overdue may be payable to the Vessel Manager and the Charterer on a monthly basis;
