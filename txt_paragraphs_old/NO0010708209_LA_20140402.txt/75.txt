A Mandatory Prepayment Event or a Total Loss Event shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least thirty (30) Business Days prior to the expected settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or the Total Loss Event. The settlement date may however, upon further notice to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders, be reduced or prolonged, due to the settlement of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event, but not with shorter notice than five (5) Business Days before the settlement day of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event.
On the settlement date of the Mandatory Prepayment Event or Total Loss Event, the Issuer shall pay (if on or about the date upon which the sale or disposal of shares or a Vessel is completed, by transferring the redemption amount to an escrow account on the date upon which the sale or disposal of the relevant shares or Vessel is completed, for the redemption of Bonds to be carried out as soon as possible thereafter, as provided for in Clause 10.7.1) to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.9.3 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds.
10.9.4
In the event that a Mandatory Prepayment Event relates to the sale or Total Loss of one of the Vessels or one of the Vessel Owners, then, after such prepayment has been implemented, the remaining Scheduled Instalments shall be reduced by 60% and all future First Bareboat Charter Hire and Second Bareboat Charter Hire after the date of that prepayment shall be amended accordingly (i.e. covering at least 100% of (i) daily accruals of interest on the Bonds and Scheduled Instalments due within the next six months, and (ii) amounts of out of pocket costs or expenses or operational or administrative expenses incurred by the Issuer Group).
10.9.5
Once (i) the relevant amount of the outstanding Bonds referred to above have been redeemed and (ii) any accrued and outstanding daily bareboat hire under the relevant Bareboat Charter have been paid in full and the relevant Vessel Owner has transferred all its rights related to the relevant Vessel Owners' Earnings Account to the Issuer, the corresponding Vessel Owner should further be released from any obligation under the Finance Documents.
