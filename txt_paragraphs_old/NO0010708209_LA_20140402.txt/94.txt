(d) Financial Indebtedness restrictions
No Vessel Owner shall incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than the (i) Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue, (ii) any Subordinated Loan or (iii) any Internal Loan.
(e) Financial support restrictions
No Vessel Owner shall grant any loans, guarantees, or other financial assistance to any party, other than (i) the Security Documents and any lien, (ii) any Vessel Owner Receivable and (iii) guarantees issued for the benefit of third parties in the ordinary course of business (including guarantees relating to the ownership and employment of its Vessel).
ff) Project Documents
(i) Each Vessel Owner shall enforce any rights that it has to ensure that the relevant Vessel is to the best of its knowledge at all times operated, insured and maintained in accordance with the Project Documents.
(ii) No Vessel Owner shall agree to or permit any changes to the Project Documents, except as permitted under paragraph (e) of Clause 13.8 (IntraGroup charter contracts covenants and application of earnings), changes to the Management Agreement as a consequence of Alternative Charter Arrangements and variations to the service fee payable under the Business Service Agreement.
