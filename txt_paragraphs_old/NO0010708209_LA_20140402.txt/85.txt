13.4
The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
(b) Mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities.
(c) De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
(d) Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of its business from that carried on at the date of this Bond Agreement, and/or as set out in this Bond Agreement.
The Issuer shall ensure that each Vessel Owner remains a single-purpose company (with no Subsidiaries) owning and bareboat chartering the relevant Vessel.
