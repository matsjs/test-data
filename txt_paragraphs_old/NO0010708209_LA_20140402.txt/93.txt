13.7
Vessel Owner's Special Covenants (a) Charter Contracts
The Vessel Owners shall procure under any First Bareboat Charter (and any replacement thereof) that any Charter Contract shall be entered into directly between the Charterer or any Qualified Subsidiary and any Client.
(b) Investment restrictions
No Vessel Owner shall make any investments or capital expenditures in any other activity than solely related to the ownership and ordinary operation of each respective Vessel.
(c) Negative pledge
No Vessel Owner shall create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets or revenues, including assignment of any of its rights under the Project Documents, or enter into arrangements having a similar effect except for (i) the Security Documents, (ii) any Security arising by operation of law or (iii) any Security granted over an asset which has been provided on retention of title terms in the ordinary course of business (including collateral in connection with credit purchases of goods and services).
