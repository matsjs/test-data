(c) Negative pledge
The Issuer shall not create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets or revenues, including assignment of any of its rights under the Project Documents, or enter into arrangements having a similar effect, except for (a) any Security Interest or (b) any lien or security arising by operation of law.
(djj Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than the (i) Financial Indebtedness arising under the Bond Issue, (ii) any Subordinated Loans or (iii) any Vessel Owner Receivable.
(e) Financial support restrictions
The Issuer shall not grant any loans, guarantees, or other financial assistance to any party, other than any Internal Loan.
(f) Subordinated Loans
(i) The Issuer shall on the disbursement date accept a Subordinated Loan from the Issuer Parent by way of the Existing Bareboat Charter Receivable (as defined and described in Schedule 1 (Settlement Procedure) as the required Additional Capital.
