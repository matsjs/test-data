Early voluntary redemption by the Issuer (call option)
Provided that the Outstanding Bond has been redeemed in full (or shall be redeemed in full prior to, or in immediate connection with, a redemption of the Bonds in accordance with this Clause 10.3), the Issuer may redeem all, but not only some, of the Bonds in full on any Business Day before the Final Redemption Date, at the applicable Call Option Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest.
Redemption in accordance with Clause 10.3.1 shall be made by the Issuer giving not less than fifteen (15) Business Days' notice to the Holders and the Trustee. Any such notice shall state the Redemption Date and the relevant Record Date and is irrevocable but may, at the Issuer's discretion, contain one or more conditions precedent. Upon expiry of such notice and the fulfilment o f the conditions precedent (if any), the Issuer is bound to redeem the Bonds in full at the applicable amounts.
