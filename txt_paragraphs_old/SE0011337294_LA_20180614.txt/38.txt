INTEREST
9.1
The Bonds will bear Interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal Amount from, but
excluding, the Issue Date up to and including the relevant Redemption Date. Any
Subsequent Bond will carry Interest at the Interest Rate from (but excluding) the Interest
Payment Date falling immediately prior to their issuance up to (and including) the relevant
Redemption Date.
