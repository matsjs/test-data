11.6
Disposal of Avito.ru
In the event that the Issuer disposes of more than fifty (50.00) per cent. of its holding in Avito.ru ("Avito"), the Issuer undertakes to, within thirty (30) Business Days of receipt of the proceeds from the disposal of Avito, either:
(a) redeem all of the Bonds in full in accordance with these Terms and Conditions and redeem the Outstanding Bond in full in accordance with the terms and conditions for the Outstanding Bond; or
(b) place an amount equivalent to the redemption amount required to redeem the Bonds and the Outstanding Bond as set out in paragraph (a) above (including, for the avoidance of doubt, any applicable premium payable on the redeemed Bonds and Outstanding Bond) on a blocked escrow account, pledged in favour of the Trustee (and the trustee for the Outstanding Bond) and the bondholders (represented by the Trustee and the trustee for the Outstanding Bond), where it shall remain until redemption of the Bonds and the Outstanding Bond. The funds standing on the credit of the escrow account may not be used for any other purpose than for redemption of the Bonds and the Outstanding Bond as set out in paragraph (a) above and shall, in the event of redemption, firstly be applied towards redemption of the Outstanding Bond and secondly towards redemption of the Bonds.
