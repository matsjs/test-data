13.5 Preservation o f Equity and Financial Covenants
(a) Dividends and other distributions The Issuer shall not within a calendar year declare or make any dividend payments or other distributions or loans to its shareholders - whether in cash or in kind - including without limitation repurchase of shares, any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect and reductions in its share capital or equity (but always distributions of bonus shares not affecting its equity) that constitute more than 50 per cent of (on a consolidated basis) the Issuer's net profit after taxes for the previous financial year, provided that the Book Equity Ratio remains above 35 % immediately after such payment described herein. Any un-utilized portion of the permitted distribution may not be carried forward.
(b) Book Equity Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group, on a consolidated basis, maintains at all times a Book Equity Ratio of at least 30 %.
