2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 100,000,000 (Norwegian kroner hundred million).
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Villa Organic AS Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2012/2015".
The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0654544.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3 Purpose and utilization
2.3.1 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be employed for general corporate purposes and refinancing of existing maturities.
