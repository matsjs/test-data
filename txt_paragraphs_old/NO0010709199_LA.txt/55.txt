INTEREST
9.1
Interest rate
(a) The Issuer shall pay interest on the par value of the Bonds from and including, the Issue Date at a fixed rate of 11 per cent. (11%) per annum (the "Fixed Rate"). The accrued interest on the Outstanding Bonds shall be settled through the issuance of additional Bonds to the Bondholders (the "Additional Bonds").
(b) The number of Additional Bonds allocated to a Bondholder holding Bonds shall be made by dividing (x) the total accrued interest amount of the Outstanding Bonds for the six months ending on (and including) the relevant Interest Payment Date with (y) the total number of Bonds of that Bondholder, provided that the total number of Additional Bonds allocated to a Bondholder shall be rounded to the nearest USD 1.00.
