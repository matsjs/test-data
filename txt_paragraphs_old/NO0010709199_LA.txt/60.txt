10.3
Call Option
(a) The Issuer may at any time redeem the Bond Issue (in whole or in part on a pro rata basis between the Bonds) at the from time to time prevailing Repayment Price (the "Call Option").
(b) Exercise of the Call Option shall be notified by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders at least ten Business Days prior to settlement date of the Call Option.
(c) Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.4
Change of control
(a) Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at the relevant Repayment Price.
