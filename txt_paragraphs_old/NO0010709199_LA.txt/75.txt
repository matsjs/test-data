15.2
Dividends and other distributions
(a) The Project Guarantor shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make any loans (including intercompany loans) or other distributions to its shareholder (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued) (for the avoidance of doubt, the Parent Guarantor shall be entitled to convert short term loans extended to the Project Guarantor into equity), except for the Permitted Upstream Advances (subject to Clause 15.11) and any financial support created under any Working Capital Facility, Prepayment Facility, and Permitted Cash Collateral.
(b) Subject to the provisions of this Agreement, the Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make any other distributions to its direct or indirect shareholders (including but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares) (for the avoidance of doubt, the Parent Guarantor shall be entitled to convert the Bond Intercompany Claim into equity in the Issuer in the same proportion as the Second Lien Bonds are converted to equity from time to time), except for the Permitted Upstream Advances (subject to Clause 15.11) and any financial support created under any Working Capital Facility, Prepayment Facility and Permitted Cash Collateral.
