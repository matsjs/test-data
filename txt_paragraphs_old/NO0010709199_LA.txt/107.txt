(d) Resolutions shall be passed by simple majority of the Voting Bonds represented at the Bondholders' Meeting, unless otherwise set out in this Clause 18.3(e).
(f) The Bondholders' Meeting may not adopt resolutions which may give certain Bondholders or others an unreasonable advantage at the expense of other Bondholders.
(g) The Bond Trustee shall ensure that resolutions passed at the Bondholders' Meeting are properly implemented, however, the Bond Trustee may refuse to carry out resolutions being in conflict with this Bond Agreement (or any other Finance Document) or any applicable law.
(h) The Issuer and the Bondholders shall be notified of resolutions passed at the Bondholders' Meeting.
18.4
Repeated Bondholders'meeting
(a) If the Bondholders' Meeting does not form a quorum pursuant to Clause 18.3(c), a repeated Bondholders' Meeting may be summoned to vote on the same matters. The attendance and the voting result of the first Bondholders' Meeting shall be specified in the summons for the repeated Bondholders' Meeting.
(b) A valid resolution may be passed at a repeated Bondholders' meeting even though less than half (1 /2) of the Voting Bonds are represented.
