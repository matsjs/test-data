THE BONDS
Binding nature of this Bond Agreement
By virtue of being registered as a Bondholder (directly or indirectly) with the Securities Depository, the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document, without any further action required to be taken or formalities to be complied with, see also Clause 20.1.
2.2 The Bonds
(a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the principal nominal amount of USD 60,447,639 each with a Face Value of USD 1.
(b) The Bonds will be issued at a 5 % discount to their principal nominal amount, giving an aggregate subscription price of USD 57,000,000.
(c) The Bond Issue will be described as "Northland Resources AB (publ.) - Super Senior Bridge Bonds 2014.
(d) The ISIN of the Bonds is NO 0010709199.
(e) The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date, unless the tenor is extended in accordance with Clause 10.1 item (b), in which case the Bonds shall mature on the Extended Maturity Date.
