The Issuer shall not, and no Issuer Subsidiary shall invest or take part in any activity other than solely related to the Kaunisvaara Project and related matters, always subject to the restrictions in the Existing Bond Loan Agreements, and, as long as it is funded by sources outside the Project Guarantor, the Issuer and the Issuer Subsidiaries, exploration and development activities in the Kaunisvaara area.
Arm's length transactions
(a) The Project Guarantor shall not engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Project Guarantor's and upon fair and reasonable terms and, except for any transaction with the Obligors, on terms that are no less favourable to the Parent Guarantor or the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
(b) The Issuer shall not, and no Issuer Subsidiary shall engage in, directly or indirectly, any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms and, except for any transaction within the Obligors (other than with the Parent Guarantor), on terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
