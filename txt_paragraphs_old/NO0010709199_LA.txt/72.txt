14.3
Financial statements
(a) The Parent Guarantor shall without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and Interim Accounts, and make them available on its website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 150 days after the end of the financial year (for Financial Statements) and 90 days after the end of the relevant quarter (for Interim Accounts).
(b) The Issuer shall without being requested to do so, prepare Financial Statements and Interim Accounts, and make them available on the Parent Guarantor's website in the English language (alternatively by arranging for publication at Stamdata) as soon as they become available, and not later than 180 days after the end of the financial year (for Financial Statements) and 90 days after the end of the relevant quarter (for Interim Accounts).
14.4
Compliance certificate
Each of the Issuer and the Parent Guarantor shall in connection with the publication of its financial reports under Clause 14.3, confirm to the Bond Trustee in writing its compliance with the covenants in Clause 15, unless the Bond Trustee explicitly waives such requirement. Such confirmation shall be undertaken in a certificate, substantially in the form set out in Schedule 1 hereto, signed by the Chief Executive Officer or the Chief Financial Officer of the Issuer or the Parent Guarantor (as the case may be) (a "Compliance Certificate"). In the event of non-compliance, the Compliance Certificate shall describe the non-compliance, the reasons therefore as well as the steps which the Issuer and/or the Parent Guarantor has taken and will take in order to rectify the non compliance.
