NOTICE OF REDEMPTION. At least 30 days but not more than 60 days before a
redemption date, the Issuer shall deliver, pursuant to Section 12.01 of the Indenture, a notice of
redemption to each Holder whose Notes are to be redeemed, except that redemption notices may be
mailed more than 60 days prior to a redemption date if the notice is issued in connection with a
defeasance of the Notes or the satisfaction and discharge of the Indenture pursuant to Articles 8 or 11 of
the Indenture. Notes in denominations larger than $200,000 may be redeemed in part but only in whole
multiples of $1,000 in excess thereof, unless all of the Notes held by a Holder are to be redeemed.
