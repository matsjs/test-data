The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, enter into any de-merger, merger or other corporate restructuring (except for the Coiporate Restructuring, Intra-Group Vessel Transfers and any intra-issuer Group reorganisation which would not have a Material Adverse Effect).
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, sell or dispose of any of its equity interests in any Vessel Owning Companies, the Vessels or other assets that are pledged as Security for the Bond Issue, except for any Intra-Group Vessel Transfers and any disposals carried out as a Permitted Disposal.
14.8 Listing
The Issuer shall apply for the Bonds to be admitted to listing on the Exchange within 9 months ofthe Issue Date, cf. Clause 4.
14.9 Financial Indebtedness restriction
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company shall, incur, create or permit to subsist any Financial Indebtedness other than Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
