14.4 Ownership The Issuer shall:
(a) save for any Minority Interest, remain the sole owner (directly and indirectly) of the other Vessel Owning Companies; and
(b) ensure that the Vessel Owning Companies shall continue to directly hold legal title to and own the entire beneficial interest in the respective Vessels, except for any such cease or change resulting from Permitted Disposals.
14.5 Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, cease to carry on its business or change the general nature of its business from that earned on by the Issuer Group at the Issue Date, except for any such cease or change resulting from Permitted Disposals or Additional Vessel Acquisitions.
14.6 Mergers and de-mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, enter into any de-merger, merger or other corporate restructuring (except for the Coiporate Restructuring, Intra-Group Vessel Transfers and any intra-issuer Group reorganisation which would not have a Material Adverse Effect).
