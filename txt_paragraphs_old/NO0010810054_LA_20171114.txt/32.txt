The Tap Issue Test is met if the Issuer meets the requirements of either Tap Issue Test A or Tap Issue Test B, and no Event of Default is outstanding or will occur from such Tap Issue.

(b) the net proceeds to be deposited into the Escrow Account from such relevant Tap Issue shall be included; and
(c) the Outstanding Bond Amount shall take into account the additional Bonds resulting from the relevant Tap Issue.
"TEU" means twenty-foot equivalent unit, as used to describe the capacity of container ships.
"Total Loss Event" means an actual or constructive total loss of any of the Vessels.
"Transaction Security" means the Security created or expressed to be created in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Secured Parties) pursuant to the Transaction Security Documents (subject to any mandatory limitations under applicable law).
"Transaction Security Documents" means, collectively, all of the documents which shall be executed or delivered pursuant to Clause 2.5 (Transaction Security) expressed to create any Security by the relevant grantor thereof in respect of the Issuer's obligations under any of the Finance Documents.
