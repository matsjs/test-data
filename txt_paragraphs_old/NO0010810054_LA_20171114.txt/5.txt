INTERPRETATION
1.1 Definitions The following terms will have the following meanings:
"Account Pledges'' means the first priority charges over all bank accounts held by the Issuer Group, pledged on first priority, but not blocked, in favour of the Security Agent.
"Additional Bonds" means Bonds issued under a Tap Issue.
"Additional Vessel Acquisitions" means any acquisition by an Issuer Group Company of Additional Vessel(s), including Vessel(s) initially acquired by a Group Company outside of the Issuer Group, provided that such Vessel or Vessel Owning Company is transferred to the relevant Issuer Group Company within 60 calendar days after the relevant Group Company's initial purchase and on terms and conditions, hereunder the Transaction Value, no less attractive than the initial purchase as described and confirmed by the Issuer in writing to the Bond Trustee.
"Additional Vessels" means any container and bulker vessel which may be acquired by the Issuer Group, provided that such vessel:
