Tap Issue pre-disbursement
(e) In connection with a Tap Issue, the net proceeds from the Tap Issue will not be released (in frill or parts) from the Escrow Account and disbursed to the Issuer unless the Bond Trustee (subject to customary closing conditions) shall have received prior to such disbursement to the Issuer each of the corresponding relevant documents mentioned in Clause 6.1 (Conditions Precedent for disbursement to the Issuer) paragraph (d) (Pre-disbursement - Additional Vessel Acquisitions) above together with any relevant Transaction Security Documents and the following documents, in form and substance satisfactory to the Bond Trustee:
(i) any other transaction specific conditions precedent as may be required under a Tap Issue; and
(ii) in case the Tap Issue is conducted in compliance with Tap Issue Test B, funds shall be released from the Escrow Account according to the debt to equity ratio as set out under Tap Issue Test B.
If, in relation to net proceeds from a Tap Issue, at any time, the amount standing to the credit of the Escrow Account is less than USD 2,000,000 and such amount has been standing to the credit of the Escrow Account for more than three (3) months, such amount may freely be released to the Issuer and applied for general coiporate purposes of the Issuer Group.
(f) The Bond Trustee, acting in its reasonable discretion, may waive the deadline or the requirements for documentation set out in this Clause 6.1 {Conditions precedent for disbursement to the Issuer), or decide in its discretion that delivery of certain documents as set out in this Clause 6.1 {Conditions precedent for disbursement to the Issuer) shall be made subject to an agreed closing mechanism between the Bond Trustee and the Issuer.
