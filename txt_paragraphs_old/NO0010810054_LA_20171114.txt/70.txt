13. PARENT UNDERTAKINGS The Parent undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that the other Group Companies, including for the avoidance of doubt the Issuer Group Companies, will) comply with the undertakings set forth in this Clause 13 {Parent Undertakings).
13.1 Pari passu ranking The Parent shall ensure that the Obligors' obligations under the Bond Terms and any other Finance Document shall at all times rank at least pari passu with any other obligations of the Obligors and the Parent (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application, including maritime liens) as set out under Status of the Bonds.
13.2 Compliance with laws The Parent shall, and shall procure that each other Group Company will, comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations to which it may be subject from time to time, if failure so to comply would have a Material Adverse Effect.

13.3 Corporate status The Parent shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation.
13.4 Continuation of business The Parent shall procure that no material change is made to the general nature of the business from that carried on by the Group at the Issue Date and that the sole business activity of the Group will relate to the container and bulker shipping industry.
13.5 Mergers and de-mergers The Parent shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, enter into any de merger, merger or other corporate restructuring (except for the Coiporate Restructuring and any intra-Group reorganisation which would not have a Material Adverse Effect).
13.6 Arm's length transactions The Parent shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
