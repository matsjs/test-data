12.2 Requirements as to Financial Reports and Compliance Certificate (a) The Issuer shall provide to the Bond Trustee, in connection with the publication of its Financial Reports pursuant to Clause 12.1 {Financial Reports) a Compliance Certificate with a copy of the Financial Report attached thereto with supporting documentation (including, as applicable, Vessel LTV Ratio and Minimum Liquidity, cf. Clause 14.13 {Financial Covenants) below).
(b) In addition the Issuer shall provide a Compliance Certificate to the Bond Trustee no later than thirty (30) calendar days after the occurrence of;
(i) a Permitted Disposal (and the corresponding Mandatory Prepayment); or
(ii) the Issuer meeting the requirements of Tap Issue Test A.
(c) The Compliance Certificate shall be duly signed by the chief executive officer or the chief financial officer of the Issuer, certifying, inter alia, that the Financial Statements are fairly representing its financial condition as at the date of those financial statements and setting out (in reasonable detail) computations evidencing compliance with Clause 14.13 {Financial Covenants) as at such date.
(d) The Issuer shall procure that the Financial Reports delivered pursuant to Clause 12.1 {Financial Reports) are prepared using IFRS consistently applied.
