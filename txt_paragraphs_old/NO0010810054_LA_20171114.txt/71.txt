13.5 Mergers and de-mergers The Parent shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, enter into any de merger, merger or other corporate restructuring (except for the Coiporate Restructuring and any intra-Group reorganisation which would not have a Material Adverse Effect).
13.7 Issuer ownership The Parent shall maintain direct or indirect ownership of 100% of the equity interests and voting rights of the Issuer.
13.8 Financial indebtedness restriction The Parent shall not incur, create, permit to subsist or provide guarantee for any unsecured debt under any bond or debt capital markets transaction, unless such unsecured indebtedness is non-amortizing and with a final maturity date falling after the Maturity Date and with no Financial Support from any Issuer Group Company.
14. ISSUER UNDERTAKINGS The Issuer undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that the other Issuer Group Companies will) comply with the undertakings set forth in this Clause 14 (Issuer Undertakings).
14.1 Distributions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, make any Distribution other than a Permitted Distribution.
14.2 Investments The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company will, acquire any company, equity interests, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them) or make any other investments or capital expenditures, other than Additional Vessel Acquisitions or otherwise solely related to the direct or indirect ownership in and operation, maintenance and improvement of the Vessels.
