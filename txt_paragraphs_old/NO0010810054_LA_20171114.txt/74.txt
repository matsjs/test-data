Financial support restriction
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Issuer Group Company shall, grant any Financial Support to or for the benefit of any third party or other Group Company, other than any Financial Support:
(a) granted under the Finance Documents; and
(b) provided as a Group Loan between the Obligors.
