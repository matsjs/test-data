13.13
Financial support
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not grant any Financial Support to or for the benefit of any party.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Financial Support provided in relation to Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
