"M andatory Prepayment Event" means:
(a) a sale or disposal of the Collateral Shares; or
(b) the shares issued by Fjordl ceases to be listed on the Exchange.
"Margin" means 5.00 per cent.
"Market Value of the Collateral Shares" means the market value of the Collateral Shares being determined by applying the volume weighted average share price (VWAP) of the Fjordl shares on the Exchange during the last twenty (20) trading days immediately preceding the testing date.
"Material Adverse Effect" means a material adverse effect on:
(a) the ability of the Issuer to perform and comply with its obligations under any of the Finance Documents; or
(b) the validity or enforceability o f any of the Finance Documents.
"Maturity Date" means 19 June 2023, adjusted according to the Business Day Convention.
