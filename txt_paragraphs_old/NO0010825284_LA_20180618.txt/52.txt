Disposals
(a) The Issuer shall not sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or substantially all of its assets, save in case for a Mandatory Prepayment Event.
13.15 Financial Covenants and Incurrence Test (a) The Issuer undertakes to at all times comply with the following financial covenants:
(i) Loan to Value Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Loan to Value Ratio does not exceed 70 per cent.
(ii) Minimum liquidity
The Issuer shall maintain Liquidity in an amount o f no less than the estimated next twelve (12) months of interest payments under the Bonds.
(b) Compliance with the above financial covenants shall be certified by way of a Compliance Certificate to be delivered in accordance with Clause 12.2 (a).
