(d) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation (other than to a public limited company) or jurisdiction of organisation.
(e)
Ownership to Material Subsidiaries
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company shall, sell, transfer, assign or otherwise dilute or dispose of any shares or any other ownership interest in any of the Material Subsidiaries, unless:
(i) such sale, transfer or disposal is made to another Group Company; or
(ii) the Outstanding Bonds are redeemed in accordance with Clause 10.4 {Mandatory Redemption) (subject to the suspensions rights set out in Clause 10.5 {SuspendedRedemption)).
