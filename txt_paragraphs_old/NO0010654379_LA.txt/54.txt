Financial support restrictions
The Issuer shall ensure that no Material Subsidiary shall, grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance (including, but not limited to the granting of security) to or for the benefit of any third party, any Material Subsidiary or any other Group Company other than in relation:
(i) the Existing Vessel Debt; and
(ii) intra-group obligations between members of the AFP Group.
