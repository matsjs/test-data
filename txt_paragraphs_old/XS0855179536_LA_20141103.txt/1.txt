Principal, Maturity and Interest
Interest on the Dollar Senior Secured Notes will accrue at the rate of 8.75% per annum and will be payable semi annually in arrear on May 15 and November 15, commencing May 15, 2013. Interest on the Euro Senior Secured Notes will accrue at the rate of 9.00% per annum and will be payable semi-annually in arrear on May 15 and November 15,
152
commencing May 15, 2013. The Company will make each interest payment to the holders of record on the immediately preceding May 1 and November 1.
Interest on the Notes will accrue from the date of original issuance or, if interest has already been paid, from the date it was most recently paid. Interest will be computed on the basis of a 360-day year comprised of twelve 30-day months. Interest on overdue principal and interest and all Additional Amounts (if any) then due will accrue at a rate that is 1% higher than the applicable interest rate on the Notes.
