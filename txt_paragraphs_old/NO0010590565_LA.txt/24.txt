Registration in the Securities Depository
4.1 The Bonds shall be registered in the Securities Depository according to the Securities Depository Act and the conditions agreed with the Securities Depository, effective from and including the Disbursement Date.
4.2 The Borrower shall promptly arrange for notification to the Securities Depository of any change in the terms and conditions of the Loan. The Loan Trustee shall be provided with a copy of such notification.
4.3 The Borrower is responsible for the correct registration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository. The registration may be executed by an agent for the Borrower provided that the agent is qualified according to relevant regulations.
