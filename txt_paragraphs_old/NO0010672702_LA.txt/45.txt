11.4
Set-off and counterclaims
The Issuer may not apply or perform any deductions, counterclaims or set-off against any payment obligations pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document.
11.5 11.5.1
Interest in the event of late payment
In the event that any amount due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document is not made on the relevant due date, the unpaid amount shall bear interest from the due date at an interest rate equivalent to the interest rate according to Clause 9 plus five per cent. (5.00%) per annum (meaning that in the case of the Initial Bonds and Secondary Bonds (if any), default interest will be charged at 5.00% per annum).
11.5.2 The interest charged under this Clause 11.5 shall be added to the defaulted amount on each respective Payment Date relating thereto until the defaulted amount has been repaid in full.
