"Defeasance Pledge" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Enforcement Bond Costs" means the costs and expenses incurred by each of the Newco Group, the Existing New Bondholders, the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders in each case, including the fees, costs, disbursements and expenses of their respective professional advisers) in connection with;
(a) the negotiation and preparation of (i) the termsheet regarding the Bonds, (ii) the Finance Documents and any registration or notifications relating thereto (including any stamp duty), (iii) the listing of Bonds on an Exchange (if applicable), and (iv) the registration and administration of the Bonds in the Securities Depository;
(b) the establishment of the Newco Group (including the fees of the directors of the Newco Group);
(c) the Newco Group's costs in relation to the Gibraltarian judicial sale of the Vessel; and
(d) the enforcement of the Bonds (however, this sub section shall be limited to costs and expenses incurred by each of the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders (in each case, including the fees, costs, disbursements and expenses of their respective professional advisers).
"Escrow Account" means an account(s) in the name of the Issuer, pledged and blocked on first priority as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Event of Default" means the occurrence of an event or circumstance specified in Clause 15.1.
"Exchange" means (i) a securities exchange or other reputable regulated market, or (ii) Oslo Alternative Bond Market, on which the Bonds are listed, or where the Issuer has applied for listing of the Bonds.
