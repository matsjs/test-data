"Security" means any encumbrance, mortgage, charge, pledge, lien or other security interest securing any obligation of any person or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security Documents" means, collectively, all the documents evidencing, creating or granting the Security Interests.
"Security Interests" means:
(a) the Guarantee;
(b) the first ranking Portuguese law governed mortgage, together with an English law governed deed of covenants in favour of the Bond Trustee over the Vessel (the "Bond Mortgage") ;
(c) the Cayman Islands law governed first ranking share mortgage over the shares of the Issuer granted to the Bond Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders (the "Bond Share Charge");
(d) the Norwegian law governed first ranking charge over the Escrow Account to the Bond Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders (the "Bond Escrow Charge");
(e) the Cayman Island law governed assignment over the intra-group loan agreement between the Issuer and the Guarantor, granted to the Bond Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders (the "Bond Intra-Group Loan Assignment"); and
(f) the Cayman Island law governed floating charge over the Issuer's and Guarantor's assets in favour of the Bond Trustee on behalf of the Bondholders (the "Bond Floating Charge").
