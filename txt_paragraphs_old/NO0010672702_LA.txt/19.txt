The Additional Bonds may be interest bearing Bonds. The Face Value of each Additional Bond shall be USD 1. The Additional Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves, and with all other senior indebtedness of the Issuer (subject to Clause 8.1), including the Initial Bonds and the Secondary Bonds (if any).
2.5.4 The tenor of any Additional Bonds is from and including the relevant Additional Bonds Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.5.5
For the avoidance of doubt, existing Bondholders shall not be required to purchase any Additional Bonds, and purchasers of any Additional Bonds shall become "Bondholders" for the purposes of this Bond Agreement. The Additional Bonds shall be offered to the Bondholders as at the date of the offer to participate in the Additional Bonds.
2.6 Purpose and utilization The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied for the following purposes;
(a) in relation to the purchase by the Guarantor of the Vessel by means of participation in a judicial private treaty sale in Gibraltar; and/or
(b) in relation to fees, costs and expenses incurred in connection with the establishment of the Newco Group, the issuance of the Bonds and paragraph (a) above; and/or
(c) funding of the working capital requirements of the Newco Group.
