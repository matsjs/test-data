5.2 The Non-Participating Bondholders shall have a period of ten (10) Business Days from the date of service of the Subscription Notice to deliver duly completed and executed Commitment Letters to the Manager, pursuant to which the Issuer shall issue and any such Non-Participating Bondholder shall be entitled to subscribe on a pro rata basis (calculated by reference to their holdings of Existing New Bonds as at the Issue Date as against the aggregate outstanding number of Existing New Bonds as at the Issue Date, their "Pro Rata Share") for (i) Secondary Bonds, and (ii) Subscription Shares, in accordance with the provisions of Clause 5.3 below.
5.3
Non-Participating Bondholders who have submitted Commitment Letters to the Manager in
accordance with the provisions of Clause 5.2 above shall be entitled to:
(a) subscribe for Secondary Bonds in an aggregate principal face amount equal to their Pro Rata Share of USD20,000,000; and
(b) subscribe for that a number of Subscription Shares that represents their Pro Rata Share to shares of USD0.01 each in the capital of the Issuer.
