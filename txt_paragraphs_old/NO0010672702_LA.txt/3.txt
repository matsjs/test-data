"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Schedules to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means collectively, the Initial Issue, the Secondary Issue (if any) and the Additional Issue (if any).
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
"Bondholders' Meeting" means a meeting of Bondholders, as set out in Clause 16.
"Bonds" means collectively, the Initial Bonds, the Secondary Bonds (if any) and the Additional Bonds (if any).
"Business Day" means any day on which commercial banks are open for general business and can settle foreign currency transactions in Oslo, London and New York.
