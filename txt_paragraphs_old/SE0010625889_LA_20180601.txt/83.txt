19.3
The Trustee shall promptly notify the Bondholders of any amendments or waivers made
in accordance with Clause 19.1, setting out the date from which the amendment or
waiver will be effective, and ensure that any amendments to the Finance Documents are
published in the manner stipulated in Clause 11.3 (Publication o f Finance Documents).
The Issuer shall ensure that any amendments to the Finance Documents are duly
registered with the CSD and each other relevant organisation or authority.
