17.3
The notice pursuant to Clause 17.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for
the meeting, (iii) agenda for the meeting (including each request for a decision by the
Bondholders) and (iv) a form of power of attorney. Only matters that have been
included in the notice may be resolved upon at the Bondholders' Meeting. Should prior
notification by the Bondholders be required in order to attend the Bondholders'
Meeting, such requirement shall be included in the notice.
