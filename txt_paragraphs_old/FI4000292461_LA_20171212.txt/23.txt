3. Use of Proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Bonds, less the costs and expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Bonds, for (i) refinancing the Existing Debt, (ii) finance payment(s) of one-off dividend in an aggregated amount of EUR 24,200,000 (the "One-Off Dividend"), and (iii) finance general corporate purposes. The proceeds from any Subsequent Bonds shall be used to finance general corporate purposes of the Group, including investments and acquisitions.
