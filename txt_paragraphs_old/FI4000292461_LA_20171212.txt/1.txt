1.1 Definitions
In these terms and conditions (the "Terms and Conditions"):
"Act on Noteholders' Agent" means the Finnish Act on Noteholders' Agent (Fin: Laki joukkolainanhaltijoiden edustajasta 574/2017).
"Adjusted Nominal Amount" means the Nominal Amount less the Nominal Amount of all Bonds owned by a Group Company or an Affiliate of the Issuer, irrespective of whether such Person is directly registered as owner of such Bonds.
