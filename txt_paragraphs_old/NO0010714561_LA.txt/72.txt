13.6.2 Flag and registry
The Issuer shall not cause the flag, name or registry of the Rig to be changed, without the prior written consent of the Bond Trustee, such approval not to be unreasonably withheld, always provided that such approval will not be required if a change is required and permitted pursuant to the Project Documents and the Security Documents are not impaired.
13.6.3 Inspections
The Issuer shall upon the request of the Bond Trustee arrange for the Bond Trustee (and/or any person appointed by the Bond Trustee) to undertake a technical inspection of the Rig without interference of the daily operation of the Rig and at the expense of the Issuer (however, limited to one (1) yearly inspection at the reasonable expense of the Issuer unless an Event of Default has occurred);
13.6.4 Classification and repairs
(a) The Issuer shall procure that the Rig is classified and maintained in class with the highest possible class notation, free of overdue requirements or recommendations, with DNY (or any other IACS classification society), and at all times comply with the rules and regulations of the relevant class society.
(b) The Issuer shall procure that the Rig comply with relevant class requirements and is kept in good, safe and efficient condition consistent with prudent ownership and industry standards.
