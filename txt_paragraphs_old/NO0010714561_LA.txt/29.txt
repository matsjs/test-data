2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of up to EUR 190,000,000 (EUR One Hundred and Ninety Million).
The Face Value is EUR 100,000 each with a minimum subscription and allotment of EUR 100,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "7.00 per cent Jacktel AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2014/2019".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0714561.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
