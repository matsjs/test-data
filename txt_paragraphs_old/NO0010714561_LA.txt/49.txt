10 MATURITY OF THE BONDS AND REDEMPTION
If the aggregate amount of the Outstanding Bonds at any Interest Payment Date falling in January and July, exceeds the Borrowing Limit, the Issuer shall on such Interest Payment Date redeem Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount) in an amount equal to the difference between the aggregate amount of Outstanding Bonds and the Borrowing Limit. Partial redemption must be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures for the Securities Depositary).
10.2 Maturity
The Bonds not already redeemed shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
