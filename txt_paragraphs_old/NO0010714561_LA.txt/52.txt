10.4.3
The Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the redemption request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end of the twenty-one (21) days exercise period of the Put Option.
10.4.4
On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.4.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.5 Sale or disposal of the Rig
Upon a sale or other disposal of the Rig, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer receives the proceeds following such sale or other disposal of the Rig, redeem all the Outstanding Bonds at a price equalling the Call Option price set out in Clause 10.3 above, based on the date the sale or other disposal occurred and not on the date the redemption is completed.
10.6 Total Loss
Upon the occurrence of a Total Loss, the Issuer shall, on or about the day the Issuer receives the insurance proceeds following such Total Loss, redeem all the Outstanding Bonds at 100% of par value (plus accrued interest on the redeemed amount).
