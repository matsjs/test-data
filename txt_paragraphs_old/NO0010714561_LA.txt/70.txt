13.6 Rig covenants
13.6.1 Insurances
(a) The Issuer shall ensure that the Rig and all relevant equipment are insured against such risks, including but not limited to, hull and machinery, protection and indemnity (including maximum cover for pollution liability as normally adopted by the industry for similar accommodation units), hull interest, freight interest and war risk insurance, and any other industry standard insurances and loss payable clauses of the Rig and equipment related thereto in accordance with the Nordic Marine Insurance Plan or at least similar terms (in the reasonable opinion of the Bond Trustee), and in such amount, as are consistent with prudent business practice and with first class insurers.
