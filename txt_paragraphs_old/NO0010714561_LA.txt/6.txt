"Call Option" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 10.3.
"Change of Control Event" means if any person or group under the same Decisive Influence, other than (i) Nordic Capital VII Alpha, L.P., Nordic Capital VII Beta, L.P., or any other fund within the Nordic Capital group of private equity funds or (ii) any international reputable company operating in the oil & gas industry holding minimum an investment grade rating by a reputable investment grade agency, obtains Decisive Influence over the Parent.
"Charter Contract" means any charter contract and/or other contract of employment in respect of the Rig entered into by the Issuer with a Charterer.
"Charter Contract Assignment" means the first priority assignment by way of security from the Issuer in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Bondholders) of all its rights to and title and interest under, whether present or future, any Charter Contract as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, provided that such assignment is permitted pursuant to the terms of the Charter Contract(s) (it being understood that the Issuer shall use its reasonable endeavours to agree Charter Contract(s) that allows such assignment) and applicable law.
"Charter Novation Agreement" means the novation agreement dated 7 April 2010 between the Parent and the Issuer for the ConocoPhillips Charter Contract whereby the Parent novated all of its rights and obligations under the ConocoPhillips Charter Contract to the Issuer.
"Charterer" means:
(a) in respect of ConocoPhillips Charter Contract, ConocoPhillips Scandinavia AS; and
(b) in respect of any other Charter Contract, any client under a Charter Contract (such client not being the Parent or a Subsidiary of the Parent).
