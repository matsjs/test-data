13.3.9 Subordinated Loans
Each of the Issuer and the Parent shall ensure that any existing and future Subordinated Loans to the Issuer from the Parent shall be fully subordinated to the Bonds and without any enforcement rights during the term of the Bonds. Save for the Initial Parent Loan Repayment, any debt service (including interest payments) of any Subordinated Loans may be made to the extent permitted by Clause 13.5 (a) below and provided that no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing under the Bonds.
