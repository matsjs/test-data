13.3 General covenants
13.3.1 Pari passu ranking
The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
13.3.2 Mergers
The Issuer shall not cany out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer with any other companies or entities.
13.3.3 De-mergers
The Issuer shall not cany out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
