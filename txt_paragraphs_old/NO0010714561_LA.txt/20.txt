"Quarter Date" means each 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December.
"Quiet Enjoyment Letter" means a quiet enjoyment letter to be issued by the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) in favour of any Charterer (and its creditors) under a Charter Contract, if so required by that Charterer, in such form as the Issuer shall reasonably require in order to be able to comply with the requirements of such Charterer. The Quiet Enjoyment Letter shall be given on industry standard terms, and shall include an undertaking that for as long as:
(a) the Charterer is not in breach of any obligations under the Charter Contract which breach would permit the termination of the Charter Contract; and
(b) payment of all amounts due and payable under the Charter Contract is duly made pursuant to the terms thereof,
then, the rights and remedies of the Bond Trustee under the Mortgage shall be subordinated and subject to the rights and remedies of the Charterer under the Charter Contract, and the Bond Trustee shall not disturb or interfere with the quiet and peaceful use, possession and enjoyment of the Rig by the Charterer under the Charter Contract; provided that the Quiet Enjoyment Letter may only be issued if the Quiet Enjoyment Letter provides for the Bond Trustee:
(i) to (if applicable) be notified by the Charterer if the Issuer is in default under the Charter Contract and is granted the right to remedy such breach; and
(ii) in case of any Event of Default under this Bond Agreement which is not remedied, to have a right to transfer the Rig and assign the Charter Contract to a nominee of the Bond Trustee, being reasonably satisfactory to the Charterer.
