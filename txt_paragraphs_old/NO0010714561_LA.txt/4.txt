"Assignments of Insurances" means the first priority assignment by way of security from the Issuer in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Bondholders) of all its rights to and title and interest under, whether present or future, all insurances in respect of the Rig as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Assignments of Management Agreements" means the first priority assignment by way of security from the Issuer in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Bondholders) of all its rights to and title and interest under, whether present or future, any Management Agreement as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents, provided such assignment is permitted pursuant to the terms of the relevant Management Agreement (it being understood that the Issuer shall use its reasonable endeavours to agree to Management Agreements that allows such assignment) and applicable law, which assignment shall contain direct cure and stepin rights and termination rights for the Bond Trustee in case of default.
"Assignments of Subordinated Loans" means the first priority assignment by way of security from the Parent in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Bondholders) of all its rights to and title and interest under, whether present or future, any Subordinated Loans as security for the Issuer's obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Attachment" means each of the attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Defeasance" shall have the meaning given to it in Clause 18.2.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachment to it, each as amended from time to time.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Depository, from time to time.
