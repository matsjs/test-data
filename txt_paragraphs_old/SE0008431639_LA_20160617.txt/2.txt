"Accounting Principles" means the international financial reporting standards (IFRS) within the meaning of Regulation 1606/2002/EC (or as otherwise adopted or amended from time to time).
"Adjusted Profits Before Taxes" means the consolidated profit before taxes from ordinary activities according to the latest Financial Report of the Group, adjusted for:
(a) depreciations;
(b) impairments;
(c) expenses for property sales;
(d) Total Interest Expenses;
(e) changes in the value of Properties;
(f) exchange rate differences (if any) in operating income; and
(g) change in value of any derivative instruments,
