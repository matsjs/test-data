The Issuer is only obliged to inform the Agent according to Clause 13.4 if informing the
Agent would not conflict with any statute or the Issuer's registration contract with Nasdaq
Stockholm (or any other Regulated Market, as applicable). If such a conflict would exist

Nasdaq Stockholm (or any other Regulated Market, as applicable) or undertake other

order to be able to timely inform the Agent according to Clause 13.4.
