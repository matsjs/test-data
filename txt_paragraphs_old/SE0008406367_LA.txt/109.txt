24.3
The provisions of Clause 24.1 shall not in any way limit an individual Holder's right to
claim and enforce payments which are due to it under Clause 11.4 (Mandatory repurchase
due to a Change of Control Event or a De-listing Event (put option)) or other payments
which are due by the Issuer to some but not all Holders.
