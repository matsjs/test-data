The Issuer shall ensure that the Security Documents and all documents relating thereto are
duly executed in favour of the Trustee and the Holders (as represented by the Trustee) and
that such documents are legally valid, perfected, enforceable and in full force and effect
according to their terms. The Issuer shall execute and/or procure the execution of such

Trustee to at all times maintain the security position envisaged hereunder.
