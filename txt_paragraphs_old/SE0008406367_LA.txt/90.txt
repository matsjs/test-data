If the Trustee, in breach of these Terms and Conditions, has not convened a Holders'
Person may convene the Holders' Meeting itself. If the requesting Person is a Holder, the

from the register kept by the CSD and, if no Person to open the Holders' Meeting has been
appointed by the Trustee, the meeting shall be opened by a Person appointed by the

18.6
At a Holders' Meeting, the Issuer, the Holders (or the Holders' representatives/proxies) and
the Trustee may attend along with each of their representatives, counsels and assistants.
