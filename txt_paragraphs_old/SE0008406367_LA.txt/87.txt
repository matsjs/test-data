HOLDERS' MEETING
The Trustee shall convene a Holders' Meeting by sending a notice thereof to each Holder no
later than five (5) Business Days after receipt of a request from the Issuer or the Holder(s)
(or such later date as may be necessary for technical or administrative reasons). If the
Holder's Meeting has been requested by the Holder(s), the Trustee shall send a copy of the
notice to the Issuer.
