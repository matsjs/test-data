11.5 Irregular payments
11.5.1 In case of irregular payments, the Bond Trustee may instruct (at no cost or additional liability to the Issuer unless (i) such irregular payments are at the request of the Issuer or any Obligor or (ii) a Default is pending) the Issuer/any Obligor or Bondholders of other payment mechanisms than described in Clause 11.1 (Payment mechanics) or 11.2 (Currency) above. The Bond Trustee may also obtain payment information regarding Bondholders' accounts from the Securities Register or Account Managers.
