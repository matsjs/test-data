17.3.1 Subject to the prior written consent of the Issuer, change of Bond Trustee shall be carried out pursuant to the procedures set forth in Clause 16 (Bondholders' meeting). 17.3.3 The Bond Trustee undertakes to co-operate so that the new bond trustee receives without undue delay following the Bondholders' Meeting the documentation and information necessary to perform the functions as set forth under the terms of this Bond Agreement.

Each of the Bondholders appoints the Security Agent to act as its attorney in fact and agent to execute an appointment letter appointing the Bond Trustee or any other Security Agent appoint in accordance with this Clause 17.4. to act as security trustee on behalf of the Bondholders.
