18.3 Limitation of claims
18.3.1 All claims under the Bonds and tliis Bond Agreement for payment, including interest and principal, shall be subject to the time-bar provisions of the Norwegian Limitation Act of May 18,1979 No. 18.
18.4 Access to information
18.4.1 The Bond Agreement is available to anyone and copies may be obtained from the Bond Tnistee or the Issuer. The Issuer shall ensure that the Bond Agreement is available in copy form to the general public until all the Bonds have been fully discharged.
