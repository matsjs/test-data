18.1 The community of Bondholders
(a)
the Bondholders are bound by the terms of this Bond Agreement;
(b)
the Bond Tmstee has power and authority to act on behalf of the Bondholders;
(c)
the Bond Tmstee has, in order to administrate the terms of this Bond Agreement,
access to the Securities Register to review ownership of Bonds registered in the
Securities Register; and
