Force Majure and Limitation of Liability
24.1
Neither the Agent nor the Issuing Agent shall be held responsible for any damage
arising out of any legal enactment, or any measure taken by a public authority, or war,
strike, lockout, boycott, blockade or any other similar circumstance (a "Force Majeure
Event"). The reservation in respect of strikes, lockouts, boycotts and blockades applies
even if the Agent or the Issuing Agent itself takes such measures, or is subject to such
measures.
