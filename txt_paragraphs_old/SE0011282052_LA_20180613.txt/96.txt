21.3
The provisions of Clause 21.1 shall not in any way limit an individual Noteholder's
right to claim and enforce payments which are due to it under Clause 9.5 (Mandatory
repurchase due to a Change o f Control Event or a Listing Failure) or other payments
which are due by the Issuer to some but not all Noteholders.
