13.15
Holding company
The Issuer shall not trade, carry on any business, incur any liabilities or own any material assets, except for (i) the provision of administrative services to the Group Companies of a type customarily provided by a holding company (excluding any treasury services), (ii) the taking of any administrative actions necessary to maintain its existence; (iii) any liabilities under the Finance Documents, (iv) ownership of the shares in the Company, (v) any liabilities under any Subordinated Loan, (vi) ownership of credit balances in any bank accounts; and (vii) ownership of cash and cash equivalent investment.
13.16
Subordinated Loans
The Issuer shall (i) not make any payment or discharge of any principal or interest under any Subordinated Loan, (ii) not propose or consent to any amendment of terms of any Subordinated Loan, and (iii) ensure that any Subordinated Loan remains fully subordinated to the Bonds, provided, however, that the Issuer may convert a Subordinated Loan (in part or in full) owed to the Company into shares as long as the new shares immediately upon their issue become subject to the Share Pledge and may service a Subordinated Loan to the Majority Shareholder in accordance with paragraph (a) (Distributions) above.
