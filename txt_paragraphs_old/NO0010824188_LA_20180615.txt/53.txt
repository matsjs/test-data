Issuer Subscribed Bonds
(a) Immediately after the sale by the Issuer of any Issuer Subscribed Bonds, the Issuer shall deposit into the Debt Service Retention Account an amount equal to 11.25 per cent, of the product of (A) the Nominal Amount of the Issuer Subscribed Bonds that are subject to the sale; and (B) the Top-Up Fraction as at the date of the deposit; and
(b) immediately after the receipt by the Issuer of any interest on or the Nominal Amount of any Issuer Subscribed Bonds, the Issuer shall transfer the full amount of such interest or principal to the Debt Service Retention Account.
13.18
Insurances
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that each other Group Company will, maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies, funds or underwriters, or otherwise receive the benefit of adequate insurance or captive arrangements with respect to its assets, equipment and business against such liabilities, casualties and contingencies and of such types and in such amounts as would normally be maintained by owners and/or operators owning similar assets to those owned by the relevant Group Company, in accordance with sound industry practice.
