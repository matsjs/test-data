(a) the present value on the Call Option Repayment Date of 103,25 per cent, of the Nominal Amount as if such payment had taken place on the First Call Date; and

where the present value shall be calculated by using a discount rate of 1.7 per cent., and where the interest rate applied for the remaining interest payments shall be 7.5 per cent.
