1.
INTERPRETATION
1.1 Definitions The following terms will have the following meanings:
"Additional Bonds" means Bonds issued under a Tap Issue.
"Adjusted Book Equity" means the Issuer's Book Equity adjusted for values of other assets (e.g. data centres, fibre assets). The value of other assets shall be determined by valuations obtained from an independent and well reputed valuator appointed by the Issuer and approved by the Bond Trustee.
