Continuity and further assurance
Continuing obligations The provisions of the Finance Documents shall, save as amended by this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement, continue in full force and effect.
No repetition of activities Save as otherwise expressly stated in this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement, no conditions precedent under the Bond Agreement already satisfied by the Parent or the Issuer in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Original Bond Agreement before the Effective Date shall be repeated by the Parent or the Issuer on the Effective Date.
Further assurance Each of the Parent and the Issuer shall, at the request of the Bond Trustee and at its own expense, do all such acts and things necessary or desirable to give effect to the amendments effected or to be effected pursuant to this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement.
