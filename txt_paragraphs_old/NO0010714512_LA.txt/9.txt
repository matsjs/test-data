No repetition of activities Save as otherwise expressly stated in this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement, no conditions precedent under the Bond Agreement already satisfied by the Parent or the Issuer in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Original Bond Agreement before the Effective Date shall be repeated by the Parent or the Issuer on the Effective Date.
Finance Document
This Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement shall constitute a Finance Document for the purpose of the Amended and Restated Bond Agreement.
Fees and expenses
The Parent shall promptly on demand pay to the Bond Trustee the amount of all costs and expenses (including legal fees) incurred by the Bond Trustee in connection with the negotiations, preparation and execution of this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement and any other documents or matters related to or directly arising out of or in connection with this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement.
Dispute resolution and legal venue
This Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement and all disputes arising out of, or in connection herewith between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders, the Parent and the Issuer, shall be governed by Norwegian law.
All disputes arising out of, or in connection with this Second Amendment and Restatement Agreement between the Bond Trustee, the Bondholders, the Parent and/or the Issuer, shall be resolved as set out in Clause 19.7 in the Amended and Restated Bond Agreement.
