13.10
Dividends
(a) The Issuer may not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make loans or other distributions ("Distributions") to its shareholders including servicing or repaying any Shareholder Loans or other Subordinated Loans (each a "Dividend Payment").
(b) Notwithstanding paragraph (a) above, the Issuer may repurchase its own shares to cover its potential obligations under any bonus share program for its employees and board members, provided that no Event of Default is outstanding or would result from such Distribution.
13.11
Negative pledge
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its respective assets (whether present or future).
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Security.
