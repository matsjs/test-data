11.8
Dealings with related parties
11.8.1
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that its Subsidiaries, conduct all dealings with the direct and indirect shareholders of the Group Companies (excluding other Group Companies) and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders at arm's length terms.
11.9
Admission to trading
11.9.1
The Issuer shall use its best efforts to ensure that the loan constituted by these Terms and Conditions and evidenced by the Bonds is admitted to trading on the Regulated Market of NASDAQ OMX Stockholm within 30 days after the First Issue Date, and that it remains admitted. Upon the issuance of any Subsequent Bonds, the Issuer shall promptly, but not later than 60 days after the relevant issue date, procure that the number of Bonds admitted to trading is increased accordingly.
