9.1
Redemption at maturity
The Issuer shall redeem all, but not some only, of the outstanding Notes in full on the Final Maturity Date (or, to the extent such day is not a Business Day and if permitted under the CSD's applicable regulations, on the Business Day following from an application of the Business Day Convention, and otherwise on the first following Business Day) with an amount per Note equal to the Nominal Amount together with accrued but unpaid Interest.
9.2
Purchase of Notes by the Issuer and any other Group Company
The Issuer and any other Group Company may, subject to applicable law, at any time and at any price purchase Notes on the market or in any other way. Notes held by the Issuer or any other Group Company may at their discretion be retained or sold and the Issuer may cancel Notes held by it.
