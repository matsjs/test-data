Definitions
In these terms and conditions (these "Terms and Conditions"):

"Accounting Principles" means the international financial reporting standards (IFRS) within the meaning of Regulation 1606/2002/EC (or as otherwise adopted or amended from time to time) as applied by the Issuer in preparing its financial statements.
