16.2 Leverage Ratio The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Leverage Ratio of not more than 4.75.
16.3 Minimum Adjusted Book Equity The Issuer shall at all times on a consolidated basis maintain a Adjusted Book Equity of minimum NOK 150,000,000.
16.4 Calculation and compliance For the purpose of calculating the Leverage Ratio, (i) the figures for EBITDA on a 12 months rolling basis set out in the latest delivered quarterly report and the 3 quarterly reports delivered prior thereto shall be used, but adjusted so that entities acquired or disposed of after the end of the measurement period but before the relevant testing date will be included or excluded (as applicable) for the entire measurement period, and (ii) the amount of Net Interest Bearing Debt shall be measured on the relevant testing date but include the new financial indebtedness.
