14.16 Dividend restrictions The Issuer shall not declare or make any Distribution, save for any Permitted Distribution, in the excess of 50% o f Issuer's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year, provided always that immediately after such Distribution has taken place:
the Leverage Ratio for the Group is less than 3.75; and
(b)
the Adjusted Book Equity is minimum NOK 170,000,000 plus any new
Shareholder Loan (No reduction of such amount shall apply in the event a
Shareholder Loan is repaid and refinanced within four weeks o f repayment).
Notwithstanding anything to the contrary herein, in the event that any bond or guarantee as described under Permitted Financial Support paragraph (i) has been granted, the Issuer shall not declare or make any Distribution during the remaining term of the Bonds.
14.17 Subsidiaries' distribution The Issuer shall not permit any Subsidiaries to create or permit to exist any contractual obligation (or encumbrance) restricting the right o f any Subsidiary to (i) pay dividends or make other distributions to its shareholders, (ii) service any financial indebtedness to the Issuer, or (iii) make any loans to the Issuer, if the creation of such contractual obligation is reasonably likely to prevent the Issuer from complying with its payment obligations under this Bond Agreement.
