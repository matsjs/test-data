14.9 Shareholder Loans The Issuer shall upon entering into a Shareholder Loan immediately deliver to the Bond Trustee a copy of relevant documentation evidencing the terms of such Shareholder Loan.
14.10 Arm's length transactions The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, engage directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the Ordinary Course of Business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
14.11 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
14.12 Compliance with laws The Issuer shall, and shall ensure that all other Group Companies shall, carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
