10.1 Maturity The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
(a)
The Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue (all or nothing) as follows (Call Option):
(i) at any time from and including Issue Date to, but not included, the Interest Payment Date in April 2014 at Make Whole Price,
(ii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2014 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2015 at a price equal to 105.00 % o f par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(iii) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2015 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2016 at a price equal to 104.00 % of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
(iv) at any time from and including the Interest Payment Date in April 2016 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in April 2017 at a price equal to 102.50 % of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
