18. EVENTS OF DEFAULT
The Bond Trustee may declare the Bonds to be in default upon occurrence of any o f the following events:
18.1 Non-payment Any Obligor fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, it is likely that such payment will be made in full within five Business Days following the original due date.
18.2 Breach o f other obligations Any Obligor does not comply with any provision pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any other Finance Document, unless, in the opinion o f the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable o f being remedied and is remedied within ten Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
