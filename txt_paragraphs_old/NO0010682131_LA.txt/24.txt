"IFRS" means International Financial Reporting Standards and refers to the international accounting standard within the meaning of IAS Regulation 1606/2002 to the extent applicable to the relevant financial statements.
"Intercom pany Loan Pledge" means the first priority pledge in any Intercompany Loans, save for Intercompany Loans arising pursuant to the Group's cash pooling system(s).
"Interest Paym ent Date" means 10 January, 10 April, 10 July and 10 October each year and the Maturity Date. Any adjustment will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Interim Accounts" means the unaudited unconsolidated and consolidated quarterly financial statements o f the Issuer for any quarter ending on a Quarter Date, drawn up according to IFRS in due course to be able to list the Bonds on the Oslo Stock Exchange within 12 months from the Issue Date.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number o f the Bond Issue.
"Issue Date" means 7 June 2013.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds owned by the Issuer, any person or persons who has Decisive Influence over the Issuer, or any person or persons over whom the Issuer has Decisive Influence.
"Leverage Ratio" shall have the meaning given to such term in Clause 16.1.
"Make Whole Price" means the discounted present value of the Bonds to bid NOK Swap plus 50 basis points (for the avoidance of doubt; the discount rate used to find the net present value shall be Bid NOK Swap for the intended duration of the Bond at the time of call date plus 50 basis points), but in no event less than 100 % o f their principal amount together with any accrued interest.
