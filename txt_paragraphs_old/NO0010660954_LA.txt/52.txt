13.4 Corporate and operational matters (a) Intra-group transactions
(b)
Transactions -with shareholders, directors and affiliated companies
The Issuer shall cause all transactions between any Group Company or JV Company and (i) any shareholder thereof not part of the Group, (ii) any director or senior member of management in any Group Company or JV Company, (iii) any company in which any Group Company or JV Company holds more than 10 per cent of the shares, or (iv) any person controlled by or affiliated with any of the foregoing, to be entered on commercial terms, not less favourable to the Group Company or JV Company than would have prevailed in arm's length transaction with a third party.
All such transactions shall comply with all applicable provisions of applicable corporate law applicable to such transactions, including, in respect of Norwegian companies, Section 3-8 of the Private and Public Limited Companies Act 1997.
