22.2
If a limitation period is duly interrupted in accordance with the Swedish Act on Limitations
[preskriptionsiag (1981:130)), a new limitation period of ten (10) years with respect to the
right to receive repayment of the principal of the Bonds, and of three (3) years with
respect to receive payment of interest (excluding capitalised interest) will commence, in
both cases calculated from the date of interruption of the limitation period, as such date
is determined pursuant to the provisions of the Swedish Act on Limitations.
