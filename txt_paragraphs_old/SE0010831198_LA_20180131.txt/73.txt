21.3
The provisions of Clause 21.1 shall not in any way limit an individual Bondholder s right to
claim and enforce payments which are due to it under Clause 9.5 (Mandatory repurchase
due to a Change of Control Event, Delisting Event or a Listing Failure) or other payments
which are due by the Issuer to some but not all Bondholders.
