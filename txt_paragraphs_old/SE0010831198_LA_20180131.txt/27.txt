If, due to any obstacle for the CSD, the Issuer cannot make a payment or repayment, such

7.4
If payment or repayment is made in accordance with this Clause 7, the Issuer and the CSD
shall be deemed to have fulfilled their obligation to pay, irrespective of whether such
payment was made to a person not entitled to receive such amount, unless the Issuer or
the CSD (as applicable) was aware of that the payment was being made to a person not
entitled to receive such amount.
