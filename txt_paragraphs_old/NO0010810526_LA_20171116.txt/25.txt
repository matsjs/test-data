"Security" means a mortgage, charge, pledge, lien, security assignment or other security interest securing any obligation of any person or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security Agent Agreement" means any agreement whereby the Security Agent is appointed to act as such in the interest of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of itself and the Bondholders).
"Shareholder Loans" means any loan granted (or to be granted) to the Issuer by any of the Issuer's direct or indirect shareholders, and which loans shall be pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and have terms (acceptable to the Bond Trustee) ensuring that such loans (a) are fully subordinated to the Group's obligations under the Finance Documents, (b) are paying no cash interest, (b) does not require repayment of principal prior to or on the Maturity Date and (c) does not have any acceleration rights (before all present and future obligations and liabilities under the Finance Documents having been discharged in full).
"SPA" means the share purchase agreement between the Issuer and the current shareholders of the Property Owning Company regulating the acquisition of all the shares of the Property Owning Company by the Issuer.
