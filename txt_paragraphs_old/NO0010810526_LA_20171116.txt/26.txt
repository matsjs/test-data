"Summons" means the call for a Bondholders' Meeting or a Written Resolution as the case may be.
"Total Net Debt" means the Outstanding Amount (for the avoidance of doubt, excluding any Bonds owned by the Issuer) less the Liquidity of the Group.
"Transaction Security" means the Security created or expressed to be created in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Secured Parties) pursuant to the Transaction Security Documents.
"Transaction Security Documents" means, collectively, the Escrow Account Pledge and all of the documents which shall be executed or delivered pursuant to Clause 2.5 (Transaction Security) expressed to create any Security by the relevant grantor thereof in respect ofthe Issuer's obligations under any of the Finance Documents.
"Valuation Report" means:
(a) a report prepared semi-annually (as of 31 December and 30 June each year) (the first such Valuation Report to be dated 30 June 2018) with respect to the Property by a reputable independent property advisor acceptable to the Bond Trustee; or
(b) a report prepared by a reputable independent property advisor, upon the request of the Bond Trustee in accordance with Clause 12.4 (Information: Miscellaneous) paragraph (c),
stipulating the market value at such time of the Property.
"Voluntary Partial Redemption" means a voluntary partial redemption as set out in Clause 10.8 (Voluntary Partial Redemption).
