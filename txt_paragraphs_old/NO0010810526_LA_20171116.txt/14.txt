"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bonds.
"Issuer" means the company designated as such in the preamble to these Bond Terms.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds which are owned by the Issuer or any Affiliate of the Issuer.
"Liquidity" means the consolidated free and unrestricted cash and bank deposits of the Group (including any amount standing on the Escrow Account).
"Longstop Date" means the date falling sixty (60) Business Days after the Issue Date.
"Loss Event" means an event which shall be deemed to have occurred if:
(a) there is a total or constructive loss of the Property;
(b) the Property (or any part thereof) is damaged to the extent that commercial letting of the Property will not be feasible for a period exceeding 6 months; or
(c) if damages (and any related repair costs) to the Property exceed 20 per cent. of the Property's Market Value.
