12.4 Permitted Distribution The Issuer shall prior to completing a Permitted Distribution submit to the Bond Trustee a Compliance Certificate, and which shall include (in reasonable detail) calculations and figures evidencing that the Issuer will be in compliance with Clause 13.17 (Financial undertakings) following such Permitted Distribution.
(a) promptly inform the Bond Trustee in writing of any Event of Default or any event or circumstance which the Issuer understands or could reasonably be expected to understand may lead to an Event of Default and the steps, if any, being taken to remedy it;
(b) at the request of the Bond Trustee, report the balance of the Issuer's Bonds (to the best of its knowledge, having made due and appropriate enquiries);
(c) upon the request of the Bond Trustee (at the cost of the Issuer) provide an additional Valuation Report, provided however that the Bond Trustee shall only have the right to request such additional Valuation Report if (i) an Event of Default has occurred or (ii) the Bond Trustee (in its opinion) has strong reason to believe that the latest Valuation Report is inaccurate or misleading in nature;
(d) send the Bond Trustee copies of any statutory notifications of the Issuer, including but not limited to in connection with mergers, de-mergers and reduction of the Issuer's share capital or equity;
(e) if the Bonds are listed on an Exchange, send a copy to the Bond Trustee of its notices to the Exchange;
(f) if the Issuer and/or the Bonds are rated, inform the Bond Trustee of its and/or the rating of the Bonds, and any changes to such rating;
(g) inform the Bond Trustee of changes in the registration of the Bonds in the CSD; and
(h) within a reasonable time, provide such information about the Issuer's and the Group's business, assets and financial condition as the Bond Trustee may reasonably request.
