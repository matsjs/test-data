"Business Day Convention" means that if the relevant Payment Date originally falls on a day that is not a Business Day, an adjustment of the Payment Date will be made so that relevant Payment Date will be the first following day that is a Business Day unless that day falls in the next calendar month, in which case that date will be the first preceding day that is a Business Day (Modified Following Business Day Convention).
"Charges over Inventory" means charges over inventory (No. pant i varelager) in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) made by each Norwegian Guarantor, ranking on first priority as security for the Obligors obligations under the Finance Documents.

"Charges over Machinery and Plant" means charges over machinery and plant (No. pant i driftstilbehør) in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) made by each Norwegian Guarantor, ranking on first priority as security for the Obligors obligations under the Finance Documents.
"Contract Backlog" means the Group's aggregate contracted revenues (to be calculated on consolidated bases on a 36-month forward rolling basis).
"Current Assets" means the aggregate book value of the Group's assets (on a consolidated basis) which are treated as current assets in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer) less the aggregate book value of any restricted cash (where restricted cash means cash which is pledged and/or blocked).
"Current Liabilities" means the aggregate book value of the Group's liabilities (on a consolidated basis) which are treated as current liabilities in accordance with GAAP (including IFRS if applied by the Issuer), excluding the current portion of long term debt.
"Current Ratio" means the ratio of Current Assets to Current Liabilities.
