The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Solør Bioenergi Holding AS Senior Secured Bond Issue 2012/2017".
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of legal costs, fees of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall be employed for refinancing of existing debt and for general corporate purposes.
