"Permitted Restructuring" means a restructuring of the Group by:

(c) merging the Swedish Subsidiaries into one or several Subsidiaries, provided that the surviving Subsidiaries remain wholly owned (directly or indirectly) by the Issuer or the Swedish Holdco as the case may be; and/or
(d) merging the Norwegian Subsidiaries into one or several Subsidiaries, provided that the surviving Subsidiaries remain wholly owned (directly or indirectly) by the Issuer ;
provided always that any such restructuring shall be subject to satisfactory guarantees and security from the Group Companies and that the Security and Guarantees contemplated in this Bond Agreement is not in any way negatively affected.
