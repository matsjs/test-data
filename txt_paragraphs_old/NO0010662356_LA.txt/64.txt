(d) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall ensure that no Guarantor shall enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
(e) Corporate status
The Issuer shall ensure that no Guarantor shall change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
(f) Compliance with laws
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall carry on its business in accordance with acknowledged, careful and sound practices in all material aspects and comply in all material respects with all laws and regulations it or they may be subject to from time to time.
