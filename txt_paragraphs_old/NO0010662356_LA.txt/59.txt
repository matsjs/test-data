(iv) Financial Support granted by the Issuer and the Primary Holdco in connection with Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no Group Company shall, create, permit to subsist or allow to exist any Security over any of its present or future respective assets (including shares in Subsidiaries) or its revenues, other than Security granted to secure any of the following:
(i) this Bond Issue;
(ii) the Permitted Financial Indebtedness; and
(iii) any lien arising by operation of law.
