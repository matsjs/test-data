The Loan Trustee has through the Bondholders subscription been granted authority to act on behalf o f the Bondholders to the extent provided for in the Loan Agreement.
1 Definitions
Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Banking Day:
a day when the Norwegian Central Bank's Settlement System is open and when Norwegian banks can settle foreign currency transactions.
Bondholders:
the holders of the Bonds.
Bonds:
bonds in the Loan, as described in Clause 2.1.
