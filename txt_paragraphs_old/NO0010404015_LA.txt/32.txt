10.3 If the Vessel is sold or disposed of or terminated (in respect of the Construction Contracts) at any time from and including Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date, the Borrower shall redeem the entire Loan as following:
(i) if occurring anytime from and including the Disbursement Date to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2008, at 104 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount; and
(ii) if occurring anytime from and including the Interest Payment Date in December 2008 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in December 2009, at 102.5 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount
(iii) if occurring anytime from and including the Interest Payment Date in December 2009 to, but not including, the Maturity Date, at 101.5 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
10.4 If a Change of Control Event has occurred, each Bondholder shall at any time in the Put Period have the option to (at each Bondholder's discretion) require early redemption of its Bonds (put option) at the price of 100% per cent of par value.
