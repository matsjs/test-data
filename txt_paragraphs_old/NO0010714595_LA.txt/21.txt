"Maturity Date" means 10 July 2017. Any adjustments will be made according to the Business Day Convention.
"Net Finance Charges" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 14.1.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 16.1.
"New Debt" means any new Financial Indebtedness (or commitment under Financial Indebtedness) incurred by the Issuer in accordance with paragraph (h) and (i) of the definition of "Permitted Financial Indebtedness".
"New Lenders" means the finance parties in respect of any New Debt (except to the extent that the New Debt is incurred as a result of a Tap Issue) and which is or becomes a party to the Intercreditor Agreement as a new lender.
"New Security" means Security in favour of the Security Agent (on behalf of the Secured Parties) and any other finance parties according to the terms and conditions of the Intercreditor Agreement to be granted over any new assets having been acquired with proceeds received from a Restricted Disposal.
"NIBOR" means the interest rate fixed for a defined period on Oslo Børs' webpage at approximately 12.15 Oslo time or, on days on which Oslo Børs has shorter opening hours (New Year's Eve and the Wednesday before Maundy Thursday), the data published at approximately 10.15 a.m. shall be used. In the event that such page is not available, has been removed or changed such that the quoted interest rate no longer represents, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, a correct expression of the relevant interest rate, an alternative page or other electronic source which in the opinion of the Bond Trustee and the Issuer gives the same interest rate shall be used. If this is not possible, the Bond Trustee shall calculate the relevant interest rate based on comparable quotes from major banks in Oslo. If any such rate is below zero, NIBOR will be deemed to be zero.
"NOK" means Norwegian kroner, being the lawful currency of Norway.
"Obligor" means the Issuer and the Guarantors.
