16.5 Insolvency
Any Obligor is unable or admits inability to pay its debts as they fall due, suspends
making payments on any of its debts or, by reasons of actual or anticipated financial
difficulties, commence negotiation with one or more of its creditors with a view to
rescheduling any of its indebtedness;
(b) the value of the assets of any Obligor is less than its liabilities (taking into account contingent and prospective liabilities); or
(c) a moratorium is declared in respect of any indebtedness of any Obligor.
