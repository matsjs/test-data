"Finance Charges" means, for the Relevant Period, the aggregate amount of the cash interest expenses, (excluding fees and expenses in respect of the issuance of the Initial Loan Amount, any Tap Issue, the Revolving Credit Facility and any New Debt), in respect of Financial Indebtedness (calculated on a consolidated basis), and excluding payment-in-kind interest capitalized on Shareholder Loans or other subordinated indebtedness, or gains or losses (whether realized or unrealized) on any derivative instrument (including but not limited to in respect of any foreign exchange fluctuations), pension liabilities, any interest element related to a lease or hire purchase contract.
"Interest Bearing Debt" means, at the date of calculation (on a consolidated basis for the Group), the aggregate outstanding interest bearing Permitted Financial Indebtedness (excluding the items in sub-paragraphs (b) - (g) and (j) - (I) of the definition of Permitted Financial Indebtedness, any leases, any guarantees entered into in the ordinary course of business, any Permitted Hedging Obligations, any Shareholder Loans, any Intercompany Loan and any other Financial Indebtedness which is non-interest bearing).
"Interest Coverage Ratio" means the ratio of EBITDA to Net Finance Charges.
"Net Finance Charges" means, for the Relevant Period, the Finance Charges according to the latest Financial Report(s), after deducting any interest income relating to Cash.
"Net Interest Bearing Debt" means Interest Bearing Debt less Cash.
"Relevant Period" means each period of twelve (12) consecutive calendar months.
