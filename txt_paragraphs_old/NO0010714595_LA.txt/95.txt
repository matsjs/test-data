13.8 Mergers The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of any Group Company with any other companies or entities, if such transaction would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.9 Nomination of Material Group Companies The Issuer shall nominate as Material Group Companies such Group Companies as are necessary to ensure that the Issuer and the Material Group Companies in aggregate always account for at least 80% of the total EBITDA of the Group on a consolidated basis, based on the preceding last twelve months. The Issuer shall ensure that Group Companies whose assets or EBITDA
______constitutes more than 15% of the Group's assets or EBITDA (on a consolidated basis) as applicable, shall provide Pledge over Machinery and Plant and Pledge over Trade Receivables (or similar in applicable jurisdiction) and accede to the Intercreditor Agreement, provided that the parties shall, when deciding whether or not such Group Company shall grant security, give due regard to any legal and practical difficulties (including registration costs) in obtaining such security. For the avoidance of doubt, Swedish and Danish Group Companies shall not be obliged to provide Pledge over Machinery and Plant and Pledge over Trade Receivable due to the registration costs. It is understood that applicable legal limitations under mandatory provisions of law may apply to such Security.
