12. ISSUER'S ACQUISITION OF BONDS
The Issuer has the right to acquire and own Bonds (Issuer's Bonds), provided that Bonds held by (i) the Issuer, (ii) Norvestor Equity funds (iii) an entity with Decisive Influence over the Issuer, (iv) an entity over which the Issuer has Decisive Influence, or (v) any other Group Company, may not be voted for. The Issuer's holding of Bonds may at the Issuer's discretion be retained by the Issuer, sold or discharged.
