(b) The Issuer may after the Settlement Date, provided that the Incurrence Test is met, at one or more occasions issue additional Bonds under the Bond Issue (each such issue, a "Tap Issue"), until the earlier of (i) the outstanding loan amount equals the Maximum Loan Amount (less the nominal value of any previously redeemed Bonds, i.e. additional Bonds in the amount of up to NOK 350,000,000), and (ii) five Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
(c)
The additional Bonds issued in a Tap Issue shall be subject to the terms and conditions
of the Finance Documents. All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all
respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for
later Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will in the event of a Tap Issue make an addendum
to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
