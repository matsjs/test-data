"Subsidiary" means any company over which another Person has Decisive Influence.
"Tap Issue" shall have the meaning set out in Clause 2.3.
"US Securities Act" means the U.S. Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
"Voting Bonds" means the Outstanding Bonds less the Issuer's Bonds.
