13.10 Acquisitions The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will, acquire any company, shares, securities, business or undertaking (or any interest in any of them), unless the transaction is carried out at fair market value and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and provided that it does not have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.11 Disposals The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company will sell or otherwise dispose of assets or operations in any Group Company, outside the ordinary course of business, to any Person not being the Issuer or any of its wholly-owned Subsidiaries or where the Issuer (directly or indirectly) maintains the same pro rata ownership share in that Group Company before or after such disposal, (a "Restricted Disposal"), unless such Restricted Disposal is carried out at fair market value and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction.
