A Noteholder may issue one or several powers of attorney to third parties or provide
proof of authorisation to represent it in relation to some or all of the Notes held by it.
Any such representative may act independently under the Finance Documents in
relation to the Notes for which such representative is entitled to represent the
Noteholder and may further delegate its right to represent the Noteholder by way of a
further power of attorney.
