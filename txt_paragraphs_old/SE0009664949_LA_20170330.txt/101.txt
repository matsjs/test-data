No Direct Actions by Noteholders
25.1
A Noteholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer to enforce or
recover any amount due or owing to it pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to
initiate, support or procure the winding-up, dissolution, liquidation, company
reorganisation (företagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy {konkurs) (or its equivalent in
any other jurisdiction) of the Issuer in relation to any of the liabilities of the Issuer
under the Finance Documents. Such steps may only be taken by the Agent.
