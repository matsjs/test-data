Use of proceeds
The Issuer shall use the proceeds from the issue of the Initial Notes, less the costs and
expenses incurred by the Issuer in connection with the issue of the Initial Notes, for
refinancing of existing debt (including the Existing Market Loan), debt service and
general corporate purposes of the Group.
