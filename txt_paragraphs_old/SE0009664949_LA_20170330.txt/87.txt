Amendments and Waivers
The Issuer and the Agent (acting on behalf of the Noteholders) may agree to amend
the Finance Documents or waive any provision in a Finance Document, provided that:
(a) it is obvious to the Agent that such amendment or waiver is not detrimental to the interest of the Noteholders as a group;
(b) it is made solely for the purpose of rectifying obvious errors and mistakes;
(c) such amendment or waiver is required by applicable law, a court ruling or a decision by a relevant authority; or
(d) such amendment or waiver has been duly approved by the Noteholders in accordance with Clause 18 (Decisions by Noteholders).
