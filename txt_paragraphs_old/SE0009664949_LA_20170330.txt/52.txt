Restrictions regarding pledge of assets
The Issuer shall not create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets for any Market Loan raised by the Issuer, other than (i) the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement and (ii) until the Existing Market Loan is redeemed in accordance with Clause 12.7 (Voluntary Early Redemption), the Existing Pledge.
