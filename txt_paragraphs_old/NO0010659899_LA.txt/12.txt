"Claims under SPA" means any claims (whether existing, future and/or contingent) held by the Issuer against the sellers in relation to the sellers' non-contractual fulfilment of the share purchase agreement dated 12 July 2012 relating to the Issuer's acquisition of the Target, including without limitation claims for damages and guarantee/warranty claims.
"Clean-up Period" means:
(a) in respect of the Acquisition, the period from the date of the Bond Agreement to 9 November 2012; and
(b) in respect of any new acquisition of any company, business, undertaking or asset (other than the Acquisition), the period of three months from the completion date for such acquisition.
"Compliance Certificate" means a certificate substantially in the form set out in Schedule 3.
"Costs" means all costs, expenses, disbursements, payments, charges, losses, demands, claims, liabilities, penalties, fines, damages, judgments, orders, sanctions, fees (including travel expenses, VAT, court fees and legal fees) and any other outgoings of whatever nature.
"Escrow Account" means an escrow account denominated in NOK in the name of the Issuer (with a bank acceptable to the Bond Trustee), pledged on first priority in favour of the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Bondholders) and blocked in such way that no withdrawals can be made therefrom without the Bond Trustee's written consent, and where the bank operating the account has waived any set-off rights.
"Escrow Account Pledge" means first priority pledge over the Issuer's claim against the relevant account bank for the amount from time to time standing to the credit of the Issuer in the Escrow Account.
