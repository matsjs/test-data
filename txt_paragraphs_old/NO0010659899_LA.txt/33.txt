(a) the Bond Agreement duly executed by the Issuer and each Original Guarantor;
(b) confirmation that no potential or actual Event of Default exists under the Bond Agreement;
(c) the agreement set forth in Clause 17.2, duly executed;
(d) the Intercreditor Agreement duly executed by all parties thereto;
