2.3 The Bonds (a) The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the amount of NOK 380,000,000 (Norwegian kroner three hundred and eighty million), with denominations o f NOK 1,000,000.
(b) The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
(c) The Bond Issue will be described as "Helly Hansen Group Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2012/2017".
(d) The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 065989.9.
(e) The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.4 Purpose and utilisation The proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of fees, legal costs of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and other Costs) shall be used to refinance the Bridge Facility in full and may be only be utilised in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Bond Agreement.
3. LISTING The Issuer shall apply for listing of the Bonds on Oslo ABM. If the Bonds are listed (whether on Oslo ABM or any other Exchange), the Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds remain listed until they have been discharged in full.
