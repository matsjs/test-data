17.4 Extraordinary expenses In addition to the fee due to the Bond Trustee pursuant to Clause 17.2 and normal expenses pursuant to Clauses 17.1 and 17.3, the Issuer shall, on demand, cover
extraordinary expenses incurred by the Bond Trustee in connection with the Bonds, as determined in a separate agreement between the Issuer and the Bond Trustee.
17.5 Withholding tax The Issuer is responsible for withholding any withholding tax imposed by applicable law on any payments to the Bondholders. If the Issuer is required by law to make a tax deduction or withholding from any payment under any Finance Document:
(a) the amount of the payment due from the Issuer shall be increased to such amount which is necessary to ensure that the Bondholders receive a net amount which is (after making the required tax deduction or withholding) equal to the payment which would have been due if no tax deduction or withholding had been required; and
(b) the Issuer shall at the request of the Bond Trustee deliver to the Bond Trustee evidence that the required Tax reduction or withholding has been made.
18. EVENTS OF DEFAULT The Bonds may be declared by the Bond Trustee to be in default upon occurrence of any of the following events:
18.1 Non-payment The Issuer fails to fulfil any payment obligation due under this Bond Agreement or any Finance Document when due, unless payment in full is made, within five Business Days following the original due date.
18.2 Breach of other obligations Any Obligor fails to duly perform any other covenant or obligation pursuant to this Bond Agreement or any of the Finance Documents, unless the failure is (i) capable of remedy and (ii) remedied within 15 Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
18.3 Cross default (a) Any Financial Indebtedness of the Group exceeding NOK 20,000,000 (or the equivalent thereof in other currencies) is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period.
