The interest is calculated on the basis of the actual number of elapsed calendar days from and including the Disbursement date to the following Interest Payment Date, and subsequently from each interest fixing date to the next or the maturity date. The number of days is divided by 360.
10.1 The Bonds shall be repaid as follows:
(i) with NOK 130,000,000 on the Interest Payment date in October 2010, (ii) with NOK 130,000,000 on the Interest Payment date in October 2011, (iii) with NOK 65,000,000 on the Interest Payment date in April 2012, (iv) Remaining Loan at Maturity Date.
Instalments shall be carried out by drawing of lots between the Bonds (by a full repayment of individual Bonds, allocated randomly between the Bonds).
10.2 The Borrower may redeem parts of the Loan or the entire Loan as follows (Call Option):
(i) anytime during the period ranging from and included the date 6 months after Acceptance Date to and included the date 12 months after Acceptance Date at 105.00% of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(ii) at the Interest Payment date in October 2010 at a price of 104.00 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount,
(iii) at the Interest Payment date in October 2011 at a price of 103.25 % of par plus accrued interests on redeemed amount.
Should the Borrower exercise its Call Option, the Loan Trustee and the Bondholders must be informed of this (the Bondholders in writing via VPS) no later than 30 - thirty - Banking Days before the date of redemption.
