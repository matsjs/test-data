2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds at a maximum amount of NOK 870,000,000 (Norwegian krone eighthundred and seventy million), herein referred to as "the Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu. The Loan will be described as "FRN Sevan Marine ASA Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2007/2012". The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 039164.2. The term of the Loan is from and including the Disbursement Date to the Maturity Date. 2.2 The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be used for (i) finance the construction of the FPSO included costs related thereto, and (ii), provided that the Acceptance Date has taken place, for general corporate purposes.
3 Listing
3.1 An application will be made for the Bonds to be listed on Oslo Børs. Delisting or change of listing may take place without a mandatory Bondholders' meeting and without a mandatory repurchase offer of the Loan to the Bondholders.
