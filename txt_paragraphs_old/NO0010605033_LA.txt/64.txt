The Issuer shall ensure that the Group on a consolidated basis maintains a Market Adjusted Equity Ratio of minimum as follow:
(i) from and including the Issue Date to, but not including, 30 June 2011; 25.00 %,

(b) Liquidity
From the earlier of (i) the release of the funds in the Escrow Account and (ii) delivery of the Vessel to the Owning Company, the Issuer shall (on a consolidated basis) maintain Cash and Cash Equivalent of no less than NOK 150,000,000.
