"Quarterly Financial Reports" means the unaudited consolidated management accounts of the Issuer as of each Quarter Date, such accounts to include a profit and loss account, balance sheet, cash flow statement and management commentary.
"Securities Register" means the securities register in which the Bond Issue is registered.
"Securities Register Act" means the Norwegian Act relating to Registration of Financial Instruments of 5 July 2002 No. 64.
"Security Agent" means the Bond Trustee, unless any other legal entity is appointed as security agent pursuant to Clause 17.4 (Appointment of Security Agent).
"Security Documents" means any document establishing, recording, confirming or preserving any Security Interest relating to any Finance Document.
