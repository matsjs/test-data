13.2 Other Obligations
The Issuer does not comply with any provision under the Finance Documents , in any other way than as set out in Clause 13.1 (Non-Payment), provided that the Agent has requested the Issuer in writing to remedy such failure and the Issuer has not remedied the failure within fifteen (15) Business Days from such request (if the failure or violation is not capable of being remedied, the Agent may declare the Notes payable without such prior written request).
13.3 Cross-Acceleration
Any Financial Indebtedness o f a Group Company is not paid when due as extended by any originally applicable grace period, or is declared to be due and payable prior to its specified maturity as a result of an event o f default (however described), provided that no Event o f Default will occur under this Clause 13.3 if the aggregate amount of Financial Indebtedness declared to be or otherwise becoming due and payable is less than EUR 1,000,000 and provided that it does not apply to any Financial Indebtedness owed to a Group Company.
