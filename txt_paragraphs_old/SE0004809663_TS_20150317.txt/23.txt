Each Subsequent Note issued in accordance with Clause 2.4 shall entitle its respective

2.6
The Notes constitute direct, unconditional, unsubordinated and unsecured obligations of
the Issuer and shall at all times rank pari passu and without any preference among them,
and at least pari passu with all other unsubordinated and unsecured obligations of the
Issuer other than those mandatorily preferred by law.
