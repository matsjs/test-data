17.3 Inspections and class records
It shall, on behalf of the Bond Trustee, appoint a an independent rig inspector
acceptable to the Bond Trustee, and shall procure that any charterers permit, the
Bond Trustee and/or the independent rig inspector appointed by it on behalf of
the Bond Trustee upon request to inspect the Unit, without interference of the
daily operations of the relevant Unit at the Issuer's cost.
(b)
It shall provide confirmation of class and instruct the classification society to
send to the Bond Trustee, upon its reasonable request, copies of all class records
