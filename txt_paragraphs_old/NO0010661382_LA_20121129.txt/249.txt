22.6 Notices, contact information
Written notices, warnings, summons etc to the Bondholders made by the Bond
Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Register with a copy to the Issuer and the
Exchange. Information to the Bondholders may also be published at Stamdata
only. Any such notice or communication shall be deemed to be given or made
as follows;
(i) if by letter via the Securities Register, when sent from the Securities Register; and
(ii) if by publication on Stamdata, when publicly available.
