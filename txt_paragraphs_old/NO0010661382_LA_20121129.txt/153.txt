16.4 Disposal of business The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall sell or otherwise dispose of all or a substantial part of the Group's assets, subsidiaries or operations to any person not being a member o f the Group, unless:
(a)
the transaction is carried out at fair market value, on terms and conditions
customary for such transactions; and
(b)
such transaction would not have a Material Adverse Effect.
Certain disposals of business/assets shall, however, be allowed subject to mandatory redemption or the Bondholders' put option as set out in Clause 10.
16.5 Amendment o f constitutional documents The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that none of its Subsidiaries shall, amend its constitutional documents in such a way that it would have a Material Adverse Effect.
