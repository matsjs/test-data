19.11 Acceleration
(a)
In the event that one or more o f the circumstances mentioned in Clauses 19.1 --
19.10 occurs and is continuing, the Bond Trustee can, in order to protect the
interests of the Bondholders, declare the Outstanding Bonds including accrued
interest, costs, and expenses to be in default and due for immediate payment.
