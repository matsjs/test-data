17.4 Rig operator
(a)
The technical and commercial management of the Existing Accommodation
Units (and any New Accommodation Unit owned by an Obligor) shall be
undertaken by the Issuer or a wholly owned Subsidiary of the Issuer, and no
Obligor shall, without prior written notice to the Bond Trustee, change the rig
manager and/or rig operator.
