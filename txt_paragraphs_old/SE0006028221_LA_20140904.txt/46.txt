The Bonds are not offered to and may not be subscribed by investors located in the United
States except for "Qualified Institutional Buyers" ("QIB") within the meaning of Rule 144A
under the Securities Act. In the application form relating to the Bonds, each person applying

Regulation S under the Securities Act, and if it is a U.S. person it must confirm, inter alia,
that it is a QIB.
