"Security" means a mortgage, charge, pledge, lien, security assignment or other security interest securing any obligation of any person, or any other agreement or arrangement having a similar effect.
"Security Documents" means:
(a) the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement;
(b) a share pledge agreement pursuant to which perfected Security is created over the shares in the Issuer;
(c) a real property mortgage agreement pursuant to which perfected Security is created over all mortgage certificates with a total face value of not less than SEK 350,000,000 in the Property;
(d) a business mortgage agreement pursuant to which perfected Security is created over all business mortgage certificates in the Issuer with a total face value of not less than SEK 24,396,000;
