Payments from the Lessee
17.3
Lease default
The Issuer shall:
(a) immediately inform the Agent of any notice of default or termination under the Lease Agreement;
(b) provide information regarding the Lease Agreement which the Agent reasonably requests;
(c) comply with all its material obligation under the Lease Agreement; and
(d) use all reasonable efforts to ensure that the Lessee operates the Property in accordance with good hotel industry standard.
