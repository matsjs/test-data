WRITTEN PROCEDURE
The Agent shall instigate a Written Procedure as soon as practicable and in any event no
later than ten (10) Business Days after receipt of a valid request from the Issuer or the
Bondholder(s) (or such later date as may be necessary for technical or administrative
reasons) by sending a communication to each person who is registered as a Bondholder on
a date selected by the Agent which is no more than five (5) Business Days earlier than the
date on which the communication is sent.
