The notice pursuant to Clause 21.1 shall include (i) time for the meeting, (ii) place for the
Bondholders), (iv) the day on which a person must be Bondholder in order to exercise
Bondholders' rights at the Bondholders' Meeting, and (v) a form of power of attorney.
Only matters that have been included in the notice may be resolved upon at the
Bondholders' Meeting. Should prior notification by the Bondholders be required in order
to attend the Bondholders' Meeting, such requirement shall be included in the notice.
21.3
The Bondholders' Meeting shall be held no earlier than ten (10) Business Days and no later
than thirty (30) Business Days after the effective date of the notice.
