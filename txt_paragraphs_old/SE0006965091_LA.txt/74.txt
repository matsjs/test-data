15.10 Financial Indebtedness restrictions
The Issuer shall not incur or permit to remain outstanding any Financial Indebtedness (whether secured or unsecured) other than:
(a) the Bonds;
(b) any intra-group loans from the Parent which is comprised by the Pledge of Issuer's Intra-Group Loans;
(c) prior to release of the Refinancing Proceeds from the Escrow Account, the Existing Debt; and
(d) arising as a result of the refinancing of the Notes in full.
