17.
PROPERTY COVENANTS
17.1
Inspection
The Issuer shall upon reasonable request of the Agent arrange for the Agent, and/or any persons appointed by the Agent, to have access to and to inspect the Property at the reasonable expense of the Issuer.
17.2
Payments from the Lessee
The Issuer shall procure, and quarterly provide satisfactory documentation to the Agent evidencing, that all payments from the Lessee to the Issuer under the Lease Agreement are paid on time. Delayed payment from the Lessee of 30 days or more shall in any event be reported to the Agent. The Issuer may not give the Lessee a grace period for any payments in excess of 45 days without the Agent's prior written approval.
