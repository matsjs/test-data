The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Issuer from that carried on at the Settlement Date, and/or as set out in the Finance Documents.
Disposal of business
The Issuer shall not sell or dispose of the Property unless a redemption is carried out in accordance with Clause 10.5 (Mandatory total redemption due to a Disposal Event or a Loss Event (call option)).
15.6
Arm's length transactions and affiliated parties
The Issuer shall not enter into any transaction with any person except on arm's length terms and for fair market value.
15.7
Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organisation or jurisdiction of incorporation, save for as set out in Clause 16.7.2.
