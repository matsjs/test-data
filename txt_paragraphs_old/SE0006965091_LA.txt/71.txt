15.2
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganisation involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer.
15.3
De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganisation involving a split of the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
15.4
Continuation of business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business, and shall procure that no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business of the Issuer from that carried on at the Settlement Date, and/or as set out in the Finance Documents.
