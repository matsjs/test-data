Access to staff and complying with instructions
Following an Event of Default and for as long as it is continuing, the Issuer shall, and shall ensure that the Lessee will, act in accordance with the Agent´s instructions and provide the Agent with access to staff, inventory and documentation for on-going hotel operations.
17.7
Insurance
The Issuer shall procure that in any event insurance (with reputable independent insurance companies or underwriters) shall be maintained on and in relation to its business and assets as is usual for companies carrying on the same or substantially similar business and to:
(a) include comprehensive insurance cover (fullvärdesförsäkring) of the Property and the plant and machinery on the Property (including fixtures and improvements but excluding fittings owned by any tenant) on a full reinstatement value basis;
(b) include comprehensive insurance cover for damages caused by natural forces (including all risk insurance cover for, but not limited to, fire, water leakage (including in respect of water pipes), storm, hail and flooding);
