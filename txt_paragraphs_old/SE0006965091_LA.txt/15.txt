"Property Market Value" means the market value on a Free and Clear Basis for the Property, as determined by the Property Advisor in the latest available valuation report obtained by the Issuer and delivered to the Agent pursuant to Clause 12.2.1(h) plus the nominal amount of any Refurbishment Proceeds applied in the Refurbishment of the Property after the date of the latest available valuation report.
"Property Portfolio" means the hotel properties at any time owned by a Group Company.
"Record Date" means the fifth (5) Business Day prior to (i) an Interest Payment Date, (ii) a Redemption Date, (iii) a date on which a payment to the Bondholders is to be made under Clause 19 (Distribution of proceeds) or (iv) another relevant date, or in each case such other Business Day falling prior to a relevant date if generally applicable on the Swedish bond market.
"Redemption Date" means the date on which the relevant Bonds are to be redeemed or repurchased in accordance with Clause 10 (Redemption and repurchase of the Bonds).
"Reference Date" means each of 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December, in each year.
"Reference Period" means, at any calculation date, the period of twelve months preceding such date.
"Refinancing Proceeds" means a maximum amount of SEK 306,000,000.
"Refurbishment" means the refurbishments and upgrades in the minimum amount of SEK 30,000,000 to be carried out after the Settlement Date and which shall be made for the purpose of maintaining or enhancing the Property Market Value.
