15.16 Investments
The Issuer shall not make any investments or capital expenditures, other than solely related to the ownership in and operation and maintenance of the Property provided such investments and capital expenditures are fully funded, including the Refurbishment Proceeds.
15.17 Business and management agreements
Except in relation to Permitted Management Fee Payments, the Issuer shall procure that its payments obligations under intra-group business and management agreements shall be subordinated to its obligations under the Finance Documents.
