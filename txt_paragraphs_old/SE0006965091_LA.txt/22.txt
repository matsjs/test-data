STATUS OF THE BONDS
The Bonds are denominated in Swedish Kronor and each Bond is constituted by these Terms and Conditions. The Issuer undertakes to make payments in relation to the Bonds and to comply with these Terms and Conditions.
By subscribing for Bonds, the initial Bondholder agrees that the Bonds shall benefit from and be subject to the Finance Documents and by acquiring Bonds, each subsequent Bondholder confirms such agreement.
The initial nominal amount of each Bond is SEK 1,000,000 (the "Initial Nominal Amount"). The aggregate nominal amount of the Bonds as of the Settlement Date is SEK 350,000,000. All Bonds issued on the Settlement Date are issued on a fully paid basis at an issue price of 99.55 per cent. of the Initial Nominal Amount.
