27.
NO DIRECT ACTIONS BY BONDHOLDERS
27.1
A Bondholder may not take any steps whatsoever against the Issuer or with respect to the
Transaction Security or the Guarantee to enforce or recover any amount due or owing to it
pursuant to the Finance Documents, or to initiate, support or procure the winding-up,
dissolution, liquidation, company reorganisation (företagsrekonstruktion) or bankruptcy
