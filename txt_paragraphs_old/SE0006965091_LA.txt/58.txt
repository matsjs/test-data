12.2
INFORMATION TO BONDHOLDERS
Information from the Issuer
The Issuer will procure that the following information is made available to the Bondholders by publication on the website of the Group or provision of copies thereof to the Bondholders and the Agent:
(a) as soon as the same become available, but in any event within 120 days after the end of each financial year, the audited non-consolidated financial statements for that financial year for the Parent and the Issuer, prepared in accordance with applicable accounting principles; and
(b) as soon as the same become available, but in any event within 30 days after the end of each quarter of the Issuer's financial year, its financial statements for such period prepared in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
The Issuer or the Parent, as applicable, shall without being requested to do so, promptly notify the Agent (with full particulars) in writing upon becoming aware of the occurrence of any event or circumstance which constitutes an Event of Default, or any event or circumstance which could reasonably be expected to lead to an Event of Default, and any other event which could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect, and shall provide the Agent with such further information as it may reasonably request in writing following receipt of such notice. Should the Agent not receive such information, the Agent is entitled to assume that no such event or circumstance exists or can be expected to occur, provided that the Agent does not have actual knowledge of such event or circumstance.
