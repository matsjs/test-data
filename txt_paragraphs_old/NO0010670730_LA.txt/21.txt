2.2 The Bonds
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 550,000,000 (Norwegian kroner fivehundredandfiftymillion).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rankpari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN AGR Petroleum Services Holdings AS Senior Secured Bond Issue 2013/2018".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0670730.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
