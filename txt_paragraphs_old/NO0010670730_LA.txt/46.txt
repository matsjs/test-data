13.3 General Covenants
The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at leastpari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
Each Guarantor's obligations under the Guarantees shall at all time rank at least pari passu with the claims of all its other unsubordinated creditors (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application).
(b) Mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization involving a consolidation of the assets and obligations of the Issuer or any Group Company with any other companies or entities if such transaction has or could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect.
(c) De-mergers
The Issuer shall not, and shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split of the Issuer or any Group Company into two or more separate companies or entities, if such transaction has or could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect.
(d) Continuation o f business
The Issuer shall not cease to carry on its business. The Issuer shall further ensure that no Group Company shall, cease to carry on its business if such transaction has or could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect.
