10.1 Maturity
The Bonds shall mature in full on the Maturity Date, and shall be repaid at par (100%) by the Issuer.
10.2 Call Option
10.2.1 Always subject to the Intercreditor Agreement, the Issuer may redeem the Bond Issue in whole (all or nothing) at any time from and including (Call Option):
(i) the Interest Payment Date in February 2014 to, but not including, the Interest Payment Date in February 2015 at a price equal to 110.00% of par value (plus accrued interest on redeemed amount);
