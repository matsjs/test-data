13.7 Covenants regarding the Parent
(a) Reporting The Parent shall of its own accord make management and financial reports (quarterly, on a consolidated basis, written in English) available to the Bond Trustee and on its web pages for public distribution not later than 120 days after the end of the financial year and not later than 90 days after the end of the relevant interim period.
