The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 400,000,000 (Norwegian kroner fourhundredmillion). The Bonds may comprise one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 200,000,000 (Norwegian kroner twohundredmillion)
The Bond Loan is an open bond loan, under which subsequent issues ("Tap Issues") may take place up to the maximum amount set out in Clause 2.2.1 in the period from and including the Issue Date to and including the Business Day falling five - 5 - Business Days prior to the Maturity Date.
All Tap Issues will be subject to identical terms in all respects. The rights and obligations of all parties to the Bond Agreement also apply for subsequent Tap Issues. The Bond Trustee will on the issuing of additional Tap Issues make an addendum to the Bond Agreement regulating the conditions for such Tap Issue.
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "10.50% Norwegian Car Carriers ASA Senior Unsecured Open Bond Issue 2010 / 2015".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 058826.2.
