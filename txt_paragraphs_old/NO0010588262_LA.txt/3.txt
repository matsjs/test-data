Definitions
"Account Manager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Register.
"Actual Value" means the fair market value of the Vessels in NOK determined as the arithmetic mean of independent valuation of the Vessels obtained from two of the Approved Ship Brokers for each of the Vessels. Such valuation shall be made (at the Issuer's request and expense) in connection with presentation of the Quarterly Financial Reports as per the Quarter Dates, on the basis of a sale for prompt delivery for cash at arm's length on normal commercial terms between a willing seller and willing buyer, on an "as is where is" basis, free of any existing charters or other contracts for employment.
"Adjusted Equity" means (on a consolidated basis for the Group) the aggregated booked equity adjusted for the difference between the Actual Value of the Vessels and the book value of the Vessels (positive or negative as the case may be).
"Approved Ship Brokers" means Hesnes Shipping AS, R.S. Platou Shipbrokers AS, Fearnleys AS and Maersk Broker KS and, with respect to valuation of Ro-Ro vessels and combined PCTC/RoRo Vessels only, Trollship S.A. and Barry Rogliano Salles, or any such other internationally renowned brokers as the Issuer may appoint and the Bond Trustee approve.
"Attachment" means any attachments to this Bond Agreement.
"Bond Agreement" means this bond agreement, including any Attachments to which it refers, and any subsequent amendments and additions agreed between the Parties.
"Bond Issue" means the bond issue constituted by the Bonds.
"Bondholder" means a holder of Bond(s), as registered in the Securities Register, from time to time.
