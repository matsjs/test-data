"Overdue Amount" means any amount required to be paid by the Issuer under any of the Finance Documents but not made available to the Bondholders on the relevant Payment Date or otherwise not paid on its applicable due date.
"Paying Agent" means DNB Bank ASA.
"Payment Date" means any Interest Payment Date or any Repayment Date.
"Permitted Financial Indebtedness" means:
(a) any Financial Indebtedness incurred pursuant to the Finance Documents (including any Tap Issue);
(b) any Financial Indebtedness incurred under the Existing Facility and any Financial Indebtedness incurred in the future under the Existing Facility;
(c) any Financial Indebtedness incurred under any exploration finance facility, provided that such exploration financing arrangement is provided by commercial banks on ordinary terms customary for such arrangements and whereby, under the terms thereof, the amount of Financial Indebtedness made available thereunder is set by reference to (and shall not exceed the tax value of the amount of) the Issuer's costs eligible for annual tax refund under the Norwegian Petroleum Tax Act of 13 June 1975 Section 3(c);
(d) any credits for goods or services in the ordinary course of trading;
