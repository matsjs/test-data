A Group Company may only enter into a hedge or derivatives arrangement to manage or hedge, directly or indirectly, actual or anticipated exposures arising in the ordinary course of business and on a non-speculative basis.
Investment in the FPSO Group
Unless all members of the FPSO Group are comprised by the definition of "Group", no Group Company shall make any equity injections into any member of the FPSO Group for an aggregate amount exceeding USD 10,000,000.
