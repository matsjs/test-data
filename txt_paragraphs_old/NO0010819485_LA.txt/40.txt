8.4 Taxation
(a) If an Obligor is required by law to withhold any tax from any payment in respect of the Bonds or other payments under the Finance Documents the amount of the payment due will be grossed-up to such amount which is (after making the required withholding) equal to the payment which would have been received if no withholding had been required.
(b) Any public fees levied on the trade of Bonds in the secondary market shall be paid by the Bondholders, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation, and the Issuer shall not be responsible for reimbursing any such fees.
