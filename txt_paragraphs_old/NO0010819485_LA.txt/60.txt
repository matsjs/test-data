The Parent shall ensure that the Group at the time of testing has a Leverage Ratio of maximum 3.50x calculated on a rolling last twelve months basis in accordance with GAAP.
The Leverage Ratio shall be tested on 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December each year.
(c) General
Compliance with the requirements in paragraphs (a) and (b) above shall be calculated and measured on a consolidated basis for the Group by reference to the relevant Interim Accounts the Parent (adjusted to exclude Subsidiaries not part of the Group) and shall be certified by way of a Compliance Certificate provided together with the relevant Interim Accounts.
