14.6 Negative pledge
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Parent shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, create or allow to subsist, retain, provide, prolong or renew any Security over any of its/their assets (whether present or future).
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Security.
14.7 Financial support
(a) Except as permitted under paragraph (b) below, the Parent shall not, and shall procure that no other Group Company will, be a creditor in respect of any Financial Support to or for the benefit of any person not being a Group Company.
(b) Paragraph (a) above does not apply to any Permitted Loan or Permitted Guarantee.
