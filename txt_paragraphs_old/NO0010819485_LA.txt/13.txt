"Interest Period" means, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Business Day Convention, the six months period ending on 20 March and 20 September each year, provided however that an Interest Period shall not extend beyond the Maturity Date. "Interest Rate" means 8.5 % percentage points per annum.
"IPO Event" means an initial public offering of shares in the Issuer or the Parent, after which such shares shall be quoted, listed, traded or otherwise admitted to trading on the Exchange.
"ISIN" means International Securities Identification Number - the identification number of the Bonds.
"Issue Date" means 20 March 2018.
"Issuer" means the company designated as such in the preamble to these Bond Terms.
"Issuer's Bonds" means any Bonds which are owned by an Obligor or any Affiliate of an Obligor.
"Leverage Ratio" means Net Debt to EBITDAX.
"Liquidity" means the aggregate book value of the Group's Cash and Cash Equivalents.
