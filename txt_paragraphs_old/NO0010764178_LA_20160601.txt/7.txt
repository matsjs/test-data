1.1 Definitions
In this Bond Agreement, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
"Account M anager" means a Bondholder's account manager in the Securities Depository.
"Amendment Agreement" means the amendment to the Existing Bond Agreement dated 19 April 2016 and entered into between the Issuer, the Ultimate Parent and the Bond Trustee (on behalf of the Existing Bondholders) being attached as Attachment 1.
"Assignment of G roup Loans" means an assignment by way o f security from a Group Company as a lender of all its rights to and title and interest, whether present or future, under any Group Loans to the Issuer, the Parent or ShaMaran Services SA as borrower, in favour of the Bondholders and the Existing Bondholders represented by the Bond Trustee. The assignment agreement to be applied is enclosed to this Agreement as Attachment 2.
"Assignment of Insurances" means an assignment o f the Issuer's insurances (other than any third party liability insurances).
"Atrush Block" means the contract area as defined in the Atrush PSC covering an area of 269 km2 and located approximately 85 km northwest of Erbil, the capital of the Kurdistan Region o f Iraq.
"Atrush PSC" means the production sharing contract between the Issuer and the Kurdistan Regional Government of Iraq under which the Issuer holds a 26.8% working interest in the Production Sharing Contract in respect of the Atmsh Block until such time that the Kurdistan Regional Government ("KRG") has completed the exercise of its right to acquire up to a 25.0% interest (upon which the working interest of the Issuer would be reduced to 20.1%), being named "Production Sharing Contract, Atrush Block, Kurdistan Region between The Kurdistan Regional Government of Iraq and General Exploration Partners, Inc." and dated 10 November 2007, with "First Amendment" dated 1 August 2010, "TPI Assignment, Novation, and Second Amendment Agreement" dated 20 October 2010 and "Assignment, Novation, and Third Amendment Agreement" dated 31 December 2012.
