15.1.1.3 Breach o f obligations by Obligors
Any Obligor does not comply with any provision pursuant to any Finance Document binding on such Obligor, unless, in the opinion of the Bond Trustee, such failure is capable of being remedied and is remedied within ten Business Days after notice thereof is given to the Issuer by the Bond Trustee.
