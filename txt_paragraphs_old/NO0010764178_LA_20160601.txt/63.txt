13.3 General Covenants
The Issuer shall ensure that its obligations under this Bond Agreement and any other Finance Document shall at all time rank at least pari passu as set out in Clause 8.1.
13.3.1.2 Mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any merger or other business combination or corporate reorganization.
13.3.1.3 De-mergers
The Issuer shall not carry out any de-merger or other corporate reorganization involving a split o f the Issuer into two or more separate companies or entities.
