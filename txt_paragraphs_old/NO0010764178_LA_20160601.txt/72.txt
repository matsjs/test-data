13.4.1.7 Financial Assistance restrictions
The Issuer shall not grant any loans, guarantees or other financial assistance to any Group Company and/or any third party, except (i) guarantees issued for the benefit of third parties in the ordinary course o f business, and (ii) guarantees required under the Project Documents.

13.4.1.8 Related party transactions
The Issuer shall not engage directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course o f business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement o f the Issuer's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
13.4.1.9 New Group Loans
The Issuer shall, prior to any Group Loan being established, ensure that the Assignment of Group Loans is duly executed by the relevant Group Companies and that the relevant Group Companies have acceded to the Intercreditor Agreement.
