13.4.1.2 Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase o f shares or make loans or other capital distributions to its shareholders (included but not limited to total return swaps involving any shares issued by any party) save for an aggregate annual amount limited to USD 6 million to cover technical, management and administrative services within the Group.
