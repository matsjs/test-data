2 The Loan
2.1 The Borrower has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 140,000,000 (Norwegian kroner one hundred and forty million), herein referred to as the "Loan".
The Bonds will be in denominations of NOK 500,000 each and rank pari passu.
The Loan will be described as "FRN Sølvtrans AS Callable Bond Issue 2007/2010".
The registration number (ISIN) of the Loan will be NO 001 037825.0.
