Whenever used in this Loan Agreement the following terms shall have the following meaning:
Balance Sheet Reporting
Dates:
the last day in each quarter.
Bank Debt:
Existing Debt and new secured debt established by the Group in connection with acquisition of Vessels provided that the initial amount drawn of such debt shall not exceed 70 % of the acquisition or construction cost per Vessel, always provided that for the four new build to be delivered in 2007 and 2008 initial amount drawn shall not exceed 80 % of the acquisition or construction cost per such Vessel. The Borrower can not during the term of this Loan refinance the Bank Debt except for existing debt related to the Vessels "MS RoniaFisk" and "MS Ronia" that can be refinanced with new debt not exceeding 70 % of market value of the two said Vessels. The Bank Debt shall be amortised as set forth in Clause 13.2 (j).
