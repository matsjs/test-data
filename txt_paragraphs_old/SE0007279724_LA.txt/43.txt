13.3 Listing of Bonds
The Issuer shall ensure that the Bonds are listed at the corporate bond list on Nasdaq Stockholm no later than one (1) year after the First Issue Date and shall take all measures required to ensure that the Bonds, once listed on Nasdaq Stockholm, continue being listed on Nasadaq Stockholm for as long as any Bond is outstanding (however, taking into account the rules and regulations of Nasdaq Stockholm and the CSD (as amended from time to time) preventing trading in the Bonds in close connection to the redemption of the Bonds).
13.4 Nature of Business
The Issuer shall procure that, following the Acquisition, no substantial change is made to the general nature of the business carried on by the Group if such substantial change would have a Material Adverse Effect.
13.5 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer and its Subsidiaries have a right to incur Financial Indebtedness that constitutes Permitted Debt.
