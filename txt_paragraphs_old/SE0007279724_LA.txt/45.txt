13.7 Negative Pledge
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure Financial Indebtedness.
(b) Notwithstanding paragraph (a) above, a Group Company shall (i) have the right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security, (ii) retain, but not prolong or renew, any existing security in relation to indebtedness held by an entity acquired by a Group Company, and (iii) retain, prolong or renew, any existing security in relation to indebtedness that constitutes Permitted Debt held by an entity acquired by a Group Company.
13.8 Dealings with Related Parties
The Issuer shall, and shall procure that its Subsidiaries, conduct all dealings with the direct and indirect shareholders of the Group Companies (excluding other Group Companies) and/or any Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders on arm's length terms.
