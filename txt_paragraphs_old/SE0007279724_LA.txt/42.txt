The Issuer undertakes to (and shall, where applicable, procure that each other Group Company will) comply with the undertakings set out in this Clause 13 for as long as any Bonds remain outstanding.
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries will:
(i) pay any dividend on its shares to the Issuer's direct or indirect shareholders;
(ii) repurchase any of its own shares;
(iii) redeem or reduce its share capital or other restricted equity with repayment to shareholders;
(iv) make any payments or prepayments of principal or interest under any Shareholder Loans;
(v) grant any loans, except to Group Companies; or

(vi) make any other similar distributions or transfers of value to the direct or indirect shareholders of the Issuer or its Subsidiaries, or any or the Affiliates of such direct and indirect shareholders.
