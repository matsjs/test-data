13.6 Disposal of Assets
(a) The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that no Group Company will, sell or otherwise dispose of any shares in any Group Company, or of any substantial assets (including but not limited to material intellectual property rights) or operations other than:
(i) disposals made by a Group Company to another Group Company;
(ii) disposals made in the ordinary course of business of the disposing entity;
(Hi) disposals of obsolete and redundant assets;
