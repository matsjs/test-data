10.5 Voluntary Partial Prepayment
(a) The Issuer may at any time, on one (1) occasion during each Reference Period commencing twelve (12) months after the First Issue Date, repay up to 10.00 per cent, of the Total Nominal Amount, in which case all outstanding Bonds shall be partially repaid by way of reducing the Outstanding Nominal Amount of each Bond pro rata.
(b) Partial prepayment in accordance with paragraph (a) above shall equal the repaid percentage of the Outstanding Nominal Amount (rounded down to the nearest SEK 1.00) and shall be made at a price equivalent to 102 per cent, of the prepaid amount, together with accrued but unpaid interest on the prepaid amount.
(c) Following a partial prepayment in accordance with paragraph (a), the Issuer may not, at any time, issue any Subsequent Bonds.
