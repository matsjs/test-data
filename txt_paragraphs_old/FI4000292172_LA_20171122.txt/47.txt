12.2 Listing of the Bonds
The Issuer shall ensure that (i) the Initial Bonds are listed on the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Helsinki within 60 days after the First Issue Date and with an intention to complete such listing within 30 days after the First Issue Date, (ii) any Subsequent Bonds are listed on the corporate bond list of Nasdaq Helsinki within 60 days after the issuance of such Subsequent Bonds and with an intention to complete such listing within 30 days after the issuance of such Subsequent Bonds, and (iii) the Bonds, once
