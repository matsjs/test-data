3. Purpose of the Bonds
3.1 Use of Proceeds
The Net Proceeds of the Initial Bond issue shall be applied towards:
(a) repayment in full of the Existing Bonds; and
(b) general corporate purposes of the Group, including investments and acquisitions.
Any proceeds from a Subsequent Bond issue shall be applied towards general corporate purposes of the Group, including investments and acquisitions.
