14. Events of Default and Acceleration of the Bonds
Each of the events or circumstances set out in this Clause 14 (except Clause 14.10) is an Event of Default.
14.1 Non-Payment
The Issuer fails to pay an amount on the date it is due in accordance with the Finance Documents unless the non-payment:
(a) is caused by technical or administrative error; and
(b) is remedied within five (5) Business Days after the due date.
