12.4 Financial Indebtedness
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, incur any additional Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Issuer and the Subsidiaries have a right to incur Financial Indebtedness that constitute Permitted Debt.
12.5 Negative Pledge
The Issuer shall not, and shall procure that none of its Subsidiaries, provide, prolong or renew any guarantee or security over any of its/their assets (present or future) to secure any Financial Indebtedness, provided however that the Group Companies have a right to provide, prolong and renew any Permitted Security.
12.6 Ownership and Disposal of Assets
(a) The Issuer may in its ordinary course of business (i) acquire or dispose of trucks, trailers and containers, provided that it shall at all times own at least 9,000 containers and (ii) sub-lease containers for the purpose of avoiding empty storage costs at ports.
(b) The Issuer may not dispose of the Existing Vessel unless such disposal is carried out at fair market value and on terms and conditions customary for such transaction and provided that the net proceeds from such disposal are immediately applied towards repayment of the Bonds by way of reducing the Outstanding Nominal Amount of each Bond pro rata.
