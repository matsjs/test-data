The Issuer shall procure that the Guarantors shall not change flag, name or registry without the consent of the Bond Trustee (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld).
Maintenance and repair
The Issuer shall ensure that the Guarantors will keep the Vessels in a good and safe condition and maintained and repaired consistent with prudent ownership and good industiy standards, During operation of the Vessel, the Issuer shall procure that the Guarantors exercise its rights under any Charters that the Vessel is properly maintained according to a pre-agreed planned maintenance system.
