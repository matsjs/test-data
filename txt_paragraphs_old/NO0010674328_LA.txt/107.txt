Insurance
The Issuer shall keep a mortgagee's interest insurance equal to at least no% of the outstanding amount under the Bond Agreement on standard market terms in respect of vessels of this type.
The insurances and loss payable clause shall be in accordance with the Norwegian Marine Insurance Plan or on London terms or at least similar terms.

Class
The Issuer shall ensure that the Guarantors will procure that the Vessels are classified and maintained in the following class: DNV1A1, ICE-C, OILREC, SF, LFL*, COMF-V(3)C(3), Eo, DYNPOS-AUTR, NAUT-OSV(A), CLEAN DESIGN, DK(+), HL(2.8), during the tenor o f the Bonds.
Flag, name and registry
The Issuer shall ensure that the Guarantors will procure that the Vessels are registered in the Norwegian Ordinary Ship Register and shall not cause such registration being forfeited or imperilled. The Issuer shall procure that the Guarantors shall not change flag, name or registry without the consent of the Bond Trustee (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld).
