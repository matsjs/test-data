13.5.3
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall not sell or dispose of all or a substantial part of its operations (however, for the avoidance of doubt, a disposal o f a Vessel is allowed provided that it is carried out in accordance with the Mandatory Prepayment provisions below, provided that the Bond Trustee and the Bondholders will receive full payment of any amounts due to them under the Finance Documents).
13.5.4
Merger
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall not de-merge, merge, merge or carry out any other corporate restructuring (including change of jurisdiction).
13.5.5
Contractual arrangements
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall not make any other financial or other arrangements concerning its Vessel and its employment other than provided for in the Finance Documents, which is likely to have a Material Adverse Effect on the Guarantor's ability to fulfil its obligations under the Guarantee.
