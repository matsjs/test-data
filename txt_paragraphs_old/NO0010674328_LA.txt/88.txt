13.4.2
Dividends and other distributions
The Issuer shall not without the approval of the Bond Trustee or, where necessaiy, the bondholders' meeting declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions to its shareholders.
13.4.3
Constitutional documents
The Issuer shall not without the approval of the Bond Trustee or, where necessary, the bondholders' meeting amend its constitutional documents in such a way which would have a Material Adverse Effect.
