2 .2
The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOI< 500,000,000 (Norwegian kronerfive hundred million only).
The Face Value is NOK 500,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Troms Offshore Supply AS Senior Secured Callable Bond Issue 2013/2016".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001067432.8.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
2.3
Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds of the Bonds shall be applied firstly to repay all and any existing debt relating directly to the Vessels, secondly to repay the Corporate Facility and thirdly for general corporate purposes of the Group.
