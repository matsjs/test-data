13.3.7
Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not engage, or permit to engage, directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any related party (without limitation, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service), except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer's or such member of the Group's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are not less favourable to the Issuer or such member of the Group, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained in an arm's length transaction at the time.
13.3.8 Corporate status The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or jurisdiction of incorporation.
