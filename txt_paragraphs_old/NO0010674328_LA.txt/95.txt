Information covenants
The Issuer shall ensure that each Guarantor shall prepare unconsolidated and consolidated (if applicable) annual audited and semi-annual interim unaudited reports written in English and make such reports available on their website(s) or the Issuer's website (in addition to sending them to the Bond Trustee as soon as they become available, and not later than 150 days after the end of the financial year and not later than 60 days after the end of the relevant interim period.
The Bond Trustee may and shall upon request distribute the reports above to the bondholders and any relevant party requesting such report, provided that the Bond Trustee is provided coverage by the bondholders for any costs incurred (unless covered by the Issuer).
