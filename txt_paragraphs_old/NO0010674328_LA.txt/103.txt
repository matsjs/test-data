Financial covenants
Definitions For the purpose of this Bond Agreement: "Book Equity" means the aggregate book value (on a consolidated basis) of the Group's total equity treated as equity in accordance with GAAP.
"Excess Value" means the difference (positive or negative) between the book value o f the Vessels or other vessels owned by a Group Company (as the case may be) and their Market Value on the date of such accounts.
"GAAP" means International Financial Reporting Standards or generally accepted accounting principles in Norway, applicable for the Issuer from time to time.
