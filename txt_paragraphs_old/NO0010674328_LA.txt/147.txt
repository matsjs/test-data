Notices, contact information
Written notices, warnings, summons etc to the Bondholders made by the Bond Trustee shall be sent via the Securities Depositoiy with a copy to the Issuer and the Exchange. Information to the Bondholders may also be published at Stamdata only. Any such notice or communication shall be deemed to be given or made as follows:
(a)
if by letter via the Securities Depositoiy, when sent from the Securities
Depositoiy; and
(b)
if by publication on Stamdata, when publicly available.
