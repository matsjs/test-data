Insurance
13.5 Financial Covenants
a) Equity Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains an Equity Ratio of minimum 30 %.
b) Gearing Ratio
The Issuer shall ensure that the Group maintains a Gearing Ratio of maximum 5.00x at any time.
The Financial Covenants will apply for the Issuer (on a consolidated basis) at all times and will be tested on a semi-annual basis on each Calculation Date and reported within each Reporting Date.
