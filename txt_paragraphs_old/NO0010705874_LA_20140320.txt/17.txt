2.2 The Bonds
2.2.1 The Issuer has resolved to issue a series of Bonds in the maximum amount of NOK 600 000 000 (Norwegian kroner sixhundredmillion). The Bond Issue may comprise of one or more tranches issued on different issue dates. The first tranche will be in the amount of NOK 400 000 000 (Norwegian kroner fourhundredmillion).
The Face Value is NOK 1,000,000. The Bonds shall rank pari passu between themselves.
The Bond Issue will be described as "FRN Klaveness Ship Holding AS Senior Unsecured Callable Bond Issue 2014/2020".
The ISIN of the Bond Issue will be NO 001 0705874.
The tenor of the Bonds is from and including the Issue Date to the Maturity Date.
