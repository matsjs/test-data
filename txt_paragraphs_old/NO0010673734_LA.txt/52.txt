10.6 10.6.1
Change of control
Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control Event, each Bondholder shall have the right to require that the Issuer redeems its Bonds (a "Put Option") at a price of 101% of par value plus accrued interest.
10.6.2 The Put Option must be exercised within sixty days after the Issuer has delivered notification to the Bond Trustee of a Change of Control Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event has taken place.
