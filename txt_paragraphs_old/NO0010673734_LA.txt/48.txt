10.2.3 Partial redemption shall be carried out pro rata (in accordance with the procedures of the Securities Depository).
10.2.4
On the settlement date of a Call Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, in respect of each such Bond, the principal amount of such Bond (including any premium as stated above) and any unpaid interest accrued up to the settlement date.
10.2.5 Bonds redeemed by the Issuer in accordance with this Clause 10.2 shall be discharged against the Outstanding Bonds and immediately cancelled.
10.2.6
The Issuer may, in its absolute discretion, whether by one transaction or separate transactions and whether at any one time or over a period of time, redeem the amount of Outstanding Bonds equivalent to the Redemption Amount of any of the Vessels (each a "Refinance Event").
10.2.7
Upon receipt of written notice to the Bond Trustee of a Relevant Disposal or a Refinance Event by a Shipowning Subsidiary and subject to the conditions set out in Clause 10.3 below, all assets related to such Relevant Disposal or Refinance Event shall be released from any Security, and the Bond Trustee shall be authorized to take any action to effect any such release (including, but not limited to, effecting the release of the Vessel Mortgage over such Vessel (from which time such Vessel shall cease to be a Vessel (as defined herein)) and any share pledge granted over the shares of any Designated Shipowning Subsidiary) and such Shipowning Subsidiary shall be released from any obligations under the Finance Documents as a Shipowning Subsidiary (from which time such Shipowning Subsidiary shall cease to be a Shipowning Subsidiary (as defined herein)).
