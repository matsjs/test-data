2.3 Purpose and utilization
The net proceeds from the Bond Issue (net of legal costs, fees of the Manager and the Bond Trustee and any other agreed costs and expenses) shall be employed to (i) prepay the approximately USD 156,500,000 million outstanding as at 31 January 2013 under the HBOS Facility plus accrued interest and related obligations (including, if required, placing up to USD 15,000,000 with HBOS or Lloyds as cash collateral for any existing guarantee commitments HBOS has issued pursuant to the HBOS Facility and which are to be maintained after the Issue Date (the "Cash Collateral'1)); (if) prepay the Existing Bond Loan plus accrued interest and related obligations, (iii) finance capital expenditure related to the Vessels, Newbuildings and Jascon 18 including deposits, instalments and partial payments as accrued and paid from 1 January 2012, and (iv) general corporate purposes up to an amount of USD 50 million.
