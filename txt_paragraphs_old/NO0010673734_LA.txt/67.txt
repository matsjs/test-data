(ii) the Permitted Financial Indebtedness.
The Issuer shall on a monthly basis ensure that an amount equal to 1/3 of the next interest payment under the Bond Issue is transferred to the Debt Service Retention Account. Furthermore, the Issuer shall, on a monthly basis commencing on the date falling 30 months after the Issue Date ensure that an amount equal to 1/3 of the next Instalment is transferred to the Debt Service Retention Account.
(e) Redomiciliation
The Issuer shall ensure that following the Redomiciliation of any Group Company, the Bond Trustee shall be provided with the equivalent or substantially similar security that it had prior to such Redomiciliation.
(f) Dividend restrictions
The Issuer shall not declare or make any dividend payment, repurchase of shares or make other distributions or payments to its shareholders (including servicing of shareholder loans), whether in cash or in kind, including without limitation any total return swaps or instruments with similar effect exceeding 20% of the Group's consolidated net profit after taxes based on the audited annual accounts for the previous financial year. Any un-utilized portion of the permitted dividend pursuant to the above may not be carried forward to any subsequent financial year. For the avoidance of doubt, any salary payable to Mr Jacques Roomans shall be excluded from the restrictions constituted herein subject to an overall cap of USD 5 million per annum (which amount shall be subject to an annual escalation of 5%).
(g) Capex restrictions
The Issuer undertakes not to apply any proceeds from the Bond Issue to finance any investments or capital expenditures related to the vessel currently named Jascon 35.
