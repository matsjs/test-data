Covenants regarding the Guarantors and Shipowning Subsidiaries (a) Pari passu ranking
The Issuer shall ensure that the obligations of each Guarantor and Shipowning Subsidiary under any Finance Document to which it is a party shall at all time rank at least pari passu with all other obligations of the Guarantor (save for such claims which are preferred by bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation or other similar laws of general application) and shall rank ahead of subordinated debt.
(b) Corporate status
The Issuer shall ensure that no Guarantor or Shipowning Subsidiary shall change its type of organization or, subject to the Redomiciliation, its jurisdiction of incorporation.
(c) The accounts
The Issuer shall procure that it and / or the Guarantors maintain the Earnings Accounts and the Debt Service Retention Account with first class international banks (with minimum "A" credit rating from S6P or Moody's). The Issuer shall ensure that all earnings receivable in US Dollars related to the Vessels shall be paid directly from the relevant contracting party to the relevant Earnings Accounts.
