"Earnings Account" means an account in the name of a Guarantor, pledged in favour of the Bond Trustee.
"Earnings Account Pledge" means a first priority pledge over the claims of each Guarantor holding an Earnings Account against the banks for the amounts from time to time standing to the credit of the respective Guarantor on such Earnings Account.
"EBITDA" means the Group's aggregate earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (to be calculated on a 12-month rolling basis and excluding any exceptional, extraordinary, one-off, non-recurring and unusual accounting items according to IFRS). "Enhanced Participation Scheme" means the cash-based bonus scheme in place between the Issuer and certain key employees of the Group.
