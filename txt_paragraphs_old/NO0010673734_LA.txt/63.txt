(f) Arm's length transactions
The Issuer shall not, and the Issuer shall ensure that no other Group Company shall, engage in, either directly or indirectly, any transaction with any party (including without limitation, employment, the purchase, sale or exchange of assets or the rendering of any service, except in the ordinary course of business and pursuant to the reasonable requirement of the Issuer or such Group Company's business and upon fair and reasonable terms that are no less favourable to the Issuer or such Group Company, as the case may be, than those which might be obtained on arm's length terms and for fair market value at the time.
(g) Corporate status
The Issuer shall not change its type of organization or, subject to any Redomiciliation, jurisdiction of incorporation.
