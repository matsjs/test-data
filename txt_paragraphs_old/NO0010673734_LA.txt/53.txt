10.6.2 The Put Option must be exercised within sixty days after the Issuer has delivered notification to the Bond Trustee of a Change of Control Event. Such notification shall be given as soon as possible after a Change of Control Event has taken place.
The Put Option may be exercised by each Bondholder by giving written notice of the request to its Account Manager. The Account Manager shall notify the Paying Agent of the redemption request. The settlement date of the Put Option shall be the third Business Day after the end of the sixty days exercise period of the Put Option.
10.6.4
On the settlement date of the Put Option, the Issuer shall pay to each of the Bondholders holding Bonds to be redeemed, the principal amount of each such Bond (including any premium pursuant to Clause 10.6.1) and any unpaid interest accrued up to (but not including) the settlement date.
10.7 Jascon 18 Sale Event
On the occurrence of a Jascon 18 Sale Event, the amount of each Instalment of principal described in Clause 10.1 (excluding the amount due on the Maturity Date) shall be increased by 1% of the maximum amount of the Bonds stated in Clause 2.2 multiplied by the aggregate proportion of interest in Jascon 18 (or of the shares in the relevant Group Company owning Jascon 18) which has been sold or otherwise disposed of.
