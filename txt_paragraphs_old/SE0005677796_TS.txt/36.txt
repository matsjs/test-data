INTEREST
The Bonds will bear interest at the Interest Rate applied to the Nominal
Amount from, but excluding, the Issue Date up to and including the
relevant Redemption Date. Any Bond issued pursuant to a Subsequent
Bond Issue will, however, carry Interest at the Interest Rate from, but
excluding, the Interest Payment Date falling immediately prior to its
issuance up to and including the relevant Redemption Date.
