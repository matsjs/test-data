20.2 Duties of the Agent
20.2.1
The Agent shall represent the Holders in accordance with these Terms and Conditions. However, the Agent is not responsible for the execution or enforceability of these Terms and Conditions, the Escrow Account Pledge Agreement, the Agent Agreement, subordination agreements relating to Shareholder Loans or other documents entered into by the Agent relating to these Terms and Conditions. 20.2.2 The Agent shall upon request by a Holder disclose the identity of any other Holder who has consented to the Agent in doing so.
20.2.3
When acting in accordance with these Terms and Conditions, the Agent is always acting with binding effect on behalf of the Holders. The Agent shall carry out its duties under these Terms and Conditions in a reasonable, proficient and professional manner, with reasonable care and skill.
