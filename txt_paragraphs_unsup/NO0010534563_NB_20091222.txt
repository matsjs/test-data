A Bondholders' meeting has been held pursuant to the summons of 11 December 2009 (the "Summons"). Based on recent negotiations with the

<<<

 Bondholders, the Borrower has decided to amend the Proposal included in the Summons. The Amended Proposal, as defined below, was considered on today's Bondholders' meeting. There were sufficient Bondholders present at the meeting to form a

The Amended Proposal
The Borrower proposes the following amendment of the Proposal (the "Amended Proposal"):

1.1 Temporary Waiver of the

<<<

 Leverage Ratio covenant

The Borrower is proposing a temporary waiver of the Leverage Ratio, implying that:

(i) From and including the date of this Bondholders' meeting to and including the 4th quarter of 2010, the Leverage Ratio shall be

<<<

 equal to or lower than 7.0:1.0;
(ii) From (but not including the 4th quarter of 2010 to and including the 2nd quarter of 2011, the Leverage Ratio shall be equal to or lower than 6.0:1.0; and
(iii) Thereafter, the Leverage Ratio shall be equal

<<<

 to or lower than 5.0:1.0.

For the avoidance of doubt, EBITDA shall be calculated on a 12 months rolling basis, based on the 4 latest quarters. Further, if a sale of assets is carried out, the EBITDA calculation shall be adjusted with the EBITDA

<<<

 generated by the disposed asset.

If the Borrower is not in compliance with the aforesaid Leverage Ratio, each Bondholder shall have a put option at 100 % (par) plus accrued interest.
