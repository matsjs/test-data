Norsk Tillitsmann ASA ("NT") is appointed as loan trustee for the above mentioned bond issue (the "Loan") where Delphin Kreuzfahrten GmbH is the issuer

<<<

 (the "Company").

All capitalized terms used herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the loan agreement dated 27 February 2007 (the "Loan Agreement"), unless otherwise stated.

The Loan is a fixed rate callable first lien bond issue

<<<

 with a coupon of 9.25 % p.a. Remaining Loan amount is NOK 120 million. The Loan is secured by i.a. the cruise ship MV Delphin (the "Vessel").

Due to liquidity problems, an instalment of NOK 15,000,000 and interest payment was not paid when

<<<

 due on 28 February 2008. Pursuant to a Bondholders' meeting held on 7 March 2008 a new payment schedule plan was decided (cf Amendment Agreement of 7 March 2008), where i.a. the Loan runs with default interest (14,25 %).

The Company failed
