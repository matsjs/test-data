Rubicon Offshore Holdings Limited Group Management Financial Statements For the three months ended 31 December 2011
Background
Rubicon is an offshore oilfield service company focused on the ownership and operation of two floating, production

<<<

, storage and off-loading vessels ("FPSOs").
Management review
Rubicon is pleased to present its latest management report.
The Rubicon Intrepid is located at the Galoc Field, offshore the Philippines, where it is contracted to the Galoc Production

<<<

 Company ("GPC"). The FPSO remained at the field until 24 November 2011, when production was shut-in and the vessel disconnected from the mooring and riser system. The vessel then sailed to Singapore, where it entered Keppel Shipyard for the

<<<

 integration of the new turret and to undergo a special survey. For the period during the 4th quarter that the vessel was on location offshore, production uptime was approximately 94.6% (98.0% for 2011).
The Rubicon Vantage is located at the

<<<

 Bualuang Field, offshore Thailand, where it is contracted to Salamander Energy. The vessel continued to achieve good levels of production uptime during the 4th quarter of 99.9% (99.7% for 2011).
